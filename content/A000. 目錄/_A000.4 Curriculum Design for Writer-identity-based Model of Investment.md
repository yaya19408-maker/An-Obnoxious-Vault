# For instructor - Rubric 
## agency, capital usage

#### agency (criteria)
- **主觀 subjective voice**
	- personal ideas
	- perspective
	- past experience (esp. summative value, viz., the integration of social capital, or cultural capital)
- **客觀 objective voice**
	- authoritative voices (info from government, organization, scholars...)
	- others' voices to student himself/herself in the past
	- online information
	- ChatGPT's feedback
#### capital usage
- **context:** around second (e.g., looking for definition from ChatGPT) to third digital divide (engage in writing with ChatGPT) (Ragnedda, 2017, 2018, 2020)
- **social capital**: social relations
	- information from who (esp. family, teacher, classmates, cram school teacher...)
- **cultural capita**l: style preference, skills, knowledge
	- information from where (e.g., learned from novels, textbooks, storybooks, magazines...)
- **digital capital**: any information online
	- For example, from social media, YouTube, forums, MOOCs, online library...
- absent
	- political: sensitive in the context of academia
	- symbolic: not every student has reputation
	- economic: meaningless here
### Reference
modified based on Berzonsky (1989) 3 identity styles
1. **informational**: active seeking, self-reflection, informative orientation, constantly learning new things about the self, sceptical thinking, openness to new information, criticism.
2. **normative**: adopting expectations, values, and ChatGPTms from significant others.
3. **diffuse-avoidant**: procrastination and defensive avoidance of situations that require decision making.
### Materials
- **original essay:** to know students' pre-existing capitals, used for needs analysis & comparison
- **conversation history with ChatGPT**: know how much information they negotiate with ChatGPT
- **prompt** (Markdown, like this format), e.g., setting
	- genre (like what kind of writing)
	- writing style (tone)
	- task: such as grammar-checking, paraphrasing, active/passive voice
	- directly generating and no further revising is NOT allowed
- **revised essay** (the density of capital usage & changes)
### Grading
#### Priority

| rank | voice                                                         | agency                       | capital usage | Berzonskian identity style                        |
| ---- | ------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| 1st  | **subjective first, then objective**                          | strong                       | strong        | informational                                     |
| 2nd  | **objective first, then subjective**                          | mild                         | strong        | informational ~ normative                         |
| 3rd  | **subjective only**                                           | strong (based on experience) | weak          | informational (deep) / diffuse-avoidant (surface) |
| 4th  | **objective only**                                            | weak                         | strong        | normative                                         |
| 5th  | **authoritative voices by ChatGPT + fact-checking & writing** | weak                         | weak          | normative                                         |
| 6th  | **generate authoritative voices, then write**                 | weak                         | scarce        | normative ~ diffuse-avoidant                      |
| 7th  | **fully depend on ChatGPT**                                   | none                         | none          | diffuse-avoidant                                  |
