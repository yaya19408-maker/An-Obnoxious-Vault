☀ <span class="category">**在某地 到某地：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**home**</span> [həʊm] 
<span class="definition">adv. 到正確的位置：</span>She leaned on the door and pushed the bolt home. 她倚在門上，上好了門閂。/ He drove the ball home (= scored a goal) from 15 metres. 他從15米遠處勁射破門得分。/ The torpedo struck home on the hull of the ship. 魚雷正擊中船身。

<span class="vocabulary">**down**</span> [daʊn] 
<span class="definition">adv. [非正式] 到（或在）當地的一個地方（例如商店、酒館等）：</span>I’m just going down to the post office. 我正要到那邊的郵局去。/ I saw him down at the shops. 我剛才看到他在那邊的商店裡。

<span class="vocabulary">**somewhere**</span> ['sʌmweə] 
<span class="definition">adv.（美也用someplace）在某處；到某處（說話者不知道該處叫什麼名字，或不提它的名字）：</span>I’ve seen him somewhere before. 我以前在哪兒見過他。/ He went to school in York or somewhere. 他是在約克還是什麼地方上的學。/ I have to go somewhere else first. 我得先去別的地方。/ I needed somewhere to live. 我需要個住的地方。<span class="definition">pron. 某處，某個地方：</span>We need to find somewhere to live. 我們需要找個住處。/ I know somewhere we can go. 我知道一個地方我們可以去。

<span class="vocabulary">**at**</span> [æt] 
<span class="definition">prep. 1 在某地：</span>at the corner of the street 在街角 / They arrived late at the airport. 他們晚到了機場。/ At the roundabout take the third exit. 在環島處走第三個出口。/ I’ll be at home all morning. 我一上午都在家。/ I met her at the hospital. 我在醫院遇見了她。<span class="definition">2 在學習或工作的地點：</span>He’s been at the bank for ten years. 他在銀行工作十年了。/ She’s at Yale. 她在耶魯大學。

<span class="vocabulary">**on**</span> [ɒn] 
<span class="definition">prep. 1 在某地：</span>a town on the coast 沿海的城鎮 / We lived on an estate. 我們住在一處莊園裡。<span class="definition">2 在（或進入）某個表面的一個位置上，可以是覆蓋在…上，也可以是附著在…上：</span>a book on my desk 我書桌上的一本書 / a stain on my shirt 我襯衫上的一個汙點 / a paint on the wall 牆上的一幅油畫

<span class="vocabulary">**of**</span> [əv] 
<span class="definition">prep. 用於表示人或事物的空間位置，即在：</span>just north of Detroit 就在底特律以北 / the other side of the square 廣場的另一邊

<span class="vocabulary">**stand**</span> [stænd] 
<span class="definition">vi. 1 處於某一位置，即位於某處：</span>An old tree once stood here. 以前這兒長著一棵老樹。/ After the earthquake only a few houses were left standing. 地震過後，只有幾幢房屋還立著。<span class="definition">2 處於某種條件或狀態：</span>You never know where you stand with her--one minute she’s friendly, the next she’ll hardly speak to you. 你從來拿不準你和她的關係如何——她一會兒跟你親熱，一會兒連話也不大跟你說。/ As things stand, there is little chance of a quick settlement of the dispute. 照目前的形勢，儘快解決爭端的可能微乎其微。/ The house stood empty for a long time. 那所房子空了好長一段時間。/ ‘You’re wrong about the date--it was 1988.’ ‘I stand corrected (= accept that I was wrong).’ “你把日期搞錯了——是1988年。”“你說得對，是我搞錯了。” <span class="definition">3 一個人處於很可能會做某事的一種狀態中：</span>You stand to make a lot from this deal. 你很可能會從這筆生意中大賺一筆。

<span class="vocabulary">**lie**</span> [laɪ] 
<span class="definition">vi. 1（物品）平放（在一個表面上）：</span>Clothes were lying all over the floor. 地板上到處都堆放著衣服。/ The book lay open on his desk. 那本書攤開放在他的書桌上。<span class="definition">2 處於、保持或被保持在某種狀態：</span>Snow was lying thick on the ground. 厚厚的積雪覆蓋著大地。/ These machines have lain idle since the factory closed. 工廠關閉以來，這些機器就一直閒置著。/ a ship lying at anchor 錨泊的船 / I’d rather use my money than leave it lying in the bank. 我寧願把錢花掉也不願擱在銀行裡不用。/ The picture lay hidden in the archives for over 40 years. 這張畫在檔案室藏了40多年。<span class="definition">3（一個地方）位於某處或某個方向，即坐落：</span>The town lies on the coast. 這個小鎮位於海濱。<span class="definition">4 lie (in sth)（思想、特徵、問題、辦法、錯誤等）存在，在於…：</span>The problem lies in deciding when to intervene. 問題在於決定何時介入。
          
<span class="vocabulary">**wedge**</span> [wedʒ]
<span class="definition">vt. 坐落於一個狹窄空間，使其不能再移動：</span>The hotel's wedged right between the two airports. 那家賓館正好處於兩個機場中間。

<span class="vocabulary">**nestle**</span> [ˈnesl]
<span class="definition">vi. 處在一個受保護（或半遮掩）的地方：</span>The little town nestles snugly at the foot of the hill. 這個小鎮偎依在小山腳下。/ Nearby, nestling in the hills, was the children's home. 孩子們的家就在附近的山裡。/ The town nestles comfortably among the hills. 這個小鎮悠然地坐落在群山中。/ a tiny village nestling in a valley 坐落在峽谷中的小村莊

<span class="vocabulary">**reside**</span> [rɪˈzaɪd]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ in sb/sth 在於、由…造成（或引起）：</span>Happiness does not reside in strength or money. 幸福不是由實力或金錢決定的。/ The source of the problem resides in the fact that the currency is too strong. 問題的根源在於貨幣過於堅挺。

<span class="vocabulary">**be**</span> [bi:] 
<span class="definition">vi. 位於，在某地：</span>The town is three miles away. 鎮子距此地三英里遠。/ If you’re looking for your file, it’s on the table. 你要找的檔案在桌子上。/ Mary’s upstairs. 瑪麗在樓上。

<span class="vocabulary">**sit**</span> [sɪt] 
<span class="definition">vi. 在某個地方，在某處：</span>A car was sitting outside. 外面停著一輛汽車。/ The pot was sitting in a pool of water. 罐子放在一灘水裡。/ The jacket sat beautifully on her shoulders. 那件夾克穿在她身上很合身。/ The box sat on the shelf. 盒子擱在架子上。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**site**</span> [saɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. <span class="vocabulary">**perch**</span> [pɜ:tʃ; 美 pɜ:rtʃ]

<span class="vocabulary">**come**</span> [kʌm] 
<span class="definition">vi. 處於或位於某個位置：</span>That comes a long way down my list of priorities. 在我非做不可的事情當中，那事較不重要。/ His family comes first. 他把家庭放在首位。/ She came second in the exam. 她這次考試名列第二。

<span class="vocabulary">**patchy**</span> [ˈpætʃi]
<span class="definition">adj. 存在（或發生）於一些地方的，如散落的、分佈不均的：</span>patchy fog 團團的霧 / Thick patchy fog and irresponsible driving were to blame. 區域性濃霧和駕駛員不負責任是罪魁禍首。/ Bottle tans can make your legs, arms and face look a patchy orange colour. 瓶裝美黑劑可能會讓你的腿、手臂和臉呈現出深一塊、淺一塊的橙色。/ The grass was dry and patchy. 草都幹了，東一片西一片的。

<span class="vocabulary">**take**</span> [teɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 就座或佔座：</span>Are these seats taken? 這些座位有人嗎？/ Come in and take a seat. 進來坐下。

相關詞義延伸：
· on還可指在某地附近；
· stand還可指處於某一高度，或處於某一水平、數量、高度等；
· lie還可指（在比賽期間）處於某一位次，即名列，排名；
· be還可指仍然在某地、留在某地；
· occupy還可指佔領一個地方，尤指軍事佔領。也可指在某一體系、過程或計劃中佔據某一位置，如任職，執政；
· stop還可指旅途中的停留時間或停留地點；
· house還可表示是（某物的）保管處或貯藏處；
· strand還可表示使（船隻、魚、鯨等）擱淺（而不能回到水裡）。

周邊詞彙補充：
· away可表示在客場；at還可指在（桌子、門、某人等的）旁邊；
· run可指處於或接近某個水平；
· scatter可指在一片區域內散在的少量事物，如三三兩兩、零零星星。
