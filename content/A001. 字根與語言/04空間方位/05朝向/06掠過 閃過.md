☀ <span class="category">**掠過 閃過：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**cross**</span> [krɒs] 
<span class="definition">vt.（某種表情）掠過某人的臉龐：</span>A look of embarrassment crossed his face. 一縷尷尬的神色從他臉上掠過。

<span class="vocabulary">**across**</span> [ə'krɒs] 
<span class="definition">prep. 在身體的某個部位上：</span>to hit him across the face 打他的臉 / A grin spread across her face. 她粲然一笑。/ It’s too tight across the back. 背部太緊。  
           
<span class="vocabulary">**skim**</span> [skɪm]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (along/over, etc.) sth（使）輕快地掠過一個表面（而不接觸或偶爾接觸）：</span>We watched the birds skimming over the lake. 我們看著鳥兒貼著湖面飛過。/ seagulls skimming the waves 掠過浪尖的海鷗 / The little boat was skimming across the sunlit surface of the bay. 一葉小舟正輕輕掠過陽光下的海灣。/ The speedboat took off, skimming the waves. 快艇擦著波浪飛馳而去。/ We skimmed across the water in a small sailing boat. 我們乘小帆船掠過水麵。/ Small boys were skimming stones across the water.（英）幾個小男孩用石塊打水漂。

周邊詞彙補充：
· flash可指非常快速地經過，即掠過。
