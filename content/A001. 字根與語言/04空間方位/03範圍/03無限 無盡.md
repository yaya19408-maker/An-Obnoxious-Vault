☀ <span class="category">**無限 無盡：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**endless**</span> ['endlɪs] 
<span class="definition">adj.（尺寸）極大的且似乎沒有盡頭的，相當於limitless：</span>The road seemed endless. 這條路似乎沒有盡頭。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**limitless**</span> [ˈlɪmɪtləs]
<span class="definition">adj. 無限制的、無止境的：</span>the limitless variety of consumer products 種類繁多的消費產品 / a cheap and potentially limitless supply of energy 廉價而且可能永遠不會枯竭的能源供應 / The possibilities were almost limitless. 可能性幾乎是無窮無盡的。/ limitless potential 無限潛能 / The opportunities are limitless. 機會無限。           

<span class="vocabulary">**boundless**</span> [ˈbaʊndləs]
<span class="definition">adj. 無限的；似乎無止境的：</span>The work demanded boundless energy and theatrical imagination. 這種工作需要具備無限的精力和誇張的想象力。/ His reforming zeal was boundless. 他對於改革熱情無限。/ boundless enthusiasm 無限的熱情

<span class="vocabulary">**unlimited**</span> [ʌnˈlɪmɪtɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. 儘量多的；無限制的：</span>An unlimited number of copies can still be made from the original. 仍可以由原件影印出無數份副本來。/ You'll also have unlimited access to the swimming pool. 進泳池的次數是沒有限制的。/ The ticket gives you unlimited travel for seven days. 憑本車票在七日內可自由乘車不受限制。/ The court has the power to impose an unlimited fine for this offence. 法庭有權對這一違法行為罰款，額度不受限制。/ You will be allowed unlimited access to the files. 你可以無限制使用這些檔案。/ This new technology opens up almost unlimited possibilities. 這種新技術幾乎帶來了無限的可能性。           
           
<span class="vocabulary">**infinite**</span> [ˈɪnfɪnət]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 極大的；無法衡量的：</span>an infinite variety of plants 數不清的植物種類 / an almost infinite variety of shades 多得幾乎數不清的色彩 / an infinite variety of landscapes 變化萬千的風景 / With infinite care, John shifted position. 約翰小心翼翼地挪動了位置。/ a teacher with infinite patience 有無比耐心的教師 / The company in its infinite wisdom decided to close the staff restaurant (= they thought it was a good thing to do, but nobody else agreed).（諷刺用法）公司以無比的智慧決定關掉職工食堂。<span class="definition">2 無限的、無窮無盡的：</span>an infinite universe 無垠的宇宙 / an infinite number of atoms 無窮多的原子 / Obviously, no company has infinite resources. 顯然，沒有哪個公司的資源是無限的。<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the infinite可指無窮無盡的事物：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**infinity**</span> [ɪnˈfɪnəti]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U]（也可用infinities[pl.]）無限、無窮：</span>the infinity/infinities of space 空間的無限 <span class="definition">2 [U] 無窮遠的點：</span>The landscape seemed to stretch into infinity. 風景似乎延伸到了無窮遠處。/ the darkness of a starless night stretching to infinity 沒有星星的夜晚，黑暗無邊無際 / Theoretically, a line can extend into infinity. 理論上直線可以無限延伸。/ Parallel lines appear to meet at infinity. 平行線似乎在無限遠處相交。/ He could have jumped off the cliff into infinity. 他本來有可能跳下懸崖進入廣漠無限之中。

<span class="vocabulary">**immeasurable**</span> [ɪˈmeʒərəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 太大而無法估量的：</span>to cause immeasurable harm 造成不可估量的損害 / I felt an immeasurable love for him. 當時我無限深愛他。/ His contribution is immeasurable. 他居功至偉。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**inexhaustible**</span> [ˌɪnɪgˈzɔ:stəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. 用之不竭的：</span>an inexhaustible supply of good jokes 講不完的精彩笑話 / Her energy is inexhaustible. 她有無窮的精力。/ She has an inexhaustible supply of enthusiasm. 她有用不完的熱情。/ His energy was unbounded and his patience inexhaustible. 他有無窮的精力和無限的耐心。

相關詞義延伸：
· endless還可指持續很長一段時間且似乎沒有盡頭的，亦可指（數量）極大的且似乎沒有盡頭的；
· infinity還可指一個無窮大的數字。也可指大得無法計算的一個數量。
