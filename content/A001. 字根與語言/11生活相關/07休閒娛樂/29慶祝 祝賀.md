☀ <span class="category">**慶祝 祝賀：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**celebrate**</span> ['selɪbreɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 透過做某件特別的事來表明一天（或一個活動）是重要的，即慶祝、慶賀：</span>Jake’s passed his exams. We’re going out to celebrate. 傑克已透過考試，我們要出去慶祝一下。/ We celebrated our 10th wedding anniversary in Paris. 我們在巴黎慶祝結婚10週年。/ to celebrate New Year 慶賀新年

<span class="vocabulary">**celebration**</span> [͵selɪ'breɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 慶祝：</span>a party in celebration of their fiftieth wedding anniversary 慶祝他們結婚五十週年紀念的聚會 / family celebration 家庭慶祝活動 / celebration dinner, celebration party 慶祝宴會 / The victory was a cause for great celebration. 這次勝利值得隆重慶祝。

<span class="vocabulary">**observe**</span> [əb'zə:v] 
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 慶祝節日、生日等。不常用：</span>to observe Christmas 過聖誕節

<span class="vocabulary">**congratulate**</span> [kən'ɡrætʃuleɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt. congratulate sb (on sth) 向某人道賀，祝賀：</span>to congratulate the winner 向獲勝者表示祝賀 / She congratulated me warmly on my performance. 她就我的表現向我表示熱烈祝賀。

<span class="vocabulary">**congratulation**</span> [kən͵ɡrætʃu'leɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [pl.] 祝賀某人時所說的話，即祝詞，賀詞：</span>to offer your congratulations to sb 向某人道賀 / to shout congratulations 喊出祝賀的話 / to deserve congratulations 值得慶賀 <span class="definition">2 [U] 向某人表示祝賀的行為，即祝賀：</span>a letter of congratulation 賀信 <span class="definition">exclamation. congratulation! 用於向某人祝賀，如恭喜！：</span>‘We’re getting married!’ ‘Congratulations!’ “我們要結婚了！”“恭喜恭喜！” / Congratulations on your exam results! 祝賀你考出了好成績！

周邊詞彙補充：
· mark可指慶賀或正式紀念你認為重要的一次事件；
· open可指為建築物舉行揭幕儀式（以此宣佈啟用該建築物）。
