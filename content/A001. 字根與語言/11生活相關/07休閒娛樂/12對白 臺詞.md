☀ <span class="category">**對白 臺詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**dialogue**</span> ['daɪəlɒɡ]（美dialog）
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（書籍、戲劇或電影中的）對話，對白：</span>The novel has long descriptions and not much dialogue. 這部小說描述多對話少。/ dialogues for language learners 供語言學習者學習的對白

<span class="vocabulary">**line**</span> [laɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 電影的臺詞，對白。是一個簡單用詞：</span>to learn lines 背臺詞 / a line from the film ‘Casablanca’ 電影《卡薩布蘭卡》裡的一句臺詞

<span class="vocabulary">**monologue**</span> [ˈmɒnəlɒg; 美 ˈmɑ:nəlɔ:g; -lɑ:g]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（戲劇、電影等的）獨白：</span>inner, interior, internal monologue 內心獨白 / comic, dramatic monologue 喜劇獨白；戲劇獨白 / opening monologue 開場獨白 / She delivered her monologue in a deadpan voice. 她故作嚴肅地說著獨白。/ an entertainer who does comic monologues 表演喜劇獨白的演員
 
<span class="vocabulary">**part**</span> [pɑːt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 演員的臺詞。也是一個簡單用詞：</span>Have you learned your part yet? 你記住你的臺詞了嗎？
