☀ <span class="category">**廚房 灶臺：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**kitchen**</span> ['kɪtʃɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 廚房：</span>She’s in the kitchen. 她在廚房裡。/ open kitchen 開放式廚房 / restaurant kitchen 飯店廚房 / mobile kitchen 移動廚房 / kitchen door 廚房門 / kitchen sink 廚房洗滌槽

<span class="vocabulary">**surface**</span> ['sə:fɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（廚房櫃檯的）頂部平面：</span>kitchen surface 廚房檯面

<span class="vocabulary">**counter**</span> ['kaʊntə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 尤用於美式英語，指廚房的操作檯：</span>kitchen counter（尤美）廚房操作檯 / The kitchen had black marble counter tops. 廚房是黑色的大理石臺面。

<span class="vocabulary">**buffet**</span> ['bʌfeɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [尤美] 廚房裡（上菜前）放食物的一個傢俱（其抽屜裡可放刀、叉等餐具），可稱為餐具櫃，相當於sideboard：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**sink**</span> [sɪŋk] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（廚房裡有水龍頭的用於洗碗碟的）洗滌池，洗碗槽：</span>The sink was full of dirty dishes. 水池裡滿是髒碟子。/ kitchen sink 廚房洗碗槽 / She was at the sink, washing the dishes. 她正在水槽旁洗碗。

<span class="vocabulary">**hatch**</span> [hætʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 兩個房間之間的一個小開口，尤指廚房和餐廳之間供傳遞食物的小視窗：</span>serving hatch（尤英）遞菜視窗 / a hatch between the kitchen and the dining room 廚房和餐廳之間的遞菜口
