☀ <span class="category">**營養 滋養：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**nutrition**</span> [nju:'trɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 生物吸收營養的過程：</span>sources of nutrition 營養來源 / adequate nutrition 足夠的營養 / human nutrition 人體營養 / nutrition facts, nutrition information 營養成分表 / nutrition consultant 營養師 / nutrition research 營養研究 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**nutritious**</span> [njuˈtrɪʃəs; 美 nu-]
<span class="definition">adj. [褒]（食物）營養豐富的：</span>tasty and nutritious meals 既可口又有營養的飯菜 / It is always important to choose enjoyable, nutritious foods. 選擇好吃的、營養價值高的食物總是很重要。/ Some ready made meals are nutritious and very easy to prepare. 一些預製膳食很有營養，而且烹調起來很簡單。/ Adapt your usual eating regime to make it more nutritious. 調整你的日常飲食習慣，使之更富有營養。
     
<span class="vocabulary">**carbohydrate**</span> [ˌkɑ:bəʊˈhaɪdreɪt; 美 ˌkɑ:rboʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 碳水化合物、糖類：</span>Food is made up of carbohydrates, proteins and fats. 食物由碳水化合物、蛋白質和脂肪構成。/ simple carbohydrate 單一碳水化合物 / complex carbohydrate 複合碳水化合物 / refined carbohydrate 精製碳水化合物 / unrefined carbohydrate 非精製碳水化合物 / Nuts are high in carbohydrates. 堅果富含碳水化合物。/ carbohydrate content 碳水化合物含量 / carbohydrate consumption, carbohydrate intake 碳水化合物攝取量；碳水化合物攝入量 / a source of carbohydrate 碳水化合物的來源
     
<span class="vocabulary">**protein**</span> [ˈprəʊti:n; 美 ˈproʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 蛋白質：</span>natural protein 天然蛋白 / animal, vegetable protein 動物蛋白；植物蛋白 / a high protein diet 高蛋白飲食 / Cereals supply essential protein and vitamins. 穀類食物可提供人體必需的蛋白質和維生素。/ Gluten is a protein found in wheat and other grains. 麵筋是小麥和其他穀物含有的一種蛋白質。/ protein content 蛋白質含量 / protein level 蛋白質水平 / protein intake 蛋白質攝入量 / protein deficiency 蛋白質的缺乏 / a source of protein 蛋白質來源 / Peas, beans and lentils are a good source of vegetable protein. 豌豆、豆莢和扁豆是植物蛋白質的大好來源。/ Fish was a major source of protein for the working man. 魚肉曾是勞動者的主要蛋白質來源。

<span class="vocabulary">**vitamin**</span> [ˈvɪtəmɪn; 美 ˈvaɪt-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 維生素；維他命：</span>antioxidant vitamin 抗氧化維生素 / fat-soluble vitamin 脂溶性維生素 / prenatal vitamin（美）產前維生素 / multiple vitamin（美）複合維生素 / breakfast cereals enriched with vitamins 增加了維生素的早餐穀物食品 / vitamin deficiency 維生素缺乏 / vitamin D pills 維生素D丸 / Butter, margarine, and oily fish are all good sources of vitamin D. 黃油、人造黃油和多脂魚都含有豐富的維生素D。/ Healthy people do not need vitamin supplements. 健康的人不需要額外補充維生素。

<span class="vocabulary">**fibre**</span> ['faɪbə]（美fiber）
<span class="definition">n. [U]（食物中可助消化的）纖維素：</span>dietary fibre 膳食纖維 / foods that are rich in fibre 纖維含量豐富的食物
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**cholesterol**</span> [kəˈlestərɒl; 美 -rɔ:l]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 膽固醇：</span>a dangerously high cholesterol level 高得危險的膽固醇水平 / o raised blood cholesterol levels 升高的血膽固醇水平 / He has high cholesterol. 他膽固醇高。/ foods that are low in cholesterol 膽固醇含量低的食物 / Avocados contain no cholesterol. 鱷梨不含膽固醇。/ Eating garlic can significantly reduce cholesterol in the blood. 吃大蒜可以大大減少血液中的膽固醇。/ This is the fat that won't raise your cholesterol. 這種脂肪不會使你的膽固醇水平增高。/ cholesterol level 膽固醇水平 / cholesterol test 膽固醇檢測 / cholesterol screening 膽固醇篩查 / cholesterol drug, cholesterol medication（尤美）, cholesterol medicine 膽固醇藥物

<span class="vocabulary">**supplement**</span> [ˈsʌplɪmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (to sth) 營養補充品：</span>diet, dietary, food, herbal, natural, nutritional supplement 補充性的飲食；草藥補充劑；天然補充劑；營養補充劑 / women who take a daily supplement of folic acid 每日補充葉酸的婦女 / mineral, protein, vitamin supplement 礦物質補充劑；蛋白質補充劑；維生素補充劑 / a multiple vitamin and mineral supplement 含複合維生素和礦物質的營養劑 / I take a vitamin supplement every day. 我每天服一次維生素補充劑。/ I took regular supplements and exercised every day. 我每天都按時吃營養片並堅持鍛鍊。/ The farmer adds a supplement to the horse's feed. 農場主在馬的伺料中加了一份補充物。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**nourish**</span> [ˈnʌrɪʃ; 美 ˈnɜ:rɪʃ]
<span class="definition">vt. 為一個人、動物或植物提供食物、營養或養分：</span>All the children were well nourished and in good physical condition. 所有這些孩子都營養良好，身體健康。/ The food she eats nourishes both her and the baby. 她吃的食物給她和嬰兒提供了營養。/ Patients recover quickly if they are well nourished. 如果營養充足，病人會恢復得很快。/ microbes in the soil which nourish the plant 土壤中為植物提供營養的微生物

<span class="vocabulary">**nourishment**</span> [ˈnʌrɪʃmənt; 美 ˈnɜ:r-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] [正式或術語] 食物；營養品；養分：</span>The mother provides the embryo with nourishment and a place to grow. 母親為胎兒提供營養和成長的空間。/ He was unable to take nourishment for several days. 他有幾天不能吃東西了。/ Can plants obtain adequate nourishment from such poor soil? 土壤這樣貧瘠，植物能獲得足夠的養分嗎？/ Every animal in the food chain draws nourishment from other animals or plants. 食物鏈中的每一種動物都從其他動物或植物中獲取養分。/ As a child, she was starved of intellectual nourishment.（比喻用法）作為一個孩子，她渴望吸取知識的養分。/ emotional, spiritual nourishment 情感滋養；精神食糧 <span class="definition">2 [U] [正式或術語] 提供營養或吸收營養的過程，如滋養：</span>Sugar gives quick relief to hunger but provides no lasting nourishment. 糖能快速緩解飢餓感，但不能提供持久的營養。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**intake**</span> [ˈɪnteɪk]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 食物、飲食等的攝入量：</span>dietary, nutritional 食物攝入量；營養攝入量 / caloric（尤美）, calorie intake 熱量攝取；卡路里攝入量 / alcohol, calcium, carbohydrate, energy, fat, fibre/fiber, fluid, food, nutrient（尤美）, oxygen, protein, salt, sugar, vitamin, water intake 酒精攝入量；鈣攝入量；碳水化合物攝入量；能量攝取量；脂肪攝取量；纖維攝入量；流體攝入量；食物攝取量；養分攝入量；氧氣吸入量；蛋白質攝取量；鹽攝入量；糖分攝取量；維生素攝取量；水分攝入量 / high fluid intake 高液攝取量 / Your intake of alcohol should not exceed two units per day. 每天的飲酒量不應超過兩個計量單位。/ Reduce your salt intake. 減少鹽的攝入量。/ to reduce your daily intake of salt 減少每天的食鹽量 / Make sure you have a balanced intake of vitamins A, B, C and D. 要確保均衡地攝入維生素A、B、C和D。/ You should maintain a low intake of fat. 你應該維持較低的脂肪攝入量。/ You need to watch your alcohol intake. 你需要注意酒精攝入量。/ One of the best ways to get to your ideal size is to cut fat intake right down. 塑造完美身材的最佳辦法之一就是減少脂肪攝入量。
