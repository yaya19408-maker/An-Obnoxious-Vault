☀ <span class="category">**食物儲存：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**leave**</span> [li:v] 
<span class="definition">vt. 剩下（常是不喜歡的）食物或飲料：</span>If you don’t like the cocktail you ordered, just leave it and try a different one. 如果你不喜歡你點的雞尾酒，剩著吧，嚐嚐別的。

<span class="vocabulary">**keep**</span> [ki:p] 
<span class="definition">vi.（食物）保持不壞。是一個基礎用詞：</span>Finish off the pie--it won’t keep. 把餡餅都吃了吧——擱不住。/ Milk should keep well in a refrigerator. 牛奶應該放在冰箱裡儲存。

<span class="vocabulary">**pack**</span> [pæk] 
<span class="definition">vt. pack sth (in sth) 用某種物質來儲存食物：</span>fish packed in ice 用冰塊儲存的魚

<span class="vocabulary">**preserve**</span> [prɪ'zə:v] 
<span class="definition">vt.（透過以某種方式來處理而）貯存某東西以防止其腐爛（尤指食物）：</span>olives preserved in brine 鹽水橄欖

<span class="vocabulary">**ice**</span> [aɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 用於儲存食物和飲料的冰塊：</span>I’ll have lemonade please--no ice. 請給我來杯檸檬水，不加冰塊。

<span class="vocabulary">**salt**</span> [sɔ:lt] 
<span class="definition">vt. salt sth (down) 用鹽儲存食物（如醃製）：</span>salted fish 鹹魚
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pickle**</span> [ˈpɪkl]
<span class="definition">vt.（用醋或鹽）醃製：</span>Select your favourite fruit or veg and pickle them while they are still fresh. 挑選最喜歡的水果或蔬菜，趁著新鮮把它們醃起來。/ Herrings can be salted, smoked and pickled. 鯡魚可以鹽醃、熏製和泡製。

<span class="vocabulary">**bottle**</span> ['bɒtl] 
<span class="definition">vt. 將水果或蔬菜裝入玻璃瓶以儲存之：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**can**</span> [kæn] 
<span class="definition">vt. [尤美] 把食品或飲料裝罐儲存：</span>fruits that will be canned 將要經過裝罐的水果
