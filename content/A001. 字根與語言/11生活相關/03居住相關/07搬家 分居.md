<span class="vocabulary">**move**</span> [mu:v] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. move (from ...) (to ...) 變換（居住或工作等的）地方，如搬家；搬遷：</span>The company’s moving to Scotland. 公司準備遷往蘇格蘭。/ She’s been all on her own since her daughter moved away. 自從她女兒搬走以後，她一直獨自生活。<span class="definition">n. [C] 搬家，或變換工作的地點：</span>What’s the date of your move? 你什麼時候搬家？/ move from Italy to the US 從義大利遷到美國
           
<span class="vocabulary">**removal**</span> [rɪˈmu:vl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [英] 搬家：</span>house removals 搬家 / removal company, removal firm 搬家公司 / When are the removal men coming? 搬家工人什麼時候到？/ Home removals are best done in cool weather. 最好在天氣涼爽時搬家。/ They were in the furniture removal business. 他們做傢俱搬運生意。

<span class="vocabulary">**separation**</span> [sepə'reɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 合法夫妻間的分居：</span>formal, legal separation 正式分居；合法分居 / trial separation 試驗性分居 / the separation between Mary and her husband 瑪麗和丈夫的分居

<span class="vocabulary">**separate**</span> ['sepəreɪt] 
<span class="definition">vi. separate (from sb)（與配偶或情侶）分居：</span>He separated from his wife after 20 years of marriage. 他和妻子在結婚20年後分居了。/ Slave parents were forcibly separated from their children. 身為奴隸的父母被迫與自己的孩子分離。
