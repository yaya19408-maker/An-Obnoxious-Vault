☀ <span class="category">**家庭 家人：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**family**</span> ['fæmɪlɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 家，家庭（由一個或兩個父母及其子女所構成）。是人口意義上的家：</span>a family of four 四口之家 / families with young children 有小孩的家庭 / entire, whole family 全家 / single-parent family 單親家庭 / military family 軍人家庭 / working family 工人家庭 / a member of a family 家庭成員 <span class="definition">2 [C] 大家庭（由父母、子女及近親組成），可稱為親屬：</span>All our family came to Grandad’s eightieth birthday party. 我們所有的親屬都來參加了祖父八十壽宴。/ immediate family 直系親屬 <span class="definition">adj. 1 只用於名詞前，與家庭或某個家庭有關的：</span>family life 家庭生活 / family background 家庭背景 <span class="definition">2 只用於名詞前，由一家所擁有的：</span>a family business 家族企業 <span class="definition">3 只用於名詞前，適合全家人的（成人和孩子全都算在內）：</span>a family show 一檔家庭節目

<span class="vocabulary">**home**</span> [həʊm] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] 你所居住的房子或公寓（尤指與家人一起），即住所意義上的家：</span>We are not far from my home now. 我們現在離我家不遠了。/ She leaves home at 7 every day. 她每天7點鐘離家。/ permanent, temporary home 永久住所；臨時住處 <span class="definition">2 [C] 用於指居住在一起的一個家庭，以及行為方式意義上的家庭：</span>a violent home 一個有暴力行為的家庭 / He had always wanted a real home with a wife and children. 他一直想要一個有妻子和孩子的真正的家庭。/ broken home 破碎的家庭 / single-parent home 單親家庭 / working-class home 工薪階層家庭 <span class="definition">adj. 1 只用於名詞前，與自己的居住地有關的，即家的、家庭的：</span>a free home delivery service 免費送貨上門的服務 / home address 住址 / home care, home nursing 家庭護理 / home leave 探親假 / home background, home conditions, home environment 家庭背景；家庭條件；家庭環境 <span class="definition">2 只用於名詞前，在家裡被製作或被使用的：</span>home cooking 家常飯菜 / home appliance 家用電器 / home entertainment 家庭娛樂 / home study 函授學習 <span class="definition">adv. 到（或向、在）自己的居住地（即家）：</span>Come on, it’s time to go home. 快點，該回家了。/ What time did you get home last night? 你昨晚什麼時間到家的？/ I like to stay home in the evenings.（美）我喜歡晚上待在家裡。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**domestic**</span> [dəˈmestɪk]
<span class="definition">adj. [只用於名詞前] 1 家用的、家庭的、家務的：</span>domestic appliances 家用器具 / a plan for sharing domestic chores 分擔家務活的計劃 / the growing problem of domestic violence (= violence between members of the same family) 日趨嚴重的家庭暴力問題 / I was called out to attend a domestic dispute. 我被叫出去解決一場家庭糾紛。/ It was a scene of such domestic bliss. 這是一幅家庭如此幸福的畫面。/ domestic service (= the work of a servant in a large house) 家政服務 / domestic help (= help with the work in a house; the person or people who do this work) 家務幫助；家傭 <span class="definition">2 喜愛家庭生活的、享受家庭樂趣的、樂於操持家務的：</span>I'm not a very domestic sort of person. 我不是那種很喜歡待在家裡的人。/ She was kind and domestic and put her family before her part-time job. 她人很好也顧家，把家庭看得比兼職工作更為重要。<span class="definition">n. [C]（也可用domestic help或domestic worker）家庭裡的傭人、家政工：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**domesticated**</span> [dəʊ'mestɪkeɪtɪd；美dəˈmɛstəˌketɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. 樂於做家務事的、愛操持家務的：</span>Mum wasn't very domesticated. 媽媽不是很喜歡幹家務活。/ They've become a lot more domesticated since they got married. 他們婚後戀家多了。/ He is thoroughly domesticated and cooks a delicious chicken casserole. 他完全適應了家居生活，烹製的砂鍋燉小雞非常可口。

<span class="vocabulary">**house**</span> [haʊs] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [sing.] 住在一所房子裡的人（例如全家人）：</span>Be quiet or you’ll wake the whole house! 安靜點，別把全家人都吵醒！<span class="definition">2 [C] 常用the House of ... 指古老的有名望的家族，即名門望族：</span>the House of Windsor (= the British royal family) 溫莎王室
           
<span class="vocabulary">**household**</span> [ˈhaʊshəʊld; 美 -hoʊld]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 一家人；住在同一幢房子裡的一群人：</span>growing up in a male-only household 在一個只有男性的家庭里長大 / Many poor households are experiencing real hardship. 很多貧窮家庭正經歷嚴重的困難。/ Most households now own at least one car. 大多數家庭現在至少有一輛汽車。/ The head of the household is responsible for completing the form. 戶主負責填寫表格。/ The average household spends more on housing than on food. 一般家庭在住房上的花銷多於食物。/ rural, urban household 鄉村家庭；城市家庭 / poor, wealthy household 貧窮家庭；富裕家庭 / He was a member of the royal household. 他是王室成員。/ middle-class, working-class household 中產階級家庭；工人階級家庭 / high-income, low-income household 高收入家庭；低收入家庭 / family, married-couple, single-parent, single-person household 家庭；夫妻家庭；單親家庭；單身家庭 / households headed by a single parent單親支撐的家庭 / Becoming an adult and setting up a household no longer mean the same thing. 長大成人與成家立室已不再是一回事了。<span class="definition">2 [sing.] 家；家務：</span>My husband gave me cash to manage the household, but none of it was ever my own. 丈夫給我錢來持家，但這些錢一毫一釐都不屬於我。/ household appliance, household contents（尤英）, household furniture, household goods, household item, household product 家用電器；家居財物；傢俱；家庭用品 / The household contents are covered by a separate insurance policy. 家庭財物上了單獨的保險。/ household cleaner 家用清潔劑 / household bills, household budget, household debt, household expenditure, household expenses, household income, household savings 家庭賬單；家庭預算；家庭債務；家庭開支；家庭開銷；家庭收入；家庭儲蓄 / household chore, household duties, household task 家務 / household garbage（美）, household rubbish（英）, household trash（美）, household waste 生活垃圾
           
<span class="vocabulary">**housekeeping**</span> [ˈhaʊski:pɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 家務（尤指採購和管理開支）：</span>I thought that cooking and housekeeping were unimportant, easy tasks. 我認為做飯和料理家務微不足道，一下子就能搞定。/ My financial problems were made worse by my bad housekeeping. 我持家無方，使得經濟狀況更加糟糕。<span class="definition">2 [U]（也可用housekeeping money[尤英]）家務開支：</span>housekeeping allowance（英）, housekeeping money（尤英）家用；家務開支 / Her husband spent the housekeeping money on gambling. 她丈夫把家用錢拿去賭博了。/ the housekeeping money Jim gave her each week 吉姆每週給她的家用錢

<span class="vocabulary">**mouth**</span> [maʊθ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 需要食物來供養的人：</span>Now there would be another mouth to feed. 現在又多一個要吃飯的人。/ Twins would mean two extra mouths to feed. 雙胞胎就意味著需要多養活兩口人。

<span class="vocabulary">**place**</span> [pleɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [非正式] 某人的家：</span>What about dinner at my place? 到我家吃晚飯好不好？

<span class="vocabulary">**nest**</span> [nest] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 家，強調所居住的一個安全的地方，即安樂窩：</span>Her children have flown the nest.（比喻用法）她的孩子們已經離家獨立。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**haven**</span> [ˈheɪvn]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（對人來說）一個安全的地方：</span>The hotel is a haven of peace and tranquility. 這家旅館是一處安寧的去處。/ It's a real haven at the end of a busy working day. 忙碌了一整天后，這真是一個安樂窩。/ This house is a haven of peace compared with ours. 和我們家相比，這所房子是一個寧靜的去處。/ The aim is to create a safe haven for the thousands of refugees. 目的是為數千難民建立一處安全的避難所。/ They were living in a safe haven away from the fighting. 他們生活在一個遠離戰火的安全地方。/ The island is a popular tax haven for the very rich. 這個島是鉅富青睞的避稅天堂。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**homeless**</span> [ˈhəʊmləs; 美 ˈhoʊm-]
<span class="definition">adj. 無家可歸的：</span>The scheme has been set up to help homeless people. 這個計劃的目的是幫助無家可歸的人。/ the growing number of homeless families 越來越多的居無定所家庭 / Hundreds were made homeless. 數百人落得無家可歸。/ He found himself homeless after his marriage broke up.婚姻破裂後他發現自己已經無家可歸了。/ Three hundred people were left homeless by the earthquake.地震使三百人無家可歸。/ to be chronically homeless（美）長期無家可歸的 <span class="definition">n. [pl.] the homeless 可指無家可歸的人：</span>helping the homeless 幫助無家可歸者
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**derelict**</span> [ˈderəlɪkt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [正式] 沒有家庭、工作或資產的一個人，如無家可歸者：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**inmate**</span> [ˈɪnmeɪt]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（收容所的）一個居住者：</span>asylum inmate 收容所室友

相關詞義延伸：
· home還可指專門接納對自己無照顧能力（而需要別人照顧）的人的一個地方，尤指標對老年人的養老院或針對兒童的養育院。也可指寵物相關收養所（專門照看無家可歸的寵物）；
· domestic還可指家庭成員之間的糾紛或矛盾；
· household還可指家喻戶曉的；眾人皆知的。
