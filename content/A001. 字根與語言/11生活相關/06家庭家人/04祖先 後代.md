☀ <span class="category">**祖先 後代：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**ancestor**</span> ['ænsestə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 人的祖先，相當於forebear：</span>direct ancestor 直系祖先 / distant, remote ancestor 遠祖 / human ancestor 人類的祖先 / literary, spiritual（比喻用法）ancestor 文學先祖；精神先祖
           
<span class="vocabulary">**ancestry**</span> [ˈænsestri]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing., U] 祖先、祖宗、列祖列宗：</span>African，Japanese ancestry 非洲人的祖先；日本人的祖先 / to have Scottish ancestry 祖籍蘇格蘭 / He is of Chinese ancestry. 他祖籍中國。/ He was able to trace his ancestry back over 1 000 years. 他的祖先可追溯至1 000多年以前。/ We can trace our ancestry back to the 12th century. 我們的祖先可追溯到17世紀。/ a family who could trace their ancestry back to the sixteenth century 世系可上溯到16世紀的家族

<span class="vocabulary">**father**</span> ['fɑːðə] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] [文學] fathers 一個人的歷代祖先：</span>the land of our fathers 我們祖先的土地
           
<span class="vocabulary">**progenitor**</span> [prəʊˈdʒenɪtə(r); 美 proʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [正式]（人的）祖先：</span>He was the progenitor of a family of distinguished actors. 他是一個著名演藝世家的先輩。/ He was also a progenitor of seven presidents of Nicaragua. 他也是尼加拉瓜七任總統的祖先。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**ancestral**</span> [ænˈsestrəl]
<span class="definition">adj. 祖先的：</span>ancestral house 祖宅 / the family's ancestral home in southern Germany 這個家族在德國南部的祖居 / the ancestral portraits in the hallway 走廊裡祖先的畫像 / the tribe's ancestral lands 部落的祖居地 / ancestral past 祖先的歷史 / ancestral spirit 祖先的靈魂 / ancestral tradition 祖先的傳統           

<span class="vocabulary">**descendant**</span> [dɪˈsendənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一個人的後代、後裔、子孫：</span>He was an O'Conor and a direct descendant of the last High King of Ireland. 他屬於奧康納家族，是愛爾蘭最後一位君王的嫡系後裔。/ Many of them are descendants of the original settlers. 他們中許多人都是早期移民的後裔。/ They are descendants of the original English and Scottish settlers. 他們是最初的英格蘭和蘇格蘭定居者的後代。/ Lord Cochrane and his descendants 科克倫勳爵及其後裔 / immediate descendant 直接後代 / distant, remote descendant 遠親後代 / living, modern descendant 在世的後人；現代的後裔 / He is Brecht's spiritual descendant. 他是布萊希特的精神傳人。

<span class="vocabulary">**seed**</span> [si:d] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] [文學] 某個男人的全部後代，即子孫、後裔：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**descend**</span> [dɪˈsend]
<span class="definition">vi.（人）是…的後裔：</span>He claims to be descended from a Spanish prince. 他聲稱自己是一位西班牙王子的後人。
