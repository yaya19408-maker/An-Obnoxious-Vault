☀ <span class="category">**壽命 年齡：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**life**</span> [laɪf] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 一個人從生到死的一段時間，即一生，終身，壽命；還可指一生中的一個部分：</span>He fought for the freedom of slaves all his life. 他一生都在為奴隸的解放事業而奮鬥。/ the rest of his life 他的餘生 / long, short life 長命；短命 / early life 早年 / adult life 成年期 / later life 晚年 / past, previous life 過去的生活；前世 / life after death 來世 / life membership 終身會員資格 / life imprisonment, life sentence 終身監禁 / life span（通常用lifespan）壽命 / He met the love of his life at college. 他在上大學時遇到了自己一生的摯愛。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**lifetime**</span> [ˈlaɪftaɪm]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一個人的一生時間：</span>During my lifetime I haven't got around to much travelling. 我一生中沒怎麼去旅行過。/ a trust fund to be administered throughout his wife's lifetime 為他妻子終生管理的信託基金 / This could be not just the trip of a lifetime but the experience of a lifetime. 這可能不僅是一趟終生難遇的旅行，也將是一次終生難遇的體驗。/ His diary was not published during his lifetime. 他的日記在他生前未曾發表過。/ wisdom gained in the course of a long lifetime 在漫長的一生中獲得的智慧 / A lifetime is too short for all the great books there are! 一生太短，讀不完所有的名著!/ He devoted a lifetime to working with disabled children. 他畢生致力於幫助殘疾兒童。/ It took a whole lifetime to solve the mystery of her father's disappearance. 她用了一生的時間才解開父親失蹤之。/ This watch should last you a lifetime. 這塊表你能用一輩子。/ It seems a lifetime since we first met. 我們初次見面似乎是上輩子的事了。/ You can live a whole lifetime and not witness such an event. 你有可能一輩子都沒機會親歷這樣的事件。/ The veteran director won a lifetime achievement award. 這位資深導演獲得了終身成就獎。/ lifetime employment 終身職業 / lifetime membership（美）終身會員資格 / lifetime earnings, lifetime income 畢生所得；終身收入 / lifetime guarantee, lifetime warranty 終身保證；終身保修 / lifetime ban 終身禁令 / lifetime commitment 終生承諾 / After a lifetime as a journalist in the trouble spots of the world, he retired to the country. 在世界不安定地區做了一輩子記者之後，他退休到了鄉下。/ I've seen many changes during my lifetime. 我一生中經歷了許多變革。/ The artist was little known in his lifetime. 這位藝術家一生默默無聞。/ She gave the performance of a lifetime. 她進行了一次難得的表演。/ She spent a lifetime in politics. 她一生從政。/ It's hard to break the habits of a lifetime. 很難改變一輩子養成的習慣。/ Callas was a legend in her own lifetime. 卡拉斯是她那個時代的傳奇人物。/ half a lifetime半輩子 / College seems half a lifetime away. 大學好像是上半輩子的事了。/ The images are part of a lifetime's worth of photographs from Cohen's long career. 那些影象是科恩漫長攝影生涯中傾注畢生心血的作品的一部分。/ That sort of thing happens only once in a lifetime. 那種事一生只有一次。

<span class="vocabulary">**age**</span> [eɪdӡ] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] 人的年齡。是此義的常規用詞：</span>She lived to the age of 75. 她活到了75歲。 / retirement age 退休年齡 / children of school age 學齡兒童 / mental age 心理年齡 / age structure 年齡結構（或年齡分佈）<span class="definition">2 [C, U] 一個人的年齡段：</span>at an early age 幼年 / young age 年輕時代（或童年）/ middle age 中年 / 30 is an awkward age. 30歲是個尷尬的年齡。/ When you get to my age you get a different perspective on life. 你到我這個年紀時就會對人生有不同的看法。

<span class="vocabulary">**year**</span> [jə:] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl.] 年齡，年紀。常與數字聯用，表示具體的年齡（而age則無此限制）：</span>He was 14 years old when it happened. 這件事情發生的時候，他14歲。/ In his later years, he drifted away from politics. 他晚年逐漸遠離了政治。

<span class="vocabulary">**at**</span> [æt] 
<span class="definition">prep.（某人做某事時）在…歲：</span>She got married at 25. 她25歲結的婚。/ He left school at the age of 16. 他16歲便離開了學校。

<span class="vocabulary">**old**</span> [əʊld] 
<span class="definition">adj. be ... years, months, etc. old 年齡為…：</span>The baby was only a few hours old. 嬰兒才出生幾個小時。/ fifteen years old 十五歲 / How old is this building?（擬人用法）這座建築已有多少年了？/ He’s the oldest player in the team. 他是隊裡年齡最大的隊員。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**chronological**</span> [ˌkrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkl; 美 ˌkrɑ:nəˈlɑ:dʒ-]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] ~ age 按時間計算的（年齡）（相對於身體、智力或情感等方面的發展而言）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**under**</span> ['ʌndə] 
<span class="definition">prep. 在某個年齡以下：</span>jobs for those under 35 適合35歲以下人士的工作 / Nobody under 18 is allowed to play the game. 未滿18歲者不得玩本遊戲。<span class="definition">adv. 在某個年齡以下：</span>children aged 12 and under 12歲及以下的兒童

相關詞義延伸：
· age還可指事物的年齡。

周邊詞彙補充：
· majority可指成年的法定年齡；
· minority可指未成年的法定年齡；
· turn可指達到或超過（某一年齡）；
· teens可指十幾歲（13至19歲之間）。
