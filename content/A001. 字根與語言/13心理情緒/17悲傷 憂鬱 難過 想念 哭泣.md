☀ <span class="category">**悲傷、憂鬱、沮喪的形容詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**sad**</span> [sæd] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 sad (to do sth) / sad (that ...) 悲傷的，難過的，不高興的；或表現得不高興的。是此義的常規用詞：</span>I was sad that she had to go. 知道她得走了，我心裡很難過。/ This music always makes me sad. 這支曲子總是讓我傷感。/ She was still feeling very sad about her father’s death. 她依然為父親的去世傷心不已。<span class="definition">2 sad (to do sth) / sad (that ...) 令人悲傷的，讓人難過的：</span>a sad story 悲傷的故事 / a deeply sad occasion 非常令人傷心的場面 / It is sad that so many of his paintings have been lost. 他的畫作有很多已經失傳了，真可惜。

<span class="vocabulary">**sorry**</span> ['sɒrɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 sorry (that ...) / sorry (to see, hear, etc.) 不用於名詞前，感到悲傷和同情的：</span>We’re sorry to hear that your father’s in hospital. 聽說你父親住院了，我們心裡都不好受。/ No one is sorrier than I am about what happened. 發生了這樣的事，我比誰都難過。<span class="definition">2 只用於名詞前，非常悲傷的（尤指使你感到同情或對其持否定態度的），如悲慘的，可憐的等：</span>They were a sorry sight when they eventually got off the boat. 他們最終從船上下來，一副慘兮兮的樣子。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**unhappy**</span> [ʌnˈhæpi]
<span class="definition">adj.（心情）不快樂的；不幸福的；難過的；悲傷的：</span>an unhappy childhood 不快樂的童年 / I didn't realize but he was deeply unhappy at that time. 我沒有察覺到原來他那時非常不開心。/ Her marriage is in trouble and she is desperately unhappy. 她的婚姻陷入困境，她為此非常鬱悶。/ He was a shy, sometimes unhappy man. 他是一個靦腆的人，有時候還很憂鬱。/ She grew more unhappy as the years went by. 這些年來她越來越鬱鬱寡歡。/ It makes me very unhappy to see you so miserable. 看到你這麼痛苦我感到很難過。/ She is deeply unhappy. 她很不快樂。

<span class="vocabulary">**gloomy**</span> [ˈglu:mi]
<span class="definition">adj. 心情悲傷且沒有希望的，如憂鬱的、沮喪的：</span>a gloomy expression 沮喪的表情 / We sat in gloomy silence. 我們鬱鬱不樂默不作聲地坐著。/ Miller is gloomy about the fate of the serious playwright in America. 米勒對這位嚴肅劇作家在美國的命運頗為憂慮。/ Now, don't start to get gloomy. 嗨，別發愁。           
           
<span class="vocabulary">**bleak**</span> [bli:k]
<span class="definition">adj.（人看上去或聽起來）無精打采的、陰鬱的、沮喪的：</span>His face was bleak. 他臉色陰鬱。/ She gave him a bleak stare. 她沮喪地凝視著他。/ bleak despair 淒涼的絕望 / bleak expression 陰鬱的表情

<span class="vocabulary">**bad**</span> [bæd] 
<span class="definition">adj. feel bad 對某事感到難過。是一個簡單用詞：</span>She felt pretty bad about leaving him. 要離開他，她覺得很難過。

<span class="vocabulary">**blue**</span> [blu:] 
<span class="definition">adj. [非正式] 悲傷的，憂鬱的，相當於depressed：</span>He’d been feeling blue all week. 他整個星期都鬱鬱寡歡。

<span class="vocabulary">**low**</span> [ləʊ] 
<span class="definition">adj. [非正式] 虛弱的；沮喪的；消沉的；無精打采的，相當於down：</span>I’m feeling really low. 我現在很消沉。/ They were in low spirits. 他們精神不振。/ We are all very tired and morale is low. 我們都疲憊不堪，士氣低落。

<span class="vocabulary">**down**</span> [daʊn] 
<span class="definition">adj. [非正式] 不用於名詞前，悲傷的、沮喪的、情緒低落的：</span>I feel a bit down today. 我今天有點悶悶不樂。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**downcast**</span> [ˈdaʊnkɑ:st; 美 -kæst]
<span class="definition">adj.（人或表情）悲傷的、沮喪的、垂頭喪氣的：</span>Barbara looked increasingly downcast as defeat loomed. 當敗象已呈時，芭芭拉看上去越來越沮喪。/ a glum, downcast expression 憂鬱、沮喪的表情 / A group of downcast men stood waiting for food. 一群人垂頭喪氣地站在那兒等著吃飯。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**depressed**</span> [dɪˈprest]
<span class="definition">adj. 沮喪的、抑鬱的：</span>She felt very depressed about the future. 她感到前途無望。/ He seemed somewhat depressed. 他似乎有些抑鬱。/ She's been very depressed and upset about this whole situation. 她對整個情形感到極其沮喪和不快。/ You mustn't let yourself get depressed. 你一定不能讓自己消沉下去。/ It makes me depressed just looking at him. 只要看看他，我就沮喪起來。/ She became severely depressed after her mother's death，媽媽死後她情緒非常低落。/ She's terribly depressed about losing her job. 她為失去工作感到極為沮喪。/ I was depressed by our lack of progress. 我們沒有取得什麼進展，我感到很沮喪。

<span class="vocabulary">**dejected**</span> [dɪˈdʒektɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. 沮喪失望的、垂頭喪氣的：</span>She looked so dejected when she lost the game. 她輸掉比賽時情緒顯得非常低落。/ Everyone has days when they feel dejected or down. 每個人都有沮喪失意或情緒低落的時候。/ They were thoroughly dejected and miserable. 他們情緒極其低落，愁眉苦臉。/ I was feeling rather dejected about the future. 我對未來感到頗為灰心。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**despondent**</span> [dɪˈspɒndənt; 美 -ˈspɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">adj. [美]（因面臨無法克服的困難而）悲傷且沒什麼希望的，如沮喪的、洩氣的：</span>Patients often feel despondent. 病人經常會感到沮喪。/ His work was rejected again and again, and he grew more and more despondent. 他的作品一次又一次被退回，他越來越洩氣了。/ She was feeling utterly despondent. 她感到心灰意冷。/ He had become rather despondent about his lack of progress. 因為沒有進展，他頗為灰心。/ He is increasingly despondent over losing his family. 他因失去家人而變得越來越消沉。/ She was becoming increasingly despondent about the way things were going. 她對情況的發展越來越失望。
   
<span class="vocabulary">**dreadful**</span> [ˈdredfl]
<span class="definition">adj. [尤英]（看上去或感覺到）非常沮喪的：</span>I feel absolutely dreadful about what has happened. 我對已發生的一切感到極其失望。/ I feel really dreadful about letting you down. 讓你失望了，我真過意不去。

<span class="vocabulary">**frustrated**</span> [frʌˈstreɪtɪd; 美 ˈfrʌstreɪtɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 ~ (at/with sth)（因不能做自己想做的事，或沒有實現願望而）懊惱、懊喪、沮喪：</span>It's very easy to get frustrated in this job. 這個工作很容易令人懊惱。/ I'm starting to get frustrated. 我開始變得灰心喪氣。/ This failure leaves the child depressed and frustrated. 這次失敗使這個小孩灰心喪氣。/ She sounded rather frustrated to me. 我覺得她聽上去有幾分沮喪。/ Both sides in the dispute appeared very frustrated at the lack of progress. 爭執雙方因事情毫無進展而顯得十分沮喪。/ We were frustrated by the long delays. 長時間耽擱使我們頗感沮喪。/ Sometimes he gets really frustrated with his violin playing. 他有時會對拉小提琴感到十分灰心。<span class="definition">2（情感）無效的；沒有得到滿足的：</span>He stamped his foot in frustrated rage. 他憤懣鬱積得跺腳。/ frustrated desires 沒有得到滿足的慾望 <span class="definition">3 [只用於名詞前] 在職業生涯上不得志的、失意的：</span>a frustrated artist 不得志的藝術家 <span class="definition">4 性慾沒有得到滿足的：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**subdued**</span> [səbˈdju:d; 美 -ˈdu:d]
<span class="definition">adj.（一個人）異乎尋常地安靜的（且常不快樂），如悶悶不樂的：</span>He seemed a bit subdued to me. 我覺得他當時有點悶悶不樂。/ She was in a subdued mood. 她心情抑鬱。/ I felt strangely subdued when it was all over. 一切都結束的時候，我卻有一種奇怪的壓抑感。/ He faced the press, initially, in a somewhat subdued mood. 他最初面對媒體時情緒有點憂鬱。

<span class="vocabulary">**terrible**</span> ['terəbl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 不用於名詞前，不愉快的：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**sombre**</span> [ˈsɒmbə(r); 美 ˈsɑ:m-]
<span class="definition">adj. 心情陰鬱的、沮喪的；嚴肅的。相當於melancholy：</span>She was in a somewhat sombre mood. 她的心情有些憂鬱。/ The year ended on a sombre note. 那一年在沉悶的氣氛中結束了。/ The pair were in sombre mood. 這兩個人情緒低落。/ His expression became increasingly sombre. 他的表情變得越來越嚴肅。/ Everyone looked very sombre. 大家看上去都悶悶不樂。/ His eyes grew sombre. 他的眼睛變得憂鬱起來。/ The mood turned sombre. 心情變得沉重。/ The mood in the city remained sombre. 城市的氣氛依然沉重。/ The funeral cortege passed, to suitably sombre music. 送葬隊伍在憂傷的音樂中走過。

<span class="definition">adj. 令人抑鬱、沮喪或消沉的：</span>depressing sight, depressing thought 令人沮喪的景象；令人沮喪的想法 / Looking for a job these days can be very depressing. 如今求職有時會令人非常沮喪。/ Yesterday's unemployment figures were depressing. 昨天的失業數字令人沮喪。/ I'm rather keen to go on holiday in February because I think it's a very dismal, depressing month. 我很想2月份去度假，因為我覺得那是一個非常陰沉慘淡、令人抑鬱的月份。/ We found it a deeply depressing experience.（deeply尤為英式用法）我們覺得這次經歷非常令人沮喪。
   
<span class="vocabulary">**distressing**</span> [dɪˈstresɪŋ]
<span class="definition">adj.（尤指別人的痛苦遭遇）令人極悲傷的：</span>I found the story deeply distressing. 我覺得這是個非常悲慘的故事。

<span class="vocabulary">**frustrating**</span> [frʌˈstreɪtɪŋ; 美 ˈfrʌstreɪtɪŋ]
<span class="definition">adj.（因阻止自己做想做的事，或阻止自己實現願望而）令人懊喪的、令人沮喪的：</span>It's frustrating to have to wait so long. 要等這麼長時間，真令人懊惱。/ The current situation is very frustrating for us. 當前的情形令我們十分灰心。/ It is a frustrating and difficult time for Pat. 對帕特來說，這是一個令人沮喪和頗為艱難的時期。/ I found the delays intensely frustrating. 我覺得這些延誤極其令人沮喪。/ It was an ultimately frustrating experience. 那是一次令人沮喪至極的經歷。/ It was rather frustrating for all of us. 這事令我們所有人都頗為沮喪。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**miserable**</span> [ˈmɪzrəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. 1（地方、形勢等）令人非常不悅或難受的：</span>miserable housing conditions 惡劣的住房條件 / I spent a miserable weekend alone at home. 我獨自一人在家度過了一個慘兮兮的週末。/ There was nothing at all in this miserable place to distract him. 這個鬼地方根本沒有什麼東西可以讓他分心。<span class="definition">2（失敗）悲慘的，徹底的：</span>The play was a miserable failure. 這部話劇是個可悲的失敗。/ The film was a miserable commercial failure both in Italy and in the United States. 這部電影在義大利和美國均遭遇票房慘敗。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dismal**</span> [ˈdɪzməl]
<span class="definition">adj.（尤指環境）令人悲傷的，如陰沉的、淒涼的、慘淡的：</span>dismal conditions, dismal surroundings, dismal weather 悲慘的狀況；淒涼的環境；陰沉的天氣 / The main part of the hospital is pretty dismal but the children's ward is really lively. 醫院的主體部分死氣沉沉，但是兒科病房卻充滿生氣。/ a dark dismal day with rain falling steadily 天空昏暗、陰雨綿綿的一天 / Israel's dismal record in the Olympics 以色列在奧運會上讓人沮喪的記錄 / It was a really dismal day. 真是令人沮喪的一天。/ My prospects of returning to a suitable job are dismal. 我重新找到一個合適的工作崗位的希望很渺茫。/ Last year's results were fairly dismal. 去年的結果相當令人失望。

<span class="vocabulary">**melancholy**</span> [ˈmelənkəli; -kɒli; 美 -kɑ:li]
<span class="definition">adj. 令人悲傷的：</span>melancholy thoughts, melancholy memories 悲傷的想法；悲傷的記憶 / melancholy expression 悲傷的表情 / melancholy mood 悲傷的心情 / The melancholy song died away. 哀婉的歌聲漸漸消失了。

周邊詞彙補充：

<span class="vocabulary">**tear**</span> [tɪə] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] tears用於一些固定結構中可表示特定的哭泣動作：</span>to burst into tears 放聲大哭 / to be on the verge of tears 快哭了 / The film brought a tear to her eye. 這部電影把她感動得落淚了。/ The tears welled up in her eyes. 她熱淚盈眶。/ She left the room in tears. 她哭著離開了房間。/ She was in floods of tears on the phone. 她在電話裡大哭。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**tearful**</span> [ˈtɪəfl; 美 ˈtɪrfl]
<span class="definition">adj. 1（人）哭泣的；將要哭泣的（如含淚的）：</span>She suddenly became very tearful. 她突然痛哭流涕。/ She became very tearful when pressed to talk about it. 她被逼談論此事時開始淚如雨下。/ She sounded angry and tearful. 她聽起來很氣憤，帶著哭腔。/ I felt emotional--almost tearful. 我情緒激動——差點兒落淚。/ Defeat left her tearful, pale and drained. 失敗使她淚水漣漣、面色蒼白、精疲力竭。<span class="definition">2（事件等）令人傷心的；催人淚下的：</span>a tearful farewell 揮淚送別

<span class="vocabulary">**dry**</span> [draɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. 無淚的：</span>There were few dry eyes in the house when he told the story. 當他講述那個故事的時候，屋裡幾乎沒有人不掉眼淚的。

相關詞義延伸：
· sob還可指哭泣的同時說某事，即哭訴，抽噎著說；
· choke還可指哽咽、哽咽聲。

周邊詞彙補充：
· sniff可指（哭泣時）發出聲音地把空氣從鼻子吸入，即抽鼻子。
