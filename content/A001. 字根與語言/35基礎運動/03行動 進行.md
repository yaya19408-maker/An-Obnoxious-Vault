☀ <span class="category">**行動、舉動的名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**action**</span> ['ækʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 為使某事發生或為了處理一種情況而做某事的過程，即行動，行為過程：</span>He urged the government to take decisive action against music piracy. 他敦促政府採取果斷行動打擊音樂盜版。/ strike action 罷工行動 / Disciplinary action will be taken against students who cheat. 對作弊的學生將予以懲戒。/ to put these ideas into action 將這些想法付諸行動 / a plan of action 行動計劃 / a course of action 行動方案

<span class="vocabulary">**act**</span> [ækt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 人的某一種（或某一個）行為、作為或行動：</span>charitable, kind act 善舉；仁慈的行為 / heroic, selfless act 英勇的行為；無私的行為 / aggressive, violent act 攻擊性行為；暴力行為 / to plan terrorist acts 策劃恐怖行動 / criminal, illegal, wrongful act 犯罪行為；非法行為；錯誤行為 / foolish, impulsive act 愚蠢的行為；衝動的行為 / a private act of revenge 個人報復行為 / political, symbolic act 政治行動；象徵性的舉動 / homosexual, sex, sexual act 同性戀行為；性行為 / the act of writing, the act of stealing 寫東西；行竊 / an act of love 出於愛心的舉動

<span class="vocabulary">**move**</span> [mu:v] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] move (towards/to sth) / move (to do sth)（為實現或達到…所採取或需要採取的）行動、舉動、做法：</span>The management have made no move to settle the strike. 資方沒有采取任何措施來解決罷工問題。/ The talks represented the first significant move towards/toward peace. 會談代表著向實現和平邁出了有實際意義的第一步。/ The one-point cut in interest rates was a wise move. 利率下調一個點是明智之舉。/ She wondered whether she had made the right move in telling the truth. 她不知道講了實話是否做得對。/ One false move could lead to war. 走錯一步就有可能引發戰爭。/ strategic, tactical move 戰略行動；戰術行動 / legal, military, political move 法律行動；軍事行動；政治行動 / pre-emptive, cost-cutting move 先發制人的行動；降低成本的舉措 / a career move 職業生涯上走出的一步

<span class="vocabulary">**operation**</span> [͵ɒpə'reɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（若干人所進行的有組織的）活動或行動：</span>to launch a major operation against drug suppliers 發動一次打擊毒販的大規模行動 / relief, rescue, security, police, undercover operation 救助行動；營救行動；安全行動；警方行動；秘密行動
           
<span class="vocabulary">**blitz**</span> [blɪts]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, usually sing.] 集中力量的行動；閃擊式行動：</span>advertising, marketing, media, PR, promotional, propaganda, safety blitz 廣告宣傳風暴；閃電式營銷活動；媒體宣傳戰；公共關係攻勢；促銷戰；宣傳攻勢；安全突擊檢查 / On December 8 the media blitz began in earnest. 12月8日，一場大規模媒體宣傳活動轟轟烈烈地展開了。<span class="definition">2 [C, usually sing.] ~ (on sth) 閃電戰式的努力或下功夫，算是一種比喻用法：</span>a blitz on passengers who avoid paying fares 對逃票乘客的突襲檢查 / a blitz on illegal parking 針對非法停車的突擊行動 / an all-out blitz on crime 對犯罪的全面打擊 / We decided to have a blitz on the kitchen（= to clean the kitchen thoroughly），我們決定徹底清掃一下廚房。/ I've had a blitz on the house (= cleaned it very thoroughly). 我徹底清掃了房子。/ Regional accents are still acceptable but there is to be a blitz on incorrect grammar. 地方口音還可以接受，但要下大力氣糾正語法錯誤。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**offensive**</span> [əˈfensɪv]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 為達成某事所發起的（引人注意的）系列行動、運動、攻勢，尤指（表示憤怒或反對的）強硬行動：</span>The government has launched a new offensive against crime. 政府發動了新的打擊犯罪攻勢。/ The government is launching an all-out offensive against the drug cartels. 政府止對販毒集團發起全面打擊。/ Republicans acknowledged that they had little choice but to mount an all-out offensive on the Democratic nominee. 共和黨人承認他們別無選擇，只有對獲提名的民主黨人展開全面攻擊。/ diplomatic, government, propaganda offensive 外交攻勢；政府攻勢；宣傳攻勢 / The politician has launched a charm offensive in an attempt to clean up his image.（比喻用法）這位政治家發起了魅力攻勢，以修復他受損的形象。/ a sales offensive 銷售攻勢 / She took the offensive, challenging her critics to prove their allegations. 她先發制人，要求批評她的人證明他們的指控。

<span class="vocabulary">**sweep**</span> [swi:p] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 大規模的行動：</span>Thousands of jobs were lost in one broad sweep. 一次大範圍的調整使數千人失去了工作。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**stroke**</span> [strəʊk; 美 stroʊk]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (of sth) 成功的行動：</span>Your idea was a stroke of genius. 你的主意很高明。/ At the time, his appointment seemed a stroke of genius. 當時，對他的任命猶如神來之筆。/ I had a sudden stroke of inspiration. 我忽然來了靈感。/ It was a bold stroke to reveal the identity of the murderer on the first page. 在頭版上披露謀殺犯的身份，這是一個大膽的舉措。/ She never does a stroke (of work) (= never does any work). 她一向什麼活兒都不幹。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**proceeding**</span> [prəˈsi:dɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] [正式] proceedings 可指一系列行動：</span>The proceedings of the enquiry will take place in private. 問訊將會秘密進行。

相關詞義延伸：
· operation還可指軍事行動。

<span class="vocabulary">**administer**</span> [ədˈmɪnɪstə(r)]

<span class="vocabulary">**enforcement**</span> [ɪn'fɔ:smənt]

[U]（法律或規定、規則的）執行、實施：</span>strict enforcement of regulations 規章的嚴格執行 / The doctors want stricter enforcement of existing laws, such as those banning sales of cigarettes to children. 醫生們希望加大現行法律的執行力度，如禁止向孩童出售香菸。/ law enforcement 法令的執行 / soldiers involved in peace enforcement operations overseas 參與海外維和行動計程車兵 / civil rights enforcement（美）民權執法 / drug enforcement（尤美）緝毒 / border, immigration enforcement（均美）邊境執法；移民法的執行 / Newspapers called for tougher enforcement of the existing laws on drugs. 報紙上呼籲加強對現行毒品法律的執行力度。/ the selective enforcement of immigration laws（selective尤為美式用法）對移民法的選擇性執行 / judicial, legal, police enforcement 司法實施；執法；警察執法 / enforcement agent, enforcement officer, enforcement official 執行代理人；執行警員；執行官員 / law enforcement officers 執法官員 / enforcement personnel 執行人員 / enforcement agency, enforcement authority, enforcement body 執行機構；執行當局；執行機關 / federal law enforcement agencies 聯邦執法機構 / law enforcement bodies 執法機構 / enforcement machinery, enforcement mechanism, enforcement system 執行機構；執行機制；執行體制 / The court is ineffective because it lacks the necessary enforcement machinery. 法院效率低是因為缺乏必要的執行機構。/ enforcement powers 執行權力 / enforcement action, enforcement efforts, enforcement measures, enforcement methods, enforcement operations, enforcement practices, enforcement procedures, enforcement proceedings 執法行動；執法措施；執法方法；執法慣例；執法程式

<span class="vocabulary">**imposition**</span> [ˌɪmpəˈzɪʃn]

<span class="vocabulary">**prosecution**</span> [ˌprɒsɪˈkju:ʃn; 美 ˌprɑ:s-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式] 使某事發生的行為，如實施、從事、進行：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**engaged**</span> [ɪnˈgeɪdʒd]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (in/on sth) [正式] 從事某事：</span>They found the three engaged in target practice. 他們發現那三個人在練習打靶。/ She was engaged in conversation with a client. 她當時正在和一個客戶交談。/ He is engaged with a customer at the moment.（英）他正在接待一位客戶。/ otherwise engaged（正式用法）另有安排 / I'm afraid Mr Wilson cannot see you now as he is otherwise engaged. 恐怕威爾遜先生現在不能見你，他有別的事。/ I can't come to dinner on Tuesday─I'm otherwise engaged (= I have already arranged to do something else). 我星期二不能來參加宴會——我另有安排。/ He is now engaged on his second novel. 他正埋頭寫他的第二部小說。/ those who are deeply engaged in party politics 深深捲入黨派政治的人們 / He is actively engaged in strategic discussions. 他積極參與戰略討論。/ He is actively engaged on several projects. 他積極參與了幾個專案。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**occupied**</span> [ˈɒkjupaɪd; 美 ˈɑ:k-]
<span class="definition">adj. [名詞前不可用] ~ (doing sth/in doing sth/in sth) / ~ (with sth/with doing sth) 從事於…的：</span>Only half her time is occupied with politics. 她只用自己一半的時間從事政治活動。/ You will be mainly occupied in checking sales records. 你主要的工作是核對銷售記錄。

相關詞義延伸：
· execute還可指製作一件藝術品。

周邊詞彙補充：
· give可指公開進行…或表演…；
· action可指確保某事得以執行或處理。

☀ <span class="category">**做的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**do**</span> [du:] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 用於指你沒提到名字（或不知道）的動作或行為，如做、幹、辦（某事）：</span>What are you doing this evening? 你今晚打算做什麼？/ We will do what we can to help. 我們會盡力幫忙。/ What can I do for you? 我能為您做點什麼？<span class="definition">2 do the ironing, cooking, shopping, etc. / do some, a little, etc. acting, writing, etc. 做、從事（熨燙、烹調、購物、演戲、寫作等）：</span>I like listening to the radio when I’m doing the ironing. 我喜歡邊熨衣服邊聽收音機。/ She did a lot of acting when she was at college. 她在大學時演過很多戲。<span class="definition">3 通常用於疑問句，從事（某項職業）：</span>What do you do? 你幹什麼工作？/ What’s Tom doing these days? 湯姆最近在幹什麼？<span class="definition">4 do sth (for sb) / do (sb) sth 做出、產出或製作某事物：</span>to do a painting 繪畫 / Does this pub do (= provide) lunches? 這家酒館供應午餐嗎？/ Who’s doing the food for the wedding reception? 誰在承辦婚宴的酒席？/ I’ll do a copy for you. 我將為你複製一份。<span class="definition">5 處理或照料（某人或某事物）：</span>The hairdresser said she could do me (= cut my hair) at three. 理髮師說她三點鐘可以給我理髮。<span class="definition">auxiliary verb. 1 用於實義動詞前以構成否定句和疑問句：</span>I don’t like fish. 我不喜歡魚。/ Does she speak French? 她會說法語嗎？<span class="definition">2 用於做出一個附加疑問（即陳述結尾的短問句）：</span>You live in New York, don’t you? 你住在紐約，不是嗎？<span class="definition">3 副詞被移至句首時，用以改變主語和動詞的語序：</span>Not only does she speak Spanish, she’s also good with computers. 她不僅會說西班牙語，還精通計算機。  

<span class="vocabulary">**make**</span> [meɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 寫；出產：</span>My lawyer has been urging me to make a will. 我的律師一直在催促我立遺囑。/ She has made (= directed or acted in) several movies. 她已經出了好幾部電影。<span class="definition">2 與某些名詞連用，表示把某事做得好或糟，即使…存在；引發；使發生；使產生：</span>to make a noise, to make a mess, to make a fuss 產生噪音；弄糟；小題大做 / She tried to make a good impression on the interviewer. 她努力給面試官留個好印象。/ I keep making the same mistakes. 我總是犯同樣的錯誤。<span class="definition">3 make a decision, guess, comment, etc. 與廣泛的名詞連用，表示做出某個動作，或說出…（如做出決定、估計、評論等）：</span>Come on! It’s time we made a start. 快點！我們該出發了。/ I’d just like to make a comment. 我只想做一下評論。/ I made a few phone calls. 我打了幾個電話。<span class="definition">4 make a case for sth 可表示為某事做解釋（以證明其是最佳做法）：</span>You could certainly make a case for this point of view. 你當然可以為這種觀點做解釋。<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. [正式] make as if to do sth或make to do sth可表示擺出（要做某事的姿態）：</span>Mary made as if to protest, then hesitated. 瑪麗似乎要抗議，又猶豫了。

<span class="vocabulary">**form**</span> [fɔ:m] 
<span class="definition">vt. form sth (into sth) / form sth (from/of sth) 常用被動式，以某種方式生產或製作…，或使其具有某種形狀：</span>Form the dough into balls with your hands. 用手把生麵糰揉成一些球團。/ Bend the wire so that it forms a ‘V’. 把鐵絲彎成V形。/ Rearrange the letters to form a new word. 重新排列字母，組成另一單詞。/ to form the past tense 構成過去時 / The chain is formed from 136 links. 這根鏈條由136個環組成。/ These panels folded up to form a screen. 這些嵌板折起來形成了一個屏風。/ a perfectly formed body 完美的軀體

<span class="vocabulary">**repeat**</span> [rɪ'pi:t] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 再次（或多於一遍地）做（或產生）…：</span>Lift and lower the right leg 20 times. Repeat with the left leg. 將右腿提放20次。再將左腿提放20次。/ to repeat a process 重複一個過程 / to repeat an exercise 反覆練習 / to repeat the class 重修這門課 / to repeat the grade 留級 / The programmes will be repeated next year. 明年將繼續開設這些課程。/ to repeat the same mistakes 重複同樣的錯誤 / It may be necessary to repeat the dose several times to effect a cure. 為確保療效，有必要多服幾劑。

<span class="vocabulary">**duplicate**</span> [ˈdju:plɪkeɪt; 美 ˈdu:-]
<span class="definition">vt.（尤指不必要時）把某事再做一次：</span>There's no point in duplicating work already done. 重複別人已經做過的工作毫無意義。/ His task will be to duplicate his success overseas here at home. 他在海外取得了成功，現在的任務就是要在國內再創輝煌。/ Scientists hope the work done in collaboration with other researchers may be duplicated elsewhere. 科學家們希望與其他研究人員合作的成果可以適用於別處。/ The original experiment cannot be exactly duplicated. 最初的實驗不能精確地重現。

<span class="vocabulary">**undertake**</span> [͵ʌndə'teɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 使自己對某事負責並開始做這件事：</span>University professors both teach and undertake research. 大學教授既要教學又要從事研究工作。/ to undertake a full investigation into the accident 對事故進行全面調查

<span class="vocabulary">**get**</span> [ɡet]

<span class="vocabulary">**pay**</span> [peɪ]

☀ <span class="category">**做的其它詞性：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**to**</span> [tu:] 
<span class="definition">infinitive marker. 1 表示自己想做（或別人建議你去做）的某事：</span>I’d love to go to France this summer. 今年夏天我想去法國。/ The leaflet explains how to apply for a place. 這本小冊子介紹如何申請職位。/ I don’t know what to say. 我不知道說什麼好。<span class="definition">2 置於動詞原形前構成不定式，該不定式結構可置於某些動詞、名詞和形容詞以及如how、which和where等詞之後：</span>The management wanted to know what I was doing there. 管理層想知道我在那兒做什麼。/ She told the family of her decision to resign. 她告訴她家裡人她要辭職的決定。

<span class="vocabulary">**at**</span> [æt] 
<span class="definition">prep. 用於動詞後，表示某人嘗試去做某事，或部分地做某事，但並未成功或完成：</span>He clutched wildly at the rope as he fell. 他墜落時拼命想抓住繩子。/ She nibbled at a sandwich (= ate only small bits of it). 她一小口一小口地吃三明治。

相關詞義延伸：
· go還可指在移動中做某事；
· make還可指制定（規則、法律等）。也可指做筆記，做批註，寫清單等。亦可指造成（破壞、破損等）；
· hustle還可指推搡、猛推或硬拉某人，以使其快走；
· at還可指某人正在做某事。

周邊詞彙補充：
· experience可指由實踐所得來的經驗，也可指得到經驗的過程，即實踐；
· set可指有可能做某事的、為某事或為做某事做好準備的、某事有可能發生或將要發生的；
· prepare可指預備飯菜（以供食用），做飯。也可指製作一種藥物或化學物質（例如透過將不同的物質混合在一起），可稱為調製，配製等；
· plot可指（為小說、戲劇等）寫情節，設計故事情節，即佈局；
· cut可指灌製唱片，製作鐳射唱片等；
· composition可指（藝術家的）創作（如作曲）；
· invent可指虛構或編造沒有的事情（以欺騙別人）；
· invention可指虛構、編造；
· copy可指複製或仿造某個東西、模仿或效仿某人的做法；
· practical可指（一個人）善於製作或修理東西的，如心靈手巧的。
