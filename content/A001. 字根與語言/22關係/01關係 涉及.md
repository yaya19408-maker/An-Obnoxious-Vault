<span class="vocabulary">**relevance**</span> [ˈreləvəns]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 相關性：</span>Politicians' private lives have no relevance to their public roles. 政治家的私生活與他們的公眾角色不相關。/ What he said has no direct relevance to the matter in hand. 他所說的話與眼下的事沒有直接關係。/ This debate has limited relevance to our current concerns. 這場辯論與我們當前關注的問題關係不大。/ the continuing relevance of art to our daily lives 藝術與日常生活的息息相關 / The book is of particular relevance to student nurses. 這本書是專門面向實習護士的。/ Her ideas have lost all relevance to the modern world. 她的想法與現代社會完全脫節。/ Skim the book in order to establish its relevance to your needs. 瀏覽一下這本書，找找你所需要的東西。/ A Marxist approach emphasizes the relevance of class power. 馬克思主義的方法強調階級力量所起的作用。/ I can't see the relevance of his comment to the debate.我不明白他的評說與這一爭論有何關係。

<span class="vocabulary">**relation**</span> [rɪ'leɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [pl.] relations (between A and B) / relations (with sb/sth) 兩個人（或團體、國家）之間的關係（是雙方之間的一種互動方式或處理與對方有關的事務的方式）：</span>bilateral, foreign, international, trade relations 雙邊關係；對外關係；國際關係；貿易關係 / class, gender relations 階級關係；兩性關係 / social relations 社會關係 / public relations 公共關係 / customer, investor relations 客戶關係；投資者關係 / interpersonal relations 人際關係 / family, marital relations 家庭關係；婚姻關係 / parent-child relations 親子關係 <span class="definition">2 [U, C] relation (between A and B) / relation (to sth) 兩個或多個事物之間的聯絡方式，如關係、聯絡、關聯（relation、relationship、association和link均可表示事物之間的“關係”或“聯絡”，如邏輯聯絡、因果關係。其中relation和relationship為可數名詞和不可數名詞，是此義的基礎用詞，二者幾無差別。association為不可數名詞，也較為常用，link為可數名詞，也算常用）：</span>causal relation 因果關係 / The energy an animal uses is in direct relation to speed and body mass. 動物消耗的能量與其速度和體重直接相關。/ the relation between rainfall and crop yields 降雨量和農作物產量之間的關係 / the relation of the farmer to the land 農民和土地的關係 / the relation between technology and culture 技術與文化之間的關係 / the relation of the subject to the object 主語和賓語的關係 / spatial relations between objects 物體之間的空間關係

<span class="vocabulary">**relationship**</span> [rɪ'leɪʃnʃɪp] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] relationship (between A and B) / relationship (with sb) 兩個人（或團體、國家）之間的關係（是雙方之間的一種互動方式或處理與對方有關的事務的方式）：</span>a master-servant relationship 主僕關係 / Britain’s special relationship with the US 英美兩國的特殊關係 / loving relationship 愛的關係 / family, interpersonal relationship 家庭關係；人際關係 / parent-child, father-son relationship 親子關係；父子關係 / business, contractual, social, working relationship 業務關係；契約關係；社會關係；工作關係 / a network of relationships, a web of relationships 關係網 / the relationship between the police and the local community 警察和當地社群之間的關係 / She has a very close relationship with her sister. 她和她妹妹非常親密。<span class="definition">2 [C, U] relationship (between A and B) / relationship (to sth) 兩個或多個事物之間的聯絡方式，如關係、聯絡、關聯：</span>a close relationship between increased money supply and inflation 貨幣供應的增加與通貨膨脹之間的密切關聯 / the inverse relationship between gas consumption and air temperature 汽油消耗量與氣溫的反向關係 / the relationship between spatial awareness and mathematical ability 空間想象能力與數學學習能力之間的關係 / positive, negative relationship 正相關；負相關 / causal, dynamic, reciprocal, symbiotic relationship 因果關係；動態的關係；互惠的關係；共生關係 / linear, spatial relationship 線性關係；空間關係 / economic, legal relationship 經濟聯絡；法律關係
           
<span class="vocabulary">**bearing**</span> [ˈbeərɪŋ; 美 ˈber-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] ~ on sth 關係：</span>Recent events had no bearing on our decision. 近期的事件與我們的決定沒有關係。/ Experts generally agree that diet has an important bearing on your general health. 專家們普遍認為飲食和健康總體狀況密切相關。/ My father's achievements really don't have any bearing on what I do. 我父親的成就與我做的事情真的毫無關聯。/ The rise in interest rates had a direct bearing on the company's profits. 利率的提高對公司的利潤有直接的影響。/ You are describing ideal conditions that have little bearing on the real world. 你描述的是理想條件，與真實世界沒有多少關係。           

<span class="vocabulary">**correlation**</span> [ˌkɒrəˈleɪʃn; 美 ˌkɔ:r-; ˌkɑ:r-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] ~ (between A and B) / ~ (of A with B) 兩者間的相互關聯（即一方的變化會造成另一方的變化）：</span>There is a direct correlation between exposure to sun and skin cancer. 皮膚暴露在太陽下與皮膚癌直接相關。/ The study showed a significant correlation between smoking and heart disease. 研究表明吸菸與心臟病有很大關聯。/ A strong correlation exists between obesity in parents and in their children. 父母的肥胖和子女的肥胖之間存在著密切關聯。/ the correlation between speed and risk of accident 速度和事故風險之間的相關性 / the correlation of height with weight 身高與體重的關聯性 / the correlation of social power with wealth 社會權力與財富的相關性 / positive correlation 正相關 / inverse, negative correlation 逆相關；負相關 / statistical correlation 統計相關 / The second group of measurements had a high correlation with the first. 第二組測量資料與第一組具有很強的相關性。/ lack of correlation 沒有相關性
           
<span class="vocabulary">**correspondence**</span> [ˌkɒrəˈspɒndəns; 美 ˌkɔ:rəˈspɑ:n-; ˌkɑ:-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] ~ (between A and B)（兩個事物間的）相關；相似：</span>There is a close correspondence between the two extracts. 這兩段摘錄如出一轍。/ In African languages there is a close correspondence between sounds and letters. 非洲語言中的發音與字母關係密切。/ correspondences between Eastern religions and Christianity 東方宗教與基督教的關聯 / The child can see the one-to-one correspondence of the buttons and buttonholes.這個孩子能看出紐扣和釦眼之間一一對應的關係。/ a close correspondence between theory and practice 理論和實踐之間的密切聯絡

<span class="vocabulary">**connection**</span> [kə'nekʃən] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] connection (between A and B) / connection (with sth)（兩個事實、觀念、事物、區域等的）聯絡、關聯，相當於link：</span>causal connection 因果關係 / a connection between air pollution and asthma 空氣汙染與哮喘之間的聯絡 / the connection between crime and alcohol 犯罪和酗酒之間的關係 / a set of connections among brain regions 大腦各個區域間的一系列關聯 / His death had no connection with drugs. 他的死與毒品無關。<span class="definition">2 [C] 人與某個外界事物或事件之間的關係：</span>emotional, spiritual connection 情感聯絡；精神聯絡 / We need to feel a connection to nature. 我們需要感覺到與大自然的聯絡。/ What is your connection with the school? 你跟這所學校是什麼關係？/ He denied any connection to the scam. 他否認與該騙局有關。<span class="definition">3 [C] 人際關係，組織之間的關係，人與組織之間的關係：</span>His deepest connection is with his father, Frank Sr. 與他感情最深厚的人是他的父親老弗蘭克。/ This helps companies strengthen their connections to their customers. 這有助於公司加強與客戶的聯絡。/ She wanted to sever all her connections with the company. 她想斷絕和那家公司的所有聯絡。<span class="definition">4 [C, usually pl.] 你所知道的、能夠在你的社會生活或專業領域為你提供幫助或建議的一個人或一個組織機構（如有社交關係或業務關係的人或機構），相當於contact：</span>business, family, personal, professional, social connection 業務關係；家庭關係；私人關係；職業關係；社會關係 / He got his job through connections. 他透過關係找到了工作。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**liaison**</span> [liˈeɪzn; 美 liˈeɪzɑ:n; ˈliəzɑ:n]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, sing.] ~ (between A and B) 機構之間或機構內部的部門之間的聯絡、聯絡（常涉及交換資訊或觀點）：</span>Our role is to ensure liaison between schools and parents. 我們的作用是確保學校與家長間的聯絡。/ She is responsible for liaison with researchers at other universities. 她負責與其他大學的研究人員聯絡。/ We work in close liaison with the police. 我們與警方密切配合。/ Liaison between police forces and the art world is vital to combat art crime. 警方和藝術界的聯手對於打擊藝術品犯罪至關重要。/ those who work in close liaison with alcoholics 在工作中與酗酒者密切接觸的人們 / We maintained a close liaison with the trade union. 我們和工會保持密切聯絡。/ We are hoping to establish good liaisons with these groups. 我們希望與這些團體建立良好的聯絡。/ liaison committee, liaison group, liaison team, liaison unit 聯絡委員會；聯絡小組；聯絡團隊；聯絡部門 / liaison officer 聯絡員 / a community liaison officer (= for example in the police) 社群聯絡員 / a customer liaison manageer (英) 客戶聯絡經理 / military liaison 軍事聯絡 / liaison work 聯絡工作 / good liaison between management and staff 管理層與員工之間暢通的聯絡 <span class="definition">2 [C] ~ (to/with sb/sth) 兩個群體或機構之間的聯絡員、聯絡人：</span>the White House liaison to organized labor 白宮與工人組織的聯絡人 / I have a professor on my staff here as liaison with our higher education institutions. 我的員工中有一名教授充當我們和高等教育機構的聯絡人。/ She acts as a liaison between patients and staff. 她在病人與醫護人員間充當溝通的橋樑。/ We hired someone as customer liaison.（美）我們僱用了一名客戶聯絡員。

<span class="definition">vt. 顯示（兩個或多個事實或數字等）的緊密聯絡，將…聯絡起來：</span>Researchers are trying to correlate the two sets of figures. 研究人員正試圖展示這兩組數字的相關性。/ Attempts to correlate specific language functions with particular parts of the brain have not advanced very far. 人們試圖把特定的語言功能與大腦的某些特定部位聯絡起來，這一努力目前還沒有取得很大進展。/ Lieutenant Ryan closed his eyes, first mentally viewing the different crime scenes, then correlating the data. 瑞安中尉閉上眼睛，先是在腦海裡回想了一遍不同的犯罪現場，然後將這些資訊聯絡起來。

<span class="vocabulary">**concern**</span> [kən'sə:n] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 常用被動式，影響某人、涉及或牽涉某人。是一個常規用詞：</span>Don’t interfere in what doesn’t concern you. 不要管與自己無關的事。/ Where our children’s education is concerned, no compromise is acceptable. 在事關我們的孩子的教育問題上，那是毫無妥協餘地的。/ Everyone who was directly concerned in the incident has now resigned. 所有與該事件有直接牽連的人現在均已辭職。<span class="definition">2 也可用be concerned with sth 關於…，與…有關，涉及：</span>The story concerns the prince’s efforts to rescue Pamina. 這故事講的是王子奮力解救帕米娜。/ The book is primarily concerned with Soviet-American relations during the Cold War. 這部書主要講的是冷戰時期的蘇美關係。/ This chapter concerns itself with the historical background. 本章旨在講述歷史背景。/ One major difference between these computers concerns the way in which they store information. 這些計算機之間的一個主要差異就是其儲存資訊的方式。

<span class="vocabulary">**implicate**</span> [ˈɪmplɪkeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb (in sth) 一件壞事或一樁罪案把某人牽涉進去：</span>He tried to avoid saying anything that would implicate him further. 他盡力避免說出任何會進一步牽連他的事情。/ He was obliged to resign when one of his own aides was implicated in a financial scandal. 當他的一位助手牽涉進一起金融醜聞時,他被迫辭職。/ Allegations had appeared in the press implicating the army and police in some of the killings. 有關軍方和警方與幾起命案有牽連的指控出現在了新聞報道中。/ These groups are very strongly implicated in the violence. 這些團體與這起暴力事件有很大關聯。/ He was falsely implicated in an attempted murder case. 他被誤認為與一起未遂謀殺案有牽連。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**implication**</span> [ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] ~ (of sb) (in sth)（被）牽連，牽涉（進罪案之中）：</span>He resigned after his implication in a sex scandal. 他在涉及一件性醜聞之後辭職了。

<span class="vocabulary">**incriminate**</span> [ɪnˈkrɪmɪneɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. 使某人看起來好像做了錯事或犯了罪，如牽連、連累：</span>They were afraid of answering the questions and incriminating themselves. 他們擔心因回答這些問題而受到牽連。/ He claimed that the drugs had been planted to incriminate him. 他聲稱毒品是別人為了陷害他而故意放在那裡的。/ They are afraid of incriminating themselves and say no more than is necessary. 他們害怕受牽連，一句話也不肯多說。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**involve**</span> [ɪnˈvɒlv; 美 ɪnˈvɑ:lv]
<span class="definition">vt.（一種情況、一個事件或活動）牽涉或影響某事物：</span>Many of the crimes involved drugs. 許多罪案都與毒品有關。/ A late booking may involve you in extra cost. 預訂晚了的話你可能要額外多花錢。<span class="definition">vt.（一種情況、一個事件或活動）牽涉或影響某人：</span>There was a serious incident involving a group of youths. 有一起涉及一群年輕人的嚴重事件。/ How many vehicles were involved in the crash? 這次撞車事故涉及多少輛汽車？/ If there was a cover-up, it involved people at the very highest levels of government. 如果有人想要隱瞞實情，那其中就涉及政府部門最高層的人物。/ a riot involving a hundred inmates 有一百名囚犯參與的暴動 <span class="definition">vt. ~ sb (in sth) 用言行來表明某人參與某事（尤指表明其參與了犯罪）。相當於implicate：</span>His confession involved a number of other politicians in the affair. 他的自白供出其他一些政治人物也涉及此事。/ I didn't mean to involve you in all this. 我沒打算把你牽連進來。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**involvement**</span> [ɪnˈvɒlvmənt; 美 -ˈvɑ:lv-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] ~ (in/with sth) 牽連；牽涉：</span>Winters denies any involvement in the robbery. 溫特斯否認與搶劫有任何牽連。 / There is no evidence to suggest criminal involvement. 沒有證據顯示與犯罪有牽涉。/ He was found to have a deep involvement in drug dealing. 發現他與販毒活動牽涉很深。

<span class="vocabulary">**pertain**</span> [pəˈteɪn; 美 pərˈt-]
<span class="definition">vi. [正式] 在某種情況下（或在某個時間）與…相關：</span>Living conditions are vastly different from those pertaining in their country of origin. 生活條件與他們祖國大不相同。/ The restrictions he imposed pertained to the type and height of buildings and the activities for which they could be used. 他限制的是建築物的種類和高度以及它們的用途。/ I would much rather that you asked Mrs Zuckerman any questions pertaining to herself. 我寧願你問楚克爾曼夫人的是與她本人相關的問題。

<span class="vocabulary">**cover**</span> ['kʌvə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 涉及：</span>The lectures covered a lot of ground. 這些講座涉及的內容極為廣泛豐富。

<span class="vocabulary">**surround**</span> [sə'raʊnd] 
<span class="definition">vt. 與…緊密相關，即圍繞：</span>publicity surrounding the divorce 媒體圍繞這樁離婚事件的報道

<span class="vocabulary">**follow**</span> ['fɒləʊ] 
<span class="definition">vt.（書籍或電影）涉及…生活；或有關…的發展：</span>The novel follows the fortunes of a village community in Scotland. 小說敘述了蘇格蘭一個村落的變遷。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**peg**</span> [peg]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] ~ sth (at/to sth) 使工資、價格等與…掛鉤：</span>Loan repayments are pegged to your income. 分期付還貸款按你的收入計算。/ Its currency is pegged to the dollar. 其貨幣與美元掛鉤。

☀ <span class="category">**有關、有聯絡的形容詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**related**</span> [rɪˈleɪtɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (to sth/sb) 與…有關的、有聯絡的：</span>Much of the crime in this area is related to drug abuse. 這一地區的許多犯罪都與吸毒有關。/ These problems are closely related. 這些問題都是密切相關的。/ related issue, related question 相關的問題 / a stress-related illness 壓力導致的疾病 / The philosophical problems of chance and of free will are closely related. 或然性和自由意志這兩個哲學問題是緊密相關的。/ equipment and accessories for diving and related activities 潛水及相關活動所需的裝備和配件 / The two ideas are very closely related. 這兩種觀點緊密相關。/ Scores in the test were not significantly related to gender. 這項測試的分數與性別沒有太大關聯。/ What she was reading was not remotely related to her work 她讀的東西與她的工作毫不相關。/ Tax rates were indirectly related to income. 稅率與收入有間接關係。/ positively related 正相關的 / inversely, negatively related 負向關係的；負相關的 / The traditional approach has tended to regard unemployment and inflation as being inversely related. 傳統觀點傾向於認為失業與通貨膨脹成反比關係。/ causally, contextually, functionally, logically, structurally, thematically related 有因果關係的；有上下文關係的；功能相關的；邏輯相關的；結構相關的；主題相關的 / Is unemployment causally related to crime?失業與犯罪有因果關係嗎? / The occurrence of the disease is apparently related to standards of hygiene. 這種病的發生率顯然與衛生狀況相關。

<span class="vocabulary">**relevant**</span> ['reləvənt] 
<span class="definition">adj. relevant (to sth/sb) 與你正在討論的主題（或你正在思考的情況）緊密相關的。極為常用：</span>a relevant question 相關的問題 / These comments are not directly relevant to this enquiry. 這些意見與這項調查沒有直接聯絡。/ Do you have the relevant experience? 你有相關的經驗嗎？/ If a story is personally relevant, people are more likely to remember it. 如果一個故事與某人相關，人們更可能記住它。/ information relevant to this case 此案的相關資訊

<span class="vocabulary">**relative**</span> ['relətɪv]

<span class="vocabulary">**interrelated**</span> [ˌɪntərɪˈleɪtɪd]

<span class="vocabulary">**pertinent**</span> [ˈpɜ:tɪnənt; 美 ˈpɜ:rtnənt]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] ~ (to sth)（對某種情況來說）合適的、恰當的；與某一主題有關的、相關的：</span>a pertinent fact 有關的事實 / She had asked some pertinent questions. 她問了一些相關的問題。/ Pertinent information will be forwarded to the appropriate party. 有關資訊將會轉發給相關方。/ Please keep your comments pertinent to the topic under discussion. 請勿發表與討論主題無關的言論。/ These examples are directly pertinent to the question asked. 這些例子與提出的問題直接相關。/ The issues dealt with in the report are highly pertinent to our own situation.報告中談及的問題與我們自己的處境有很大關聯。

<span class="vocabulary">**involved**</span> [ɪnˈvɒlvd; 美 ɪnˈvɑ:lvd]
<span class="definition">adj. [名詞前不可用] ~ (in sth) 與某事有關聯：</span>Some people tried to stop the fight but I didn't want to get involved. 有人設法阻止打鬥，但我不想牽涉進去。/ He was involved in a road accident. 他牽涉到一場交通事故當中。/ We need to examine all the costs involved in the project first. 我們首先應該仔細考慮與這一專案有關的所有費用。/ We'll make our decision and contact the people involved. 我們將作出決定，再與有關人員聯絡。/ Drugs were not directly involved in her death. 藥物與她的死亡沒有直接關係。

<span class="vocabulary">**allied**</span> [əˈlaɪd,ˈælaɪd]
