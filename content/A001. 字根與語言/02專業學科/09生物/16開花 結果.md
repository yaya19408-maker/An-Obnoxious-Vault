☀ <span class="category">**開花 結果：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**flower**</span> ['flaʊə] 
<span class="definition">vi. 開花。算是名詞用作動詞：</span>It will flower this June. 它今年六月會開花。

<span class="vocabulary">**blow**</span> [bləʊ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（植物或花）開放：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**open**</span> ['əʊpən] 
<span class="definition">adj.（花兒等）開放的：</span>The flowers are all open now. 花現在都開了。

<span class="vocabulary">**opening**</span> ['əʊpənɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（花朵的）開放：</span>the opening of a flower 花朵的綻放

<span class="vocabulary">**out**</span> [aʊt] 
<span class="definition">adv., prep.（花）完全開放，盛開：</span>The wild flowers are coming out. 野花正盛開著。<span class="definition">adj.（花）盛開的：</span>Well, the daffodils are out in the gardens! 哇，花園裡的水仙花開了！

<span class="vocabulary">**bear**</span> [beə] 
<span class="definition">vt. [正式]（植物或樹木）開花、結果或長枝葉：</span>to bear fruit 結果實 / to bear flowers 開花 / to bear leaves 長葉子

<span class="vocabulary">**fruit**</span> [fru:t] 
<span class="definition">vi. [術語]（樹、花、草等植物）結果實：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**seed**</span> [si:d] 
<span class="definition">vi. 植物產生種子，即結籽：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">vt. seed itself 用自己的種子繁殖出新的植株，即（植物）繁殖：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**green**</span> [ɡri:n] 
<span class="definition">adj. 指果實未成熟的、青的：</span>green tomatoes 青西紅柿
