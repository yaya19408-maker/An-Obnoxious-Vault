☀ <span class="category">**比率 比例：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**rate**</span> [reɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 比率；率：</span>success rate 成功率 / divorce rate 離婚率 / death rate 死亡率 / recovery rate 治癒率 / unemployment rate 失業率

<span class="vocabulary">**per cent**</span> [pə'sent]（尤英）（美尤用percent）
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一百份之中的份數，即百分之…（通常用%表示）。用於表示一個具體的比率：</span>10 per cent 10% <span class="definition">adj. 百分之…的：</span>a 15 per cent rise in price 價格上揚15% <span class="definition">adv. 百分之…地：</span>House prices rose five per cent last year. 去年房價上漲百分之五。

<span class="vocabulary">**percentage**</span> [pə'sentɪdӡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 百分率；百分比。如果說per cent前面一般都要加數字，那麼percentage雖然前面也可加數字，但這種情況不常見：</span>The figure is expressed as a percentage. 數字是用百分率表示的。/ The area has a high percentage of unemployed men. 該地區失業率很高。/ on a percentage basis 按照一定的百分比 / Interest rates are expected to rise by one percentage point. 利率預計將提高一個百分點。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**scale**</span> [skeɪl]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（地圖、圖表或模型的）比例、比例尺：</span>a scale of 1:25 000 1：25 000的比例 / The map has a scale of one centimetre to the kilometre. 這幅地圖的比例是一釐米代表一千米。/ The map, on a scale of 1:10,000, shows over 5,000 individual paths. 該地圖以 1:10,000 的比例尺顯示了 5,000 多條不同道路。/ a scale drawing 按比例縮放的圖畫 / Is this diagram to scale (= are all its parts the same size and shape in relation to each other as they are in the thing represented)? 這個示意圖是按比例畫的嗎？/ Both plans are drawn to the same scale. 兩張平面圖是按同一比例繪製的。/ The plan of the building is not drawn to scale. 大樓的平面圖畫得不合比例。/ He's made a scale model of the Eiffel Tower. 他按比例做了一個埃菲爾鐵塔模型。/ Franklin made his mother an intricately detailed scale model of the house. 富蘭克林為他的母親製作了一個複雜精細的房屋模型。
                       
<span class="vocabulary">**ratio**</span> [ˈreɪʃiəʊ; 美 -oʊ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (of A to B)（兩組之間的）比率、比例：</span>What is the ratio of men to women in the department? 這個部門的男女比例是多少？/ The school has a very high teacher-student ratio. 這所學校的師生比例很高。/ In 1978 there were 884 students at a lecturer/student ratio of 1:15. 1978年有884名學生，師生比為1比15。/ the worsening student/teacher ratio in our schools 我們各學校日趨不合理的師生比例 / The ratio of applications to available places currently stands at 100:1. 目前，申請人數和就業崗位的比例為100:1。/ The bottom chart shows the ratio of personal debt to personal income. 下面的圖表顯示了個人負債與個人收入之比。/ gender, sex ratio 性別比例 / Some universities take steps to keep their gender ratio 50-50. 有些大學採取多種措施以保持男女性別比例各佔一半。/ Staffing ratios were generally better in smaller hospitals. 小醫院的人員配備比通常要好些。/ male-female, nurse-patient ratio 男女比例；護士與病人比例 / benefit-to-risk, power-to-weight ratio 利益與風險之比；權力與責任之比 / price-earnings, price-to-earnings ratio（均為商業用法）市盈率 / The stock's price-earnings ratio has dropped to 24. 該股票的市盈率已跌至24。/ They have a high ratio of imports to exports. 他們進口對出口的比率很高。/ The hospital is trying to improve its staff/patient ratio. 該醫院正努力提高醫患比例。/ Waist-to-hip ratios vary according to body type. 腰與臀的比例隨體型的不同而不同。/ We mixed the oil and water in a ratio of one to five. 我們以1比5的比例混合油和水。/ the ratio between the amount of time spent on the work and the profit produced 該工作所花時間與獲益之比 / the ratio of house prices to incomes 房價與收入之比

<span class="vocabulary">**proportion**</span> [prəˈpɔ:ʃn; 美 -ˈpɔ:rʃn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] ~ (of sth to sth) 比例關係：</span>direct proportion 正比 / inverse proportion 反比 / The human population in the region is expanding in inverse proportion to the wildlife. 這個地區的人口在膨脹，而野生生物的數量卻在減少。/ The cost of insurance increases in proportion to the performance of the car. 汽車的效能越好，保險費用就越高。/ The proportion of men to women in the college has changed dramatically over the years. 近年來，這個學院的男女學生比例出現了劇變。/ The proportion of women in the profession had risen to 17.3%. 從事該職業的女性比例已升高到17.3%。/ Women's bodies tend to have a higher proportion of fat to water. 女性的身體內脂肪與水分的比例往往更高。/ The radio station has to include a substantial proportion of classical music. 該電臺不得不播放大量的古典音樂。/ The basic ingredients are limestone and clay in the proportion 2:1. 基本成分是石灰石和黏土，比例為2:1。/ The proportion of sand to cement used was three to one. 所用的沙子與水泥的比例是3比1。/ The room is very long in proportion to (= relative to) its width. 這房間的長度比寬度大很多。/ The pressure in the cylinders would go up in proportion to the boiler pressure. 汽缸內的壓力會隨著汽鍋壓力的增加而相應增加。/ Children tend to have relatively larger heads than adults in proportion to the rest of their body. 與成人相比，兒童頭部佔整個身體的比例相對較大。<span class="definition">2 [U, C, usually pl.]（在尺寸、程度、重要性等方面）正確的比例、勻稱：</span>correct proportion 正確的比例 / You haven't drawn the figures in the foreground in proportion. 你的前景人物畫得不合比例。/ The head is out of proportion with the body. 頭部和身體不成比例。/ an impressive building with fine proportions 比例諧調的雄偉建築物 / You can vary the relative proportions of things in a picture very simply. 你能很簡單地改變一幅畫中事物的相對比例。/ Always try to keep a sense of proportion (= of the relative importance of different things). 應常記在心，事有輕重緩急之分。/ We must not lose our sense of proportion. 我們不能喪失主次觀念。/ Try to keep your view of the situation in proportion (= not think it is more serious than it is). 看待情勢要客觀。/ The punishment was out of all proportion to the crime. 罰不當罪。/ The costs of the plan are out of proportion to the budget available. 計劃的成本與現有的預算不成比例。/ Everything just got blown out of proportion. 所有一切都變得不可收拾了。/ We've got to keep this in proportion. 我們應當正確對待這一點。/ This issue was about to be blown out of proportion. 這個問題快要被誇大得沒邊沒沿兒了。/ The problem has been exaggerated out of all proportion. 問題被過分誇大了。<span class="definition">3 [U] [數學] 兩隊數字之間的等比例關係（如4/8=6/12）：</span>瞭解即可
 
<span class="vocabulary">**incidence**</span> [ˈɪnsɪdəns]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] [正式] ~ of sth 某事（尤指壞事）的發生率：</span>an area with a high incidence of crime 犯罪率高的地區 / the highest reported incidence of air pollution 有報告以來最高的空氣汙染發生率 / The incidence of breast cancer increases with age. 乳腺癌的發病率隨年齡增長而遞增。/ the high incidence of child mortality 兒童的高死亡率 / There is a greater incidence of cancer in the families of radiation workers. 接觸放射性物質的工作人員，其家屬的癌症發病率要更高一些。/ The country had the lowest incidence of AIDS cases proportional to its population. 相對於人口而言，該國艾滋病發病率是最低的。/ The medical histories of our patients show a high incidence of past diseases. 我們的病人病歷表明他們大都有既往病史。/ They found an increased incidence of childhood leukaemia in some areas. 他們發現有些地區兒童白血病發病率上升。/ We compared the incidence of coronary heart disease and total mortality. 我們把冠心病的發病率與總死亡率作了比較。/ an incidence rate of 4 or 5 per 10 000 of the population 發生機率為每1萬人中4至5人 / The study noted an increased incidence of heart disease in women.該研究發現女性心臟病發病率有所上升。

<span class="vocabulary">**proportional**</span> [prəˈpɔ:ʃənl; 美 -ˈpɔ:rʃ-]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (to sth)（尺寸、數量、程度等）成比例的、相稱的、均衡的：</span>Salary is proportional to years of experience. 薪金視資歷而定。/ Loss of weight is directly proportional to the rate at which the disease is progressing. 體重的下降與病情發展的速度成正比。/ a proportional fee based on the final sale price 與最後銷售價格成比例的費用 / The amount of food a child needs is roughly proportional to her size. 小孩需要的食物量與其個頭兒大致成比例。/ The speed of the glider is directly proportional to the speed of the wind. 滑翔機的速度與風速成正比。/ The amount of force needed is inversely proportional to the rigidity of the material. 所需要的力的大小與材料的硬度成反比。

<span class="vocabulary">**in**</span> [ɪn] 
<span class="definition">prep. 用於表明比率或相對數量：</span>one in three 三分之一

<span class="vocabulary">**out**</span> [aʊt] 
<span class="definition">adv., prep. out of sth 從（某個數目或集）中：</span>three out of five 五分之三 / He scored eight out of ten. 總分十分他得了八分。

<span class="vocabulary">**to**</span> [tu:] 
<span class="definition">prep. 表示數量或比率：</span>There are 2.54 centimetres to an inch. 一英寸等於2.54釐米。/ This car does 30 miles to the gallon. 這輛汽車每加侖汽油可行駛30英里。
