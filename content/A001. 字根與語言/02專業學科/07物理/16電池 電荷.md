☀ <span class="category">**電池 電荷：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**battery**</span> ['bætərɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 包含一個或多個單芯電池（cell）的一種裝置，可稱為“電池”、“電池組”：</span>a rechargeable battery 可充電的電池 / The battery is flat. 電池沒電了。/ dead, flat battery 電量用完了的電池 / spare battery 備用電池 / car, laptop, phone battery 汽車蓄電池（電瓶）；膝上型電腦電池；手機電池 / battery life 電池壽命 / battery pack 電池組

<span class="vocabulary">**cell**</span> [sel] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 單芯電池：</span>a photoelectric cell 光電池

<span class="vocabulary">**charge**</span> [tʃɑːdӡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 電池（或帶電物質）的充電量、帶電量、電荷：</span>positive, negative charge 正電荷；負電荷 <span class="definition">vt. charge (sth) (up) 給…充電：</span>Before use, the battery must be charged. 電池使用前必須充電。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**charged**</span> [tʃɑ:dʒd; 美tʃɑrdʒd]
<span class="definition">adj.（粒子）帶電的：</span>negatively charged ions. 負離子 / It's wise to take a fully charged spare battery with you. 隨身帶一塊充滿電的備用電池是明智之舉。/ electrically charged particles帶電粒子

<span class="vocabulary">**flat**</span> [flæt] 
<span class="definition">adj. [英]（電池）電用完了的：</span>瞭解即可
