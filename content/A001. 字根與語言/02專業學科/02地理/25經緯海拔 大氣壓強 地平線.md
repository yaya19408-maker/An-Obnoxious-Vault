☀ <span class="category">**經緯海拔 大氣壓強：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**longitude**</span> [ˈlɒŋgɪtju:d; ˈlɒndʒɪ-; 美 ˈlɑ:ndʒətu:d]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（縮寫為long.）經度：</span>the longitude of the island 那座島的經度 / The town is at longitude 28° west. 該鎮位於西經28度。/ He noted the latitude and longitude, then made a mark on the admiralty chart. 他記下緯度和經度，然後在航海圖上做了個標記。/ He tried to find out how a ship at sea could determine its longitude. 他試圖弄清海上船隻如何才能測定出所在位置的經度。/ This line of longitude cuts through the jungle. 這條經線穿過叢林地帶。           

<span class="vocabulary">**meridian**</span> [məˈrɪdiən]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 經線、子午線：</span>The meridian is an imaginary line drawn from pole to pole. 子午線是從極點到極點的一條假想線。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**latitude**</span> [ˈlætɪtju:d; 美 -tu:d]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（縮寫為lat.）（一個地方的）緯度：</span>high, low latitude 高緯度；低緯度 / northern, southern latitude 北緯；南緯 / polar, temperate, tropical latitude 極地緯度；溫帶緯度；熱帶緯度 / These birds only survive in temperate latitudes. 這些鳥只有在溫帶才能存活。/ a line of latitude 緯線

<span class="vocabulary">**parallel**</span> ['pærəlel] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（也可用parallel of latitude）（地球或地圖上的）緯線（是一種虛擬的線）：</span>the 49th parallel 第49度緯線
           
<span class="vocabulary">**equator**</span> [ɪˈkweɪtə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.]（通常用the equator）赤道：</span>Rainforests occur around the equator. 雨林出現在赤道周圍。/ The sun heats the sea more at the equator than at the poles. 太陽使赤道附近的海水溫度高於極地附近的水溫。/ in an area near the equator 在赤道附近的一個地區 / The lake lies exactly on the equator. 這湖正好位於赤道上。/ The island is just 80 miles north of the equator. 該島在赤道以北僅80英里處。

<span class="vocabulary">**altitude**</span> ['æltɪtju:d] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] 高出海平面的高度，即海拔高度。是此義的常規用詞：</span>We are flying at an altitude of 6 000 metres. 我們的飛行高度是6000米。/ high altitude 高海拔 / The plane suddenly started to lose altitude. 飛機突然開始下降。

<span class="vocabulary">**level**</span> ['levl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（與地面相對的）高度：</span>the water level of the Mississippi River 密西西比河的水位 / the problem of rising sea levels 海平面日益升高的問題 / to raise the level of the bank 加高堤岸 / one metre below ground level 地面下一米深的地方 / 200 m above sea level 海拔200米 / The plane was flying at a very low level. 飛機正在超低空飛行。/ below the level of the clouds 在雲層以下
           
<span class="vocabulary">**contour**</span> [ˈkɒntʊə(r); 美 ˈkɑ:ntʊr]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（也可用contour line）（地圖上表示相同海拔各點的）等高線：</span>a contour map showing two hills and this large mountain in the middle 標示著兩座小山和中間這座大山的等高線地圖 / There were three moderate climbs to just below the 450 feet contour. 剛好在450英尺的等高線之下有三個緩坡。

<span class="vocabulary">**pressure**</span> ['preʃə] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 地球表面的大氣壓：</span>A band of high pressure is moving across the country. 一股高氣壓正經過該國。/ atmospheric, barometric pressure 大氣壓
            
<span class="vocabulary">**ridge**</span> [rɪdʒ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (of high pressure) [術語]（大氣層的）高壓脊，高壓帶：</span>瞭解即可          
<span class="vocabulary">**depression**</span> [dɪˈpreʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [術語]（常指下雨引起的）低氣壓、氣壓降低：</span>瞭解即可

周邊詞彙補充：
· barometer可指氣壓計；晴雨表。

☀ <span class="category">**地平線：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**horizon**</span> [həˈraɪzn]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the horizon可指地平線：</span>The sun sank below the horizon. 太陽落到了地平線下。/ A ship appeared on the horizon. 一艘船出現在地平線上。/ northern horizon 北方的地平線 / The sea stretched away to the distant/far horizon . 海平面伸到遙遠的天際。/ The captain scanned the horizon for any sign of other vessels. 船長仔細眺望海天相接處看是否有其他船隻的蹤影。/ I watched the pale sun climb over the horizon. 我注視著蒼白的太陽爬上地平線。/ Land was still out of sight beyond the horizon. 天際之外仍然看不見陸地的蹤影。/ A cloud of dust on the horizon announced the arrival of the cavalry. 天邊揚起的塵土宣告騎兵的到來。/ The moon was rising over the horizon. 月亮從地平線上冉冉升起。
