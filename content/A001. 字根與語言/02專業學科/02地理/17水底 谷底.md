☀ <span class="category">**水底 谷底：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**bed**</span> [bed] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（河、海等的）底。強調一種床狀地貌：</span>the ocean bed 海底 / oyster beds 牡蠣層（有很多牡蠣的一片區域）

<span class="vocabulary">**bottom**</span> ['bɒtəm] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.]（湖、河、游泳池、井、山谷等的）底部。在本組含義最廣。bottom僅表示水體的底部，並不強調該底部的任何特徵：</span>He dived in and hit his head on the bottom. 他跳進水裡，頭撞到了水底。/ the bottom of a swimming pool 游泳池的底 / the bottom of a well 井底 / the bottom of the sea 海底 / the bottom of the valley 谷底

<span class="vocabulary">**floor**</span> [flɔ:] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.]（海洋、山谷等的）底；（森林等的）地面。不過也需要與表示具體地貌的詞連用：</span>canyon, cave, desert, forest, ocean, sea, valley floor 峽谷底部；洞底；沙漠地面；枯枝落葉層；海底；谷底 

<span class="vocabulary">**rock**</span> [rɒk] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 站立海中的一大塊岩石聚合體，即礁石：</span>The ship crashed into a rock and broke into two pieces. 這艘船撞上了一塊礁石，斷為兩截。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**trench**</span> [trentʃ]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（也可用ocean trench）海底的長長的深海溝、大洋溝：</span>瞭解即可
