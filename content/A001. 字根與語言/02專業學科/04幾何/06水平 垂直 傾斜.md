☀ <span class="category">**垂直 傾斜：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**horizontal**</span> [ˌhɒrɪˈzɒntl; 美 ˌhɔ:rəˈzɑ:ntl; ˌhɑ:r-]
<span class="definition">adj. 水平的、與地面平行的、橫的：</span>horizontal lines 橫線 <span class="definition">n. 1 [U] the horizontal指水平位置：</span>He shifted his position from the horizontal. 他從水平姿勢變換成其他姿勢。<span class="definition">2 [C] 水平的線或表面：</span>The undulating planes of the countryside provide relief from the hard horizontals and verticals of the urban scene. 鄉村起伏不平的地勢使人們擺脫了城市刻板的橫平豎直景象。

<span class="vocabulary">**vertical**</span> ['və:tɪkl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1（線、杆等）與一個水平面垂直的，或（在一個圖片等中從頂部到底部）豎直的，相當於perpendicular：</span>the vertical axis of the graph 圖的縱軸 / The cliff was almost vertical. 那懸崖幾乎是垂直的。<span class="definition">2 擁有頂部、中間及底部結構的，即縱向的：</span>a vertical flow of communication 上下級縱向交流 <span class="definition">n. [C]（常用the vertical）垂直的線（或位置），相當於perpendicular：</span>The wall is several degrees off the vertical. 這堵牆傾斜了有好幾度。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**perpendicular**</span> [ˌpɜ:pənˈdɪkjələ(r); 美 ˌpɜ:rp-]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (to sth) [通常為術語] 與一條線或一個平面形成90°角的，即垂直的、成直角的：</span>Are the lines perpendicular to each other? 這些直線相互垂直嗎？/ The staircase was almost perpendicular (= very steep). 樓梯幾乎成垂直的了。/ The left wing dipped until it was perpendicular to the ground. 左翼下垂至與地面成90度角。/ The sides of the loch are almost perpendicular. 湖岸幾乎都是垂直的。/ We made two slits for the eyes and a perpendicular line for the nose. 我們劃了兩道縫兒做眼睛,另外劃了一條豎線做鼻子。/ two mutually perpendicular directions 兩個互為直角的方向 / The axis of the moon will now be exactly perpendicular to that of the earth. 月軸此時與地軸正好成直角。<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the perpendicular 指垂直的線、位置或方向：</span>The wall is a little out of the perpendicular. 牆壁有點傾斜。           

<span class="vocabulary">**erect**</span> [ɪˈrekt]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 垂直的；豎直的；直立的。相當於straight：</span>He was standing perfectly erect. 他站得筆直。/ Her head was erect and her back was straight. 她抬頭挺胸。/ Stand with your arms by your side and your head erect. 手放兩邊，昂首站立。/ She held her head erect as she walked proudly up to the platform. 她昂著頭自豪地走向講臺。/ Stand reasonably erect, your arms hanging naturally. 挺身站直，雙臂自然下垂。/ He sat very erect, listening intently. 他坐得筆直，專心地聽著。/ Keep your trunk erect throughout the exercise. 在整個練習過程中都要挺直身軀。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**upright**</span> [ˈʌpraɪt]
<span class="definition">adj. 1（人）直立的（可以是站直，也可以是坐直的，而非躺下或背部彎曲的）：</span>to stand bolt upright 直挺挺地站著 / rows of children sitting upright at their desks 一排排端坐在課桌旁的學生 / He was sitting bolt upright on his chair, looking very tense. 他直挺挺地坐在椅子上，顯得很緊張。/ She sat upright in bed. 她挺直地坐在床上。/ a fully upright posture 筆挺的姿勢 / She held herself rigidly upright. 她保持筆挺的姿勢。/ He managed to pull himself upright. 他設法直起了身子。/ Pulling himself upright, he squared his shoulders. 他直起身子，端平肩膀。/ Gradually raise your body into an upright position. 慢慢起身，成直立狀態。/ to shoot upright 直立射擊 / to spring upright 躍起 / Polly jerked upright, wild-eyed and blinking. 波莉猛地跳起來，眼睛睜得很大，還不停地眨著。<span class="definition">2（事物）豎直的、直立的、垂直的：</span>Keep the bottle upright. 保持瓶子直立。/ an upright bar 直立的欄杆 / The flag pole wouldn't stay upright. 旗杆總是立不直。/ an upright freezer (= one that is taller than it is wide) 立式冰櫃 / an upright vacuum cleaners 立式吸塵器 / an upright chair (= one with a high straight back) 高直背椅子 / the uprights of a four-poster bed 四柱床的立柱

相關詞義延伸：
· Perpendicular在建築學中可指垂直式的（英國14、15世紀盛行的建築風格，以使用垂直線和大拱為特徵）。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**slope**</span> [sləʊp; 美 sloʊp]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 傾斜的平面；土地的斜坡：</span>a grassy slope 長滿草的斜坡 / The town is built on a slope. 這座城建在斜坡上。/ the continental slope off the American coast 美洲海岸線上的大陸坡 / The street must have been on a slope. 這條街一定是建在一個斜坡上。/ a slope leading down to the river 通往下面河邊的一個斜坡 / The lower slopes rise quite gently. 較低處的斜坡坡度和緩。/ a west-facing slope overlooking the river 俯瞰河流的西向斜坡 / I scrambled down the icy slope. 我爬下了冰封的斜坡。<span class="definition">2 [sing., U] 某事物的傾斜度、坡度：</span>gentle, steep slope 緩坡；陡坡 / The football pitch has a slope of about one metre. 足球場有一米的坡度。/ a slope of 45 degrees 45度的坡度 / the angle of slope 傾角 / positive slope 正斜率 / The unemployment-income curve on the graph has a negative slope. 圖上失業——收入曲線斜率為負。/ the slope of a line 直線的斜率 / The slope increases as you go up the curve. 曲線上升，斜率變大。<span class="definition">vi.（水平面）傾斜、有斜坡：</span>The garden slopes away towards the river. 花園向河邊傾斜下去。/ The ground slopes away steeply at the back of the house. 房後的地面形成陡坡。/ The field slopes down to a small river. 那片地是下坡，直通小河邊。/ The bank sloped down sharply to the river. 河岸到河面的斜坡非常陡。/ sloping shoulders 斜肩 <span class="definition">vi.（垂直或豎直的某事物）向左右傾斜：</span>His handwriting slopes backwards. 他寫的字向右斜。/ It was a very old house with sloping walls. 這房子已經很舊，牆都歪了。/ The writing sloped backwards. 筆跡朝右傾斜。/ He wonders why the digits on his calculator slope to the right. 他不明白為什麼他的計算器上面的數字向右傾斜。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**skew**</span> [skju:]
<span class="definition">vi. [英] 以一定角度移動或躺下（尤指不正常的姿勢），如偏離、傾斜：</span>The ball skewed off at a right angle. 球呈直角偏離彈了出去。

<span class="vocabulary">**descent**</span> [dɪˈsent]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 向下的斜坡：</span>gentle descent, steep descent 緩坡；陡坡 / There is a gradual descent to the sea. 有一片斜坡緩緩伸延到海邊。/ On the descents, cyclists spin past cars, freewheeling downhill at tremendous speed. 在坡道上，腳踏車手飛馳著超越了一輛輛汽車，靠著慣性一路疾衝下山。          
           
<span class="vocabulary">**descend**</span> [dɪˈsend]
<span class="definition">vi. [正式]（山、道路等）向下傾斜：</span>At this point the path descends steeply to the village. 小路從這裡陡然而下通向鎮子。

<span class="vocabulary">**pitch**</span> [pɪtʃ]
<span class="definition">n. [sing., U] [術語] 屋頂的傾斜度：</span>瞭解即可             

<span class="vocabulary">**incline**</span> [ɪnˈklaɪn]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. [正式] ~ (sth) (to/towards sth)（使）傾斜：</span>The land inclined gently towards the shore. 地面緩緩向海岸傾斜。<span class="definition">n. [C] [正式] 斜坡、傾斜、斜度：</span>slight incline 緩坡 / He came to a halt at the edge of a steep incline. 他在一個陡坡邊上停住了。    
           
<span class="vocabulary">**inclined**</span> [ɪnˈklaɪnd]

<span class="vocabulary">**ramp**</span> [ræmp]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 連線一條道路、一座建築物等的兩部分（其中一個部分比另一個部分位置要高）的一個斜坡：</span>Ramps should be provided for wheelchair users. 應該給輪椅使用者提供坡道。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**gradient**</span> [ˈgreɪdiənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [美式也尤用grade] 地面（尤指公路或鐵路）的坡度、傾斜度：</span>The road has a fairly steep gradient. 這條路坡度相當大。/ The football pitch was on a slight gradient. 足球場在一個緩坡上。/ The courses are long and punishing, with steep gradients. 這些賽道既長又有陡坡，極具挑戰性。/ a hill with a gradient of 1 in 4 (or 25%) 傾斜度為1:4（或25%）的小山

<span class="vocabulary">**fall**</span> [fɔ:l] 
<span class="definition">vi. fall (away/off) 向下傾斜：</span>The land falls away sharply towards the river. 地勢向河邊陡然傾斜。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**plunge**</span> [plʌndʒ]

<span class="vocabulary">**angle**</span> ['æŋɡl]

<span class="vocabulary">**tip**</span> [tɪp]

<span class="vocabulary">**drop**</span> [drɒp]

<span class="vocabulary">**diagonal**</span> [daɪˈægənl]

<span class="vocabulary">**lean**</span> [li:n]

相關詞義延伸：
· slope還可指山坡、雪坡；
· ramp還可指（裝車或上下飛機的）活動坡道或活動梯；
· oblique還可指斜槓符號，即/，此時為英式用法；
· gradient還可指地面（尤指公路或鐵路）的坡度、傾斜度；
· lean還可指倚靠。也可表示使某事物斜靠在…上。

周邊詞彙補充：
· straight可指處於（或變成）一個水平或垂直的位置，或處於（或進入）恰當的位置。即正，直，平正地；
· across可指（縱橫字謎謎底）橫寫的；
· raise可指移動…使其呈垂直狀態，即直立、站立；使直立、使站立；
· list可指（船向一側的）傾斜；
· heel可指船傾斜，傾側；
· grade可指地面的斜坡，尤指公路或鐵路的斜坡；
· steep可指（斜坡、山等）陡的、陡峭的；
· bias可指對布匹的斜裁；
· deflection可指移動中的事物的突然轉向（尤指擊中某物後的轉向）；使某事物轉向的行為，如偏斜。
