☀ <span class="category">**方法、手段、方式、戰略、策略、風格、模式的名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**method**</span> ['meθəd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] method (of sth/of doing sth) / method (for sth/for doing sth) 做某事所使用的一種方法。是一個基礎且常規的用詞，極為常用：</span>a new method of solving the problem 一種解決問題的新方法 / research, scientific, training, working methods 研究方法；科學方法；訓練方法；工作方法 / modern farming methods 現代耕種方法 / analytical, experimental, mathematical, quantitative, statistical method 分析法；實驗方法；數學方法；定量法；統計方法

<span class="vocabulary">**approach**</span> [ə'prəʊtʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] approach (to sth) 對待或處理…的一種方式，方法；做事或思考（如處理問題或任務）的一種方式，方法。相對正式：</span>The school has decided to adopt a different approach to discipline. 學校決定採取另外一種方式解決紀律問題。/ She took the wrong approach in her dealings with them. 她用錯誤的手段和他們打交道。/ We will be exploring different approaches to gathering information. 我們將探索收集資訊的不同方法。

<span class="vocabulary">**means**</span> [mi:nz] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] means (of doing sth/of sth) 達成一個結果所採用的一種行動、一個物體或一個體系；達成某事或做某事所採用的一種方法、方式、途徑，常翻譯為“手段”：</span>means of communication, means of identification, means of transport 通訊手段；身份證件；交通工具 / the means for achieving happiness 獲得幸福的方法 / legal, illegal means 合法手段；非法手段 / Painting had become his primary means of self-expression. 繪畫已成為他自我表達的主要手段。/ Is there any means of contacting him? 有沒有什麼辦法和他取得聯絡？
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**resort**</span> [rɪˈzɔ:t; 美 rɪˈzɔ:rt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] the first/last/final ~ 可首先（或最後）採取的手段：</span>Strike action should be regarded as a last resort, when all attempts to negotiate have failed. 罷工應該是最後一著，在所有的談判努力都告失敗時才使用。/ In the last resort (= in the end) everyone must decide for themselves. 最後人人都得自己作決定。/ Nuclear weapons should be used only as a last resort. 不到萬不得已時不能選擇使用核武器。/ As a last resort you may have to accept their point of view. 最終，你可能不得不接受他們的觀點。

<span class="vocabulary">**weapon**</span> [ˈwepən] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 可被用作攻擊或鬥爭的知識、言語、行動等，可看作比喻意義上的武器：</span>Education is the only weapon to fight the spread of the disease. 教育是戰勝這一疾病蔓延的唯一手段。/ This relaxation technique can serve as an effective weapon against stress. 這種放鬆方法可作為緩解壓力的有效手段。/ She used humour and wit as weapons against her enemies. 她用幽默和智慧作為武器來回擊她的對手。/ Guilt is the secret weapon for the control of children. 愧疚是控制兒童的秘密武器。/ The workers' ultimate weapon was the strike. 工人的終極武器就是罷工。/ The team's secret weapon was their new young defender. 該隊的秘密武器就是新來的年輕防守隊員。

<span class="vocabulary">**manner**</span> ['mænə] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [正式] 某事被執行（或某事發生）的方式，方法：</span>She answered in a businesslike manner. 她回答時顯出一副公事公辦的樣子。/ You are not approaching the problem in the correct manner. 你處理這個問題的方法不對。/ Chemical waste must be disposed of in an environmentally responsible manner. 處理化學廢料切不可危害環境。/ constructive, peaceful, straightforward manner 建設性的方式；和平的方式；直截了當的方式 / The inspection was conducted in a thoroughly professional manner. 這次檢查的方式十分專業。

<span class="vocabulary">**line**</span> [laɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. <span class="vocabulary">**device**</span> [dɪˈvaɪs]
<span class="definition">n. <span class="vocabulary">**antidote**</span> [ˈæntidəʊt; 美 -doʊt]

<span class="vocabulary">**basis**</span> ['beɪsɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 組織或安排事物的某種方式，即準則，基準，方式：</span>on a regular basis 以有規律的方式 / on an equal basis 以平等的方式 / Staff are employed on a monthly basis. 員工是按月僱的。/ Each application is considered on a case-by-case basis. 每份申請都要根據其不同的情況逐一進行考慮。

<span class="vocabulary">**game**</span> [ɡeɪm] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] sb’s game 比賽或遊戲時用的方法、手法或技巧：</span>That girl plays a great game of bridge. 那個女孩打得一手好橋牌。/ He pitched a perfect game at Atlanta. 他在亞特蘭大比賽中的表現堪稱完美。/ He raised his game to become the champion. 他提升了自己的水平，成為冠軍。

<span class="vocabulary">**course**</span> [kɔ:s]
