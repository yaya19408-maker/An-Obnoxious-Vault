☀ <span class="category">**應用、使用、採用、運用的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**use**</span> [ju:z] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 use sth (for sth/for doing sth) / use sth (as sth)（為某一目的而）使用、利用、運用（一部機器、一種方法、一個物體等去做某事）：</span>to use a phone, to use a software 用電話；用軟體 / to use military force 動用軍事力量 / The building is currently being used as a warehouse. 這所房子目前用作倉庫。/ You can’t keep using your bad back as an excuse. 你不能老拿腰疼當託詞呀。<span class="definition">2（為達成某事或製作某事物而）消耗一定量的某種液體、物質等：</span>This type of heater uses a lot of electricity. 這種加熱器耗電量很大。/ I hope you haven’t used all the milk. 希望你沒有把牛奶都用掉。<span class="definition">3 [貶] 透過自己對某人展現出善良、友好等手段而意圖從此人身上獲得好處，即利用別人，相當於exploit：</span>He’s just using you for his own ends. 他只是在利用你謀求私利。/ I felt used. 我感覺被人利用了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**manipulate**</span> [məˈnɪpjuleɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. 熟練地控制或使用某事物：</span>to manipulate the gears and levers of a machine 熟練地操縱機器的排擋和變速桿 / Computers are very efficient at manipulating information. 計算機在處理資訊方面效率極高。/ to manipulate a computer 操控電腦 / to manipulate puppets 操縱木偶

<span class="vocabulary">**apply**</span> [ə'plaɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 apply sth (to sth)（在某種情況中）使用某東西或使某事物執行，如使用，採用：</span>to apply economic sanctions, to apply political pressure 實施經濟制裁；施加政治壓力 / The new technology was applied to farming. 這項新技術已應用於農業。<span class="definition">2 apply sth (to sth) 將油漆、乳劑等塗抹（或敷、施）於一個表面上：</span>Apply the cream sparingly to your face and neck. 把乳霜薄薄地抹在臉和脖子上。/ Apply the insecticide sparingly. 慎用殺蟲劑。/ Apply the glue evenly over both surfaces. 在兩面均勻地塗上膠水。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**extend**</span> [ɪkˈstend]
<span class="definition">vi. 適用於某人或某事物：</span>It might be possible to extend the technique to other crop plants. 也許可以將這種技術應用於其他農作物。

<span class="vocabulary">**obtain**</span> [əb'teɪn] 
<span class="definition">vi. [正式]（規則、制度、習俗等）存在，適用，相當於apply：</span>These conditions no longer obtain. 這些條件不再適用。

<span class="vocabulary">**adopt**</span> [ə'dɒpt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 採用（某種方法）；採取（某種態度）。是此義的常規用詞：</span>All three teams adopted different approaches to the problem. 三個隊處理這個問題的方法各不相同。/ The policy has not yet been formally adopted. 這一政策還未被正式採納。<span class="definition">2 [正式] 採用（某種舉止、說話或表達等方式）：</span>He adopted an air of indifference. 他擺出一副滿不在乎的樣子。<span class="definition">3 選用（一個新名字）等：</span>to adopt a name, to adopt a language 取名；採用某語言

<span class="vocabulary">**take**</span> [teɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 採取行動或措施（以應對某一問題或達到某一目標）。是一個簡單用詞，但很常用：</span>The government is taking action to combat drug abuse. 政府正在採取措施，打擊毒品濫用。/ We need to take a different approach to the problem. 我們應該採用另一種方法來解決這一問題。<span class="definition">2 採用某種形式：</span>Our next class will take the form of a debate. 我們下一堂課將採用辯論的形式。

<span class="vocabulary">**employ**</span> [ɪm'plɒɪ]

<span class="vocabulary">**wield**</span> [wi:ld]

<span class="vocabulary">**exert**</span> [ɪgˈzɜ:t; 美 ɪgˈzɜ:rt]

相關詞義延伸：
· use還可指說或寫（詞語或特定種類的語言），即使用（詞語或語言）；
· access還可表示使用某種資訊科技的機會或權利；
· serve還可指對某人實現或達成…有用，能滿足某人…的需要；
· wield還可指抓住某事物，準備將其作為武器或工具，如操、揮、使用；
· manipulate還可為貶義用法，表示控制某人或某事物、事件、局勢，且常指暗中操控，而受控者還意識不到自己被操控。

周邊詞彙補充：
· cover可指（規律）適用於（某些人、事或情況）；
· appropriate可指（尤指非法或未經允許地）拿來某事物（如某人的想法等）以供自己使用，具體可稱為盜用、挪用等；
· affect可指為給別人留下印象而使用或穿著某事物，即炫耀性地使用或穿戴；
· wear可指被長時間使用之後依然保持良好狀態，如耐用、耐久、耐穿、耐磨等。

[U] ~ to sth（在別無選擇的情況下）對…的採用、訴諸（尤指採用或訴諸壞的手段）：</span>There are hopes that the conflict can be resolved without resort to violence. 衝突有望不需要訴諸武力而得到解決。/ Congress has a responsibility to ensure that all peaceful options are exhausted before resort to war. 國會有責任確保只有在所有和平手段都不奏效的情況下才會訴諸戰爭。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**recourse**</span> [rɪˈkɔ:s; 美 ˈri:kɔ:rs]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式]（在困境中）對…的使用、訴諸、依賴、求助：</span>Your only recourse is legal action. 你的唯一依靠就是訴諸法律。/ It enabled its members to settle their differences without recourse to war. 這樣，其成員就可以不必訴諸戰爭來解決分歧。/ The public believes its only recourse is to take to the streets. 公眾認為唯一的辦法就是上街遊行。/ She made a complete recovery without recourse to surgery. 她未做手術就完全恢復了健康。/ The government, when necessary, has recourse to the armed forces. 政府在必要時可以動用軍隊。/ Drivers have little recourse but to wait until the weather clears. 司機們除了等待天氣放晴外別無他法。/ I have no other recourse than to inform the police. 除了告知警察外我別無選擇。/ The study of these creatures has been conducted without direct recourse to living specimens. 對這些生物的研究是在未直接利用活體標本的情況下進行的。/ legal recourse 法律上的追索權 / The mother of an illegitimate child had no legal recourse to the father. 在法律上，私生子的母親對孩子的父親無求償權。/ workers who have no recourse to trade unions 得不到工會幫助的工人 / An order was made against which he sought recourse in the supreme court. 就法院決議他向最高法院提出申訴。/ Their system of dispute resolution avoids recourse to the courts. 他們處理糾紛的體制使之不必訴諸法庭。/ people who deal with emotional pain by recourse to drugs and alcohol 靠毒品和酒精緩解感情痛苦的人 / a charity for women with no recourse to public funds 為不能得到公共基金資助的婦女建立的慈善機構 / residents with no recourse to the law 不訴諸法律的居民 / They tried to settle the dispute without recourse to the courts. 他們試圖不透過法庭解決這一爭端。/ Citizens have learned that they do have recourse against governments. 公民已認識到他們有向政府求償的權利。/ She often had recourse to her dictionary. 她經常求助於詞典。/ There is no recourse available to the victim. 受害人無處可獲得幫助。

<span class="vocabulary">**artifice**</span> [ˈɑ:tɪfɪs; 美 ˈɑ:rt-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] [正式] 對詭計和策略的靈活運用：</span>His photographs are full of artfulness, and artifice. 他的攝影作品運用了豐富的藝術手段和技巧。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**assertion**</span> [əˈsɜ:ʃn; 美 əˈsɜ:rʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 使用：</span>The demonstration was an assertion of the right to peaceful protest. 這次示威遊行是使用和平抗議權。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**exploitation**</span> [ˌeksplɔɪˈteɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 對土地、石油、礦藏等的開發、開採、利用：</span>commercial exploitation of the mineral resources in Antarctica 南極洲礦物資源的商業開採 / the direct exploitation of natural forests 對天然林的直接開發 / sustainable exploitation 可持續開發 / mineral, resource exploitation 礦藏開採；資源開發 / Turtles are increasingly threatened by human exploitation. 海龜正日益受到人類開發的威脅。/ Emergent democracies created markets that were ripe for exploitation. 新興民主國家創造出了適宜開發的市場。<span class="definition">3 [U] [貶]（出於私利的）對局勢的利用：</span>exploitation of the situation for his own purposes 利用這種局勢達到他自己的目的 / her ruthless exploitation of popular fear 她無情地利用大眾的恐懼心理

<span class="vocabulary">**deployment**</span> [dɪ'plɔɪmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 資源的排程或利用：</span>Without the full deployment of resources, we cannot achieve our aims. 沒有對資源的充分利用，我們不可能實現目標。                      

<span class="vocabulary">**employment**</span> [ɪmˈplɔɪmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式] ~ (of sth) 使用；利用：</span>the employment of artillery in the capture of the town 在攻城時使用大炮

<span class="vocabulary">**consumption**</span> [kənˈsʌmpʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 做某事或說某話供某個人（或群體）享用（即使做事者或說話者的內心真實想法非常不同）：</span>Her speech to party members was not intended for public consumption (= to be heard by the public). 她對黨員發表的講話並不打算公諸大眾。/ The documents were not for public consumption. 這些檔案不對公眾公開。/ The hardline speech appears to be mostly for domestic consumption. 強硬路線的講話似乎主要是說給國內人聽的。/ The report was obviously designed for the consumption of members of the War Committee. 該報告顯然是為戰爭委員會的委員們撰寫的。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**utility**</span> [ju:ˈtɪləti]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式] 實用；有用：</span>Belief in the utility of higher education is shared by students nationwide. 全國的學生都相信高等教育是有用的。/ He inwardly questioned the utility of his work. 他內心質疑自己的工作是否有用。/ potential utility 潛在用途 / practical utility 實際用途 / clinical, military, social utility 臨床應用；軍事用途∶社會用途 / He argued that the arts have great social utility. 他認為藝術有很大的社會功用。/ A number of researchers have demonstrated the utility of this approach. 許多研究人員演示了這種方法的用途。/ This computer is of low utility for the home user. 這臺計算機對家庭使用者用處不大。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**usefulness**</span> [ˈju:sfəlnəs]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 有用；實用；可用性：</span>There are doubts about the usefulness of these tests. 這些試驗是否有用，人們有所保留。/ The building has outlived its usefulness. 這座樓房已超過使用年限了。/ Badly written questions limit the usefulness of questionnaires. 問題設計得不好，調查問卷的作用就會打折扣。/ Many people claim that the word has lost its usefulness. 許多人聲稱這個詞已經用不上了。/ The advisory group has outlived its usefulness. 顧問小組已失去了它的作用。/ The theory is of limited usefulness. 這個理論用途有限。/ the exam's usefulness for ranking students 考試在為學生排名次方面的作用 / the drug's usefulness in the treatment of cancer 這種藥在癌症治療中的功效 / The report is of potential usefulness to the government. 這個報告可能對政府有用。

<span class="vocabulary">**help**</span> [help] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 有用：</span>The map wasn’t much help. 這張地圖沒多大用處。/ With the help of a ladder, neighbours were able to rescue the children from the blaze. 鄰居們藉助一把梯子把孩子們從大火中救了出來。/ Just shouting at him isn’t going to be a lot of help. 光是對他大喊大叫不會有多大用處。/ The manual is too technical to be of help to the user. 這本手冊太專業了，對使用者沒什麼用處。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**access**</span> ['ækses] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] access (to sth) 使用某事物的機會或權利：</span>Students must have access to good resources. 學生們一定有機會使用好的資源。/ Students have easy access to libraries. 學生享用圖書館的資源很方便。/ Access to this information is severely restricted. 這一資訊的使用受到極為嚴格的限制。/ Men and women should have equal access to education. 男女應當享有平等的受教育權。

<span class="vocabulary">**fodder**</span> [ˈfɒdə(r); 美 ˈfɑ:d-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [貶]（常置於名詞後）（人或東西）被認為只能有一種用途，即只能是…的料：</span>Without education, these children will end up as factory fodder (= only able to work in a factory). 不受教育，這些孩子將來只能到工廠幹活。/ This story will be more fodder for the gossip columnists. 這個傳聞會是閒談專欄作家的又一素材。/ Old movies were the cheapest broadcast fodder. 老電影是最廉價的播放素材。/ The press conference simply provided more fodder for another attack on his character. 記者招待會只不過是提供了更多的再次擊他人格的口實。

<span class="vocabulary">**user**</span> ['ju:zə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 使用某事物的一個人或一個事物，即使用者、使用者：</span>computer, software, Internet user 計算機使用者；軟體使用者；網際網路使用者 / business, corporate, home user 商業使用者；企業使用者；家庭使用者 / car, rail, road user 坐汽車的人；火車乘客；公路使用者 / electricity, energy user 電力使用者；能源使用者 / active, potential, current, registered user 活躍使用者；潛在使用者；現有使用者；註冊使用者
           
<span class="vocabulary">**occupant**</span> [ˈɒkjəpənt; 美 ˈɑ:k-]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（車輛、座位、床位等的）乘坐者、佔用者：</span>vehicle occupant 車輛乘坐者 / The car was badly damaged but the occupants were unhurt. 汽車嚴重損壞，但車內人員安然無恙。/ He wanted the occupants of the vehicle to get out. 他想讓車上的乘客下車。/ The sole occupant of the car was an elderly man. 這輛車裡只坐了一位老人。/ The lifeboat capsized, throwing the occupants into the water. 救生艇傾覆，艇裡的人被掀入水中。
 
相關詞義延伸：
· use也可指運用自己的頭腦或身體的能力，可稱為功能；
· application還可指計算機技術中執行某一任務的一個程式或軟體，可稱為應用程式、應用軟體（縮寫為app）。也可指一條規則（或法律等）的啟用或生效；
· utility還可指計算機的實用程式；
· occupant還可指（房屋、建築等的）使用者，居住者（可以是暫時居住者，也可以是長期居住者）。

周邊詞彙補充：
· recycle還可指對事物的回收利用、再利用；
· service可指從交通工具或機器上所得到的用途，還可指其使用狀態；
· wear也可指某事物所經歷的長期的使用量或使用形式，如耐用性、經久性等；
· virtue可指一種吸引人的或有用的品質，如優點、長處、用處；
· subscriber可指服務的消費者、使用者。
