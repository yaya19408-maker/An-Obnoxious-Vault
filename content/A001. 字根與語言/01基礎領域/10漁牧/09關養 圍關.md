☀ <span class="category">**關養 圍關：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**cage**</span> [keɪdӡ] 
<span class="definition">vt. cage sth (up) 常用被動式，把動物關在籠子裡：</span>The dogs are caged (up) at night. 晚上狗被關進籠裡。

<span class="vocabulary">**pen**</span> [pen] 
<span class="definition">vt. pen sb/sth (in/up) 把動物或人關在一個狹小的地方：</span>The animals were penned up in cages. 這些動物被關在籠子裡了。

<span class="vocabulary">**stable**</span> ['steɪbl] 
<span class="definition">vt. 把馬置於馬廄（如拴在馬廄或飼養在馬廄）：</span>Where do you stable your pony? 你的矮馬養在哪兒？

周邊詞彙補充：
· fence可表示（用柵欄、籬笆或圍欄）圍住或隔開。
