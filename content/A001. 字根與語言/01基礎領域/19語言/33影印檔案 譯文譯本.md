<span class="vocabulary">**copy**</span> ['kɒpɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 檔案的影印件、副本、影印本：</span>I will send you a copy of the report. 我會把這個報告的影印本寄給你。/ Could I have ten copies of this page, please? 請把這一頁給我影印十份好嗎？/ copy machine（美）影印機 / copy shop（美）影印店 / copy paper 影印紙
           
<span class="vocabulary">**photocopy**</span> [ˈfəʊtəʊkɒpi; 美 ˈfoʊtoʊkɑ:pi]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（也可用copy（檔案等的）影印本、影印件：</span>Could you make a photocopy of this letter for me, please? 你幫我影印一下這封信好嗎？/ Make as many photocopies as you need. 你需要多少影印件就影印多少吧。
