☀ <span class="category">**習語 成語：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**idiom**</span> ['ɪdɪəm] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 習語；成語；慣用語：</span>‘Let the cat out of the bag’ is an idiom meaning to tell a secret by mistake.“讓貓從袋子裡跑出來”是慣用語，意思是無意中洩露秘密。/ familiar idioms and metaphors, such as “turning over a new leaf” 如“翻開新的一頁”這樣熟悉的習語和比喻

<span class="vocabulary">**phrase**</span> [freɪz] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 從各個單詞的意思很難看出整句意思的一句話，即成語；習語；慣用法；警句等。此時與idiom幾無差別。不過phrase的總體含義要比idiom廣泛：</span>He used a phrase I hate: “You have to be cruel to be kind.” 他用了一句我討厭的習語：“要想善良，就得殘忍。”
           
<span class="vocabulary">**formula**</span> [ˈfɔ:mjələ; 美 ˈfɔ:rm-]（複數形式為formulas，科技領域常用formulae）
<span class="definition">n. [C] 特定場合的慣用詞語，套話：</span>legal formulae 法律慣用詞語 / The minister keeps coming out with the same tired formulas. 這個牧師開口便是千篇一律、陳詞濫調的套話。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**cliché**</span> [ˈkli:ʃeɪ; 美 kli:ˈʃeɪ]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] [貶] 陳詞濫調：</span>old, tired, usual, well-worn, worn-out cliché 老掉牙的套語；陳詞濫調；常用的套語；陳舊的套語 / She trotted out the old cliché that ‘a trouble shared is a trouble halved.’ 她又重複了“與人說愁愁減半”的陳詞濫調。/ a popular cliché about Californians 用來形容加利福尼亞人的流行的陳詞濫調 / media, sports cliché 媒體套語；體育套語 / film（尤英）, movie（尤美）cliché 電影套話 / Try to avoid using clichés in your writing. 寫作時儘量避免用陳詞濫調。<span class="definition">2 [U] 陳詞濫調的使用：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**commonplace**</span> [ˈkɒmənpleɪs; 美 ˈkɑ:m-]
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] [正式] 平淡無奇的言語等；老生常談、陳詞濫調：</span>It is a commonplace to say that Northern Ireland is a backwater in the modern Europe. 說北愛爾蘭是現代歐洲死氣沉沉的落後地區已是陳詞濫調了。

· old chestnut或hoary chestnut可指陳腐的笑話；老掉牙的故事。
