☀ <span class="category">**序言 目錄：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**introduction**</span> [͵ɪntrə'dʌkʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（書籍或演講的）序言；引言；導論（該部分大致闡述了後續的正文內容）：</span>brief, short introduction 簡短的序言 / She wrote the introduction to his new book. 她為他的新書寫了序。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**preface**</span> [ˈprefəs]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 書的前言、序言：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (with sth) 為一本書或一片文章寫序言：</span>He prefaced the diaries with a short account of how they were discovered. 他在前言中簡要敘述了日記發現的經過。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**foreword**</span> [ˈfɔ:wɜ:d; 美 ˈfɔ:rwɜ:rd]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 書的前言或序言：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**preamble**</span> [priˈæmbl; ˈpri:æmbl]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] [正式]（書籍或檔案的）序言、緒論、導言；開場白：</span>The aims of the treaty are stated in its preamble. 條約的宗旨已在序言中說明。/ The controversy has arisen over the text of the preamble to the unification treaty. 針對統一條約的序文出現了爭論。/ 'I would like you to return to the villa as soon as possible,' she said without preamble. 她開門見山地說：“我想讓你盡快回到別墅。” / She gave him the bad news without preamble. 她開門見山地把壞訊息告訴了他。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**prologue**</span> [ˈprəʊlɒg; 美 ˈproʊlɔ:g; -lɑ:g]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（書籍的）序言：</span>The prologue to the novel is written in the form of a newspaper account. 這本小說的序言是以報紙報道的形式寫的。

<span class="vocabulary">**content**</span> [ˈkɒntent] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] contents可指書的目錄或目次（常在一本書的起始處以清單的形式列出該書的各個章節或部分）：</span>a table of contents 目錄 / a contents page 目錄頁

相關詞義延伸：
· preface還可指（在講話、回答問題等之前）說開場白。
