☀ <span class="category">**語法 結構：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**grammar**</span> ['ɡræmə] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 語法規則，也可叫文法：</span>English grammar rules 英語語法規則 / correct, good grammar 正確的語法；規範的語法 / bad, incorrect, poor grammar 不規範的語法；不正確的語法；糟糕的語法 <span class="definition">2 [C] 語法書：</span>a French grammar 法語語法書

<span class="vocabulary">**construction**</span> [kən'strʌkʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 用字詞來構造句子、短語等的方式，即語法結構：</span>grammatical, linguistic, sentence construction 語法結構；語言結構；句子結構 / complex verbal constructions 複雜的動詞結構 / active, passive construction 主動結構；被動結構
           
<span class="vocabulary">**structure**</span> [ˈstrʌktʃə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 語言學中的結構：</span>grammatical, language, linguistic, sentence, syntactic structure 語法結構；語言結構；句子結構；句法結構
           
<span class="vocabulary">**grammatical**</span> [grəˈmætɪkl]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 語法的：</span>a grammatical error 語法錯誤 / Should the teacher present grammatical rules to students? 老師是否應該給學生講授語法規則？<span class="definition">2 符合語法規則的；合乎文法的：</span>That sentence is not grammatical. 那個句子不合語法。/ a new test to determine whether students can write grammatical English 考查學生是否能用符合語法規則的英語進行寫作的新型考試
           
<span class="vocabulary">**infinitive**</span> [ɪnˈfɪnətɪv]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（語法）動詞的不定時（可加to，有時可不加to）：</span>bare infinitive 不帶to的不定式 / Modal verbs generally take the bare infinitive. 情態動詞通常用不帶to的不定式。/ perfect infinitive 不定式完成式 / You use 'have' to form the perfect infinitive of a verb. 用have—詞構成動詞不定式的完成式。/ passive infinitive 不定式的被動語態 / split infinitive 分裂不定式 / The use of the split infinitive is now generally acceptable. 分裂不定式的用法現在已被廣泛接受。
