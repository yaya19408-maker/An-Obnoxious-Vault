☀ <span class="category">**侵略 入侵：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**aggression**</span> [əˈgreʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（一國對另一國的）侵略：</span>a display of naked aggression 赤裸裸的侵略表現 / The president announced that the country would not tolerate foreign aggression. 總統宣佈該國絕不會容忍外敵入侵。/ American aggression 美國的侵犯 / armed, military aggression 武裝侵略；軍事侵略 / unprovoked military aggression 無端的軍事侵犯 / the threat of massive military aggression 大規模軍事侵略的威脅 / Aggression by one nationality against another often leads to war. 一個民族對另一民族的挑釁往往會導致戰爭。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**invade**</span> [ɪnˈveɪd]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 用軍事力量入侵一個國家、城鎮等，即侵略、侵犯：</span>to invade territory 入侵領土 / When did the Romans invade Britain? 古羅馬人是何時侵略英國的？/ Troops invaded on August 9th that year. 軍隊是在那年的8月9日入侵的。/ The Romans and the Normans came to Britain as invading armies. 羅馬人以及諾曼人透過軍事入侵來到英國。/ In autumn 1944 the allies invaded the Italian mainland at Anzio and Salerno. 1944 年秋，盟軍從安齊奧與薩勒諾攻入義大利本土。
         
<span class="vocabulary">**invasion**</span> [ɪnˈveɪʒn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 對別國的武裝入侵：</span>a full-scale military invasion 全面的軍事入侵 / amphibious, ground, land invasion 水陸兩棲入侵；地面入侵 / planned invasion 有預謀的入侵 / Forces were massing on the border for an imminent invasion. 軍隊正在邊境地區大量集結，準備應對步步逼近的入侵。/ unilateral invasion 單邊的入侵 / We cannot accept the unprovoked invasion of a sovereign nation. 我們無法容忍對一個主權國家的無端入侵。/ the German invasion of Poland in 1939 德國於1939年對波蘭的侵略 / the threat of invasion 入侵的威脅 / invasion force, invasion fleet 侵略軍；侵略艦隊 / seven years after the Roman invasion of Britain 羅馬人入侵英國七年以後 / He was commander in chief during the invasion of Panama. 在侵略巴拿馬的戰役中他是總司令。
