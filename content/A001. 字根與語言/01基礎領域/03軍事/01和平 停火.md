☀ <span class="category">**和平 停火：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**peace**</span> [pi:s] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.]（一國或一地區的）和平，是沒有戰爭或暴力的一種狀態或一段時期：</span>war and peace 戰爭與和平 / to keep the peace in the region 維護該地區的和平 / world peace 世界和平 / peace negotiations, peace talks, peace process 和談；和平程序 / peace agreement, peace treaty 和平協議；和平條約 / peace movement 爭取和平的運動 / England finally made peace with France. 英國最終與法國講和了。

<span class="vocabulary">**peaceful**</span> ['pi:sfl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 沒有戰爭、暴力或爭論的，即和平的：</span>peaceful protest, peaceful demonstration, peaceful solution 和平抗議；和平示威；和平解決辦法 / We hope for a peaceful settlement of the dispute. 我們希望和平解決爭端。<span class="definition">2 尋求和平的，不喜歡暴力或爭端的，與peaceable同義：</span>The aims of the organization are peaceful. 該組織的宗旨是追求和平。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**ceasefire**</span> [ˈsi:sfaɪə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（武裝衝突中的）停火；停火協議：</span>unilateral ceasefire 單方面停火 / unconditional ceasefire 無條件停火 / temporary ceasefire 暫時的停火 / The UN has passed a resolution calling for an immediate ceasefire. 聯合國透過一項決議，呼籲立即停火。/ A 24-hour ceasefire was called to allow the distribution of aid. 宣佈停火24小時以便分發救援物資。/ There are concerns that the ceasefire might not hold.有人擔心停火協議可能不會持續。/ ceasefire agreement, ceasefire deal, ceasefire terms 停火協議；停火協定；停火條款 / a ceasefire between the government and the rebels 政府和叛亂分子之間的停火 / They agreed to a ceasefire with the Egyptian government. 他們同意與埃及政府停火。/ a call for an immediate ceasefire 要求立即停火的呼籲 / Observers have reported serious violations of the ceasefire. 觀察員報告說停火協議遭到嚴重破壞。/ They have agreed to a ceasefire after three years of conflict. 經過三年的衝突，他們已經同意停火。/ UN officials are expressing cautious optimism that the latest ceasefire is holding. 聯合國官員對於最近的停火協定是否有效持謹慎的樂觀態度。

<span class="vocabulary">**armistice**</span> [ˈɑ:mɪstɪs; 美 ˈɑ:rm-]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 休戰協議、停戰協定：</span>Finally, the Bolsheviks signed an armistice with Germany. 最終布林什維克和德國簽署了停戰協議。

<span class="vocabulary">**truce**</span> [tru:s]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（兩個人、群體或國家之間的）（短時的）休戰協議或休戰期：</span>brief truce 短暫的停戰 / A temporary truce had been reached earlier that year. 那年早些時候達成了暫時停火協定。/ permanent truce 永久停戰 / three-day, week-long truce 為期三天的停戰；為期一週的停戰 / fragile, uneasy truce 不牢靠的停戰；不安定的停戰 / The fighting of recent days has given way to an uneasy truce between the two sides. 最近幾天的交戰結束了，雙方達成了暫時的休戰協議。/ the fragile truce with France 與法國不牢固的停戰協定 / a flag of truce 休戰旗 / The guerrillas have called a one-month truce. 游擊隊號召停戰一個月。/ The priest helped to negotiate a truce between the warring sides. 那個牧師推動交戰雙方透過談判達成停戰協定。/ The two-day truce collapsed in intense shellfire. 兩天的停戰協定在猛烈的炮火聲中化為泡影。/ They renewed the war as soon as the truce expired. 停戰期一滿他們就重開戰事。
