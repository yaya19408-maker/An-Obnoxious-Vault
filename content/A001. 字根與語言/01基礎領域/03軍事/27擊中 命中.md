☀ <span class="category">**擊中 命中：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**hit**</span> [hɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 常用被動式，指子彈、炸彈、導彈（或子彈的射擊者、炸彈的投擲者）等命中，為此義的基礎通用詞：</span>The terrorist was hit by the police. 那名恐怖分子被警方擊中。/ The city was hit by bombs. 這座城市曾遭到轟炸。<span class="definition">n. [C]（被扔出或發射出的物體）擊中或命中：</span>The building took a direct hit by a bomber. 那棟建築物被轟炸機直接命中。

<span class="vocabulary">**down**</span> [daʊn] 
<span class="definition">vt. [非正式] 擊落飛機等：</span>to down a plane 擊落一架飛機

<span class="vocabulary">**sink**</span> [sɪŋk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 擊沉艦船等：</span>a battleship sunk by a torpedo 被魚雷擊沉的戰列艦 / to sink the enemy’s ship 擊沉敵艦
           
<span class="vocabulary">**torpedo**</span> [tɔ:ˈpi:dəʊ; 美 tɔ:rˈpi:doʊ]
<span class="definition">vt. 用魚雷襲擊（或擊沉）船隻：</span>More than a thousand people died when the Lusitania was torpedoed. 盧西塔尼亞號被魚雷擊沉，導致 1,000 多人死亡。
