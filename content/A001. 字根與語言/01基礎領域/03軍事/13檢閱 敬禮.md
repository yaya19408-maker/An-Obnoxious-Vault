<span class="vocabulary">**parade**</span> [pəˈreɪd]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（對士兵進行的）檢閱、閱兵：</span>a military parade 軍事檢閱 / They stood as straight as soldiers on parade. 他們像接受檢閱計程車兵一樣站得筆直。/ A military parade marched slowly and solemnly down Pennsylvania Avenue. 閱兵佇列沿賓夕法尼亞大道緩慢而莊嚴地行進。/ He had them on parade at six o'clock in the morning. 他讓他們早晨6點列隊接受檢閱。/ Morning parade was in progress on the parade ground. 閱兵場正在進行早間檢閱。/ The soldiers will be on parade tomorrow. 士兵們將於明日接受檢閱。/ parade ground 閱兵場 / The battalion went on parade to welcome the new commander-in-chief. 該營列隊集合歡迎新的總司令。/ The latest software will be on parade at the exhibition.（比喻用法）最新電腦軟體將在展覽會上展出。<span class="definition">vt.&vi.（使）士兵列隊行進以接受檢閱：</span>The crowds applauded as the guards paraded past. 衛佇列隊走過時，人群鼓掌歡迎。/ The colonel paraded his men before the Queen. 上校指揮士兵列隊行進，接受女王的檢閱。
