☀ <span class="category">**氣象 天氣：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**climate**</span> ['klaɪmɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] 一地的氣候：</span>hot climate 炎熱的氣候 / mild climate 溫和的氣候 / the severe northern climate 惡劣的北方氣候 / damp climate 潮溼的氣候 / dry climate 乾燥的氣候 / desert climate 沙漠氣候 / climate change 氣候變化 / climate conditions, climate patterns 氣候條件；氣候模式 <span class="definition">2 [C] 具有某一氣候的一個地區，即氣候區：</span>a warmer climate 氣候較溫暖的一個地區

<span class="vocabulary">**weather**</span> ['weðə] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 天氣：</span>bad weather 惡劣的天氣 / cold weather 寒冷的天氣 / bright, clear weather 晴朗的天氣 / dry weather 乾燥的天氣 / extreme weather 極端天氣 / Next day the weather turned cold. 第二天天氣就變冷了。/ weather conditions 天氣狀況 / Atlantic weather systems 大西洋氣候系統 / weather forecast, weather report 天氣預報；氣象報告 / weather information 氣象資訊 / weather satellite, weather station 氣象衛星；氣象站 / weather chart, weather map 氣象圖 / a change in the weather 天氣的變化 <span class="definition">2 [C] [非正式] the weather 可指氣象預報：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 因日曬、風吹、雨淋等天氣原因而褪色或變形等：</span>The wooden post weathers to a grey colour. 這個木杆褪成了灰色。/ The stone has weathered badly. 這塊石頭已經嚴重風化了。/ to naturally weather 自然地風化
           
<span class="vocabulary">**element**</span> [ˈelɪmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] the elements 指天氣，尤指惡劣天氣：</span>I put on my thick coat ready to brave the elements. 我穿上厚外衣準備頂風冒雨出門。/ He told us stories of how he had battled the elements on his mountaineering trips. 他給我們講述他登山時是怎樣頂住壞天氣的。/ The place was completely exposed to the elements. 這個地方在惡劣天氣下完全沒有遮擋。/ The area where most refugees are waiting is exposed to the elements. 大多數難民等待救濟的地方都不能擋風遮雨。/ protection/shelter from the elements 免受惡劣天氣侵襲

<span class="vocabulary">**front**</span> [frʌnt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 氣象領域中，可指冷暖氣團相互接觸的部位，即鋒：</span>a cold front 冷鋒 / a warm front 暖鋒

<span class="vocabulary">**high**</span> [haɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 氣象學中可指高氣壓區；反氣旋：</span>a high over eastern Asia 亞洲東部上空的高氣壓

<span class="vocabulary">**low**</span> [ləʊ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 氣象學中可指大氣中的低氣壓區：</span>Another low is moving in from the Atlantic. 另一個低氣壓區正從大西洋逼近。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**trough**</span> [trɒf; 美 trɔ:f]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [術語] 氣象學中的低壓槽；槽形低壓：</span>The trough of low pressure extended over about 1000 kilometres. 低壓槽延伸約 1,000 公里。

<span class="vocabulary">**sky**</span> [skaɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 天空。是此義的常規用詞，最常用：</span>clear blue sky 清澈的藍天 / vast sky 廣闊的天空 / night sky 夜空 / winter sky 冬季的天空 / northern sky 北方的天空 / to fly in the sky 在天空中飛翔

<span class="vocabulary">**heaven**</span> ['hevn] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] 文學用法中可指天空：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**air**</span> [eə] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 地面以上或事物周圍的空間，即空中、天空：</span>to fire the gun in the air 朝天鳴槍 / He punched the air in triumph. 他勝利地揮了揮拳頭。/ Suddenly a scream pierced the air. 突然，一聲尖叫直刺上空。/ I kicked the ball high into the air. 我把球踢到空中。/ in the open air(= outside) 露天
           
<span class="vocabulary">**aerial**</span> [ˈeəriəl; 美 ˈer-]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 從飛機上的：</span>aerial attacks, aerial bombardment, aerial photography 空中攻擊；空中轟炸；空中攝影 / an aerial view of Palm Island 棕櫚島鳥瞰圖 / Weeks of aerial bombardment had destroyed factories and highways. 數週的空中轟炸已經摧毀了許多工廠和公路。/ Patterns that are invisible on the ground can be the most striking part of an aerial photograph. 地面上看不出來的圖案可能會成為航拍照片中最引人注目的東西。<span class="definition">2 在空中的；存在於地表以上的：</span>The seagulls swirled in aerial combat over the barges. 海鷗盤旋在駁船上方，展開空中的搏鬥。/ The banyan tree has aerial roots. 榕樹有氣根。

相關詞義延伸：
· air還可表示飛機飛行的空中、天空。
