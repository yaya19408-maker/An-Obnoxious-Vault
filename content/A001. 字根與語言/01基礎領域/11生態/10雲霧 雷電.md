☀ <span class="category">**雲霧 雷電：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**cloud**</span> [klaʊd] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] 雲，雲朵，雲彩：</span>a black mass of cloud 一團烏雲 / broken, scattered cloud 碎雲；散雲 / cloud bank, cloud cover, cloud layer 雲堆；雲層 / cloud formation 雲的形成 / to fly above the clouds 在雲層上方飛行 <span class="definition">2 [C] 空中的雲狀物，例如煙、灰塵、一群飛行的昆蟲等：</span>to raise a cloud of dust 揚起一團塵土 / a great cloud of black smoke 一大片黑煙 / the mushroom cloud from a nuclear bomb 原子彈爆炸形成的蘑菇雲 / gas cloud 毒氣雲 / clouds of sand 一團團沙雲 / a thick cloud of steam 水蒸氣雲團 <span class="definition">vi. cloud (over) 指天空佈滿雲：</span>It began to cloud over. 天空開始佈滿雲。

<span class="vocabulary">**fog**</span> [fɒɡ] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] 霧。是此義的常規用詞：</span>dense, heavy, thick fog 濃霧；大霧 / patchy fog 零星薄霧 / swirling fog 嫋嫋升起的霧 / morning fog 晨霧 / a freezing fog 一片寒霧/ fog drifting over the water 水面漂浮的霧 / A dense fog came down in the morning. 早上起了濃霧。/ The fog had lifted. 霧已散去。/ A bank of fog obscured the farmhouse. 一大片霧遮住了農莊。/ fog light（汽車上的）霧燈 <span class="definition">2 [sing.] 一團令人不快的霧狀事物，如建築物或室內的煙：</span>a fog of stale cigarette smoke 一團難聞的香菸煙霧 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi. fog (sth) (up) 指玻璃表面霧氣籠罩，或使霧氣籠罩（從而難以透過視線）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**foggy**</span> ['fɒɡɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. 因有霧而不清晰的，即霧茫茫的：</span>It was quite foggy that day. 那天霧很大。

<span class="vocabulary">**mist**</span> [mɪst] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 地面上的薄霧、水汽：</span>dense, heavy, thick mist 濃霧；大霧 / fine, light, slight, thin mist 細霧；輕霧；薄霧 / swirling mist 盤旋瀰漫的霧 / hazy mist 朦朧的薄霧 / red, white mist 紅霧；白霧 / morning mist 早晨的薄霧 / autumn mist（英語）秋天的薄霧 / sea mist 海上的霧氣 / the mist over the lake 湖上的霧氣 / to vanish in the mist 消失在霧中 / The fact is lost in the mists of time.（比喻用法）事實由於年代久遠而變得模糊不清。<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. mist (sth) (up) / mist (over) 某事物（如玻璃）結滿霧氣（而模糊不清），或使其結滿霧氣：</span>The glass was misted up with condensation. 玻璃上凝滿了水珠，一片模糊。/ My glasses misted up. 我的眼鏡蒙上了一層霧氣。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**haze**</span> [heɪz]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] 含有水（或塵埃）顆粒的薄霧，常在熱天形成，可阻礙人的視線：</span>A heat haze shimmered above the fields. 田野上方一層熱氣氤氳。/ They vanished into the haze near the horizon. 他們消失在天邊的霧霾中。/ The sun smouldered through a thin summer haze. 太陽透過夏日的薄霧散發著蒼白無力的光。/ The sun now had a faint golden haze around it. 此時太陽四周環繞著一層金色薄霧。/ In the evenings a blue haze hung in the valleys. 傍晚時分，山谷中懸浮著藍色的霧靄。<span class="definition">2 [sing.]（煙、塵、蒸汽等的）霧狀物：</span>a haze of dust, a haze of steam 塵霧；蒸汽霧 / Dan smiled at him through a haze of smoke and steaming coffee. 丹透過煙霧和咖啡的熱氣朝他笑了笑。/ The locomotive was surrounded by a haze of smoke. 火車頭籠罩在一片煙霧中。/ A thick haze of acrid smoke hung in the air. 空氣中瀰漫著刺鼻的濃煙。/ Meetings are always conducted in a haze of cigarette smoke. 會議總是在吞雲吐霧中進行。/ He watched the world through a haze of tobacco smoke. 他透過繚繞的煙霧觀察著世人。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**hazy**</span> [ˈheɪzi]
<span class="definition">adj. 薄霧濛濛的：</span>hazy afternoon, hazy sky 霧濛濛的下午；霧濛濛的天空 / hazy light, hazy sunshine 朦朧的光線；朦朧的陽光 / The mountains were hazy in the distance. 遠處的山巒在薄霧中若隱若現。/ The air was thin and crisp, filled with hazy sunshine and frost. 空氣稀薄清新，其間日光矇矓，寒氣逼人。/ The floodlights were hazy behind the slanting rain. 泛光燈在斜斜的雨絲中顯得很矇矓。/ The distant mountains looked hazy and mysterious. 遠山看上去煙霧迷濛，神秘莫測。/ Generally, it will be rather hazy today, with some hill fog. 總體說來，因為有山霧，今天的天氣會比較陰沉。/The summers were hazy with pollution. 因為汙染，夏天老是霧濛濛的。

<span class="vocabulary">**lightning**</span> ['laɪtnɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 閃電：</span>A flash of lightning lit up the night air. 一道閃電照亮了夜空。/ sheet lightning 片狀閃電 / forked lightning 叉狀閃電 / He was struck by lightning. 他被閃電擊中了。/ Lightning hit the tree. 閃電擊中了那棵樹。/ lightning conductor（英）, lightning rod（美）避雷針 / lightning strike（美）雷擊 / thunder and lightning 雷電交加

<span class="vocabulary">**thunder**</span> ['θʌndə] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 雷，雷聲：</span>a clap of thunder 一聲霹靂 / a roll of thunder 雷聲轟隆隆 / There was thunder and lightning last night. 昨夜雷電交加。/ The windows were shaken by a tremendous crash of thunder. 窗子被一聲巨大的悶雷震得亂晃。/ thunder clap（通常用thunderclap）驚雷；霹靂 <span class="definition">vi. 打雷：</span>It thundered a when I was watching TV. 我當時正在看電視，然後打了一個雷。
