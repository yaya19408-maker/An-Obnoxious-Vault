☀ <span class="category">**能量 電力：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**power**</span> ['paʊə] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U]（可被收集起來，並被用於操作機器、發電等活動的）能量，有時可譯為“功率”：</span>electric, electrical, solar, steam, water, wind power 電力；太陽能；蒸汽動力；水力發電；風能 / engine power 發動機的功率 / Nuclear power is cleaner than coal. 核能比煤更清潔。/ The plane was still climbing at full power. 飛機仍然以最大功率向上爬升。/ This wheel provides the power to the cutting machine. 這臺水車為切割機提供動力。<span class="definition">2 [U] 對公眾的電力供應：</span>to switch off the power 關掉電源 / Power has been restored. 供電已恢復。/ The power has gone off. 停電了。/ power line, power source, power supply, power system 輸電線；電源；供電；電力系統 / power tool 電動工具 / power plant 發電廠 / power failure 停電 / power consumption 電力消耗 / to generate power 發電 <span class="definition">vt. 常用被動式，為一部機器或交通工具的運轉提供動力或能量，即驅動、推動：</span>The aircraft is powered by a jet engine. 這架飛機由噴氣發動機驅動。/ The battery could power an electric car for 600 miles on a single charge. 這種電池每充電一次可驅動一輛電動汽車行駛600英里。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**efficiency**</span> [ɪˈfɪʃnsi]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [術語]（機器或發動機的）效率、功率：</span>energy efficiency 能效 / fuel, thermal efficiency 燃料效率；熱效率 / We have failed to improve fuel efficiency standards. 我們試圖提升燃料效能，結果失敗了 / greater efficiency in energy use 能源使用方面的更高效率
 
<span class="vocabulary">**output**</span> ['aʊtpʊt] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.] 某一裝置的輸出功率、能量等：</span>an output of 100 watts 輸出功率100瓦

<span class="vocabulary">**blast**</span> [blɑ:st; 美 blæst]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] full ~ / at full ~（加熱器等）以最大功率：</span>You are unlikely to run the heater at full blast for long periods. 你不太可能長時間把加熱器開到最大擋位。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dynamo**</span> [ˈdaɪnəməʊ; 美 -moʊ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 發電機：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**generator**</span> [ˈdʒenəreɪtə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 發電機，是此義的常規用詞：</span>diesel, gas, petrol（英）, steam, wind generator 柴油發電機；燃氣發電機；汽油發電機；蒸汽發電機；風力發電機 / electrical, electricity, power generator 發電機 / portable generator 便攜發電機 / emergency generator 應急發電機 / A water turbine drives the generator. 這臺發電機是靠水輪機驅動的。/ The wind generator delivers 120 watts in a strong breeze. 這臺風力發電機強風時可以發120瓦電。/ The factory's emergency generators were used during the power cut. 工廠應急發電機在停電期間用上了。
