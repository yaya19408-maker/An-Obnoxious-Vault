☀ <span class="category">**曠課 逃學：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**wag**</span> [wæɡ] 
<span class="definition">vt. [澳大利亞；紐西蘭] 未經許可而不去上學，即逃學：</span>to wag school 逃學

<span class="vocabulary">**cut**</span> [kʌt] 
<span class="definition">vt. [尤美，非正式] 曠課，逃學：</span>to cut class 曠課
           
<span class="vocabulary">**truancy**</span> [ˈtru:ənsi]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 曠課、逃學（即未經許可而離開學校的行為）：</span>Schools need to reduce levels of truancy. 學校需要減少曠課人數。/ measures to combat persistent truancy in our schools 我們各所學校對長期曠課行為的處理措施 / a government campaign to cut truancy 政府發起的一場減少逃學的運動 / parents who condone their children's truancy 容忍孩子曠課的父母 / truancy level, truancy rate 曠課程度；曠課率 / truancy patrol, truancy sweep（均英）逃學巡查；逃學大檢查 / A truancy sweep found 168 children skipping school. 一次曠課大檢查發現168名孩子逃學。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**truant**</span> [ˈtru:ənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [尤英] 曠課的小學生：</span>persistent truant 不斷曠課者 / school truant 逃學者 / She often played truant and wrote her own sick notes. 她經常逃學，自己寫病假條。/ truant officer（監管曠課、逃學學生的）訓導員 <span class="definition">vi. 曠課、逃學：</span>In his fourth year he was truanting regularly. 上四年級時，他頻頻曠課。/ A number of pupils have been truanting regularly. 不少小學生一直經常曠課。

<span class="vocabulary">**detention**</span> [dɪˈtenʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 放學後留校（作為對學生的處罰）：</span>They can't give me (a) detention for this. 他們不可能因為這事罰我課後留下來。/ The teacher kept the boys in detention after school. 老師將這些男生課後留堂。/ Any student caught smoking would be given detention immediately. 任何學生如被發現吸菸將會立即受到課後留校的處罰。/ My first day of school, and I have detention. 我第一天上學就受到課後留校的處罰。
