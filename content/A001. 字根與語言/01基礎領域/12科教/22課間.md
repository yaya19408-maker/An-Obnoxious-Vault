☀ <span class="category">**課間：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**break**</span> [breɪk] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] [英，英還用breaktime] [美為recess]（學校的）課間休息：</span>Come and see me at break. 課間休息時來見我。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**recess**</span> [rɪˈses; ˈri:ses]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [美] 學校的課間休息。相當於英式的break或break time：</span>lunch recess 午餐休息時間 / We had lunch and then we had recess. 我們吃完午飯後休息了一會兒。/ recess bell 課間休息鈴 / I believe kids need more recess time. 我認為小孩子需要更多的課間休息時間。/ He wouldn't play with me at recess. 他課間休息時不願跟我玩。
