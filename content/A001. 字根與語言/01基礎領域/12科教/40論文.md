☀ <span class="category">**論文：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**paper**</span> ['peɪpə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（關於某一學科的）學術論文：</span>to publish a paper in the journal Nature 在《自然》雜誌上發表一篇學術論文 / research, scientific, technical paper 研究論文；科學論文；技術論文 / scholarly paper 學術論文
            
<span class="vocabulary">**thesis**</span> [ˈθi:sɪs]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (on sth) 大學的學位論文：</span>He was awarded his PhD for a thesis on industrial robots. 他憑一篇研究工業機器人的論文獲得博士學位。/ doctoral, honors（美）, MA, Master's, MSc, PhD, senior（美） thesis 博士論文；榮譽論文；文學碩士論文；碩士論文；理科碩士論文；哲學博士論文；畢業論文 / research thesis 科研論文 / unpublished thesis 未發表的論文 / He presented this thesis for his PhD. 他提交了他的博士論文。/ thesis project（美）, thesis research, thesis topic 學位論文專案；學位論文研究；學位論文論題 / her thesis work on skin cancers 她關於皮膚癌的論文 / thesis defense（美） 論文答辯 / Many departments require their students to do a thesis defense. 很多系要求學生進行論文答辯。/ thesis committee（美） 論文委員會 / thesis adviser（美） 論文導師 / research presented in a thesis 論文中提出的研究 / He wrote a doctoral thesis on set theory. 他寫了一片關於集合論的博士論文。          
           
<span class="vocabulary">**discourse**</span> [ˈdɪskɔ:s; 美 -kɔ:rs]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] [正式] 針對某個主題的一個長篇論文：</span>a discourse on issues of gender and sexuality 關於性別和性行為的論文 / Gates responds with a lengthy discourse on deployment strategy. 蓋茨以一篇有關部署策略的鴻篇大論予以回應。

<span class="vocabulary">**dissertation**</span> [ˌdɪsəˈteɪʃn; 美 -sərˈt-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (on sth) 正式的長篇專題論文，尤指學位論文：</span>research dissertation 研究論文 / doctoral, Master's, under-graduate（英）dissertation 博士論文；碩土論文；本科生學位論文 / MSc, PhD dissertation 理學碩士論文；哲學博士論文 / 15 000-word dissertation 15，000字的論文 / unpublished dissertation 未發表的論文 / Students can either do a dissertation or take part in a practical project. 學生可以做學位論文，也可以參加實踐專案。/ Candidates are required to present a dissertation of between 8 000 and 12 000 words. 考生需要遞交一份8，000至12，000字的論文。/ He wrote his Master's dissertation on rats. 他的碩士論文寫的是關於老鼠的問題。/ He is currently writing a dissertation on the Somali civil war. 他目前正在寫一篇關於索馬利亞內戰的論文。

<span class="vocabulary">**treatise**</span> [ˈtri:tɪs; -tɪz]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (on sth) 正式的長篇專題論文：</span>Locke's Treatise on Civil Government 洛克的《政府論》

<span class="vocabulary">**correct**</span> [kə'rekt] 
<span class="definition">vt.（教師）批改（論文）：</span>I spent all evening correcting essays. 我整個晚上都在批改論文。

<span class="vocabulary">**referee**</span> [͵refə'ri:] 
<span class="definition">vt. 審閱尚未發表的技術文章：</span>瞭解即可
