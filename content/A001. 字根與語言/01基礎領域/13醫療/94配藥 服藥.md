☀ <span class="category">**配藥 服藥：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**prepare**</span> [prɪ'peə] 
<span class="definition">vt. prepare sth (from sth) 製作一種藥物（例如透過將不同的物質混合在一起），可稱為調製，配製等：</span>remedies prepared from herbal extracts 用草藥提取成分配製的藥物

<span class="vocabulary">**fill**</span> [fɪl] 
<span class="definition">vt.（按處方）配藥：</span>The pharmacist will fill you a prescription. 藥劑師會照處方給你抓藥。

<span class="vocabulary">**take**</span> [teɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 服用：</span>to take medicine 服藥 / He started taking drugs (= illegal drugs) at college. 他上大學時開始吸毒。

<span class="vocabulary">**pop**</span> [pɒp] 
<span class="definition">vt. [非正式]（經常）服用大量藥物：</span>to pop pills 服用大量藥物 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dose**</span> [dəʊs; 美 doʊs]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb/yourself (up) (with sth) 給（某人或動物）服藥：</span>She dosed herself up with vitamin pills. 她給自己服了一些維生素片。/ He was heavily dosed with painkillers. 他服用了大量止痛藥。/ The doctor fixed the rib, dosed him heavily with drugs, and said he would probably get better. 醫生接好了肋骨，給他服用了大劑量的藥，並說他可能會有所好轉。/ I dosed myself with quinine. 我服用了奎寧。           
           
<span class="vocabulary">**overdose**</span> [ˈəʊvədəʊs; 美 ˈoʊvərdoʊs]
<span class="definition">vi.（也可用非正式的OD）~ (on sth) 一次用藥過量：</span>He had overdosed on heroin. 他過量服用了海洛因。/ Medical opinion varies on how many tablets it takes to overdose. 醫生對於服用多少藥片算作過量意見不一。

<span class="vocabulary">**sedate**</span> [sɪˈdeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 給某人服用鎮靜劑（讓其變得鎮靜或使其睡去）：</span>Most of the patients are heavily sedated. 多數病人服了大劑量鎮靜藥。/ The patient is sedated with intravenous use of sedative drugs. 靜脈注射了鎮靜劑後病人安然入睡。/ Doctors have been told not to sedate children with an anaesthetic that may be linked to five deaths. 醫生們已被告知不要給兒童使用麻醉劑，因為它可能與5起死亡病例有關。

<span class="vocabulary">**on**</span> [ɒn] 
<span class="definition">prep. 按時服藥：</span>The doctor put me on antibiotics. 醫生要我服用抗生素。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**administer**</span> [ədˈmɪnɪstə(r)]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] ~ sth (to sb) 給予或施用（藥物等）：</span>The dose was administered to the child intravenously. 已給那孩子靜脈注射了這一劑量。/ Paramedics are trained to administer certain drugs. 護理人員們接受了配發某些藥物的培訓。

<span class="vocabulary">**administration**</span> [əd͵mɪnɪs'treɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 對某人施用藥物的行為，為正式用法，如：</span>the administration of antibiotics 抗生素的施用 / intravenous, oral administration 靜脈給藥；口服 / drug administration 給藥
    
<span class="vocabulary">**puff**</span> [pʌf]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 吸（藥）：</span>Take two puffs from the inhaler every four hours. 每隔四小時從吸藥器中吸兩口藥。
