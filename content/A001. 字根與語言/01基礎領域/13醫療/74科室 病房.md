☀ <span class="category">**科室 病房：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**department**</span> [dɪ'pɑːtmənt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 醫院的部門：</span>accident and emergency（英）, casualty（英）, emergency（美）, hospital, outpatient department 急診處；急診室；門診部

<span class="vocabulary">**unit**</span> ['ju:nɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指醫院中的）科室；病區：</span>intensive care unit 重症監護室 / accident and emergency, casualty（均英）unit 急診科；急救科 / psychiatric, surgical unit 精神科；外科 / baby, maternity（均英）unit 嬰兒病房；婦產科 / burn（美）, burns（英）unit 燒傷科

<span class="vocabulary">**waiting room**</span> 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 人們等候的時候可以坐在其中休息的屋子，如候診室：</span>hospital waiting room 醫院候診室

<span class="vocabulary">**ward**</span> [wɔ:d] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 醫院中的病房，常住著患有相似疾病的人：</span>hospital ward 醫院病房 / isolation ward（尤英）隔離病房 / maternity, surgical ward 產科病房；外科病房 / casualty ward 急診室 / ward nurse（英國）病房護士 / ward round（英）查病房 / to be admitted to ward（尤英）住院收治

<span class="vocabulary">**cell**</span> [sel] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 某種病房：</span>padded cell（精神病院的）軟壁病房
           
<span class="vocabulary">**casualty**</span> [ˈkæʒuəlti]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（也可用casualty department或accident and emergency，均為英式用法；美式用emergency room）醫院的急診室：</span>casualty unit, casualty ward 急診室；急診病房 / The victims were rushed to casualty. 受傷者被迅速送往急救室。/ I was taken to casualty at St Thomas's Hospital. 我被送往聖·托馬斯醫院的急救室。/ He was admitted to casualty with head injuries. 他因頭部受傷而被送進急診室。/ He works as a doctor in casualty. 他是急診醫生。

<span class="vocabulary">**surgery**</span> [ˈsɜ:dʒəri; 美 ˈsɜ:rdʒ-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 手術室：</span>瞭解即可    
           
<span class="vocabulary">**recovery**</span> [rɪˈkʌvəri]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（也可用recovery room [C]）（供剛做完手術的病人使用的）監護室：</span>瞭解即可
