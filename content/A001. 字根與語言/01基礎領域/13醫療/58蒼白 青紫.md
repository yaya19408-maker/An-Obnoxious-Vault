☀ <span class="category">**蒼白 青紫：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**pale**</span> [peɪl] 
<span class="definition">adj.（人、人臉等常由於疾病、驚嚇、震驚等而變得）蒼白的。是此義的常規用詞：</span>She looked pale and tired. 她看起來蒼白而疲憊。/ pale with fear 嚇得臉色煞白 / He went pale as the news sank in. 他聽明白那個訊息後，臉變得慘白。<span class="definition">vi. pale (at sth) 變得比平常白，變蒼白：</span>He paled visibly at the sight of the police car. 他一看見警車，臉色刷地就變白了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pallor**</span> [ˈpælə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.]（尤指疾病或恐懼造成的臉色或膚色的）蒼白：</span>Her cheeks had an unhealthy pallor. 她面色蒼白，顯得虛弱。/ The deathly pallor of her skin had been replaced by the faintest flush of color. 她如死灰般蒼白的皮膚開始現出淡淡的血色。/ My face had a greenish pallor. 我的臉色白中帶綠。/ He had a sickly pallor. 他臉色蒼白，帶有病容。

<span class="vocabulary">**grey**</span> [ɡreɪ]（尤英）（美尤用gray）
<span class="definition">adj.（人的膚色）由於疾病、勞累、悲傷而蒼白的：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**white**</span> [waɪt] 
<span class="definition">adj.（皮膚由於情緒或疾病而顯得）蒼白的：</span>white with shock 震驚得臉色發白 / She went white as a sheet when she heard the news. 她聽到這訊息時臉色變得煞白。/ He turned white with anger. 他氣得臉色發白。

<span class="vocabulary">**blue**</span> [blu:] 
<span class="definition">adj.（人或身體部位）由於寒冷或呼吸困難而發青的，青紫的：</span>Her hands were blue with cold. 她的雙手凍得發青。

周邊詞彙補充：
· green可指（人或其皮膚）蒼白的、發青的。
