☀ <span class="category">**病程階段 疾病輕重：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**grade**</span> [ɡreɪd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [術語] 疾病的程度，階段：</span>high grade fever 高燒

<span class="vocabulary">**acute**</span> [ə'kju:t] 
<span class="definition">adj. 醫學中可指（疾病）急性的：</span>a patient with acute appendicitis 一位急性闌尾炎病人

<span class="vocabulary">**chronic**</span> [ˈkrɒnɪk; 美 ˈkrɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">adj. 1（疾病）長期的、難以治癒的，即慢性的：</span>chronic bronchitis, chronic arthritis, chronic asthma 慢性支氣管炎；慢性關節炎；慢性哮喘 / chronic back pain 慢性背痛 <span class="definition">2 長期患病的：</span>chronic alcoholic, chronic depressive 慢性酒精中毒者；長期抑鬱症患者

<span class="vocabulary">**terminal**</span> ['tə:mɪnl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1（疾病）不能得到治療且將導致死亡的（過程常是緩慢的），即晚期的：</span>He has terminal lung cancer. 他患有晚期肺癌。/ The illness is usually terminal. 這種病通常為不治之症。/ She’s suffering from terminal boredom.（比喻用法）她現在感到煩得要死。<span class="definition">2（人）患有晚期疾病的：</span>a terminal patient 晚期病人
           
<span class="vocabulary">**advanced**</span> [ədˈvɑ:nst; 美 -ˈvænst]
<span class="definition">adj.（疾病）處於晚期的：</span>the advanced stages of the disease 疾病晚期 / 'Medicare' is available to victims of advanced kidney disease. 老年醫療保險也適用於腎病晚期患者。/ The disease was too far advanced for doctors to operate. 病已發展到晚期，即使醫生做手術也於事無補了。
