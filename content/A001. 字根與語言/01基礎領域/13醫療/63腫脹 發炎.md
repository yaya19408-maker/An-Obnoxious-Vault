☀ <span class="category">**腫脹 發炎：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**swell**</span> [swel] 
<span class="definition">vi. swell (up)（身體部位等）腫脹。是此義的常規用詞：</span>My left ankle was beginning to swell up. 我的左腳踝開始腫了起來。/ His right eye had almost swollen shut. 他的右眼腫得幾乎睜不開。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**swollen**</span> [ˈswəʊlən]
<span class="definition">adj.（身體的一部分）腫脹的：</span>swollen glands 腫脹的腺體 / My eyes were so swollen I could hardly see. 我的眼睛腫得很厲害，幾乎看不到東西。/ Her eyes were all red and swollen from crying. 她兩眼哭得又紅又腫。/ One of her eyes was swollen shut. 她的一隻眼睛腫得睜不開了。/ Her face was still very swollen. 她的臉仍然腫得厲害。/ Her legs got swollen from standing up all day. 因為整天站著，她的雙腿已經腫了。/ His ankle is bruised and quite badly swollen. 他的腳踝挫傷了，腫得很厲害。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**swelling**</span> [ˈswelɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 膨脹、腫脹：</span>Use ice to reduce the swelling. 用冰敷消腫。/ There is some swelling and he is being detained for observation. 他身上有些浮腫，被留院觀察。/ There is severe swelling of the glands. 腺體嚴重腫脹。<span class="definition">2 [C] 身體（由於疾病或受傷而出現的）腫脹處：</span>The fall left her with a painful swelling above her eye. 她摔了一跤，眼睛上方起了一個包，挺疼的。/ His eye was partly closed, and there was a swelling over his lid. 他的眼睛半閉著，眼瞼上有一個腫塊。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**bump**</span> [bʌmp]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（碰撞造成的）腫塊，鼓包：</span>She was covered in bumps and bruises. 她全身青腫，傷痕累累。/ How did you get that bump on your forehead? 你額頭上怎麼起了個包？/ He got a nasty bump on his head. 他頭上腫了一塊，很嚴重。/ She fell against our coffee table and got a large bump on her forehead. 她倒地時撞到我們的咖啡桌上，額頭起了個大包。/ goose bumps（通常用goosebumps）（尤美）雞皮疙搭 / bumps and bruises腫塊和瘀傷
           
<span class="vocabulary">**inflamed**</span> [ɪnˈfleɪmd]
<span class="definition">adj.（身體部位由於感染或受傷）發炎的、紅腫的：</span>Symptoms include red, itchy and inflamed skin. 症狀包括皮膚髮紅、發癢、發炎。/ Her eyes were sore and inflamed. 她的眼睛紅腫疼痛。/ Her joints are severely inflamed. 她的關節嚴重發炎。

<span class="vocabulary">**inflammation**</span> [ˌɪnfləˈmeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 發炎、炎症：</span>The drug can cause inflammation of the liver. 該藥會引起肝臟炎症。/ throat inflammations 咽喉發炎 / severe inflammation 嚴重的炎症 / mild inflammation 輕微的炎症 / acute, chronic inflammation 急性炎症；慢性炎症 / intestinal, joint inflammation 腸炎；關節炎 / Steroids often help reduce the inflammation and itching in the skin. 類固醇常常有助於減輕炎症和皮膚瘙癢。/ inflammation of the stomach 胃炎 / signs of inflammation 發炎的跡象
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**irritate**</span> [ˈɪrɪteɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. 刺激（皮膚或身體部位）（使其發癢或疼痛）：</span>Wear rubber gloves while chopping chillies as they can irritate the skin. 切辣椒時要戴橡膠手套，因為辣椒會刺激皮膚。/ Some drugs can irritate the lining of the stomach. 有些藥物會刺激胃內壁。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**irritating**</span> ['ɪrɪteɪtɪŋ]
<span class="definition">adj. 對身體有刺激性的、引發瘙癢或疼痛的：</span>In heavy concentrations, ozone is irritating to the eyes, nose and throat. 高濃度的臭氧會對眼睛、鼻子和咽喉有刺激作用。

<span class="vocabulary">**irritation**</span> [ˌɪrɪˈteɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 身體部位的輕微疼痛或不適，如刺激、發炎：</span>intense, severe irritation 強烈的刺激；嚴重的疼痛 / mild irritation 輕微的刺痛 / The infection can cause intense irritation of the throat. 這種感染可能導致喉部嚴重疼痛。/ eye irritation 眼睛發炎 / a new cream to treat skin irritation 治療皮膚髮炎的新乳膏 / Stop using the cream if you get any irritation. 如有任何不適請停止使用此乳霜。/ These oils may cause irritation to sensitive skins. 這些化妝油會導致敏感皮膚髮炎。/ This is an irritation and inflammation of the edge of the eyelid. 這是眼瞼邊緣紅腫發炎的症狀。

<span class="vocabulary">**angry**</span> ['æŋɡrɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj.（傷口）紅腫且感染的，發炎的。是一個擬人用法：</span>The accident left two angry cuts across his forehead. 那次事故讓他的腦門留下兩道紅腫的口子。

<span class="vocabulary">**boil**</span> [bɒɪl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 皮膚上的紅色、疼痛的腫脹（內有黃色黏稠狀液體），即癤，或皮下膿腫：</span>瞭解即可
