☀ <span class="category">**頭部 大腦：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**mental**</span> ['mentl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 常用於名詞前，與精神的健康狀態有關的，或與精神疾病的治療有關的，相當於psychological：</span>mental health 精神健康 / mental illness, mental hospital 精神病；精神病院 / She was suffering from physical and mental exhaustion. 她當時已經精疲力竭。

<span class="vocabulary">**head**</span> [hed] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 身體的頭部，頭。在一般的用法中，幾乎是此義的唯一用詞（因為其它表示“頭”的詞極不常用）：</span>to turn one’s head 轉過頭 / throbbing head 頭一抽一抽地作痛 / head injury 頭部受傷 / He could feel his head spinning. 他覺得天旋地轉。/ He put his head around the door. 他從門後探出頭來。

<span class="vocabulary">**brain**</span> [breɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（作為一種器官的）大腦，腦：</span>human brain 人腦 / brain cell 腦細胞 / brain region 大腦區域 / brain activity 大腦活動 / brain damage, brain injury 大腦損傷 / brain surgery 腦外科 / He was found to have a blood clot on his brain. 他的腦部發現有血塊。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**skull**</span> [skʌl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 腦袋：</span>When will he get it into his thick skull that I never want to see him again! 那笨頭笨腦的傢伙什麼時候才能明白我再也不想見他了！/ Her skull was crammed with too many thoughts. 她腦瓜子裡想法太多。

<span class="vocabulary">**nut**</span> [nʌt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [英，俚語] 人的頭或大腦：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**noodle**</span> ['nu:dl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [舊時，美，俚語] 人的頭、腦袋，相當於noddle：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**temple**</span> ['templ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 太陽穴或鬢角的部位：</span>left, right temple 左太陽穴；右太陽穴 / There was a throbbing in her temple. 她的太陽穴直跳。/ He’s going white at the temples. 他兩鬢開始斑白了。

相關詞義延伸：
· skull還可指顱骨、頭骨、頭蓋骨。
