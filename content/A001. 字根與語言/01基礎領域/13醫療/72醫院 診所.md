☀ <span class="category">**醫院 診所：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**hospital**</span> ['hɒspɪtl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 醫院：</span>community hospital 社群醫院 / private hospital 私立醫院 / general hospital 綜合性醫院 / children’s hospital 兒童醫院 / university hospital 大學醫院 / military hospital 部隊醫院 / hospital staff 醫院全體員工 / hospital inpatient 住院病人 / hospital ward 醫院病房 / hospital admission, hospital stay 入院；住院 / We went to the hospital to visit my grandmother. 我們去醫院探望奶奶。

<span class="vocabulary">**clinic**</span> ['klɪnɪk] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 診所，或醫院的門診部：</span>hospital clinic 診所；（醫院的）門診部 / health, medical clinic 保健診所；醫療診所 / private clinic 私人診所 / rural clinic 鄉村診所 / fertility, infertility clinic 不孕不育門診 / eye clinic 眼科門診 / a clinic for asthma sufferers 治療哮喘患 / drug clinic 戒毒診所 / clinic nurse 門診護士 / clinic appointment 門診預約 / clinic patient 門診病人者的診所 <span class="definition">2 [C] [尤英] 私人診所，或（專門治療某類疾病的）專科醫院：</span>He was treated at the Paris clinic. 他曾在巴黎一傢俬人診所接受治療。<span class="definition">3 [C] [美] 病人接受治療的一棟建築，即門診治療部，或稱門診樓；或一群醫生共同工作的一棟建築，即醫療中心：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**surgery**</span> [ˈsɜ:dʒəri; 美 ˈsɜ:rdʒ-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [英] [美式用office]（醫生、牙醫、獸醫的）診室、門診處：</span>open surgery 開門的診所 / dental, dentist's, doctor's, GP's, veterinary surgery 牙科診所；醫生診所；全科診所；獸醫診所 / those attending the doctor's surgery with physical symptoms 那些身體不適去看醫生的人 / I have to go to the surgery to pick up the prescription. 我要去診所取藥方。/ Dr Smith isn't in the surgery today. 史密斯醫生今天不在診所。/ Bill was in the doctor's surgery demanding to know what was wrong with him. 比爾在醫生的診療室裡想要知道自己到底得了什麼病。

<span class="vocabulary">**office**</span> ['ɒfɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [美] 醫生、牙醫或獸醫的診室，門診處。相當於英式的surgery：</span>dentist’s, doctor’s, nurse’s, physician’s, surgeon’s office（均美）牙醫診所；醫生診室；護士辦公室；內科診室；外科診室
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vet**</span> [vet]
<span class="definition">n. [C] vet's（複數形式為vets）可指獸醫診所：</span>I've got to take the dog to the vet's tomorrow. 明天我得把狗帶到獸醫診所去診治。

<span class="vocabulary">**doctor**</span> ['dɒktə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] the doctor’s 可指醫生給病人看病的地方，即診所：</span>to go to the doctor’s 去診所 / I have an appointment at the doctor’s. 我在診所有個預約。

<span class="vocabulary">**dentist**</span> ['dentɪst] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the dentist或the dentist’s可表示牙科診所：</span>an appointment at the dentist’s 牙科診所的門診預約
        
<span class="vocabulary">**asylum**</span> [əˈsaɪləm]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（舊時用法）精神病院：</span>insane, lunatic, mental asylum 瘋人院；精神病院 / He spent three years in an asylum. 他在精神病院待了三年。
