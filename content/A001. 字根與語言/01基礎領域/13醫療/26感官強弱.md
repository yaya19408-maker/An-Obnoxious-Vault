☀ <span class="category">**感官強弱：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**acute**</span> [ə'kju:t] 
<span class="definition">adj.（人或動物的感官）敏銳的，靈敏的：</span>acute sense of sight 敏銳的視力
           
<span class="vocabulary">**keen**</span> [ki:n]
<span class="definition">adj. [只用於名詞前] 感官靈敏的、敏銳的：</span>Dogs have a keen sense of smell. 狗的嗅覺很靈敏。/ Brand's keen ear caught the trace of an accent. 布蘭德敏銳的耳朵聽出了口音。/ My friend has a keen eye for (= is good at noticing) a bargain. 我的朋友最會發現便宜貨。/ an amateur artist with a keen eye for detail 對細節非常敏感的業餘畫家           

<span class="vocabulary">**shrewd**</span> [ʃru:d]
<span class="definition">adj.（目光）銳利的：</span>His grey eyes were shrewd but kindly. 他灰色的眼睛目光銳利卻很親切。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**intense**</span> [ɪnˈtens]
<span class="definition">adj.（目光）有穿透力的、洞悉一切的：</span>I felt so self-conscious under Luke's mother's intense gaze. 在盧克母親審視的目光下，我感到極不自在。/ He gazed at me with those intense blue eyes. 他用那雙深邃的藍眼睛凝視著我。

· sensitive可表示（對低溫、光、食物等）反應過敏的（即反應過快或過大的）。
