☀ <span class="category">**菸草 吸菸：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**tobacco**</span> [tə'bækəʊ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 菸葉；（香菸、雪茄等）菸草製品：</span>Try to do without tobacco and alcohol. 儘量不要抽菸喝酒。/ chewing, rolling（尤英）tobacco 嚼煙；手卷絲 / tobacco company, tobacco firm 菸草公司 / tobacco market 菸草市場

<span class="vocabulary">**chew**</span> [tʃu:] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 供咀嚼用的菸草：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**cigarette**</span> [sɪɡə'ret] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 香菸：</span>hand-rolled cigarette 手卷的香菸 / menthol cigarette 薄荷捲菸 / marijuana cigarette 大麻捲菸 / a case of cigarettes 一盒香菸 / to light a cigarette 點燃一支香菸 / cigarette ash 香菸灰 / cigarette ad 香菸廣告 / cigarette tax 菸草稅

<span class="vocabulary">**cigar**</span> [sɪ'ɡɑː] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 雪茄煙：</span>to smoke a cigar 抽雪茄 / fat cigar 粗大的雪茄 / Cuban cigar 古巴雪茄 / cigar smoke 雪茄煙霧 / cigar end 雪茄煙蒂

<span class="vocabulary">**weed**</span> [wi:d] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [幽默] the weed 可指菸草或香菸：</span>to give up the weed 戒菸
           
<span class="vocabulary">**snuff**</span> [snʌf]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 鼻菸：</span>a pinch of snuff 一撮鼻菸 / to inhale snuff, to take snuff 吸鼻菸 / soaked box 鼻菸盒
           
<span class="vocabulary">**joint**</span> [dʒɔɪnt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 含有大麻的一種香菸：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**pipe**</span> [paɪp] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（抽菸用的狹長的）菸斗：</span>opium pipe 吸食鴉片的菸斗 / pipe smoker 吸菸斗的人

<span class="vocabulary">**draw**</span> [drɔ:] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 吸菸，詞義相當於drag：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 抽（煙）：</span>He drew on a cigarette. 他吸了一口煙。

<span class="vocabulary">**smoke**</span> [sməʊk] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] [非正式] 吸菸，抽菸：</span>Are you coming outside for a smoke? 你是不是出來抽支菸？<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 吸菸，抽菸。是此義的常規用詞：</span>He was smoking a big cigar. 他那時正抽著一根大雪茄。/ Do you mind if I smoke? 我抽菸你介意嗎？<span class="definition">vi. 習慣性吸菸或抽菸：</span>Do you smoke? 你抽菸嗎？/ She smokes heavily. 她的煙癮大。/ to quit smoking 戒菸
           
<span class="vocabulary">**smoking**</span> [ˈsməʊkɪŋ; 美 ˈsmoʊk-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 吸菸、抽菸（可以是一種活動，也可以是一種習慣）：</span>No Smoking (= for example, on a notice) 禁止吸菸 / a no-smoking area 禁菸區 / Would you like smoking or non-smoking? (= for example in a restaurant) 你喜歡在吸菸區還是非吸菸區？/ the decision to scrap smoking compartments on Kent trains 取消肯特列車上的吸菸車廂的決定 / Smoking is now banned in many places of work. 現在許多工作場所禁止吸菸。/ chain, heavy smoking 一支接一支的吸菸；抽菸很兇 / passive smoking（英）被動吸菸 / teen（尤美）, teenage, underage smoking 青少年吸菸 / cigar, cigarette, pipe, tobacco smoking 抽雪茄；抽香菸；抽菸鬥；抽菸葉 / cannabis（尤英）, marijuana（尤美）smoking 吸大麻 / to give up smoking（尤英）, to quit smoking（尤美）, stop smoking 戒菸 / I'm trying to stop smoking.我正在戒菸。/ The doctor advised me to cut down on smoking and alcohol.（cut down on尤為英式用法）醫生建議我少抽菸喝酒。/ smoking habit 煙癮 / A total smoking ban has been imposed throughout the building. 整棟大樓已經完全禁菸。/ smoking room 吸菸室 / a study of the harmful effects of smoking 有關吸菸害處的研究 / The company has a strict no-smoking policy.這家公司有嚴格的禁止吸菸的規定。/ smoking-related death 與吸菸有關的死亡 / smoking-related disease, smoking-related illness（尤英）與吸菸有關的疾病
           
<span class="vocabulary">**puff**</span> [pʌf]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (at/on sth) 吸菸、抽菸；抽菸鬥：</span>He lit a cigar and puffed at it twice. 他點了一支雪茄，吸了兩口。/ He puffed away at his cigar. 他一口一口猛抽著雪茄。/ She puffed furiously at her cigarette. 她猛抽著香菸。/ I sat puffing my cigar. 我坐著抽雪茄。/ He puffed (away) on his pipe. 他（一口一口地）吸著菸斗。/ My father sat puffing contentedly on his pipe. 父親坐著，心滿意足地抽著菸斗。/ He nodded and puffed on a stubby pipe as he listened. 他邊聽邊點頭，還吸著一支粗短的菸斗。<span class="definition">n. [C] 吸（香菸）：</span>He had a few puffs at the cigar. 他吸了幾口雪茄。/ a puff on her cigarette 吸一口她的香菸 / He took a long puff at his cigar and began his story，他吸了一大口雪茄，然後開始講起故事來。

<span class="vocabulary">**suck**</span> [sʌk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 吸（煙）。不常用：</span>He sucked on his cigarette. 他吸了吸他的香菸。

<span class="vocabulary">**have**</span> [hæv] 
<span class="definition">vt. 吸菸。是一個簡單用詞：</span>I had a cigarette while I was waiting. 我等候時抽了一根菸。

<span class="vocabulary">**drag**</span> [dræɡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 吸一口（香菸等），詞義相當於draw：</span>She took a long drag on her cigarette. 她長長地抽了一大口煙。

<span class="vocabulary">**pull**</span> [pʊl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] pull (at/on sth) 大口吸菸等：</span>He took a long pull on his cigarette. 他深深地吸了一口煙。

<span class="vocabulary">**smoker**</span> ['sməʊkə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 吸菸的人，吸菸者：</span>cigar, cigarette, pipe smoker 抽雪茄的人；抽香菸的人；抽菸斗的人 / chain, heavy smoker 一支接一支吸菸的人；煙癮大的人 / light smoker 煙癮不大的人 / regular smoker 經常吸菸者 / passive smoker（英）被動吸菸者 / pot smoker（非正式用法）吸大麻的人 / rooms for smokers 吸菸室 / a smoker’s cough 吸菸過多引起的咳嗽 

相關詞義延伸：
· tobacco還可表示菸草植物；
· smoker還可指火車上允許吸菸的車廂。
