☀ <span class="category">**體力：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**power**</span> ['paʊə] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 也可用複數的powers，指身體或心智的某種能力：</span>I lost my power of speech for a while after the accident. 事故發生後有一段時間我無法說話。/ The drug may affect your powers of concentration. 這種藥可能會影響你的注意力集中。/ her powers of observation 她的觀察力 / He had to use all his powers of persuasion. 他只好使出說服人的全部本領。/ bargaining power 談判能力 <span class="definition">2 [pl.] powers 可指一個人的身體或心靈所具有的全部能力，即全部體力或智力：</span>At 26, he is at the height of his powers and ranked fourth in the world. 26歲時，他處於巔峰狀態，排名世界第四。/ In 1946 Dali was at the peak of his powers. 1946年，達利處於創作能力的頂峰。

<span class="vocabulary">**strength**</span> [streŋθ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.] strength to do sth（身體的）體力，力氣或力量：</span>muscle, physical strength 肌肉力量；體力 / arm, leg strength 臂力；腿部力量 / He pushed against the rock with all his strength. 他用全力推那塊石頭。/ brute strength 蠻力 / She didn’t have the strength to walk any further. 她再也走不動了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**muscle**</span> [ˈmʌsl]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 體力：</span>He's an intelligent player but lacks the muscle of older competitors. 他是個聰明的選手，但卻缺乏老對手的體力。
