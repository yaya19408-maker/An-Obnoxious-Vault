<span class="vocabulary">**mad**</span> [mæd] 
<span class="definition">adj. [尤英] 神經運轉不正常的，有精神疾病的，瘋的：</span>They realized that he had gone mad. 他們意識到他瘋了。/ I’ll go mad if I have to wait much longer. 如果還要等更久的話，我會發瘋的。/ The world had gone completely mad. 這個世界已徹底瘋狂了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**madness**</span> [ˈmædnəs]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [過時] 精神失常；瘋狂：</span>They say that talking to yourself is the first sign of madness! 據說自言自語是精神錯亂的先兆!/ There may be a link between madness and creativity. 在瘋狂和創造力之間也許有著某種聯絡。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**mania**</span> [ˈmeɪniə]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [心理學] 躁狂症：</span>the treatment of mania 對躁狂症的治療 / He almost wouldn't eat at all and had the oddest manias. 他幾乎顆粒未進，走火入魔。

<span class="vocabulary">**crazy**</span> ['kreɪzɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. [尤美，非正式] 患精神疾病的，精神失常（或錯亂）的：</span>She’s crazy--she ought to be locked up. 她瘋了，應該把她關起來。<span class="definition">n. [C] [尤美，非正式] 一個發瘋的人，即瘋子：</span>瞭解即可
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**deranged**</span> [dɪˈreɪndʒd]
<span class="definition">adj.（尤指由於精神疾病而）不能正常做事和思考的，如精神錯亂的、精神失常的、癲狂的：</span>They took her into hospital because she was mentally deranged. 他們因為她精神錯亂而把她送進了醫院。/ a deranged attacker 瘋狂的襲擊者 / A deranged man shot and killed 14 people. 一個精神失常的男子開槍打死了14人。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**disturbed**</span> [dɪˈstɜ:bd; 美 -ˈstɜ:rbd]
<span class="definition">adj.（尤指由於痛苦的經歷）有精神病的、精神不正常的：</span>Many of our patients are severely disturbed. 我們的許多病人精神嚴重失常。/ to be behaviourally/behaviorally disturbed, to be emotionally disturbed, to be mentally disturbed, to be psychologically disturbed 行為異常；情緒異常；精神異常；心理異常 / a special school for emotionally disturbed children 為精神異常兒童開辦的特殊學校 / The murderer was apparently mentally disturbed. 該殺人犯顯然精神失常。

<span class="vocabulary">**funny**</span> ['fʌnɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. [英，非正式] 有點瘋狂的；不像其他人的。即瘋瘋癲癲的，有點不正常的，相當於strange或peculiar：</span>She went a bit funny after her husband died. 丈夫死後她神志就有點兒不大正常了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**touched**</span> [tʌtʃt]
<span class="definition">adj. [名詞前不可用] [過時，非正式] 有點發瘋的，如神經兮兮的：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**unbalanced**</span> [ˌʌnˈbælənst]
<span class="definition">adj. [名詞前不常用]（人）心理不平衡的、情緒不穩的、精神輕微失常的：</span>I knew how unbalanced Paula had been since my uncle Peter died. 我知道葆拉自從我叔叔彼得去世後情緒一直很不穩定。/ emotionally unbalanced 情緒失常 / She became mentally unbalanced after the accident. 那次事故之後她精神失常了。

<span class="vocabulary">**insane**</span> [ɪnˈseɪn]
<span class="definition">adj. 精神失常的、精神錯亂的：</span>Doctors certified him as insane. 醫生證明他精神失常。/ she was certified clinically insane and sent to a mental hospital. 她被診斷為精神失常並被送進了精神病院。/ He later became insane and was confined to an asylum. 後來他精神失常，被關進了瘋人院。/ She claimed she was temporarily insane during the attack. 她聲稱攻擊期間她的精神不正常。/ He is criminally insane, unable to stop himself from attacking women. 他是個精神失常的罪犯，控制不住襲擊婦女的行為。/ He went almost insane when he heard that his daughter had died. 聽說女兒死去的噩耗時他幾乎精神失常。/ The prisoners were slowly going insane. 囚犯們正慢慢地變得精神錯亂起來。<span class="definition">n. [pl.] the insane 指精神失常的人、精神錯亂的人：</span>a hospital for the insane 精神病院
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**insanity**</span> [ɪnˈsænəti]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 精神失常；精神錯亂；精神病：</span>He was found not guilty, by reason of insanity. 他以精神失常為理由獲判無罪。/ The defence pleaded insanity, but the defendant was found guilty and sentenced. 辯方以精神錯亂為由進行申辯，但被告被判有罪並獲刑。/ At the murder trial, he pleaded insanity. 在謀殺案審理中，他辯稱自己精神失常。/ insanity defence/defense, insanity plea（均尤美）精神失常辯護；精神失常抗辯 / His lawyer got him off on an insanity plea. 他的律師以精神失常為由成功地為他作了無罪辯護。/ The film is a powerful study of a woman's descent into insanity. 那部電影深入剖析了一個女人墮入瘋狂的過程。/ Hamlet feigns insanity to disguise his bloody motive. 哈姆雷特佯裝瘋癲，掩飾自己的血腥動機。/ temporary insanity 短期的精神失常

<span class="vocabulary">**trolley**</span> [ˈtrɒli; 美 ˈtrɑ:li]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] off your ~ [英，非正式] 瘋癲、失去理智、愚蠢：</span>Is she off her trolley or what? 她是瘋了還是怎麼了？

<span class="vocabulary">**lunatic**</span> [ˈlu:nətɪk]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [舊時] 嚴重精神病患者、瘋子（現在具有冒犯性）：</span>lunatic asylum（舊時，尤英）瘋人院

<span class="vocabulary">**hysteria**</span> [hɪˈstɪəriə; 美 -ˈstɪr-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [醫學] 癔病；歇斯底里：</span>By now, she was screaming, completely overcome with hysteria. 現在她正在尖叫，完全處於癔病發作狀態。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**hysterical**</span> [hɪˈsterɪkl]
<span class="definition">adj.（通常指因驚嚇而）情緒異常激動的，癔病的：</span>I suffered bouts of really hysterical depression. 我曾患陣發的嚴重癔病性抑鬱症。

<span class="vocabulary">**depression**</span> [dɪˈpreʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 抑鬱症：</span>She suffered from severe depression after losing her job. 她失業後患了嚴重的抑鬱症。/ mild, minor depression 輕度抑鬱；輕微抑鬱 / moderate depression 中度抑鬱 / manic depression 躁狂抑鬱症 / post-natal, post-partum（美）depression 產後抑鬱症 / The actor says he suffers frequent bouts of depression. 那位演員說他經常出現抑鬱情緒。/ In a fit of depression, she threw away all her books. 她一時抑鬱，扔掉了自己所有的書。/ A period of acute depression can sometimes follow childbirth. 分娩之後可能會有一段時間的急性抑鬱期。/ She fell into a black depression and refused to leave her room. 她變得極度抑鬱，拒絕離開自己的房間。/ She was diagnosed as having clinical depression. 她被診斷患有臨床抑鬱症。/ His wife had left him and he was being treated for depression. 妻子離開了他，他正接受抑鬱症治療。/ She was gradually coming out of her depression. 她正逐漸走出抑鬱。/ Bereavement can often lead to depression. 喪失親人常會引發抑鬱症。/ a new drug used to treat depression 用於治療抑鬱症的新藥 / Her depression has lifted now. 她的抑鬱症現在痊癒了。/ Depression affects a surprising number of people. 受抑鬱症困擾者人數驚人。/ He may have killed himself in depression. 他可能是在抑鬱的狀態中自殺的。/ He's been off work for months with depression. 他患有抑鬱症，已經幾個月沒有工作了。/ The onset of depression often follows a traumatic event. 抑鬱症發作常常是在造成心理創傷的事件之後。/ He was in a state of acute depression. 他急性抑鬱症正在發作。/ She had been receiving medical treatment for depression. 她一直在接受抑鬱症治療。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**depressed**</span> [dɪˈprest]
<span class="definition">adj. 患有抑鬱症的：</span>mildly depressed 輕度抑鬱 / chronically, clinically, suicidally depressed 慢性抑鬱；臨床上抑鬱；自殺性抑鬱 / At the time he was suicidal and clinically depressed. 那時候他有自殺傾向而且臨床診斷為抑鬱。           

<span class="vocabulary">**schizophrenia**</span> [ˌskɪtsəˈfri:niə]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 精神分裂症：</span>chronic schizophrenia 慢性精神分裂症 / paranoid schizophrenia 偏執型精神分裂症 / schizophrenia patients 精神分裂症患者
           
<span class="vocabulary">**paranoia**</span> [ˌpærəˈnɔɪə]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [醫學] 妄想症；偏執狂症：</span>The regime is in the grip of severe paranoia. 該政府處於嚴重的偏執狂的掌控之中。/ I was guilty perhaps of mild paranoia. 我為自己也許有點兒偏執而內疚。/ irrational paranoia 非理性的偏執 / the anti-communist paranoia of the 1950s 20 世紀 50年代的反共狂潮 / Her passion for cleanliness borders on paranoia. 她的潔癖近平偏執。/ The drugs can induce paranoia. 這些藥物會誘發偏執。/ External threats will just fuel paranoia among those in power. 外部威脅只會加劇當權者的偏執。/ The paranoia is setting in. 開始變得偏執。/ He was gripped by paranoia. 他為偏執所累。
          
<span class="vocabulary">**neurosis**</span> [njʊəˈrəʊsɪs; 美 nʊˈroʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] [醫學] 神經（官能）症（即長期過分恐懼憂慮的狀態）：</span>He was anxious to the point of neurosis. 他焦慮得都要得神經症了。/ She got a neurosis about chemicals and imagined them everywhere doing her harm. 她對化學品有神經症，認為它們無處不在地傷害著自己。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**autistic**</span> [ɔ:ˈtɪstɪk]
<span class="definition">adj. 自閉症的、孤獨症的：</span>autistic behaviour 自閉症行為 / a school for severely autistic children 為患有嚴重自閉症的孩子開辦的學校
