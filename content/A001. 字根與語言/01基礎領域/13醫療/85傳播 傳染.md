☀ <span class="category">**傳播 傳染：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**spread**</span> [spred] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 疾病在人們之間傳播；或使疾病傳播。是此義的常規用詞，極為常用：</span>The disease is spread easily by mosquitoes. 這種疾病很容易透過蚊子來傳播。<span class="definition">n. [U] 疾病的傳播，蔓延：</span>the spread of disease 疾病的傳播
           
<span class="vocabulary">**rage**</span> [reɪdʒ]
<span class="definition">vi.（疾病）迅速蔓延：</span>A flu epidemic raged through Europe. 流感在整個歐洲肆虐。

<span class="vocabulary">**carry**</span> ['kærɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 指人、動物、昆蟲等攜帶致病源而傳播疾病。是一個簡單的基礎用詞，也算常用：</span>to carry the virus 攜帶病毒 / to carry a disease 傳播疾病

<span class="vocabulary">**give**</span> [ɡɪv] 
<span class="definition">vt. give sth to sb / give sb sth 把疾病傳染給某人。是一個簡單的生活化用詞，不太常用：</span>You’ve given me your cold. 你把感冒傳染給我了。

<span class="vocabulary">**communicate**</span> [kə'mju:nɪkeɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 常用被動式，人們之間（動物等之間）傳播疾病。是一個相對學術化的用詞：</span>The disease is communicated through dirty drinking water. 這種疾病透過不潔飲用水傳播。
             
<span class="vocabulary">**transfer**</span> [trænsˈfɜ:(r)]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi.（使）傳染疾病：</span>This disease is rarely transferred from mother to baby (= so that the baby has it as well as the mother). 這種疾病很少由母親傳給嬰兒。/ The chances of the disease being transferred to humans is extremely remote. 該疾病傳播到人類身上的機率微乎其微。/ Can the disease be transferred across species? 這種疾病能跨物種傳播嗎？         

<span class="vocabulary">**transmit**</span> [trænsˈmɪt; trænz-]
<span class="definition">vt. 傳播或傳染疾病：</span>mosquitoes that transmit disease to humans 傳染疾病給人類的蚊子 / sexually transmitted diseases 性傳播疾病 / There was no danger of transmitting the infection through operations. 不存在透過手術傳播這種傳染病的風險。           
           
<span class="vocabulary">**transmission**</span> [trænsˈmɪʃn; trænz-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式] 疾病的傳播：</span>genetic, oral, sexual transmission 遺傳傳遞；口傳播；性傳播 / human-to-human, person-to-person, mother-to-child 人際間傳播；母嬰傳播 / disease, HIV, malaria, STD transmission 疾病傳播；艾滋病病毒傳播；瘧疾傳播；性病傳播 The virus's usual transmission route is by sneezing.這種病毒通常是透過打噴嚏傳播的。/ HIV transmission among homosexual men 男同性戀者之間艾滋病病毒的傳播 / HIV transmission through blood transfusion 輸血造成的艾滋病病毒傳播 / There is a risk of transmission of the virus between hypodermic users. 接受皮下注射者有相互傳染病毒的可能。/ transmission between patients 病人間的傳染

<span class="vocabulary">**contagious**</span> [kənˈteɪdʒəs]
<span class="definition">adj. 1（疾病）透過接觸傳染的：</span>Scarlet fever is highly contagious. 猩紅熱的接觸傳染性很強。/ The new disease proved contagious. 這種新的疾病證明具有接觸傳染性。/ a highly contagious disease of the lungs. 極具傳染性的肺病 <span class="definition">2 [名詞前不常用]（人）患有接觸性傳染病的：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**epidemic**</span> [ˌepɪˈdemɪk]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 疾病的流行：</span>the outbreak of a flu epidemic 流感的爆發 / A flu epidemic is sweeping through Moscow. 一場流感正席捲莫斯科。/ Over fifty people died during the flu epidemic last winter. 在去年冬天的一場流感中有五十多人喪生。/ an epidemic of cholera 霍亂的流行 / an epidemic of measles 麻疹的流行 / a killer epidemic of yellow fever 致命的黃熱病的流行 / major epidemic 重大疫情 / global, national, worldwide epidemic 全球性流行病；全國性流行病；世界性流行病 / foot-and-mouth, typhoid epidemic 口蹄疫流行病；傷寒流行病 / A typhus epidemic struck in the winter of 1919-20. 1919年至1920年冬天爆發了斑疹傷寒。

<span class="vocabulary">**pandemic**</span> [pænˈdemɪk]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 疾病在全國或全球範圍內的流行，即大流行：</span>They feared a new cholera pandemic. 他們擔心新一輪的霍亂大流行。/ One pandemic of Spanish flu took nearly 22 million lives worldwide. 西班牙流感的大爆發奪去了全球近2,200萬人的生命。<span class="definition">adj. 大流行病的：</span>a pandemic disease 大範圍流行的疾病
          
<span class="vocabulary">**endemic**</span> [enˈdemɪk]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (in/to…)（疾病）在某地的人群中流行的：</span>Malaria is endemic in many hot countries. 瘧疾是許多氣候炎熱國家的流行病。
