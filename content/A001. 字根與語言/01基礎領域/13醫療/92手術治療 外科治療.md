☀ <span class="category">**手術治療 外科治療：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**operate**</span> ['ɒpəreɪt] 
<span class="definition">vi. operate (on sb) (for sth) 醫療上可指動手術：</span>to operate on his heart 給他的心臟動手術 / to operate on him for a brain aneurysm 為他做腦動脈瘤手術

<span class="vocabulary">**operation**</span> [͵ɒpə'reɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] operation (on sb) (for sth) / operation (to do sth) 手術。是此義的常規用詞，極為常用：</span>to recover from an operation 從手術中恢復過來 / an operation for a kidney problem 腎病手術 / an operation on his lung to remove a tumour 為他做肺部腫瘤切除手術 / You need to have an operation. 你需要動手術。/ major operation 大手術 / minor, small operation 小手術 / surgical, transplant operation 外科手術；移植手術 / heart operation 心臟手術 / the surgeon performing the operation 動手術的外科醫生
           
<span class="vocabulary">**operative**</span> [ˈɒpərətɪv; 美 ˈɑ:pərətɪv; -reɪt-]
<span class="definition">adj. [醫學] [只用於名詞前] 手術的：</span>operative treatment 手術治療
 
<span class="vocabulary">**procedure**</span> [prə'si:dӡə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 醫學上可指程式或手術。是一個概括性用詞，表示手術時不太常用：</span>medical procedure 醫療程式 / to perform a routine surgical procedure 做一臺常規的外科手術 / diagnostic, screening procedure 診斷程式；篩查程式 / cosmetic procedure 美容手術 / invasive procedure 侵入性手術 / abortion, transplant procedure 墮胎手術；移植手術
                    
<span class="vocabulary">**surgery**</span> [ˈsɜ:dʒəri; 美 ˈsɜ:rdʒ-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 外科手術；外科學：</span>major, radical surgery 大手術；根治性的手術 / minor surgery 小手術 / keyhole, laser surgery 微創手術；鐳射手術 / invasive surgery 開刀手術 / One of his specialities is minimally invasive surgery. 微創手術是他的專長之一。/ elective, emergency, experimental, exploratory surgery 非必需手術；緊急手術；實驗性手術；診察性手術 / They discussed whether patients should have to pay for all elective surgery. 他們對病人是否應該支付選擇性手術的全部費用進行了討論。/ day, out-patient（美）surgery 門診手術 / cosmetic, plastic, reconstructive surgery 整容手術；整形手術；修復手術 / cataract, eye surgery 白內障手術；眼部手術 / bypass, cardiac, heart, open-heart surgery 心臟搭橋手術；心臟外科手術；心臟直視手術 / to undergo coronary bypass surgery 接受冠狀動脈搭橋手術 / abdominal, brain, gastric, orthopaedic/orthopedic, paediatric/pediatric surgery 腹部手術；腦部手術；胃部手術；整形手術；兒科手術 / elbow, hip, knee 肘部手術；髖部手術；膝部手術 / patients recovering from hip replacement surgery 髖關節置換手術後正在康復的病人 / general surgery 普通外科手術 / She had minor surgery on her knee. 她的膝蓋動過一次小手術。/ She had three surgeries over ten days.（美）十天當中她做了三次手術。/ He has been performing heart surgery for ten years. 他為病人做心臟手術已有十年之久。/ She felt weak for six months after undergoing major abdominal. 腹部動了大手術後，她有六個月時間一直感到身體虛弱。/ emergency surgery for appendicitis 闌尾炎緊急手術 / She had surgery on her left knee. 她左膝蓋動了手術。/ After the accident, she needed extensive plastic surgery to her face. 事故後她臉部需要做大面積的整形手術。/ His father has just recovered from heart surgery. 他父親剛剛從心臟外科手術中康復過來。

<span class="vocabulary">**anatomy**</span> [əˈnætəmi]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 解剖：</span>He had worked extensively on the anatomy of living animals. 他在活體解剖方面經驗豐富。

<span class="vocabulary">**implant**</span> [ɪmˈplɑ:nt; 美 -ˈplænt]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ (sth) (in/into sth) 在醫療上將某事物（通常是人造事物）植入人體部位（通常透過手術來植入）：</span>an electrode implanted into the brain 植入大腦中的電極 / Doctors in Arizona say they have implanted an artificial heart into a 46-year-old woman. 亞利桑那州的醫生稱他們已經把一顆人造心臟移植到一名 46 歲婦女的體內。<span class="definition">vi. ~ (sth) (in/into sth)（卵子或胚胎）被移植到（人或動物體內以讓其發育）：</span>Two days later, they implanted the fertilized eggs back inside me. 兩天以後,他們把受精卵重新植入了我的體內。/ Non-identical twins are the result of two fertilised eggs implanting in the uterus at the same time. 非同卵雙生子是兩個受精卵同時在子宮內著床的結果。/ Two or three embryos are implanted into the woman's body. 這個婦女體內已經移植了兩三個胚胎。<span class="definition">n. [C] 透過手術而植入人體中的移植物、植入物：</span>silicone breast implants 矽酮乳房填充物 / A woman can choose to have breast implants. 女人可以選擇進行隆胸。
                    
<span class="vocabulary">**transplant**</span> [trænsˈplɑ:nt; trænz-; 美 -ˈplænt]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (from sb/sth) (into sb/sth) 移植（人或動物的器官、皮膚等）：</span>Surgeons have successfully transplanted a liver into a four-year-old boy. 外科醫生成功地給一個四歲的男孩移植了肝臟。/ Patients often reject transplanted organs. 患者經常排斥移植的器官。/ The operation to transplant a kidney is now fairly routine. 腎臟移植手術如今已相當常見。/ transplanted organs such as hearts and kidneys 類似心臟、腎臟這樣的移植器官 / Organs are transplanted from donors into patients who need them. 器官由供者移植到有需求的病人體內。<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U]（器官等的）移植：</span>organ transplant 器官移植 / bone-marrow, heart, kidney, liver, lung, stem-cell transplant 骨髓移植；心臟移植；腎臟移植；肝臟移植；肺移植；幹細胞移植 / transplant operation, transplant surgery 移植手術；移植外科手術 / transplant surgeon 移植外科醫生 / transplant team 移植團隊 / transplant candidate, transplant patient, transplant recipient 器官移植候選人；器官移植病人；器官移植受者 / to have a heart transplant 接受心臟移植 / He was recovering from a heart transplant operation. 他做了心臟移植手術，正在康復。/ the controversy over the sale of human organs for transplant 出售移植用人體器官引起的爭議 / a shortage of suitable kidneys for transplant 適合移植的腎臟的短缺 <span class="definition">2 [C] 移植所用的器官：</span>There is always a chance that the body will reject the transplant. 身體總是有排斥移植器官的可能。/ A suitable transplant donor has been found. 找到了一名合適的器官移植供者
                  
<span class="vocabulary">**graft**</span> [grɑ:ft; 美 græft]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 移植的皮膚（或骨骼等）；（皮膚或骨骼的）移植：</span>I am having a skin graft on my arm soon. 我馬上就要接受手臂的皮膚移植手術。/ Doctors are hoping to do a bone graft to repair the damaged bone. 醫生希望進行接骨手術來修復受損的骨骼。/ Linda had to undergo four skin grafts. 琳達必須接受四次植皮手術。/ If the skin graft takes, surgeons will do another operation a few weeks later. 如果皮膚移植成功，幾周後外科醫生就會施行另一項手術。<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (onto/to/into sth) / ~ sth (on) (from sth) 移植（皮膚、骨骼等）：</span>newly grafted tissue 新移植的組織 / New skin had to be grafted on from his back. 需從他的背部移植新皮膚。/ The top layer of skin has to be grafted onto the burns. 必須將表層皮膚移植到燙傷處。

<span class="vocabulary">**transfer**</span> [trænsˈfɜ:(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 生物學上的移植：</span>embryo, gene transfer 胚胎移植；基因移植      

<span class="vocabulary">**bypass**</span> [ˈbaɪpɑ:s]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（給心臟接旁通管的）分流術、搭橋術；旁通管：</span>coronary bypass surgery 冠狀動脈搭橋術 / heart bypass surgery 心臟搭橋手術 / a triple bypass operation 接三通管的手術 <span class="definition">vt. 對（動脈等）作分流術；為…作搭橋術：</span>Small veins are removed from the leg and used to bypass the blocked up stretch of coronary arteries. 從腿部取出一些細小血管，用來對堵塞的冠狀動脈進行分流。

<span class="vocabulary">**pump**</span> [pʌmp] 
<span class="definition">vt. 洗胃，常用被動式：</span>to have his stomach pumped 去洗他的胃 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 透過用泵或泵式裝置讓液體、氣體等朝某個方向流動：</span>The heart pumps blood around the body. 心臟把血液輸送到全身。

<span class="vocabulary">**abortion**</span> [ə'bɔ:ʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 墮胎手術，相當於termination：</span>She decided to have an abortion. 她決定墮胎。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**termination**</span> [ˌtɜ:mɪˈneɪʃn; 美 ˌtɜ:rm-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [醫學] 終止妊娠（手術）：</span>pregnancy termination 妊娠終止 / She chose to have an early termination. 她選擇了早期終止妊娠。/ a termination of（a）pregnancy 終止妊娠
 
<span class="vocabulary">**tuck**</span> [tʌk]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 去贅皮手術；減肥手術：</span>She'd undergone 13 operations, including a tummy tuck. 她已經做了13次手術，包括一次腹部瘦身整形手術。

<span class="vocabulary">**bleed**</span> [bli:d] 
<span class="definition">vt.（舊時所用的）透過放血來給人治病：</span>瞭解即可 

<span class="vocabulary">**section**</span> ['sekʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 醫學上可指手術中的切開或切斷：</span>The surgeon performed a section on the vein. 外科醫生施行了靜脈切開手術。<span class="definition">vt. 醫學上可指切開或切斷身體組織：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**lance**</span> [lɑ:ns; 美 læns]
<span class="definition">vt. 用刀切開身體的感染部位以放出膿液：</span>to lance an abscess 切開膿腫 / It is a painful experience having the boil lanced. 把癤子切開是很痛的。

<span class="vocabulary">**set**</span> [set] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 為斷了的骨頭復位，接骨：</span>to set the broken leg 接上腿上的斷骨
           
<span class="vocabulary">**scale**</span> [skeɪl]
<span class="definition">vt. 刮除牙結石：</span>The dentist scaled and polished my teeth. 牙醫為我刮除牙石，拋光了牙齒。

<span class="vocabulary">**cap**</span> [kæp] 
<span class="definition">vt. 常用被動式，在牙齒外面包上一層人工材料（使其更好看），相當於crown：</span>He’s had his front teeth capped. 他包了門牙。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**crown**</span> [kraʊn]
<span class="definition">vt.（在牙齒上）鑲人造牙冠。相當於cap：</span>I've had one of my teeth crowned. 我的一顆牙齒鑲了假齒冠。

<span class="vocabulary">**stitch**</span> [stɪtʃ]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (up) 縫合傷口：</span>The cut will need to be stitched. 這傷口需要縫合。/ Jill washed and stitched the wound. 吉爾清洗並縫合了傷口。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**cosmetic**</span> [kɒzˈmetɪk; 美 kɑ:z-]
<span class="definition">adj. 整容的：</span>cosmetic surgery 整容外科 / cosmetic dental work 牙齒整形

· chip可指（為辨認而在狗或其他動物的皮下）植入微晶片。
