<span class="vocabulary">**spit**</span> [spɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt. spit sth (out)（從口中）吐出液體、食物等：</span>to spit out the gum 吐掉口香糖 / to spit blood 吐血 / to spit a mouthful of food out 吐出一口食物 <span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] 將液體或食物從口中吐出的行為：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**blow**</span> [bləʊ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 擤鼻子：</span>to blow one’s nose 擤鼻子 <span class="definition">n. [C] 作為吹或擤等動作的名詞：</span>Give your nose a good blow. 把你的鼻子擤乾淨。
