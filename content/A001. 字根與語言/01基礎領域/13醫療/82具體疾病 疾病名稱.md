☀ <span class="category">**具體疾病 疾病名稱：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**cold**</span> [kəʊld] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 感冒：</span>bad cold 重感冒 / mild, slight cold 輕微感冒 / common cold 感冒 / chest, head cold 支氣管炎；鼻傷風 / to catch a cold 得感冒
           
<span class="vocabulary">**chill**</span> [tʃɪl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 由溼冷引起的疾病，常引起發燒和顫抖，如著涼、受寒：</span>He caught a chill while performing at a rain-soaked open-air venue. 他在大雨瓢潑的露天場地演出時受了寒。/ I caught a nasty chill after my swim last week. 我上週游泳後得了嚴重的風寒。

<span class="vocabulary">**flu**</span> [flu:] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 流行性感冒，即流感：</span>I got the flu. 我得了流感。/ mild flu 輕度流感 / bad flu 嚴重流感 / avian, bird flu 禽流感 / flu virus 流感病毒 / flu symptoms 流感症狀 / flu victim 流感患者 / flu season 流感高發季節

<span class="vocabulary">**influenza**</span> [ˌɪnfluˈenzə]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式] 流行性感冒。相當於flu：</span>avian, human influenza 禽流感；人流感 / influenza epidemic, influenza outbreak, influenza pandemic 流感流行；流行爆發；流行大流行 / influenza season（尤美）流感季節 / influenza virus 流感病毒 / influenza infection 流感感染/ influenza symptoms 流感症狀 / influenza strain 流感毒株 / the influenza strain known as H7 被稱作H7的流感毒株 / influenza vaccine 流感疫苗 / influenza immunization, influenza vaccination 流感免疫；流感疫苗接種

<span class="vocabulary">**AIDS**</span> [eɪdz] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] [英常Aids] 艾滋病（全稱為Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome 即獲得性免疫缺陷綜合徵），英通常用Aids：</span>Six cases of AIDS have been reported. 已報告了六例艾滋病。/ AIDS patient 艾滋病病人 / AIDS virus 艾滋病病毒 / the spread of AIDS 艾滋病的傳播

<span class="vocabulary">**slim**</span> [slɪm] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 表示艾滋病的非洲用詞：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**cancer**</span> ['kænsə] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 癌症：</span>lung cancer 肺癌 / cancer of the stomach 胃癌 / early-state cancer 早期癌症 / terminal cancer 晚期癌症 / cancer cell 癌細胞 / cancer patient 癌症病人 / cancer specialist 癌症專家 / cancer prevention 癌症預防 / cancer drug 抗癌藥物 / The cancer has spread to his brain. 癌症已擴散到了他的大腦。
