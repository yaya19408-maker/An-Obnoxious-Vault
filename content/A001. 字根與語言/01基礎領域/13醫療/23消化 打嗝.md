☀ <span class="category">**消化 打嗝：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**digest**</span> [daɪ'dʒest] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 消化（食物）：</span>to allow food to digest 讓食物消化 / to digest meat 消化肉類 / His body can’t digest fat. 他的身體消化不了脂肪。/ Cheese is very difficult to digest. 乳酪很難消化。

<span class="vocabulary">**digestion**</span> [daɪˈdʒestʃən; dɪ-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U]（對食物的）消化：</span>No liquids are served with meals because they interfere with digestion. 上飯菜時不要同時上湯水或飲品，因為這會影響消化。/ the digestion of fats 對脂肪的消化 / Peppermint aids digestion.（胡椒）薄荷有助於消化。<span class="definition">2 [C, usually sing.]（對食物的）消化能力：</span>She has very poor digestion. 她消化很不好。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**hiccup**</span> [ˈhɪkʌp]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 嗝；呃逆：</span>small hiccup 小的打嗝聲 / She gave a loud hiccup. 她打了一個響嗝。<span class="definition">2 [pl.] (the) hiccups 可指一連串的打嗝：</span>I ate too quickly and got hiccups.（英）我吃得太快，結果不斷地打嗝。/ I ate too quickly and got the hiccups.（美）我吃得太快，結果不停地打嗝。/ He had hiccups.（英）他接連打嗝。/ He had the hiccups.（美）他接連打嗝。/ A young baby may frequently get a bout of hiccups during or soon after a feed. 小嬰兒進食過程中或剛剛進食後經常會打嗝。<span class="definition">vi. 打嗝：</span>She was still hiccupping from the egg she had swallowed whole. 剛才一口吞下整個雞蛋，她現在還在打嗝。

相關詞義延伸：
· digestion還可指身體的消化系統。
