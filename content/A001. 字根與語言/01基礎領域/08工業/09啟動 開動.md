☀ <span class="category">**啟動 開動：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**press**</span> [pres] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 按壓裝置的部位（以使其運轉）：</span>to press a button 按下按鈕 / to press a switch 按下開關 / to press any key 按任意鍵 / She pressed down hard on the gas pedal. 她用力踩下油門踏板。

<span class="vocabulary">**start**</span> [stɑːt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 啟動機器；（機器）啟動。是一個廣義用詞：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**trigger**</span> [ˈtrɪgə(r)]
<span class="definition">vt. 使一個裝置啟動：</span>to trigger an alarm 觸發警報器 / The bomb was triggered by a wire. 那顆炸彈是由一根導線引爆的。

<span class="vocabulary">**fire**</span> ['faɪə] 
<span class="definition">vi. 發動機點火，發動。其主語一般是發動機：</span>Engines of the plane began to fire. 這架飛機的發動機開始發動。

<span class="vocabulary">**roll**</span> [rəʊl] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（機器）開動，啟動或運轉；使機器開動，啟動或運轉。是一個形象化用法，不過不太常用：</span>The cameras weren’t rolling. 攝影機沒有啟動。/ Roll the cameras! 開拍！
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**rev**</span> [rev]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth) (up)（使）發動機快速旋轉：</span>The taxi driver revved up his engine. 計程車司機把發動機發動起來。/ The old bus was revving its engine, ready to start the journey back towards Madrid. 這輛舊巴士發動了引擎，準備啟程返回馬德里。/ I could hear the car revving outside. 我可以聽到外面汽車發動的聲音。/ The engine started, revved and the car jerked away down the hill. 引擎發動了，油門加大後車衝下了山坡。

周邊詞彙補充：
· stutter可指（車輛或發動機）突突突地吃力啟動；
· activate可指啟用一個裝置或化學過程。
