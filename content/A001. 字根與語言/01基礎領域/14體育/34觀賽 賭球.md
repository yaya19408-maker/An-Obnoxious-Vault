☀ <span class="category">**觀賽 賭球：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**gate**</span> [ɡeɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 出席體育比賽的觀眾人數：</span>Tonight’s game has attracted the largest gate of the season. 今晚比賽吸引的觀眾人數之多創本賽季之最。<span class="definition">2 [U] 也可用gate money，指一場體育比賽的門票收入總額：</span>Today’s gate will be given to charity. 今天的門票收入將捐給慈善事業。

<span class="vocabulary">**return**</span> [rɪ'tə:n] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 體育賽事的退票：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**support**</span> [sə'pɔ:t] 
<span class="definition">vt. [英] 喜歡某一支運動隊、觀看其比賽等，即支援：</span>Which team do you support? 你喜歡哪個隊？

<span class="vocabulary">**pool**</span> [pu:l] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] the pools也叫football pools，指英國的一種猜足球賽輸贏的賭博，即賭球，足球普爾：</span>He’s had a big win on the football pools. 他在賭球中贏了一大筆錢。/ He does the pools every week. 他每週都去賭足球普爾。

<span class="vocabulary">**back**</span> [bæk] 
<span class="definition">vt.（在比賽中）下賭注於某個人、某支隊伍或某匹馬等（即以金錢做賭注來賭其贏得比賽）：</span>He backed A to beat B by at least 1-0. 他下賭注A至少會以1-0擊敗B。/ I backed the winner and won eight hundred pounds. 我押對了賭注，贏了八百英鎊。
