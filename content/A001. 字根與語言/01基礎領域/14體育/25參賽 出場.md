☀ <span class="category">**參賽 出場：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**field**</span> [fi:ld] 
<span class="definition">vt. 讓一個候選人、團隊等代表你去參加一場比賽：</span>to field a football team 派出一支足球隊

<span class="vocabulary">**draft**</span> [drɑːft] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [美] 某些職業運動隊每年在大學生中選拔新隊員的體制：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**fence**</span> [fens] 
<span class="definition">vi. 參加擊劍運動：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**hurdle**</span> [ˈhɜ:dl; 美 ˈhɜ:rdl]
<span class="definition">vi. 參加跨欄賽：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**cap**</span> [kæp] 
<span class="definition">vt. [英] 常用被動式，（為某一體育比賽）將某人選入國家隊：</span>He has been capped for England. 他已入選英格蘭隊。

<span class="vocabulary">**outing**</span> ['aʊtɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 體育運動中可指參賽，比賽：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**race**</span> [reɪs] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. race (against) sb/sth 和…進行速度比賽；參加速度比賽：</span>Who will you be racing against in the next round? 下一輪你和誰比賽？<span class="definition">vt. 讓動物或交通工具參加速度比賽：</span>to race horses 賽馬 / to race motorbikes 賽摩托車

<span class="vocabulary">**play**</span> [pleɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. play (sth) (with/against) / play sb (at sth) 參加比賽，與…比賽：</span>She has played in every game this season. 她參加了本賽季所有的比賽。/ to play basketball 打籃球 / to play chess 下棋 / to play cards 打撲克 / I’ve never played John at tennis. 我從來沒有同約翰打過網球。/ He plays for the Chicago Bears. 他為芝加哥熊隊效力。<span class="definition">vt. 讓某人加入運動隊，或派某人出場：</span>The coach played him on the wing. 教練讓他打邊鋒。

<span class="vocabulary">**compete**</span> [kəm'pi:t] 
<span class="definition">vi. compete (in sth) (against sb) 參加體育比賽：</span>I will be competing in the 100-metre race. 我將參加100米比賽。

<span class="vocabulary">**fight**</span> [faɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. fight sb/sth (for sth) 參加體育競賽：</span>The second half was fiercely fought. 下半場比賽非常激烈。<span class="definition">vi. 參加拳擊比賽：</span>Doctors fear he may never fight again. 醫生認為他可能再也不能重返拳壇了。

<span class="vocabulary">**defend**</span> [dɪ'fend] 
<span class="definition">vt. 參加比賽或競爭等以保住頭銜、席位等：</span>He is defending champion. 他在參加衛冕賽。/ to defend his title 捍衛他的冠軍頭銜

周邊詞彙補充：
· swim可指參加游泳比賽；
· substitute可指在體育比賽中替補出場。
