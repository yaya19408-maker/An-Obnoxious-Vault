<span class="definition">n. [C] 國內的重大政治、軍事性事變；兩國間的分歧，常涉及軍事衝突：</span>domestic incident 國內事件 / international incident 國際事件 / racial incident 種族事件 / border incident 邊境事件 / diplomatic incident 外交事件 / security incident 安全事件

<span class="vocabulary">**march**</span> [mɑːtʃ]  
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 為了抗議或示威而遊行。與demonstrate近義：</span>to march against the war 遊行示威反對戰爭 / to march for peace 為爭取和平而遊行 / The demonstrators marched on the British embassy. 示威者向英國大使館進發。<span class="definition">n. <span class="vocabulary">**demonstrate**</span> [ˈdemənstreɪt]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (against sth) / ~ (in favour/support of sth) 集會示威、遊行示威（以抗議或支援…）：</span>students demonstrating against the war 舉行反戰示威遊行的學生 / They are demonstrating in favour of free higher education. 他們舉行示威遊行，要求實行免費高等教育。/ 30,000 angry farmers demonstrated against possible cuts in subsidies. 3萬名憤怒的農場主示威抗議可能對農產品補貼進行的削減。/ In the cities vast crowds have been demonstrating for change. 在城市裡，大批的人群舉行示威遊行，要求進行變革。/ Campaigners were demonstrating against the slaughter of dolphins. 活動人士示威抗議宰殺海豚。/ Their objective was to demonstrate peacefully for civil rights. 他們的目標是為公民權利而進行和平示威。

<span class="vocabulary">**demonstration**</span> [ˌdemənˈstreɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤其在英式英語中，也可用非正式的demo）~ (against sb/sth) （為抗議或支援某人或某事所進行的）集會示威、遊行示威：</span>mass demonstrations against cuts in the health service 反對公共醫療衛生服務經費削減的群眾示威遊行 / The president's decision provoked public demonstrations. 總統的決定引發了公眾的示威。/ street demonstration 街頭示威 / spontaneous demonstration 自發示威 / Thousands gathered for a peaceful demonstration. 數千人聚在一起舉行和平示威。/ violent demonstration 暴力示威 / a protest demonstration against the war 反戰抗議示威 / student demonstration 學生示威 / political demonstration 政治示威 / anti-war, anti-government, pro-democracy demonstration 反戰示威；反政府示威；親民主示威 / Taxi drivers staged a demonstration against the new law. 計程車司機舉行示威反對新法律。/ to call off demonstration（尤英）取消示威 / The demonstration was called off at the last minute. 示威在臨舉行的一刻取消了。/ to disperse demonstration（尤英）驅散示威人情 / Police in riot gear dispersed the demonstration. 防暴警察驅散了示威人群。/ The government does not wish to provoke further demonstrations. 政府不希望再激起示威活動。/ demonstrations calling for an end to sanctions 呼籲停止制裁的示威 / The visiting president was greeted by hostile demonstrations. 到訪的總統遭遇了充滿敵意的示威。/ police intervention at demonstrations 警方對示威的干涉 / Hundreds were arrested during demonstrations in the capital. 發生在首都的示威遊行中幾百人被捕。/ demonstrations in protest at the arrests（英，美式將at改為of）反對拘捕的示威 / Riot police broke up a demonstration by students. 防暴警察驅散了示威的學生。/ student demonstrations in favour of a multi-party system 支援多黨制的學生示威 / mass demonstrations in support of the exiled leader 支援流亡領導人的群眾集會
           
<span class="vocabulary">**sit-in**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [C]（建築物內的）靜坐罷工，靜坐示威：</span>The campaigners held a sit-in outside the Supreme Court. 運動參與者在最高法院門外舉行了靜坐示威。

<span class="vocabulary">**protest**</span> [ˈprəʊtest; 美 ˈproʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. a storm of protest. 這個宣告引起了一場抗議風潮。/ The Samoan team lodged a formal protest against the decision. 薩摩亞隊針對這項決定提出了正式抗議。/ The workers staged a protest against the proposed changes in their contracts. 工人們進行抗議，反對擬議中的對他們合同的修改。/ The government has made an official protest. 政府已經提出了正式抗議。/ protest group, protest movement 抗議組織；抗議運動 / protest demonstration, protest march, protest meeting, protest rally 抗議示威；抗議遊行；抗議會議；抗議集會 / protest strike（尤英）罷工抗議 / protest petition（尤英）抗議請願 / protest vote 抗議投票 / protest song 抗議歌曲 / Prisoners shouted and hurled slates in protest. 囚犯們大聲呼喊並投擲石塊表示抗議。/ The crowd dispersed 該黨對選舉進行了抵制，以抗議所謂的投票舞弊。/ The unions called a two-hour strike in protest at the railway authority's announcement. 各工會號召罷工兩小時，以對鐵路當局所發通告表示抗議。/ The director resigned in protest at the decision. 主任辭職以示抗議這項決定。/ Winters quit the company board in protest of her plans. 溫特斯退出了公司董事會，以表示對她的計劃的抗議。/ The building work will go ahead, despite protests from local residents. 儘管當地居民反對，建築工程將照樣進行。/ The riot 學生們走上街頭，抗議這項決定。/ Groups of women took to the streets to protest against the arrests. 成群結隊的婦女上街抗議逮捕行動。/ The students were protesting at overcrowding in the university hostels. 學生們在抗議大學宿舍過於擁擠。/ The the right to protest peacefully 和平抗議的權利 / When he tried to protest, she insisted. 他試圖抗議，但她卻堅持這樣做。/ Crowds gathered to protest about the police violence. 人群聚集起來抗議警察的暴力。/ Many people protested over the <span class="vocabulary">**outcry**</span> [ˈaʊtkraɪ]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] ~ (at/over/against sth) 人們在公共場合表達憤怒或強烈抗議時的一種反應，即抗議的怒吼：</span>an outcry over the proposed change 對擬議的改革所發出的強烈抗議 / The new tax provoked a public outcry. 新稅項引起了公眾的強烈抗議。/ There was outcry at the judge's statement. 法官的陳詞引起一片譁然。/ There was public outcry from those opposed to abortion. 反墮胎人士公開大聲抗議。/ The killing caused an international outcry. 這起謀殺引起了國際社會的強烈抗議。/ His comments raised an outcry from religious leaders.（raise尤為美式用法）他的評論激起了宗教領袖們的強烈抗議。/ an immediate outcry from workers over pay reductions 工人們對降薪的即刻抗議

<span class="vocabulary">**vigil**</span> [ˈvɪdʒɪl]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 夜間抗議：</span>candlelight（尤美）, candlelit（尤英）vigil 點燭光的守夜抗議 / Protesters held a candlelight/candlelit vigil against the war. 抗議者舉行了燭光守夜以反對戰爭。/ a silent vigil outside the presidential palace 總統府外無聲的守夜抗議 / prayer vigil 守夜祈禱 / peace vigil 為和平守夜祈禱 / a vigil for the murdered politician 為遭謀殺的從政者舉行的靜默抗議

<span class="vocabulary">**picket**</span> [ˈpɪkɪt]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 站在某處（如工作地點）對某事表示抗議：</span>100 union members and supporters picketed outside. 100名工會成員和支持者在外頭抗議。           

<span class="vocabulary">**remonstrate**</span> [ˈremənstreɪt; 美 rɪˈmɑ:nstreɪt]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. [正式] ~ (with sb) (about sth) 對…表示抗議或抱怨：</span>They remonstrated with the official about the decision. 他們就這一決定向這位官員提出了抗議。/ He remonstrated with the referee. 他向裁判抗議。/ I jumped in the car and went to remonstrate. 我跳進汽車去提出抗議。           

<span class="vocabulary">**rally**</span> [ˈræli]
<span class="definition">n. <span class="vocabulary">**assembly**</span> [əˈsembli]
<span class="definition">n. <span class="vocabulary">**gathering**</span> [ˈgæðərɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 人們為某一目的進行的集會：</span>a gathering of religious leaders 宗教領袖的集會 / big, large gathering 大型集會 / public gathering 公共集會 / informal gathering 非正式集會 / formal gathering 正式集會 / political, religious gathering 政治集會；宗教集會 / international, national gathering 國際性集會；全國性集會 / annual gathering 年度集會 / In some cultures, women are not allowed to attend public gatherings. 在某些文化中，婦女不得參加公共集會。/ The president addressed a gathering of local government officials. 總統在當地政府官員的一次集會上演講。
