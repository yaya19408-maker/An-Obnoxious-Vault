<span class="vocabulary">**democratic**</span> [ˌdeməˈkrætɪk]
<span class="definition">adj. 1（國家、政府、制度等）民主的；民主制度的；民主政體的：</span>a democratic country 民主國家 / a democratic system 民主制度 / democratic government 民主政府 / Bolivia returned to democratic rule in 1982, after a series of military governments. 在經歷了數屆軍人政府後，玻利維亞於 1982 年恢復了民主政權。/ the country's first democratic elections 該國的首次民主選舉 / There have been major changes in the constitution, but the system remains democratic. 憲法有了重大變更，但實行的仍然是民主制度。/ They have a fairly democratic form of government. 他們有一個比較民主的政府。<span class="definition">2（社會或組織機構的組織或管理原則）基於民主精神的、有民主作風的：</span>democratic reforms 民主改革 / Education is the basis of a democratic society. 教育是民主社會的基礎。/ He called for widespread changes to make the armed forces more democratic and less expensive. 他呼籲進行廣泛的改革，使武裝部隊作風更加民主，開支更加儉省。/ democratic participation 民主參與 / I think it was a reasonably democratic decision. 我認為這是一個相當民主的決定。<span class="definition">3 Democratic（縮寫為Dem.或D）（用於政黨名稱中）民主的：</span>the Democratic senator from Oregon（美國）俄勒岡州的民主黨參議員 / the Social Democratic Party 社會民主黨 / the People's Democratic Party of Afghanistan 阿富汗人民民主黨

<span class="vocabulary">**democracy**</span> [dɪˈmɒkrəsi; 美 -ˈmɑ:k-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 民主政體、民主制度：</span>political democracy 政治民主 / constitutional, parliamentary, participatory, representative democracy 立憲民主；議會民主；參與式民主；代議制民主 / multi-party democracy 多黨民主 / Western democracy 西方民主 / people who believe in true democracy 相信有真正民主的人們 / The military regime has promised to restore democracy soon. 軍事政權承諾將很快恢復民主。/ a pro-democracy demonstration in the capital 首都的親民主示威 / the road to democracy 通往民主之路（或稱民主程序）/ the principles of democracy 民主原則 / the spread of democracy in Eastern Europe 民主制度在東歐的傳播 <span class="definition">2 [C] 民主國家：</span>Western democracies 西方民主國家 / We live in a multi-party democracy. 我們生活在一個多黨制民主國家。/ I thought we were supposed to be living in a democracy. 我還以為我們應該是生活在一個民主國家裡。/ The new democracies face tough challenges. 這些新興的民主國家面臨著嚴峻挑戰。<span class="definition">3 [U]（公司、組織機構等的內部的）民主精神、民主權利、民主管理制度、民主作風：</span>the fight for justice and democracy 為正義和民主的鬥爭 / the union's emphasis on industrial democracy 該工會對行業民主的強調

<span class="vocabulary">**anarchy**</span> [ˈænəki; 美 ˈænərki]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 一個國家的無政府、無秩序或失控狀態：</span>The overthrow of the military regime was followed by a period of anarchy. 軍事統治政權被推翻以後，接著是一段時期的無政府狀態。/ Civil war and famine sent the nation plunging into anarchy. 內戰和饑荒使該國陷入了無政府狀態。/ In June, 1960, the Congo was plunged into five years of civil war and political anarchy. 1960年的六月，剛果陷入了五年的內戰和政治上的無政府狀態。/ the lawless days of mob rule and anarchy 暴民統治和無政府狀態的無法無天的時代 / a collapse into anarchy 陷入無政府狀態 / The country is in a perpetual state of anarchy. 該國長期處於無政府狀態。
