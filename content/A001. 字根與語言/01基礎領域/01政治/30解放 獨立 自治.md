☀ <span class="category">**解放 獨立 自治：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**liberation**</span> [͵lɪbə'reɪʃn]  
<span class="definition">n. [C] 使一個國家或人從另一人的控制中獲得自由，即解放：</span>a war of liberation 解放戰爭 / national liberation 民族解放 / liberation movement 解放運動 / the liberation of the capital by allied forces 盟軍解放首都 / the struggle for liberation from colonial rule 爭取擺脫殖民統治的解放鬥爭
           
<span class="vocabulary">**emancipate**</span> [ɪˈmænsɪpeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] ~ sb (from sth) 解放某人，尤指將其從法律、政治或社會束縛中解放出來：</span>Slaves were not emancipated until 1863 in the United States. 美國奴隸直到1863年才獲得自由。/ That war preserved the Union and emancipated the slaves 那場戰爭保住了聯邦政府，並解放了奴隸。/ Catholics were emancipated in 1792. 天主教徒在1792年獲得瞭解放。           

<span class="vocabulary">**emancipation**</span> [ɪˌmænsɪˈpeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 解放，尤指使某人不受法律、政治或社會中不好的或不公平的限制的束縛：</span>the emancipation of slaves 奴隸的解放

<span class="vocabulary">**independence**</span> [͵ɪndɪ'pendəns]  
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] independence (from sb/sth) 表示一國從其它國家的政治統治中獲得的獨立：</span>local, national independence 地方的獨立；國家的獨立 / to achieve independence 獲得獨立 / independence movement 獨立運動 / the American War of Independence 美國獨立戰爭 <span class="definition">2 [U] 一國從其它國家的政治統治中獲得獨立的時間：</span>independence celebrations 獨立紀念慶典 / independence day 獨立紀念日 / the first elections since independence 獨立之後的第一次選舉 / Namibia became a full member of the UN at independence. 奈米比亞在取得獨立之時成為聯合國的正式成員。

<span class="vocabulary">**independent**</span> [͵ɪndɪ'pendənt]  
<span class="definition">adj. independent (from/of sth) 指國家擁有自己的政府的，即獨立的、自治的：</span>an independent country 一個獨立的國家 / The country was declared independent. 該國宣佈獨立。

<span class="vocabulary">**autonomous**</span> [ɔ:'tɒnəməs]  
<span class="definition">adj.（一個國家、地區或組織）自治的。與independent相反：</span>autonomous republic, autonomous region 自治共和國；自治區 / Higher education is relatively autonomous from the government. 高等教育相對來說不受政府控制。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**autonomy**</span> [ɔ:ˈtɒnəmi; 美 ɔ:ˈtɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（一個國家、地區或組織機構的）自治；自治權；自主權：</span>a campaign in Wales for greater autonomy 威爾士爭取更大自治權的運動 / Activists stepped up their demands for local autonomy last month. 上個月激進分子對地方自治的呼聲更高了。/ The cantons and communes of Switzerland have preserved their autonomy. 瑞士的州和市鎮仍然享有自治權。/ The Act granted limited autonomy to the republics. 該法案賦予各共和國的自治權是有限的。/ local, national, regional autonomy 地方自治；國家自治；區域自治

<span class="vocabulary">**commonwealth**</span> [ˈkɒmənwelθ; 美 ˈkɑ:m-]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [美]（與美國聯絡緊密的）自治政區：</span>Puerto Rico remains a US commonwealth, not a state. 波多黎各至今仍然是美國的一個自治政區，而不是一個州。          

<span class="vocabulary">**sovereignty**</span> [ˈsɒvrənti; 美 ˈsɑ:v-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] [正式] ~ (over sth) 對一個國家的絕對控制權，即主權、最高統治權：</span>absolute, full, unlimited 絕對主權；無限主權 / Demonstrators demanded full sovereignty for the self-proclaimed republic. 示威者要求宣佈獨立的共和國擁有絕對主權。/ limited sovereignty 有限的主權 / joint, shared sovereignty（均尤英）聯合主權；共享主權 / national, popular sovereignty 國家主權；人民主權論 / British sovereignty 英國的主權 / economic, legal, parliamentary（尤英）, political, state, territorial sovereignty 經濟主權；法律主權；議會主權；政治主權；國家主權；領土主權 / Native American tribal sovereignty 美洲印第安人的部落主權 / China exercises sovereignty over Hong Kong. 中國對香港行使主權。/ In 1949 the Dutch ceded sovereignty of the Dutch East Indies to the Indonesian Republic. 1949年荷蘭人把荷屬東印度群島的主權移交給了印度尼西亞共和國。/ We must respect the sovereignty of member states. 我們必須尊重成員國的主權。/ Sovereignty resides with the people. 主權屬於人民。/ The treaty gave Edward III sovereignty over Calais and the whole of Aquitaine. 這一條約授予愛德華三世對加來和整個阿基坦大區的主權。/ the handover of sovereignty to the new government 主權移交新政府 / fears about the transfer of sovereignty to the European Union 對主權移交歐盟的擔憂 / Politicians were alarmed over the potential loss of national sovereignty. 政治家們擔心國家主權有可能喪失。/ the partial loss of sovereignty to supranational institutions 部分主權落入超國家組織手中 / Two countries have a claim to sovereignty over the islands. 兩個國家聲稱對這些島嶼擁有主權。/ the sovereignty of Parliament（尤英）議會的主權 / the sovereignty of the people 人們的主權 / the idea of consumer sovereignty（比喻用法）消費者至上的觀念 <span class="definition">2 [U] [正式] 一個國家的獨立自主：</span>The declaration proclaimed the full sovereignty of the republic. 份宣言宣告這個共和國完全獨立自主。
