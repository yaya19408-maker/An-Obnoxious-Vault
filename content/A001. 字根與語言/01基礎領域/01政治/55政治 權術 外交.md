☀ <span class="category">**政治 權術：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**politics**</span> ['pɒlɪtɪks]  
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 政治活動，政治：</span>international politics 國際政治 / the country’s internal politics 國家的內政 / to be active in local politics 活躍於地方政壇 / the world of politics 政界 / a major figure in British politics 英國政壇的一位風雲人物 / to go into politics 從政 / to followe politics closely 密切關注政局 <span class="definition">2 [U] 一個團體內的權術，勾心鬥角，為貶義，如：</span>to get involved in office politics 捲入辦公室政治 / the internal politics of the legal profession 法律界內部的鉤心鬥角 / sexual politics 兩性間的權勢之爭 / dirty politics 卑劣的權術 <span class="definition">3 [C] 一個人的政治觀點或政治信仰，即政見：</span>His politics are extreme. 他的政治觀點偏激。<span class="definition">4 [U] 政治學：</span>a degree in Politics 一個政治學學位 <span class="definition">5 [C] 為單數名詞，指一套政治信仰的體系，或一種政治局勢：</span>A politics of the future has to engage with new ideas. 未來的政治制度必須同新思想結合。

<span class="vocabulary">**political**</span> [pə'lɪtɪkl]  
<span class="definition">adj. 1 與國家、政府或公共事務有關的：</span>political power 政治權力 / a political prisoner 政治犯 / a highly political issue 高度政治化的問題 / political works 具有政治性的作品 / political decision 政治性的決定 / I have no intention of getting political. 我無意涉及政治。<span class="definition">2 與不同的政治團體相關的，尤指與各黨派的政策和爭鬥有關的：</span>political debate, political party, political leader 政治辯論；政治黨派；政治領袖 / What are your political sympathies? 你認同哪個政黨？<span class="definition">3（人）關心政治的，政治上活躍的，或有堅定政治信仰的：</span>She became very political at university. 她上大學時開始十分熱衷於政治。/ I’m not a political animal. 我不是搞政治的那種人。<span class="definition">4 與一個組織內的權力、地位等相關的，即爭權奪利的，人事糾紛的：</span>The company is full of political disputes. 這家公司內部充滿了人事糾紛。/ to be dismissed for political reasons 因為人事上的原因而被解僱
           
<span class="vocabulary">**interior**</span> [ɪnˈtɪəriə(r); 美 -ˈtɪr-]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the interior 可指一國的內部事務：</span>the Department of the Interior 內政部 / the Minister of the Interior 內政大臣 / An official from the Ministry of the Interior said six people had died. 內政部的一位官員說已有 6 人喪生。<span class="definition">adj. [只用於名詞前] 內政的：</span>The French Interior Minister has intervened in a scandal over the role of a secret police force. 法國內政部長對一起涉及秘密警察部隊所扮演角色的醜聞進行了干預。

☀ <span class="category">**外交：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**diplomacy**</span> [dɪˈpləʊməsi; 美 -ˈploʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 外交；外交技巧；外交手腕：</span>international diplomacy 國際外交 / Diplomacy is better than war. 採取外交手段勝於訴諸戰爭。/ Today's Security Council resolution will be a significant success for American diplomacy. 今天的安理會決議將是美國外交取得的一個重大成功。/ The talks have now gone into a stage of quiet diplomacy. 會談現在已經進入了“溫和外交”階段。/ It will take deft diplomacy to sustain the fragile momentum. 需要靈活的外交技巧才能維持這種脆弱的勢頭。/ His skilful/skillful diplomacy has secured new European allies. 他巧妙的外交手段拿下了新的歐洲盟友。/ Effective trade diplomacy is vital. 有效的貿易外交至關重要。/ He also wants more aggressive unilateral diplomacy. 他也想要更加強勢的單邊外交。/ Sanctions and coercive diplomacy have failed.（coercive尤為美式用法）制裁措施和高壓外交已經失敗。/ gunboat diplomacy 炮艦外交 / checkbook diplomacy（美）金錢外交 / We will continue to seek solutions to the problems of this region through multilateral diplomacy. 我們將繼續透過多邊外交來尋求解決這一地區的問題。/ secret diplomacy 秘密外交 / Churchill's highly personal diplomacy in seeking a meeting with the Russians 在謀求與俄羅斯人的會晤中丘吉爾展現出的極大個人外交魅力 / public diplomacy（尤美）/ Cultural diplomacy between the two countries was an integral part of this development. 兩國之間的文化外交是這一發展中必不可少的一部分。/ traditional diplomacy 傳統外交 / The understanding between the two countries came about through quiet diplomacy. 這兩個國家透過秘密外交達成了諒解。/ The raid followed years of failed diplomacy. 多年外交失敗後發生了這場突然襲擊。/ preventive diplomacy 預防性外交 / a round of shuttle diplomacy between Washington and Brussels 在華盛頓和布魯塞爾之間的一輪穿梭外交 / European diplomacy 歐洲外交 / We prefer to use diplomacy rather than force to get people to move. 我們更願使用外交手段而不是武力去讓人們行動起來。/ This was no way to conduct diplomacy. 這絕不是開展外交的方法。/ She urged the leaders to pursue diplomacy. 她敦促領導人尋求外交手段。/ if diplomacy fails and combat is necessary 如果外交手段失敗而必須打仗 / There's a familiar rule that diplomacy works best when backed by the threat of force.有一條大家都熟悉的定律：以武力威脅為後盾的外交最有效。/ We must try and resolve this situation through diplomacy rather than conflict. 我們必須力爭透過外交而不是衝突來解決這一局面。
