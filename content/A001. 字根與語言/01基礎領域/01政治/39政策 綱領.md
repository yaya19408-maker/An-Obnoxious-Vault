☀ <span class="category">**政策 綱領：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**policy**</span> ['pɒləsɪ]  
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] policy (on sth) 某一政黨、官方機構的政策、措施：</span>party policy 黨的政策 / national policy 國家政策 / foreign policy 外交政策 / tax policy 稅收政策 / to have a zero-tolerance policy for drugs 對毒品採取零容忍政策

<span class="vocabulary">**platform**</span> ['plætfɔ:m]  
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] 一個政黨在參加競選時所宣稱的施政綱領，即其當選後要達成的目標。是一種潛在的、計劃中的、尚未執行的policy：</span>political platform 政治綱領 / party platform 黨綱 / election, electoral platform 競選綱領 / to announce a platform of economic reform 宣佈一份關於經濟改革的施政綱領
           
<span class="vocabulary">**guideline**</span> [ˈgaɪdlaɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] guidelines 可指官方機構釋出的做某事（尤指難事）的指導方針、行動綱領：</span>The government has drawn up guidelines on the treatment of the mentally ill. 政府制訂了對待精神病人的指導方針。/ The government should issue clear guidelines on the content of religious education. 政府應當就宗教教育的內容頒佈明確的指導方針。/ The accord also lays down guidelines for the conduct of American drug enforcement agents. 該協議也為美國毒品稽查人員的行為制訂了準則。/ A task force has formed to develop voluntary guidelines. 成立了特別小組以制定志願準則。/ federal, government guideline 聯邦指導方針；政府指導方針 / international, national, state guideline 國際準則；國家的指導方針；州級指導方針 / planning（尤英）, safety, security guideline 規劃方針；安全準則 / clinical, ethical, legal guideline 臨床指導方針；道德準則；法律準則 / The US Supreme Court is reviewing sentencing guidelines. 美國最高法院正在複審量刑準則。/ The organization has issued a set of guidelines for builders to follow. 該機構頒佈了一套建築商須遵守的指導方針。/ The following guidelines was approved in October 1995. 下述指導方針是1995年10月透過的。/ The document gives clear guidelines on the use of pesticides. 檔案對殺蟲劑的使用給出了清楚的說明。/ We have to follow the safety guidelines laid down by the government. 我們得遵循政府制定的安全準則。/ A number of deals have not met the guidelines. 相當數量的交易不符合指導方針的要求。/ The minister is accused of allowing the company to breach guidelines on arms sales. 部長被指控放任這家公司違反武器銷售的準則。/ The same general guidelines on when to dress formally apply to both men and women. 何時著正裝的一般性準則男女均適用。/ The guidelines require this information to be made available to the public. 指導方針要求將這一資訊公之於眾。/ The guidelines recommend that children under 12 avoid these foods. 指導方針建議12歲以下的兒童應避免吃這些食品。/ Under federal guidelines, they must serve at least five years in prison. 根據聯邦政府準則，他們必須在獄中服刑至少五年。/ Within clear guidelines, managers can use their budget to entertain clients. 在明確的方針指導下，經理們可以在預算允許的範圍內招待客戶。/ guidelines from the Department of Education 教育部的指導方針 / new EU guidelines on food hygiene 歐盟關於食品衛生新的指導方針
           
<span class="vocabulary">**manifesto**</span> [ˌmænɪˈfestəʊ; 美 -ˈfestoʊ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一群人（尤指一個政黨）的書面宣言（該宣言闡釋了其信仰，並宣稱如果其贏得選舉，將推行哪些政策）：</span>political manifesto 政治宣言 / Conservative manifesto 保守黨的宣言 / party manifesto（尤英） 政黨宣言 / election manifesto（尤英） 競選宣言 / The manifesto promised reform of the social security system. 宣言承諾要改革社會保障制度。/ manifesto commitment, manifesto pledge, manifesto promise（均英）宣言做出的承諾 / The policy is outlined in the party's election manifesto. 該黨在競選宣言中概述了它的政策。/ a manifesto for reform 改革宣言 / The Tories are currently drawing up their election manifesto. 保守黨人目前正在起草競選宣言。/ His manifesto promised measures to protect them. 他在宣言裡保證要為他們採取保護措施。
