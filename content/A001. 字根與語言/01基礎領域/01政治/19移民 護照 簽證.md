☀ <span class="category">**移民 護照 簽證：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**immigration**</span> [͵ɪmɪ'ɡreɪʃn]  
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U]（從另一國）入境移居；還可指外來的移民人數：</span>illegal immigration 非法移民 / Mexican immigration 墨西哥移民 / immigration bill 移民法案 / immigration authority 移民局 <span class="definition">2 [U] 港口、機場等的移民局檢查站，也可用immigration control：</span>to go through immigration 辦理移民入境檢查手續
           
<span class="vocabulary">**emigration**</span> [ˌemɪˈgreɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（向國外的）移民、移居：</span>the mass emigration of Jews from Eastern Europe 猶太人從東歐往其他地區的大批移居 / emigration from Europe to America 從歐洲移居美國 / He called for a halt to the recent wave of emigration. 他呼籲停止近期的移民潮。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**immigrant**</span> [ˈɪmɪgrənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 外國來的移民：</span>immigrant communities, immigrant families, immigrant workers 僑民社群；移民家庭；移民勞工 / immigrant visas 移民簽證 / illegal immigrants 非法移民 / the number of legal immigrants to the US 移居美國的合法移民數量 / foreign immigrant 外國移民 / Hispanic immigrant 西班牙移民 / Like many first-generation immigrants, they worked hard and saved most of their earnings. 和許多第一代移民一樣，他們辛苦工作並把賺到的錢大部分都存起來。/ These were working-class immigrants, mainly from rural parts of Mexico. 這些移民都是勞動階層，主要來自墨西哥鄉村地區。/ poor immigrants from Latin American countries 來自拉丁美洲國家的貧困移民 / The government is eager to attract skilled immigrants. 政府渴望吸引有一技之長的移民。/ These factors attracted new immigrants. 這些因素吸引了新的移民。/ She was the daughter of Chinese immigrants to America. 她父母是移居美國的中國僑民。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**migrant**</span> [ˈmaɪgrənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指為找工作）（從一地到另一地的）移居者、移民：</span>economic migrant 經濟移民 / illegal, undocumented（尤美）migrant 非法移民；無證移民 / rural migrant 農村流動人員 / first-generation 第一代移民 / migrant labourer/laborer, migrant worker（到處尋找工作的）流動工人 / They entered the country as migrant workers. 他們以流動工人的身份進入該國。/ migrant population 流動人口 / migrants from rural areas 來自農村地區的流動人員 / Migrants to the city send money home to their relatives. 進城的流動人口把錢寄回家給親屬。/ an influx of migrants移民的湧入 / The government divides asylum-seekers into economic migrants and genuine refugees. 政府把尋求避難者劃分為經濟移民與真正的難民。/ migrant workers following harvests northward 隨著收穫季節到來而北上的外來務工人員
           
<span class="vocabulary">**passport**</span> ['pɑːspɔ:t]  
<span class="definition">n. [C] 護照：</span>valid passport 有效護照 / fake passport 假護照 / Canadian passport 加拿大護照 / foreign passport 外國護照 / diplomatic passport 外交護照 / passport holder 護照持有人 / passport application 護照申請 

<span class="vocabulary">**visa**</span> ['vi:zə]  
<span class="definition">n. [C] 在護照上所做的簽證（由外國官員所做，以允許你進入、經過或離開該國）：</span>entry, exit, transit visa 入境簽證；出境簽證；過境簽證 / tourist, travel, work visa 旅遊簽證；旅行簽證；工作簽證 / permanent, temporary visa 永久簽證；臨時簽證 / valid visa 有效簽證 / visa applicant, visa holder 簽證申請人；簽證持有人
