☀ <span class="category">**所有權 所有制：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**ownership**</span> ['əʊnəʃɪp]  
<span class="definition">n. [U] 擁有某事物的事實，具體可稱為所有制，所有權，產權，物主身份：</span>collective ownership 集體所有權 / private ownership 私有產權 / public, state ownership 公有制；國有制 / car, home, land ownership 汽車所有權；住房所有權；土地所有權 / the legal ownership of his property 他財產的法定所有權 / The business has changed ownership several times. 這家企業已幾易其主。/ The restaurant is under new ownership. 這個餐廳已換了新的東家。
