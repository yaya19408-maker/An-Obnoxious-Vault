☀ <span class="category">**指控 控告：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**accuse**</span> [ə'kju:z] 
<span class="definition">vt. accuse sb (of sth) 指控某人犯罪，或指責其犯錯或不義等：</span>to accuse sb of theft 控告某人盜竊 / He stands accused of murdering his wife. 他被控謀殺妻子。/ She accused me of starting the fire! 她指控是我放的火！/ They stand accused of crimes against humanity. (正式用法) 他們被控違反人道罪。/ She accused him of lying. 她指責他說謊。/ You couldn’t accuse him of being selfish. 你不能說他自私。/ The government was accused of incompetence. 政府被指責無能。

<span class="vocabulary">**charge**</span> [tʃɑːdӡ] 
<span class="definition">vt. charge sb (with sth/with doing sth) 正式控告或起訴：</span>He was charged with murder. 他被指控犯有謀殺罪。/ Several people were arrested but nobody was charged. 有數人被捕，但均未受到起訴。/ He has been charged in connection with the attack. 他被指控與那起襲擊案件有關。<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 由警方對某人所提起的正式指控，控告：</span>civil, criminal, disciplinary charge 民事指控；刑事指控；違反紀律指控 / murder charge 謀殺指控 / charge sheet（英）案情記錄 / to drop, to withdraw charge 撤銷控告；撤回指控 / to face a charge 面臨指控 / Police have brought a charge of dangerous driving against the man. 警方以危險駕駛罪控告那名男子。/ The court dismissed the charge against him. 法庭駁回了對他的指控。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**allege**</span> [əˈledʒ]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] [正式]（未提出證據而）斷言、聲稱、指稱：</span>The prosecution alleges (that) she was driving carelessly. 控方指控她粗心駕駛。/ It is alleged that he mistreated the prisoners. 據稱他虐待犯人。/ He is alleged to have mistreated the prisoners. 他被指控虐待犯人。/ This procedure should be followed in cases where dishonesty has been alleged. 指控欺詐的案件應遵循本訴訟程式。/ She alleged that there was rampant drug use among the male members of the group. 她聲稱該團體中有大量的男性成員吸毒。/ The accused is alleged to have killed a man. 被告據稱謀殺了一名男子。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**indict**</span> [ɪnˈdaɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] [尤美，法律] ~ sb (for sth) / ~ sb (on charges/on a charge of sth) 正式控告或起訴（某人有罪）：</span>The senator was indicted for murder. 那位參議員被控犯謀殺罪。/ She has been indicted for possessing cocaine. 她被指控藏有可卡因。/ She was indicted on charges of corruption. 她被控貪腐，受到起訴。/ He was later indicted on corruption charges. 他後來被指控貪汙。

說明：accuse和charge表“指控”時，①accuse可表示“指控”某人犯罪，不過更常用來表示“指責”某人犯錯或不義等，其程度通常不嚴重。是一種直接的、帶有強烈個人感情色彩的指責或貶低行為；②charge是對嚴重罪行的一種正式“指控”。不過這裡的“嚴重罪行”，只是在原告看來“嚴重”。同樣一個行為，一個人可能只會accuse（此人雖然認為該行為可恥，但不至於提起正式指控），而另一個人則可能charge（因為此人認為該行為是嚴重的）。此行為被accused後，一般面臨的懲罰較輕，而被charged後，則面臨的懲罰通常較重。在用法上，charge一般用be charged with，而accuse一般用be accused of。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**accusation**</span> [ˌækjuˈzeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尚未證實的被控告的）罪名，罪狀：</span>accusations of corruption, accusations of cruelty, accusations of racism 對貪汙腐化的指控；對殘暴行為的指控；對種族主義的指控 / people who have made public accusations of rape 公開指控強姦罪名的人們 / The government denied the accusation that it was involved in the murders. 政府否認了參與謀殺案的指控。/ I don't want to make an accusation until I have some proof. 我要有一些證據以後才提出控告。/ Their father now faces an accusation of murder. 他們的父親正面臨謀殺罪的指控。/ New evidence has emerged which supports the accusation against her. 支援對她的指控的新證據已出現 / He fled the country amid accusations of fraud. 他身陷欺詐指控，於是潛逃出國。/ He was forced to defend himself against accusations of plagiarism. 他被迫對自己涉嫌抄襲的指控進行辯護。/ She rejected all the accusations brought against her. 她否認所有對自己的指控。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**allegation**</span> [ˌæləˈgeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (of sth) (against sb) / ~ (that…) / ~ (about sb/sth)（對某人所做的錯事或非法之事的）無證據的公開指控、指責：</span>damaging, serious allegation 詆譭性的指控；嚴肅的指控 / baseless, false, spurious（尤英）, unfounded, unproven, unsubstantiated, untrue, wild allegation 沒有根據的指控；虛假的指控；偽造的指控；無證據的指控；未經證實的指控；經不起推敲的指控；不實的指控；荒誕的指控 / malicious allegation 惡意的指控 / defamatory allegation 誹謗的言辭 / credible, true 可信的指控；真實的指控 / The defendants in the libel case maintain that their allegations are true. 誹謗案的被告堅稱他們的說法是真實的。/ There have been fresh allegations of atrocities. 又有關於暴行的新指控。/ corruption, fraud, rape allegation 腐敗指控；詐騙指控；強姦指控 / I advise you to withdraw your allegation before I contact my lawyer. 我建議你在我同我的律師聯絡之前收回你的指控。/ the school at the centre/center of these allegations 面臨多項指控、處於風口浪尖的學校 / She refused to admit the allegations. 她不承認這些指控。/ The president has the right to answer specific allegations. 總統有權回應各種具體的指控。/ He will need to counter allegations that he accepted money from criminals. 他需要對他曾接受過罪犯賄賂的指控加以反駁。/ The committee found no evidence to support allegations of smuggling. 委員會沒有發現能夠支援走私指控的證據。/ It took over two months to disprove the allegation. 證明該指控不成立用了兩個多月的時間。/ The sudden collapse of the business led to allegations of corrupt deals. 企業的突然倒閉引出腐敗交易的種種說辭。/ The governor of the prison is investigating allegations that a prisoner was attacked and beaten by a prison warden. 典獄長正在調查一位監獄看守攻擊和毆打一名犯人的指控。/ He has resigned amid corruption allegations. 他在腐敗的指控聲中辭職了。/ allegations about the president's private life 關於總統私生活的種種說法 / He has made certain allegations against the company. 他已經對這家公司提出了若干指控。/ Several newspapers made allegations of corruption in the city's police department. 有幾家報紙聲稱該市警察部門腐敗。/ allegations of dishonesty against him 關於他不誠實的多種說法 / an allegation that he had been dishonest 一種關於他不誠實的說法 / The company has denied the allegations. 公司否認了這些指控。/ Allegations of brutality and theft have been levelled at the army. 有人指責軍隊中存在著野蠻暴行和盜竊行為。

<span class="vocabulary">**complaint**</span> [kəmˈpleɪnt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] ~ (about/against sb/sth) / ~ (that…) 投訴、控告：</span>The most common complaint is about poor service. 最常見的投訴與服務差有關。/ We received a number of complaints from customers about the lack of parking facilities. 我們收到了來自顧客的許多投訴，抱怨缺乏停車設施。/ There's been a record number of complaints about the standard of service on Britain's railways. 有關英國鐵路服務水平的投訴空前的多。/ People have been reluctant to make formal complaints to the police. 人們不願向警方作出正式投訴。/ I'd like to make a complaint about the noise. 我要就噪音問題提出投訴。/ a formal complaint 正式控告 / to file/lodge (= make) a complaint（正式）提出控告 / consumer, customer complaint 消費者投訴；顧客投訴 / citizen complaint（美）市民投訴 / civil, criminal（美）民事投訴；刑事投訴 / discrimination, harassment complaint 歧視投訴；騷擾投訴 / ethics complaint（美）道德方面的投訴 / He brought a complaint against his former manager. 他投訴他以前的經理。/ They filed a complaint with the Commission. 他們向委員會操交投訴。/ We have had some serious complaints from parents. 找們收到家長的一些嚴正投訴。/ The tribunal heard complaints against the director. 審理委員會聽取了對那名主管的控告。complaints arising from late payment 由拖欠付款引發的投訴 / a complaint against the police 對警方提出的控告 / a complaint of unfair dismissal 對不正當解僱的投訴! / to make a complaint to the authorities 向當局申訴 <span class="definition">2 [U] 投訴或控告的行為：</span>complaint procedure（美）, complaints procedure（英）, complaint system（美）申訴程式；投訴程式；控告制度 / complaint letter（尤美）（英美通常用letter of complaint）投訴信 / complaint form 投訴表
