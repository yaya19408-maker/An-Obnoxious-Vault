☀ <span class="category">**保釋 取保：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**bail**</span> [beɪl]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 保釋金；保釋：</span>Can anyone put up bail for you? 有人保釋你嗎？/ She was released on £2 000 bail. 她以2 000英鎊獲得保釋。/ Bail was set at $1 million. 保釋金定為100萬元。/ The high court set bail at $8,000. 高等法院將保釋金定為8,000美元。/ The judge set bail at £50,000. 法官將保釋金定為5萬英鎊。<span class="definition">2 [U] 保釋：</span>The judge refused bail. 法官不准保釋。/ She jumped/skipped bail (= did not appear at her trial). 她棄保潛逃（未如期到庭受審）。/ He was yesterday given bail by South Yorkshire magistrates. 昨天南約克郡法院准予他獲得保釋。/ She has been granted conditional bail. 她被准予有條件保釋。/ For very serious crimes, it is hard to get bail. 犯了重罪很難獲得保釋。/ The police were successful in opposing bail. 警方成功地阻止了保釋。/ A wealthy businessman has stood bail for him. 一位富商保釋了他。/ They were released on police bail pending further enquiries. 警方允許他們保釋等候進一步調查。/ bail application（尤英） 保釋申請 / bail hearing 保釋聽證會 / bail bond 保釋保證書 / bail conditions（尤英） 保釋條件 / bail bondsman（尤美） 保釋擔保人 / bail hostel（英） 保釋犯人招待所 / He was sent to a bail hostel until the case came to court. 他被送到保釋招待所直到該案開庭。/ He committed another robbery while out on bail. 他在保釋期間再次搶劫。/ He is currently free on bail. 他目前取保獲釋。/ He was freed on bail pending an appeal. 他被取保候審。/ The accused were held without bail. 被告被拘留，不得保釋。/ an application for bail（英） 保釋申請 <span class="definition">vt. 允許保釋某人：</span>He was bailed to appear in court on 15 March. 他獲得保釋，定於3月15日到庭候審。/ He was bailed for probation reports. 他因等待鑑定報告而獲得保釋。/ He was bailed to appear before local magistrates on 5 November. 他獲得保釋，將於11月5日在地方法庭出庭受審。

<span class="vocabulary">**post**</span> [pəʊst] 
<span class="definition">vt. [尤美] post bail/(a) bond 向法院交納保釋金（這樣犯人就能在審判前不必待在看守所而自由行動）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**bond**</span> [bɒnd] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 法律領域可指保釋金，但尤用於北美：</span>He was released on $5 000 bond. 他以5000美元取保釋放。
