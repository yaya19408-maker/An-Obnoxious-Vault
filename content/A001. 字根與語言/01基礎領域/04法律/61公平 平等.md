☀ <span class="category">**公平 平等：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**fair**</span> [feə] 
<span class="definition">adj. fair (to sb) 根據規則或法律而對所有人一視同仁，即公正的，反義詞為unfair：</span>a fair distribution of wealth 財富的公平分配 / to receive a fair trial 得到公正審判 / free and fair elections 自由公正的選舉 / It’s not fair! He always gets more than me. 這不公平！他得到的總比我多。/ I’ll give you ten pounds each to make it fair. 為公平起見，我給你們每人十英鎊。<span class="definition">adv. 公平地，公正地，按照規則地：</span>Come on, you two, fight fair! 得了，你們倆，要按規則比賽！/ to play fair (= to behave honestly) 為人正直
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fairness**</span> [ˈfeənəs; 美 ˈfernəs]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 公平、公正、公道：</span>He says the new document will guarantee fairness for blacks in Georgia. 他說這個新檔案將保證佐治亞州的黑人受到公平對待。/ The new system of waiting lists should guarantee fairness to all patients. 新的候診名單系統能保證對所有患者都公平 / concern about the fairness of the election campaign 對於競選公平性的關注 / the fairness of the judicial system 司法制度的公正性 / In fairness to Bates, he always made it very clear that Webb was only on trial until the end of the season. 替貝茨說句公道話，他意思一直很明確，韋布在本賽季結束前只是試用。/ There is much more to be said, in all fairness, on both sides of the issue. 公平地說，問題的正反兩面都還有很多要說的。/ In all fairness to him, I should say that most of his story is true. 公平地講，應該說他所敘述的大部分是真實的。/ This is a matter of basic fairness. 這是一件關乎基本公正的事情。/ procedural fairness（法律）程式公正 / economic fairness 經濟上的公平 / a way of achieving fairness to the accused 為被告求得公正的方法 / Many people questioned the fairness of the election. 許多人對這次選舉的公正性提出了質疑。/ Fairness demanded an equal division of the winnings. 要做到公正，就要平均分配贏得的錢。/ They were all treated with strict fairness. 他們都受到了絕對公正的對待。/ the need for fairness in applying these rules 執行這些規則時所需要的公平。/ Children have a very strong sense of fairness. 兒童有很強的公平意識。

<span class="vocabulary">**square**</span> [skweə] 
<span class="definition">adj.（尤指在生意上）公平的：</span>a square deal 公平交易
                  
<span class="vocabulary">**equity**</span> [ˈekwəti]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式] 公平、公正（即每個人均受到公平對待的一種情況）：</span>We base this call on grounds of social justice and equity. 我們基於社會正義和公平發出這一呼籲。
