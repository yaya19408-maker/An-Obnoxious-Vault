☀ <span class="category">**放寬：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**relax**</span> [rɪ'læks] 
<span class="definition">vt. 放寬規則、法律等限制（使其不再那麼嚴格）：</span>to relaxed the ban on smoking 放寬對吸菸的禁令
           
<span class="vocabulary">**relaxation**</span> [ˌri:lækˈseɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C, usually sing.]（對規章制度或管控措施的）放鬆、放寬：</span>the relaxation of foreign currency controls 對外匯管制的放寬 / a relaxation of travel restrictions 旅遊限制的放寬 / Financiers are calling for a relaxation of these stringent measures. 金融家呼呼對這些嚴厲的措施予以放寬。/ a relaxation in the rules 對規則的放寬
           
<span class="vocabulary">**relaxed**</span> [rɪˈlækst]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (about sth) 不用紀律加以約束的，或不用讓人們遵守規則的：</span>I take a fairly relaxed attitude towards what the kids wear to school. 孩子穿什麼上學，我覺得無所謂。
