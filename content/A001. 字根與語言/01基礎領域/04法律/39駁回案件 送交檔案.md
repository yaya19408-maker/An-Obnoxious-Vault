☀ <span class="category">**駁回案件 送交檔案：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**dismiss**</span> [dɪs'mɪs] 
<span class="definition">vt. 駁回（或不受理）法律案件，通常由於證據不足：</span>The case was dismissed. 此案已被駁回。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dismissal**</span> [dɪsˈmɪsl]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] [法律] 訴訟的駁回、不予受理（通常因為證據不足）：</span>the dismissal of the appeal 駁回上訴
           
<span class="vocabulary">**void**</span> [vɔɪd]
<span class="definition">vt. [法律] 正式宣佈…在法律上無效：</span>The Supreme Court threw out the confession and voided his conviction for murder. 最高法院駁回了供詞，宣佈他謀殺罪名不成立。

<span class="vocabulary">**serve**</span> [sə:v] 
<span class="definition">vt. serve sth (on sb) / serve sb with sth 法律中可指把官方檔案送達某人，尤指送達要求對方出庭的檔案：</span>to serve summons on sb 把傳票送達某人 / Immigration officers tried to serve her with a deportation order. 移民局官員試圖向她送達驅逐令。

<span class="vocabulary">**service**</span> ['sə:vɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 法律中可指官方檔案等的正式送達（即交給某人）：</span>the service of a demand for payment 繳款通知的送達
