<span class="vocabulary">**suspect**</span> [sə'spekt] 
<span class="definition">vt. suspect sb (of sth/of doing sth) 懷疑某人有罪，但沒有明確證據：</span>He was wrongly suspected of theft. 有人錯誤地懷疑他有偷竊行為。/ Whom do the police suspect? 警方懷疑誰？/ The drug is suspected of causing over 200 deaths. 人們懷疑這種藥物造成200多人死亡。/ It was widely suspected that they had been acting on orders. 人們普遍懷疑他們是奉命採取行動的。<span class="definition">adj. 疑有危險的，或疑似非法的，相當於suspicious：</span>a suspect package 可疑包裹
