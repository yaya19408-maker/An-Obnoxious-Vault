☀ <span class="category">**合法 有效：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**legal**</span> ['li:ɡl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 被法律所允許或要求的，即合法的，法律所要求的：</span>It’s perfectly legal. 這完全合法。/ legal limit 法定限度（尤指酒後駕駛者血液酒精含量的法定限度，但又不止於此，還可指輻射的法定限度等）
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**legality**</span> [li:ˈgæləti]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 合法；合法性：</span>The auditor has questioned the legality of the contracts. 審計員質疑這些合同是否合法。/ They intended to challenge the legality of his claim in the courts. 他們打算在法庭上對他的要求的合法性提出質疑。/ The arrangement is of doubtful legality. 這項約定是否合法值得懷疑。/ The court ruling today upheld the legality of military tribunals. 今天的法院判決支援軍事法庭的合法性。

<span class="vocabulary">**lawful**</span> [ˈlɔ:fl]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 為法律所承認或允許的，即合法的：</span>his lawful heir 他的合法繼承人 / It was lawful for the doctors to treat her in whatever way they considered was in her best interests. 醫生們採用他們認為對她最有利的方法為她治療是合法的。/ Hunting is a lawful activity. 狩獵是一項合法活動。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**legitimate**</span> [lɪˈdʒɪtɪmət]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 合法的：</span>the legitimate government of the country 該國的合法政府 / Is his business strictly legitimate? 他的生意是否絕對合法？/ The government will not seek to disrupt the legitimate business activities of the defendant. 政府無意干擾被告的合法經營活動。<span class="definition">2（小孩）合法婚姻所生的：</span>We only married in order that the child should be legitimate. 我們結婚純粹是為了給孩子一個合法身份。<span class="definition">vt. 使…合法化，相當於legitimize：</span>We want to legitimate this process by passing a law. 我們希望透過立法來使這一程式合法化。           

<span class="vocabulary">**licit**</span> [ˈlɪsɪt]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 被准許的或合法的：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**kosher**</span> [ˈkəʊʃə(r); 美 ˈkoʊ-]
<span class="definition">adj. [非正式] 誠實的；合法的：</span>Their business deals are not always completely kosher. 他們的商業活動並不總是光明正大的。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**valid**</span> ['vælɪd] 
<span class="definition">adj.（票或其他檔案、行為）為法律或官方所認可的，是此義的常規用詞：</span>a valid passport 有效的護照 / to be legally valid 有法律效力 / The original written contract was held valid. 合同原件被認為有效。/ These discounts are valid for students. 這些折扣對學生有效。

<span class="vocabulary">**validity**</span> [vəˈlɪdəti]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 法律上的有效、合法；官方的認可：</span>The period of validity of the agreement has expired. 本協議的有效期已過。/ Shocked by the results of the elections, they now want to challenge the validity of the vote. 選舉結果令他們很震驚，他們現在要對選舉的有效性提出質疑。/ The legal validity of the claims has been challenged. 這些索賠的合法性受到質疑。/ The judges upheld the validity of the previous judgement. 法官維持了原判。

<span class="vocabulary">**effective**</span> [ɪˈfektɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式]（法律、協議、規則等）生效的、起作用的：</span>The new speed limit on this road becomes effective from 1 June. 這條路上新的限速規定自6月1日起生效。/ The new rules will become effective in the next few days. 新規定將在幾天後生效。/ directly effective treaty provisions 直接生效的條約條款
 
<span class="vocabulary">**good**</span> [ɡʊd] 
<span class="definition">adj. good for sth（對某事物來說）有效的（valid）。表此義時不太常用：</span>The ticket is good for three months. 這張票三個月內有效。

<span class="vocabulary">**run**</span> [rʌn] 
<span class="definition">vi. run (for sth) 在一段時間內有效：</span>The permit runs for three months. 許可證的有效期為三個月。/ The lease on my house only has a year left to run. 我房子的租期只剩下一年了。

<span class="vocabulary">**hold**</span> [həʊld] 
<span class="definition">vi.（法律、法規等）有效：</span>These laws also hold for universities. 這些法律同樣適用於大學。

<span class="vocabulary">**force**</span> [fɔ:s] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 權威所賦予的效力：</span>legal force 法律效力 / The contract was not signed and has no binding force. 合同沒有簽字因而沒有約束力。/ The new law comes into force as from midnight tomorrow. 新法律從明天零點起開始生效。/ These standards have force in British law.這些標準在英國法律中有效。
