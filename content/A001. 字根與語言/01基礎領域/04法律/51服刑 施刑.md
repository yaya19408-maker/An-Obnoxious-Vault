☀ <span class="category">**服刑 施刑：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**serve**</span> [sə:v] 
<span class="definition">vt.（在監獄中）服刑一段時間：</span>to serve a life sentence for murder 因犯有謀殺罪而服無期徒刑

<span class="vocabulary">**stretch**</span> [stretʃ]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] [非正式] 某人的服刑期：</span>He did a ten-year stretch for fraud. 他因欺詐罪服刑十年。

<span class="vocabulary">**hang**</span> [hæŋ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（hanged, hanged）絞死，或被絞死；或施以絞刑，被施以絞刑：</span>to hang oneself from a beam 懸樑自盡 / He was hanged for murder. 他因犯謀殺罪而被施以了絞刑。

<span class="vocabulary">**capital**</span> ['kæpɪtl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 只作前置定語，涉及死罪的，可處死刑的：</span>Espionage is a capital offence in this country. 間諜活動在這個國家是死罪。
           
周邊詞彙補充：
· section可指（依法命令精神病人）強制入院治療；
· freeze可表示（根據法院指令）凍結資金、銀行賬戶、資產等。
