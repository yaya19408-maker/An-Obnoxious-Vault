☀ <span class="category">**原告 被告 當事人：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**plaintiff**</span> [ˈpleɪntɪf]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [法律] 原告、起訴人：</span>The plaintiff claimed that the correct procedures had not been followed. 原告聲稱未履行正確的程式。/ The plaintiffs obtained an injunction in the High Court. 原告方在高等法院得到了禁制令。/ Costs were awarded against the plaintiff. 訴訟費判定由原告承擔。/ the lawyer appearing on behalf of the plaintiff 代表原告出庭的律師
   
<span class="vocabulary">**prosecution**</span> [ˌprɒsɪˈkju:ʃn; 美 ˌprɑ:s-]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the prosecution可指原告、控方（包括原告和控方律師等）：</span>The prosecution alleged that he murdered his wife. 控方稱他謀殺了自己的妻子。/ prosecution case, prosecution evidence 原告提出的論據；控方提出的證據 / prosecution counsel（英）, prosecution lawyer, prosecution team, prosecution witness 原告律師；控方團隊；控方證人 / prosecution costs（英）原告承擔的費用 / He was a witness for the prosecution. 他是原告證人。/ The prosecution has/have failed to prove its/their case. 控方未能證明所控屬實。/ defence and prosecution 被告和原告 / Colonel Pugh, for the prosecution, said that the offences occurred over a six-year period. 皮尤上校為控方律師作證時說這些罪行是在六年的時間裡犯下的。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**defendant**</span> [dɪˈfendənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [法律] 被告人：</span>criminal defendant 刑事被告人 / convicted, guilty defendant（被判）有罪的被告 / The defendant was charged with disturbing the peace. 被告被控擾亂治安。/ a defendant being sued by an insurance company 被一家保險公司起訴的被告 / The defendant was convicted of murder. 被告被判犯有謀殺罪。/ The jury found the defendant guilty on all counts.陪審團裁定被告所有罪名成立。/ The defendant was sentenced to three years in prison. 被告被判入獄三年。/ the plaintiffs claim against the defendant 原告向被告索賠 / Several witnesses gave evidence for the defendant. 幾名證人提供了有利於被告的證詞。/ a defendant in bankruptcy proceedings 破產訴訟案的被告

<span class="vocabulary">**party**</span> ['pɑːtɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 法律上的“當事方”（如訴訟當事人、合同當事人等）。可以是一個人，也可以是一個組織。是表示此義的常規用詞，也是一種正式用法，如：</span>the guilty party 有罪的一方 / the innocent party 無罪的一方 / aggrieved, injured, wronged party 受害方；受害人 / offending party 違規方 / concerned, interested, involved party 當事人 / disinterested, neutral party 中立方 / in the presence of an independent third party 有獨立的第三方在場 / This agreement binds both parties. 該協議對雙方均具有約束力。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**principal**</span> [ˈprɪnsəpl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [術語]（尤指在法律事務中）你所代表的一個人，即當事人，委託人：</span>瞭解即可           

<span class="vocabulary">**prosecutor**</span> [ˈprɒsɪkju:tə(r); 美 ˈprɑ:s-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 公訴人、檢察官：</span>the public prosecutor, the state prosecutor 公訴人；州檢察官
           
<span class="vocabulary">**crown**</span> [kraʊn]
<span class="definition">n. [sing] the Crown可指英國刑事案件中的公訴人：</span>Who's appearing for the Crown (= bringing a criminal charge against sb on behalf of the state) in this case? 誰將在此案中代表王國政府出庭？/ Crown Court（英格蘭和威爾士的）刑事法庭


周邊詞彙補充：
· side可表示爭論（或糾紛）中的一方或一派（可以是一個人，也可以是一群人）。還可指法院的管轄範圍。
