☀ <span class="category">**出版 發行：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**publish**</span> ['pʌblɪʃ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 出版或發行（圖書、雜誌、光碟等）。是此義的常規用詞：</span>The first edition was published in 2018. 第一版於2018年發行。/ a newly published book 新出版的一本書 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（作者）發表作品（以供售賣）：</span>University teachers are under pressure to publish. 大學教師有發表作品的壓力。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**publisher**</span> [ˈpʌblɪʃə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 出版人；出版機構；發行人；發行機構：</span>The publishers planned to produce the journal on a weekly basis. 出版者計劃將該期刊定為週刊。/ one of the country's biggest book publishers 該國最大的圖書出版商之一 / commercial publisher 商業出版社 / He's writing a history of the town for a local publisher. 他正在為一家當地出版社寫一部關於該鎮歷史的書。/ specialist publisher（英）專業出版社 / print publisher 印刷出版商 / electronic, online, Web publisher 電子出版社；線上出版社；網路出版商 / art, book, magazine, music, newspaper, software publisher 美術出版社；圖書出版商；雜誌社；唱片發行公司；報社；軟體出版商 / academic, educational, trade 學術出版社；教育出版社；一般圖書出版社 / Finding a publisher is hard for all writers. 對所有作家來說，找出版商都並非易事。/ She now has a publisher for her book. 她現在為她的書找到了出版商。

<span class="vocabulary">**publishing**</span> [ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 出版或發行；出版業或發行業：</span>book, newspaper publishing 圖書出版；報紙的出版 / academic, scholarly publishing 學術出版 / medical, scientific publishing 醫療出版；科學出版 / She works in music publishing. 她從事音樂出版。/ desktop publishing 桌面出版 / electronic, online publishing 電子出版；線上出版 / publishing agreement, publishing deal 出版協議 / publishing arm, publishing company, publishing group, publishing house 出版部門；出版公司；出版集團；出版社 / The Cranfield Press is the publishing arm of the Cranfield Institute. 克蘭菲爾德出版社是克蘭菲爾德工學院的出版部門。/ publishing business, publishing industry, publishing world 出版業；出版界 / The publishing world is extremely competitive. 出版界競爭極其激烈。/ I had a very high-powered job in publishing. 我曾在出版業擔任要職。

<span class="vocabulary">**appear**</span> [ə'pɪə] 
<span class="definition">vi.（書籍）將被出版：</span>His new book will be appearing in the spring. 他的新書將於春季出版。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**publication**</span> [ˌpʌblɪˈkeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C]（書刊、雜誌等的）出版；發行：</span>the publication of his first novel 他首部小說的出版 / the posthumous publication this year of his unedited journals 在他去世後於今年出版的他那未經編輯的日記 / The book is scheduled for publication in the spring. 該書定於春季出版。/ electronic, online, print publication 電子出版；線上出版；印刷出版 / publication date 出版日期 <span class="definition">2 [C]（書刊、雜誌等）出版物；發行物：</span>His writing has appeared in many other print and online publications. 他的寫作還以許多印刷出版物和線上出版物的形式出現。/ Our German sister publication, Diese Woche', went out of business. 我們在德國的姊妹刊物《每週關注》停刊了。/ government, official publication 政府出版物；官方出版物 / specialist publication（尤英）專業出版物 / glossy publication（尤英）用高光紙印刷的出版物 / academic, business, industry, scholarly, scientific, trade publication 學術出版物；商業出版物；工業出版物；科學出版物；貿易出版物 / She has several publications to her name on local history. 她出版了幾部有關當地歷史的書。/ They have started legal proceedings against two publications which spoke of an affair. 他們開始對兩本提及一樁婚外情的出版物提起法律訴訟。

<span class="vocabulary">**issue**</span> [ˈɪʃu:; British English also ˈɪsju:]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（郵票、錢幣、股份）的一次發行額（或一套）：</span>The company is planning a new share issue. 公司正計劃發行新股。/ a special issue of stamps 特別發行的一套郵票 <span class="definition">2 [U] 發行（以供人們購買或使用）：</span>I bought a set of the new stamps on the date of issue. 我在新郵票發行的當天就買了一套。<span class="definition">vt. 出版、發表雜誌、文章等：</span>We issue a monthly newsletter. 我們出版一份通訊月刊。<span class="definition">vt. 發行（新的一批郵票、硬幣、股票等）：</span>They issued a special set of stamps to mark the occasion. 他們特別發行了一套紀念郵票。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**circulation**</span> [ˌsɜ:kjəˈleɪʃn; 美 ˌsɜ:rk-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.]（報刊或雜誌的）發行量：</span>a daily circulation of more than one million 日發行量超過一百萬份 / The newspaper has a daily circulation of 20 000. 這份報紙的日發行量為2萬份。/ The Daily News once had the highest circulation of any daily in the country.《每日新聞報》的發行量曾位居全國所有日報之首。/ The paper has proved unable to maintain its circulation figures. 事實證明這家報紙難以維持其發行量。/ circulation area 發行區域

<span class="vocabulary">**out**</span> [aʊt] 
<span class="definition">adv., prep. 圖書的出版、面世（或面市）：</span>His new book will come out this July. 他的新書將於今年七月出版。/ The French edition came out in early 2006. 法文版在2006年初面市。<span class="definition">adj.（圖書）面世（或面市）的：</span>His new novel is out now. 他的新小說現已面市。

<span class="vocabulary">**reject**</span> [rɪ'dӡekt] 
<span class="definition">vt.（因某東西質量不夠好而）決定不去出版：</span>Imperfect articles are rejected. 有問題的文章都被退回。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**rejection**</span> [rɪˈdʒekʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 退稿：</span>a publisher's rejection slip 出版社的退稿通知
