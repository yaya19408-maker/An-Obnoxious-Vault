☀ <span class="category">**啟動 啟程：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**start**</span> [stɑːt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 啟動車輛；（車輛）啟動：</span>I can’t get the car started. 這輛車我發動不起來。/ The car won’s start. 這輛車發動不起來。<span class="definition">vi. start (out) 開始一個旅程；離開。即出發，動身，啟程，相當於set off或set out：</span>What time are we starting tomorrow? 我們明天什麼時候出發？

<span class="vocabulary">**departure**</span> [dɪ'pɑːtʃə] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 在特定時間離開一地（即啟程）的飛機、火車等，與arrival相反：</span>arrivals and departures 到站和離站班次 / All departures are from Manchester. 所有離站班次都從曼徹斯特出發。/ departure lounge, departure time, departure gate 候機（或車）室；離站時間；登機（或上車）口 / departures board 離站時刻牌 <span class="definition">2 [C, U] departure (from ...) 啟程、出發，與arrival相反：</span>The last check-in time is 45 minutes before flight departure. 最遲在飛機起飛前45分鐘辦理登機手續。/ the train’s departure 火車的離站 / his departure for Naples 他啟程前往那不勒斯 / the departure point 行程的起點 / The author takes Freud’s dream theories as the point of departure for his essay.（比喻用法）作者以弗洛伊德的釋夢理論作為他論文的出發點。

<span class="vocabulary">**light**</span> [laɪt]
