☀ <span class="category">**違章 罰單：**</span> 
<span class="vocabulary">**ticket**</span> ['tɪkɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 交通違章（駕駛或停車違章）的罰款通知單（或交通違章後由法院所發出的出庭傳票），相當於fine：</span>parking, speeding, traffic（美）ticket 非法停車罰單；超速駕駛罰單；交通罰單 / The police officer gave us a ticket for going through a red light. 警察給我們開了一張罰單，因為我們闖紅燈。<span class="definition">vt. [尤美] 常用被動式，發出交通違章的罰款通知單：</span>He was ticketed for parking illegally. 他由於違章停車而收到了一張罰單。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**endorse**</span> [ɪnˈdɔ:s; 美 ɪnˈdɔ:rs]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] [英]（在某人的駕駛執照上）記錄違章事項：</span>You risk having your licence endorsed. 你這樣做可能被記錄違章。/ For failing to report the accident, his licence was endorsed. 由於發生事故未能報告，他的駕駛執照被註明違章。/ He also had his licence endorsed with eight penalty points. 他的駕駛執照也被扣了8分。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**endorsement**</span> [ɪnˈdɔ:smənt; 美 -ˈdɔ:rs-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] [英]（駕駛執照上的）違章記錄：</span>瞭解即可
