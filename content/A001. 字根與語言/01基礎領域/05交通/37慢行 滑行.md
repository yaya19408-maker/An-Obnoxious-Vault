<span class="vocabulary">**coast**</span> [kəʊst] 
<span class="definition">vi.（汽車或腳踏車）移動（尤指不用任何動力而向山坡下滑行，可叫做慣性滑行）：</span>He pushed in the clutch and coasted to a halt. 他踩下離合器，滑行了一會兒便停下來了。/ She took her feet off the pedals and coasted downhill. 她把腳從腳踏車的踏板上鬆開，沿山坡滑行而下。

<span class="vocabulary">**low**</span> [ləʊ] 
<span class="definition">adj.（交通工具）處於低速檔的：</span>The car is in low gear. 車處於低速檔。
