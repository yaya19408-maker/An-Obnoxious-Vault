☀ <span class="category">**航向 航線：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**course**</span> [kɔ:s] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C, usually sing.] 船等交通工具的航向，航線：</span>The boat altered course during the storm. 船在暴風雨中改變了航線。/ The boat was blown off course. 這艘船被吹離了航線。/ We set course for the island. 我們確定了去該島的路線。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**lane**</span> [leɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（船的）固定航道、航線：</span>The collision took place in the busiest shipping lanes in the world. 撞船事故發生在全世界最繁忙的航道上。           

<span class="vocabulary">**route**</span> [ru:t; North American English also raʊt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 公共汽車、火車等的固定或常規路線，或運輸貨物的常規路線：</span>sea route 海上航線

周邊詞彙補充：
· drift可指（船隻由於氣流或風向所導致的）偏航。
