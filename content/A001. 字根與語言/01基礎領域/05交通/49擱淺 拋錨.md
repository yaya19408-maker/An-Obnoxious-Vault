☀ <span class="category">**擱淺 拋錨：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**ground**</span> [ɡraʊnd] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（船）觸海底且不能移動，即擱淺；使（船）擱淺：</span>The boat had been grounded on rocks. 船在礁石上擱淺了。/ to ground on a soft, underwater bank 擱淺在一個鬆軟的水下淺灘上
           
<span class="vocabulary">**grounding**</span> [ˈgraʊndɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（尤指因機件故障等導致的）船隻的留港停航：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**strand**</span> [strænd]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 使（船隻、魚、鯨等）擱淺（而不能回到水裡）：</span>The ship was stranded on a sandbank. 船在沙洲上擱淺了。

<span class="vocabulary">**anchor**</span> ['æŋkə] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 拋錨（使船停止）：</span>We could anchor off the pier. 我們可以在碼頭拋錨。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**berth**</span> [bɜ:θ; 美 bɜ:rθ]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi.（使船）停泊：</span>The ship is berthed at Southampton. 船停泊在南安普敦。 / As the ship berthed in New York, McClintock was with the first immigration officers aboard. 船停靠在紐約後，麥克林托克和第一批移民局官員登上了船。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**moor**</span> [mɔ:(r); mʊə(r); 美 mʊr]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ sth (to sth) （使）船隻（透過繩索的拴系而）停泊在某處，即把船系在某地的物體上使其停泊：</span>She had moored her barge on the right bank of the river. 她把駁船停泊在河右岸。/ I decided to moor near some tourist boats. 我決定在一些觀光船附近停泊。/ We moored off the north coast of the island. 我們停泊在島的北部岸邊。/ A number of fishing boats were moored to the quay. 很多漁船繫泊在碼頭。
           
周邊詞彙補充：
· sink可表示擊沉船隻；
· stranded可指被困的；
· beached可指擱淺的；
· wrecked可指（船隻）毀壞的。
