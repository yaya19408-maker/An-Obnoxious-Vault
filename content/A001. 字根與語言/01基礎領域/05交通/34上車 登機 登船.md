☀ <span class="category">**上車 登機 登船：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**on**</span> [ɒn] 
<span class="definition">adv. 登上車輛：</span>The bus stopped and four people got on. 公共汽車停下來，四個人上了車。/ He hurried on to the plane. 他匆忙登上飛機。

<span class="vocabulary">**jump**</span> [dӡʌmp] 
<span class="definition">vt. [美] 非常快地跳上交通工具：</span>to jump a bus 跳上公共汽車 / Do you want a ride? Jump in. 你想搭車嗎？快上來吧。/ She immediately jumped on a plane to France. 她立刻搭乘飛機飛往法國。

<span class="vocabulary">**board**</span> [bɔ:d] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 1上船、上飛機、上火車等交通工具，是一種正式用法：</span>The ship was boarded by customs officials. 海關官員登上了這艘船。/ Passengers are waiting to board. 乘客們正在候機。<span class="definition">2 be boarding 表示讓乘客登機或上船：</span>Flight BA193 for Paris is now boarding at Gate 37. BA193次飛往巴黎的乘客現在可以在37號登機口登機。

<span class="vocabulary">**aboard**</span> [ə'bɔ:d] 
<span class="definition">adv., prep.（尤英）在船（或飛機、公交、火車）上；到船（或飛機、公交、火車）上，相當於on board：</span>We went aboard. 我們上了船。/ He was already aboard the plane. 他已經登機了。/ Welcome aboard! 歡迎乘坐（或歡迎加入）！

<span class="vocabulary">**join**</span> [dӡɒɪn] 
<span class="definition">vt. [英] 上火車（或飛機）等：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**embark**</span> [ɪmˈbɑ:k; 美 ɪmˈbɑ:rk]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 上船；裝船：</span>We stood on the pier and watched as they embarked. 我們站在突碼頭上目送他們登船。/ They embarked the troops by night. 他們讓部隊在夜裡上了船。/ They travelled to Portsmouth, where they embarked on the battle cruiser HMS Renown. 他們行至樸次茅斯，在那裡登上了英國皇家海軍“聲望號”戰列巡洋艦。/ Bob ordered brigade HQ to embark. 鮑勃命令旅指揮部登船。
