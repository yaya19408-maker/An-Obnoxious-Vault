☀ <span class="category">**進場 著陸：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**approach**</span> [ə'prəʊtʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 飛機飛行過程中的進場，或進場著陸：</span>to begin the final approach to the runway 開始著陸進入跑道

<span class="vocabulary">**land**</span> [lænd] 
<span class="definition">vi. 1 指飛機降落到地面或其它表面，與take off相反：</span>The plane landed safely. 飛機安全著陸了。/ The plane was forced to land in a nearby field. 飛機被迫降落在附近的田野裡。<span class="definition">2（乘飛機或宇宙飛船）抵達：</span>We shall be landing shortly. 我們很快就要著陸。/ to land on the moon 登上月球 <span class="definition">vt. 1 指飛行員使飛機平穩著陸：</span>The pilot landed the plane safely. 飛行員駕駛飛機安全著陸。<span class="definition">2 將人或事物從飛機上放到地面，即（使）降落，著陸：</span>The troops were landed by helicopter. 部隊乘直升機降落。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**landing**</span> [ˈlændɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 飛行器的著陸：</span>bumpy, hard, rough landing 顛簸著陸；硬著陸；粗猛著陸 / crash, emergency, forced landing 摔機著陸；緊急著陸；迫降 / safe, smooth, soft landing 安全著陸；平穩著陸；軟著陸 / successful landing 成功著陸 / lunar, moon landing 登月著陸 / the first Apollo moon landing 阿波羅航天飛船首次登月著陸 / The pilot had to make an emergency landing in a field. 飛行員不得不緊急降落在一片田野中。/ The pilot was forced to make an emergency landing. 飛行員被迫緊急著陸。/ I had to make a controlled landing into the sea. 我只好將飛機迫降在海上。/ The plane had been cleared for landing at Brunswick's Glynco Airport. 飛機獲准降落於不倫瑞克的格林科機場。/ landing area, landing site, landing strip 著陸區；著陸場；簡易跑道
           
<span class="vocabulary">**touchdown**</span> [ˈtʌtʃdaʊn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（飛機或宇宙飛船的）著陸：</span>a perfect touchdown 完美著陸 / The astronauts are preparing for touchdown tomorrow morning. 宇航員正在為明天早上的著陸作準備。
