☀ <span class="category">**上岸 登陸：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**beach**</span> [bi:tʃ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 上岸；使…上岸：</span>He beached the boat. 他把小船拖上岸。/ a beached whale 擱淺在海灘上的鯨 / The boat beached on a mud flat. 船在泥沼上擱淺了。

<span class="vocabulary">**land**</span> [lænd] 
<span class="definition">vi.（乘船）抵達：</span>The troops landed at dawn. 部隊已在黎明登陸。/ The ferry is due to land at 3 o’clock. 渡輪預定3點鐘到岸。<span class="definition">vt. 將人或事物從船上放到地面，即（使）靠岸，登陸。是此義的基礎用詞：</span>瞭解即可
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**landing**</span> [ˈlændɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 敵軍透過艦船或飛機在他國領土進行的登陸：</span>American forces have begun a big landing. 美軍已開始大規模登陸。

<span class="vocabulary">**dock**</span> [dɒk; 美 dɑ:k]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi.（使）船停靠碼頭、進入船塢：</span>The ferry is expected to dock at 6. 渡船預計在6點停靠碼頭。/ The vessel docked at Liverpool in April 1811. 這艘輪船於 1811 年 4 月在利物浦靠岸。/ Russian commanders docked a huge aircraft carrier in a Russian port. 俄羅斯指揮官指揮一艘巨型航空母艦駛入一個俄羅斯港口。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**ashore**</span> [əˈʃɔ:(r)]
<span class="definition">adv. 向岸上、到岸上、在岸上，向陸地、到陸地、在陸地：</span>to come/go ashore 上岸 / a drowned body found washed ashore on the beach 被人發現的一具衝到海灘上的溺水者屍體 / The cruise included several days ashore. 這次航行包括幾天陸上行程。/ Oil has come ashore on a ten mile stretch to the east of Plymouth. 綿延10英里的浮油已經到達了普利茅斯東岸。/ Once ashore, the vessel was thoroughly inspected. 船隻剛一靠岸就接受了徹底檢查。
