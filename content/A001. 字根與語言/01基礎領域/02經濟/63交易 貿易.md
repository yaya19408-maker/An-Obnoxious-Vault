☀ <span class="category">**交易 貿易：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**deal**</span> [di:l] 
<span class="definition">vt. 經營，買賣，交易：</span>The business deals in second-hand books. 這家店經營二手書。/ Our factory deals directly with its customers. 我們工廠直接與客戶交易。<span class="definition">n. [C] 商業交易，常指透過達成協議的方式所完成的交易，且常指具體的一筆商業交易，且口語會話中比較常見：</span>Did you cut a deal (= make one)?（非正式用法）你們交易做成了嗎？/ cut-price deal（英）折價交易 / back-room, secret deal 幕後交易；地下交易 / business, financial, political deal 商業交易；金融交易；政治交易 / arms, weapons deal 軍火交易；武器交易 / deal maker 交易員 / deals with arms dealers 與軍火商的交易 / The deal fell through. 那筆交易吹了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dealing**</span> [ˈdi:lɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [pl.] dealings 可指商業活動、商業往來、商業關係：</span>Have you had any previous dealings with this company? 你曾與這家公司有過業務往來嗎？/ I knew nothing of his business dealings. 我對他生意上的事一概不知道。/ She has always been very polite in her dealings with me. 她在與我交往的過程中總是彬彬有禮。/ They had extensive dealings with officials in Kabul. 他們與喀布林的官員過從甚密。/ We need to be very careful in our dealings with these distressed young people.我們與這些苦惱的年輕人打交道要非常小心。/ direct, personal dealings 直接業務往來；私人業務往來 / The new arrangements will help the banks in their ordinary day-to-day dealings. 新的安排將會促進銀行一般日常交易的進行。/ future dealings 未來的交易 / corrupt, illegal, shady, underhand（尤英）dealings 腐敗的商業往來；非法業務往來；可疑的商業往來；地下交易 / business, commercial, financial dealings 商業往來；貿易往來；金融往來 <span class="definition">2 [U] 與某人做生意時的方式：</span>a reputation for fair/honest dealing 公平/誠信經營的美名 <span class="definition">3 [U, C] 買賣、交易：</span>drug dealing 毒品交易 / dealings in shares 股票交易

<span class="vocabulary">**trade**</span> [treɪd] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 商業交易，貿易。是“交易”的基本用詞，相對正式。使用範圍極廣，即可指宏觀的、地理範圍和時間跨度極大的、涉及領域或行業眾多的“貿易”，也可指微觀的一筆具體的“交易”，不過常用於指前者：</span>foreign trade 對外貿易 / domestic, regional trade 國內貿易；區域貿易 / export trade 出口貿易 / maritime trade 海上貿易 / wine trade 葡萄酒貿易 / trade surplus 貿易盈餘；貿易順差 / trade barrier 貿易壁壘 / trade tariff 貿易關稅 / trade war 貿易戰 / trade agreement 貿易協定 / trade talks 貿易談判 / trade partner 貿易伙伴 / trade mark（通常用trademark）, trade name 商標；品牌 / trade cycle 商業週期 / All around the pyramids, salespeople were doing a roaring trade in souvenirs. 在金字塔周圍，商販們紅紅火火地做著紀念品生意。<span class="definition">2 [U, C] 營業額或交易量，相當於business：</span>Trade was very good last month. 上月的交易量很大。<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 1 trade (in sth) (with sb) 做生意，做買賣，從事貿易：</span>Our products are now traded worldwide. 我們的產品現在銷往世界各地。/ The company openly traded in arms. 這家公司公然進行武器交易。/ They now trade as a partnership. 他們現在合夥做生意。/ to trade with the Indians 與印第安人進行交易 <span class="definition">2 在證券交易所進行（或被）買賣或交易：</span>Shares were trading at under half their usual value. 那些股份被以低於通常價值的一半買賣。/ publicly traded securities 公開交易的證券 
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**trading**</span> [ˈtreɪdɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 買賣活動，如貿易、經商、交易：</span>busy, heavy, hectic, intensive trading 繁忙的交易 / new laws on Sunday trading (= shops being open on Sundays) 關於星期日營業的新法律 / Supermarkets everywhere reported excellent trading in the run-up to Christmas. 各地超級市場報告說聖誕節前生意火爆。/ Shares worth $8 million changed hands during a day of hectic trading. 是日股市交易頻繁，成交量為800萬元。/ In heavy trading, the index closed down 38 points.（今天）交易暢旺，股票指數收跌38點。/ electronic, online trading 電子交易；網上交易 / free trading 自由貿易 / fraudulent, illegal, improper, insider trading 欺詐交易；非法交易；不正當交易；內幕交易 / a Wall Street dealer jailed for insider trading 因內幕交易而遭監禁的華爾街交易商 / global, international trading 全球貿易；國際貿易 / stock-market trading 股票市場交易 / commodity, currency, equity, share, stock, foreign-exchange trading 商品交易；貨幣交易；股票交易；外匯交易 / trading partner 貿易伙伴
           
<span class="vocabulary">**transaction**</span> [trænˈzækʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (between A and B) 兩者之間的商業交易、買賣：</span>cross-border, international transaction 跨境交易；國際交易 / private transaction 私人交易/ fraudulent transaction（尤英）欺詐交易 / The president had entered into fraudulent property transactions. 總裁參與了欺詐性的房地產交易。/ illegal, suspicious transaction 非法交易；可疑交易 / secure Internet transactions 安全的網上交易 / cash, credit transaction 現金交易；信用交易 / credit-card, debit-card transaction 信用卡交易；簽帳金融卡交易 / electronic, Internet, online transaction 電子交易；網際網路交易；線上交易 / business-to-business, e-commerce, over-the-counter transaction 企業對企業的交易；電子商務交易；場外交易 / Electronic banking may make over-the-counter transactions obsolete. 電子銀行服務也許會淘汰場外交易。/ banking, business, commercial, consumer, economic, financial, market, monetary transaction 銀行業務；生意上的業務；商業業務；消費業務；經濟業務；金融業務；市場交易；貨幣交易 / financial transactions between companies 公司之間的財務往來 / currency, foreign-exchange transaction 貨幣交易；外匯交易 / foreign currency transactions 外幣交易 / property（尤英）, real estate（尤美）transaction 房地產交易 / share（尤英）, stock（尤美）股票交易 / to handle a transaction for a client為客戶處理一筆交易 / to ensure the transaction proceeds smoothly 確保交易順利進行 / transaction charges（尤英）, transaction cost 交易費用；交易成本 / Using the Internet can significantly reduce transaction costs. 網際網路的使用可以大大減少交易成本。/ The system records all transactions between the company and its suppliers. 這一系統記錄公司與供應商之間的所有交易。/ transactions over the Internet 網上交易

<span class="vocabulary">**barter**</span> [ˈbɑ:tə(r); 美 ˈbɑ:rt-]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ sth (for sth) / ~ (with sb) (for sth) 同某人以貨物、財產、服務等（而非金錢）做交換，以換取貨物等：</span>The local people bartered wheat for tools. 當地人用小麥換取工具。/ They have been bartering wheat for cotton and timber. 他們一直用小麥交換棉花和木材。/ The prisoners tried to barter with the guards for items like writing paper and books. 囚犯們試著從看守那裡換取稿紙和書之類的東西。/ The market-place and street were crowded with those who'd come to barter. 市場和街道上擠滿了前來做物物交換的人。

<span class="vocabulary">**handle**</span> ['hændl] 
<span class="definition">vt. 買或賣某東西，詞義相當於deal in，但不太常用：</span>to handle stolen goods 買賣贓物

<span class="vocabulary">**protect**</span> [prə'tekt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 常用被動式，指保護（本國或本地的）貿易方，即透過徵收關稅來保護本國的相關產業：</span>to protect domestic markets 保護國內市場

<span class="vocabulary">**protection**</span> [prə'tekʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 貿易保護制度，即透過對進口商品徵收一定的關稅來保護本國的相關產業：</span>trade protection 貿易保護
           
<span class="vocabulary">**protective**</span> [prəˈtektɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. 有貿易保護傾向的，實行關稅保護的：</span>protective tariffs 保護性關稅

相關詞義延伸：
· deal還可指買賣毒品，販毒。

周邊詞彙補充：
· traffic可指非法交易；
· square可指（尤指在生意上）公平的。
