☀ <span class="category">**節約：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**save**</span> [seɪv] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. save (sth) on sth / save sth (on sth) 避免浪費或不必要地使用，即節約，節省：</span>to save water 節約用水 / to save time 節省時間 / save you two hours 節省你兩個小時 / Book early and save £50! 及早預訂，可省50英鎊！/ to save £1 million on defence 在國防開支上節省100萬英鎊 / to save on fares 節省交通費 / to save thousands in travel expenses 節省數千元的差旅費 / to save on printing costs 節省印刷成本 / to scrimp and save 省吃儉用
       
<span class="vocabulary">**saving**</span> [ˈseɪvɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 節省出來的時間、金錢、能源等：</span>cost, financial saving 成本節餘；財務節餘 / efficiency saving（英）效益節約 / energy, fuel, power saving 能源節約；燃料節約；電力節約 / We will pass on this saving to our customers. 我們將會把結餘部分回報給顧客。/ Crushing fuel costs are offsetting other savings. 壓得人喘不過氣來的燃油費用抵消了其他方面的節餘。/ The major savings come from reduced labour costs. 大部分節餘源於勞動力成本的降低。/ a saving for club members 俱樂部會員可享受的優惠 / Buy three and make a saving of 55p. 買三件，就能節省55便士。/ Fill in the form below and you will be making a saving of £6.60 on a one-year subscription. 填寫以下表格，全年訂閱即可為您節省 6.60英鎊。/ a program of household savings on energy use 家庭節能計劃 / With the new boiler you can make big savings on fuel bills. 用這種新鍋爐，能省一大筆燃料開銷。/ The potential savings are enormous. 潛在的節約量非常大。<span class="definition">2 [sing.]（構成形容詞）節約的、節省的：</span>energy-saving modifications 節能改裝 / labour-saving devices 節省人力的裝置 / space-saving fitted furniture 節省空間的定做的傢俱
           
<span class="vocabulary">**saver**</span> [ˈseɪvə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（常構成複合詞）有助於節省的事物：</span>money, time saver 省錢的事物；省時的事物

<span class="vocabulary">**stretch**</span> [stretʃ]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (to sth)（用於否定句和疑問句）（一筆錢）剛能夠節約下來：</span>She suggested to me that I might like to start regular savings and I said Well, I don't know whether I can stretch to that. 她建議我可以開始定期存錢，我說我還不知道錢是不是夠花呢。<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 節儉地使用一定數量的某事物，以延長其使用時間：</span>They're used to stretching their budgets. 他們習慣於精打細算。/ During his senior year his earnings stretched far enough to buy an old car. 他上年紀時省下的收入足夠買一輛舊車了。

<span class="vocabulary">**conservation**</span> [͵kɒnsə'veɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 對某事物的流失、浪費所採取的防止行為，即節約：</span>the conservation of water 節約用水 / energy conservation 能源的節約
            
<span class="vocabulary">**economy**</span> [ɪˈkɒnəmi; 美 ɪˈkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 對時間、金錢、資源、力氣等的節約、節省、節儉：</span>We could achieve major economies in time with this new machinery. 用這臺新機器我們可以節約大量時間。/ possible economies in telephone costs 有可能節約的電話費開支 / We're on an economy drive at home (= trying to avoid waste and spend as little money as possible).（英）我們正在家裡實行勤儉節約。/ Buy the large economy pack (= the one that gives you better value for money)! 買這大包的實惠裝吧！/ an economy pack containing 150 assorted screws 內含150枚各種螺絲的經濟裝 / to fly economy (class) (= by the cheapest class of air travel) 乘坐經濟艙 / an economy fare (= the cheapest) 經濟艙票價 / We need to make substantial economies. 我們需要厲行節約。/ They will make economies by hiring fewer part-time workers. 他們將透過減少僱用兼職工人來節省開支。/ It's a false economy to buy cheap clothes (= it seems cheaper but it is not really since they do not last very long). 買便宜衣服實際上划不來。/ A cheap bed can be a false economy. 便宜的床看似省錢，其實可能不然。/ Bigger markets can provide significant economies of scale. 擴大市場能夠形成強大的規模經濟。/ Savings are being planned as part of a huge economy drive. 計劃中節約資源是這個大規模節約運動的一部分。/ improvements in the fuel economy of cars 汽車節油方面的改進 / It seems a false economy to me to cut down on libraries. 在我看來，減少圖書館的數量並不是真正的節約。/ economy of effort/movement 省力 / It was impressive to see her economy of movement as she worked the machine. 令人印象深刻的是她操作機器十分省力。/ She writes with a great economy of words (= using only the necessary words). 她寫作文字非常簡練。/ There was mostly silence. I have never known such economy with words. 大部分時候都是沉默，我還從未見過如此惜字如金的情形。          

<span class="vocabulary">**economical**</span> [ˌi:kəˈnɒmɪkl; ˌekə-; 美 -ˈnɑ:m-]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 對於所花費的時間或金錢來說很值得的，即經濟實惠的：</span>an economical car to run (= one that does not use too much petrol/gas) 節油型汽車 / Solid fuel would be more economical. 固體燃料會更經濟實惠。/ It would be more economical to buy the bigger size. 買尺寸大點的更實惠。/ This new oven is highly economical. 這個新烤箱非常節能。/ plans to trade in their car for something smaller and more economical 把他們的車折價貼換成更小、更經濟型車的計劃 / It is more economical to wash a full load. 滿缸洗衣更經濟。<span class="definition">2 節約的、節儉的、不超過必要的限度的、簡練高效的：</span>an economical use of space 節約利用空間 / This arrangement is more economical in its use of staff. 這種安排比較節約員工人力。/ more economical of time and resources 更節約時間和資源 / This arrangement is more economical with space. 這種安排更節省空間。/ an economical prose style (= one that uses no unnecessary words) 簡練的散文文體 / His gestures were economical, his words generally mild. 他的肢體語言極盡簡化，措詞總體溫和。/ economical with the truth 隱瞞事實 <span class="definition">3（人）精打細算的：</span>He was economical in all areas of his life. 他在生活的各個方面都精打細算。/ ideas for economical housekeeping. 勤儉持家的觀念           

<span class="vocabulary">**frugal**</span> [ˈfru:gl]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 對金錢、食物等節約的、節儉的（即只能維持基本的生存）：</span>a frugal existence 儉樸的生活 / She lives a frugal life. 她生活儉樸。<span class="definition">2（飯菜）簡單且省錢的：</span>a frugal lunch of bread and cheese 麵包夾乳酪的簡單午餐 / The diet was frugal: cheese and water, rice and beans. 飲食很簡單：乳酪、水、米飯、豆類。

周邊詞彙補充：
· husband可指非常節儉地使用…；
· intensive可指（農業方法）集約的（即用盡量少的土地或資金來生產儘量多的糧食）。
