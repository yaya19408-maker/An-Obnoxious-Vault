☀ <span class="category">**損失 虧損：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**loss**</span> [lɒs] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 商業虧損，與profit相反：</span>quarterly loss 季度虧損 / economic, financial loss 經濟虧損；財務虧損 / trading loss 貿易虧損 / a net loss 淨虧損 / to take losses of £2 million 蒙受200萬英鎊的虧損 / to cut losses 減少虧損 / to absorb losses 抵消虧損 / The store is operating at a loss. 這家商店在虧本經營。/ There’s no way you can make a loss on this deal. 這筆生意你無論如何虧不了。<span class="definition">2 [C] 其他經濟上的損失：</span>If he isn’t prepared to accept this money, then that’s his loss. 如果他不打算接受這筆錢，那他就虧了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**deficit**</span> [ˈdefɪsɪt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] [經濟學] 赤字、逆差、虧損（即花的錢或欠的錢比掙的錢多）：</span>federal, financial, fiscal, trade deficit 聯邦赤字；財務赤字；財政赤字；貿易逆差 / If the government didn't run such huge deficits, the country would not have financial problems. 如果政府沒有造成數額如此巨大的赤字，國家就不會有財務問題。/ to prevent the country moving into deficit 防止國家陷入赤字 / The trade balance has been in deficit for the past five years. 過去五年來貿易狀況一直是逆差。/ The company has run up a deficit of £30,000. 該公司累計虧損了3萬英鎊。/ You cannot cut a budget deficit simply by raising taxes. 不能只透過增稅來削減預算赤字。/ The government was forced to sell state-owned companies to fund the budget deficit. 政府被迫出售國有公司以彌補預算赤字。/ a budget deficit running at 7% of GDP 相當於國內生產總值7%的預算赤字 / The trade balance shows a deficit of two million pounds. 貿易收支顯示有兩百萬英鎊的逆差。/ The US trade deficit ballooned to a record $167 billion. 美國貿易逆差猛增到創紀錄的1,670億美元。/ The UK remained in deficit with all countries outside the EU. 英國對歐盟之外的所有國家都有逆差 / the US trade deficit with Japan 美國對日本的貿易逆差 <span class="definition">2 [C] 金錢的虧空：</span>We will find it hard to make up this deficit. 我們會發現很難彌補這一虧空。/ There's a deficit of $3 million in the total needed to complete the project. 完成這項工程所需資金中有300萬元的虧空。

<span class="vocabulary">**down**</span> [daʊn] 
<span class="definition">adv. 失去了某個數額的錢：</span>At the end of the day we were £20 down. 一天下來我們少了20英鎊。
