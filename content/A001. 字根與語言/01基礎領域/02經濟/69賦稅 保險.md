<span class="category">**保險：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**insurance**</span> [ɪn'ʃʊərəns] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] insurance (against sth) 保險。是此義的常規用詞，最為常用：</span>comprehensive insurance 綜合險 / additional insurance 附加險 / long-term insurance 長期險 / social insurance 社會保險 / commercial insurance 商業保險 / life insurance 人壽保險 / medical insurance 醫療保險 / travel insurance 旅行保險 / accident insurance 意外保險 / insurance coverage 保險範圍 / insurance policy 保險單 / insurance rates 保險費率 / insurance claim 保險索賠 <span class="definition">2 [U] 保險業：</span>insurance business, insurance industry, insurance market 保險業；保險市場 / insurance company, insurance firm 保險公司 / insurance agent, insurance salesman 保險代理人；保險推銷員 / He works in insurance. 他從事保險行業。<span class="definition">3 [U] 向保險公司所繳的保費，或保險公司向被保險人支付的保險金：</span>to pay insurance on your house 交住房保險金 / I haven’t paid the insurance yet this month. 我這個月還沒繳保險金呢。/ When her husband died, she received £50 000 in insurance. 她丈夫去世，她得到了一筆5萬英鎊的保險金。/ We claimed for the car repairs on the insurance. 我們向保險公司索要汽車修理費用。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**assurance**</span> [əˈʃʊərəns; -ʃɔ:r-; 美 əˈʃʊr-]

<span class="vocabulary">**assure**</span> [əˈʃʊə(r); -ʃɔ:(r); 美 əˈʃʊr]

<span class="vocabulary">**policy**</span> ['pɒləsɪ]
