☀ <span class="category">**需求 供給：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**demand**</span> [dɪ'mɑːnd] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] demand (for sth/sb)顧客對商品和服務的需求或需求量：</span>customer, market demand 顧客需求；市場需求 / domestic demand 國內需求 / energy demand 能源需求 / demand for credit 貸款需求 / seasonal demand 季節性的需求 / the law of supply and demand 供求規律 / Demand is exceeding supply. 供不應求。/ Demand for the product is enormous. 這種產品需求量很大。

<span class="vocabulary">**supply**</span> [sə'plaɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 商家對商品和服務的供應或供應量；某事物（一般指物資）的供應量，供給量：</span>Hurry, as we only have a limited supply of these TVs in stock! 這些電視存貨有限，欲購從速！/ Supply did not increase to meet demand. 並沒有增加供給來滿足需求。
