<span class="vocabulary">**warranty**</span> [ˈwɒrənti; 美 ˈwɔ:r-; ˈwɑ:r-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 商品的保修單：</span>The television comes with a full two-year warranty. 這臺電視機有整兩年的保修期。/ Is the car still under warranty? 這輛汽車仍在保修期內嗎？/ specific warranty（尤英）專門保修 / We can not give a specific warranty for the work done on your property. 我們不能為您的房屋裝修工程提供專門的保修服務。/ extended warranty 延期保修 / The tool comes with a lifetime warranty. 這件工具可享受終身保修。/ limited warranty 有限保修 / The computer comes with a year's warranty on all parts. 這臺電腦的所有部件享有一年保修。/ They've extended the warranty and the mileage. 他們延長了保修期和英里數。/ The model carries a three-year warranty. 這種型號附帶三年的保修。/ to void warranty（尤美）取消保修 / Manufacturers void their warranties if the labels have been tampered with. 若擅自塗抹標籤，廠家不予保修。/ Corrosion is not covered by the warranty. 腐蝕不在保修範圍之內。/ warranty period 保修期 / warranty claim 保修要求 / warranty coverage 保修範圍；保修期限 / The warranty coverage is 60 months. 保修期限是60個月。/ warranty repair, warranty service, warranty work（均尤美）保修期內的維修；保修服務 / warranty cost 保修成本 / warranty card（尤美）保修卡 / warranty plan, warranty policy（均尤美）保修計劃；保修條款 / a warranty against storm damage 對風暴造成的損壞的保修 / The warranty on my watch ran out just before it broke. 我手錶壞的時候剛過了保修期。/ He took legal action against the company for breach of warranty. 他起訴該公司違反保修規定。

<span class="vocabulary">**guarantee**</span> [͵ɡærən'ti:] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 公司對自己的商品所附加的保修或包退換憑證，相當於warranty：</span>All our products come with a two-year guarantee. 我們的所有產品都提供兩年質保。/ to gives a year’s guarantee for the product 對該產品提供一年的質保 / under guarantee（英）（美用under warranty）處於質保期內 / a full money-back guarantee 全額退款保障 <span class="definition">vt. guarantee sth (against sth) 對產品的質量做出書面承諾（當產品發生問題的時候給予修理或退換）：</span>This product is guaranteed for a year against any quality problem. 本產品如有任何質量問題可保修一年。/ We guarantee good quality of our products. 我們保證自身的產品質量良好。

<span class="vocabulary">**support**</span> [sə'pɔ:t] 
<span class="definition">n. [U]（公司向其客戶提供的）技術支援（如電腦的技術支援）：</span>customer, tech support 客戶服務（部）；技術支援（部）/ We offer free technical support. 我們免費提供技術支援。

<span class="vocabulary">**change**</span> [tʃeɪndӡ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. change sth (for sth) 英式用法中可指退換貨品，尤指因為貨品有瑕疵。可指買方的退換行為，也可指賣方給買方提供換貨服務的行為：</span>This shirt I bought’s too small--I’ll have to change it for a bigger one. 我買的這件襯衫太小，得換件大的。

<span class="vocabulary">**exchange**</span> [ɪks'tʃeɪndӡ] 
<span class="definition">vt. exchange A for B 退換商品，相當於change：</span>If it doesn’t fit, take it back and the store will exchange it. 如果不合適就把它拿回來，商店將給您退換。

周邊詞彙補充：
· reject可指（因某東西質量不夠好而）決定不去售賣。
