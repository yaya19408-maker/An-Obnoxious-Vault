☀ <span class="category">**預付款項 訂金押金：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**advance**</span> [əd'vɑːns] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] 在工作完成之前對該工作所支付的款項（或支付時間早於預期的款項），如預付款：</span>They offered an advance of £5 000 after the signing of the contract. 他們在合同簽訂後預付了5000英鎊。/ She asked for an advance on her salary. 她要求預支薪金。/ cash advance 預付現金 <span class="definition">vt. advance sth (to sb) / advance (sb) sth 早於通常的付款時間而付款，即預先付款：</span>We will advance you the money. 我們將把款子預付給你。

<span class="vocabulary">**down**</span> [daʊn] 
<span class="definition">adv. 預付一些錢（剩下的後來再付）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**deposit**</span> [dɪ'pɒzɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] a deposit (on sth) 常用單數，指訂金，保證金：</span>We’ve put down a 5% deposit on the house. 我們已支付了房款的5%作為訂金。/ to pay £100 (as a) deposit 支付100英鎊（作為）訂金 <span class="definition">2 [C] 常用單數，指押金：</span>All deposits are refundable. 押金可全數退還。/ non-refundable, non-returnable（英）deposit 不可退還的押金 / security deposit 保證金 / to pay a deposit of $200 支付200美元的押金 / to leave a £50 deposit on the bike 留下50英鎊作為腳踏車的押金 / You’ll get back your deposit. 你可以取回押金。/ If the bike is damaged, you will forfeit your deposit. 如果腳踏車損壞，你的押金會被沒收。<span class="definition">vt. 付訂金，付押金：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**lay-by**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [U] [澳大利亞，紐西蘭，南非英語] 一種預付訂金的貨物購買方式（付了定金之後，貨品給你留著，之後要付全款才能取貨）：</span>You could secure it on lay-by. 你可以預付訂金方式購買此貨。
