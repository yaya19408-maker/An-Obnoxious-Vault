# tan- 黃褐色
- tan 鞣(革)；硝(皮)；曬黑；鞣製
- tanbark, tanoak 鞣革樹皮色
	- 鞣革樹皮色（Tanbark）：一種淺到中等棕色，比小山羊皮棕色稍紅，比阿拉伯山羊皮鞣製的本色手套革棕色稍紅。亦稱：阿爾及利亞棕色（Algerian brown）。
	- ![[RR001. tan- 黃褐色-20250307225412807.webp]]
- tanling 被太陽曬黑的人
- tannable 可鞣製的；適合鞣製的
- tannage 鞣革；鞣製
- tannate 鞣酸
	- albumin tannate 鞣酸蛋白
		- 胃腸疾病用藥等藥品類
	- berberine tannate 鞣酸小檗鹼；無味黃連素
		- 黃色或淡黃色粉末，無臭或微有特異臭，無味，在水、乙醇中幾乎不溶，含小檗鹼25%~32%。適用於痢疾桿菌、大腸桿菌等引起的腸炎、痢疾
	- vasopressin tannate 血管加壓素的單寧酸鹽
	- gelatin tannate 鞣酸明膠
- tannbuschite 暗橄霞玄巖
	- a dark-colored nepheline basalt
- tanned 已鞣製的, 被曬成棕褐色的, 深褐色的
- tanner 鞣皮工；製革工
- tannery, tanyard 製革廠
	- The yard of a tannery
- tannic acid 鞣酸；單寧酸
- tannigen 乙酰鞣酸
	- (obsolete) An acetyl derivative of tannic acid once used as an astringent.
- tannin, tannoid 丹寧；單寧；鞣質
- tanning 鞣皮, 製革, (皮膚)曬黑, 鞭打
- tannish 淡褐色的, 淺棕色的
- tannoform 鞣仿
	- (organic chemistry) A reddish solid that is a condensation product of tannin and formaldehyde.
	- Title: Tannoform
	- CAS Registry Number: 9010-29-1
	- CAS Name: Methyleneditannin
	- Additional Names: tannin-formaldehyde
	- Trademarks: Helgotan
	- Literature References: Prepd by condensing one mole formaldehyde with two moles tannin: Chemnitius, Pharm. Zentralhalle 68, 273 (1927); Schwyzer, Pharm. Ztg. 74, 1334 (1929).
	- Properties: Reddish, odorless, tasteless, bulky powder, mp ~230° with decompn. Practically insol in water; sol in alcohol, alkaline fluids.
	- Melting point: mp ~230° with decompn
	- Therap-Cat: Astringent.
	- Therap-Cat-Vet: Externally as astringent, antiseptic (skin lesions and otorrhea). Has been used internally for diarrhea.
	- Keywords: Astringent.
