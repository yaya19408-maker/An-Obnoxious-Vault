# verb- 說話
- **verb 動詞**
	- **adverb 副詞**
	- **proverb 諺語**
- **verbal 口頭的**
- **verbarian 首創新詞的人**
- **verbatim 逐字地**
- **verbiage 冗詞贅字**
	- **verbose 冗長**
- **verbicide 曲解字義**
- **verbiculture 新詞生成**
- **verbigeration 重複言語**
- **verve 精力**
- **cruciverbalist 填字遊戲專家**
- **in totidem verbis 一字不漏；悉如原文**
- **irony 反諷；諷刺**
	- c. 1500, 
		- from Latin ironia, 
		- from Greek eironeia "dissimulation, assumed ignorance," 
		- from eiron "dissembler," 
			- perhaps related to eirein "to speak," 
				- from PIE *wer-yo-, 
					- suffixed form of root *were- (3) 
						- "to speak" 
							- verb-
- **Laertes 拉爾特斯；拉埃爾特斯**
	- king of Ithaca, father of Odysseus, his name is Greek, literally "gatherer of the people," or "urging the men," 
	- from laos "people" (lay (adj.))
	- + eirein "to fasten together" (series (n.)) 
		- or eirein "to speak, say" (verb).
- **rhetoric 修辭**
	- related to 
		- **rhesis** "speech," (發表演說)
			- parrhesia 自由言說: 暢所欲言
				- Foucault: truth-telling 說真話；坦言不諱
				- pas (所有) + rhesis (發言)
		- **rhema** "word, phrase, verb," literally "that which is spoken" (話語)
			- from PIE *wre-tor-, 
			- from root *were- (3) 
				- "to speak" (verb)
- **word 文字**
	- from PIE *were- (3) "speak, say" (verb)
