# -wise 行為
## in a ... manner
- otherwise 否則
- likewise 同理；同樣地
- leastwise, leastways, at least 至少
- slantwise, slantways 斜著
- endwise, endways 朝向尾端
- nowise, noways, not at all 一點也不
- anywise, at all 無論如何
- crosswise, thwartwise 交叉
- heartwise 呈現心形
- lengthwise, lengthways 縱向地
- stepwise 逐步地；循序漸進
- circlewise 繞圈的
- clockwise 順時針方向
	- counterclockwise (US), anticlockwise (UK) 逆時針方向
- crabwise 偷偷摸摸行進
- stagewise 演出經驗豐富
- streetwise 適應都市生活的
- sunwise, sunward, deasil, deosil, deiseal, deisal, deisul 順時針；向日的
- widdershins, withershins, widershins, widderschynnes 逆時針的；向左的 (lefthandwise)
- timewise 就時間上而言；論時間
- costwise 就成本上而言；論成本
- wise 明智；聰穎
	- wisdom 智慧
		- wisdom tooth 智齒
	- wisecrack 嘴砲
	- wise guy, wiseacre, wiseass, wisenheimer, wisehead, smart aleck 
		- 自以為是的人
	- wise up 幡然醒悟；看清
	- wisehearted, visionary, discerning, insightful 很有眼光
	- unwise 不明智
	- wizard 巫師
- wisent 歐洲野牛
	- bison 野牛
	- buffalo 水牛
