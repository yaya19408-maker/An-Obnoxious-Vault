# -log(ue) 話
- **logistic 物流運輸**
- **analog 類比**
	- analogy 類比; 類推
		- near analogy 近似類推
		- far analogy 延伸類推
		- pure matching 純粹比對
		- carry-over 傳達性比對
	- (stuctural/chemical) analog (結構/化學) 類似物
		- **azalogue 氮基類似物**
			- A compound that is analogous to another, the only difference being that a carbon atom (normally a CH group) has been replaced with a nitrogen one
		- **rapalogue 雷帕黴素類似物**
			- rapamycin 雷帕黴素
- **isotopologue 同位素異數體**
- **catalog(ue) (商品)型錄; 目錄; 一連串壞事; 編入目錄**
- **dialogue 對話**
	- **duologue (限兩人) 對話**
	- **trialogue 三方對話**
	- **tetralogue, quadralogue 四人對話**
	- **megalogue, polylogue 群聊**
		- **multilogue (特指社交軟體) 群聊**
	- **halfalogue 通話**
	- **civilogue 文明討論; 理性討論**
	- **pianologue 鋼琴伴奏獨角戲**
- **prologue 序言**
- **epilogue 後記**
- **gametolog 同源性染色體**
- **interolog 同源蛋白質交互作用**
- **homolog** 
	- homology
	- 同源 (演化/發育意義上的祖先)
	- (organic chemistry)同系物
		- vinylogue 乙烯同系物
- **heterolog 異源**
- **homoplasy 同形質; 非同源相似: 相像但無關**
	- convergency 趨同演化; 收斂進化
	- **orthology 直系同源**
		- ortholog 直系同源體
			- heterospecific, homologous ortholog
				- **xenolog 異種直系同源體**
	- **paralogy 旁系同源**
		- paralog 旁系同源體
			- gene duplication 基因複製
				- speciation 物種形成
					- 前: outparalog 前重複同源基因
					- 後: inparalog 後重複同源基因
				- **ohnolog 基因組重複旁系序列對**
					- One of a pair of paralogous sequences that result from complete genome duplication.
					- coined as an eponymous tribute to Susumo Ohno (大野乾，美國遺傳學家)
- **logarithm (log) 對數**
	- natural logarithm (ln) 自然對數
	- **antilog 反對數: antilogb(n) 或 ${log}^{-1}b(n)$ (也就是 $b^{n}$)**
		- antilogarithm
	- **semilog 半對數**
		- semilog plot 半對數圖
- **product backlog 產品訂單**
- **travelog 旅遊紀錄片; 遊記**
	- vlog (video blog) 影片部落格
- **theologue 神學生**
- **isologue 同構異素物**
	- isologous: having similar structure but different atoms of the same valency
- **apologue 辨惑寓言**
- **actinologue**: an organ or part of an actinomere that corresponds to another in a different actinomere 
	- actinomere 輻射狀體節
		- radial segments composing the body of a radiate animal (Radiata 輻射對稱動物)
- **drunkalogue 酒後說的故事**
- **geologue** (obsolete) **地質學家**
- **monopolylogue 一人飾演多角的戲劇**
- **oenologue** (obsolete) **調酒師**
