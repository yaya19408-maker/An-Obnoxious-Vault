# -(l)ization 化 (變成)
- discursivization 語篇化
	- syntactical organization of discursive elements which are expressed on the textual surface
	- actorialization (who) 制定行動者
		- being an actor (thematic role + narrative function)
	- spatialization (where) 套用空間
		- serving the installation of narrative programs and their sequences
		- places and locations are established in discourse
			- spatial structuring $\longrightarrow$ narrative programs and sequence
	- temporalization (when) 套用時間
		- presenting to turn a narrative arrangement into a story
	- débrayage (disengagement)
	- embrayage (engagement)
- textualization 文本化
	- condensing 濃縮文本
	- expanding 拓展文本
- figurativization 
- thematization 主題化
	- theme
	- rheme
- virtualization 虛擬化
- actualization
- realization
- aspectualization
	- spatial aspectualization
	- temporal aspectualization
	- actorial aspectualization
- focalization
	- internal focalization
	- external focalization
- perspectivization 觀點化
- identitization
- anthropolization (very rare, by Julia Kristeva "'Who' and the Body")
- individualization 個體化
- collectivization 集體化
- axiologization 賦予價值觀
- ideologization 意識形態化
	- deideologization 去意識形態化
		- resymbolization 再象徵化
		- solidarity-making 生成團結感
		- mobilizing progressive ideologies 組織漸變意識形態
	- reideologization 再意識形態化
	- ideologeme 意理素
- mythologization 神話化
	- mythologeme 神話素
- culturalization 文化化
	- cultureme 文化素
	- inculturation (基督教)本地化
	- interculturation 文化互化
	- acculturation 涵化
		- Dimensional Accrual and Dissociation (DAD)
			- idolic
			- symbolic
			- signalic
		- Fourfold Model
			- assimilation
			- integration
			- segregation
			- marginalization
	- enculturation 濡化
		- learning by immersion 沉浸學習
		- tacit knowledge 知識默契
		- socialization 社交
		- language and discourse 語言和言談
		- community membership 社區成員
	- transculturation, or conculturation 跨文化
	- deculturation 去文化
	- neoculturation 新文化
	- ethnoconvergence 族群文化匯流
- politicization 政治化
	- depoliticization 去政治化
	- repoliticization 再政治化
- ethnicization, racialization 族群化
	- deethnicization 去族裔化
	- reethnicization 再族裔化
- neighborization 鄰里化
- ruralization, counterurbanization 鄉村化；反都市化
- urbanization 都市化
- metropolization 大都市化
- megapolization 巨型都市化
- ecumenopolization 普世城化
- globalization 全球化
	- deglobalization 去全球化
	- reglobalization 再全球化
- territorialization 疆域化
	- deterritorialization 去疆域化；解疆
	- reterritorialization 再疆域化
- ethicization 倫理化
- moralization 道德化
- economization 經濟化
- financialization 財政化
- marketization 市場化
- capitalization 資本化
- pedagogization 教學化
