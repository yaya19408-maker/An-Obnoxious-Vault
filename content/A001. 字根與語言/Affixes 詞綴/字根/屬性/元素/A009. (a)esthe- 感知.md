# aesthetics, esthetics 美學
對美的本質及其意義的研究爲主題的學科，乃哲學其中一個重要分支。αἰσθητικός，最初的意義是「對感觀的感受」。由德國哲學家亞歷山大·鮑姆加登首次使用。
美學研究的是審美主體對客體情感反應。判斷美學價值需要審美主體使用感官能力（味覺、觸覺、聽覺、視覺、味覺等）， 然而，審美判斷活動又常超越感官層面。
- **Schönheit, beauty 美**
	- 在1712年由記者約瑟夫·艾迪生在《旁觀者》雜誌上發表的「想像的樂趣」系列文章中提出。1735年，鮑姆加登在他的論文《對詩的哲思》中化用了這一概念，強調對（詩歌）藝術的體會即為感知美的一種方式。
	- **aesthetic judgements 美的判斷**
		- 休謨 (David Hume): taste is not merely "the ability to detect all the ingredients in a composition", but also the sensitivity "to pains as well as pleasures, which escape the rest of mankind."
		- 康德 (Immanuel Kant): "enjoyment" is the result when pleasure arises from sensation, but judging something to be "beautiful" has a third requirement: sensation must give rise to pleasure by engaging reflective contemplation. Judgements of beauty are sensory, emotional and intellectual all at once.
		- Denis Dutton: 6 universal signatures
			- expertise/virtuosity 技術專業
			- nonutilitarian pleasure 非功利愉悅
			- style 風格
			- criticism 評論批判
			- imitation 模仿
			- special focus 特別焦點
- **aesthetic 美感的** = artistic
	- aestheticism 唯美主義
		- 藝術哲學應獨立於所有哲學之外，藝術只能以藝術自身的標準來評判
			- aesthetic movement 唯美主義運動
				- Oscar Wilde 王爾德
				- 1868-1901
		- aesthete 審美家
		- aestheric distance 審美距離; 心理距離
			- 19世紀德國美學家R·費歇爾曾說：「我們只有隔著一定的距離才能看到美，距離本身能美化一切。」但是，布勞所謂審美距離既不是空間距離，也不是時間距離，而是心理距離，即指審美主體與對象在實用性、具體性方面的一種心理間隔。
			- 不能太遠也不能太近
				- 太遠: over-distance
				- 太近: under-distance
- **aesthetician, beautician 美容師; 面容護理專家**
	- cosmetology 美容, skincare

## 正常情況下的整體感覺
- **coenesthesia** = **coenesthesis 綜合體感**
	- the blend of numerous bodily sensations that produces an implicit awareness of being alive and of being in a particular physical condition, for example, well or ill, energetic or tired.
	- 知道自己現在有什麼樣的感覺
	- Coenesthesia--an important differential diagnosis in chronic pain syndromes
		- Coenesthesias can be defined as disorders of body perception or body hallucinations, projected in different parts of the body. Patients complain of intractable pain or dysesthesia. These symptoms are reported even from organs where we usually have no perception. Coenesthesia must be regarded as a symptom that can be observed in various psychiatric diseases, e.g., schizophrenia. However, in rare cases coenesthesias can be documented in neurological diseases, e.g., intracranial tumors or infections. Therefore, accurate assessment of possible differential diagnoses is important. In late stages, coenesthesias can easily be recognized because of their "bizarre" character. In early stages, however, patients often complain of intractable, unlocalizable burning pain. They frequently undergo operations because of their great suffering. The drug treatment of first choice is neuroleptics, but the results are unpredictable.
- **panaesthesia (某人某時的) 整體感覺**
- **somaesthesia 體感**
	- cutaneous and kinesthetic senses
- **bathyesthesia**, deep sensation, Tiefenempfindung **深部感覺**
	- awareness of sensations in the deep (subcutaneous) tissues of the body
	- **proprioception 本體覺; 肌肉運動知覺**

			- 對身體各部位的位置和運動狀況的感覺，也就是肌肉、腱和關節的感覺

		- 在不同情境下評估最舒適的光強度
		- 虚拟技术+日光设计加强自然感受
			- 经过长期的进化，人体和自然采光之间有着千丝万缕的联系。在自然照明中，一天的色温变化参与了人的昼夜节律调节，对人的压力调节、睡眠等生理活动起到了重要作用。长期在恒定人工照明环境下的人可能会产生节律紊乱，同时单调的照明也会影响人的情绪，进而导致工作效率下降。目前评价室内光环境和舒适度的物理指标包括照度、亮度和色温等等，然而此类自上而下的评价指标往往缺乏对“光感受”的考量。从健康角度考虑，未来的建成环境设计需要从自然中学习，把自然照明中变化的色温、照度、亮度等纳入设计，创造有视感变化的人工照明。结合沉浸式体验，虚拟技术能评测室内日光和人工照明、视觉感知和情绪。新技术能自下而上捕捉使用者的主观感受，辅助前期立面和智能照明系统设计，增进方案优选的效率。

- **chemesthesis 化學味覺**
	- 提到舌頭，很容易想到五味——甜、鹹、苦、酸、鮮。當味蕾細胞表面的受體被食物激活，並觸發了大腦中的味覺神經時，人類便會產生味覺，感受到美味的燒烤或新鮮的草莓等各種味道。但舌頭的功能遠不止於此。它還能感應溫度和壓力，以及模擬這兩種感覺的化學物質——很多食物中都包含這些化學物質，由此產生的古怪味覺被稱作化學味覺(chemesthesis)，你大概每天都能嘗到一些這樣的味道(像是辣味)。

## Synaesthesia 聯覺
- **synaesthesia 聯覺; 通感**
	- 一個誘發刺激 (inducer) $\rightarrow$ 多種感官即時反應 (concurrent)
	- synaesthete 聯覺者
	- Colizoli et al. (2014) 如何判斷有無聯覺
		- consistent 感官一致性: 一感官同個多種反應
		- automatic 非自願自動觸發: 不能控制自己何時產生甚麼反應，而這些感覺亦是自動衍生的 
		- conscious 有意識觸發: 可以在沒有外部刺激下有意識地體驗到聯覺。例如聯覺者能在沒有顏色的刺激或提示下看到字母 A 是黃色的 
		- perceptual 有感知: 可透過心理生理學的視覺測量 (psychophysical visual tasks) 或神經影像學 (neuroimaging) 來量度
		- perceptual presence 感知存在: 聯覺的反應是真實存在的，並非只在腦中聯想出來，而聯覺者亦能清楚分辦聯覺與幻覺的體驗
	- [[MMDL000. 多模態(教學) Multimodality#**Synaesthesia** 通感; 聯覺]]
	- Types:
		- **grapheme-color synaesthesia 字素-顏色聯覺**
			- 當人們看到某個特定的數字或字母時，他們會同時看見某個顏色。例如當數字「 2 ｣ 被放入一堆數字「 5 」中，正常人需要幾要幾秒鐘才能找到「 2 」（圖左）；但聯覺者會看到不同顏色的「 5 」和「 2 」，所以他們能即時看到分別 （圖右）
			- ![[A009. (a)esthe- 感知-20240814160941762.webp]]
		- **auditory-tactile synaesthesia 聽觸聯覺**
			- sounds cause you to feel touch-based sensations, such as temperature changes, pressure or pain.
			- **autonomous sensory meridian response (ASMR) 自發性感官經絡反應**
				- 屬於euphoric paresthesia
		- **mirror-touch synaesthesia 鏡觸聯覺**
			- 聯覺者看見別人被觸碰時，也會同時喚起他們自己的觸覺，令他們亦感覺被觸碰。早前亦有學者指出鏡像觸覺與同理心（empathy）有相關性，因為影響同理心的共享情感神經系統會在被動觀察中被激活 (Banissy & Jamie, 2007)，所以在這些聯覺者或許是比較有同理心，容易理解他人的感受。
		- **lexical gustatory synaesthesia 詞味聯覺**
			- 佔大約 0.2% 的人口，指當聯覺者聽到某些詞語時會感覺到特定的嗅覺或味覺 (Simner & Ward, 2006)。例如聽到「足球」，他們可能會感覺到「巧克力」的味道
		- **spatial sequence synaesthesia (SSS) 空間序列聯覺**
			-  visualising certain sequences in physical space.
			- "Spatial sequencers" perceive the months, dates, numbers, letters or other elements in a sequence in the physical space around them or in front of them. Apart from spatial position, these elements may also have their own colour, texture and shape.
			- ![[A009. (a)esthe- 感知-20240814182743972.webp]]
			- types:
				- calendar synaesthesia, time-space synaesthesia 時空聯覺
					- ![[A009. (a)esthe- 感知-20240814182454133.webp]]
				- number form synaesthesia 數字形式聯覺
					- 數字心智圖
					- ![[A009. (a)esthe- 感知-20240814182502088.webp]]
				- letter form synaesthesia 字形聯覺
					- ![[A009. (a)esthe- 感知-20240814182549237.webp]]
				- spatial visualization (or other sequences)
					- school subjects
					- Bible
					- signs of the zodiac
					- ![[A009. (a)esthe- 感知-20240814182604689.webp]]
		- **kinesthetic synaesthesia 運動聯覺**
		- **ordinal linguistic personification 序數語言人格化**
			- 賦予字母或數字形象
		- **misophonia 恐音症**(嚴格意義上不算聯覺)
			- Misophonia is a disorder where people have abnormally strong and negative reactions to the ordinary sounds humans make, such as chewing or breathing.
			-  It is a neurophysiological condition, an auditory perception disorder that affects people with and without synesthesia (it’s been estimated that between 15% and 20% of the general population could have moderate or severe misophonia and up to 37% mild symptoms), and while the more severe version may possibly be more common in synesthetes, particularly those with types relating to sound and music, there are no actual figures clarifying whether or not this is true or to what extent. 
			- 恐音症是一種神經生理病癥，且只是在聯覺者的身上症狀更明顯更嚴重，非聯覺者也
		- **chromesthesia 連帶色覺**
			- 聽到日常生活的各種聲音或音樂時會看見不同的顏色，有些人更能看見不同音符顏色
		- **graphaesthesia, graphagnosia 皮膚書寫覺: 能感知並辨識皮膚上寫的字**
			- the ability to recognize symbols when they’re traced on the skin.
			- $\leftrightarrow$ agraphesthesia 皮膚書寫覺缺失
			- **stereognosis 立體感知** $\leftrightarrow$ astereognosis
				- the recognition of solid objects through touch. Loss of stereognosis also indicates a problem with the somatosensory cortex. It’s known as astereognosis and can be diagnosed during a neurological exam.
		- **ideaesthesia 概念聯覺** (嚴格意義上不算聯覺)
			- 由概念引發出知覺
			- it is actually an alternative name for synesthesia or perhaps a more adequate way of describing it. In accordance with this theory, rather than being an interaction between one sense and another, synesthesia actually consists of an interaction between a concept or idea and a sense.
		- **phonaesthesia 音義聯覺** (目前不算在聯覺之內)
			- = sound symbolism 語音象徵主義
			- Psychologists Vilayanur S. Ramachandran and Edward Hubbard, in deference to an earlier study by Wolfgang Kohler, showed two shapes to a test group and asked which one they would call a “bouba” and which a “kiki.” One shape had rounded edges and the other had sharp edges with points. Over 95% of the test subjects said that the bouba had round edges and the kiki had sharp edges, suggesting that the participants equate certain sounds with shapes. The phenomenon that connects the sound of a word and its meaning is called phonesthesia or, as the Oxford English Dictionary describes it, “sound symbolism.”
			- **Bouba/Kiki effect 波巴/奇奇效應**
			- [[phonestheme]] 語音象徵素; 音表徵素; 聯覺音組 [(collections)](https://web.archive.org/web/20011115092708/http://www.geocities.com/SoHo/Studios/9783/phond1.html)
				- $\in$ Peirce's icon
				- A phonestheme is different from a phoneme (a basic unit of word-differentiating sound) or a morpheme (a basic unit of meaning) because it does not meet the normal criterion of compositionality.
			- phonesthetics 悅耳學; 美音學
				- euphony 悅耳
					- Eurythmics 舞韻樂隊
						- Sweet Dreams Are Made of This
					- eurythmy 優律思美
				- cacophony 難聽
					- Cacofonix (in Asterix)
						- 高盧英雄傳
						- 他自認是個天才，但是其他人都覺得他五音不全。
