Lex 即詞，單詞編排的樣式，可以理解為word
>A lex may have only one phone: English I (if the diphthong is thought of as being just one phone) or Swedish i ( = in). In this case some other segments ("syllable", lex and its prosode) happen to be as short as the phone but in most cases the phone is indeed the shortest segment.
>一個lex可以只包含一個 phone，例如英語中的 "I"（如果將diphthong視為一個 phone）或瑞典語中的 "i"（表示 "in"）。在這種情況下，其他單位（如syllable、lex和它們的韻律prosode）可能和 phone 一樣短。
>
>The other things are syllabs, syllab prosodes, lexes, lex prosodes, syntagms, syntagm prosodes and meaning. It is of interest to note- that, although, in a sentence, one or more phones may be so unclear that they provide insufficient clues for their identification, the rest may be sufficient to allow the hearer so to speak to fill in what he has only partly heard clearly, or perhaps not heard at all. Thus it is clear that not only phones but also other factors contribute to the identification of spoken "words".
>其他相關的語音單位包括音節、音節韻律、詞、詞韻律、句子、句子韻律和語義。值得注意的是，即使在一個句子中，一個或多個 phones 可能因模糊不清而無法被清晰辨識，但其餘部分可能足夠讓聽者填補他只聽到部分或根本沒有聽到的部分。因此，不僅 phones 而且其他因素也有助於辨識口語中的 "詞語"。

The term "lex building" implies "syllab building" and "morph building" as syllabs and morphs are segments of lexes. It would be a possible alternative to say that a phone has basically "syllab building" (or "morph building") function.

lexeme是一組涵蓋字的原型和變化的集合
e.g., 一個"RUN lexeme"包含了run, runs, running, ran
而run是詞目(lemma)，作用為詞目化 (lemmalization)
running的-(n)ing則為末尾 (desinence)
加上詞幹 (stem: 詞根root (即run) + 派生詞素derivational morpheme)
