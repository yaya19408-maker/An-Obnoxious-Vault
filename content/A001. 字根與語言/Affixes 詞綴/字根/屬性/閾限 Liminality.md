# Liminality 閾限 (anthropology)
- Zohar (2023) - Betwixt and Between Liminality and Marginality: Mind the Gap
	- (anthropology) Arnold van Gennep (1909) - The Rite of Passage
		- pre-liminal (separation 分離) 前閾限
			- to symbolically detach the individual from an existing point in the social structure, and the former social status no longer applies to the individual after this separation
		- liminality (transition 過渡) 閾限：曖昧不明、充滿不確定性的階段
			- the ambivalence, confusion, or disorientation experienced in the middle stage of a rite of passage, when participants no longer hold their pre-ritual status but have not yet begun the transition to the status they will hold at the end of the rite
		- post-liminal (reintegration/reincorporation 重聚) 後閾限
			- to allow the individual to adopt a new social status and re-enter society, and if this re-entry does not occur, liminality does not end, a status possible in hypermodern society but not in small-scale society
	- marginality 邊緣性
		- Wiebke Bebermeier et al (2016)
		- 國家權力以首都為中心發散至邊界，均等運作在每一部分，也因此使邊緣地區受中心 (mitte)支配、依賴之、屈從之、迎合其需求，也象徵著劣勢，是應避免或逃脫的狀態
			- mitte 中心
				- 對中心 (center) 的隱喻，同樣也可包含「處在中間的中心」
			- ecological marginality 生態邊緣性
			- economic marginality 經濟邊緣性
			- social marginality 社會邊緣性
			- spatial marginality 空間邊緣性
- Victor & Edith Turner (1969, p. 32)
	- communitas 共同體：透過閾限狀態，原有的社會階級、文化規範被排除，這時人們處於一種超越正式關係的狀態，使得人們團結一致
		- existential/spontaneous communitas 自發共同體
			- the transient personal experience of togetherness
		- normative communitas 規範共同體
			- transformed from its existential state to being organized into a permanent social system due to the need for social control
		- ideological communitas 意識形態共同體
			- an outward form of an inward experience of happening
		- spontaneity 自發性
		- instantaneity 即時性
		- concreteness 具體性
	- social drama 社會劇：閾限儀式與社會結構的功能關係
		1. breach 分裂；違法
			- 象徵性決裂是利他的，違規者將自己視為代表，而非孤軍奮戰
		2. crisis 危機產生
			- 社會過程相對穩定狀態間之門檻，有別於閾限，危機並非隔離
		3. redress 修補行動
			- 補救能力決定回復穩態的可能性
		4. reintegration 重整
			- 可能回復，可能大洗牌
	- anti-structure 反結構
		- 閾限狀態下形成充滿曖昧與不確定性(betwixt and between)的共同體 (communitas)
		- Liminality and communitas, which together constitute "ritual anti-structure", call attention to the arbitrariness and artificiality of social structure and social norms.
