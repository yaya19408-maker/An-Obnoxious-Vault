# -fix 綴
## affix 詞綴；字根, affixation 加綴
affix是morpheme的下义词。也就是集合概念，一个affix一定是一个morpheme，但是一个morpheme不一定是affix。affix都是bound morpheme，但是bound morpheme不一定是affix
- derivational affix 派生詞綴
- inflectional affix 屈折詞綴
- word-formation affix 構詞詞綴；詞變詞綴
- grammatical affix 語法詞綴
### adfix, adfixation 外綴
#### prefix, prefixation 前綴；字首
- metric prefix, SI prefix
#### suffix, suffixation 後綴；字尾
### circumfix, circumfixation (CIRC) 環綴；框綴
- 像是
	- en(m)-en
		- enlighten, enliven, embolden
	- a-ing (archaic, dialectical, figure of speech)
		- a-going, a-hunting, a-blooming, a-ploughing, a-painting, a-running
	- (u)in-ble(y), (u)in-ly
		- inadvisable, incredible, ineffable...
		- indifferently, unbelievably, undeniably...
	- supercalifragilisticexpialidociously
又稱
- 接周辭（parafix, parafixation）
- 前後綴（confix, confixation）
- 兩面接辭（ambifix, ambifixation）
### infix, infixation 中綴；詞嵌；接中辭
a derivational or inflectional affix appearing in the body of a word (such as Sanskrit _-n-_ in _vindami_ "I know" as contrasted with _vid_ "to know")
- "That is fan-fucking-tastic!"
- "Abso-freaking-lutely!"
- "Back-ass-wards." (from Oklahoma) 
- editor-in-chief
- writer-cum-publisher
