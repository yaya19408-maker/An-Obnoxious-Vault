☀ <span class="category">**姿勢姿態 手勢步態：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**posture**</span> [ˈpɒstʃə(r); 美 ˈpɑ:s-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（坐或站立的）姿勢：</span>comfortable, relaxed posture 舒適的姿勢；輕鬆的姿勢 / upright, sitting, supine postures 直立的姿勢；坐著的姿勢；仰臥的姿勢 / Good posture is essential when working at the computer. 用電腦工作時良好的姿勢極其重要。/ Back pains can be the result of bad posture. 腰背疼可能是不良姿勢造成的。/ You can make your stomach look flatter instantly by improving your posture. 透過採取更好的坐姿，你的腹部馬上就會看起來平坦一些。/ Exercise, fresh air, and good posture are all helpful. 鍛鍊、新鮮空氣和好的坐立姿勢都很有益。/ body posture 體態 / yoga posture 瑜伽姿勢 / She has very good posture. 她儀態很優雅。/ He gulped as he struggled to regain his posture.（regain尤為美式用法）他一飲而盡，掙扎著要恢復先前的姿勢。/ He sat in a posture of absolute respect. 他畢恭畢敬地坐著。/ a poor posture for driving 不良的駕駛姿勢

<span class="vocabulary">**attitude**</span> ['ætɪtju:d] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [正式] 身體的一個姿勢：</span>Her hands were folded in an attitude of prayer. 她雙手合攏成祈禱姿勢。

<span class="vocabulary">**position**</span> [pə'zɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 某人的坐立方式，即姿勢，姿態：</span>kneeling, sitting, standing position 跪姿；坐姿；站姿 / sleeping, lying position 睡姿；臥姿 / foetal position 胎位 / Make sure that you are working in a comfortable position. 工作時一定要保持舒適的姿勢。/ My arms were aching so I shifted (my) position slightly. 我胳膊疼了，所以稍微變了變姿勢。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**footwork**</span> [ˈfʊtwɜ:k; 美 -wɜ:rk]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（體育、舞蹈的）步法、腳法、腳步動作：</span>This exercise improves your coordination, balance, timing and footwork. 這項鍛鍊可以提高協調能力、平衡力、把握時機的能力和腳法。/ deft（尤英）, fancy, good, neat（英）, nifty（英）, nimble（尤英）footwork 靈巧的步法；華麗的步法；優雅的步法；乾淨利落的步法；熟練的步法；靈活敏捷的步法 / She showed some nimble footwork and looked a promising player. 她展示出的步法極其靈活，看來是一個有前途的運動員。/ He needs to improve his footwork around the basket. 他需要提高在籃下的腳步動作。

<span class="vocabulary">**walk**</span> [wɔ:k] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 步態：</span>I recognized her by her walk. 我根據她走路的樣子認出了她。/ stately walk 莊重的步伐 / funny, ungainly walk 滑稽的步態；笨拙的步態 / She did a silly walk to amuse her friends. 她走著滑稽的步子逗朋友們開心。
     
<span class="vocabulary">**tread**</span> [tred]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 步態、步法：</span>A policeman walked by with a slow, measured tread. 一名警察邁著小心的步子慢慢走了過去。

<span class="vocabulary">**stride**</span> [straɪd]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 走路或跑步的方式，如步態、步伐：</span>loping stride 大步 / confident, determined, purposeful stride 自信的腳步；堅決的腳步；堅定的腳步 / his familiar purposeful stride 他那熟悉而堅定的步伐 / He lengthened his stride to keep up with her. 為了跟上她，他加大了步子。/ Without breaking her stride she ducked the ball. 她躲開了那個球，步伐一點兒沒亂。/ She did not slow her stride until she was face to face with us. 她沒有放慢腳步，徑直走到我們面前。/ He matched his stride to her slower pace. 他放慢自己的步子，與她保持一致。/ find your stride（比喻用法）, get into your stride（英，比喻用法）, hit your stride（尤美，比喻用法）進入狀態 / The team took time to get into their stride. 該隊逐漸進入了狀態。/ The show finally hit its stride in the second season. 這個節目在第二季終於進入了狀態。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**gait**</span> [geɪt]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 走路的方式，即步態：</span>shuffling gait 拖著腳步的走 / unsteady gait 不穩的腳步 / slow gait 緩慢的腳步 / He walked with a rolling gait. 他走起路來搖搖晃晃。/ a tubby little man in his fifties, with sparse hair and a rolling gait 一位50多歲的矮胖男子，頭髮稀疏，走路晃晃悠悠 / His movements were clumsy, and his gait peculiarly awkward. 他動作笨拙，步態尤其難看。

<span class="vocabulary">**carry**</span> ['kærɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 保持自己的某種姿勢；或做出某種姿勢：</span>to carry yourself well 姿勢正確 / She carried themselves with great pride and dignity. 她端著非常驕傲和高貴的姿態。

周邊詞彙補充：
· gesture可指用你的手、頭、臉等所做出的示意性動作（該動作表達了某種意思）。
