<span class="definition">n. <span class="vocabulary">**correspondence**</span> [ˌkɒrəˈspɒndəns; 美 ˌkɔ:rəˈspɑ:n-; ˌkɑ:-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] ~ (with sb) 來往的信件、往來的書信：</span>confidential, personal, private correspondence 機密信函；私人信件 / business, diplomatic, official correspondence 商業信函；外交文書；公函 / regular correspondence 定期書信 / email, written correspondence 電子郵件；手寫信件 / Numerous items of correspondence have been received on this subject. 關於這個話題，我們已經收到大量來信。/ He was leafing through piles of correspondence.（pile尤為英式用法）他正在迅速翻閱一摞摞的信件。/ He always replied to his correspondence. 來信他總是會回。/ She virtually never mentions him in her correspondence or notebooks. 她在書信和筆記中幾乎從不提及他。/ Please send correspondence to 'Money Monthly'. 請將信件寄往《財富月刊》/ The secretary deals with all the correspondence. 秘書處理所有的信件。/ I would spend the time reading or catching up on my correspondence. 我要花點兒時間讀信，處理一下堆積的信函。/ The department intercepted the correspondence of foreign diplomats. 該部門擷取了外國外交官的信件。/ correspondence course, correspondence school（美）函授課程；函授學校 / I did a correspondence course in economics. 我學習了一門經濟學的函授課程。/ correspondence column（尤英）讀者來信專欄 / the correspondence columns of the London Review of Books'《倫敦書評》的讀者來信專欄 / All our business is conducted by correspondence. 我們所有的生意都是透過信函進行的。/ files full of confidential correspondence relating to the company's expansion plans 全是有關公司擴張計劃機密信函的卷宗 / I have seen the correspondence between the company and the college.我曾看過公司與大學之間的往來信函。/ The editor welcomes correspondence from readers on any subject. 編輯歡迎讀者就任何話題來信。/ copies of her correspondence with the composer 她與作曲家來往信件的影印件

<span class="vocabulary">**note**</span> [nəʊt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一則簡短的、非正式的書信，即便條：</span>She left a note for Ben on the kitchen table. 她在廚房的餐桌上給本留了個便條。/ brief note 便條 / credit, love, sick（英）, suicide, thank-you note 欠條；情書；病假條；絕命書；致謝便函 / sticky note（尤美）便利貼 / scribbled note 草草寫下的留言 / crumpled note 皺巴巴的便條 / I saw you two passing notes during class. 我看到你倆在上課時傳紙條了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**correspondent**</span> [ˌkɒrəˈspɒndənt; 美 ˌkɔ:rəˈspɑ:n-; ˌkɑ:-]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（與形容詞連用）給別人寫信的一個人：</span>She's a poor correspondent (= she does not write regularly). 她是個懶於寫信的人。

· note可指外交照會；
· above可指（在書信中）前文述及的。
