☀ <span class="category">**簡訊 電報：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**text**</span> [tekst] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 手機簡訊，相當於text message：</span>text message 手機簡訊 / to send a text 傳送簡訊 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 傳送手機簡訊：</span>Text me when you get home. 你到家後給我發個簡訊。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**telegram**</span> [ˈtelɪgræm]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 用電訊號傳遞的一則資訊，即電報：</span>Scores of congratulatory telegrams and letters greeted Franklin on his return. 富蘭克林一回來，就收到了好幾十封賀電和賀信。/ The President received a briefing by telegram. 總統收到用電報發來的一份簡報。

<span class="vocabulary">**wire**</span> ['waɪə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式；尤美] 電報，相當於telegram：</span>to send a wire 發電報 <span class="definition">vt. [尤美] wire (sth) (to sb) / wire sb (sth) 給某人發電報：</span>He wired the news to us. 他用電報把這個訊息通知了我們。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**cable**</span> [ˈkeɪbl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [過時] 電報系統傳輸的資訊：</span>She sent a cable to her mother. 她給母親發了封電報。<span class="definition">vi. [過時] 給某人發電報：</span>'Don't do it again,' Franklin cabled her when he got her letter. 富蘭克林收到她的信後給她拍了封電報,說:“別再那麼做了。” / She had to decide whether or not to cable the news to Louis. 她得決定要不要將這個訊息電告路易斯。
