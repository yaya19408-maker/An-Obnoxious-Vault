☀ <span class="category">**裝竊聽器：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**wire**</span> ['waɪə] 
<span class="definition">vt. wire sth (for sth) 給…安裝竊聽器（以竊聽其他人之間的談話），相當於bug：</span>The room had been wired for sound. 這個房間已經裝上了竊聽器。

<span class="vocabulary">**tap**</span> [tæp] 
<span class="definition">vt. [尤英] 在電話上安裝竊聽器（使其通話可被竊聽）：</span>His phone was being tapped. 他的電話在被人竊聽。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**bug**</span> [bʌg]
<span class="definition">vt. 在（某處）裝竊聽器：</span>They bugged her hotel room. 他們在她的旅館房間裡裝了竊聽器。/ He heard that they were planning to bug his office. 他得知他們打算在他辦公室安裝竊聽器。
