☀ <span class="category">**預測預言 預報預告：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**forecast**</span> ['fɔ:kɑːst] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（基於現有的資訊對未來將要發生的事所進行的）預測：</span>economic, financial, market, weather forecast 經濟預測；財務預測；市場預測；天氣預報 / The interest rate is in line with the forecast. 利率與預測的結果一致。<span class="definition">vt. 基於現有的資訊對未來將要發生的事進行預測，類似於predict：</span>Experts are forecasting a recovery in the economy. 專家預測經濟將復甦。/ The report forecasts that prices will rise by 3% next month. 報告預測下月物價將上漲3%。/ Snow is forecast for tomorrow. 預報明天有雪。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**scenario**</span> [səˈnɑ:riəʊ; 美 səˈnærioʊ]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（對將來情況的）設想、預測：</span>Let me suggest a possible scenario. 我來設想一種可能出現的情況。/ The worst-case scenario (= the worst possible thing that could happen) would be for the factory to be closed down. 最壞的情況可能是工廠關閉。/ In a worst-case scenario, the disease will reach epidemic proportions. 最壞的情況是這種病流行起來。/ the nightmare scenario of a divided and irrelevant Royal Family 對於王室四分五裂、無足輕重的可怕設想 / The more likely scenario is that the president will resign and an election will be held. 更可能出現的情況是總統下臺，重新舉行選舉。/ the scenario painted by some sections of the Western press 部分西方媒體描繪的情形 / He enjoyed playing out the various scenarios in his own mind. 他喜歡在心裡把各種各樣的局面都設想一番。/ Similar scenarios are playing out across the country. 類似情形正在全國各地蔓延開來。/ Under any of these scenarios, the company will run into debt. 這些情況出現任何一種時，公司都將負債。

<span class="vocabulary">**predict**</span> [prɪ'dɪkt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 說將來要發生什麼，即預言，預測，預告，預報等，相當於forecast，二者未見明顯差別。是一個基礎且常規的用詞，極為常用：</span>Nobody could predict the outcome. 誰也無法預料結果如何。/ It was predicted that inflation would continue to fall. 據預測，通貨膨脹率將繼續下降。/ The trial is predicted to last for months. 預料審訊將持續數月之久。/ to predict the flight of a ball 預測球體的飛行路線 / to predict the weather 預測天氣
           
<span class="vocabulary">**prediction**</span> [prɪˈdɪkʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 預言、預測、預告（可以是一種言論，也可以是一種行為）：</span>Not many people agree with the government's prediction that the economy will improve. 沒有多少人贊同政府認為經濟將會有所改善的預測。/ The results of the experiment confirmed our predictions. 實驗結果證實了我們的預測。/ Skilled readers make use of context and prediction. 閱讀能力強的人會利用上下文及推測來理解文意。/ It's difficult to make accurate predictions about the effects on the environment. 很難準確預測對環境產生的影響。/ He was unwilling to make a prediction about which books would sell in the coming year. 他不願意預測來年哪些書會有銷路。/ Predictions that the recession will be short are small comfort to those already affected. 關於經濟衰退不會延續很長時間的預測對那些已經受到影響的人來說只是小小的安慰。/ the best available prediction of future interest rates 手頭現有的對未來利率的最好的預測 / It turned out my prediction was right. 結果證明我的預測是正確的。/ the dire predictions by economists of a worldwide recession 經濟學家對全球性經濟衰退悲觀的預測 / The sales results exceeded even the most optimistic predictions. 銷售業績甚至超過了最樂觀的預測。/ In this study, we made no specific predictions about likely outcomes. 在這項研究中，我們沒有對可能的結果作具體的預測。/ the discrepancy between the theoretical predictions and the results 理論預測和結果之間的差異 / computer 計算機預測 / earthquake, weather prediction 地震預測；氣象預測 / Could the panel give their prediction as to who he will appoint to the post? 委員會可以預測出他會委任誰擔任此職位嗎?/ This study tests these predictions. 這項研究測試了這些預測。/ The results of the experiment confirmed their predictions. 實驗結果證實了他們的預測。/ Their success defies the predictions made by most experts. 他們的成功使大多數專家的預測落空。/ Our prediction turns out to be correct. 我們的預測證明是正確的。/ Six hundred workers there lost their jobs today, amid gloomy predictions that there could be worse to come. 今天那裡有六百名工人丟了工作，同時還有各種悲觀的傳言說更糟的還在後面。/ Contrary to almost all predictions, however, the government did not fall. 與幾乎所有的預言相反，政府並沒有垮臺。/ Despite earlier dire predictions, shares remained steady. 儘管早先有一些不祥的預言，股價依舊穩定。/ I've learned not to make predictions about the weather. 我已經吸取教訓，不再預測天氣了。/ the government's prediction for unemployment 政府的失業預測 / their predictions of future growth 他們對未來增長的預測 / Let me get your predictions on the final score. 告訴我你對最後比分的預測吧。                      

<span class="vocabulary">**predictable**</span> [prɪˈdɪktəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. 可預見的、可預料的：</span>a predictable result 可預見的結果 / The ending of the book was entirely predictable. 那本書的結局完全是可以想見的。/ In March and April, the weather is much less predictable. 三、四月份的天氣非常不好預測。/ This was a predictable reaction, given the bitter hostility between the two countries. 考慮到這兩個國家之間激烈的敵對情緒，這種反應是可以預見的。/ The result was entirely predictable. 這個結果完全在預料之中。/ Recent changes make the future even less predictable. 最近的變化使未來更難預測。/ The government's reaction was sadly predictable. 可悲的是，政府的反應完全可以預料到。/ He asked whether this was predictable from previous performances. 他問這是否可以從以前的表現中預料到。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**prophecy**</span> [ˈprɒfəsi; 美 ˈprɑ:f-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 預言，尤指具有宗教力量或具有魔力的人所說的預言：</span>to fulfil a prophecy (= make it come true) 實現預言 / The youth, too, fulfilled the prophecy. 那個年輕人，同樣地，讓預言說中了。/ self-fulfilling prophecy 自我應驗的預言 / low expectations that become a self-fulfilling prophecy 自我應驗的低期望 / Biblical, Messianic, Old Testament prophecy《聖經》中的預言；彌賽亞的預言；《舊約》中的預言 / ancient prophecy 古老的預言 / false prophecy 假預言 / Macbeth believed the witches' prophecy about his future. 麥克白相信女巫對他的未來的預言。/ The poem contains a bleak prophecy of war and ruin. 這首詩含有對戰爭與毀滅的悲觀的預言。<span class="definition">2 [U] [正式] 預言的能力：</span>She was believed to have the gift of prophecy. 據信她有預言的天賦。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**prophetic**</span> [prəˈfetɪk]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 [正式] 預見的事情真的發生的，即正確預言的、有預見的：</span>Many of his warnings proved prophetic. 他的許多警告都證明是有先見之明的。/ This ominous warning soon proved prophetic. 這個不祥的警告很快證明是有預見性的。/ Friends recalled Elisabeth's prophetic words of several years ago. 朋友們回想起幾年前伊麗莎白預言性的一番話。/ His warnings about the trip proved prophetic. 他警告說旅途中可能發生事情，事實證明他的預見是對的。<span class="definition">2 [正式] 預言的；先知的；預言者的：</span>a charming romance intermingled with scientific fact and prophetic vision 融科學事實和先知遠見於其中的一個動人浪漫故事 / a book that was prophetic of future developments in science 預示未來科學發展的書 / the prophetic books of the Old Testament《〈聖經〉舊約》先知書

<span class="vocabulary">**anticipate**</span> [ænˈtɪsɪpeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. 預料、預期：</span>We don't anticipate any major problems. 我們預料不會發生什麼大問題。/ I don't anticipate it being a problem. 我認為它不會成為一個問題。/ It is widely anticipated that she will resign. 大家都預料她會辭職。/ They anticipate moving to bigger premises by the end of the year. 他們預期年底前遷入較大的經營場址。/ At the time we couldn't have anticipated the result of our campaigning. 那時我們不可能預料到我們這項運動的結果。/ We anticipate that sales will rise next year. 我們預料明年銷售量將會增加。/ It is anticipated that inflation will stabilize at 3%. 據預測，通貨膨脹將穩定在3%。/ It is anticipated that the equivalent of 192 full-time jobs will be lost. 預計將失去相當於192份全職工作的崗位。/ Our anticipated arrival time is 8.30. 我們預計抵達的時間是8:30。<span class="definition">vt. 預見（將來會發生的事並採取行動為此事做好準備），如未待提出就回答（問題）；預先考慮並滿足（請求、需要等）：</span>What Jeff did was to anticipate my next question. 傑夫預先回答了我的下一個問題。/ Do you expect your partner to anticipate your needs? 你是否期望你的伴侶不等你提出就主動滿足你的需要？/ We need someone who can anticipate and respond to changes in the fashion industry. 我們需要一個能預見時裝業變化並做相應安排的人。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**anticipation**</span> [ænˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（對未來之事的）預料、預計、預見：</span>He bought extra food in anticipation of more people coming than he'd invited. 他預料來的客人會比邀請的多，就多買了食物。/ Troops in the Philippines have been put on full alert in anticipation of trouble during a planned general strike. 因料到在有組織的大罷工期間可能會出現騷亂，菲律賓的軍隊已進入全面戒備狀態。/ There is growing anticipation that the chairman will have to resign. 越來越多的人預料主席將不得不辭職。/ People are buying extra groceries in anticipation of heavy snowstorms. 人們預計會有強烈暴風雪來襲，正在多購買一些食品雜貨。

<span class="vocabulary">**project**</span> ['prɒdʒekt, 'prəʊ-] 
<span class="definition">vt. 預測、估計現存的事物（或正發生的事件）在將來的尺寸、成本或數量，相當於forecast，但不如forecast常用：</span>A growth rate of 4% is projected for next year. 預計明年的增長率為4%。/ The unemployment rate has been projected to fall. 據預測失業率將下降。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**projection**</span> [prəˈdʒekʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 預測、推測、估計：</span>demographic, population projection 人口預測 / budget, financial, growth, profit, revenue, sales projection 預算預測；財務預測；增長預測；利潤預測；稅收預測；銷售預測 / optimistic, realistic projection 樂觀的預測；現實的預測 / to make forward projections of population figures 推斷未來的人口數量 / the company's projection of 11 million visitors for the first year 公司對第一年將有1,100萬遊客的預測 / Sales have exceeded our projections. 銷售量超過了我們的預測。/ The divisions revenue projection is based on net customer growth. 各部門的收入預測是以客戶的淨增長為基礎的。/ On current projections, there will be more than fifty million people over 65 in 2020. 根據當前的預測，2020年65歲以上的人口將超過5，000萬。/ He declined to make projections about the next quarter's earnings. 他拒絕對下一季度的盈利作出預測。/ They presented profit projections for the rest of the year. 他們提交了對本年度餘下時間的盈利預測。/ We revised our projections of funding requirements upwards/upward. 我們調高了對資金需求的預測。

<span class="vocabulary">**give**</span> [ɡɪv] 
<span class="definition">vt. 預計…將持續某一時間長度，或預計某件事的走勢。是一個簡單用詞：</span>That marriage won’t last. I’ll give them two years, at the outside. 那樣的婚姻不會持久。我看他們最多不過兩年。/ A recent poll gave Toml a 68 percent support rating. 最近的一次民意調查預計湯姆的支援率為68%。

<span class="vocabulary">**tip**</span> [tɪp] 
<span class="definition">vt. 預言某人或某事將會成功，常用被動式：</span>The band is being tipped for the top. 人們說這支樂隊將位居榜首。/ He has been widely tipped as a future CEO. 他被普遍認為是將來的執行長。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**augur**</span> [ˈɔ:gə(r)]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ well/badly [正式] 是…成敗的預兆、主（吉或兇）：</span>Conflicts among the various groups do not augur well for the future of the peace talks. 各派之間的衝突對和平談判不是一個好兆頭。/ The renewed violence this week hardly augurs well for smooth or peaceful change. 本週新一輪的暴力事件預示著順利平和的轉變幾乎不可能實現。/ Already there were problems. It didn't augur well. 已經有問題了，這不是好兆頭。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**prophesy**</span> [ˈprɒfəsaɪ; 美 ˈprɑ:f-]
<span class="definition">vt.（過去用宗教力量或魔法的力量來對將來的事進行）預告、預言：</span>to prophesy war 預言有戰爭 / to prophesy disaster 預言有災難 / He prophesied that within five years his opponent would either be dead or in prison. 他預言5年內他的敵人要麼會死去，要麼會進監獄。/ She prophesied a bad ending for the expedition. 她預言這次探險結局不會好。/ She prophesied that she would win a gold medal. 她預言自己將贏得金牌。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**prophet**</span> [ˈprɒfɪt; 美 ˈprɑ:f-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 預言者、預言家：</span>I promised myself I'd defy all the prophets of doom and battle back to fitness. 我發誓要打破所有的死亡預言，力爭恢復健康。/ Some believe that he was not a true prophet. 有人認為他不是真正的預言家。/ a prophet of doom（非正式用法）預言厄運的人 / In spite of the prophets of doom, her business proved very successful. 儘管很多人並不看好，但她的生意證明非常成功。

周邊詞彙補充：
· preview可指在一部電影或一檔電視節目對公眾播放之前，報刊雜誌所對其進行的預先評述或報告。也可指電影或電視節目的預告片（其旨在對該節目進行宣傳）；
· weather可指氣象預報；
· nap可指賽馬專家給出的建議（即哪匹馬最有可能贏），即賽馬情報、賽馬結果預測。
