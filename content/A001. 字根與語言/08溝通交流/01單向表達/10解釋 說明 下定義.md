☀ <span class="category">**解釋、說明、下定義的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**explain**</span> [ɪk'spleɪn] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 1 explain sth (to sb)以一種易於理解的方式將…告訴給某人，即解釋，說明。是此義的常規用詞：</span>to explain the rules of the game 解釋遊戲規則 / to explain the problem to beginners 對初學者解釋這個問題 / to explain the plan 解釋這個計劃 / Let me explain what I mean. 讓我解釋一下我的意思。/ Can you explain how the system works? 你能解釋一下該系統的執行原理嗎？/ She explained to them what to do in an emergency. 她向他們說明了緊急情況下應採取的行動。/ He explained who each person in the photo was. 他對照片裡的人一一作了介紹。<span class="definition">2 explain sth (to sb)給出…的原因（或理由）；是…的原因（或理由）。即解釋…的原因（或理由）：</span>He explained that his car had broken down. 他解釋說他的汽車出了毛病。/ I have a letter here which explains everything. 我這裡有一封信可以解釋一切。/ This phenomenon can be easily explained. 這種現象很容易解釋。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**interpret**</span> [ɪnˈtɜ:prɪt; 美 -ˈtɜ:rp-]
<span class="definition">vt. 解釋…的含義、詮釋：</span>The students were asked to interpret the poem. 學生們被要求詮釋那首詩的意義。/ These figures cannot be easily interpreted. 這些數字解釋起來並不容易。/ These results must be interpreted cautiously. 這些結果必須謹慎地加以詮釋。/ We all seek to interpret what we hear and what we read. 對所聽到的和讀到的東西我們都會試圖做出解釋。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**illustrate**</span> [ˈɪləstreɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. 用示例、圖片等使…的意思更清晰，即解釋、說明：</span>To illustrate my point, let me tell you a little story. 為了說明我的觀點，讓我來給你們講個小故事。/ Last year's sales figures are illustrated in Figure 2. 圖2顯示了去年的銷售數字。/ What she attempts to illustrate is the difference between her company and her competitors. 她試圖說明的是她的公司與其競爭者之間的差別。/ He is showing these silent films to help illustrate his story of survival. 他正在放映這些無聲電影，用以更好地說明他劫後餘生的過程。/ Throughout, she illustrates her analysis with excerpts from discussions. 自始至終，她摘引討論內容來闡明她的分析。/ a way of illustrating to the chairman the folly of his decision 向主席表明其決定有多麼愚蠢的一個方法          
           
<span class="vocabulary">**illuminate**</span> [ɪˈlu:mɪneɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 闡明、解釋（使之更易理解）：</span>This text illuminates the philosopher's early thinking. 這篇課文解釋了這位哲學家的早期思想。/ They use games and drawings to illuminate their subject. 他們用遊戲和圖畫來闡明他們的主題。/ an incident which greatly illuminated the problems we faced 極為清楚地揭示出我們所面臨問題的事件 / The study of the present also helps to illuminate the past. 研究現在也有助於瞭解過去。/ These confessions serve to illuminate his argument. 這些告白用以闡明他的觀點。

<span class="vocabulary">**unravel**</span> [ʌnˈrævl]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 將難以理解或神秘的事情解釋清楚：</span>The discovery will help scientists unravel the mystery of the Ice Age. 這一發現將有助於科學家揭開冰川時代的奧秘。/ A young mother has flown to Iceland to unravel the mystery of her husband's disappearance. 一位年輕的母親飛抵冰島，要揭開她丈夫失蹤之謎。/ The legal tangle was never really unravelled. 這起法律糾葛從未真正得到解決。/ Gradually, with an intelligent use of flashbacks, Yves' story unravels. 透過巧妙運用倒敘手法，伊夫的故事慢慢呈現出來。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**elaborate**</span> [ɪˈlæbərət]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (on/upon sth) 詳盡闡述；詳細描述：</span>He said he was resigning but did not elaborate on his reasons. 他說他準備辭職但未詳細說明原因。/ She went on to elaborate her argument. 她進而詳盡闡述了她的論點。/ A spokesman declined to elaborate on a statement released late yesterday. 發言人拒絕對昨天晚些時候發表的宣告詳加說明。/ Would you care to elaborate? 你願意詳細說明一下嗎？/ This point will be elaborated further in the next chapter. 下一章將進一步闡述這一點。/ Let me briefly elaborate on this. 讓我對此簡要說明一下。/ They refused to elaborate on the reasons for their decision. 他們拒絕對其決定作出詳細說明。

<span class="vocabulary">**justify**</span> [ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪ]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth/yourself (to sb) 對某事做出解釋；為某事辯解或辯護：</span>The Prime Minister has been asked to justify the decision to Parliament. 要求首相就這一決定向議會做出解釋。/ Can you really justify the destruction of such a fine old building? 你確實能為毀壞這樣一幢精美的古建築找出正當的解釋嗎？/ The university could not easily justify spending the money on this. 這所大學很難為此項花銷做出合理的解釋。/ You don't need to justify yourself to me. 你不必向我解釋你的理由。/ No argument can justify a war. 任何理由都不能為戰爭開脫。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**specify**</span> [ˈspesɪfaɪ]
<span class="definition">vt. 明確說明：</span>He has not specified what action he would like them to take. 他尚未明確說明自己希望他們採取什麼樣的行動。/ She did not specify precisely how many people were involved in the incident. 她沒有確切說明這起事件涉及多少人。/ Are all the details correctly specified? 所有細節都準確無誤地說清楚了嗎？<span class="definition">vt.（尤指透過給出具體的資訊）說明：</span>Remember to specify your size when ordering clothes. 訂購服裝時記著要詳細說明你要的號碼。/ Unless otherwise specified, all fields have a maximum length of 20 characters. 除非另作說明，每個欄位最大長度是20個字元。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pinpoint**</span> [ˈpɪnpɔɪnt]
<span class="definition">vt. 準確解釋原因，或準確描述：</span>if you can pinpoint exactly what the anger is about 如果你能確切地解釋憤怒從何而來 / The report pinpointed the areas most in need of help. 報告精確說明了亟待援助的地區。

<span class="vocabulary">**plead**</span> [pli:d]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (for sth) 給出…作為解釋：</span>He pleaded family problems for his lack of concentration. 他解釋說注意力分散是因為有家庭問題。

<span class="vocabulary">**elucidate**</span> [iˈlu:sɪdeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. [正式] 透過更充分地解釋來使…更明瞭，如解釋、闡明、說明：</span>He elucidated a point of grammar. 他解釋了一個語法要點。/ I will try to elucidate what I think the problems are. 我將盡力闡明我認為問題的所在。/ Let me elucidate. 讓我來說明一下吧。/ Haig went on to elucidate his personal principle of war. 海格繼續解釋他個人的戰爭原則。/ There was no need for him to elucidate. 他不需要解釋。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**expound**</span> [ɪkˈspaʊnd]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ sth (to sb) / ~ on sth [正式] 透過詳細講述來解釋某事（如觀點、想法等）：</span>He expounded his views on the subject to me at great length. 他詳細地向我闡述了他在這個問題上的觀點。/ We listened as she expounded on the government's new policies. 我們聽她詳細講解了政府的新政策。/ Schmidt continued to expound his views on economics and politics. 施密特繼續闡述他的經濟和政治觀點。

<span class="vocabulary">**explicate**</span> [ˈeksplɪkeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 詳細解釋或詳細分析一個思想或文學作品：</span>We shall have to explicate its basic assumptions before we can assess its implications. 在評估它的影響之前，我們應該解釋清楚它的基本假設。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**enunciate**</span> [ɪˈnʌnsieɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 清楚而準確地闡明一個想法或計劃：</span>He enunciated his vision of the future. 他闡明瞭自己對未來的看法。/ He was ever ready to enunciate his views to all who would listen. 他總是樂意向所有願意傾聽的人闡明他的觀點。          
           
<span class="vocabulary">**motivate**</span> [ˈməʊtɪveɪt; 美 ˈmoʊ-]
<span class="definition">vt. [南非，正式] 就所說的話給出理由或原因：</span>Please motivate your answer to question 5. 請解釋你對第5題所作的回答。

<span class="vocabulary">**annotate**</span> [ˈænəteɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. 給一本書或一篇文字作註解：</span>Historians annotate, check and interpret the diary selections. 歷史學家對日記選篇進行加註、核查以及闡釋。/ an annotated bibliography 附有註釋的參考書目
            
<span class="vocabulary">**detail**</span> [ˈdi:teɪl; US also dɪˈteɪl]
<span class="definition">vt. 詳細列舉、詳細說明：</span>The brochure details all the hotels in the area and their facilities. 這本小冊子詳細介紹了當地所有旅館及其設施。/ The report detailed the human rights abuses committed during the war. 這份報告詳述了戰爭期間所發生的踐踏人權的情況。          

<span class="vocabulary">**define**</span> [dɪˈfaɪn]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (as sth) 給詞語下定義：</span>The term ‘mental illness’ is difficult to define. “精神病”這個詞很難下定義。/ He was asked to define his concept of cool. 他被要求說明自己關於“酷”的定義。/ Life imprisonment is defined as 60 years under state law. 按照州法律終身監禁定義為60年。/ When people are asked 'What is intelligence?' they tend to reply: 'I don't know how to define it, but I can certainly recognize it when I see it.' 當被問及“智力是什麼”的時候，人們往往會回答說：“我不知道該如何定義它，但我見到了肯定就能認得出。”/ Collins English Dictionary defines a workaholic as 'a person obsessively addicted to work'. 《柯林斯英語詞典》給“工作狂”一詞下的定義是“一個過分沉溺於工作中的人”。/ It is important to define these terms accurately. 準確定義這些術語很重要。/ Thread count is defined as the number of threads in one square inch of fabric. 織物經緯密度的定義為每平方英寸織物中線的根數。<span class="definition">vt. 準確地描述、闡明、表明、界定：</span>We need to define the task ahead very clearly. 我們需要明確今後的任務。/ There may be problems if responsibilities are not adequately defined. 如果責任沒有充分界定，就可能出現問題。/ We have chosen to define the scope of our study quite broadly. 我們已決定把研究範圍界定得籠統些。/ The difficulty of a problem was defined in terms of how long it took to complete. 問題的難易度是以解決這個問題所花時間的長短而定的。/ It is difficult to define what makes him so popular. 很難解釋清楚什麼原因使他如此走紅。/ We were unable to define what exactly was wrong with him. 我們說不清楚他到底哪裡不對勁。/ the culturally defined role of women 從文化角度定義的女性角色 / Social values are not easy to define. 社會價值觀定義起來並非易事。/ This type of lymphoma is defined by the presence of specific malignant cells. 這種淋巴瘤確診的依據是某些惡性腫瘤細胞的存在。

☀ <span class="category">**解釋、說明的名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**explanation**</span> [͵eksplə'neɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] explanation (for sth/for doing sth) 向你揭示某事為何發生（或給出…的原因）的一個陳述、事實或情況，即解釋，說明：</span>You owe me an explanation. 你欠我一個解釋。/ The most likely explanation is that his plane was delayed. 最可能的解釋是他的飛機晚點了。/ The book opens with an explanation of why some drugs are banned. 本書在開篇闡述了禁用某些藥物的原因。/ scientific, theoretical explanation 科學的解釋；理論上的解釋 <span class="definition">2 [C] 關於…的工作原理（或使…更易於理解）的一個陳述或一篇文字，即解釋性說明；說明性文字：</span>an explanation of how the machine works 關於機器工作原理的解釋 / She launched into a detailed explanation of every aspect of her work. 她開始詳細地解釋她工作的每一個方面。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**interpretation**</span> [ɪnˌtɜ:prɪˈteɪʃn; 美 -ˌtɜ:rp-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 某種方式的解釋或說明：</span>Her evidence suggests a different interpretation of the events. 她的證據顯示這些事件可能有另外一種解釋。/ It is not possible for everyone to put their own interpretation on the law. 不可能讓每個人自行闡釋法律。/ The opposition Conservative Party put a different interpretation on the figures. 反對黨保守黨對這些數字提出一番不同的解釋。/ Analysis and interpretation is a very personal thing. 分析與說明是個很主觀的事情。/ artistic interpretation 藝術的詮釋 / historical, textual, theological interpretation 歷史角度的詮釋；基於文字的解釋；神學的解釋 / feminist historical interpretations of marriage customs 女性主義關於婚俗的歷史解讀 / Biblical, scriptural（尤美）interpretation《聖經》解釋 / His writings reflect his conservative views on Biblical interpretation. 他的作品反映了他對《聖經》解讀所持的保守觀點。/ constitutional（尤美）, judicial, statutory interpretation 憲法解釋；司法解釋；法規解釋 / In any system of law, there is an inevitable element of judicial interpretation. 在任何法律體系中都不可避免地存在著司法解釋。/ Scientists made an interpretation based on the data available. 科學家根據掌握的資料作出瞭解釋。/ Most modern historians support this interpretation. 多數現代歷史學家支援這種解釋。/ She challenges many orthodox interpretations of religious texts. 她質疑宗教文字的許多正統解釋。/ His work defies simplistic interpretation. 他的作品不能進行簡單化的解釋。/ It is possible to put an entirely different interpretation on her comments. 對她的那些評論作出完全不同的解釋是可能的。

<span class="vocabulary">**account**</span> [ə'kaʊnt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 對一種思想或想法、理論、過程的解釋或說明、敘述。其含義比explanation要模糊和廣泛，其所解釋的物件也比較多元化，而不像explanation那樣一般僅限於對原因和原理的解釋：</span>the Biblical account of the creation of the world《聖經》對創世的解釋
           
<span class="vocabulary">**understanding**</span> [ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ; 美 -dərˈs-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] ~ (of sth) 對某事的理解方式，如解釋：</span>The statement is open to various understandings. 這個宣告可以有各種不同的詮釋。

☀ <span class="category">**解釋的形容詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**explicable**</span> [ɪkˈsplɪkəbl; ˈeksplɪkəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. [名詞前不可用] [正式] 可被解釋的：</span>His behaviour is only explicable in terms of (= because of) his recent illness. 他的行為只能用他最近患病來解釋。/ The differences in the children's achievements were not wholly explicable in terms of their social backgrounds. 孩子成績的差異不能完全從社會背景來解釋。/ These atrocities are not readily explicable by any of the usual analyses of politics.（readily尤為英式用法）通常的政治分析無法輕易地解釋這些暴行。/ The delay is partly explicable by the roadworks. 延誤的部分原因是道路施工。

周邊詞彙補充：
· statement可指對某事物的一種表徵或詮釋；
· spin可指某人對某一事件或情況以某種方式所做的解釋、詮釋與呈現，是一種資訊的報道傾向；
· reading可表示解讀；
· construction可表示詮釋；
· interpretation可表示解釋；
· legend可指地圖（或書中圖表）的解釋。
