☀ <span class="category">**佔線 等待：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**busy**</span> ['bɪzɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. [尤美]（電話）正被佔用的，即佔線的，相當於engaged：</span>The line is busy--I’ll try again later. 佔線——我過會兒再打。/ the busy signal 忙音

<span class="vocabulary">**hold**</span> [həʊld] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（打電話時）等待，不結束通話：</span>That extension is busy right now. Can you hold? 分機現在佔線。您能等一會嗎？/ Could you hold the line and I’ll just get my pen. 請別結束通話，我去拿支筆，好嗎？<span class="definition">vt. 代接電話並記錄下對方留言（以轉交給對方所要與之通話的人）：</span>He tells his secretary to hold his calls for him. 他讓他的秘書為其代接電話。

周邊詞彙補充：
· engaged可指（電話線）正被佔用的、線路正忙的。
