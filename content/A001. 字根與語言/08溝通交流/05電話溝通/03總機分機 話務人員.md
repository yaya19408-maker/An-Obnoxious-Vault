☀ <span class="category">**總機分機 話務人員：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**switchboard**</span> [ˈswɪtʃbɔ:d; 美 -bɔ:rd]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 電話的交換機、交換臺、總機：</span>telephone switchboard 電話交換機 / Call the switchboard and ask for extension 410. 你先打電話到總機，然後要求轉分機410。/ He asked to be connected to the central switchboard at London University. 他要求轉接到倫敦大學的電話總機。/ Hundreds of fans jammed the switchboard for over an hour. 在一個多小時裡，好幾百個崇拜者打去電話，交換臺應接不暇。/ The switchboard was jammed with furious calls. 憤怒的投訴電話令總機應接不暇。/ switchboard operator 總機接線員 / The switchboard operator was the hotel owner's wife. 電話總檯的接線員是旅館老闆的妻子。/ the man on the switchboard 總機接線員 / All calls must pass through the switchboard. 所有的電話都必須透過交換機。

<span class="vocabulary">**exchange**</span> [ɪks'tʃeɪndӡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 電話交換臺，電話總機，電話局：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**extension**</span> [ɪk'stenʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 縮寫為ext. 指電話分機線；電話分機號碼：</span>We have an extension in the bedroom. 我們的臥室裡有一個分機。/ What’s your extension number? 你的分機號碼是多少？/ Can I have extension 4332 please? 請接4332號分機。

<span class="vocabulary">**operator**</span> ['ɒpəreɪtə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 英也用telephonist，指電話接線員：</span>switchboard, telephone operator 電話總機接線員；電話接線員
