☀ <span class="category">**商討 雙向討論：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**discuss**</span> [dɪs'kʌs] 
<span class="definition">vt. discuss sth (with sb) 與某人談論某事（尤指為就某事做出決定），即討論，商量：</span>The plan was discussed in great detail. 對這項計劃作了極為詳細的討論。/ I’d like to discuss this matter with you later. 我想稍後再與你討論這個問題。/ We need to discuss when we should go. 我們需要商量一下什麼時候動身。/ Students should be encouraged to discuss critically the information they are given. 應該鼓勵學生對獲得的資訊進行批判性的討論。/ The proposals have been widely discussed in the media. 這些提議已經在媒體上進行了廣泛討論。

<span class="vocabulary">**discussion**</span> [dɪs'kʌʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] discussion (with sb) (about/on sb/sth) 討論，商討：</span>They had a detailed discussion of the issues. 他們詳細討論了這些問題。/ The plan was agreed without further discussion. 計劃未經進一步討論就透過了。/ a wide-ranging discussion on women’s rights 關於女性權利的廣泛討論 / informal discussion 非正式的討論 / bilateral, classroom, group, round-table discussion 雙邊磋商；課堂討論；小組討論；圓桌會談 / The two governments are to hold discussions on border issue. 兩國政府將就邊界問題舉行會談。/ We are hoping to enter into discussions with union leaders. 我們希望能與工會領導進行商談。  
           
<span class="vocabulary">**confer**</span> [kənˈfɜ:(r)]
<span class="definition">vi. [正式] ~ (with sb) (on/about sth) 與某人討論某事，以交換意見或得到建議：</span>He wanted to confer with his colleagues before reaching a decision. 他想與他的同事先商議一下再做出決定。/ He conferred with Hill and the others in his office. 他與希爾及其他人在辦公室裡商議了一番。/ His doctors conferred by telephone and agreed that he must get away from his family for a time. 診治他的幾位醫生在電話裡討論了一番，一致認為他必須離開家人一段時間。

<span class="vocabulary">**consult**</span> [kən'sʌlt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. consult (with) sb (about/on sth)與某人討論某事（以得到其許可或幫助做出決策）：</span>You shouldn’t have done it without consulting me. 你不該不和我商量就做了這件事。/ I need to consult with my colleagues on the proposals. 我需要和我的同事商討這些建議。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**consultation**</span> [ˌkɒnslˈteɪʃn; 美 ˌkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U]（做決策前向某人或某個群體所做的）商討、磋商：</span>acting in consultation with all the departments involved 和所有有關部門磋商後行事 / The plans were drawn up in consultation with the World Health Organization. 這些方案是在與世界衛生組織磋商之後起草的。/ The decision was taken after close consultation with local residents. 這項決定是在與當地居民仔細磋商後作出的。<span class="definition">2 [C] 對某事的一次正式協商、商討：</span>extensive consultations between the two countries 兩國之間的廣泛磋商 / Next week he'll be in Florida for consultations with President Mitterrand. 下週，他將在佛羅里達與密特朗總統進行磋商。/ a joint consultation with doctors and patients 醫生和病人的共同協商 / a consultation between teachers and parents 教師和家長之間的討論會 / We need more consultation with the unions. 我們需要和工會進行更多的協商。/ The police chiefs will hold a consultation with all the relevant groups. 警察局長們將同所有相關群體舉行協商會議。/ The plan was developed in close consultation with the local community. 該計劃是在同當地社群的密切磋商中逐漸形成的。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**deliberation**</span> [dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] 對某個嚴肅事情的正式商議、審議、評議：</span>the outcome of the deliberations 評議的結果 / Their deliberations were rather inconclusive. 他們的審議幾乎沒有得出什麼結論。/ The jurors will now begin their deliberations. 陪審員馬上開始評議。/ After ten hours of deliberation, the jury returned a verdict of ‘not guilty’. 經過十小時的商議，陪審團宣告了“無罪”的裁決。/ The deliberations of the committee are completely confidential. 委員會的審議過程是絕對保密的。

<span class="vocabulary">**exhaust**</span> [ɪgˈzɔ:st]
<span class="definition">vt. 詳盡討論或研究一個主題（直到沒什麼可以再說的）：</span>She and Chantal must have exhausted the subject of babies and clothes. 她和尚塔爾肯定已經充分討論過寶寶和衣服的問題。
