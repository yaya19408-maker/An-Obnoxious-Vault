☀ <span class="category">**質詢 盤問：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**question**</span> ['kwestʃən] 
<span class="definition">vt. question sb (about/on sth) 就某事向某人問問題（尤指正式提問），即質詢：</span>A man is being questioned in connection with the robbery. 一名男子正就那起搶劫案接受查問。/ Over half of those questioned said they rarely took any exercise. 被問到的人有一半以上說他們很少鍛鍊身體。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**questioning**</span> [ˈkwestʃənɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 提問、詢問、盤問：</span>aggressive, hostile, tough questioning 咄咄逼人的質問；充滿敵意的質問；嚴厲的質問 / constant, repeated questioning 不斷的質問；反覆的質問 / He was taken to the police station for questioning. 他被帶到警察局盤問。/ They faced some hostile questioning over the cost of the project. 關於這個專案的費用，他們面臨著咄咄逼人的盤問。/ She decided to confront her boss about the situation with direct questioning. 她決定就這一情形直接質問老闆。/ Four suspects have been detained for questioning in connection with the incident. 就那起事件，已有四名嫌疑人被拘留審問。/ The suspect remained silent under questioning. 嫌疑人在訊問過程中一直保持沉默。/ The disaster prompted questioning about safety standards. 那場災難促使人們對安全標準產生質疑。/ The man was released after questioning by detectives. 那名男子接受警探盤問之後就被放走了。/ The group chairman faced hostile questioning from angry shareholders. 集團主席面臨憤怒的股東們充滿敵意的質問。

<span class="vocabulary">**challenge**</span> ['tʃælɪndӡ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 命令某人停下來並讓其說出身份（或讓其交代自己正在做什麼），即盤問：</span>We were challenged by police at the border. 我們在邊境受到警察盤問。

<span class="vocabulary">**grill**</span> [ɡrɪl] 
<span class="definition">vt. grill sb (about sth)（常以一種令人不快的方式）就某人的想法、行為等向其提出很多問題，如盤問，追問，審問，責問等。可算作一種比喻用法：</span>They grilled her about where she had been all night. 他們盤問她整晚待在什麼地方。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**probe**</span> [prəʊb; 美 proʊb]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (into sth) 為找出秘密或隱藏的資訊而詢問，如盤問、追問：</span>He didn't like the media probing into his past. 他不願意媒體追問他的過去。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**interrogate**</span> [ɪnˈterəgeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt.（尤指警察對某人進行長時間的）審問、訊問、盤問：</span>He was interrogated by the police for over 12 hours. 他被警察審問了12個多小時。/ I interrogated everyone even slightly involved. 我訊問了每個人,甚至連稍有關聯的人都沒放過。/ to interrogate the suspect 審訊犯罪嫌疑人 / to interrogate the witness 訊問目擊證人
           
<span class="vocabulary">**interrogation**</span> [ɪnˌterəˈgeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（尤指兇惡的）長時間的盤問、審問、訊問：</span>He confessed after four days under interrogation. 他在受訊問四天之後招認了。/ the right to silence in police interrogations 在警方訊問時保持沉默的權利 / ill-treatment of suspects during interrogation 審問期間虐待疑犯 / She revealed the name of her accomplice during interrogation. 在審訊中她供出了自己的同謀。/ She had to undergo a lengthy interrogation. 她不得不經受長時間的審訊。/ coercive interrogation（美）強制審訊 They have been subject to coercive interrogations without access to lawyers. 他們在沒有見過律師的情況下受到了強制審訊。/ The interrogation was conducted by senior police officers. 審訊由幾名高階警官進行。/ He had been captured and was undergoing interrogation. 他已經被抓而且正在接受審訊。/ interrogation cell, interrogation room 審訊室 / interrogation methods, interrogation procedures, interrogation tactics, interrogation techniques 審訊方法；審訊程式；審訊策略；審訊技巧 / She hated her parents' endless interrogations about where she'd been. 她討厭她的父母沒完沒了地盤問她去哪裡了。

<span class="vocabulary">**cross-examination**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（尤指在法庭上對證詞細節的）盤問，反詰：</span>He broke down under cross-examination (= while he was being cross-examined) and admitted his part in the assault. 他經不起嚴密的詰問，招認了他曾參與那次毆打。/ Evidence is given on oath and witnesses are subject to cross-examination. 證人宣誓後作證，並接受盤問。/ His alibi would not have withstood cross-examination. 他不在犯罪現場的證據應該經不起盤問。/ He was found to have lied twice in cross-examination. 人們發現他在盤間中兩度撒謊。/ On cross-examination, she sounded confused.（on尤為美式用法）盤問時她聽起來有些糊塗。
