<span class="vocabulary">**definition**</span> [ˌdefɪˈnɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U]（尤指詞典裡給出的）定義，釋義：</span>clear simple definitions 簡單明瞭的釋義 / Neighbours by definition live close by (= this is what being a neighbour means). 按照釋義，“鄰居”就要居住在鄰近的地方。/ There is no general agreement on a standard definition of intelligence. “智力”一詞目前還沒有一個普遍認可的標準釋義。/ The term partner' requires careful definition. partner這個詞需要慎重定義。/ My definition of a good hospice is one where some of the patients start feeling too good to die. 我對“好的晚期病人安養院”的定義是一個能讓一些病人開始感到生有所戀的地方。/ According to a strict definition, the expenses of a self-employed person can be deducted from tax. 根據嚴格的定義，個體經營者的開支可從稅款中扣除。/ formal, official, statutory definition 正式的定義；官方的定義；法律上的定義 / The firm falls within the statutory definition of a 'small company'. 這家公司屬於法律上定義的"小公司"。/ dictionary definition 字典定義 / legal definition 法律定義 / The mathematical definition of an even number is one that is exactly divisible by 2. 偶數在數學上的定義是能被2整除的數。/ accepted, conventional, standard, textbook, traditional definition 公認的定義；常規定義；標準定義；課本上的定義；傳統的定義 / workable, working definition 初步的定義 / We need a good working definition of 'pollution'. 我們需要為"汙染"下一個合適的初步定義。/ This unusual building barely fits the definition of a house. 這幢獨特的建築幾乎稱不上房子。/ He has expanded the definition of sculpture. 他擴充套件了對雕塑的定義。/ According to a strict definition of 'assault', she was not assaulted. 根據"侵犯他人身體"的嚴格定義，她並沒有受到侵犯。/ A clinic for women would, by definition, deal with pregnancy and childbirth. 婦科診所據其定義負責處理妊娠和分娩事宜。/ Under the broader definition of 'poverty', thousands more people would be included. 根據"貧困"的廣義定義，還有數以千計的人要包括在內。<span class="definition">2 [C] 定義：</span>What's your definition of happiness? 你對幸福的定義是什麼？/ They have adopted a very narrow definition of success. 他們採用了一種非常嚴格的關於"成功"的定義。/ The author provides a clear definition of cultural awareness. 作者給文化意識下了一個清楚的定義。/ She refused to accept the definition of woman as man's subordinate. 她拒絕接受女人是男人附屬品的定義。

<span class="vocabulary">**gesture**</span> ['dӡestʃə] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] 用你的手、頭、臉等所做出的示意性動作（該動作表達了某種意思）：</span>a rude gesture 粗野的手勢 / a vague gesture 含糊的手勢 / They communicated entirely by gesture. 他們完全用手勢交流。/ She made an expansive gesture with her arms. 她做了個大張雙臂的動作。/ She waved us away with an impatient gesture. 她不耐煩地揮手打發我們走開。<span class="definition">2 [C] 某人所做出（或說出）的表示某一情感或意圖的行為（或話語），可看作比喻義的“姿態”：</span>a gesture of sympathy 表示同情 / a gesture of goodwill 善意的表示 / It was a nice gesture to invite his wife too. 把他的妻子也請來是友好的表示。/ The government has made a gesture towards public opinion. 政府已就民意表了態。/ Words and empty gestures are not enough--we demand action! 講空話和擺姿態是不夠的——我們需要的是行動！
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**overture**</span> [ˈəʊvətʃʊə(r); -tjʊə(r); 美 ˈoʊvərtʃər; -tʃʊr]
<span class="definition">n. [usually pl.] ~ (to sb) ~ (to sb)（為與別人建立友好關係、討論某事等而做出的）友好的姿態，提議：</span>He began making overtures to a number of merchant banks. 他開始主動同一些投資銀行接觸。/ He had lately begun to make clumsy yet endearing overtures of friendship. 最近他開始主動表示友好，樣子笨拙卻又招人喜愛。/ If only the West had been more responsive to his peace overtures in the fifties. 要是西方在五十年代能更積極地響應他的和平姿態就好了。/ diplomatic overture 外交姿態 / romantic, sexual overture 情愛意向表示；性意向表示 / She spurned his overtures of love. 她輕蔑地拒絕了他的求愛。/ overtures of friendship 友好的表示 / He made friendly overtures to the new people next door. 他向隔壁新鄰居示好。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**posture**</span> [ˈpɒstʃə(r); 美 ˈpɑ:s-]
<span class="definition">vi. [正式] ~ (as sth) 故作姿態、裝樣子（以給別人留下印象或愚弄別人）：</span>She says the President may just be posturing. 她說總統也許只是在做樣子而已。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**motion**</span> [ˈməʊʃn; 美 ˈmoʊʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 用手或頭所做的為傳遞資訊的動作：</span>At a single motion of his hand, the room fell silent. 他手一揮，屋子裡便安靜了下來。           

<span class="vocabulary">**sign**</span> [saɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 用以向某人傳遞資訊的動作或聲音，如示意、手勢：</span>A V sign V字型手勢 / He gave a thumbs-up sign. 他豎起了大拇指表示贊同。/ She nodded as a sign for us to sit down. 她點頭示意我們坐下。

<span class="vocabulary">**meaning**</span> ['mi:nɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] meaning (of sth)（聲音、文字、符號等所代表的）意思。是一個常規用詞：</span>What’s the meaning of this word? 這個單詞是什麼意思？/ the exact meaning of this phrase 這個短語的準確含義 / the meaning of the sentence 句子的意思 / The context largely determines the meaning. 語境在很大程度上決定了意思。<span class="definition">2 [U, C] 某人透過自己的言語或行動所想要表達的事情或想法，即意思：</span>I don’t quite get your meaning. 我不太明白你的意思。/ hidden meaning in what he says 他的言外之意 / I think you misunderstood my meaning. 我想你誤解我的意思了。<span class="definition">3 [U, C] 作家、藝術家等想要透過一本書、一幅畫等來表現出的想法，即意義，含義，思想等：</span>several layers of meaning 若干層意義 / There are deeper meanings in the poem. 這首詩裡還有更深層次的含義。<span class="definition">adj. 常用於名詞前，不用說話而想要向某人表達…的，即意味深長的，意在言外的，不過此義項更常用meaningful：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**meaningful**</span> [ˈmi:nɪŋfl]
<span class="definition">adj. 1（也可用不太常用的meaning）不說話而要對某人表達某事的，如（眼神、姿勢）意味深長的、意在言外的：</span>She gave me a meaningful look. 她意味深長地看了我一眼。/ Upon the utterance of this word, Dan and Harry exchanged a quick, meaningful look. 這句話一出口，丹和哈里馬上交換了一個意味深長的眼神。<span class="definition">2 擁有易於理解的意思的，即意思明顯的：</span>These statistics are not very meaningful. 這些統計數字說明不了什麼問題。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**significant**</span> [sɪgˈnɪfɪkənt]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 [常用於名詞前] 有特別含義的（並非所有人都能理解的），意味深長的。相當於meaningful：</span>significant look, significant smile 意味深長的眼神；意味深長的微笑 / Mrs Bycraft gave Rose a significant glance. 拜克拉夫特夫人意味深長地瞥了羅絲一眼。<span class="definition">2 有某種意義的：</span>It is significant that he changed his will only days before his death. 他在臨終前幾天修改遺囑，這很能說明問題。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**loaded**</span> [ˈləʊdɪd; 美 ˈloʊd-]
<span class="definition">adj.（問題或詞語）意味深長的，另有所指的，哄騙性的：</span>It was a loaded question and I preferred not to comment. 這是個帶有圈套的問題，還是不作評論為好。/ That's a loaded question. 這是個誘導性的問題。/ the loaded word 'sexist' 言外有言的“性別歧視者”一詞

<span class="vocabulary">**sense**</span> [sens] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 文字、字詞、言語所具有的含義（此時與meaning沒有明顯不同，只是sense僅為可數名詞，且不如meaning常用）；理解…的方式。常翻譯為“意義”（如狹義、廣義等）：</span>figurative, metaphorical sense 修辭義；比喻義 / literal sense 字面意義 / legal sense 法律意義 / spiritual sense 精神含義 / pejorative sense 貶義 / That word has three senses. 那個詞有三個意思。/ education in its broadest sense 最廣義上的教育 / He was a true friend, in every sense of the word. 無論從哪個角度講，他都是個真正的朋友。/ In a certain sense, she’s right. 從某種意義上說，她是對的。/ in the traditional sense 在傳統意義上 / in the usual sense of the word 在通常意義上 

<span class="vocabulary">**significance**</span> [sɪɡ'nɪfɪkəns] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 某事物的意思或含義。不常用：</span>She couldn’t grasp the full significance of what he had said. 她未能充分領會他那番話的意思。/ Do these symbols have any particular significance? 這些符號有什麼特別的含義嗎？

<span class="vocabulary">**point**</span> [pɒɪnt] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.] 意思。是一個簡單用詞：</span>I missed the point. 我沒聽懂。/ Do you see my point? 你明白我的意思嗎？/ I see your point. 我明白你的意思。/ I didn’t get the point of the story. 我不明白這個故事想要說明什麼。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**connotation**</span> [ˌkɒnəˈteɪʃn; 美 ˌkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 文字（或名字）的主要意思之外的隱含意思：</span>The word ‘professional’ has connotations of skill and excellence. professional這個詞隱含著技藝和專長的意思。/ 'Urchin', with its connotation of mischievousness, may not be a particularly apt word. urchin有淘氣的含義，可能不是一個特別恰當的詞。/ the obvious symbolic connotations of his name 他名字顯而易見的象徵意義 / broad（尤美）, wider（尤英）connotation 廣泛的引申義；更廣泛的引申義 / The term ‘at-risk youth’ has taken on broad connotations. "高危青少年"這一術語有了廣泛的引申意義。 / The notion of abuse has wider connotations than the physical. 虐待這一概念的內涵不單指身體上的虐待。/ negative, sinister, unfortunate connotation 負面的含義；不祥的含義；不吉利的含義 / It's just one of those words that's got so many negative connotations. 這只是那種有太多負面含義的詞之一。/ derogatory, pejorative connotation 貶損的含義 / the derogatory connotation of the term ‘diva’ diva （名媛）一詞的貶損含義 / positive 正面含義 / cultural, moral, political, racial, religious, sexual connotation 文化內涵；道德含義；政治含義；種族含義；宗教含義；性暗示 / the cultural connotations of these conventions 這些習俗的文化內涵 / There were political connotations in such choices. 這樣的選擇具有政治含義。/ words that today have religious connotations 如今有了宗教含義的詞語 / There is no sexual connotation to this behaviour/behavior. 這一行為沒有任何性暗示。/ That word has strong sexual connotations. 那個詞有很強的性暗示。/ The term 'native' has acquired pejorative connotations among some groups. native（土著）一詞在某些群體中逐漸有了貶低的意味。/ 'Catholic' lost its original connotation of universality. catholic一詞失去了其原本的"普世"含義。/ the negative connotations attached to the word 'academic' 單詞academic （學術氣）帶有的負面含義 / The word carries connotations of romance. 這個詞帶有浪漫意味。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**overtone**</span> [ˈəʊvətəʊn; 美 ˈoʊvərtoʊn]
<span class="definition">n. [usually pl.] 弦外之音；言外之意；暗示：</span>There were political overtones to the point he was making. 他的論點有政治含義。/ The organization's cultural activities took on political overtones. 該組織的文化活動加入了政治色彩。/ The strike has taken on overtones of a civil rights campaign. 罷工帶上了民權運動的意味。/ It's a quite profound story, with powerful religious overtones. 這個故事寓意深遠，具有強烈的宗教色彩。/ a play with strong religious overtones 有強烈宗教色彩的戲劇 / emotional, moral, racial, sexual overtone 感情方面的含義；道德方面的含義；種族方面的含義；性方面的含義 / racist overtone 種族主義色彩 / The word 'cheap' has negative overtones. “廉價"一詞帶有貶義。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**undertone**</span> [ˈʌndətəʊn; 美 ˈʌndərtoʊn]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（某人的話語或行為）間接表達出來的意思、感情等，如寓意、弦外之音：</span>His soft words contained an undertone of warning. 他溫和的話中蘊涵著警告之意。/ The sobbing voice had an undertone of anger. 啜泣聲中隱隱透著憤怒。/ The play does not have the political undertones of the novel. 這部戲沒有小說的那種政治寓意。/ a witty, racy story with surprisingly serious undertones 詼諧卻寓意深刻的葷段子 / dark, sinister undertone 邪惡的暗示；陰險的弦外之音 / subtle undertone 隱約的弦外之音 / bitter, sarcastic undertone 憤憤不平的弦外之音；尖酸刻薄的弦外之音 / sexual undertone 性暗示 / The article was full of racist undertones .這篇文章充滿了隱晦的種族主義論調。/ religious undertone 宗教寓意 / His voice carried bitter undertones. 他的聲音中帶著憤憤不平的弦外之音。

<span class="vocabulary">**drift**</span> [drɪft]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 某人所說或所寫的大意、主旨、要點。相當於gist：</span>Do you catch my drift? 你明白我的大意嗎？/ My German isn't very good, but I got the drift of what she said. 我的德語不太好，但我大致明白她說的意思。/ Grace was beginning to get his drift. 格雷絲慢慢明白了他的意思。/ Anybody who's listening will get the drift of what he was saying. 任何一個在專心聽的人都會領悟他所說的大意。/ I didn't follow the speech exactly, but I caught the main drift of what was being said. 我沒有完全聽懂演講，但領會了其主旨。/ I lost the drift of what she was saying. 我沒懂他講話的大意。
                       
<span class="vocabulary">**gist**</span> [dʒɪst]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.]（通常用the gist ~ (of sth)（文章、講話或交談的）要點、主旨、大意：</span>general gist 主旨；大意 / It is difficult to convey the gist of Reich's ideas simply. 很難將賴克的思想要旨簡單地表述出來。/ I could follow the general gist of their conversation. 我能聽懂他們談話的大體意思。/ to get (= understand) the gist of an argument 理解辯論的主旨 / He related the gist of his conversation to Naseby. 他將他談話的要點告訴了納斯比。/ I missed the beginning of the lecture--can you give me the gist of what he said? 我未聽到講座的開頭——給我講講他說話的要點好嗎？/ I'm afraid I don't quite follow your gist (= what you really mean). 對不起，我還不太明白你的意思。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**substance**</span> [ˈsʌbstəns]

<span class="vocabulary">**tenor**</span> [ˈtenə(r)]

<span class="vocabulary">**thrust**</span> [θrʌst]

<span class="vocabulary">**import**</span> [ɪm'pɔ:t] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 單數名詞，the import (of sth)指意思，含義，尤指不能立即理解（或難以理解）的含義，為正式用法。不常用：</span>It is difficult to understand the full import of this statement. 很難理解這份宣告的全部含意。

<span class="vocabulary">**intend**</span> [ɪn'tend] 
<span class="definition">vt. intend sth (by sth) / intend sth (as sth) 意思是…，意指，類似於mean，但遠不如mean常用：</span>What exactly did you intend by that remark? 你那句話到底想說什麼？/ He intended it as a joke. 他只是想開個玩笑。

<span class="vocabulary">**mean**</span> [mi:n]
