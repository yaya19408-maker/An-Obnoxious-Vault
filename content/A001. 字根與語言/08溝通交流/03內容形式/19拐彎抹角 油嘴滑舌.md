<span class="vocabulary">**roundabout**</span> ['raʊndəbaʊt] 
<span class="definition">adj. 常作前置定語，指說話兜圈子的、迂迴的、拐彎抹角的：</span>He told me, in a roundabout way, that he would borrow money from me. 他拐彎抹角地告訴我他缺錢，要從我這裡借錢。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**oblique**</span> [əˈbli:k]
<span class="definition">adj.（言語或行為）間接的、不直截了當的：</span>oblique reference, oblique approach, oblique comment 隱約提及；間接途徑；婉轉的評論 / It was an oblique reference to his mother. 這影射到了他的母親。/ Mr Golding delivered an oblique warning, talking of the danger of sudden action. 戈爾丁先生談及草率行事的危險，這是他在委婉地發出警告。

<span class="vocabulary">**clever**</span> ['klevə] 
<span class="definition">adj. [英，非正式，貶] 說話較快且使人惱怒或對聽話者不尊敬的，如油腔滑調的，油嘴滑舌的：</span>Don’t you get clever with me! 別跟我油嘴滑舌的！
           
<span class="vocabulary">**flannel**</span> [ˈflænl]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [英，非正式]（不告訴對方想要的資訊的）兜圈子的話語、應付性言語：</span>No amount of flannel could disguise that this was, at best, a minimalist solution. 說多少廢話都無法掩飾這最多隻是一個極簡主義的解決方案。
