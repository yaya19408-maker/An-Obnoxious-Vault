☀ <span class="category">**保證、確保、擔保的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**guarantee**</span> [͵ɡærən'ti:] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 承諾去做某事，或承諾某事將發生：</span>All rights of the public will be guaranteed. 公眾的所有權利將得到保障。/ We guarantee to deliver you goods within three days. 我們承諾三天內交貨。/ I can absolutely guarantee that you will enjoy the show. 我完全可以保證你會喜歡這場演出。<span class="definition">vt. 使某事必然發生，即確保：</span>Enough money alone does not guarantee happiness. 僅有足夠的金錢並不能確保幸福。/ A degree does not automatically guarantee you a job. 獲得學位並不一定能保證你找到工作。/ The outcome is by no means guaranteed. 結果完全無法保證。

<span class="vocabulary">**insure**</span> [ɪn'ʃʊə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 保證某事發生（或保證…是明確的、確切無疑的、肯定的），即確保。不過insure在此義項下尤用於美式英語，非美式英語用ensure：</span>We must ensure the task is completed on time. 我們必須確保按時完成任務。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**ensure**</span> [ɪnˈʃʊə(r); -ˈʃɔ:(r); 美 ɪnˈʃʊr]
<span class="definition">vt. 確保某事發生：</span>The book ensured his success. 這本書保證了他的成功。/ Please ensure (that) all lights are switched off. 請務必將所有燈都關掉。/ Britain's negotiators had ensured that the treaty which resulted was a significant change in direction. 英國談判代表已經確保由此而簽訂的條約將是方向上的重大改變。/ Ensure that it is written into your contract. 確保把這一點寫入合同中。/ The system not only ensures maximum discounts, but also helps the company track usage. 這一制度不僅保證產品最大限度的優惠，而且能幫助公司追蹤產品的使用。/ The alternative simply ensures that the problem will get worse. 另一種方案只會使問題變得更糟。/ We must take steps now to ensure the survival of these animals. 我們現在必須採取措施確保這些動物能生存下去。/ provisions designed to ensure safe conditions of work 旨在確保工作環境安全的規定 / It is important to ensure that delegates have been properly briefed. 重要的是要確保代表們事先了解基本情況。/ The therapist must treat the cause of the problem, thereby ensuring the symptoms do not return. 治療專家必須從病根上著手治療，以確保此類症狀不會復發。          

<span class="vocabulary">**assure**</span> [əˈʃʊə(r); -ʃɔ:(r); 美 əˈʃʊr]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb (of sth)（尤指某人對某事表示懷疑的時候）向其保證此事的真實性或保證此事一定會發生：</span>You think I did it deliberately, but I assure you (that) I did not. 你認為這是我故意乾的，不過我向你保證不是的。/ We were assured that everything possible was being done. 我們確信是在盡一切努力。/ She's perfectly safe, I can assure you. 我可以向你保證，她絕對安全。/ We can assure you of our full support. 我們保證全力支援你。/ Let me assure you that I will try to help you. 我向你保證，我會盡力幫你。/ He hastened to assure me that there was nothing traumatic to report. 他忙不迭地向我保證，並沒有什麼悲慘的事要訴說。/ 'Are you sure the raft is safe?' she asked anxiously. 'Couldn't be safer,' Max assured her confidently. “你確定木筏安全嗎？”她不安地問道。“再安全不過了，”馬克斯信心十足地向她保證道。/ ‘He'll come back, ’ Susan assured her. “他會回來的。”蘇珊安慰她道。/ He hastened to assure us that the press would not be informed. 他急忙向我們保證不會把此事告知新聞界。/ I can assure you that the animals are well cared for. 我可以向你保證動物們得到了精心照顧。/ This may sound trivial, but I assure you it is quite important! 這聽上去也許微不足道，但是，相信我，它十分重要！<span class="definition">vt. 確保某事發生、確保得到某事物：</span>Victory would assure a place in the finals. 勝利將確保能參加決賽。/ Victory would assure them a place in the finals. 勝利將確保他們能參加決賽。/ Last night's resounding victory over Birmingham City has virtually assured them of promotion. 昨晚大勝伯明翰城隊實際上已確保他們能夠晉級。/ Ways must be found to assure our children a decent start in life. 必須想辦法保證我們的孩子有一個不錯的人生開端。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**warrant**</span> [ˈwɒrənt; 美 ˈwɔ:r-; ˈwɑ:r-]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] ~ that 官方或正式保證（某事是真實的或某事將發生）：</span>All entrants must warrant that their entry is entirely their own work. 所有參賽者必須保證其參賽作品完全是自己創作的。/ The contract warrants that an experienced person is on board all the time. 合同確保一位富有經驗的人會一直呆在船上。

<span class="vocabulary">**see**</span> [si:] 
<span class="definition">vt. 確保；務必（做到）。是一個簡單用詞：</span>See that all the doors are locked before you leave. 一定要確保所有的門都鎖好了再走。/ See that you take care of him. 你一定要好好照顧他。

相關詞義延伸：
· assure還可指投保，尤指投保人壽保險。

周邊詞彙補充：
· reassure可表示使某人安心、打消某人的恐懼或疑慮。

☀ <span class="category">**保證、確保、擔保的名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**guarantee**</span> [͵ɡærən'ti:] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 關於你將做某事或某事將要發生的一個堅定的承諾，即保證，擔保：</span>to give a guarantee 做出保證 / to demand guarantees for fair elections 要求確保選舉公正 / government, legal guarantee 政府保證；法律保證 / personal guarantee 個人擔保 / loan guarantee 貸款擔保 / security guarantee 安全保證 <span class="definition">2 [C] guarantee (of sth) / guarantee (that ...) 使其他事件發生的因素：</span>a guarantee of quality 一個保證質量的因素 / There’s no guarantee that I must win the game. 不能保證我一定會贏得這場比賽。/ Health is a guarantee of success. 健康是成功的一個保證。/ Arriving early is no longer a guarantee of getting a place. 早到已無法確保有位置。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**assurance**</span> [əˈʃʊərəns; -ʃɔ:r-; 美 əˈʃʊr-]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指人們存在疑慮的時候做出的）保證：</span>They called for assurances that the government is committed to its education policy. 他們要求保證政府切實執行其教育方針。/ Unemployment seems to be rising, despite repeated assurances to the contrary. 儘管反覆擔保減少失業，失業率看來卻在上升。/ He would like an assurance that other forces will not move into the territory that his forces vacate. 他想確保其他勢力不會在他的軍隊撤離後進入這個地區。/ He will have been pleased by Marshal Yazov's assurance of the armed forces' loyalty. 當亞佐夫元帥向他保證武裝部隊的忠誠時，他會感到心滿意足的。/ He gave me his personal assurance that the vehicle was safe. 他親自向我保證，這輛車是安全的。/ We now have a firm assurance of support from the government. 現在我們得到了政府將給予支援的明確承諾。/ They accepted his assurance that patients would be treated as soon as possible. 他們接受了他作出的病人會盡快得到治療的承諾。/ He accused the president of going back on assurances given earlier. 他指責總統違背了先前的諾言。/ an assurance about the safety of this equipment 對這臺裝置的安全性的保證 / assurances from the manager 經理的承諾 / Despite assurances from the government, the chemicals are known to be dangerous. 儘管有政府的保證，化學品的危險性是人所共知的。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**undertaking**</span> [ˌʌndəˈteɪkɪŋ; 美 -dərˈt-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (to do sth) / ~ (that…) [正式] [尤英，法律] 正式保證（去做某事）：</span>The landlord gave a written undertaking that the repairs would be carried out. 房東書面保證將進行維修。/ The MOD gave an undertaking to Saville that it had provided him with all relevant material. 國防部向薩維爾保證已向他提供了所有相關的資料。/ contractual undertaking 合同擔保 
            
<span class="vocabulary">**reassurance**</span> [ˌri:əˈʃʊərəns; -ˈʃɔ:r-; 美 -ˈʃʊr-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] ~ (that…)（讓人消除疑慮或擔憂的）保證：</span>The company tried to offer reassurance on the safety of its products. 該公司試圖讓人打消對其產品安全性的顧慮。<span class="definition">2 [C] ~ (that…) 讓人消除疑慮或擔憂的話語或行動，如保證：</span>We have been given reassurances that the water is safe to drink. 我們得到保證說這水適合飲用。/ reassurances that pesticides are not harmful 殺蟲劑沒有危害的保證
