[U, C] 證實；確認書、證明書：</span>I'm still waiting for confirmation of the test results. 我仍在等待考試結果的通知書。/ Practically every new piece of documentary evidencee provides additional confirmation that the charges are true. 實際上，每一份新的證明檔案都進一步證實這些指控屬實。/ final confirmation 最終的確認 / email, verbal, visual, written confirmation 電子郵件確認；口頭確認；親眼證實；書面確認 / judicial（美）, official confirmation 司法的確認；官方的確認 / experimental, laboratary confirmation（均為術語）實驗的證據；實驗室的證據 / Our data provide experimental confirmation of these predictions. 我們的資料在實驗方面證實了這些推測。/ The police are seeking independent confirmation of certain details of the story. 警方正在尋找案件某些細節的獨立證據。/ Written confirmation came three days later. 三天後獲得了書面確認。/ confirmation hearing（美）批准任命的聽證會 / She nodded in confirmation. 她點點頭表示認可。/ We need confirmation in writing before we can send your order out. 給你們傳送訂購的貨物之前，我們需要書面確認。/ subject to confirmation 有待確認

<span class="vocabulary">**proof**</span> [pru:f]

<span class="vocabulary">**identification**</span> [aɪ͵dentɪfɪ'keɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 識別…的存在、重要性或真實性，即確認：</span>early identification of adolescents at risk for violence 對有遭受暴力侵害危險的青少年的早期確認 / the early identification of health problems 健康問題的早期確認
           
<span class="vocabulary">**circular**</span> [ˈsɜ:kjələ(r); 美 ˈsɜ:rk-]
<span class="definition">adj.（論點或理論）迴圈論證的（以一種觀點證明另一觀點，接著再用後一種觀點反過來去證明前一觀點）：</span>瞭解即可

相關詞義延伸：
· confirm還可表示使某人徹底成為一名基督徒或天主徒，即給某人施堅信禮、施放堅振；
· prove還可表示（在一段時間後）發現（或被發現）是。也可表示向別人展示自己在某方面的才能或能力。亦可表示向別人展示你是某類人或你有某種品質；
· witness還可指看到某事發生，尤指犯罪或事故。也可指連署。

周邊詞彙補充：
· check可指查明…是否正確或真實，或查明其是否像你想的一樣，即確定；
· clear可指證明某人無罪（或無辜）；
· hold可指（論點、理論等）站得住腳；
· nail可指抓住某人並證明其有罪（或證明其做了壞事）。也可指證明某事不屬實。

<span class="vocabulary">**evidence**</span> ['evɪdəns] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] evidence (of/for sth) / evidence (that ...) / evidence (to suggest, show, etc.) 讓人相信某事的事實、跡象或物體，即證據，根據。是一個常規用詞，最常用：</span>documentary, photographic evidence 書面證據；影像證據 / archaeological evidence 考古學證據 / DNA, forensic, scientific evidence DNA證據；法醫證據；科學證據 / false evidence 假證據 / convincing evidence 可靠的證據 / There is ample evidence that the world is getting hotter. 有大量的證據表明全球正在變暖。/ The room bore evidence of a struggle. 房間裡有搏鬥過的痕跡。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**testimony**</span> [ˈtestɪməni; 美 -moʊni]
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.] ~ (to sth)（證明某事物存在或真實的）證據：</span>This increase in exports bears testimony to the successes of industry. 出口增長證明了產業的成功。/ The pyramids are an eloquent testimony to the ancient Egyptians' engineering skills. 金字塔是古埃及人非凡工程技術的明鑑。/ This book is testimony to a very individual kind of courage. 這本書顯示了個人非凡的勇氣。/ His thick, swollen fingers bore testimony to a lifetime of toil. 他粗壯腫脹的手指是一生勞作的見證。/ Her living room is also her office, filled with desks, books, papers, a testimony to her dedication to her work. 她的客廳也是她的辦公室，裡面擺滿了書桌、書籍和檔案，充分證明了她的敬業精神。
          
<span class="vocabulary">**testament**</span> [ˈtestəmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing., U] [正式] ~ (to sth)（證明某事物存在或真實的）證據：</span>The new model is a testament to the skill and dedication of the workforce. 這種新型產品顯示了全體員工的技術水平和敬業精神。/ Braka's house, just off Sloane Square, is a testament to his Gothic tastes. 布拉卡那幢緊靠斯隆廣場的房子是他喜好哥特式建築的一個證據。/ The fact that these scandals are now public is testament to the relative openness of America's government. 這些醜聞如今公之於眾的事實說明了美國政府的相對透明。/ He is a living testament to the power of following one's dream. 他是追尋夢想的力量的活生生的證明。/ The decline of the port is sad testament to the state of the shipping industry. 該港的衰落是航運業現狀的可悲證明。/ The crumbling ruins bear mute testament to the ravages of war. 頹垣斷壁無言地訴說著戰爭的創傷。/ That so many people came is (a) testament to her power of persuasion. 這麼多人來說明她有強大的說服力。/ The immaculate state of the garden bears testament to a lifetime's effort. 花園料理得盡善盡美是畢生心血的證明。/ The monument is a worthy testament to the courage of the men who fought in the war. 這座紀念碑是那些參戰者勇氣的最佳證明。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**proof**</span> [pru:f]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] ~ (of sth/that…)（作為某事物之真實性的）證據、證明：</span>Can you provide any proof of identity? 你能提供什麼身份證明嗎？/ Keep the receipt as proof of purchase. 儲存收據，作為購物證明。/ Proof of purchase must be provided before a refund can be made. 退款前必須提供購買憑證。/ There is no proof that the knife belonged to her. 沒有證據證明那把刀子是屬於她的。/ These results are a further proof of his outstanding ability. 這些成果進一步證明了他的傑出才幹。/ You have to have proof of residence in the state of Texas, such as a Texas ID card. 你必須有在得克薩斯州的居住證明，比如得克薩斯身份證。/ This is not necessarily proof that he is wrong. 這不一定就證明他是錯的。/ Do you have positive proof that she took the money? 你有確鑿證據證明她拿了那些錢嗎?/ I have no real proof that he was in the country at the time. 我沒有可靠的證據證明當時他在該國。/ The photo was final proof of her husband's infidelity. 這張照片最終證明了她丈夫的不忠。/ legal proof 法律證據 / scientific proof 科學證據 / He is living proof that some players just get better with age. 有些運動員年紀越大狀態越好，他就是個活生生的例子。/ I am living proof that the treatment works. 我是這一治療有效的活生生的證明。/ He is unlikely to make wild accusations without proof. 沒有證據他不可能胡亂指控。/ Her account gives us no concrete proof of his guilt. 她的敘述並不能成為證明他有罪的確鑿證據。/ the burden of proof, the onus of proof 舉證責任 / The burden of proof lies on us to prove negligence. 證明對方犯有疏忽罪的舉證責任落在我們身上。/ The men were acquitted for lack of proof. 因為證據不足，這些人被宣告無罪。/ Civil proceedings require a lower standard of ~than criminal cases.民事訴訟比刑事案件的舉證標準低。
