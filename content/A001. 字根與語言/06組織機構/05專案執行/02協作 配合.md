☀ <span class="category">**協作 配合：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**cooperate**</span> [kəʊˈɒpəreɪt; 美 koʊˈɑ:p-]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (with sb) (in/on sth) 與別人合作、協作（以達成某事）：</span>They had cooperated closely in the planning of the project. 他們曾密切合作，共同規劃這一專案。/ The two companies are cooperating in the development of a new engine. 兩家公司正在合作開發一種新的發動機。/ We are cooperating on a research project. 我們正在合作搞一個研究專案。/ The UN had been co-operating with the State Department on a plan to find countries willing to take the refugees. 聯合國一直都在與美國國務院合作推進一項計劃，尋找願意接納難民的國家。/ The two groups agreed to cooperate with each other. 這兩個組同意相互協作。/ The couple spoke about how they would co-operate in the raising of their child. 這對夫婦講述了他們將如何共同撫養孩子。<span class="definition">vi. 按照某人的要求來做事以對此人起到幫助作用，如協助、配合：</span>Their captors told them they would be killed unless they cooperated. 抓住他們的人說如果他們不配合就殺掉他們。/ The plan failed because the soldiers refused to co-operate. 因為士兵們不肯配合，這項計劃宣告失敗。/ He has said he will cooperate fully with the police enquiries. 他說他將全力配合警方的調查。/ The firm has agreed to cooperate with the employment survey. 這家公司已同意配合進行就業調查。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**cooperation**</span> [kəʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃn; 美 koʊˌɑ:p-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] ~ (with sb) (in doing sth) / ~ (between A and B)（為達成某個目標而與其它人員或機構展開的）合作、協作：</span>a report produced by the government in cooperation with the chemical industry 政府與化工行業合作提出的一份報告 / We would like to see closer cooperation between parents and schools. 我們希望學生家長和學校有更加緊密的合作。/ a society founded on mutual cooperation and shared prosperity 建立在相互合作和共同繁榮基礎上的社會 / cultural, economic, military, political, social, technical cooperation 文化合作；經濟合作；軍事合作；政治合作；社會合作；技術合作 / She called for closer cooperation on drugs control. 她呼籲在毒品控制上進行更為密切的合作。/ They are seeking the cooperation of senior medical staff. 他們正在尋求高階醫療人員的合作。/ an attempt to promote cooperation between universities and industry 促進大學與企業之間合作的努力 / The film was made in cooperation with the Board. 該影片是與董事會合作製作的。/ increased technical cooperation among large companies 大公司之間進一步的技術合作 / political cooperation between the two groups 兩個團體之間的政治合作 / We asked for their cooperation in the collection of data. 我們請求與他們在資料蒐集方面進行合作。/ We should like to thank you for your cooperation with us. 我們非常感謝你與我們合作。/ There is a need for greater economic cooperation. 有必要加強經濟合作。<span class="definition">2 [U] 協助、配合（別人的要求）：</span>We would be grateful for your cooperation in clearing the hall as quickly as possible. 你們若大力協助儘快把大廳騰空，我們將不勝感激。/ We are hoping to enlist the cooperation of women's groups. 我們希望爭取到婦女團體的協助。/ With the cooperation of the public, the police may be able to catch this man. 在公眾的配合之下，警方有可能抓到這個人。/ You will need some cooperation from your family. 你會需要家人的一些協助。/ They offered their cooperation on the project. 他們在這個專案上提供了協助。/ We have the full cooperation of all the departments involved. 我們得到了所有相關部門的全力配合。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**cooperative**</span> [kəʊˈɒpərətɪv; 美 koʊˈɑ:p-]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 [常用於名詞前]（為達成某個目標而與其它人員或機構）合作的、同心協力的：</span>Cooperative activity is essential to effective community work. 要把社群工作做好，協作活動是必不可少的。/ He was transferred to FBI custody in a smooth cooperative effort between Egyptian and US authorities. 在埃及當局和美國當局的共同努力下他被順利移交美國聯邦調查局進行羈押。/ The President said the visit would develop friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries. 總統說這次訪問將會促進兩國之間的友好合作關係。<span class="definition">2（對別人的要求）配合的、協助的：</span>Employees will generally be more cooperative if their views are taken seriously. 如果僱員的意見得到認真對待，他們一般都會更加配合。/ I made every effort to be cooperative. 我盡了最大努力配合。<span class="definition">3 [常用於名詞前] [商業] 共同擁有、共同經營且共享利益的，即合作的：</span>a cooperative farm 合作農場

<span class="vocabulary">**collaborate**</span> [kəˈlæbəreɪt]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (with sb) (on sth) / ~ (with sb) (in sth/in doing sth) 與某人合作以生產…或達成某事（尤指著書或做研究）：</span>She agreed to collaborate with him in writing her biography. 她同意與他合作撰寫她的傳記。/ We have collaborated closely with the university on this project. 我們與該大學在這個專案上密切合作。/ Researchers around the world are collaborating to develop a new vaccine. 世界各地的研究人員正在合作培育一種新的疫苗。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**collaboration**</span> [kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] ~ (with sb) (on sth) / ~ (between A and B)（與別人或別的組織機構進行的）合作、協作（以做成某事）：</span>She wrote the book in collaboration with one of her students. 她和她的一個學生合寫了這本書。/ The government worked in close collaboration with teachers on the new curriculum. 政府和教師就新的課程進行了緊密協作。/ collaboration between the teachers and the government 教師和政府間的合作 / It was a collaboration that produced extremely useful results. 這是一次帶來極其有益的成果的合作。/ There is substantial collaboration with neighbouring departments. 與相鄰的一些部門有大量的合作。/ Close collaboration between the Bank and the Fund is not merely desirable, it is essential. 銀行與基金會的緊密合作不僅可取，而且必不可少。/ The project is designed to foster collaboration between the university and industry. 該課題旨在促成大學與業界之間的合作。/ Rock musicians are working in collaboration with an orchestra to create a new opera. 搖滾音樂家正在與管絃樂隊合作創作一出新歌劇。/ a collaboration among experts in various fields 各領域專家間的合作 <span class="definition">2 [C]（人員或群體之間）合作的成果：</span>He was also a writer of beautiful stories, some of which are collaborations with his fiancée. 他還寫了一些美妙的故事，其中有些是和他的未婚妻合著的。

<span class="vocabulary">**coordinate**</span> [kəʊˈɔ:dɪneɪt; 美 koʊˈɔ:rd-]
<span class="definition">vt. 使一項活動的各個部分和人員相互協調（以使其順利進行）：</span>They appointed a new manager to coordinate the work of the team. 為協調這個團隊的工作，他們任用了一位新經理。/ We try to coordinate our activities with those of other groups. 我們盡力協調我們組與其他組的活動。/ Government officials visited the earthquake zone on Thursday morning to co-ordinate the relief effort. 星期四上午，政府官員視察地震災區，以協調救援工作。/ We need to develop a coordinated approach to the problem. 我們需要拿出解決這一問題的協調一致的辦法。/ the setting up of an advisory committee to co-ordinate police work 建立諮詢委員會以協調警務工作 / a carefully coordinated policy 經過周密協調的政策 <span class="definition">vt. 使身體各部分動作協調：</span>They spend several weeks each year undergoing intensive treatment which enables them to coordinate their limbs better. 他們每年都花費幾周的時間接受強化治療，以使四肢能夠更好地協調。<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth) (with sth)（衣服、傢俱等）搭配，協調（從而看起來順眼）：</span>This shade coordinates with a wide range of other colours. 這種深淺的顏色可與很多顏色搭配。/ She'll show you how to co-ordinate pattern and colours. 她會教你如何搭配款式和顏色。/ Tie it with fabric bows that co-ordinate with other furnishings. 繫上與其他裝飾相配的布質蝴蝶結。

<span class="vocabulary">**coordination**</span> [kəʊˌɔ:dɪˈneɪʃn; 美 koʊˌɔ:rd-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U]（某事物的各部分、某群人的各個子群體等之間的以一種有組織的且高效的方式所進行的）協作、協調、配合：</span>a need for greater coordination between departments 各部門間加強配合的必要 / the lack of coordination between the civilian and military authorities 老百姓和軍事當局之間缺乏協調 / the coordination of economic policy 經濟政策的協調 / a lack of coordination in conservation policy 環保政策的不協調 / inter-agency, internal, international coordination 機構間的協作；內部協作；國際協作 / economic, policy coordination 經濟協調；政策協調 / advice on colour/color coordination 有關色彩搭配的建議 / We must make a real effort to ensure greater coordination between the different groups. 我們必須認真作出努力以確保不同團體之間的更緊密配合。/ a leaflet produced by the government in coordination with professional bodies 政府在專業團體協助下製作的傳單 / coordination of conservation activities 自然保護活動的配合 / a need for coordination with the training department 與培訓部協調的必要 <span class="definition">2 [U] 身體的協調動作的能力：</span>clumsiness and lack of coordination 身體笨拙而且缺乏協調能力 / To improve hand-eye coordination, practise throwing and catching balls. 要想提高手眼協調能力，就練習投球和接球。/ You need good hand-eye coordination to play ball games. 玩球類遊戲，手和眼的動作要協調好。/ motor, physical coordination 運動協調能力；身體協調能力 / coordination problem 身體協調性問題

<span class="vocabulary">**teamwork**</span> ['ti:mwə:k] 
<span class="definition">n. [U]（作為一個團隊）良好地共同工作，即團隊合作，協同工作，協作，配合：</span>She stressed the importance of good teamwork. 她強調了團隊合作的重要性。

<span class="vocabulary">**team**</span> [ti:m] 
<span class="definition">vt. 常用被動式，指為做某事或達成某個目標而使兩個或多個人或事物相互配合、協作：</span>Tom was teamed with Jerry to perform the task. 湯姆被安排與傑瑞來共同執行此項任務。

<span class="vocabulary">**click**</span> [klɪk] 
<span class="definition">vi. [非正式] 在一起工作（或運轉）得良好，即配合默契，運作協調：</span>The team don’t seem to have clicked yet. 這支隊伍好像還沒有配合默契。

相關詞義延伸：
· cooperative還可指合作企業；合作社組織；
· collaborate還可指幫助已經佔領了自己國家的敵人，即通敵、勾結敵人。

周邊詞彙補充：
· touch可指與某人共事；
· partner可指在跳舞、遊戲等活動中成為某人的搭檔。
