☀ <span class="category">**職位空缺 職位懸空：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**opening**</span> ['əʊpənɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一個空缺的職位，相當於vacancy。是一個簡單用詞：</span>There are several openings in the sales department. 銷售部有幾個空缺。/ She is the strongest candidate to fill this opening. 她是此空缺最有競爭力的人選。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vacancy**</span> [ˈveɪkənsi]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (for sb/sth) 職位的空缺：</span>job vacancies 職位空缺 / staff vacancy（尤英）人員空缺 / temporary/casual vacancy 臨時空缺 / Her going on maternity leave will create a temporary vacancy. 她休產假時將會有一個臨時空缺。/ a vacancy for head chef 廚師長的職位空缺 / a vacancy in the IT department 資訊科技部的一個職位空缺 / to fill a vacancy 填補空額 / They had a short-term vacancy for a person on the foreign desk. 他們在國際新聞組有一個短期的空缺職位。/ Most vacancies are at senior level, requiring appropriate qualifications. 大多數空缺屬於高層職位，要求具備相應的資歷。/ The agency will let you know if they have any suitable vacancies. 如果有合適的職位空缺，職業介紹所會通知你的。/ A vacancy has arisen in our sales department.（arise尤為英式用法）我們銷售部出現了一個職位空缺。

<span class="vocabulary">**vacant**</span> ['veɪkənt] 
<span class="definition">adj. [正式]（一家公司中的一個職位）空缺的：</span>Situations Vacant (= a section in a newspaper where jobs are advertised)（英）（報紙上的）招聘廣告欄目 / A job fell vacant in the accounting department. 財務部出現了一個空缺。
