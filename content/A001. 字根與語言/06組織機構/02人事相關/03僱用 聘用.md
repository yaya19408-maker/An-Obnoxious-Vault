☀ <span class="category">**僱用 聘用 招募：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**employ**</span> [ɪm'plɒɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. employ sb (as sth) 僱用。是此義的基礎和常規用詞，極為常用：</span>How many people does the company employ? 這家公司僱了多少人？/ to be employed as a firefighter 受僱當消防員 / The arms industry employed millions of people. 軍工行業僱用數百萬人。/ to be employed in agriculture 受僱於農業 / to be permanently employed, to be temporarily employed 長期受僱；臨時受僱
           
<span class="vocabulary">**employment**</span> [ɪmˈplɔɪmənt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C]（尤指為賺錢而進行的）工作；受僱傭的狀態：</span>to be in paid employment 有拿工資的工作 / full-time, part-time employment 全職工作；兼職工作 / conditions of employment, terms of employment 僱用條件；僱用條款 / Graduates are finding it more and more difficult to find employment. 畢業生感到找工作越來越難。/ She was unable to find employment. 她沒能找到工作。/ He regularly drove from his home to his place of employment. 他定期開車往返於家和工作地點之間。/ pensions from previous employments 以前工作的退休金 <span class="definition">2 [U]（在一國或一個地區）人們擁有工作的一種狀態，如就業：</span>economic policies designed to secure full employment 旨在確保充分就業的經濟政策 / The government is aiming at full employment. 政府在力求達到全面就業。/ Changes in farming methods have badly affected employment in the area. 耕作方法的改變嚴重影響了這個地區的就業。<span class="definition">3 [U]（對某人的）僱用：</span>The law prevented the employment of children under ten in the cotton mills. 法律禁止棉紡廠僱用十歲以下的童工。

<span class="vocabulary">**hire**</span> ['haɪə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 僱用，錄用，聘請，此義項尤用於美式英語：</span>She was hired three years ago. 她是三年前獲錄用的。/ He is responsible for hiring and firing in our company. 他在我們公司負責員工的聘用和辭退。/ Workers were hired by the day. 工人們是按天僱的。<span class="definition">2 短期（或臨時）僱用：</span>to hire a lawyer 聘請律師 / to hire a firm of consultants 聘請諮詢公司 <span class="definition">n. [C] 一個新僱員或新員工，此義項尤用於美式英語：</span>new, recent hire 新僱員；近期僱用的人 / potential hire 可能僱用的人
            
<span class="vocabulary">**engage**</span> [ɪnˈgeɪdʒ]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] ~ sb (as sth) 僱用、聘用（某人去做某項工作）：</span>He is currently engaged as a consultant. 他現在受僱為顧問。/ We engaged the services of a recognized engineer. 我們僱用了一個公認的傑出工程師。/ He had been able to engage some staff. 他已經能夠僱用一些員工。

<span class="vocabulary">**engagement**</span> [ɪnˈgeɪdʒmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] [英] 僱用、聘用：</span>The terms of engagement are to be agreed in writing. 聘用條款應有書面協議。
             
<span class="vocabulary">**recruit**</span> [rɪˈkru:t]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 招募新人（使其加入一個公司、組織機構、學校、武裝部隊、警隊等）：</span>Soldiers were recruited from the local villages. 士兵都是從當地村莊招募的。/ Some of the men were recruited into the army. 一些男子被徵召入伍。/ The police are trying to recruit more officers from ethnic minorities. 警察機關正試圖從少數民族中徵募更多的新警員。/ Recruiting students to Computer Systems Engineering, the University looks for evidence of all-round ability. 在招收計算機系統工程專業的學生時，這個大學看重的是學生的綜合能力。/ They recruited several new members to the club. 他們吸收了幾名新成員進入俱樂部。/ He's responsible for recruiting at all levels. 他負責各級徵募工作。/ The specialist institutions directly recruit their own staff. 專家機構直接招募其所需人員。/ Senior managers are being aggressively recruited by companies. 公司正在千方百計地招募高階管理人員。/ GM recruited heavily in the South. 通用汽車公司在南方大量招人。/ Most of the workers will be recruited locally. 大多數工人都會在本地招募。/ She personally recruited the teachers. 她親自招聘了這些教師。/ Staff were recruited specially for the event. 為此次活動特別招聘了一些工作人員。/ He has been recruited as Sales Manager. 他受聘為銷售經理。/ A hundred patients were recruited for the study. 招募了一百名患者來參加此項研究。/ Ten new members were recruited to the committee. 十位新成員被吸收進委員會。/ a drive to recruit and retain federal employees 招募並留住聯邦僱員的活動
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**recruitment**</span> [rɪˈkruːtmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 成員（如士兵、員工等）的招募：</span>labour/labor, staff recruitment 勞動力招聘；招聘工作人員 / executive, faculty（美）, teacher, volunteer recruitment 行政人員招聘；大學教職員工招聘；教師招聘；志願者招聘 / graduate（美）, student recruitment 畢業生招聘；學生招聘 / army, military, police recruitment 新兵招募；警察招募 / ethnic-minority, minority（尤美）recruitment 對少數民族人員的招募 / the forced recruitment of children as soldiers 強制性徵召兒童兵 / the successful recruitment of a new chairman 成功招募到一位新的主席 / recruitment policy 招聘政策/ recruitment strategy 招聘策略 / recruitment procedure, recruitment process 招聘程式；招聘過程 / the company's recruitment officer 公司的招聘負責人 / recruitment site, recruitment website 招聘網站 / recruitment by large companies 大公司的招聘 / recruitment and retention of workers with essential skills 招聘並留住掌握關鍵技能的工人 / recruitment drive 招聘運動 / the recruitment of new members 新成員的招募 / the examination system for the recruitment of civil servants 招募公務員的考試體制 / a crisis in teacher recruitment 教師招聘遭遇的危機

<span class="vocabulary">**retain**</span> [rɪˈteɪn]
<span class="definition">vt. [更正式] [法律]（以預付金的形式）聘請律師：</span>a retaining fee 給所委託律師的預付辯護費 / to retain the services of a lawyer 聘定律師 / He decided to retain him for the trial. 他決定聘請他為出庭律師。

<span class="vocabulary">**for**</span> [fɔ:] 
<span class="definition">prep. 受僱於…，強調為…效力，有很強的效忠感：</span>She’s working for IBM. 她在IBM公司工作。/ to write for radio 為電臺撰稿

<span class="vocabulary">**with**</span> [wɪð] 
<span class="definition">prep. 受僱於…，使用…的服務，傾向於以平穩的語氣來陳述在某家機構工作的事實，並不如for那樣刻意強調對機構的效忠感：</span>He worked with a software company for eight years. 他在一家軟體公司裡工作了八年。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**resident**</span> [ˈrezɪdənt]
<span class="definition">adj.（大機構的專家）特聘的：</span>Having begun her career at Gray's Pottery, she stayed there as resident designer for seven years. 在格雷陶器場開始自己的職業生涯後，她在那裡做了七年的特聘設計師。/ Tom's our resident expert (= our own expert) on foreign movies. 湯姆是我們自己的外國電影專家。

周邊詞彙補充：
· probation可指工作的試用期、見習期；
· mercenary可指僱傭兵。

<span class="vocabulary">**poach**</span> [pəʊtʃ; 美 poʊtʃ]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sb/sth) (from sb/sth)（尤指秘密地、耍手段或不公平地）挖走人員：</span>Several of our employees have been poached by a rival firm. 我們公司有好幾名職員被對手公司給挖走了。/ The company authorised its staff to poach customers from the opposition. 公司指使其職員挖對手的客戶。/ allegations that it had poached members from other unions 它從其他工會挖走了成員的說法
