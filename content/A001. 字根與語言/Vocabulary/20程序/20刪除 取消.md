☀ <span class="category">**刪除、取消、解除、廢除：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**delete**</span> [dɪ'li:t] 
<span class="definition">vt. delete sth (from sth) 清除已被寫上、印上或存在電腦中的內容，即刪除：</span>Your name has been deleted from the list. 你的名字已從名單上刪除。/ This command deletes files from the directory. 這一指令把文件從目錄中刪除。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**omit**</span> [əˈmɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] ~ sth/sb (from sth)（故意或無意地）未將…包括在內，如刪除、省略、排除：</span>This scene is usually cut down or omitted altogether. 這種場景通常被剪掉或刪除。/ This fact had been conveniently omitted from his account of events. 出於方便考慮，他在講述事件經過時略去了這一事實。/ If you are a student, you can omit questions 16–18. 學生可以免做16–18題。/ People were surprised that Smith was omitted from the team. 人們感到驚訝，史密斯竟未列入該隊。/ Omit the salt in this recipe. 省略菜譜中放鹽這一步。

<span class="vocabulary">**omission**</span> [əˈmɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] ~ (from sth) 不包括、不做某事；不被包括、某事不被做。如省略；刪節；排除：</span>Everyone was surprised at her omission from the squad. 她未列入該編組使大家感到驚訝。/ the prosecution's seemingly malicious omission of recorded evidence 控方似乎惡意將錄下的證據排除在外的做法 / The play was shortened by the omission of two scenes. 此劇刪減了兩場戲。<span class="definition">2 [C] 被省略、刪除或排除的東西：</span>The duke was surprised by his wife's omission from the guest list. 公爵對他的夫人未在獲邀賓客之列感到意外。

<span class="vocabulary">**cut**</span> [kʌt] 
<span class="definition">vt. cut sth (from sth) 刪除，刪減，刪節：</span>The scene was cut from the movie. 這場戲在這部電影中被刪除了。/ to cut the essay from 8 000 to 5 000 把這篇文章從8000字刪減到5000字

<span class="vocabulary">**expunge**</span> [ɪkˈspʌndʒ]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (from sth) [正式] 除去某事物，例如從清單上或頭腦中抹去、刪去一個名字、一段資訊或一段記憶：</span>Details of his criminal activities were expunged from the file. 他犯罪活動的詳細情況已從檔案中刪去。/ What happened just before the accident was expunged from his memory. 事故前一刻發生的事他都記不得了。/ The experience was something he had tried to expunge from his memory. 他曾努力將那段經歷從記憶中抹去。/ The revolutionaries expunged domestic opposition. 革命者肅清了國內敵對勢力。            

<span class="vocabulary">**excise**</span> [ɪkˈsaɪz]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (from sth) [正式] 徹底清除某事物：</span>Certain passages were excised from the book. 書中某些段落已刪去。/ to excise racist and sexist references in newspapers 消除報紙中涉及種族和性別歧視的內容 / the question of permanently excising madness from the world 關於永久清除世界上瘋狂舉動的問題
           
<span class="vocabulary">**erase**</span> [ɪˈreɪz; 美 ɪˈreɪs]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (from sth) 徹底清除某事物（如想法或感受）：</span>She tried to erase the memory of that evening. 她試圖忘卻那天晚上的事。/ They are desperate to erase the memory of that last defeat in Cardiff. 他們急於抹去上次在卡迪夫遭遇失敗的記憶。/ They are determined to erase the bad memories of last year's defeats. 他們決心徹底忘記去年的失敗。/ All doubts were suddenly erased from his mind. 他心中所有的疑慮突然煙消雲散了。/ You cannot erase injustice from the world. 任何人都不可能讓非正義從世界上消失。/ Love was a word he'd erased from his vocabulary since Susan's going. 自從蘇珊離開後，他再也不提“愛情”這個詞了。<span class="definition">vt.（尤指為了更改而）擦掉、抹掉（筆跡等）：</span>He had erased the wrong word. 他擦去了寫錯的字。/ All the phone numbers had been erased. 所有的電話號碼都被抹掉了。/ This event has been effectively erased from written records. 這一事件從文字記載中被有效地抹去了。/ These people have been virtually erased from the history book. 這些人實際上已經從歷史書中抹去了。<span class="definition">vt. 抹去（磁帶上的錄音或儲存器中的資訊）；刪除（計算機檔案）：</span>Parts of the recording have been erased. 部分錄音已被抹掉。/ He was in the studio tearfully erasing all the tapes he'd slaved over. 他在錄音棚裡流著眼淚抹掉他辛苦錄製的所有磁帶。/ It appears the names were accidentally erased from computer disks. 看起來那些名字被意外地從計算機磁碟上刪除了。/ She wanted to erase the message before her parents heard it. 她想刪掉這條留言，不讓她父母聽到。

<span class="vocabulary">**cancel**</span> ['kænsl] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 決定已安排好的事現在不再發生，如取消、撤銷、終止：</span>All flights have been cancelled because of bad weather. 因天氣惡劣，所有航班均被取消。<span class="definition">2 給一張票或郵票做標記（使其不能被再次使用），如蓋銷、登出：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 表示你不再想履行一項協議（尤指已具有法律效力的協議），如撤銷、取消、廢除：</span>to cancel policy, to cancel subscription 取消保單；取消訂閱 / to cancel my order 取消我的訂單 / to cancel debts 免除債務 / to cancel within 10 days 在10天內取消
           
<span class="vocabulary">**cancellation**</span> [ˌkænsəˈleɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] 取消；撤銷；登出（是一種決定）：</span>We need at least 24 hours' notice of cancellation. 如欲取消，請至少提前24小時告知。/ Heavy seas can cause cancellation of ferry services. 海上起風浪會導致渡輪航班取消。/ Cancellations must be made in writing. 撤銷必須有書面通知。/ They opted for rescheduling rather than outright cancellation. 他們選擇了重新安排時間，而非就此取消。/ flight, train（英）cancellation 航班取消；火車取消 / In the event of a flight cancellation, you will be compensated. 倘若航班取消，你會獲得賠償。/ They campaigned for debt cancellation for the poorest countries of the world. 他們發起了為世界上最貧困國家免除債務的運動。/ cancellation charge, cancellation fee 取消預訂須交納的費用 <span class="definition">2 [C] 被取消（或被撤銷、被登出）了的事物：</span>Are there any cancellations for this evening's performance? (= tickets that have been returned) 今晚的演出有退票嗎？/ We may be able to offer you some tickets if we have any cancellations. 如果有人退票、我們也許能給你幾張票。<span class="definition">3 [U] 使某事物失去法律效力，如作廢；廢除；取消；中止（是一種事實）：</span>the cancellation of the contract 合同的取消

<span class="vocabulary">**reverse**</span> [rɪˈvɜ:s; 美 rɪˈvɜ:rs]
<span class="definition">vt. 撤銷，廢除（決定、法律等）：</span>The Court of Appeal reversed the decision. 上訴法庭撤銷了這項裁決。/ They have made it clear they will not reverse the decision to increase prices. 他們已經明確表示不會撤銷提價的決定。                      

<span class="vocabulary">**reversal**</span> [rɪˈvɜ:sl; 美 rɪˈvɜ:rsl]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（決定、法律等的）撤銷：</span>the reversal of a decision 決定的撤銷

<span class="vocabulary">**chop**</span> [tʃɒp; 美 tʃɑ:p]
<span class="definition">vt. [非正式] [常用被動式] 取消：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**sunset**</span> [ˈsʌnset]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（法律或法律效力的）自動廢止期、效力消減期：</span>There is a five-year sunset on the new tax. 新稅種的有效施行期限為五年。<span class="definition">adj. [只用於名詞前]（法律或法律效力）定期廢止的：</span>a two-year sunset clause in the new law 新法律中的一條實施期限為兩年的“落日條款” <span class="definition">vt.&vi.（法律或法律效力）定期屆滿廢止：</span>The tax relief will sunset after a year. 稅款減免將在一年後廢止。

<span class="vocabulary">**scrap**</span> [skræp]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 取消不再有用的東西：</span>They had been forced to scrap plans for a new school building. 他們已被迫撤銷了建築新校舍的計劃。

<span class="vocabulary">**scratch**</span> [skrætʃ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. scratch (sb/sth) (from sth) 在…開始之前就決定該事不能發生，或…不能參加或加入…，即取消，撤銷，退出。算是一種形象化用法，還算常用：</span>to scratch a rocket launch 取消火箭發射計劃 / He had scratched from the race because of his injury. 他因傷退出了比賽。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**scrub**</span> [skrʌb]
<span class="definition">vt. [非正式] 取消原有安排：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**call**</span> [kɔ:l] 
<span class="definition">vt. [美] 取消（比賽，如體育賽事）：</span>We called the next game. 我們取消了下一場比賽。

<span class="vocabulary">**pull**</span> [pʊl] 
<span class="definition">vt. [非正式] 取消一次活動或事件；撤銷廣告等：</span>The gig was pulled at the last moment. 特約演出臨時取消了。

<span class="vocabulary">**abolish**</span> [ə'bɒlɪʃ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 正式終結一項法律、制度、習俗等，即廢除：</span>to abolish the death penalty 廢除死刑 / The tax should be abolished. 這種稅應該取消。/ Foreign exchange controls were largely abolished. 外匯管制基本已取消。/ to abolish quotas on immigrants 取消移民人數限額
           
<span class="vocabulary">**annul**</span> [əˈnʌl]
<span class="definition">vt. 正式宣告…在法律上失效：</span>Opposition party leaders are now pressing for the entire election to be annulled. 反對黨領導人現正強烈要求宣告這次選舉無效。/ The marriage was annulled last month. 上個月這樁婚姻被宣告無效。

<span class="vocabulary">**revoke**</span> [rɪˈvəʊk; 美 -ˈvoʊk]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 正式取消、廢除或撤銷…（如許可、法律、協議等）：</span>The government revoked her husband's license to operate migrant labor crews. 政府撤銷了她丈夫管理外來打工人群的許可證。

<span class="vocabulary">**abrogate**</span> [ˈæbrəgeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [術語] 正式廢除或撤銷（一項法律、協議、習俗等）：</span>The next prime minister could abrogate the treaty. 下一任首相可能會廢除這個條約。

<span class="vocabulary">**ditch**</span> [dɪtʃ]
<span class="definition">vt. [非正式] 廢除（不再想要或不再需要的某事物或某人）：</span>Unpopular policies such as unilateral disarmament were ditched. 諸如單邊裁軍之類不得人心的政策已被廢除了。/ The new road building programme has been ditched. 新的道路建設計劃已廢棄。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**axe**</span> [æks]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [尤英] [美式英語用ax] [非正式] the axe可指（某個專案）取消（通常指為節省資金而進行的取消）：</span>His prime-time TV show is likely to get the axe. 他在黃金時段的電視節目很可能會被取消。/ Wielding the axe on the prison plan would be one way of saving money.（wield尤為英式用法）取消監獄規劃會是省錢的一種方法。/ We were expecting bad news but had no idea where the axe would fall(= where the loss would be). 我們預料到有壞訊息，卻不知道遭殃的會是什麼。<span class="definition">vt. [英] [美式英語用ax] [常用被動式] 將專案砍掉：</span>Community projects are being axed by hard-pressed social services departments. 處於困境的社會服務部門被迫砍掉社群專案。

<span class="vocabulary">**lift**</span> [lɪft] 
<span class="definition">vt. 解除…的限制、撤銷法律或規則：</span>to lift a blockade 解除封鎖。/ to lift the ban on arms exports 解除武器出口禁令 / The curfew was partially lifted. 宵禁部分解除

☀ <span class="category">**清除、根除、戒除、排除、擺脫、清理的動詞和介詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**remove**</span> [rɪ'mu:v] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 remove sth/sb (from sth/sb) 將…從某地移開、拿開等：</span>He removed his hand from her shoulder. 他將手從她的肩膀上拿開。/ Fittings should be completely removed for cleaning. 裝置得全部移走以方便清掃。/ to remove the dirty dishes 端走髒盤子 / Illegally parked vehicles will be removed. 非法停放的車輛將被拖走。/ He was removed from the school. 他被學校開除。<span class="definition">2 remove sth (from sb/sth) 去除或清除令人不快的、髒的等事物；使某事物消失：</span>She has had the tumour removed. 她的腫瘤已經切除了。/ These stains can be difficult to remove. 這些汙漬可能很難清除。/ to remove problems, to remove obstacles, to remove objections, to remove hopes 解決問題；排除障礙；消除異議；斷絕希望 / to remove barriers to communication between people 消除人們之間的溝通障礙 / The news removed any doubts about the company’s future. 這個訊息消除了一切有關公司未來的疑慮。
                                  
<span class="vocabulary">**obliterate**</span> [əˈblɪtəreɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式]（透過徹底毀掉或覆蓋掉）清除某事物的印記：</span>The building was completely obliterated by the bomb. 炸彈把那座建築物徹底摧毀了。/ The snow had obliterated their footprints. 白雪覆蓋了他們的足跡。/ Their warheads are enough to obliterate the world several times over. 他們擁有的彈頭足以將世界毀滅好幾次。/ Whole villages were obliterated by fire. 整座整座的村莊都被大火所吞噬。/ The sect was totally obliterated by the state. 該教派在該州被徹底剷除了。<span class="definition">vt. 抹掉（回憶、感情、想法等）：</span>Everything that happened that night was obliterated from his memory. 那天夜裡發生的一切都從他的記憶中消失了。/ There was time enough to obliterate memories of how things once were for him. 時間足以抹去他對過去經歷的記憶。

<span class="vocabulary">**rehabilitate**</span> [ˌri:əˈbɪlɪteɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. 使某人戒毒或戒酒：</span>a unit for rehabilitating drug addicts 幫助吸毒者恢復正常生活的機構       

<span class="vocabulary">**dissolve**</span> [dɪˈzɒlv; 美 -ˈzɑ:lv]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth) (away)（尤指以化學手段）除去某事物：</span>a new detergent that dissolves stains 新型去汙洗滌劑
           
<span class="vocabulary">**shed**</span> [ʃed]
<span class="definition">vt.（常用於報章）去除、擺脫（不想要的東西）：</span>The factory is shedding a large number of jobs. 這家工廠正大批裁員。/ The firm is to shed 700 jobs. 公司將裁員 700 人。/ He had maintained a rigid diet, shedding some twenty pounds. 他嚴格地堅持節食，減了差不多20磅。/ a quick way to shed unwanted pounds (= extra weight or fat on your body) 快速減肥的方法 / Museums have been trying hard to shed their stuffy image. 博物館一直努力改變自己沉悶的形象。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**strip**</span> [strɪp]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (off) / ~ A (off/from B)/~ B (of A) 從某事物上除去一層（尤指使其完全暴露）：</span>Strip off all the existing paint. 把現有的油漆全部刮掉。/ We stripped all the paint off the walls. 我們把油漆全部從牆上刮下來。/ The floorboards in both this room and the dining room have been stripped, sanded and sealed. 這個房間和餐廳的木地板都已經被刮除塗層並進行了打磨上蠟。/ Deer had stripped the tree of its bark. 鹿啃掉了樹皮。/ Deer had stripped all the bark off the tree. 鹿把樹皮全都啃光了。/ The bark is stripped from the trees by hand. 樹皮是用手撕下來的。/ After the guests had gone, I stripped all the beds (= removed all the sheets in order to wash them). 客人走後，我把被單床單全都撤了下來。<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (out) 從某處拿走所有東西，使某處空無一物：</span>We had to strip out all the old wiring and start again. 我們不得不將原有的線路全部拆除，從頭再來。/ Strip out any damaged wiring. 清除所有損壞的配線。/ Thieves had stripped the house bare. 竊賊把房子劫掠一空。

<span class="vocabulary">**eliminate**</span> [ɪˈlɪmɪneɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth/sb (from sth) 移除、清除、排除某人或某事物（尤指不需要之物）：</span>Credit cards eliminate the need to carry a lot of cash. 有了信用卡就用不著攜帶很多現金。/ The police have eliminated two suspects from their investigation. 警方透過調查已經排除了兩名嫌疑犯。/ This diet claims to eliminate toxins from the body. 這種飲食據稱具有排除體內毒素的作用。/ The Sex Discrimination Act has not eliminated discrimination in employment.《反性別歧視法》並未根除工作中的歧視。/ Academic departments are being eliminated. 純理論研究院系即將被裁撤。/ The risk cannot be eliminated altogether. 這個危險還不能完全排除。/ This procedure does not completely eliminate the possibility of an accident. 這一程式不能徹底排除事故出現的可能。/ Getting this job has effectively eliminated his financial worries. 找到這份工作有效地消除了他的經濟擔憂。/ a policy that they claim will eventually eliminate corruption in the industry 他們聲稱最終會清除業界腐敗的一項政策 / The single market is designed to eliminate barriers to the free movement of goods, services and people. 建立單一市場是為了消除阻礙貨物、服務和人員自由流通的壁壘。/ Try to eliminate fatty foods from your diet. 儘量不吃含脂肪多的食物。<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] ~ sb (from sth)（在比賽中）淘汰一個人或團隊：</span>All the English teams were eliminated in the early stages of the competition. 所有英格蘭隊伍在比賽初期就被淘汰了。/ I was eliminated from the 400 metres in the semi-finals. 我在400米半決賽中被淘汰。/ If you are eliminated in the show-jumping then you are out of the complete competition. 如果在馬術障礙賽中被淘汰，就意味著完全出局。          
           
<span class="vocabulary">**exclude**</span> [ɪkˈsklu:d]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (from sth)（做某事或考慮某事時）故意將…排除在外：</span>The cost of borrowing has been excluded from the inflation figures. 通脹數字未包括借貸成本。/ Unlawfully obtained evidence is not automatically excluded from a criminal trial. 非法獲得的證據並不自動排除在刑事審判之外。/ a clause that seeks to exclude liability for death or serious injury 一項旨在免除對造成死亡及重傷擔負責任的條款 / Try excluding fat from your diet. 平時用餐時儘量避免含脂肪的食品。/ They eat only plant foods, and take care to exclude animal products from other areas of their lives. 他們只吃素食，在生活的其他方面也注意拒絕使用動物產品。/ In some schools, Christmas carols are being modified to exclude any reference to Christ. 某些學校正在對聖誕頌歌進行修改，刪除提及耶穌的內容。/ Buses run every hour, Sundays excluded. 公共汽車每小時一班，星期天除外。<span class="definition">vt. 阻止某人或某事物進入一個地方或參加某事：</span>Women are still excluded
