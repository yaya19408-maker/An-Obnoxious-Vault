☀ <span class="category">**防止、預防的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**prevent**</span> [prɪ'vent] 
<span class="definition">vt. prevent sb/sth (from doing sth) 防止某人做某事；防止某事發生。是一個常規用詞：</span>The accident could have been prevented. 這次事故本來是可以避免的。/ to prevent sunburn 防止曬傷 / prevent the disease from spreading 防止疾病蔓延
           
<span class="vocabulary">**combat**</span> [ˈkɒmbæt; 美 ˈkɑ:m-]
<span class="definition">vt. 防止壞事發生、防止事情變得更糟：</span>measures to combat unemployment, measures to combat disease 防止失業的措施；防止疾病的措施 / She argued that the only way to combat inflation effectively was to keep interest rates high. 她提出理由說有效防止通貨膨脹的唯一方法就是保持高利率。/ Measures to combat violent crime have been proposed. 已提出打擊暴力犯罪的措施。

<span class="vocabulary">**insure**</span> [ɪn'ʃʊə] 
<span class="definition">vt. insure sb against sth 可表示預防將來發生的壞事：</span>This measure can effectively insure people against traffic accidents. 該項措施能夠有效預防交通事故的發生。/ Having a lot of children is a way of insuring themselves against loneliness in old age. 養很多孩子是他們預防老年孤寂的一種辦法。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**hedge**</span> [hedʒ]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ against sth 防備或防範壞事，尤指防範金錢的損失：</span>You can hedge against redundancy or illness with insurance. 可以購買保險以防失業或患病。/ Today's clever financial instruments make it possible for firms to hedge their risks. 現今各種巧妙的金融手段使得各公司防範風險成為可能。

☀ <span class="category">**防止、預防的其它詞性：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**prevention**</span> [prɪˈvenʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 預防、防止：</span>accident, crime, disease, drug, fire, flood, pollution, pregnancy（尤美）prevention 事故的預防；犯罪的預防；疾病的預防；吸毒的預防；火災的預防；洪災的預防；汙染的預防；懷孕的預防 / AIDS, cancer, HIV, osteoporosis prevention 艾滋病的預防；癌症的預防；艾滋病病毒的預防；骨質疏鬆症的預防 / prevention efforts, prevention measures, prevention programme/program, prevention scheme（英）, prevention strategy 預防工作；預防措施；預防方案；預防計劃；預防策略 / crime prevention strategies 預防犯罪策略 / the prevention of disease 疾病的預防 / a fire prevention officer 消防官員           

<span class="vocabulary">**precaution**</span> [prɪˈkɔ:ʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [usually pl.] ~ (against sth) 預防措施：</span>fire, safety, security precaution 防火措施；安全措施；保安措施 / precautions against fire 防火措施 / You must take all reasonable precautions to protect yourself and your family. 你必須採取一切合理的預防措施，保護自己和家人。/ I'll keep the letter as a precaution. 我要儲存這封信，以防萬一。/ Could he not, just as a precaution, move to a place of safety? 就算僅僅是為了以防萬一，難道他就不能挪到安全的地方嗎？/ I had taken the precaution of doing a little research before I left London. 我已在離開倫敦前進行了準備，做了些調查研究。/ Condoms are a necessary precaution. 避孕套是一種必要的預防措施。/ the need to take great precautions to protect sources 採取大量的防範措施保護資源的必要性 / You'll be safe if you observe certain basic precautions. 如果遵守一些基本的預防措施，你就不會有危險。/ We take every precaution to ensure your safety. 我們採取一切預防措施以確保你的安全。/ Remember to use all necessary safety precautions. 請記住，要採取所有必要的安全防範措施。/ She had to stay in hospital overnight, just as a precaution. 她得在醫院待一晚上，以防萬一。/ a precaution against customers who try to leave without paying 預防顧客企圖不付錢就離開的措施 / Staff are expected to take reasonable precautions for their own safety. 希望員工為了自身安全而採取合理的預防措施。/ I took the precaution of turning the water supply off first. 我先關閉了供水系統，以防萬一。

<span class="vocabulary">**insurance**</span> [ɪn'ʃʊərəns] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] insurance (against sth)（為防止將來發生壞事而採取的）預防措施：</span>At that time people had large families as an insurance against some children dying. 那時人們養的子女很多，以防有孩子夭折。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**hedge**</span> [hedʒ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ against sth 一種防止金錢損失的手段：</span>to buy gold as a hedge against inflation 購買黃金以抵消通貨膨脹造成的損失
           
<span class="vocabulary">**preventable**</span> [prɪˈventəbl]
<span class="definition">adj.（壞事）可以預防的：</span>Forty-thousand children a day die from preventable diseases. 每天有4萬個孩子死於本可以預防的疾病。/ These injuries are entirely preventable. 這些傷害完全可以預防。/ Many potentially preventable cancers are currently missed. 許多可防治的癌症目前被漏診。/ Most common health upsets that spoil a holiday are easily preventable. 導致假期泡湯的大多數常見的身體不適都是很容易預防的。

<span class="vocabulary">**against**</span> [ə'ɡenst, ə'ɡeɪnst] 
<span class="definition">prep. 以防止…發生，或以減少由…造成的損害：</span>an injection against rabies 狂犬病預防注射 / They took precautions against fire. 他們採取了防火措施。/ Are we insured against theft? 我們投了盜竊險沒有？
