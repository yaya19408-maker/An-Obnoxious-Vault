☀ <span class="category">**準時 及時：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**punctual**</span> ['pʌŋktʃʊəl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 準時的，按時的；不晚的，守時的：</span>a punctual start at 9 o’clock 9點鐘準時開始 / He’s always very punctual. 他一向很準時。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**prompt**</span> [prɒmpt; 美 prɑ:mpt]
<span class="definition">adj. [不用於名詞前]（人）準時到達的。相當於punctual：</span>Please be prompt when attending these meetings. 參加上述會議，請準時出席。<span class="definition">adv. 準時地：</span>The meeting will begin at ten o'clock prompt. 會議將於十點鐘準時開始。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**timely**</span> [ˈtaɪmli]
<span class="definition">adj. 及時的；適時的：</span>A nasty incident was prevented by the timely arrival of the police. 警察的及時到來阻止了一次嚴重事故。/ This has been a timely reminder to us all. 對我們大家來說這個提醒非常及時。/ a timely reminder to people that leaving their doors open is an invitation to thieves 給人們的一個及時的提醒，即不鎖門等於引賊入室 / The recent outbreaks of cholera are a timely reminder that this disease is still a serious health hazard. 最近霍亂的爆發及時提醒了人們該疾病仍然會嚴重危害健康。/ The exhibition is timely, since 'self-taught' art is catching on in a big way. 展會召開得非常及時，因為“自學”藝術正在廣泛流行。/ timely warning 及時預警 / timely intervention 及時干預 / All claims must be settled in a professional and timely manner. 所有的索賠必須專業、及時地解決。

周邊詞彙補充：
· prompt可指（人）做事不拖延的，即立即的、迅速的、及時的。
