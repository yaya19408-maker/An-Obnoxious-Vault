☀ <span class="category">**期限 截止：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**deadline**</span> ['dedlaɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] deadline (for sth) 最後期限或截止日期（在此時必須完成某事）。是此義的常規用詞，最為常用：</span>The deadline for applications is 30 April. 提交申請書的截止日期是4月30日。/ the deadline for entries 報名參賽的截止期限 / to extend the deadline 延長期限 / He missed the deadline. 他錯過了截止日期。

<span class="vocabulary">**time**</span> [taɪm] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 生命的期限，或做某事所用的時間總量（即期限）：</span>Now that Martin has begun to suffer the effects of AIDS, he says his time is running out. 馬丁已開始遭受艾滋病的折磨，他說自己時日不多了。
