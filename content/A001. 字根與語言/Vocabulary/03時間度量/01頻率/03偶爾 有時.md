☀ <span class="category">**偶爾 有時：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**sometimes**</span> ['sʌmtaɪmz] 
<span class="definition">adv. 有時，間或：</span>Sometimes I go by car. 有時我坐車去。/ He sometimes writes to me. 他偶爾給我寫封信。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**occasionally**</span> [occasionally]
<span class="definition">adv. 偶爾、有時（而非經常）：</span>We occasionally meet for a drink after work. 我們下班後偶爾相聚小酌。/ Jane used to visit him for the occasional days and weekends. 簡過去會偶爾在週末或別的日子來探望他。/ This type of allergy can very occasionally be fatal. 這類過敏症在極個別情況下有可能是致命的。/ I've had occasional mild headaches all my life. 間或發作的輕微頭疼困擾了我一輩子。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**sporadic**</span> [spəˈrædɪk]
<span class="definition">adj. 偶爾發生的、間或發生的、陣發性的、斷斷續續且不規律的：</span>sporadic fighting, sporadic gunfire, sporadic violence 零星的戰鬥；一陣一陣的炮火；零星的暴力事件 / The sound of sporadic shooting could still be heard. 仍能聽見零星的槍聲。/ sporadic outbreaks of the disease 那種疾病的零星爆發 / I make sporadic efforts to sort out my files. 我偶爾會整理一下檔案。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**odd**</span> [ɒd; 美 ɑ:d]
<span class="definition">adj. the odd [只用於名詞前]（無比較級和最高階）偶然發生或出現的；不是很規律或不是很頻繁的：</span>He makes the odd mistake─nothing too serious. 他偶爾會犯錯誤，但不怎麼嚴重。

<span class="vocabulary">**can**</span> [kæn, kən] 
<span class="definition">modal verb. 用於表示有時屬實，或在某些場合下屬實：</span>He can be very tactless sometimes. 他有時太莽撞。/ It can be quite cold here in winter. 這裡的冬天有時還真夠冷的。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**occasion**</span> [əˈkeɪʒn]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] on ~ 偶爾、有時：</span>He has been known on occasion to lose his temper. 大家都知道他有時會發脾氣。/ He treated them seriously and, on occasion, entertained them hilariously. 他對待他們嚴肅認真，偶爾又逗得他們捧腹不已。/ He translated not only from the French but also, on occasion, from the Polish. 他不僅做法文翻譯，有時也做些波蘭語翻譯。/ He has even been known to go shopping himself on occasion. 人們甚至知道他有時自己出去買東西。/ They came to visit us on the odd occasion, but only when they had nothing better to do. 他們偶爾會來訪，但那只是在他們無事可做的時候。
