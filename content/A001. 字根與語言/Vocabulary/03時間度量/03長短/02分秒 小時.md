☀ <span class="category">**分秒 小時：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**second**</span> ['sekənd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（符號）（簡寫為sec）秒（時間單位）：</span>The light flashes every 5 seconds. 燈光每5秒鐘閃一次。/ The seconds ticked by. 時間分分秒秒地流逝。

<span class="vocabulary">**minute**</span> ['mɪnɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（縮寫為min.）分鐘：</span>It’s four minutes to six. 差四分鐘六點。/ Boil the rice for 20 minutes. 將米煮20分鐘。

<span class="vocabulary">**quarter**</span> ['kwɔ:tə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 整點之前或之後的15分鐘，即一刻鐘：</span>It was a quarter to six. 當時是5:45。

<span class="vocabulary">**hour**</span> ['aʊə] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（縮寫為hr或hr.）小時：</span>It will take about an hour to get there. 到那裡大約需要一小時。/ I’m paid by the hour. 我是論小時獲得報酬的。<span class="definition">2 [C, usually sing.] 大約一小時的時間（用於某一目的）：</span>peak, rush hour 高峰時段 / lunch hour 午餐時間 / happy hour 歡樂時光（酒吧的酒水減價時間）/ an hour of rest 休息時間
