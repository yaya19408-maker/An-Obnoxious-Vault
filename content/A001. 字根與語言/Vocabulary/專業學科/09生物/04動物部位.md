☀ <span class="category">**動物部位：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**part**</span> [pɑːt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（人或動植物的）身體部位：</span>the parts of the body 身體各部位

<span class="vocabulary">**flesh**</span> [fleʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 動物的肉：</span>flesh-eating animals 食肉動物 / rotting flesh 腐肉
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**knuckle**</span> [ˈnʌkl]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（也可用hock[尤美]）動物（尤指豬）的腿部下面的肉（如肘子肉、蹄子等）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**hamstring**</span> [ˈhæmstrɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 馬或其他動物後腿跗關節後部的肌腱：</span>瞭解即可           

<span class="vocabulary">**stalk**</span> [stɔ:k]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指一些動物器官上的）長細肉柄或肉莖（起支撐和連線作用）：</span>Crabs have eyes on stalks. 螃蟹的眼睛長在肉柄上。           

<span class="vocabulary">**gut**</span> [gʌt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（人或動物的）腸道：</span>healthy gut 健康的腸道 / gut flora（術語）, gut wall 腸內菌群；腸壁 <span class="definition">2 [pl.] guts 人或動物的內臟，尤指動物內臟：</span>I'll only cook fish if the guts have been removed. 魚若已收拾乾淨，我只需燒一下即可。/ By the time they finish, the crewmen are standing ankle-deep in fish guts. 到他們完工時，船員站的地方魚內臟已堆至腳踝。/ I don't like movies that are full of blood and guts.（比喻用法）我不喜歡充滿血腥鏡頭的電影。/ a pain in your gut（美）, a pain in your guts（英）肚子疼 / I had a terrible pain in my guts after eating too many plums. 我李子吃得太多了，肚子疼得厲害。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**muzzle**</span> [ˈmʌzl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 動物（尤指狗、馬）的口鼻：</span>The mongrel presented his muzzle for scratching. 那隻雜種狗張嘴要傷人。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**beak**</span> [bi:k]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 鳥嘴、鳥喙：</span>The gull held the fish in its beak. 海鷗嘴裡叼著魚。/ a black bird with a yellow beak 一隻黃喙黑鳥

<span class="vocabulary">**tongue**</span> [tʌŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 人或動物的舌頭：</span>long tongue 長舌頭 / forked tongue 叉狀舌 / The snake’s tongue flicked out of its mouth. 那條蛇吐出芯子。

<span class="vocabulary">**claw**</span> [klɔ:] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 禽類等動物的爪子：</span>The chicken tried to cling to the edge of the roof by its claws. 那隻雞試圖用爪子抓緊房頂的邊緣。/ The cat held a bird in its claws. 貓爪子下按著一隻鳥。<span class="definition">2 [C] 龍蝦、螃蟹、蠍子等有殼動物的螯、鉗：</span>the claws of a crab 螃蟹的螯 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi. claw (at) sb/sth 用爪子或指甲抓、撕、撓等：</span>to claw at a tree 用爪子抓撓樹木

<span class="vocabulary">**web**</span> [web] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一些禽鳥等動物（如鴨子、青蛙）的蹼：</span>瞭解即可
          
<span class="vocabulary">**pad**</span> [pæd]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 動物（如貓、狗等）腳底的肉墊：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**digit**</span> [ˈdɪdʒɪt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [解剖學] 動物的腳趾：</span>Many animals have five digits. 許多動物有五趾。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**horn**</span> [hɔ:n; 美 hɔ:rn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（羊、牛、鹿等動物的）角：</span>buffalo, bull's, cow's, rhino horn 水牛角；公牛角；奶牛角；犀牛角等 / ornaments made of rhino horn 犀牛角製成的裝飾品 / curled, curved horn 彎曲的椅角 / a large bull with curved horns 長著彎曲椅角的大公牛 / A mature cow has horns. 成年奶牛有犄角。<span class="definition">2 [U] 構成動物角的堅硬物質：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**antenna**</span> [ænˈtenə]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（複數形式為antennae）一些昆蟲或甲殼綱動物的觸角、觸鬚：</span>Certain words make reporters' antennae quiver.（比喻用法）某些話能觸動記者的敏感神經。/ The minister was praised for his acute political antennae (= ability to understand complicated political situations).（比喻用法）這位部長以政治觸覺敏銳而為人稱道。

<span class="vocabulary">**breast**</span> [brest] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 禽鳥的胸脯：</span>the cock’s breast 公雞的胸脯 / breast feathers 胸部羽毛 <span class="definition">adj. -breasted 用於構成形容詞，指禽鳥有某類胸脯的：</span>a red-breasted cock 胸脯為紅色的公雞

<span class="vocabulary">**wing**</span> [wɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 禽鳥或昆蟲的翅膀：</span>bat, butterfly wing 蝙蝠的翅膀；蝴蝶的翅膀 / a pair of wings 一對翅膀
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vein**</span> [veɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（昆蟲翅膀上的）翅脈：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**tail**</span> [teɪl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 動物、禽類、魚等的尾巴：</span>a rat with a long tail 一隻長尾巴耗子 / The dog ran out with its tail wagging madly. 狗跑了出來，瘋狂地擺著尾巴。/ tail bone（通常用tailbone）尾骨 / tail feather, tail fin 尾羽；尾鰭

<span class="vocabulary">**brush**</span> [brʌʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 狐狸尾巴（常作為狩獵的戰利品）；某些品種的狗的尾巴：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vent**</span> [vent]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [術語] 鳥、魚等小動物的肛門：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**coat**</span> [kəʊt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 動物的皮毛：</span>a dog with a long shaggy coat 皮毛又長又蓬亂的狗 / The dog sheds its winter coat once the weather becomes warmer. 這條狗天一變暖就會蛻掉過冬的毛。

<span class="vocabulary">**fur**</span> [fə:] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 哺乳動物身上的毛：</span>cat’s fur 貓毛

<span class="vocabulary">**hair**</span> [heə] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 動物的毛：</span>cat, dog hair 貓毛；狗毛
           
<span class="vocabulary">**mane**</span> [meɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 馬或獅子的脖子上的長毛：</span>a horse's mane, a lion's mane 馬鬃；獅鬃 / The horse tossed its flowing mane behind it. 馬兒把飄垂的鬃毛甩到頸後。

<span class="vocabulary">**down**</span> [daʊn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 禽鳥的非常精細柔軟的羽毛，即絨毛：</span>duck down 鴨絨

<span class="vocabulary">**feather**</span> ['feðə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]禽類的一根羽毛或翎毛：</span>wing feather 翅膀上的羽毛 / peacock feather 孔雀毛 / downy, fluffy feather 絨羽 / feather pillow 羽絨枕頭 / feather duster 羽毛撣子
           
<span class="vocabulary">**crest**</span> [krest]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 禽鳥頭頂的一撮向上的羽毛，如鳥冠、羽冠：</span>Both birds had a dark blue crest. 兩種鳥都長著深藍色的羽冠。

<span class="vocabulary">**bald**</span> [bɔ:ld]
<span class="definition">adj.（通常有毛的動物）沒毛的：</span>Our dog has a bald patch on its leg. 我們的狗腿上脫了一片毛。

<span class="vocabulary">**beard**</span> [bɪəd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 動物鬍鬚：</span>a goat’s beard 山羊的鬍子
     
<span class="vocabulary">**spine**</span> [spaɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（動物的）像針一樣的刺毛：</span>Hedgehogs are covered with sharp spines. 刺蝟身上長滿利刺。/ Porcupines use their spines to protect themselves. 豪豬用身上的刺毛來自衛。/ fine spine 細刺 / poisonous, venomous spine 毒刺
           
<span class="vocabulary">**sting**</span> [stɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [美也用stinger] （昆蟲的）螫針，刺：</span>the sting of a bee 蜜蜂的螫針 / Remove the bee sting with tweezers. 用鑷子拔掉蜜蜂的螫刺。/ The scorpion has a sting in its tail. 蠍子尾巴上有螫針。

<span class="vocabulary">**skin**</span> [skɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 常構成複合詞，指從死獸身上剝下的皮或毛皮（可用於製成衣服等）：</span>tiger skin 虎皮 / animal skin 動物毛皮

<span class="vocabulary">**hide**</span> [haɪd] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] [英] 人們可以觀看野生動物或鳥類的一個地方（但被觀看的物件卻不能發現此地的人），即（觀看野生動物時的）隱蔽處或藏身處：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">2 [C, U] 動物的皮，尤指為人們所買賣或用作皮革的動物皮：</span>Elephants have a very tough hide. 象皮非常堅韌。/ leathery, scaly hide 皮革；鱗皮 / animal hide 獸皮 / cow hide 牛皮 / boots made from buffalo hide 用水牛皮做的靴子

<span class="vocabulary">**plate**</span> [pleɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 動物表面對其起保護作用的一塊鱗甲、盾甲等：</span>the armadillo’s protective shell of bony plates 犰狳的一層防護性鱗甲
           
<span class="vocabulary">**scale**</span> [skeɪl]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（魚或爬行動物的）鱗、鱗片：</span>fish scale 魚鱗 / overlapping scale 交疊的鱗片 / armoured/armored scale 甲鱗

<span class="vocabulary">**mail**</span> [meɪl] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 一些動物（如海龜、龍蝦等）的硬質保護殼：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**shell**</span> [ʃel]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（海貝、龜、蝸牛、蟹等動物的）殼：</span>sea shell（通常用seashell）海貝殼 / conch, cowrie, mussel, oyster, snail shell 海螺殼；寶貝殼；貽貝殼；牡蠣殼；蝸牛殼 / tortoise shell（通常用tortoiseshell）龜殼 / creatures that have shells 有殼類動物 / The snail went back into its shell. 蝸牛縮回殼裡。/ We collected shells on the beach. 我們在海灘拾貝殼。/ Remove the mussels from their shells. 把貽貝從殼中取出。/ Male crabs shed their shells twice a year. 公螃蟹每年蛻殼兩次。
       
<span class="vocabulary">**blaze**</span> [bleɪz]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.]（動物面部的）白斑：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**shed**</span> [ʃed]
<span class="definition">vt. 動物蛻皮、脫毛：</span>a snake who has shed its skin 已經蛻皮的蛇

<span class="vocabulary">**cast**</span> [kɑːst] 
<span class="definition">vt. 蛇蛻皮，相當於shed：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**wool**</span> [wʊl] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 羊等動物的毛：</span>pure wool 純羊毛 / raw wool 原毛 / lamb’s, sheep’s wool 羔羊毛；綿羊毛 / wool merchant 羊毛商
            
<span class="vocabulary">**fleece**</span> [fli:s]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 羊毛；一隻羊一次剪下的毛：</span>瞭解即可          

<span class="vocabulary">**hump**</span> [hʌmp]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 某些動物背部的大隆起物，尤指駱駝的駝峰：</span>Camels rebuild fat stores in their hump. 駱駝在自己的駝峰內重新貯備了脂肪。
                       
<span class="vocabulary">**flank**</span> [flæŋk]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（動物的）肋腹：</span>He put his hand on the dog's flank. 他把手放在狗的肋腹上。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**camouflage**</span> [ˈkæməflɑ:ʒ]
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.] 動物的保護色或保護形狀：</span>Confident in its camouflage, being the same colour as the rocks, the lizard stands still when it feels danger. 由於和岩石顏色一樣，蜥蜴對自己的保護色充滿信心；遇到危險時，它便一動不動。/ The animal's markings provide effective camouflage. 這種動物身上的斑紋是很有效的偽裝。

相關詞義延伸：
· flesh還可指人類的皮膚或肉體。

周邊詞彙補充：
· crop可指禽類的嗉囊；
· bush可指看起來像灌木一樣的東西，尤指一片濃密的毛髮或皮毛；
· limb可指動物的翅膀。
