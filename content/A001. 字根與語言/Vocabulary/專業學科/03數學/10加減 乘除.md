☀ <span class="category">**加減 乘除：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**add**</span> [æd] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. add A to B / add A and B (together) 把若干數字或數量加到一起以得到一個總數，即加，反義為subtract：</span>Add 9 to the total. 總數再加上9。/ Banks add all the interest and other charges together. 銀行把所有利息和其他費用加在一起。

<span class="vocabulary">**addition**</span> [ə'dɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 加；加法。反義詞為subtraction：</span>children learning addition and subtraction 學習加減法的兒童 / She can do addition. 她會做加法。

<span class="vocabulary">**and**</span> [ænd] 
<span class="definition">conj. 加，加上，相當於plus：</span>5 and 5 makes 10. 5加5等於10。/ What’s 47 and 16? 47加16得多少？ 

<span class="vocabulary">**plus**</span> [plʌs] 
<span class="definition">prep.（兩個數量或數額）相加，加：</span>Two plus five is seven. 二加五等於七。/ The cost is £22, plus £1 for postage. 費用為22英鎊，另加1英鎊的郵費。

<span class="vocabulary">**positive**</span> ['pɒzɪtɪv] 
<span class="definition">adj.（數量或數目）正數的（即比0大的）：</span>negative and positive numbers 正負數

<span class="vocabulary">**carry**</span> ['kærɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 加法中可指（向左）進位：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**minus**</span> ['maɪnəs] 
<span class="definition">prep. 將一個數字減去，減：</span>Seven minus three is four (7–3 = 4). 七減去三等於四。/ the former Soviet Union, minus the Baltic republics and Georgia 前蘇聯，不包括波羅的海諸共和國及喬治亞
           
<span class="vocabulary">**negative**</span> [ˈnegətɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. 數量小於零的，負數的：</span>a negative trade balance 貿易逆差 / The weakest students can end up with a negative score. 最差的學生可能會以負分收場。

<span class="vocabulary">**take**</span> [teɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. take A (away) from B / take A away 數學中可指減去，相當於subtract：</span>Take 5 from 12 and you’re left with 7. 12減5，剩7。/ 80 take away 5 is 75.（非正式用法）80減去5等於75。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**deduct**</span> [dɪˈdʌkt]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] ~ sth (from sth)（從總量中）扣除，減去：</span>The cost of your uniform will be deducted from your wages. 制服費將從你的工資中扣除。/ This amount will be automatically deducted from your salary. 這一金額將從你的工資中自動扣除。/ The company deducted this payment from his compensation. 公司從他獲得的賠償金中扣去了這筆付款。/ Tax is deducted at source.（at source為英式用法）從一開始就已扣除了稅款。/ Ten points will be deducted for a wrong answer. 答錯一題扣十分。/ Up to 5% of marks in the exams will be deducted for spelling mistakes. 試卷中最多將會有 5% 的分數因為拼寫錯誤而被扣掉。

<span class="vocabulary">**deduction**</span> [dɪˈdʌkʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（從總數中）減去或扣除某個數額，尤指扣除某個金錢的數額：</span>After the deduction of tax at 20 per cent, the interest rate will be 6.2 per cent. 扣除了 20% 的稅後，利率將為 6.2%。/ You should claim the deduction when you file your tax return. 你填寫納稅申報單時應當要求扣除。

<span class="vocabulary">**minus**</span> ['maɪnəs] 
<span class="definition">adj. 數學中可指小於零的，即負的：</span>minus figure, minus number 負數 / minus 65 degrees 零下65 度

<span class="vocabulary">**multiply**</span> ['mʌltɪplaɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. multiply (A by B) / multiply A and B (together) 乘以，乘：</span>The children are already learning to multiply and divide. 孩子們已經開始學習乘法和除法了。/ 2 multiplied by 4 is/equals 8 (2×4 = 8). 2乘以4等於8。/ What do you get if you multiply six by nine? 六乘以九得多少？/ Multiply 2 and 6 together and you get 12. 2和6相乘得12。

<span class="vocabulary">**time**</span> [taɪm] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] 用於數字之後表示倍數：</span>Its profits are rising four times faster than the average company. 其利潤正以快於一般公司四倍的速度增長。<span class="definition">conj. 乘，乘以：</span>Four times six is 24. 4乘以6等於24。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**factor**</span> [ˈfæktə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] a factor of 增減（一般是增加）的倍數：</span>The real wage of the average worker has increased by a factor of over ten in the last 70 years. 近70年來工人的實際工資平均增長超過了十倍。/ The cost of butter quadrupled and bread prices increased by a factor of five. 黃油價格漲了四倍，麵包價格漲了五倍。

<span class="vocabulary">**raise**</span> [reɪz] 
<span class="definition">vt. 使…自乘若干次：</span>3 raised to the power of 3 is 27 (= 3×3×3). 3的三次方等於27。

<span class="vocabulary">**power**</span> ['paʊə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] 數學中的乘方或冪：</span>4 to the power of 3 is 4^3 (= 4×4×4 = 64). 4的3次方是4^3。

<span class="vocabulary">**table**</span> ['teɪbl] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 數學中可指（尤指從1到12的）乘法表，等於multiplication table：</span>Do you know your six times table? 你會背六的乘法口訣嗎？

<span class="vocabulary">**divide**</span> [dɪ'vaɪd] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 1 divide (sth) by sth 除以：</span>30 divided by 6 is 5 (30÷6 = 5). 30除以6等於5。/ Measure the floor area of the greenhouse and divide it by six. 測量溫室的地表面積，再除以6。<span class="definition">2 divide (sth) into sth（數學）除：</span>5 divides into 30 6 times. 5除30等於6。

<span class="vocabulary">**division**</span> [dɪ'vɪӡn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 除或除法：</span>the division sign (÷) 除號 / Can you do division? 你會做除法運算嗎？/ division by three 用三除

<span class="vocabulary">**go**</span> [ɡəʊ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. goes (into sth)（數字）除盡；除：</span>3 into 12 goes 4. 3除12得4。/ 7 into 15 won’t go. 7除15除不盡。/ 7 into 15 doesn’t go.（美）7除15除不盡。/ 7 won’t go into 15. 7除不盡15。/ Six goes into thirty five times. 六除三十得五。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**divisible**</span> [dɪˈvɪzəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. [數學] [名詞前不可用] ~ (by sth) 可被除的，通常指可被除盡（即沒有餘數）的：</span>8 is divisible by 2 and 4, but not by 3. 8可被2和4除盡，但不能被3除盡。/ Twenty-eight is divisible by seven. 28可以被7除盡。/ A prime number is evenly divisible only by itself and 1. 質數僅能被其自身和1整除。/ He argued that all matter was infinitely divisible. 他認為所有物質都是無限可分的。

<span class="vocabulary">**into**</span> ['ɪntu,'ɪntə] 
<span class="definition">prep. 用於除數，即除：</span>3 into 24 is 8. 24除以3等於8。

<span class="vocabulary">**by**</span> [baɪ] 
<span class="definition">prep. 用於乘法或除法運算：</span>12 multiplied by 5 equals 60. 12乘以5等於60。/ 12 divided by 4 equals 3. 12除以4等於3。

相關詞義延伸：
· deduction還可指被扣除的數額。

· embroider可指對（故事、敘述等）添枝加葉渲染（新增的細節資訊並非總是真實的）。
