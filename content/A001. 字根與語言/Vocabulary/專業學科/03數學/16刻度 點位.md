☀ <span class="category">**刻度 點位：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**mark**</span> [mɑːk] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [英] Mark後接數詞，指燃氣烤箱上的某個溫度水平的刻度：</span>Preheat the oven to gas Mark 6. 將烤箱預熱到6擋。

<span class="vocabulary">**graduation**</span> [ɡrædӡu'eɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 刻度，相當於gradation。是一個廣義用詞：</span>the graduations of the thermometer 溫度計的刻度
           
<span class="vocabulary">**scale**</span> [skeɪl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 量具上的刻度：</span>How much does it read on the scale? 刻度顯示的是多少？

<span class="vocabulary">**point**</span> [pɒɪnt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 計量刻度的一個單位點（或單位標記）：</span>basis, percentage point 基點；百分點 / The Nikkei index rose 830 points to 18905. 日經指數上升了830點，升至18905點。
