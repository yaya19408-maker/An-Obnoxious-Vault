☀ <span class="category">**等於 得出：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**equal**</span> ['i:kwəl] 
<span class="definition">linking verb. 指在尺寸、數量、價值等方面與…相等，即等於。是此義的常規用詞：</span>1 plus 1 equals 2. 1加1等於2。/ A metre equals 39.38 inches. 1米等於39.38英寸。<span class="definition">adj. equal (to sb/sth) 尺寸、數量、價值、實力、重要性、結果等方面相等的：</span>to be equal in weight 重量相等 / The two books are more or less equal in length. 這兩本書篇幅差不多。/ equal numbers 數量相等 / One pound is roughly equal to two dollars. 一英鎊大約相當於兩美元。

<span class="vocabulary">**be**</span> [bi:] 
<span class="definition">linking verb. 等於。是一個簡單用詞，口語中較為常用：</span>Three and three is six. 三加三等於六。/ Let x be the sum of a and b. 設x為a加b之和。

<span class="vocabulary">**get**</span> [ɡet] 
<span class="definition">vt. 從自己的行動中透過計算或實驗而得出某個結果：</span>What do you get if you multiply six by nine? 六乘九得幾？

<span class="vocabulary">**leave**</span> [li:v] 
<span class="definition">vt. 數學中可指剩餘或餘下（某個數量）。常用於減法中：</span>Seven from ten leaves three. 10減7得3。

<span class="vocabulary">**make**</span> [meɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 合計；等於（表“合計”時一般用於加法）：</span>5 and 7 make 12. 5加7等於12。/ Four twos make eight. 四個二是八。/ A hundred cents make one euro. 一百分幣等於一歐元。

<span class="vocabulary">**at**</span> [æt] 
<span class="definition">prep. 以（或達到）某個速度、比率、頻率、水平、價格、數量等：</span>He was driving at 70 mph. 他以每小時70英里的速度駕車行駛。/ The noise came at two-minute intervals (= once every two minutes). 每兩分鐘傳來一次噪音。

<span class="vocabulary">**of**</span> [əv] 
<span class="definition">prep. 與表示計量、時間、年齡、價值等的詞連用（以表示計量、時間、年齡、價值等）：</span>2 kilos of potatoes 2公斤馬鈴薯 / an increase of 2% 2%的增長 / a girl of 12 12歲的女孩 / the fourth of July 七月四日

<span class="vocabulary">**between**</span> [bɪ'twi:n] 
<span class="definition">prep. 介於兩個數量、重量、距離等之間：</span>It weighed between nine and ten kilos. 重量在九到十公斤之間。/ The temperature remained between 25˚C and 30˚C all week. 整個星期氣溫都保持在25到30攝氏度之間。

<span class="vocabulary">**stand**</span> [stænd] 
<span class="definition">vt. 處於某一高度：</span>The tower stands 30 metres high. 塔高30米。<span class="definition">vi. stand at sth 處於某一水平、數量、高度等：</span>Interest rates stand at 3%. 利率為3%。/ The world record then stood at 6.59 metres. 當時的世界紀錄是6.59米。

<span class="vocabulary">**run**</span> [rʌn] 
<span class="definition">vi. run as sth 處於或接近某個水平：</span>Inflation was running at 26%. 通貨膨脹達到了26%。

<span class="vocabulary">**at**</span> [æt] 
<span class="definition">prep. 用於說明兩者之間的角度：</span>瞭解即可
