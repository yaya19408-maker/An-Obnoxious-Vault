☀ <span class="category">**數數 計算：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**count**</span> [kaʊnt] 
<span class="definition">vi. count (from sb) (to/up to sth)（按正確順序）數數：</span>Billy can’t count yet. 比利還不會數數。/ to count from 1 to 10 從1數到10 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi. count (sth) (up) 計算（或清點）一個群體中的人、事物等的總數：</span>The diet is based on counting calories. 這個飲食安排以熱量統計為根據。/ She began to count up how many guests they had to invite. 她開始計算他們得邀請多少位客人。/ There are 12 weeks to go, counting from today. 從今天算起還有12個星期。<span class="definition">vt. 計算總數的時候把…包括在內，即算在內：</span>We have invited 50 people, not counting the children. 不算小孩，我們邀請了50人。<span class="definition">n. 1 [usually sing.] 數出總數：</span>We did a quick count of the children and there were none missing. 我們很快地數了數孩子，一個都不少。<span class="definition">2 [usually sing.]（從1開始按順序所做的）數數或點數：</span>He was knocked to the ground and stayed down for a count of eight.（拳擊用語）他被擊倒在地，躺在地上直至數到八。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**recount**</span> [ˌri:ˈkaʊnt]
<span class="definition">vt. 重新清點（尤指重新清點選票）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**recount**</span> [ˈri:kaʊnt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 重新清點（尤指對選票的重新清點）：</span>The defeated candidate demanded a recount. 落選的候選人要求重新計票。/ She wanted a recount. She couldn't believe that I had got more votes than her. 她想要重新計票。她不相信我比她得票多。

<span class="vocabulary">**calculate**</span> ['kælkjuleɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt.（利用數字來）計算或核算出一個總數、數量、距離等，相當於work out。是此義的常規用詞：</span>to calculate the volume of the container 計算容器的容積 / Benefit is calculated on the basis of average weekly earnings. 補助金按平均周收入計算。/ It’s difficult to calculate precisely what we’ve spent. 要精確計算出我們的花費很難。/ mentally calculate 心算
           
<span class="vocabulary">**calculation**</span> [ˌkælkjuˈleɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 數學計算：</span>Cathy did a rough calculation. 凱茜作了一個粗略的計算。/ By my calculation(s), we made a profit of £20 000 last year. 我算了一下，我們去年贏利2萬英鎊。/ Our guess was confirmed by calculation. 我們的猜測透過計算得到證實。/ the calculation of their assets 對他們資產的估算 / an exact calculation of the amount spent so far 對目前花費總額的確切計算 / Your calculations are correct. 你的計算是正確的。/ It turned out that our calculations were incorrect. 結果證明我們計算錯了。/ Even a rough calculation shows that you have spent too much. 即使是粗略計算，你花銷也太大。/ quick calculation 快速計算 / mental calculation 心算 / arithmetical（尤英）, mathematical, numerical, statistical calculation 算術運算；數學運算；數字運算；統計計算 / economic, financial calculation 經濟核算；財務核算 / She did a rapid calculation in her head. 她在腦中快速計算了—番。/ Leonard made a rapid calculation: he'd never make it in time. 倫納德快速盤算了一下：他肯定趕不上了。/ We cannot make a precise calculation of the price until we have all the costs. 除非我們知道所有成本，否則不能準確地計算出價格。/ The calculations are based on aver-age annual data.這些計算結果是根據年度平均資料得來的。/ a method of calculation 計算方法

<span class="definition">n. [U, C] [術語] 計算：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**figure**</span> ['fɪɡə] 
<span class="definition">vt. [美] 計算（數量或成本等）。詞義上相當於calculate：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**assess**</span> [ə'ses] 
<span class="definition">vt. assess sth (at sth) 核算（或核定、估算）某事物的數量或價值，相當於estimate：</span>They have assessed the amount of compensation to be paid. 他們已經核定了賠償額。/ Damage to the building was assessed at £40 000. 該建築物的損失估定為4萬英鎊。/ to assess the building’s value 估算出該建築的價值 / The legal costs have been assessed at $75 000. 訴訟費估計為75000美元。

<span class="vocabulary">**make**</span> [meɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 估計；計算。是一個簡單用詞，常用於口語中：</span>What time do you make it? 你估計現在幾點了？/ I make that exactly $50. 我算出正好是50美元。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**tally**</span> [ˈtæli]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (up) 計算某事物的總數、總成本等：</span>as we tally the number of workers who have been laid off this year 我們統計今年下崗工人數量的時候 / When the final numbers are tallied, sales will almost certainly have fallen. 等到最終的數字統計出來後，幾乎可以肯定銷售額下降了。

<span class="vocabulary">**maths**</span> [mæθs]（英式，非正式；美式非正式為math）
<span class="definition">n. [U] 計算，運算。不太常用：</span>Your maths is not correct? 你算錯了。/ simple maths 簡單的數學運算 / mental maths（尤美）心算

<span class="vocabulary">**operation**</span> [͵ɒpə'reɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 數學運算。相對正式：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**arithmetic**</span> [ə'rɪθmətɪk,͵ærɪθ'metɪk] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 計算，算術運算。也不太常用：</span>mental arithmetic 心算 / My arithmetic is right. 我的計算是對的。/ to do simple arithmetic 做簡單的算術
           
<span class="vocabulary">**sum**</span> [sʌm]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 簡單的數字計算：</span>to do a sum in your head 做心算 / I was good at sums at school. 我上學時擅長算術。/ If I've got my sums right, I should be able to afford the rent. 要是我算對了的話，我應該負擔得起這筆租金。/ I can't do my sums. 我算不出來。/ I did a quick sum to work out how much it would cost. 我很快算出了需要花費的錢數。/ to get your sums right/wrong（英）算對了；算錯了 / The company got its sums wrong when estimating how many customers it would attract. 這家公司把他們能夠吸引的顧客人數估算錯了。

相關詞義延伸：
· operation還可指機器（尤指計算機）所執行的任務，即運算。

周邊詞彙補充：
· proof可為數學用語，指（對某個說法的）證明、求證；（對自己計算結果的）驗算；
· cost可指估算…的成本或價格。
