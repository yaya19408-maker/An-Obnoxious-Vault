<span class="vocabulary">**value**</span> ['vælju:] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 數學中的值，數值：</span>Let y have the value 33. 假設y的值為33。

<span class="vocabulary">**product**</span> ['prɒdʌkt, -əkt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 乘積：</span>The product of 21 and 16 is 336. 21和16的乘積是336。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**remainder**</span> [rɪˈmeɪndə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] [數學]（減法的）差數；（除法的）餘數：</span>Divide 2 into 7, and the answer is 3, remainder 1. 7除以2，商3餘1。
