☀ <span class="category">**放調味料：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**season**</span> ['si:zn] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. season sth (with sth) 給食物加調料（以調節味道）：</span>Season the lamb with garlic. 給羊肉加蒜調味。/ highly seasoned food 佐料多、味道濃的食物
           
<span class="vocabulary">**seasoned**</span> [ˈsi:znd]
<span class="definition">adj.（食物）調好了味的，加了佐料的：</span>The sausage was very highly seasoned. 這香腸調味很濃。             

<span class="vocabulary">**flavour**</span> [ˈfleɪvə(r)]
<span class="definition">vt. sth (with sth) 給食物或飲料調味：</span>Flavour your favourite dishes with exotic herbs and spices. 把異國風味的芳草和香料加到你最喜愛的菜餚裡調味。/ Lime preserved in salt is a north African speciality which is used to flavour chicken dishes. 鹽漬的酸橙是北非的一種特產，在烹製雞肉時用來調味。         

<span class="vocabulary">**spice**</span> [spaɪs]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (up) (with sth) 在食物中加香料：</span>Their bread is spiced with cinnamon. 他們的麵包用了肉桂調味。/ I don't really like highly spiced food. 我不太喜歡味道很重的食物。/ a dish of lightly spiced rice 一盤淡味米飯

<span class="vocabulary">**pepper**</span> ['pepə] 
<span class="definition">vt.（在食物上）撒胡椒粉：</span>peppered steak 撒了胡椒粉的牛排 / Salt and pepper the potatoes. 給土豆放上鹽和胡椒粉。

<span class="vocabulary">**salt**</span> [sɔ:lt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 在食物中放鹽：</span>salted peanuts 鹹花生米

<span class="vocabulary">**smooth**</span> [smu:ð] 
<span class="definition">vt. smooth sth on/into/over sth 將一層柔軟物質均勻置於食物表面：</span>Smooth the icing over the top of the cake. 在蛋糕頂上均勻地鋪一層糖霜。

<span class="vocabulary">**ice**</span> [aɪs] 
<span class="definition">vt. 將糕餅裹上糖霜：</span>to ice the cake 給蛋糕裹上糖霜

<span class="vocabulary">**frost**</span> [frɒst] 
<span class="definition">vt. [尤美] 給蛋糕覆上糖霜：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**sugar**</span> ['ʃʊɡə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 給…加糖；給…覆蓋上糖：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**butter**</span> ['bʌtə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 在…上塗黃油：</span>lightly buttered toast 抹了少量黃油的烤麵包片

<span class="vocabulary">**pipe**</span> [paɪp] 
<span class="definition">vt.（透過將糖霜等從一個特別的袋子或管狀容器裡擠出成細線而）裝飾食物（尤指裝飾蛋糕）：</span>The cake had ‘Happy Birthday’ piped on it. 蛋糕上裱了“生日快樂”的字樣。

<span class="vocabulary">**spread**</span> [spred] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. spread (A on/over B) / spread (B with A) 把一種物質塗抹到食物的表面；或能夠被塗抹到食物表面：</span>to spread butter on a piece of toast 在烤麵包片上抹黃油

<span class="vocabulary">**coat**</span> [kəʊt] 
<span class="definition">vt. coat sth (with/in sth) 給食物塗上（或覆蓋上）一層…：</span>to coat the meat with salt 給肉撒上鹽 / Coat the fish in the sauce. 把魚裹上一層調味醬。

<span class="vocabulary">**glaze**</span> [gleɪz]
<span class="definition">vt. [美] ~ sth (with sth) 澆漿汁於（食物的表面）：</span>a glazed doughnut 掛糖漿的炸面圈 / Glaze the pie with beaten egg. 將攪拌過的蛋漿澆在餡餅上。<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（澆在蛋糕、麵包等食物上增加光澤的）稀蛋漿、奶漿、糖漿：</span>Brush the glaze over the top and sides of the hot cake. 在熱蛋糕的頂上和周圍刷上一層蛋漿。
