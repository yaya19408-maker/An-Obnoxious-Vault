☀ <span class="category">**肉類 禽蛋 豆腐：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**meat**</span> [mi:t] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 被作為食物的（動物或禽鳥的）肉；某類供食用的肉：</span>meat-eating animals 食肉動物 / I’m not a great meat eater. 我吃肉不多。/ salted, smoked meat 醃肉；煙燻肉 / frozen meat 凍肉 / meat production 肉類生產 / meat consumption 肉類消費

<span class="vocabulary">**cut**</span> [kʌt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 從動物軀體上割下的一塊肉：</span>a cheap cut 一塊便宜的肉 / a good cut of beef 一塊上好的牛肉
           
<span class="vocabulary">**joint**</span> [dʒɔɪnt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [英]（適於燒烤的）大塊肉；一塊烤肉：</span>bacon joint 燻肉塊 / a joint of beef 一大塊（帶骨）牛肉 / a joint of lamb 一大塊（帶骨）羔羊肉 / a joint of pork 一大塊（帶骨）豬肉 / the Sunday joint (= one traditionally eaten on a Sunday) 禮拜天吃的大塊烤肉
 
<span class="vocabulary">**side**</span> [saɪd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] a side of beef/bacon, etc. 一扇（即一半）牛肉、豬肉等：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**pork**</span> [pɔ:k] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 豬肉（常為新鮮的，未用鹽醃過或未用煙燻過的豬肉）：</span>a leg of pork 豬腿肉 / roast pork 烤豬肉 / pork sausage 豬肉香腸 / pork butcher（尤英）豬肉販

<span class="vocabulary">**beef**</span> [bi:f] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 牛肉：</span>fatty beef 肥牛肉 / tough beef 嚼不動的牛肉 / medium beef 中等熟度的牛肉 / sliced beef 牛肉片

<span class="vocabulary">**mutton**</span> ['mʌtn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 供食用的成年綿羊的肉，即羊肉：</span>a leg of mutton 羊腿肉 

<span class="vocabulary">**lamb**</span> [læm] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 羔羊肉：</span>the leg of lamb 羔羊腿肉 / grilled lamb 烤羔羊肉

<span class="vocabulary">**fish**</span> [fɪʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 作為食物的魚，魚肉：</span>fresh fish 鮮魚 / frozen, salted fish 凍魚；鹹魚 / fried, grilled fish 炸魚；烤魚 / fish soup 魚湯
            
<span class="vocabulary">**fillet**</span> [ˈfɪlɪt; 美 fɪˈleɪ]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（美式英語也可用filet）無骨的肉（尤指牛肉）或魚：</span>fish fillet 魚片 / cod, salmon fillet 鱈魚片；鮭魚片 / beef, pork fillet 牛肉片；豬肉片 / fillet steak（尤英）無骨牛排 / fillet of beef with shallots 蔥香無骨牛肉片 / chicken breast fillets 雞脯肉片 / plaice fillets 鰈魚片 / anchovy fillets 去骨鯷魚片 / I ordered a fine fillet of salmon. 我點了一份精品去骨鮭魚片。/ a fillet of cod 一片鱈魚 / fillet knife 片魚刀          

<span class="vocabulary">**poultry**</span> [ˈpəʊltri; 美 ˈpoʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 可實用的禽肉：</span>Eat plenty of fish and poultry. 要多吃魚和禽肉。/ poultry dish 用家禽做的菜餚 / a wine that goes well with fish and poultry dishes 一種適宜在吃魚和家禽時飲用的葡萄酒

<span class="vocabulary">**chicken**</span> ['tʃɪkɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 雞肉：</span>fried chicken 炸雞 / roast chicken 烤雞肉（或燒雞）/ chicken soup 雞湯
           
<span class="vocabulary">**broiler**</span> [ˈbrɔɪlə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（也可用broiler chicken）[尤美]（適於烤焙的）嫩雞：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**quail**</span> [kweɪl]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 鵪鶉肉：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**turkey**</span> ['tə:kɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 火雞肉：</span>a turkey dinner 火雞宴 / roast turkey 烤火雞 / turkey sandwich 火雞三明治

<span class="vocabulary">**duck**</span> [dʌk] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 鴨肉：</span>roasted duck 烤鴨 / Slice the duck breast and serve. 把鴨胸脯切成薄片後端上來。

<span class="vocabulary">**goose**</span> [ɡu:s] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 鵝肉：</span>roast goose 烤鵝

<span class="vocabulary">**rabbit**</span> ['ræbɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 兔肉：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**brain**</span> [breɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] 供食用的動物大腦：</span>sheep’s brains 羊腦

<span class="vocabulary">**tongue**</span> [tʌŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 供烹飪或食用的動物舌頭：</span>a slice of ox tongue 一條牛舌

<span class="vocabulary">**leg**</span> [leɡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] leg (of sth) 供烹飪和食用的動物腿肉：</span>a chicken leg 一隻雞腿 / roast leg of lamb 烤羊腿
           
<span class="vocabulary">**thigh**</span> [θaɪ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 可烹飪並食用的動物的大腿（如雞大腿）：</span>chicken thigh 雞大腿          

<span class="vocabulary">**liver**</span> [ˈlɪvə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 動物的可供烹飪和食用的肝：</span>chicken, goose, pork liver 雞肝；鵝肝；豬肝 / calf's, lamb's liver 小牛肝；小羊肝 / liver pâté 肝醬 / liver and onions 洋蔥炒肝

<span class="vocabulary">**kidney**</span> [ˈkɪdni]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 動物的可供烹飪和食用的腎臟，即腰子：</span>lambs' kidneys 羊腰子 / steak and kidney pie 牛肉腰花餡餅

<span class="vocabulary">**wing**</span> [wɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 供烹飪和食用的動物翅膀：</span>to fry the chicken wings in the oil 把雞翅放到油裡炸 / spicy wing 香辣翅膀

<span class="vocabulary">**breast**</span> [brest] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 禽鳥或動物的胸脯肉：</span>a chicken breast 雞胸肉 / breast of lamb 羊羔胸脯肉

<span class="vocabulary">**shoulder**</span> ['ʃəʊldə] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] shoulder (of sth) 動物的前腿連肩肉：</span>shoulder of lamb 羊的前腿肉

<span class="vocabulary">**neck**</span> [nek] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] neck (of sth) 用以烹製和食用的動物頸部：</span>neck of duck 鴨脖
         
<span class="vocabulary">**rack**</span> [ræk]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ of lamb/pork（羊、豬等帶前肋的）頸脊肉：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**saddle**</span> [ˈsædl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 動物（如羊、兔或鹿）的脊背上的肉：</span>瞭解即可
            
<span class="vocabulary">**chop**</span> [tʃɒp; 美 tʃɑ:p]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指豬或羊的）帶骨頭的厚肉片，如排骨：</span>pork, lamb chop 豬排；小羊排 / grilled lamb chops 烤小羊排 / mutton chop（尤英）羊排 / veal chop 小牛排 / loin chop 大排 / pork loin chops 豬大排
           
<span class="vocabulary">**rib**</span> [rɪb]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（可食用的）動物排骨、肋條（肉）：</span>a rib of beef 牛排 / pork ribs in sweet sauce 帶甜醬的豬排

<span class="vocabulary">**fat**</span> [fæt] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U]（烹呼叫的）動植物脂肪：</span>dietary fat 食用油脂 / animal, vegetable fat 動物油脂；植物油脂 / unsaturated fat 不飽和脂肪 / pork fat 豬油 <span class="definition">2 [C, U]（人體所攝入的或食物中的）動植物脂肪：</span>to cut the amount of fat in your diet 減少你飲食的脂肪攝入量 / foods which are low in fat 低脂肪食物 / This ham has too much fat on it. 這塊火腿肥肉太多。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**skinny**</span> [ˈskɪni]
<span class="definition">adj. [美，非正式] 低脂肪的：</span>a skinny latte 低脂肪的熱奶沫咖啡          

<span class="vocabulary">**lean**</span> [li:n]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 瘦肉：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">adj.（肉）脂肪少的、無脂肪的：</span>It is a beautiful meat, very lean and tender. 這塊肉很好，又瘦又嫩。/ the leanest ground beef you can get 買得到的最瘦的碎牛肉

<span class="vocabulary">**steak**</span> [steɪk] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] 厚厚的一片上好牛肉，即牛排：</span>How would you like your steak done? 您的牛排要幾成熟？/ medium steak 半熟牛排 / juicy steak 多汁的牛排 / grilled steak 烤牛排 <span class="definition">2 [U, C] 厚厚的一片任何肉，即肉排，肉塊：</span>pork steak 豬排 <span class="definition">3 [U] 常構成複合詞，質量並非最佳的牛肉（常以小塊的形式出售，用於做餡或燉煮等）：</span>stewing steak 適合於燉的牛肉塊 / a steak and kidney pie 牛肉腰子餡餅 <span class="definition">4 [C] 一片大而厚的魚肉，即魚排，魚塊：</span>a cod steak 鱈魚排

<span class="vocabulary">**ham**</span> [hæm] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 用鹽醃過或用煙燻過的豬大腿肉（作為食物），即火腿；還可指此種火腿肉：</span>a slice of ham 一片火腿 / a ham sandwich 火腿三明治 / smoked ham 燻火腿

<span class="vocabulary">**sausage**</span> ['sɒsɪdӡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（用肉、肥肉、麵包等灌製的）香腸，臘腸：</span>pork sausages 豬肉香腸 / blood, garlic sausage 血腸；蒜腸 / smoked sausage 燻腸 / sausage sandwich 香腸三明治
           
<span class="vocabulary">**snag**</span> [snæg]
<span class="definition">n. [C][澳大利亞；紐西蘭；非正式] 香腸：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**skin**</span> [skɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 香腸的外皮，腸衣：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**bacon**</span> ['beɪkən] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 用鹽醃過或用煙燻過的豬肉（常切成片以供食用），即鹹豬肉，燻豬肉：</span>Fry the bacon until crisp. 把燻肉煎至酥脆。

<span class="vocabulary">**roast**</span> [rəʊst] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（英也用joint）在烤箱裡烤的一大塊肉：</span>the Sunday roast 星期日烤肉大餐

<span class="vocabulary">**grill**</span> [ɡrɪl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一盤燒烤出來的食物（尤指烤肉）：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**jerk**</span> [dʒɜ:k; 美 dʒɜ:rk]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 在烹飪前用油和香辛料醃製，然後放在木火上烤的肉：</span>jerk chicken 烤雞
 
<span class="vocabulary">**egg**</span> [eɡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（用作食物的）禽蛋，尤指雞蛋：</span>Break the eggs into a bowl and beat them lightly. 把雞蛋打在一個碗裡並且稍微攪打。/ egg white, egg yolk 蛋清；蛋黃 / egg wash 蛋漿 / egg sandwich 雞蛋三明治

<span class="vocabulary">**white**</span> [waɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 蛋清（圍繞著蛋黃的那一部分）：</span>egg white 蛋清；蛋白 / to beat white 攪打蛋清
           
<span class="vocabulary">**shell**</span> [ʃel]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（蛋等的）殼：</span>egg shell（通常用eggshell）蛋殼 / Once the eggs have hatched the shells are left behind. 一旦雞蛋孵化後，蛋殼就沒用了。

<span class="vocabulary">**bean curd**</span> ['bi:nkə:d] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 豆腐，相當於tofu：</span>瞭解即可
