☀ <span class="category">**生熟程度：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**raw**</span> [rɔ:] 
<span class="definition">adj.（食物）生的，未烹製的：</span>raw meat 生肉 / These fish are often eaten raw. 這些魚常用來生吃。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**uncooked**</span> [ˌʌnˈkʊkt]
<span class="definition">adj.（食物）未烹煮的、生的。相當於raw：</span>Eat plenty of uncooked fruit and vegetables. 要多吃新鮮水果和生蔬菜。/ Don't use pickled beetroot, but buy it uncooked and cook it yourself. 不要買醃製的甜菜根，買生的來自己煮。

<span class="vocabulary">**rare**</span> [reə] 
<span class="definition">adj.（肉）只烹調了很短一段時間因而其內部依然是生的，即半生不熟的：</span>Waiter, I specifically asked for this steak rare. 服務員，我特別強調了這塊牛排要做得嫩些。

<span class="vocabulary">**tough**</span> [tʌf] 
<span class="definition">adj.（肉）難以切開或難以咀嚼的，與tender相反：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**undercook**</span> [ˌʌndəˈkʊk; 美 -dərˈk-]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式]（食物）未煮透：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**tender**</span> [ˈtendə(r)]
<span class="definition">adj.（食物）嫩的、容易咀嚼切割的：</span>This meat is extremely tender. 這肉嫩得很。/ Cook for a minimum of 2 hours, or until the meat is tender. 至少煮兩個小時，或一直煮到肉變爛為止。/ tender young dwarf beans 幼嫩的四季豆 / Boil the potatoes in salted water until just tender. 把土豆放在鹽水中煮到剛好變軟。

周邊詞彙補充：
· done可表示（飯菜）熟了。
