☀ <span class="category">**衣褲部位：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**collar**</span> ['kɒlə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 衣領；領子。是此義的常規用詞：</span>I turned up my collar against the wind. 我把衣領豎起來擋風。/ His collar was undone. 他的領子沒扣上。/ high collar 高領 / shirt collar 襯衫領子 / collar button 領釦

<span class="vocabulary">**neck**</span> [nek] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 衣領，領子，領口：</span>the low neck of the blouse 低胸的襯衫領口 / a casual shirt with an open neck 一件開領的休閒襯衫 / round neck 圓領 / V-neck V字領 <span class="definition">adj. -necked 用於構成形容詞，指有某類衣領的：</span>a round-necked T-shirt 圓領T恤
           
<span class="vocabulary">**lapel**</span> [ləˈpel]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl.] lapels指（西服上衣或夾克上部胸前的）翻領（單數形式lapel指兩個翻領之一）：</span>narrow, wide lapel 窄翻領；寬翻領 / a coat with wide lapels 寬翻領外套 / A brooch was pinned to her lapel. 她的翻領上彆著一枚胸針。/ lapel badge（英）, lapel button（美）, lapel pin（尤英）領章；領釦；領針 / He was wearing a carnation in his lapel. 他的翻領裡彆著一支康乃馨。/ a jacket with a pin on the lapel 帶領針的夾克 / He grabbed her by the lapels and shook her violently. 他抓住她的領子，狠命搖晃。

<span class="vocabulary">**breast**</span> [brest] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 衣服的前胸部分，即前襟：</span>A medal was pinned to the breast of his uniform. 他的制服胸前彆著一枚勳章。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**bust**</span> [bʌst]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤用於談論衣服）女子的胸圍：</span>What is your bust measurement, Madam? 您的胸圍是多少，太太？

<span class="vocabulary">**shoulder**</span> ['ʃəʊldə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 衣服的肩部：</span>a coat with padded shoulders 帶墊肩的外套

<span class="vocabulary">**waist**</span> [weɪst] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 衣服的腰部：</span>the waist of the trousers 褲腰 / These jeans have a 32-inch waist. 這些牛仔褲的腰圍是32英寸。/ waist size 腰圍的尺寸 <span class="definition">adj. -waisted 用於構成形容詞，指有某類腰的：</span>high-waisted dress 高腰連衣裙
           
<span class="vocabulary">**waistline**</span> [ˈweɪstlaɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 衣服的腰部：</span>high, low waistline 高腰；低腰
 
<span class="vocabulary">**sleeve**</span> [sli:v] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 袖子。是此義的常規用詞：</span>coat sleeve 外套的袖子 / removable sleeve 可拆卸的袖子 / She rolled up her sleeves and got down to work. 她捲起袖子開始幹活兒。<span class="definition">adj. -sleeved用於構成形容詞，指有某類袖子的：</span>a short-sleeved shirt 短袖襯衫

<span class="vocabulary">**arm**</span> [ɑːm] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 衣服的袖子，相當於sleeve：</span>a coat with short arms 一件短袖外套
           
<span class="vocabulary">**elbow**</span> [ˈelbəʊ; 美 -boʊ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 衣服的肘部：</span>The jacket was worn at the elbows. 這件夾克衫的肘部磨破了。

<span class="vocabulary">**fly**</span> [flaɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [英也用複數flies] 褲子的前襠開口（即用拉鍊或釦子扣住的部分）：</span>Your fly is undone. 你的前襠沒拉上。

<span class="vocabulary">**seat**</span> [si:t] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 褲子的臀部：</span>the seat of the jeans 牛仔褲的臀部

<span class="vocabulary">**knee**</span> [ni:] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 褲子的膝部：</span>a knee patch 膝蓋的補丁 / These jeans are torn at the knee. 這條牛仔褲膝蓋那兒破了。

<span class="vocabulary">**leg**</span> [leɡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 褲腿：</span>pant leg（美）, trouser leg（英）褲腿 / The trousers are too long in the leg. 這條褲子的褲腿太長。/ He rolled up the legs of his jeans. 他捲起了牛仔褲的褲腿。

<span class="vocabulary">**opening**</span> ['əʊpənɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 衣服的開襟或開口部分（其開啟和閉合可使穿衣服更容易）：</span>The skirt has a side opening. 這裙子是側面開口的。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vent**</span> [vent]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 大衣、夾克等後面或側面的底部開衩：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dart**</span> [dɑ:t; 美 dɑ:rt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 為使衣服更合身而在衣料上縫進去的部分，可稱為縫褶：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**hem**</span> [hem]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 衣服等的褶邊、卷邊（即被摺疊且縫起來的邊緣），裙子的下襬：</span>to take up the hem of a dress (= to make the dress shorter) 把連衣裙改短
           
<span class="vocabulary">**liner**</span> [ˈlaɪnə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤用於構成複合詞）襯裡、內襯：</span>bin, nappy liners 襯在垃圾桶內的塑膠袋；尿布襯墊

<span class="vocabulary">**lined**</span> [laɪnd]
<span class="definition">adj. 1（衣服）有襯裡的；有內襯的：</span>a lined skirt 帶襯裡的裙子 / The coat is fully lined. 大衣全部配有襯裡。/ The case was lined with black velvet. 箱子有黑色天鵝絨襯裡。<span class="definition">2 -lined有…作襯裡的：</span>瞭解即可
