☀ <span class="category">**手套 雨傘：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**glove**</span> [ɡlʌv] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（有五個手指的）手套：</span>a pair of gloves 一副手套 / fingerless glove 無指手套 / protective glove 防護手套 / baseball, boxing, surgical glove 棒球手套；拳擊手套；外科手術手套
           
<span class="vocabulary">**mitten**</span> [ˈmɪtn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 大拇指和其餘四個手指分開（其餘四個手指在一起）的手套：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**finger**</span> ['fɪŋɡə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 手套的手指部位：</span>He bit the fingers of his right glove and pulled it off. 他咬住右手手套的指套，把它扯了下來。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**thumb**</span> [θʌm]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（手套的）拇指部分：</span>There's a hole in the thumb. 手套的拇指上有個窟窿。

<span class="vocabulary">**umbrella**</span> [ʌm'brelə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 英也用非正式的brolly，指傘，雨傘或遮陽傘：</span>beach, sun umbrella 沙灘遮陽傘；遮陽傘 / golf umbrella 高爾夫傘
