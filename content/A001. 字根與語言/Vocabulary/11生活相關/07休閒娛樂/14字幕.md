☀ <span class="category">**字幕：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**subtitle**</span> [ˈsʌbtaɪtl]
<span class="definition">n. [usually pl.]（電影或電視上外國電影的）字幕、對白譯文：</span>a Japanese movie with English subtitles 一部帶英語字幕的日本電影 / Is the movie dubbed or are there subtitles? 這部電影是配音的還是帶字幕的？/ The dialogue is in Spanish, with English subtitles. 對話是西班牙語，配有英語字幕。/ The film is presented in French with optional English subtitles. 影片對白為法語，但有可選的英語字幕。<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 給電影等加字幕：</span>a Spanish film subtitled in English 一部加了英語字幕的西班牙影片

<span class="vocabulary">**credit**</span> ['kredɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 常用複數，指電影或電視節目中對攝製人員的介紹，常以片頭或片尾字幕的形式出現：</span>She was given a programme credit for her work on the costumes for the play. 她為這出戏準備服裝，被列入劇目製作人員名單。/ opening credits 片頭演職人員名單 / We left before the final credits began to roll. 片尾演職人員名單還未出現我們就退場了。
