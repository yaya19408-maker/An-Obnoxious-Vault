☀ <span class="category">**晚輩 兒孫：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**child**</span> [tʃaɪld] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 任何年齡的兒子或女兒：</span>adult child 成年的孩子 / only child 獨生子女
           
<span class="vocabulary">**progeny**</span> [ˈprɒdʒəni; 美 ˈprɑ:dʒ-]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] [正式或幽默] 人的孩子：</span>He was surrounded by his numerous progeny. 眾多的子孫簇擁著他。/ Davis was never loquacious on the subject of his progeny. 戴維斯說到他的子女時總是寡言少語。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**issue**</span> [ˈɪʃu:; British English also ˈɪsju:]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [法律] 子女、子嗣：</span>He died without issue. 他死後無子嗣。           

<span class="vocabulary">**brood**</span> [bru:d]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [幽默] 一大家孩子：</span>a large brood of children 一大群子女
 
<span class="vocabulary">**family**</span> ['fæmɪlɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 一對夫婦（或一個人）的子女，尤指年幼的子女：</span>They have a large family. 他們子女成群。/ Do they plan to start a family? 他們打算生孩子嗎？/ young family 年幼的子女

<span class="vocabulary">**daughter**</span> ['dɔ:tə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 女兒。是此義的常規用詞：</span>We have two sons and a daughter. 我們有兩兒一女。/ only daughter 獨生女 / eldest, oldest daughter 長女 / adopted daughter 養女

<span class="vocabulary">**girl**</span> [ɡə:l] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 女兒：</span>Our youngest girl is at college. 我們的小女兒在上大學。

<span class="vocabulary">**son**</span> [sʌn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 兒子。是此義的常規用詞：</span>eldest, oldest son 長子 / adopted son 養子

<span class="vocabulary">**boy**</span> [bɒɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 年少的兒子：</span>They have two boys and a girl. 他們有兩個兒子和一個女兒。

<span class="vocabulary">**junior**</span> ['dӡu:njə] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [美，非正式] 一個人的年幼的兒子：</span>I leave junior with Mom when I’m at work. 我工作時就把兒子留給母親照看。

<span class="vocabulary">**blood**</span> [blʌd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [舊時，英] 有錢人家的時尚男子，即紈絝子弟，花花公子：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**sister**</span> ['sɪstə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（同父母的）姐姐或妹妹：</span>Do you have any brothers or sisters? 你有兄弟姐妹嗎？/ big, elder, older sister 姐姐 / little, younger sister 年幼的妹妹；妹妹 / full sister 親姐姐；親妹妹

<span class="vocabulary">**brother**</span> ['brʌðə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（同父母的）哥哥或弟弟，即兄；弟：</span>big, elder, older brother 兄長；哥哥 / little, small, younger brother 弟弟 / full brother 親兄弟 / blood brothers 結拜兄弟

<span class="vocabulary">**kid**</span> [kɪd] 
<span class="definition">adj. [尤美，非正式] kid sister/brother 一個人的妹妹或弟弟：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**sibling**</span> [ˈsɪblɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [正式或術語] 兄弟姐妹：</span>elder, eldest, older, younger sibling 哥哥（或姐姐）；大哥（或大姐）；弟弟（或妹妹）/ The younger children were badly treated by older siblings. 小一些的孩子受到哥哥姐姐的虐待。/ adoptive sibling 收養的兄弟（或姐妹）/ female, male sibling 姐妹；兄弟 / poor relationships between siblings 兄弟姐妹之間的糟糕關係 / squabbles between siblings 兄弟姐妹間的口角 / His siblings are mostly in their early twenties. 他的兄弟姐妹大多二十出頭。/ Sibling rivalry often causes parents anxieties. 手足之爭常讓父母憂心忡忡。

<span class="vocabulary">**grandchild**</span> ['ɡrændtʃaɪld] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 兒子（或女兒）的孩子，即孫子；外孫子；孫女；外孫女：</span>Mary loves her grandchildren. 瑪麗很疼愛她的孫子們。

<span class="vocabulary">**granddaughter**</span> ['ɡrændɔ:tə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 兒子（或女兒）的女兒，即孫女；外孫女：</span>my granddaughter 我的孫女

<span class="vocabulary">**grandson**</span> ['ɡrændsʌn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 兒子（或女兒）的兒子，即孫子；外孫子：</span>my grandson 我的孫子
