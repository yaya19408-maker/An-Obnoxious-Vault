☀ <span class="category">**思考、考慮的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**think**</span> [θɪŋk] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 1 think (about sth) 用大腦進行思考（形成關聯性的想法以解決問題等），或稱思索。是此義的常規用詞，含義最為基礎、廣泛：</span>Are animals able to think? 動物會思考嗎？/ Let me think. 讓我想一想。/ All he ever thinks about is money. 他滿腦子想的只是錢。/ I have often thought about this problem. 我經常思考這個問題。/ I often think of Jane. 我經常想起簡。<span class="definition">2 通常用於進行時，（在頭腦中）想或琢磨（主意、字詞、影象等）：</span>You’re very quiet. What are you thinking? 你一聲不吭，在想什麼？<span class="definition">3 用於對自己當初所做過或經歷過的事情做出評論（該事情現在看來讓人感到驚訝、愚蠢或荒唐）：</span>To think I left you alone in a strange place. 想想我居然把你留在一個陌生的地方。/ When I think of how you’ve behaved and the trouble you’ve caused! 想想你的行為，還有你惹的麻煩！<span class="definition">4 用於使說話語氣不太肯定，或較為委婉：</span>I thought we could go out tonight. 我本想我們今晚可以出去。/ Do you think you could open the window? 請開啟窗子好嗎？/ ‘You’ve made a mistake.’ ‘I don’t think so.’ “你出錯了。”“我想不會吧。”/ I thought he was seventeen years old. 我覺得他有十七歲。/ The storm is thought to be responsible for as many as four deaths. 這場暴風雨據信造成多達四人死亡。<span class="definition">5 [非正式] 無被動式，指以某種方式來想，或想某一主題：</span>Let’s think positive. 咱們往好的方面想吧。/ You need to think big. 你要敢想。/ to think like the machine 像機器那樣思考 / If you want to make money, you’ve got to think money. 你要想賺錢，就得一門心思琢磨錢。<span class="definition">vi. 1 考慮（採取某行動）：</span>Martin was thinking of taking legal action against Zuckerman. 馬丁正考慮起訴朱克曼。<span class="definition">2 用於引出一個例子（或為自己正在談論的東西做出更精確的解釋）：</span>The parts of the enterprise which are scientifically the most exciting are unlikely to be militarily useful. I am thinking here of the development of new kinds of lasers. 這份計劃中，從科學角度看最讓人興奮的部分在軍事上卻可能無用。我指的是新型鐳射研發。<span class="definition">vt. 1（在某一時刻）心想：</span>She must be sick, Tatiana thought. 她一定是病了，塔蒂亞娜心想。<span class="definition">2 用於疑問句，表示生氣或吃驚：</span>What were you thinking of? You shouldn’t steal. 你當時在想什麼？你不該偷東西。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**rethink**</span> [ˌri:ˈθɪŋk]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 重新考慮（一個計劃、行動方案等，尤指為改變該計劃或行動方案等）：</span>to rethink a plan 重新考慮一項計劃 / Both major political parties are having to rethink their policies. 兩個主要政黨都必須反思其政策。/ I think all of us need to rethink our attitudes toward health and sickness. 我想我們都需要反思自己對待健康和疾病的態度。

<span class="vocabulary">**consider**</span> [kən'sɪdə] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 仔細考慮或思考某事（尤指為了做出一個決定）。比think相對正式： </span>a carefully considered response 一個經過仔細考慮的回覆 / We’re considering buying a new car. 我們在考慮買一輛新車。/ He was considering what to do next. 他在考慮下一步怎麼辦。/ I’d like some time to consider. 我希望花些時間考慮一下。/ Each case is considered separately. 對每種情況分別進行考慮。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**contemplate**</span> [ˈkɒntəmpleɪt; 美 ˈkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">vt. 考慮（是否該去做某事，或如何做某事）：</span>You're too young to be contemplating retirement. 你考慮退休還太年輕。/ I have never contemplated living abroad. 我從未考慮過去國外居住。/ For a time he contemplated a career as an army medical doctor. 有段時間，他考慮去當軍醫。/ She contemplates leaving for the sake of the kids. 她考慮為了孩子而離開。/ How could you even contemplate such an idea? 你居然想得出這麼一個主意？/ Are you willing to contemplate retraining? 你願意仔細考慮再培訓的事嗎？<span class="definition">vt. 考慮接受（發生某事的可能性）：</span>The thought of war is too awful to contemplate. 戰爭太可怕了，真不敢去想。/ I can't contemplate what it would be like to be alone. 我不能想象獨自一人會是個什麼樣子。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**stew**</span> [stju:; 美 stu:]
<span class="definition">vi. 思考：</span>I've been stewing over the problem for a while. 這個問題我已經考慮了一會兒。                   

<span class="vocabulary">**linger**</span> [ˈlɪŋgə(r)]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (on sb/sth) 持續思考更長時間：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**see**</span> [si:] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 透過思考來弄清…或定奪…。日常口語中較常用： </span>‘Will you be able to help us?’ ‘I don’t know, I’ll have to see.’ “你能幫助我們嗎？”“不好說，我得考慮一下。”

<span class="vocabulary">**reason**</span> ['ri:zn] 
<span class="definition">vi. 運用思考和理解能力，即思考，理解。表此義時不太常用： </span>the human ability to reason 人的思考能力

<span class="vocabulary">**say**</span> [sei] 
<span class="definition">vt. say sth to yourself 可指心中暗想： </span>Perhaps I’m still dreaming, I said to myself. 我心中暗想，可能我仍在做夢吧。

<span class="vocabulary">**flow**</span> [fləʊ] 
<span class="definition">vi.（思想）以一種輕鬆自然的方式發展或被產生： </span>The best thing is when ideas flow in both directions. 最好的是思想雙向流動。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**brood**</span> [bru:d]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (over/on/about sth) 對（讓自己煩惱、憂慮或不安的事）想太多：</span>You're not still brooding over what he said, are you? 你不是還在為他的話悶悶不樂吧？/ I guess everyone broods over things once in a while. 我猜想每個人都會不時地琢磨一些事情。/ She constantly broods about her family. 她一直為她的家人擔憂。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**buzz**</span> [bʌz]
<span class="definition">vi.（想法）在腦海中縈繞，翻騰：</span>Many more questions were buzzing around in my head. 還有很多問題在我的腦海裡翻騰。/ Top style consultants will leave you buzzing with new ideas. 高階時尚顧問會使你的頭腦充滿新鮮想法。

周邊詞彙補充：
· haunt指（不愉快的事）縈繞於腦際，難以忘卻。

☀ <span class="category">**思考、考慮的名詞和形容詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**consideration**</span> [kənsɪdə'reɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] [正式] 對某事所進行的仔細考慮，如斟酌：</span>Careful consideration should be given to issues of safety. 安全的問題應予以認真考慮。/ The proposals are currently under consideration. 那些提案目前正在審議中。/ After careful consideration, I have decided to resign. 經過仔細考慮，我決定辭職。/ His argument deserves further consideration. 他的論點值得進一步考慮。<span class="definition">2 [C]（做計劃或決定時）必須考慮的東西（如因素、原因）：</span>Pricing is a key consideration to users. 定價是使用者考慮的一個關鍵因素。/ commercial, financial, health, political consideration 商業上的考慮；財務上的考慮；健康上的考慮；政治上的考慮

<span class="vocabulary">**thinking**</span> ['θɪŋkɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 思考某事的過程，如思考，思維：</span>This topic requires a lot of deep thinking. 這個課題需要進行大量深刻的思考。/ abstract, analytical, critical thinking 抽象思維；分析性思維；批判性思維 / creative, innovative thinking 創造性思維；創新思維 / lateral thinking 橫向思維 / wishful thinking 痴心妄想 / to do thinking 思考 / to apply, to use thinking 運用思維 / to stimulate thinking 激發思考

<span class="vocabulary">**thought**</span> [θɔ:t] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 思考的能力或思維的過程：</span>independence of thought 獨立思考 <span class="definition">2 [U] 嚴肅認真地思考某事的行為，即思考，考慮，深思，相當於consideration：</span>serious thought 認真考慮 / logical, rational thought 邏輯思考；理性思考 / abstract, analytical, critical thought 抽象的思考；分析性的思考；批判性的思考 / creative thought 創造性的思考 / independent, original thought 獨立的思考；有創見的思考 / to provoke thought 引發思考 / She was deep in thought. 她陷入沉思。/ thought pattern, thought process 思維模式；思考過程 / thought experiment 思想實驗 / a train of thought 思考脈絡 / We have to give the matter fresh thought. 我們必須重新思考這個問題。/ Don’t give it another thought (= to tell sb not to worry after they have said they are sorry). 別多想了。
     
<span class="vocabulary">**forethought**</span> [ˈfɔ:θɔ:t; 美 ˈfɔ:rθ-]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（對未來所需，或某事的結果等所做的）事先的考慮，如深謀遠慮：</span>Some forethought and preparation is necessary before you embark on the project. 你著手進行這個專案之前必須有所考慮，有所準備。/ With a little forethought many accidents could be avoided. 如果事先稍微多想想，許多事故是可以避免的。/ We had had the forethought to book places in advance. 我們事先預定了位置，頗有預見。/ He had made the remark completely without forethought. 他說這番話完全沒有經過事先考慮。/ a lack of forethought 缺少深謀遠慮

<span class="vocabulary">**second thought**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [C] 重新考慮：</span>As for the money, don't give it a second thought. 至於錢，就不要再考慮了。/ on second thought（美）, on second thoughts（尤英）經過重新考慮 / On second thoughts, maybe I won't apply for the job after all. 轉念一想，也許我根本不會申請這份工作。/ He dismissed the allegations without a second thought. 他完全不相信那些說法。/ We're beginning to have second thoughts about buying a car. 關於買汽車的事，我們開始另有想法了。

<span class="vocabulary">**thoughtful**</span> [ˈθɔ:tfl]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 沉思的、深思的：</span>He looked thoughtful. 他一臉沉思的神情。/ They sat in thoughtful silence. 他們坐著，沉思不語。/ Nancy, who had been thoughtful for some time, suddenly spoke. 南希沉思了一會兒，突然開口說話了。<span class="definition">2 表現出縝密思考的跡象的，（書籍、電影、講話等）嚴肅的、思想性強的：</span>a player who has a thoughtful approach to the game 對這場比賽縝密研究過對策的運動員 / a thoughtful and scholarly book 一本兼具思想性和學術性的書

<span class="vocabulary">**think**</span> [θɪŋk] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [英] 思考：</span>I’ve had a long, hard think about it. 我已經苦苦思考那個問題好長時間了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**rethink**</span> [ˌri:ˈθɪŋk]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.]（對一個計劃、行動方案等的）重新考慮：</span>a radical rethink of company policy 對公司規劃的徹底反思 / There must be a rethink of government policy towards this vulnerable group. 必須重新考慮政府對待這一弱勢群體的政策。

<span class="vocabulary">**look**</span> [lʊk] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] look (at sb/sth) 考慮：</span>We’ll be taking a close look at these proposals. 我們會仔細審查這些方案。/ to take a fresh look at our these techniques 重新審視一下這些銷售技巧

<span class="vocabulary">**reason**</span> ['ri:zn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 大腦的邏輯思考力、理解力、形成觀點的能力等：</span>Only human beings are capable of reason. 只有人類才有理性思考的能力。/ to lose your reason 神經失常 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**perception**</span> [pəˈsepʃn; 美 pərˈs-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式]（對某事物本質的）洞察力、悟性：</span>She showed great perception in her assessment of the family situation. 她對家庭狀況的分析顯示出敏銳的洞察力。/ It did not require a great deal of perception to realise the interview was over. 並不需要多少洞察力也能意識到面試已經結束了。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**flow**</span> [fləʊ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing., U] flow of sth 想法在講話或寫作中的連貫或流暢：</span>Too many examples can interrupt the smooth flow of the text. 例子太多會使行文不流暢。/ We like to allow a free flow of ideas in our company. 我們希望大家在公司裡能自由交流思想。
          
☀ <span class="category">**權衡、盤算：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**weigh**</span> [weɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. weigh sth (up) / weigh (up) sth (against sth) 在做決策之前仔細考量（尤指透過對比該情況所涉及的各個方面及因素等），即權衡，斟酌：</span>You must weigh up the pros and cons. 你必須權衡利弊。/ She weighed her options. 她在各種選擇間權衡斟酌。/ The jury weighed up the evidence carefully. 陪審團仔細考量了證據。

<span class="vocabulary">**balance**</span> ['bæləns] 
<span class="definition">vt. balance A against B 比較兩個事物的相對重要性，也可稱為權衡。此時與weigh沒有本質區別，只是略不如weigh常用：</span>We have to balance the risks of the new strategy against the possible benefits. 我們得權衡新策略的風險與可能帶來的收益。

<span class="vocabulary">**debate**</span> [dɪ'beɪt] 
<span class="definition">vi. debate (with yourself)（對某事進行決策前）仔細考慮：</span>He sat there debating with himself what to do. 他坐在那兒左思右想該怎麼辦。/ We’re debating whether or not to go skiing this winter. 我們盤算著今年冬天是否去滑雪。

☀ <span class="category">**沉思、深思：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**reflect**</span> [rɪ'flekt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. reflect (on/upon sth) 細緻且深深地思考某事，即認真思考，沉思：</span>Before I decide, I need time to reflect. 在做出決定以前，我需要時間考慮。/ We should all give ourselves time to reflect. 我們都應該給自己時間來深思。/ He reflected ruefully that his money didn’t buy as much as it used to. 他懊惱地想著自己的錢已經貶值。/ She paused to reflect on what she had achieved. 她停下來，回想自己所取得的成績。

<span class="vocabulary">**reflection**</span> [rɪˈflekʃn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 對某事的沉思、深思（有時思考的事件比較長）：</span>She decided on reflection to accept his offer after all. 經過審慎的思考，她決定還是接受他的提議。/ A week off would give him time for reflection. 歇上一週會使他有時間考慮考慮。/ After days of reflection she decided to write back. 想了幾天之後她決定回信。/ He paused, absorbed by his reflections. 他停下來，陷入了沉思。/ careful, deep, mature, profound, serious, sober reflection 縝密的思考；深思；熟慮；深入的思考；認真的思考；清醒的思考 / quiet reflection 靜思 / personal reflection 自我反省 / thoughtful reflection 沉思 / Such decisions may be made after thoughtful reflection. 這種決定可能會在深思後作出。/ critical, ethical, moral, philosophical, spiritual, theological, theoretical reflection 批判性的思考；倫理思考；道德思考；哲學思考；精神反思；神學思考；理論思考 / Teachers should encourage reflection on the part of their students. 教師應該鼓勵學生進行反思。/ A moment's reflection will show you that that can't be true. 稍加思考，你就會發現這不可能是真的。/ On further reflection, I'm not so sure it's a good idea. 再仔細一想，我真不敢肯定這是個好主意了。/ The party needs a period of sober reflection about what went wrong. 該黨需要一段時間來認真反省，看著到底什麼地方出了問題。/ a period of reflection 思考期 / a period for reflection, a time for reflection 反思階段；思考時間 <span class="definition">2 [C, usually pl.]（寫下的或說出的關於某課題的）思考：</span>In his latest collection of poems readers are confronted with a series of reflections on death. 在他最新的詩集裡，讀者能夠讀到他對死亡的一系列感想。/ They took a few minutes to offer their personal reflections. 他們花了幾分鐘提出他們的個人想法。/ She has some interesting reflections about the spiritual state of the country. 她對這個國家的精神狀態有些值得思考的見解。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**reflective**</span> [rɪˈflektɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 沉思的；深思的：</span>I walked on in a reflective mood to the car. 我心事重重地向車子走去。/ Mike is a quiet, reflective man. 邁克是一個文靜、喜歡沉思的人。

<span class="vocabulary">**contemplate**</span> [ˈkɒntəmpleɪt; 美 ˈkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. [正式] 對某事長時間深思：</span>She lay in bed, contemplating. 她躺在床上冥思苦想。/ As he lay in his hospital bed that night, he cried as he contemplated his future. 那天晚上，他躺在醫院的病床上，想到未來，他哭了起來。/ That makes it difficult to contemplate the idea that the present policy may not be sustainable. 那讓人難以認真思考這個可能，即目前的政策也許並不能持續下去。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**contemplation**</span> [ˌkɒntəmˈpleɪʃn; 美 ˌkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式] 對某事的沉思、深思：</span>quiet, silent contemplation 靜思；默想 / deep, serious contemplation 深思；認真的思索 / He spent many hours in deep contemplation. 他深思了好幾個鐘頭。/ the contemplation of beauty 對美的沉思 / He sat there deep in contemplation. 他坐在那裡沉思著。/ a few moments of quiet contemplation 默默沉思的片刻 / a life of prayer and contemplation 祈禱與冥思的生活

<span class="vocabulary">**meditate**</span> [ˈmedɪteɪt]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (on/upon sth) 安靜地沉思，尤指宗教冥想或為了使身心安寧而沉思：</span>I was meditating, and reached a higher state of consciousness. 我在冥想，並進入了一個更高的意識境界。<span class="definition">vi. 長時間仔細而深沉地思考：</span>He meditated on the problem. 他仔細斟酌著這個問題。/ On the day her son began school, she meditated on the uncertainties of his future. 兒子開始上學的那一天，她就在考慮他未來的種種不確定因素了。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**meditation**</span> [ˌmedɪˈteɪʃn]
