☀ <span class="category">**聲音反射：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**echo**</span> [ˈekəʊ; 美 ˈekoʊ]
<span class="definition">vi.（聲音）反射回來，即迴響、迴盪：</span>Her footsteps echoed down the corridor. 她的腳步聲在走廊裡迴盪。/ His feet echoed on the bare board floor. 他的腳步聲在沒鋪地毯的木地板上發出迴響。/ Laughter echoed through the house. 笑聲迴盪在整個房子裡。/ The protest seemed to echo across the room. 抗議聲似乎在房間裡迴盪。/ His voice echoed around the room. 他的聲音在整間屋子裡迴盪。/ The bang came suddenly, echoing across the buildings, shattering glass. 突如其來的一聲巨響在建築物間迴盪著，震碎了玻璃。/ The call echoed off the walls of the cave. 叫喊的回聲從洞壁反射過來。/ The gunshot echoed through the forest. 槍炮聲在林中迴盪。/ Their voices echoed back across the water. 他們的聲音穿過水麵傳了回來。/ Her screams still echoed in his ears. 她的尖叫聲仍在他的耳邊迴響。<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (to/with sth) / ~ sth (back) 將聲音反射回來；充滿回聲：</span>The whole house echoed. 整個房子充滿回聲。/ The great hall echoed with laughter. 大廳裡迴盪著笑聲。/ The corridor echoed with the barking of a dozen dogs. 十來只狗的叫聲在走廊裡迴響。/ The street echoed with the cries of children. 街上回蕩著孩子的哭聲。/ The valley echoed back his voice. 山谷裡迴盪著他的聲音。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**echo**</span> [ˈekəʊ; 美 ˈekoʊ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 牆體或封閉空間對聲音的反射：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**reflect**</span> [rɪ'flekt] 
<span class="definition">vt.（從一個表面上）反射（聲音等）：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**reflection**</span> [rɪˈflekʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（聲的）反射：</span>sound reflection 回聲

相關詞義延伸：
· echo還可指回聲、迴音、迴響。
