☀ <span class="category">**單色 雜色：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**solid**</span> ['sɒlɪd] 
<span class="definition">adj.（色彩）純色的，單色的：</span>solid black 純黑色

<span class="vocabulary">**plain**</span> [pleɪn] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 上面沒有標記或圖案的，如素色的、單色的、無花紋的：</span>covers in plain or printed cotton 單色或印花的罩布 / plain paper (= without lines) 無格紙張 <span class="definition">2 純色的：</span>a plain white shirt 一件純白色襯衫

<span class="vocabulary">**pure**</span> [pjʊə] 
<span class="definition">adj.（顏色）非常清晰的、純正的、完美的。是此義的常規用詞：</span>pure white 潔白的

<span class="vocabulary">**flat**</span> [flæt] 
<span class="definition">adj.（顏色或圖畫）色彩單調的；無明暗對比反差的；無景深的：</span>flat blocks of colour 單一色調的圖塊

<span class="vocabulary">**shot**</span> [ʃɒt] 
<span class="definition">adj. shot (with sth)（紡織物、毛髮等）在主色中有其他顏色的，即雜色的，或波動時產生一種顏色變化的效果，即閃色的：</span>shot silk 閃色的絲綢 / a shot sweater 雜色的毛衣
