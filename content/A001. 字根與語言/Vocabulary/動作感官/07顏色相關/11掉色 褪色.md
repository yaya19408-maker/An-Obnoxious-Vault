☀ <span class="category">**掉色 褪色：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**fade**</span> [feɪd] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 褪色，使…褪色。是此義的常規用詞：</span>The curtains had faded in the sun. 窗簾已經給曬褪了色。/ faded blue jeans 褪色的藍牛仔褲

<span class="vocabulary">**run**</span> [rʌn] 
<span class="definition">vi. 衣物上的顏色遇水而褪色：</span>The colour of his trousers runs. 他褲子掉色。

<span class="vocabulary">**fast**</span> [fɑːst] 
<span class="definition">adj.（衣服的顏色）不太可能改變的（或洗滌的時候不太可能褪色的）：</span>瞭解即可
