☀ <span class="category">**濃淡色度 層次感：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**depth**</span> [depθ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U]（一種顏色的）濃度或強度：</span>Strong light will affect the depth of colour of your carpets. 強烈的陽光會使地毯退色。

<span class="vocabulary">**shade**</span> [ʃeɪd] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] shade (of sth) 色彩的濃淡或深淺，也可稱為色度：</span>towels in warm shades 暖色調的毛巾 / In the mornings the sky appeared a heavy shade of mottled grey. 清晨，天空看上去是斑駁的深灰色調。/ His face turned an even deeper shade of red. 他的臉更紅了。<span class="definition">2 [U] 繪畫中的暗淡區域，尤指用這種暗淡區域所產生的層次感或差異感等：</span>The painting needs more light and shade. 這幅畫明暗層次不夠。
