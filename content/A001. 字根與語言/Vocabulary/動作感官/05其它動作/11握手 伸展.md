☀ <span class="category">**握手：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**shake**</span> [ʃeɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. shake sb’s hand / shake hands (with sb) (on sth) / shake sb by the hand（與某人）握手（以表示寒暄問候或同意某事）：</span>They shook hands on the deal. 他們達成了協議，相互握手祝賀。/ Our host shook each of us warmly by the hand. 主人熱情地和我們每個人握手。  
           
<span class="vocabulary">**handshake**</span> [ˈhændʃeɪk]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 握手：</span>firm, hearty（尤美）handshake 緊緊的握手；親切的握手 / John smiled and gave him a hearty handshake. 約翰微笑著和他熱情地握手。/ He has a strong handshake. 他握手時很有力。/ She wasn't impressed by his limp handshake. 他軟綿綿的握手沒有給她留下印象。/ They sealed the agreement with a handshake. 他們握手成交。/ a handshake from the chairman 與主席的握手 / golden handshake（= money given to sb when they leave their job）解僱費 o He received a $150 000 golden handshake when he lost his job. 他失業時得到了15萬美元的退職金。

<span class="vocabulary">**stretch**</span> [stretʃ]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi.（身體）舒展（如伸展胳膊和腿）：</span>He stretched and yawned lazily. 他伸了伸懶腰，打了個哈欠。/ He turned out the light and stretched full-length on the bed. 他關了燈，舒展全身躺到了床上。/ He stretched out on the couch and watched TV. 他四肢伸開躺在沙發上看電視。/ The exercises are designed to stretch and tone your leg muscles. 這些活動目的在於伸展和增強你的腿部肌肉。/ Try stretching your legs and pulling your toes upwards. 試著雙腿伸直並且向上拉伸腳趾。<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 身體或肢體的伸展、舒展：</span>Only do these more difficult stretches when you are warmed up. 要做了準備活動以後再做這些較難的伸展運動。/ First let's warm up by doing some stretches. 咱們先做一些伸展運動熱熱身。/ Her arms were at full stretch. 她雙臂完全伸開。/ Stay in this position and feel the stretch in your legs. 保持這個姿勢，體會腿部繃緊的感覺。/ Hold the stretch for a count of ten. 保持伸展姿勢，一直數到十。/ Have a good stretch from time to time to prevent yourself getting stiff. 不時充分伸展一下筋骨，以防身體變僵。/ We got out of the car and had a good stretch. 我們下車好好舒展了一下身體。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**extend**</span> [ɪkˈstend]
<span class="definition">vt. 伸展，舒展，展開身體部位（尤指手臂或腿腳）：</span>He extended his hand to (= offered to shake hands with) the new employee. 他伸出手來與新僱員握手。/ The man extended his hand: 'I'm Chuck'. 那名男子伸出手說道：“我是查克。”/ to extend the hand of friendship to (= try to have good relations with) another country（比喻用法）向另一個國家伸出友誼之手
                     
<span class="vocabulary">**flex**</span> [fleks]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 彎曲、拉伸或活動（四肢或肌肉），尤指為體力活動做準備：</span>to flex your fingers, to flex your feet, to flex your legs 活動手指；活動雙腳；活動雙腿 / He stood on the side of the pool flexing his muscles. 他站在游泳池旁活動肌肉。/ He slowly flexed his muscles and tried to stand. 他緩慢地活動了一下肌肉，想盡量站起來。

<span class="vocabulary">**sprawl**</span> [sprɔ:l]
<span class="definition">vi.（放鬆或彆扭地）伸開四肢坐（或躺）：</span>He was sprawled out on the sofa. 他四肢攤開懶懶散散地靠坐在沙發上。/ He was sprawling in an armchair in front of the TV. 他伸開手腳坐在電視機前的一張扶手椅上。/ She sprawled on the bed as he had left her, not even moving to cover herself up. 他離開她後她就攤手攤腳地躺在床上，甚至都懶得往身上蓋點兒東西。/ They sprawled in lawn chairs, snoozing. 他們手腳張開地躺在草地躺椅上，打著呼嚕。/ She found him sprawled across the doorstep. 她發現他四肢攤開倒在門前的臺階上。/ She lay sprawled across the bed. 她四肢攤開躺著床上。/ The blow sent him sprawling. 這一猛擊把他打趴在地上。/ Something hit her and sent her sprawling to the ground. 不知什麼東西擊中了她，把她打趴在地上。/ I tripped and went sprawling. 我絆了一下，摔了個四腳朝天。/ She went sprawling across the boat. 她四腳朝天摔倒在船上。
