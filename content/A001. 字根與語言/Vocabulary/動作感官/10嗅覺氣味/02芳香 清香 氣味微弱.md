☀ <span class="category">**芳香 清香：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**aroma**</span> [əˈrəʊmə; 美 əˈroʊmə]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（食物等的）宜人且強烈的氣味，如香味、芳香：</span>faint, subtle aroma 淡淡的芳香；幽香 / I immediately smelled the distinctive aroma of fresh coffee. 我立刻聞到了新鮮咖啡特有的香味。/ the wonderful aroma of freshly baked bread 新鮮出爐的麵包散發出的美妙香味 / / the mouth-watering aroma of prawns cooked in garlic 蒜蓉大蝦那令人垂涎欲滴的香味 / spicy, sweet aroma 辛辣的氣味；甜甜的氣味 / A pleasant aroma filled the house. 房子裡瀰漫著一種宜人的芳香。/ The room was filled with a strong aroma of woodsmoke. 屋裡瀰漫著濃重的木柴煙燻味。/ The pungent aroma of hay and horse manure filled his nostrils. 乾草和馬糞混合在一起的刺鼻氣味兒向他撲鼻而來。

<span class="vocabulary">**perfume**</span> [pə'fju:m] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 一種令人愉悅的芳香氣味，相當於scent：</span>sweet perfume 芬芳的香氣 / strong perfume 濃烈的香味 / The perfume of the roses filled the room. 房間裡瀰漫著玫瑰的芳香。<span class="definition">vt. [文學] perfume sth (with sth) 常用被動式，（尤指花）使某地的空氣具有芳香宜人的氣味，相當於scent：</span>The garden was perfumed with the smell of roses. 花園裡瀰漫著玫瑰的芳香。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**scent**</span> [sent]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 某事物的香味：</span>the heavy scent of Indian cooking 印度菜濃郁的香味 / The air was filled with the scent of wild flowers. 空氣中瀰漫著野花的芬芳。/ These flowers have no scent. 這些花不香。/ Flowers are chosen for their scent as well as their look. 選花不僅要觀其形還要聞其香。/ the fresh scent of flowers 鮮花的清香 / floral, flowery scent 花香 / jasmine, lavender, pine scent 茉莉花香；薰衣草的香味；松木的香氣 / musky, spicy scent 麝香的芬芳；香料的芬芳 / Frank inhaled the exotic floral scent of her perfume. 弗蘭克嗅了一下她的香水散發出來的異國花香。/ The flowers give off a heady scent at night. 這些花在晚上散發出醉人的芳香。/ From the vine outside came the scent of honey. 外面的葡萄藤飄來花蜜的香味。/ The scent of pine filled the room. 松木的香氣瀰漫整個房間。/ The scent of incense lingered in the air. 薰香的氣味還留存在空氣中。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**fragrance**</span> [ˈfreɪgrəns]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 宜人的氣味，如香氣：</span>floral fragrance 花香 / spicy fragrance 香料的香氣 / This perfume has a light, fresh fragrance. 這款香水有淡淡的清香。/ a shrubby plant with a strong characteristic fragrance 散發出一種濃郁的獨特香味的灌木 / the fragrance of his cologne 他身上古龍香水的味道 / The fragrance of lavender filled the room. 房間裡充滿了薰衣草的香味。/ The bath oil comes in various fragrances. 這種沐浴油有不同的香味。/ fruits full of exquisite fragrance and sweetness 香氣撲鼻的水果

<span class="vocabulary">**bouquet**</span> [buˈkeɪ]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 某類食物或飲料的宜人氣味，尤指葡萄酒的香氣：</span>a Sicilian wine with a light red colour and a bouquet of cloves. 顏色淡紅、散發著丁香香氣的西西里葡萄酒
 
<span class="vocabulary">**savour**</span> [ˈseɪvə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] [正式或文學]（尤指美好的）氣味：</span>瞭解即可
            
<span class="vocabulary">**fragrant**</span> [ˈfreɪgrənt]
<span class="definition">adj. 氣味宜人的、芳香的：</span>fragrant herbs, fragrant flowers, fragrant oils 芳草；香花；香油 / The air was fragrant with lavender. 空氣中瀰漫著薰衣草香。/ The air was fragrant with scents from the sea and the hills. 空氣中盪漾著山和海的芬芳氣息。/ a garden full of wonderfully fragrant flowers 花香四溢的花園

<span class="vocabulary">**sweet**</span> [swi:t] 
<span class="definition">adj.（氣味）芳香宜人的，相當於fragrant。是一個形象化用法：</span>a sweet-smelling rose 芬芳的玫瑰 / The air was sweet with incense. 空氣中瀰漫著燃香的香氣。/ sickly sweet 香得讓人噁心
           
<span class="vocabulary">**sweetness**</span> [ˈswi:tnəs]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 氣味芬芳：</span>The air was filled with the sweetness of mimosa. 空氣中瀰漫著含羞草的芬芳。/ the sickly sweetness of incense 令人噁心的薰香氣味

<span class="vocabulary">**delicious**</span> [dɪ'lɪʃəs] 
<span class="definition">adj.（氣味）宜人的，芬芳的：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**delicate**</span> ['delɪkɪt] 
<span class="definition">adj.（氣味）輕且令人愉悅的，不濃烈的，如清香的：</span>delicate fragrance 清新的芳香

<span class="vocabulary">**clean**</span> [kli:n] 
<span class="definition">adj.（氣味）聞起來清新宜人的：</span>瞭解即可
                        
<span class="vocabulary">**subtle**</span> [ˈsʌtl]
<span class="definition">adj.（氣味）淡淡的：</span>subtle smells 淡淡的氣味 / delightfully subtle scents 怡人的淡淡香氣         

<span class="vocabulary">**scented**</span> [ˈsentɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. 有強烈香味的（可以是自然的香味，也可以是香水的香味）：</span>richly scented flowers 散發著濃郁芳香的鮮花 / The white flowers are pleasantly scented. 這些白花清香怡人。/ scented body lotion 氣味芬芳的潤膚露 / the delicately scented writing paper 散發著淡淡清香的信紙 / The air was scented with the smell of pines. 空氣中瀰漫著松木的香氣。

<span class="vocabulary">**scent**</span> [sent]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] ~ sth (with sth) 使某事物具有某種香味：</span>Roses scented the night air. 夜空中瀰漫著玫瑰花香。/ Jasmine flowers scent the air. 空氣中瀰漫著茉莉花香。/ Scent your drawers and wardrobe with your favourite aromas. 用你最喜歡的香味薰香你的抽屜和衣櫃。

☀ <span class="category">**氣味微弱：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**faint**</span> [feɪnt]
<span class="definition">adj. 氣味微弱的：</span>a faint smell of perfume 淡淡的香水味 / The room held the faint, sweet odour of pipe tobacco. 房間內有煙斗菸絲的淡淡馨香。

相關詞義延伸：
· perfume還可指香水；
· acent還可指香水；
· fragrance還可指香水。

周邊詞彙補充：
· incense可指香，尤指宗教儀式用的香；
· spice可指味道衝的植物調味香料；
· incense可指香，尤指宗教儀式用的香。
