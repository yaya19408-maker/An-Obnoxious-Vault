☀ <span class="category">**借的動詞和名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**borrow**</span> ['bɒrəʊ] 
<span class="definition">vt. borrow sth (from sb/sth) 借用別人的東西（以後歸還）：</span>Can I borrow your umbrella? 借你的傘用一下行嗎？/ to borrow books from a library 從圖書館借書 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi. borrow (sth) (from sb/sth) 從另一種語言（或另一個人等）借用或引用字詞、話語、想法、思想等：</span>The author borrows heavily from Henry James. 那位作家大量引用亨利·詹姆斯的作品。/ Some musical terms are borrowed from Italian. 某些音樂術語是從義大利語引入的。/ His designs borrow freely from the architecture of ancient Egypt. 他的設計大量地借鑑了古埃及的建築風格。

<span class="vocabulary">**lend**</span> [lend] 
<span class="definition">vt. lend (out) sth (to sb) / lend (sb) sth 把…借給某人，相當於loan：</span>I’ve lent the car to a friend. 我把車借給一位朋友了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**loan**</span> [ləʊn; 美 loʊn]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] ~ (of sth) 借出；貸給；被借出：</span>I even gave her the loan of my car. 我甚至把車也借給了她。/ an exhibition of paintings on loan (= borrowed) from private collections 借用私人收藏品舉辦的畫展 / I am in need of a loan of a bike for a few weeks. 我需要借輛腳踏車用幾個星期。/ He had offered the loan of his small villa at Cap Ferrat. 他願意借出在費拉角的小別墅。<span class="definition">vt. [尤英] ~ sth (out) (to sb/sth) / ~ (sb) sth 出借（貴重物品給博物館等）：</span>This exhibit was kindly loaned by the artist's family. 這件展品是藝術家的家人惠借而展出的。/ He loaned the museum his entire collection. 他把自己的全部收藏品都借給了博物館。/ He had kindly offered to loan us all the plants required for the exhibit. 他善解人意地提出願意把展覽需要的植物通通借給我們。/ We were approached by the Royal Yachting Association to see if we would loan our boat to them. 皇家遊艇協會的人找過我們，問我們能不能把船借給他們。

☀ <span class="category">**歸還的動詞和名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**return**</span> [rɪ'tə:n] 
<span class="definition">vt. return sb/sth (to sb/sth) 送回，歸還：</span>He refused to return our money. 他拒絕歸還我們的錢。/ She never returned the book to me. 她從未把那本書還給我。/ to return the goods to the store 把貨退給商店 <span class="definition">n. [U, sing.] 歸還，退回，放回：</span>the return of books to the library 將書歸還圖書館 / He demanded the return of his money. 他要求退還他的錢。/ the return of his property 歸還他的財產 / the return of the child to his mother 將孩子送還給他的母親
           
<span class="vocabulary">**restore**</span> [rɪˈstɔ:(r)]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (to sb/sth) [正式] 歸還某人丟失或被盜的東西：</span>The police have now restored the painting to its rightful owner. 警察已經把這幅油畫歸還給了它的合法主人。/ The following day their horses and goods were restored to them. 第二天，馬匹和貨物都歸還給了他們。/ The looted property was restored and the chargé d'affaires was told that the soldiers responsible had been arrested. 被劫財物已經物歸原主，代辦得知對此負有責任計程車兵已被逮捕。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**restoration**</span> [ˌrestəˈreɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] ~ (of sth)（對某事物的）歸還：</span>the restoration of the Elgin marbles to Greece 埃爾金大理石雕像之交還希臘
           
<span class="vocabulary">**reimburse**</span> [ˌri:ɪmˈbɜ:s; 美 -ˈbɜ:rs]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] ~ sb (for sth) 退還（某人已花費的錢）：</span>We will reimburse any expenses incurred. 我們將付還所有相關費用。/ All monies received will be reimbursed in full.（in full尤為英式用法）所收款項都將全額償還。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**refund**</span> [ˈri:fʌnd]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指由於支付太多，或由於退貨而產生的）被退還的款項：</span>to claim/demand a refund 要求退款 / full refund 全額退款 / partial refund 部分退款 / 10% refund 退款10% / cash refund 現金退款 / tax refund 退稅 / prompt refund 即時退款 / If there is a delay of 12 hours or more, you will receive a full refund of the price of your trip. 如果耽擱達到或超過12小時，你會得到旅費全額退款。/ They'll only give you a refund if you have the receipt. 要有收據他們才會退款。/ He has been refused a refund on his air ticket. 他就機票要求退款被拒。/ You're entitled to a full refund if you change your mind. 如果您改變主意了，您可以得到全額退款。/ There will be no refund for cancellations. 取消者不予退款。/ a full refund of the registration fee 全額退回註冊費 / She received a refund on the unused tickets. 她收到了那些沒使用過的票的退款。/ refunds to 10 000 customers across the country 給全國1萬名顧客的退款 <span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (to sb) / ~ sb sth 退款：</span>Tickets cannot be exchanged or money refunded. 門票不可退換。/ We will refund your money to you in full if you are not entirely satisfied. 如果你並不感到完全滿意，我們會退還全部金額。/ We guarantee to refund your money if you're not delighted with your purchase. 我們承諾如果您對所買物品不滿意可以退款。/ Take the goods back to your retailer who will refund you the purchase price. 把商品退還給你的零售商，他們會按原價退款的。/ Your money will be refunded in full. 您會得到全額退款。/ If you are not satisfied with the goods, the price will be refunded to you. 如果對商品不滿意，我們會給您退款。
