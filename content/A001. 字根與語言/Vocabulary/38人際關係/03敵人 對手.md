☀ <span class="category">**敵人、對手：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**enemy**</span> ['enɪmɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 敵人，仇人，反對者，對頭：</span>I didn’t want to make an enemy of Mr Evans. 我不想和埃文斯先生為敵。/ He has a lot of enemies in the company. 他在公司裡有很多對頭。/ Iraq’s traditional enemy, Iran 伊拉克的宿敵伊朗 / common, perceived enemy 共同的敵人；假想敵 / invisible, unseen, potential enemy 看不見的敵人；潛在的敵人 / external, foreign enemy 外敵 / political, public enemy 政敵；公敵 / In today’s game England play their old enemy, Scotland. 今天的比賽中，英格蘭隊將和他們的老對手蘇格蘭隊對陣。/ Birds are the natural enemies of many insect pests. 鳥類是許多害蟲的天敵。<span class="definition">2 [C] enemy (of sth) 正式用法，指危害物或（阻止…成功的）大敵：</span>Poverty and ignorance are the enemies of progress. 貧窮和愚昧阻礙進步。/ He has become his own worst enemy. 他成了自己最大的敵人。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**opponent**</span> [əˈpəʊnənt; 美 əˈpoʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 比賽、競爭、爭論等活動中的對手、競爭者：</span>political opponent 政敵 / dangerous, worthy, formidable opponent 危險的對手；勢均力敵的對手；強大的對手 / The team's opponents are unbeaten so far this season. 該隊的競爭對手本賽季尚無敗績。/ Today she faces her toughest opponent on Centre Court. 今天在中央球場，她遭遇了最難應付的對手。/ He has knocked out 15 opponents in 20 fights. 他在20場比賽中擊倒了15個對手。<span class="definition">2 [C] ~ (of sth) 某事的反對者、阻止者：</span>opponents of abortion 反對墮胎的人 / opponents of the regime 反對政權的人 / She's an outspoken opponent of the government. 她直言自己反政府。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**protester**</span> [prəˈtestə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 公開抗議者或反對者：</span>Thousands of protesters marched through the city. 數千人遊行抗議，走過全城。/ The protesters say the government is corrupt and inefficient. 抗議者稱政府腐敗無能。/ student protester 進行抗議的學生 / animal rights（尤英）, environmental, peace，political protester 動物權利抗議者；環境抗議者；和平抗議者；政治抗議者 / anti-globalization, anti-government, anti-war, anti-abortion protester 反全球化抗議者；反政府抗議者；反戰抗議人士；反墮胎抗議者 / pro-hunt（英）, pro-life protester 親狩獵抗議者；反墮胎抗議者 / non-violent（尤美）, peaceful, unarmed protester 非暴力的抗議者；和平的抗議者；手無寸鐵的抗議者 / angry protester 憤怒的抗議者 / Protesters clashed with police outside the embassy. 抗議者與警察在使館外發生衝突。/ The streets were crowded with protesters against the war. 街上擠滿了反戰抗議者。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dissident**</span> [ˈdɪsɪdənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 尤指由於政府缺乏民主而對政府持批評態度的一個人（但這樣的人常對政府造成危險），可稱為“持不同政見者”：</span>political dissident 持不同政見者 / religious dissident 持不同宗教主張者 / exiled dissident 被流放的異見人士 / regimes that murder political opponents and imprison dissidents 謀殺政治對手並監禁異見人士的政權 / former Soviet dissident Natan Sharansky 前蘇聯持不同政見者納坦·夏蘭斯基

<span class="vocabulary">**rival**</span> [ˈraɪvl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (to sb/sth) (for sth) 政治、商業、體育等領域的競爭對手（可以是一個人、一家公司、一個國家或一個事物等）：</span>political rival 政治對手 / presidential rival 競選總統的對手 / Conservative rival 保守黨的對手 / They're old political rivals. 他們是政壇上的老對手。/ rival candidate 候選人競爭者 / rival clan, rival faction, rival gang, rival group, rival tribe 敵對的家族；敵對的派系；敵對的團伙；敵對的團體；敵對的部落 / business rival 商業對手 / The Japanese are our biggest economic rivals. 日本人是我們最大的經濟競爭對手。/ The business needed to receive profits and compete with new rivals. 該公司需要恢復利潤並且與新對手競爭。/ The company is well equipped to compete with its international rivals. 該公司條件優越，足以和國際對手競爭。/ The company faces big rivals in Europe and Asia. 該公司在歐洲和亞洲都要面對強勁的競爭對手。/ rival company, rival firm, rival organization 競爭對手公司；對立的機構 / rival bid, rival offer 競爭投標；競爭的出價 / The two teams have always been rivals. 這兩個隊一直是競爭對手。/ She has no rivals for the job. 沒有任何對手和她爭這份工作。/ They wind up as romantic rivals for the same woman. 他們最後因為追求同一個女人而成為情敵。/ They were rivals in love. 他們是情敵。/ They were rivals for her love. 為了得到她的愛他們成了情敵。/ She is now regarded as the greatest potential rival to Jane. 她現在被認為是簡最大的潛在對手。/ Those two have been friendly rivals since they first met. 那兩個人從第一次見面就成了友好的對手。/ He eliminated his rivals with brutal efficiency. 他殘忍而又利落地消滅了自己的對手。/ rival claim 對立的權利要求 / rival theory 對立的理論/ Grand it may be, but this cathedral is no rival to the great cathedral of Amiens. 這個教堂雖然很雄偉，卻絕對不能和亞眠大教堂相匹敵。/ This latest design has no rivals (= it is easily the best design available). 這種最新款式獨領風騷。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**antagonist**</span> [ænˈtægənɪst]
<span class="definition">n. [正式] 與…強烈對立的一個人或事物，即對立者、對手、敵人：</span>Spassky had never previously lost to his antagonist. 斯帕斯基以往從未輸給過他的對手。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**adversary**</span> [ˈædvəsəri; 美 -vərseri]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [正式] 競爭、辯論或戰鬥中的對手、敵人：</span>Elliott crossed the finish line just half a second behind his adversary. 埃利奧特跨過終點線時僅比對手落後半秒鐘。/ His political adversaries were creating a certain amount of trouble for him. 他的政敵正在給他製造一些麻煩。/ The British considered him a worthy adversary. 英國人視他為勁敵。/ The two of them were old adversaries. 他倆是老對手了。

<span class="vocabulary">**foe**</span> [fəʊ; 美 foʊ]
<span class="definition">n. [正式] 敵人、仇敵：</span>political foe 政敵 / In the final the play against their old foes, Italy. 在決賽中他們與老對手義大利隊對壘。/ He knew that Carlton could be an implacable foe. 他明白卡爾頓可能對成為他的死敵。/ She had fought many battles, vanquished many foes.（比喻用法）她身經百戰，挫敗過很多對手。/ to join forces against a common foe 聯合起來對付共同的敵人 / His newspaper articles criticized friend and foe alike. 他在報紙上發表的批評文章不論敵友，一視同仁。/ She was unsure as yet whether he was friend or foe. 她還不確定他究竟是敵是友。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**competitor**</span> [kəmˈpetɪtə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 競爭者，尤指商業上的競爭者、對手，可以是一個人、一個機構等：</span>The company has no serious competitors in this area. 該公司在這個領域裡沒有真正的競爭對手。/ The industry is facing increasingly strong foreign competitors. 該行業正面臨著日益強大的國外競爭者的挑戰。/ fierce competitors for the dominant position in the Asian market 爭奪亞洲市場主導地位的強勁對手 / our main/major competitor 我們主要的競爭對手 <span class="definition">2 [C] 參賽者：</span>She is one of the sport's top competitors. 她是該項運動的頂尖選手之一。/ Over 200 competitors entered the race. 200多名選手參加了賽跑。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**contender**</span> [kənˈtendə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 競爭者：</span>a strong contender for a gold medal in the Olympics 奧運金牌的強有力爭奪者 / Her trainer said yesterday that she would be a strong contender for a place in Britain's Olympic squad. 她的教練昨天說，她將是英國奧運代表隊席位的有力爭奪者。/ championship, medal, title contender 冠軍爭奪者；獎牌爭奪者 / He's gone from being a serious title contender to being totally written off. 他從一個不可小覷的冠軍爭奪者變成了一個徹頭徹尾的敗將。/ a serious contender for the party leadership 該黨領導權最強勁的角逐者 / Senator Clinton was considered a leading contender for the Democratic presidential nomination. 克林頓參議員被認為是民主黨總統候選人提名的主要競爭者。/ presidential contender 總統職位角逐者 / Democratic contender 民主黨競爭者 / Our British Affairs correspondent reports on how the three contenders reacted to the news of the resignation. 我們的“英國國內事務”記者報道了三名競爭者對辭職訊息的反應。          
           
<span class="vocabulary">**opposition**</span> [ˌɒpəˈzɪʃn; 美 ˌɑ:pə-]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the opposition可指（事業、競賽、遊戲等活動中的）對手，敵手，競爭者：</span>He's gone to work for the opposition. 他去為競爭對手工作了。/ The opposition is/are mounting a strong challenge to our business. 對方正對我方企業逐漸形成強大的挑戰。/ Liverpool couldn't match the opposition in the final and lost 2–0. 利物浦隊在決賽中不敵對手，以0:2輸掉比賽。/ Poland provide the opposition for the Scots' last warm-up match at home. 蘇格蘭隊在國內最後一場熱身賽的對手是波蘭隊。

<span class="vocabulary">**challenger**</span> [ˈtʃælɪndʒə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（例如在體育運動或政治領域中）對別人擁有的重要名次、職位或頭銜發起挑戰的一個人，即挑戰者：</span>Draskovic has emerged as the strongest challenger to the leader of the Serbian government. 德拉什科維奇一躍成為塞爾維亞政府領導人最大的挑戰者。/ Her nearest challenger is the vice-president. 與她實力最接近的競爭對手是副總統。/ presidential challenger 總統職位的挑戰者 / a challenger to the prime minister 總理的挑戰者 / Conservative, Democratic, Labour, Republican challenger 保守黨挑戰者；民主黨挑戰者；工黨挑戰者；共和黨挑戰者 / The Senator faces a challenger with conservative views. 這位參議員面對的是一位持保守觀點的挑戰者。/ He took on various challengers, but refused to fight Jackson. 他與各種各樣的排戰者交過手，但拒絕與傑克遜交鋒。/ the main challengers for the world title 爭奪世界冠軍的主要挑戰者 / a challenger to their competitors' dominance of the market 對他們的競爭對手的市場優勢構成挑戰的人
           
<span class="vocabulary">**villain**</span> [ˈvɪlən]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 小說、戲劇等作品中的主要反派人物：</span>screen villain 銀幕上的反派人物 / He often plays the part of the villain. 他經常扮演反面人物。/ He has played villains in most of his films. 他在大多數影片裡都演反派。/ He doesn't fit the standard stereotype of a movie villain. 他與電影中常見的反派人物不符。/ evil villain 邪惡的反派人物 / The Queen in 'Snow White' us is one of film's greatest villains. 《白雪公主》中的王后是電影中最邪惡的反派之一。/ He wore a black cloak, like a pantomime villain. 他身披斗篷，像童話劇中的壞蛋一樣。

<span class="vocabulary">**competition**</span> [kɒmpɪ'tɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the competition 可指競爭對手，競爭者：</span>to beat the competition 打敗競爭對手 / This car consistently outperforms the competition. 這輛車的效能始終未被超越過。

<span class="vocabulary">**field**</span> [fi:ld] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the field 可指運動場或生意場上的競爭對手：</span>the crowded field 眾多的競爭對手 / His superb technique puts him head and shoulders ahead of the field. 他的精湛技藝使他遠遠領先於同場競技者。/ They lead the field in home entertainment systems. 他們在家庭娛樂裝置行業中居領先地位。

<span class="vocabulary">**match**</span> [mætʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] a match for sb / sb’s match 某人（在力量、技能、智力等方面）的匹敵者，即敵手，旗鼓相當的人：</span>He is no match for me at dash. 短跑他根本不是我的對手。/ I think he’s finally met his match in Lisa. 我想他碰到莉薩終於算是遇到了對手。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**killer**</span> [ˈkɪlə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 比喻意義上的殺手或破壞者：</span>Heart disease is the biggest killer in Scotland. 心臟病是蘇格蘭的頭號殺手。/ High blood pressure is known as a silent killer. 高血壓被稱為沉默的殺手。/ killer disease, killer strain, killer virus 致命疾病；致命菌株；致命病毒 / an electric insect killer 電殺蟲器 / This killer storm is strengthening rapidly in the Gulf of Mexico.（killer storm尤為美式用法）該致命風暴正在墨西哥灣迅速加強。/ The killer wave struck a tiny atoll in the Maldives. 驚濤狂浪襲擊了馬爾地夫一座很小的環形珊瑚島。

<span class="vocabulary">**saboteur**</span> [ˌsæbəˈtɜ:(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（為防止敵方利用或為表示抗議的）對某事物故意進行破壞的一個人，如蓄意破壞者：</span>Saboteurs blew up a small section of the track. 有人蓄意炸燬了一小段路。/ The saboteurs had planned to bomb buses and offices. 鬧事者原本計劃用炸彈襲擊公共汽車和辦公樓。/ hunt saboteurs (= people who try to stop people from hunting foxes, etc.)（英）阻撓捕獵活動的人
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vandal**</span> [ˈvændl]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指破壞公共財產的）故意破壞者、搗毀者：</span>All information systems that have been fitted at Ashford station have now been destroyed by vandals. 安裝在阿什福德車站的所有資訊查詢裝置如今都被破壞分子毀壞了。/ teenage, young vandal 青少年破壞者；年輕的破環者 / mindless vandal 盲目的破壞者 / graffiti vandal（尤英）亂塗鴉的破環者 / young graffiti vandals who blight the city with their scrawls 胡亂塗鴉影響市容的年輕人 / The windows are covered with grilles to deter vandals. 窗戶都安上了鐵柵欄，以防被故意破壞。/ The station is plagued by vandals. 車站常遭故意破壞者襲擾。/ The vandals struck at about 7.30p.m. yesterday. 破壞分子昨晚7點半左右開始行動。/ Her statue has been targeted by vandals. 她的塑像已被破壞分子選為攻擊目標。/ Vandals broke into the factory and set fire to a cabin. 破壞分子闖入工廠將小木屋付之一炬。/ Vandals caused more than £4 000 worth of damage. 破壞分子造成了4，000 多英鎊的損失。/ The pool had to close after vandals went on a ￡10 000 wrecking spree.（go on a wrecking spree為英式用法）在破壞分子肆意破壞造成高達1萬英傍的損失後，這個游泳池不得不關門大吉。/ The vandals throw stones at the building. 打砸鬧事者向大廈內扔石塊。/ This is the latest in a spate of vandal attacks on fire crews. 這是破壞分子對消防員一連串襲擊中的最新一起。

<span class="vocabulary">**intruder**</span> [ɪnˈtru:də(r)]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 一棟建築物或一個地區的非法闖入者：</span>He surprised a masked intruder in the kitchen. 他在廚房發現了一個蒙面闖入者，把他嚇壞了。/ Dogs can deter unwelcome intruders. 狗能夠阻嚇闖入的壞人。/ He was stabbed when he tackled an intruder armed with a knife.（英）他在擒抱一名持刀闖入者的時候被捅傷了。/ Staff were instructed to repel intruders with physical force, if need be. 員工們接到指示，必要時可以武力驅逐闖入者。/ Intruders had forced their way into the house.（尤英）闖入者強行闖進了房子。/ intruder alarm, intruder alert 入侵警報 / security measures against intruders 防範闖入者的安全措施 

<span class="vocabulary">**rebel**</span> [ˈrebl]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 反政府的人、叛亂者：</span>anti-government, left-wing, US-backed rebel 反政府叛亂者；左翼叛亂者；美國支援的叛亂者 / a group of rebels against the emperor 反抗君主的一幫叛亂者 / Armed rebels advanced towards the capital. 武裝叛亂分子向首都推進。/ fighting between rebels and government forces 叛軍和政府軍之間的戰鬥 / They sent in troops to back the rebels. 他們派遣軍隊支援叛亂分子。/ The rebels were based in camps along the border. 叛軍沿邊境安營紮寨。/ The rebels seized control of the national radio headquarters. 叛軍控制了國家電臺總部。/ military operations against the rebels 對抗叛亂者的軍事行動 / rebel faction, rebel group, rebel movement 造反派；叛亂幫派；反叛運動 / rebel army, rebel fighters, rebel force/forces, rebel soldiers, rebel troops, rebel officers 叛軍；反叛鬥士；叛亂武裝；叛軍士兵；叛軍部隊；叛軍軍官 / rebel commander, rebel leader 叛亂指揮官；叛亂領導者 / The southern parts of the country had fallen into rebel control. 該國南部地區已被叛亂者控制。/ rebel base, rebel camp, rebel position, rebel stronghold 叛軍基地；叛軍營地；叛軍陣地；叛軍據點 / rebel activity, rebel advance, rebel assault, rebel attack, rebel invasion 叛亂活動；叛軍推進；叛軍攻擊；叛軍進攻；叛軍入侵 / new recruits to the rebel cause 叛亂運動的新成員 <span class="definition">2 [C]（組織機構、政黨等內部的）反抗權威者、持不同意見者、反對派：</span>The rebels want another 1% cut in interest rates. 反對派希望利率再降一個百分點。/ rebel MPs / 持不同政見的國會議員 / back-bench rebel（英，政治）持反對意見的後座議員 <span class="definition">3 [C] 不喜歡遵守規則或不接受正常的行為、著裝等方面的規範的人，即叛逆者、不守規矩者：</span>I've always been the rebel of the family. 我在家裡向來是個叛逆者。/ She had been a rebel at school. 她在學校時就是個叛逆者。/ He's a bit of a rebel (= he doesn't like to obey rules). 他有點桀驁不馴。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**reactionary**</span> [riˈækʃənri; 美 -neri]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [貶] 反對政治（或社會）變革者，如反動分子：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**traitor**</span> [ˈtreɪtə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (to sb/sth) 背叛朋友、國家等的一個人，如背叛者；叛徒；賣國賊：</span>He was seen as a traitor to the socialist cause. 他被視為社會主義事業的叛徒。/ Some say he's a traitor to the working class. 一些人說他是工人階級的叛徒。/ She denied that she had turned traitor (= become a traitor). 她否認自己叛變了。/ rumours that there were traitors among us who were sending messages to the enemy 關於我們中間有叛徒向敵人通風報信的傳言 / He turned traitor and joined the opposition. 他叛變並加入了反對派。

相關詞義延伸：
· enemy還可指敵國，敵軍。

周邊詞彙補充：
· subversive可指（對政府或政治制度的）顛覆份子、破壞分子。
