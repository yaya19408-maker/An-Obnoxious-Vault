☀ <span class="category">**打算、計劃的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**plan**</span> [plæn] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 1 plan (sth) (for sth) 對自己將來想要做的事情做出詳細安排、計劃：</span>to plan a trip 計劃一次旅行 / We planned the day down to the last detail. 我們極為詳細地安排了這一天的日程。/ a centrally planned economies 中央計劃經濟 / We must plan for the future. 我們必須為將來作好打算。/ Everything went exactly as planned. 一切都嚴格按計劃進行。/ A meeting has been planned for early next year. 會議計劃明年年初召開。<span class="definition">2 plan (on sth/on doing sth) 打算或期待（去做某事）：</span>They plan to arrive some time after three. 他們預計在三點鐘以後到達。/ We’re planning a trip to France next month? 我們打算下個月去法國旅遊。<span class="definition">vt. 為某事做出設計、規劃、策劃（如規劃一個大致方案或藍圖）：</span>to plan an essay, to plan a garden 構思一篇文章；設計一個花園 / a well-planned campaign 一場精心策劃的宣傳活動

<span class="vocabulary">**intend**</span> [ɪn'tend] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（在頭腦中對要做的事情）計劃，打算。極為常用：</span>I never intended to hurt you. 我從來都沒想傷害你。/ The bomb was probably intended for him. 那顆炸彈很可能是要炸他。/ It is intended that production will start at the end of the month. 計劃月底將開始生產。

<span class="vocabulary">**mean**</span> [mi:n] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. mean sth (as sth) / mean what ... / mean sth for sb 有…目的或打算。相當於intend。也較為常用，與intend很多情況下可互換：</span>I didn’t mean you to read the letter. 我沒打算讓你看那封信。/ The chair was clearly meant for a child. 這椅子顯然是專為兒童預備的。/ He means what he says. 他說話是當真的。/ Don’t laugh! I mean it. 別笑！我是認真的。/ He means trouble. 他存心搗亂。

<span class="vocabulary">**aim**</span> [eɪm] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 美式英語可指決定或想要做某事，即打算，為非正式用法：</span>He’s didn’t aim to get caught. 他並不想被抓到。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**propose**</span> [prəˈpəʊz; 美 -ˈpoʊz]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 打算、計劃（去做某事）：</span>What do you propose to do now? 現在你打算做什麼？/ It's still far from clear what action the government proposes to take over the affair. 政府打算採取何種行動處理該事件仍然十分不明朗。/ And where do you propose building such a huge thing? 那麼你打算在哪裡建這麼一個龐然大物呢？/ How do you propose getting home? 你打算怎麼回家？

<span class="vocabulary">**think**</span> [θɪŋk] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 打算，計劃：</span>I think I’ll go for a swim. 我想去游泳。/ I’m thinking in terms of about 70 guests at the wedding. 我打算邀請70位嘉賓參加婚禮。

<span class="vocabulary">**meditate**</span> [ˈmedɪteɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 在頭腦中策劃某事、考慮去做某事：</span>They were meditating revenge. 他們在謀劃進行報復。

<span class="vocabulary">**design**</span> [dɪ'zaɪn] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 思考並規劃一個體系（或一種做某事的方式等），可稱為計劃、籌劃、制定：</span>We need to design a new syllabus for the third year. 我們需要為三年級學生制訂一個新的課程大綱。<span class="definition">2 design sth (for sth) / design sth (as sth) 常用被動式，（為某一目的或用途而）打算：</span>The programme is designed to help people who have been out of work for a long time. 這項計劃的目的是為長期失業者提供幫助。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**devise**</span> [dɪˈvaɪz]
<span class="definition">vt. 策劃新東西或做某事的新方法：</span>New long-range objectives must be devised. 必須規劃出新的長期目標。           

<span class="vocabulary">**plot**</span> [plɒt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 制定策略或行動方案、步驟：</span>to plot a survival strategy for the party 制定該黨的生存策略 / to plot a course of action 制定行動方案

<span class="vocabulary">**chart**</span> [tʃɑːt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 制定一個行動計劃：</span>to chart the marketing plan for this area 針對該地區制定營銷計劃
           
<span class="vocabulary">**frame**</span> [freɪm]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 擬定，制定（計劃或制度）：</span>After the war, a convention was set up to frame a constitution. 戰後，為制定憲法組織了一次會議。

<span class="vocabulary">**programme**</span> ['prəʊɡræm]（英）（美用program）
<span class="definition">vt. programme sth (for sth) 規劃一件事，尤指規劃一系列事件中的一件事。規劃的物件常為長期性、系統性的專案或工程。常用被動式：</span>The final section of road is programmed for completion next month. 最後一段道路計劃於下月竣工。

<span class="vocabulary">**project**</span> ['prɒdʒekt, 'prəʊ-] 
<span class="definition">vt. 為一項未來的活動或工程等擬定計劃，即規劃，計劃，常用被動式：</span>The next edition of the book is projected for publication in March. 本書的下一版計劃於三月發行。/ The projected housing development will go ahead next year. 計劃中的住宅建設將於明年動工。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**contrive**</span> [kənˈtraɪv]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式]（用欺騙的手法）巧妙地策劃：</span>They contrived a plan to defraud the company. 他們精心策劃要欺詐那家公司。/ They somehow contrived to lose tens of thousands of applications. 他們精心策劃故意遺失了數萬份申請書。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**formulate**</span> [ˈfɔ:mjuleɪt; 美 ˈfɔ:rm-]
<span class="definition">vt. 仔細規劃、構想、準備、制訂：</span>to formulate a policy, to formulate a theory, to formulate a proposal 制訂政策；創立理論；準備建議 / Little by little, he formulated his plan for escape. 他一點一點地設計構思出了逃跑的方案。

<span class="vocabulary">**be**</span> [bi:] 
<span class="definition">auxiliary verb. be to do sth 用於表示對事情的安排或計劃：</span>They are to be married in June. 他們計劃於六月份結婚。

<span class="vocabulary">**will**</span> [wɪl] 
<span class="definition">modal verb. 想要去做某事（表目的）：</span>Will you be remaining in the city? 你想留在這座城市嗎？

<span class="vocabulary">**could**</span> [kʊd] 
<span class="definition">modal verb. 表示嘴上說要做實際上沒有做（說話時帶有強烈感情）：</span>I could kill you! I swear I could! 我要殺了你！我發誓我一定要殺了你。/ “Welcome back” was all they said. I could have kissed them! “歡迎回來，”他們只說了這一句。我本想親吻他們的。

☀ <span class="category">**打算、計劃、戰略、策略的名詞和形容詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**plan**</span> [plæn] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] plan (for sth) / plan (to do sth) 打算去做（或去實現、完成）的事情，即計劃，打算：</span>Do you have any plans for the summer? 這個夏天你有什麼打算？/ There are no plans to build new offices. 現在沒有建新辦公樓的計劃。<span class="definition">2 [C] plan (for sth) / plan (to do sth) 為達成某事（尤指已經提前做過詳細考慮的事情）而需要去做的一系列事情，可稱為安排，方案，規劃：</span>The government has ambitious plans for prison reform. 對於監獄改革，政府有宏偉的計劃。/ three-year plan 三年的規劃 / What are your immediate plans? 你當前有什麼規劃？/ action, battle, strategic plan 行動計劃；作戰計劃；戰略規劃 / If Plan A fails, go to Plan B. 若甲計劃失敗，就執行乙計劃。/ career, corporate, development plan 職業規劃；公司規劃；發展規劃 / financial plan 財務規劃 / travel plan 旅行計劃 / marriage plan 結婚計劃 / treatment plan 治療方案 / training plan 訓練計劃 / emergency plan 應急方案 <span class="definition">3 [C] 尤用於構成複合詞，為未來而投資的一種方式：</span>pension, retirement plan 養老金計劃；退休金計劃 / saving plan 儲蓄計劃 / insurance plan 保險計劃 / payment plan 付款計劃 
               
<span class="vocabulary">**planning**</span> [ˈplænɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 制定計劃的行為或過程：</span>The trip calls for careful advance planning. 這次旅行需要提前精心計劃一下。/ Costs can be reduced by effective contingency planning. 有效的應急計劃可降低成本。/ the Soviet system of central planning 蘇聯的中央計劃體系 / corporate planning 企業策劃 / family planning 計劃生育 / They give free advice on contraception at the family planning clinic. 計劃生育門診部提供免費的避孕措施諮詢。/ business, economic, educational, environmental, financial, military, strategic 商業規劃；經濟計劃；教育規劃；環境規劃；財務規劃；軍事計劃；戰略規劃 / retirement planning 退休規劃 / estate planning（尤美）遺產規劃 / an attorney who specializes in estate planning 專業遺產處置規劃律師 / This can be done, but it will take careful planning. 這是可以做到的，但要制訂細緻的規劃。/ The industry needs to do some long-term planning. 這一行業需要制訂一些長期規劃。/ planning phase, planning stage 規劃階段 / The idea is still at the planning stage.這個想法仍處於規劃階段。/ The new system is still in the planning stages. 新體系仍處於規劃階段。/ planning process 規劃過程/ planning meeting籌劃會議 / planning approval, planning consent, planning permission（均尤英）建築許可 / We've applied for planning permission to build an extension to the house. 為了擴建住房，我們申請了建築許可證。/ The festival was four years in the planning. 這個藝術節已經籌劃四年了。<span class="definition">2 [C] 城鎮規劃，相當於town planning：</span>city, town, urban planning 城市規劃；城鎮規劃 / a masterpiece of 18th-century town planning 18世紀城鎮規劃的傑作 / planning authority（英）, planning committee（尤英）, planning department（尤英）規劃局；規劃委員會；規劃署 / He supported the planning authority in refusing the application because of pressures on the Green Belt. 他支援城鎮規劃部門拒絕該項申請，因為這會給綠化帶帶來損害。/ Planning for future development is vital for the community. 為將來的發展進行規劃對社群來說是至關重要的。

<span class="vocabulary">**scheme**</span> [ski:m]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] [英] ~ (for doing sth) / ~ (to do sth)（政府或其他組織機構做某事或組織某事的）計劃：</span>incentive, insurance, pension, recycling, share scheme 獎勵計劃；保險計劃；養老金計劃；回收利用計劃；共享計劃 / a local scheme for recycling newspapers 當地的報紙回收計劃 / a training scheme for unemployed teenagers 失業青少年培訓計劃 / He has an ingenious scheme to attract funding. 他有一套吸引資金的獨特計劃。/ The government set up a scheme of limited public health assistance in 1992. 1992年，政府制訂了一個有限公共保健資助計劃。/ a government-backed scheme 政府資助的計劃 / The scheme allows customers to trade in their own computer against the cost of a new one. 這一方案讓顧客用原有的電腦衝抵一部分購買新電腦的費用。/ a scheme involving local libraries 涉及當地圖書館的計劃 / The scheme applies to families with three or more children. 這一計劃適用於有三個或三個以上孩子的家庭。/ The scheme has been given approval to go ahead. 計劃已獲准實施。/ Schools in the scheme will receive an annual grant. 參與此計劃的學校每年將獲得一筆撥款。/ Under the scheme, land would be sold to building companies. 根據這一計劃，土地將出售給建築公司。/ a scheme whereby the elderly will be provided with help in the home 為老年人居家生活提供幫助的方案 / to introduce a scheme to improve links between schools and industry 推行加強學校和業界之間聯絡的方案 <span class="definition">2 [C]（設計或組織…的）方案、體系、體制：</span>colour, decorating（美）, lighting, paint scheme 配色方案；裝修方案；照明方案；塗料方案 / rhyme scheme 押韻格式 / The poem's rhyme scheme, which Dante invented, is known ass 'terza rima'. 這首詩的韻格由但丁自創，稱為“三行體”。/ classification, labelling/labeling, naming, numbering scheme 分類方案；標識方案；命名方案；計數方案 / conceptual, organizational, regulatory shceme 概念圖式；組織架構；管理機制
           
<span class="vocabulary">**proposal**</span> [prəˈpəʊzl; 美 -ˈpoʊzl]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] ~ (for sth) / ~ (to do sth) / ~ (that…) 一份正式的計劃，即提議、動議；也可指提出正式建議的行為：</span>The President is to put forward new proposals for resolving the country's constitutional crisis. 總統將提出解決國家憲法危機的新議案。/ peace, reform, research proposal 和平計劃；改革計劃；研究計劃 / formal proposal 正式提案 / The government outlined a new set of proposals on human rights. 政府簡要列出了一套新的人權提案。/ I welcome the proposal to reduce taxes for the poorly paid. 我對減少低收入者納稅這一提議表示歡迎。/ The government tried to push through the controversial proposals. 政府盡力讓這個有爭議的提案獲得透過。/ They judged that the time was right for the proposal of new terms for the trade agreement. 他們判斷，提出貿易協定新條款的時機成熟了。/ the government's proposals to abolish free health care 政府關於廢除免費醫療保健的提案  
           
<span class="vocabulary">**motion**</span> [ˈməʊʃn; 美 ˈmoʊʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一場會議上討論或表決的一項正式提議、動議：</span>to introduce a motion（尤美）, to table a motion（英）, to put forward a motion（英）, to put a motion（英）, to propose a motion 提出一項動議 / The motion was adopted/carried/passed by six votes to one. 這項提議以六比一的票數透過。/ The motion was defeated by 51 votes to 43. 這項動議以51票對43票被否決。/ The conference is now debating the motion and will vote on it shortly. 大會正在就提議進行辯論，稍後將就此表決。/ Opposition parties are likely to bring a no-confidence motion against the government. 反對黨很可能提出對政府的不信任案。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**initiative**</span> [ɪˈnɪʃətɪv]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（為解決某個問題或為達成某個目標的）新方案、倡議：</span>a United Nations peace initiative 聯合國的和平倡議 / a government initiative to combat unemployment 政府應付失業問題的新方案 / Government initiatives to help young people have been inadequate. 政府在積極幫助年輕人方面做得還不夠。/ fresh initiatives to find a peaceful end to the conflict 和平解決爭端的各項新方案 / pioneering initiatives in bioengineering 生物工程的開拓件新舉措 / collaborative, cooperative, joint initiative 協作倡議；合作計劃；聯合方案 / grass-roots initiative 基層的倡議 / strategic initiative 戰略方案 / business, e-business, e-commerce, marketing initiative 經營新思路；電子商務新思路；新的營銷方案 / diplomatic, economic, legislative, peace, policy, political initiative 新的外交舉措；新的經濟計劃；新法案；和平倡議；新的政策方案；新的政治舉措 / safety, training initiative 新的安全方案；新的培訓計劃 / development, research initiative 開發方案；研究方案 / education, educational, health initiative 新的教育方案；新的健康計劃 / conservation, environmental initiative 新的環保倡議 / reform initiative 改革方案 / The government has launched a new policy initiative. 政府已經開始推行一項新政策。/ Ten schools have been involved in the initiative. 有十所學校參與了新計劃。

<span class="vocabulary">**schedule**</span> ['ʃedju:l; 'skedӡu:l] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 工作計劃，日程安排（上面列出了必須要做的事和何時做何事）；還可指計劃的時間：</span>I have a hectic schedule for the next few days. 我今後幾天的日程緊得要命。/ Filming began on schedule. 拍攝如期開始。/ weekly schedule 每週的安排 / delivery, production, work schedule 送貨計劃表；生產進度；工作進度

<span class="vocabulary">**intention**</span> [ɪn'tenʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] intention (of doing sth) / intention (to do sth) intention (that ...) 打算，計劃：</span>He left England with the intention of travelling in Africa. 他離開英格蘭，打算去非洲旅行。/ I have no intention of changing jobs. 我無意變換工作。/ The authority has announced its intention to crack down on illegal parking. 官方宣佈了打擊違章停車的計劃。

<span class="vocabulary">**design**</span> [dɪ'zaɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 一個計劃、打算、目的或意圖：</span>It is all part of his grand design. 這都是他那宏大計劃的一部分。/ evil design 邪惡的意圖 / Had it happened by accident or by design? 這件事是意外還是人為策劃的？

<span class="vocabulary">**prescription**</span> [prɪ'skrɪpʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [正式] prescription (for sth)（為解決問題或改進狀況等所提出的）方案，提議，建議，計劃。不太常用：</span>economic prescriptions 經濟方案 / a prescription for happiness 增進幸福的建議

<span class="vocabulary">**programme**</span> ['prəʊɡræm]（英）（美用program）
<span class="definition">n. [C] 對某事（可以是將要得到執行的事，也可以是在…發展中的一些事）的一項計劃，也可叫方案。通常為一種長期的、系統性的計劃：</span>a varied programme of entertainment 豐富多彩的娛樂活動計劃 / pilot programme 試點方案 / party, political programme 政黨綱領；政治綱領 / joint programme 合作計劃 / action, development, research programme 行動方案；發展方案；研究方案 / expenditure, investment, marketing programme 開支計劃；投資計劃；營銷計劃 / national recovery programme 國家復甦計劃 / a programme of economic reform 一項經濟改革方案 / aid programme 援助計劃 / screening programme 篩查計劃 / inpatient programme 住院計劃 / nuclear, rebuilding programme 核計劃；重建計劃 / weight-loss programme 減肥計劃 / education, training programme 教育計劃；培訓計劃

<span class="vocabulary">**project**</span> ['prɒdʒekt, 'prəʊ-] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 某人所感興趣的（或想要引起人們關注的）一系列目標、想法或活動，即方案，計劃：</span>a political project 政治方案 / investment project 投資方案 / an ambitious project to translate all the works of Plato 一項翻譯柏拉圖所有著作的艱鉅計劃 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**strategy**</span> [ˈstrætədʒi]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 制定計劃（或巧妙地實施計劃）的過程，如策劃、規劃、統籌安排：</span>marketing strategy 營銷策劃
           
<span class="vocabulary">**protocol**</span> [ˈprəʊtəkɒl; 美 ˈproʊtəkɔ:l; -kɑ:l]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [術語] 科學實驗計劃；醫療方案：</span>the detoxification protocol 戒毒方案           

<span class="vocabulary">**blueprint**</span> [ˈblu:prɪnt]
