☀ <span class="category">**令人愉悅的形容詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**happy**</span> ['hæpɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1（事物）給予快樂的，使人快樂的：</span>happy marriage, happy memory, happy childhood, happy days 幸福的婚姻；幸福的回憶；幸福的童年；幸福的日子 / The story has a happy ending. 故事的結局很圓滿。<span class="definition">2 用於Happy Birthday，Happy New Year等，表示希望對方擁有一個快樂的慶祝儀式的，即生日快樂；新年快樂等：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**merry**</span> ['merɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. Merry Christmas 用於表示希望對方聖誕節快樂：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**enjoyable**</span> [ɪn'dӡɒɪəbl] 
<span class="definition">adj.（事物）有樂趣的，令人愉快的。通常持續時間偏長，常發自內心，有一種略為深沉的滿足感：</span>an enjoyable weekend, enjoyable experience 令人愉快的週末；令人愉悅的經歷 / It was much more enjoyable than I had expected. 這比我預期的有趣得多。/ the most enjoyable activity they did 他們做的最有趣的活動 / I always try to make my lessons enjoyable. 我總是設法使我的課有意思。/ We had a most enjoyable evening. 我們度過了一個非常愉快的夜晚。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**agreeable**</span> [əˈgri:əbl]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式]（某事物）令人愉快的、宜人的、討人喜歡的：</span>We spent a most agreeable day together. 我們在一起度過了非常愉快的一天。/ workers in more agreeable and better paid occupations 在條件更愜意、報酬更豐厚的行業工作的人 / an agreeable surprise 一個驚喜 / It all sounds very agreeable. 這一切聽起來讓人很愉快。/ It was a most agreeable evening. 那是一個十分令人愉快的夜晚。

<span class="vocabulary">**cheerful**</span> ['tʃɪəfl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 令人愉快的：</span>a bright, cheerful restaurant 明亮宜人的餐館 / cheerful colours 亮麗的色彩 / a chatty, cheerful letter 一封令人愉快的拉家常的信
           
<span class="vocabulary">**gorgeous**</span> [ˈgɔ:dʒəs; 美 ˈgɔ:rdʒəs]
<span class="definition">adj. [非正式]（某事物）令人愉悅的：</span>gorgeous weather (= warm and with a lot of sun) 宜人的天氣 / It's a gorgeous day. 這天過得很愉快。

<span class="vocabulary">**glad**</span> [ɡlæd] 
<span class="definition">adj. [舊時] 只用於名詞前，令人愉快的，充滿歡樂的：</span>glad news, glad tidings 令人愉快的訊息；喜訊

<span class="vocabulary">**nice**</span> [naɪs] 
<span class="definition">adj. 用於形容詞或副詞前以強調某事物是多麼令人愉悅：</span>a nice hot bath 舒舒服服的熱水浴 / a nice long walk 長時間很愉快的散步 / It was nice and warm yesterday. 昨天的天氣暖洋洋的。/ Everyone arrived nice and early. 大家都早早地到了。

<span class="vocabulary">**pleasant**</span> ['plezənt] 
<span class="definition">adj. 令人愉快的；吸引人的：</span>pleasant climate, pleasant evening, pleasant place 令人愉快的氣候；令人愉快的夜晚；令人愉快的地方 / music that is pleasant to the ear 悅耳的音樂 / a pleasant environment to work in 舒適的工作環境 / a pleasant experience 愉快的經歷 / It was pleasant to be alone again. 又只剩下一個人了，真自在。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pleasing**</span> [ˈpli:zɪŋ]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (to sb/sth) 令人愉快的；令人滿意的：</span>This area of France has a pleasing climate in August. 法國的這一地區在8月份氣候宜人。/ Such a view is pleasing. 這樣的景色令人心曠神怡。/ The new building was pleasing to the eye. 這座新樓真漂亮。/ a pleasing design 令人滿意的設計 / a design which is pleasing to the eye 悅目的圖案 / aesthetically pleasing combinations 賞心悅目的搭配

<span class="vocabulary">**pleasurable**</span> [ˈpleʒərəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. 令人愉悅的：</span>The whole experience was intensely pleasurable. 整個體驗都是非常愉悅的。/ The most pleasurable experience of the evening was the wonderful fireworks display. 這個晚上最讓人高興的就是精彩的焰火表演。/ We do everything we can to make your trip pleasurable. 我們會盡力使你的旅途愉快。/ He found sailing more pleasurable than skiing. 他覺得駕駛帆船比滑雪更好玩。/ activities which they find pleasurable and rewarding 他們覺得愉快而有益的活動 / Summertime gardening can be very pleasurable. 夏天在園子裡幹活兒有時是非常愉快的。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**delightful**</span> [dɪˈlaɪtfl]
<span class="definition">adj.（某事物或某人）令人愉快的、使人高興的：</span>delightful book, delightful restaurant, delightful town 令人愉快的書；舒適的餐館；宜人的城市 / It has been a most delightful evening. 這是個非常愉快的夜晚。/ It was a delightful little fishing village. 那是個宜人的小漁村。/ It was the most delightful garden I had ever seen. 這是我所見過的最惹人喜愛的花園。/ She remembered Lucy as beautiful, charming and absolutely delightful. 她記得的露西漂亮、迷人，而且非常討人喜歡。/ a delightful child 討人喜歡的孩子

<span class="vocabulary">**good**</span> [ɡʊd] 
<span class="definition">adj. 令人愉快的：</span>Did you have a good time in London? 你在倫敦過得愉快嗎？/ It’s good to see you again. 再次見到你真高興。/ This is very good news. 這訊息真叫人高興。/ It’s a good thing (= it’s lucky) you came early. 幸好你來得早。

<span class="vocabulary">**delicious**</span> [dɪ'lɪʃəs] 
<span class="definition">adj. [文學] 極其令人愉悅（或開心、享受）的：</span>the delicious coolness of the breeze 微風送爽

<span class="vocabulary">**grand**</span> [ɡrænd] 
<span class="definition">adj. [方言或非正式] 非常令人享受的：</span>I had a grand day out at the seaside. 我在海邊痛痛快快玩了一天。

<span class="vocabulary">**sweet**</span> [swi:t] 
<span class="definition">adj. 令人感到愉快或滿意的：</span>Goodnight. Sweet dreams. 晚安。祝你做個甜蜜的夢。/ I can’t tell you how sweet this victory is. 取得這場勝利，別提多痛快了。/ He’s promised her a new car, just to keep her sweet. 他答應給她一輛新轎車，只是為了讓她高興。

<span class="vocabulary">**convivial**</span> [kənˈvɪviəl]
<span class="definition">adj.（氣氛或性格）歡快友好的：</span>convivial evening, convivial atmosphere 歡樂的夜晚；歡快的氣氛 / convivial company 快樂的夥伴 / The atmosphere was quite convivial. 氣氛非常輕鬆愉快。   

<span class="vocabulary">**welcome**</span> ['welkəm] 
<span class="definition">adj. 你很高興擁有或收到等的，即令人愉悅的，受歡迎的：</span>Your letter was very welcome. 很高興收到你的來信。/ The fine weather made a welcome change. 天氣轉晴，令人心曠神怡。/ The 1% rate cut is very welcome. 費率下降1個百分點很受歡迎。 

☀ <span class="category">**令人愉悅的名詞和動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**delight**</span> [dɪ'laɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 令人高興的事，即樂事，樂趣，相當於joy：</span>the delights of living in the country 生活在鄉村的樂趣 / culinary delight 烹飪的樂趣 / earthly delight 塵世間的樂事 / This guitar is a delight to play. 這吉他彈起來很愜意。/ The baby was a constant delight. 這個嬰兒不斷給人帶來喜悅。<span class="definition">vt. 給予某人很多快樂或享受：</span>This news will delight his fans all over the world. 這訊息將使全世界崇拜他的人都感到高興。

<span class="vocabulary">**joy**</span> [dӡɒɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 令人高興的一個人或事物，如樂事，樂趣。也相當於delight。一般也可互換：</span>the joys of fatherhood 做父親的樂趣 / The game was a joy to watch. 這比賽看起來真開心。/ joys and sorrows 快樂與悲傷

<span class="vocabulary">**pleasure**</span> ['pleӡə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 使人高興或滿足的事情或事物，即樂事，快事。與joy和delight難見差別：</span>the pleasures and pains of everyday life 日常生活的苦與樂 / the simple pleasures of the countryside 鄉村淳樸的樂趣 / pleasure boat 遊艇
            
<span class="vocabulary">**blast**</span> [blɑ:st; 美 blæst]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [尤美] 非常熱鬧開心的一次經歷：</span>The party was a blast. 聚會非常熱鬧。/ We had a blast at the party. 我們在聚會上玩得很開心。/ Making the album was a real blast. 做這個專輯真是一種樂趣。          

<span class="vocabulary">**luxury**</span> [ˈlʌkʃəri]
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.] 不常有機會去享受的樂趣或優勢：</span>We had the luxury of being able to choose from four good candidates for the job. 我們的挑選餘地大，有四位出色的職位候選人可供選擇。/ He had the luxury of spending time at his family's summer home. 他難得有在自家的避暑別墅消遣的機會。/ We were going to have the luxury of a free weekend, to rest and do whatever we pleased. 我們將有一個難得空閒的週末，可以休息，還可以高興幹什麼就幹什麼。/ Hot baths are my favourite luxury. 泡個熱水澡是我的最大樂事。/ It was sheer luxury to step into a hot bath. 泡個熱水澡真是一種享受。/ the ultimate luxury of a sauna in your own home 在自己家裡洗桑拿的頂級享受

<span class="vocabulary">**treat**</span> [tri:t] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 讓人快樂或享受的事（尤指你給予某人的或為其所做的令其享受的事）：</span>I’ll take you to the cinema as a special treat. 我帶你到電影院享受一下。/ Chocolate is a treat for me. 吃巧克力對我來說是一種享受。/ Let’s go out for dinner, my treat. 咱們到外面去吃飯吧，我請客。/ birthday treat（尤英）生日宴請 / family treat 家庭宴請 / frozen treat（美）（甜點等）冷凍的美食 / musical, visual treat 音樂盛宴；視覺盛宴 / Snails are a tasty treat for hedgehogs. 蝸牛是刺蝟的佳餚。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**sweetness**</span> [ˈswi:tnəs]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 令人愉悅、討人喜歡：</span>a smile of great sweetness 十分甜蜜的微笑 / cloying（尤英）, saccharine（尤英）, sugary（尤美）膩人的甜蜜；虛情假意的甜蜜；甜言蜜語 / She smiled with saccharine sweetness. 她的嫣然笑容裡只有虛情假意。/ to taste the sweetness of military success 品嚐軍事勝利的喜悅 / all sweetness and light 和藹可親 / People think she's all ~ and light, but she actually has a temper. 人們都認為她親切和善，但實際上她的脾氣很環。/ Her voice was dripping with sweetness.（drip with尤為美式用法）她的聲音充滿甜蜜。

<span class="vocabulary">**spoil**</span> [spɔɪl]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb/yourself 透過做一件特別的事而讓某人或自己高興，如犒賞：</span>Why not spoil yourself with a weekend in a top hotel? 為什麼不到頂級飯店度個週末，讓自己享受享受呢？/ Spoil yourself with a new perfume this summer. 今年夏天買瓶新香水慰勞一下自己吧。/ Perhaps I could employ someone to iron his shirts, but I wanted to spoil him. He was my man. 也許我可以僱別人來熨他的襯衫，但我想好好寵他。他是我愛的男人。/ He really spoiled me on my birthday. 我過生日那天他真讓我受寵若驚。

<span class="vocabulary">**please**</span> [pli:z] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 使某人愉快（或滿意）：</span>You can’t please everybody. 你無法讓每個人都滿意。/ I did it to please my parents. 我這麼做是要讓父母高興。/ He’s a difficult man to please. 他是個難以取悅的人。/ He’s always very eager to please. 他總是極力想討好別人。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**brighten**</span> [ˈbraɪtn]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth) (up)（使）喜笑顏開、面露喜色、快活起來：</span>He brightened up at their words of encouragement. 聽到他們鼓勵的話，他高興起來。/ She brightened up a little at the thought of the cruise. 一想到要乘船遊覽，她臉上便露出一絲喜色。/ A smile brightened her face. 她的臉上露出了燦爛的笑容。/ Seeing him, she seemed to brighten a little. 看到他後，她似乎露出點兒喜色。/ 'Oh, we'd love to!' cried Nancy, her face brightening. “哦，我們很樂意！”南希面露喜色，大聲說道。/ Their rather heavy faces brightened visibly. 他們表情凝重的臉上顯然露出了喜色。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**tickle**</span> [ˈtɪkl]
<span class="definition">vt. 使高興：</span>I was tickled to discover that we'd both done the same thing. 我高興地發現我倆在做同樣的事。

☀ <span class="category">**高興、快樂、愉悅、幸福的形容詞和副詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**happy**</span> ['hæpɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. happy (to do sth) / happy (for sb) / happy (that ...) 感到快樂的；顯得快樂的；高興的。是一個基礎用詞，含義較為廣泛，日常生活也中極為常用：</span>happy smile, happy face 快樂的微笑；快樂的面容 / I’m very happy for you. 我真為你感到高興。/ Outwardly the couple appeared happy. 表面上看，這對夫妻顯得很幸福。/ Money won’t make you happy. 錢不能使人幸福。

<span class="vocabulary">**glad**</span> [ɡlæd] 
<span class="definition">adj. glad (about sth) / glad (to do sth) / glad (to know, hear, see ...) / glad (that ...) 高興，愉快。詞義色彩與delighted較為接近，不過delighted比glad略為明快，二者有時可互換：</span>I’m so glad (that) you’re safe! 你安然無恙我真高興！/ I’m just glad it’s all over. 我十分高興這一切都結束了。/ She was secretly glad of his company. 有他陪伴，她暗自歡喜。/ The smell of the sea air makes you glad to be alive! 呼吸一下海邊的空氣會使你感到活著是件非常快活的事！

<span class="vocabulary">**delighted**</span> [dɪ'laɪtɪd] 
<span class="definition">adj. delighted (to do sth) / delighted (that ...) / delighted (by/at/with sth) 非常高興的，快樂的，愉快的。程度較深，持續時間較短，且有一點俏皮靈動的色彩：</span>delighted smile 愉快的微笑 / I was delighted that you could stay. 你能留下來我很高興。/ ‘Poor Jane,’ she said, although she was secretly delighted.“可憐的簡。”她說，儘管她在偷著樂。/ He’s really delighted with his new MP3 player. 他對他的新MP3播放器愛不釋手。

<span class="vocabulary">**cheerful**</span> ['tʃɪəfl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 快樂並把自己的快樂形諸於外的，即興高采烈的。具有快樂和振奮的雙重屬性：</span>We tried to keep him cheerful. 我們盡力讓他高高興興的。/ a cheerful, hard-working employee 快快樂樂勤奮工作的僱員 / cheerful smile, cheerful voice 歡快的微笑；歡快的說話聲 / falsely cheerful 假裝高興（尤美）

<span class="vocabulary">**pleased**</span> [pli:zd] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 pleased (with sb/sth) / pleased that ... 對某事感到高興（或愉快、滿意）的：</span>She was very pleased with her exam results. 她對自己的考試成績非常滿意。/ I’m really pleased that you’re feeling better. 你覺得好些了，我真高興。/ I’m very pleased for you both. 我為你們倆感到高興。<span class="definition">2 pleased to do sth 高興（或樂意）去做某事。與happy、glad難見差別：</span>I was pleased to hear you’ve been promoted. 聽說你高升了，我很高興。/ I am always pleased to hear from former students. 收到以前學生的來信，我總是很高興。/ Pleased to meet you.（尤英）很高興認識你。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**elated**</span> [iˈleɪtɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (at/by sth)（因為好事已經發生或將要發生而）興高采烈的；歡欣鼓舞的；喜氣洋洋的：</span>They were elated at the result. 他們對這一結果感到歡欣鼓舞。/ I was elated by the prospect of the new job ahead. 我為眼前新工作的前景所鼓舞。/ I was elated that my second heart bypass had been successful. 第二次心臟搭橋手術成功了，我很高興。/ 'That was one of the best races of my life,' said an elated Hakkinen. “那是我一生中表現最好的比賽之一，”哈基寧興高采烈地說。/ I felt strangely elated at the news. 聽到這個訊息我不知為什麼很高興。/ elated by our victory 為我們的勝利感到興高采烈 / elated with his success 為他的成功感到歡欣鼓舞

<span class="vocabulary">**bright**</span> [braɪt] 
<span class="definition">adj. 快樂而生氣勃勃的：</span>His eyes were bright and excited. 他目光發亮，興奮不已。/ She gave me a bright smile. 她對我粲然一笑。/ Why are you so bright and cheerful today? 你今天怎麼這麼高興？/ His face was bright with excitement. 他興奮得滿臉放光。

<span class="vocabulary">**overjoyed**</span> [ˌəʊvəˈdʒɔɪd; 美 ˌoʊvərˈdʒ-]
<span class="definition">adj. [名詞前不可用] ~ (at sth/to do sth) / ~ (that…) 極高興的、欣喜若狂的：</span>Shelley was overjoyed to see me. 謝利見到我萬分高興。/ He was overjoyed at his son's return. 他看到兒子回來欣喜若狂。/ He was overjoyed at my success. 我的成功使他欣喜若狂。/ Patrick was overjoyed with his success. 帕特里克因成功而欣喜若狂。/ We were overjoyed to hear their good news. 聽到他們的好訊息，我們都大喜過望。/ She was overjoyed that her article had been published. 她的文章發表了，這使她高興極了。/ She was not exactly overjoyed to see us again. 她再次見到我們一點兒也不高興。/ They're all overjoyed about it. 他們都為這件事感到非常高興。/ He was overjoyed at the thought of having a son. 想到會有個兒子，他欣喜若狂。/ We are overjoyed by this decision. 這個決定令我們大喜過望。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**triumphant**</span> [traɪˈʌmfənt]
<span class="definition">adj. 對勝利或成功表現出的巨大滿足或愉悅，如歡欣鼓舞的、得意揚揚的：</span>a triumphant smile 得意揚揚的笑容 / Duncan and his triumphant soldiers celebrate their military victory. 鄧肯和他那些歡欣鼓舞計程車兵們慶祝他們取得了軍事勝利。/ The captain's voice was triumphant. 上校的聲音聽上去萬分得意。/ In the painting, St George stands triumphant over the dragon. 在這幅作品中，聖喬治戰勝了龍而顯得得意揚揚。

<span class="vocabulary">**light**</span> [laɪt] 
<span class="definition">adj. 常作前置定語，指無憂無慮的；愉快的；快活的：</span>I left the island with a light heart. 我懷著愉快的心情離開了那個小島。

<span class="vocabulary">**fine**</span> [faɪn] 
<span class="definition">adj. 開心的：</span>“How are you?” “Fine, thank you.” “你好嗎？”“好，謝謝。”

<span class="vocabulary">**sunny**</span> ['sʌnɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. 歡樂的，快樂的：</span>a sunny disposition 開朗的性情

<span class="vocabulary">**merry**</span> ['merɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. 愉快的，高興的，相當於cheery。不常用：</span>a merry grin 愉快的笑

<span class="definition">adj. content (with sth) 不作前置定語，指（對自己所擁有的東西）滿意的，滿足的。是此義的常規用詞，極為常用：</span>I’m perfectly content just to lie in the sun. 只要能在太陽下躺一躺，我就心滿意足了。/ She seemed quite content with the idea. 她對這個主意似乎相當滿意。/ He had to be content with third place. 他只好屈居第三名。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**contented**</span> [kənˈtentɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. [常用於名詞前]（尤指因生活好而）滿意的、滿足的、愜意的：</span>a contented smile 愜意的微笑 / He was a contented man. 他是個心滿意足的人。/ I felt perfectly contented. 我感到十分滿意。/ Whenever he returns to this place he is happy and contented. 每次回到這個地方，他都十分快樂，心滿意足。/ She was gazing at him with a soft, contented smile on her face. 她注視著他，臉上帶著溫柔而滿足的微笑。

<span class="vocabulary">**happy**</span> ['hæpɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. happy (with/about/ sb/sth)（因某人或某事物的好或正確、恰當等而）對其滿意的；放心的（即不擔心、不焦慮的）。也很常用：</span>Are you happy with that arrangement? 你對這一安排感到滿意嗎？/ She was happy enough with her performance. 她對自己的表現還算滿意。/ I’m not too happy about her attitude. 我對她的態度不太滿意。

<span class="vocabulary">**flattered**</span> ['flætəd]
<span class="definition">adj.（發生的某件事讓某人因為感到受重視而）感到滿意的：</span>She was flattered by Roberto's long letter. 羅伯託的一封長信讓她很是高興。

<span class="vocabulary">**satisfied**</span> [ˈsætɪsfaɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (with sb/sth) 滿意的、滿足的：</span>a satisfied smile 滿意的微笑 / a satisfied customer 滿意的顧客 / She's never satisfied with what she's got. 她對自己的所得從不感到滿足。/ We are not satisfied with these results. 我們對這些結果並不滿意。/ I felt well satisfied with my day's work. 我對自己一天的工作很滿意。/ Her parents seemed satisfied enough with her progress. 她父母對她的進步似乎已經很滿意了。/ She seemed satisfied with the arrangements. 她似乎對安排很滿意。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**satisfying**</span> [ˈsætɪsfaɪɪŋ]
<span class="definition">adj.（因能滿足所需而）令人滿意的：</span>a satisfying meal 可口的飯菜 / a satisfying experience 令人滿意的經歷 / It's satisfying to play a game really well. 一種遊戲玩得特別好是一樁愜意的事。/ I found wood carving satisfying. 我覺得木雕能給人帶來滿足感。/ There's something deeply satisfying about eating vegetables that you have grown yourself. 吃自己種的菜會讓人產生一種深深的滿足感。/ the need for a personally satisfying set of beliefs 需要一套令自己滿意的信念 / a mutually satisfying relationship 彼此滿意的關係 / emotionally, intellectually, musically, sexually satisfying 讓人在情感上得到滿足；智力上令人滿意；具有音樂美感；讓人有性滿足感           

<span class="vocabulary">**satisfactory**</span> [ˌsætɪsˈfæktəri]
<span class="definition">adj. 令人滿意的：</span>satisfactory explanation, satisfactory answer, satisfactory solution, satisfactory conclusion 令人滿意的解釋；站得住腳的回答；可行的解決辦法；足以服人的結論 / I never got a satisfactory answer. 我從未得到令我滿意的答覆。/ It seemed a very satisfactory arrangement. 這似乎是個非常令人滿意的安排。/ The arrangement has proved mutually satisfactory. 這一安排結果令雙方都很滿意。/ The work is satisfactory but not outstanding. 工作做得可以，但不出色。/ The existing law is not entirely/wholly satisfactory. 現行法律並不十分完善。/ It was all most satisfactory. 一切都非常令人滿意。/ The results were not entirely satisfactory. 結果不盡如人意。/ We hope this proposal is satisfactory to you. 我們希望這一方案能讓您滿意。
                    
<span class="vocabulary">**gratifying**</span> [ˈgrætɪfaɪɪŋ]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 令人高興的；令人滿意的：</span>It is gratifying to see such good results. 看到這麼好的結果真令人欣慰。/ It is most gratifying for me to know that my work has been useful. 最令我欣慰的是知道我所做的工作是有益的。  

<span class="vocabulary">**fine**</span> [faɪn] 
<span class="definition">adj. 用於表示對某事物滿意：</span>Your performance was fine. 你的表演很不錯。/ This paper’s not very good quality, but it’s fine for rough work. 這種紙質量不是很好，但用來打草稿很不錯。

☀ <span class="category">**滿意、滿足的名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**satisfaction**</span> [͵sætɪs'fækʃən] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 當自己已實現了某事，或自己所期望發生的事情確實已發生之時所擁有的一種好心情，即滿意、滿足、欣慰；也可指令人滿意、滿足或欣慰之事：</span>smile of satisfaction 滿意的笑容 / She looked back on her career with great satisfaction. 回顧自己的職業生涯，她深感欣慰。/ a feeling of satisfaction, a sense of satisfaction 滿足感 / emotional satisfaction 情感滿足 / customer satisfaction 顧客滿意度 / level of job satisfaction 工作滿足感

<span class="vocabulary">**content**</span> [kən'tent] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 滿意；滿足。相當於contentment。不過content作為名詞的“滿意”時，遠不如其作為形容詞時常用，也沒有satisfaction和contentment常用（satisfaction最為常用）：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**contentment**</span> [kənˈtentmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 滿意、滿足：</span>He has found contentment at last. 他最終得到了滿足。/ They finally found contentment in living a simple life. 他們終於在簡單的生活中得到了滿足。/ a sigh of contentment 滿足地舒一口氣 / She sighed with contentment. 她心滿意足地舒了口氣。/ I cannot describe the feeling of contentment that was with me at that time. 我無法描述當時我滿足的感覺。/ He gazed out to sea, with a feeling of deep contentment. 他凝視著大海，感到深深的滿足。/ She fell asleep in quiet contentment. 她平靜而滿足地睡著了。

<span class="vocabulary">**gratification**</span> [ˌgrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] [正式] 滿足、滿意；令人滿足的事物：</span>sexual gratification 性滿足 / A feed will usually provide instant gratification to a crying baby. 餵食通常可使正在哭鬧的嬰兒立即得到滿足。

<span class="vocabulary">**joy**</span> [dӡɒɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] [英，非正式] 用於疑問句和否定句，成功或滿意、滿足：</span>We complained about our rooms but got no joy from the manager. 我們抱怨房間不好，經理卻不理會。/ ‘Any joy at the shops?’ ‘No, they didn’t have what I wanted.’ “逛商店有收穫嗎？”“沒有，哪裡都沒有我想要的。”
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fulfilment**</span> [fʊl'fɪlmənt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 滿足：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">2 [U] 滿足感；成就感：</span>professional fulfilment 職業滿足感 / to find personal fulfilment 尋求個人的滿足感 / She needed greater fulfilment in her job. 她需要從工作中獲得更大的滿足感。/ human fulfilment 人類的成就感 / emotional, intellectual, professional, religious, sexual, spiritual fulfilment 情感滿足；學術成就；職業滿足感；宗教願望；性滿足；精神滿足 / Most computer games provide some kind of wish fulfilment. 大部分電子遊戲滿足人們的某種願望。/ He gets a lot of fulfilment from his charity work. 他從慈善工作中得到了很大的成就感。/ I can't find fulfilment in doing housework. 我不覺得做家務活有什麼滿足感。/ They gain a sense of fulfilment from their work. 他們從工作中獲得成就感。
           
☀ <span class="category">**滿意、滿足、迎合的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**content**</span> [kən'tent] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 content yourself with sth 滿足；滿意；知足。不常用：</span>Jane contented herself with a bowl of soup. 簡喝了一碗湯就心滿意足了。<span class="definition">2 [正式] 使某人感到滿意或滿足：</span>My apology seemed to content him. 我的道歉似乎讓他滿意。

<span class="vocabulary">**satisfy**</span> ['sætɪsfaɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt.（透過做某人想做的事或給予其想要的東西而）使其滿意或滿足：</span>Nothing satisfies him--he’s always complaining. 什麼都難如他的意——他老在抱怨。/ His answer seemed to satisfy her. 他的回答似乎令她滿意。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fulfil**</span> [fʊlˈfɪl]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb/yourself 使滿意、使滿足：</span>I need a job that really fulfils me. 我需要一份真正令我感到滿意的工作。/ The war was the biggest thing in her life and nothing after that quite fulfilled her. 這場戰爭是她生命中最重大的事件，從那以後再沒有什麼讓她特別有成就感的了。/ He was able to fulfil himself through his painting. 他透過繪畫充分發揮了自己的才能。/ They don't like the idea that women can fulfil themselves without the assistance of a man. 他們不喜歡那種女人沒有男人的幫助仍會有所成就的想法。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**accommodate**</span> [əˈkɒmədeɪt; 美 əˈkɑ:m-]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb (with sth) [正式]（透過為某人做事來）幫助對方，如迎合、遷就：</span>I have accommodated the press a great deal, giving numerous interviews. 我多次接受採訪，已給了報界許多方便。/ He has never put an arm around his wife to accommodate photographers. 他從未為了迎合攝影師而摟住妻子。/ He's already altered several of the proposals in his economic plan to accommodate demands of special interests. 他已經改動了經濟計劃中的幾項提議以遷就特殊利益集團的要求。

☀ <span class="category">**滿意、滿足的其它詞性：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**fine**</span> [faɪn] 
<span class="definition">adv. [非正式] 以一種可接受或足夠好的方式，令人滿意地，即不錯地：</span>You’re doing fine. 你做得蠻不錯。/ The machine is working fine. 機器運轉良好。

<span class="vocabulary">**well**</span> [wel] 
<span class="definition">adv. 好、對、不錯、令人滿意地、可接受地：</span>Well done! 幹得不錯！/ Everything is going well. 一切進展順利。 

<span class="vocabulary">**there**</span> [ðeə] 
<span class="definition">exclamation. 當自己說中…時（即自己對…的說法是正確的），用於表示自己的滿意：</span>There! That didn’t hurt too much, did it? 怎麼樣，那不太痛吧？ 

相關詞義延伸：
· satisfaction還可指處理投訴、抱怨、債務、傷害等事務時所採取的一種可接受的方式。

周邊詞彙補充：
· pleasure可指滿意（的一種狀態或感覺。也可表示使人滿足的事情或事物；
· please可表示使某人滿意；
· pleased可指對某事感到滿意的；
· sweet可表示令人感到滿意的。
