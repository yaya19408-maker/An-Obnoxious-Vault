☀ <span class="category">**任職 擔任：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**fill**</span> [fɪl] 
<span class="definition">vt.（在工作中）擔任或充任某個角色、職位等，做某項工作。強調對一個職位空缺的“填充”：</span>He fills the post satisfactorily (= performs his duties well). 他很盡職。/ the role of sales manager 銷售經理的職責 / to fill the role of diplomat’s wife 擔任外交家妻子的角色

<span class="vocabulary">**hold**</span> [həʊld] 
<span class="definition">vt. 與office，power和responsibility等詞連用，可表示擔任（職務）；掌握（權力）；承擔（責任）等。強調一種長期處於某一職位的狀態，而非短期性的“填充職位”的動作：</span>How long has he held office? 他任職有多久了？

<span class="vocabulary">**occupy**</span> ['ɒkjupaɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 在某一體系、過程或計劃中佔據某一位置，如任職，執政，相當於hold，稍微不如hold常用：</span>The president occupies the position for four years. 總統任期四年。

<span class="vocabulary">**sit**</span> [sɪt] 
<span class="definition">vi. sit in/on/for sth / sit as sth 擁有一個正式的…職位；作為…的一名成員而擁有一個正式的…職位。也強調一種長期處於某一職位的狀態。不過多為高階的、具有一定決策權或對決策有影響力的職位：</span>He was sitting as a temporary judge. 由他擔任臨時法官。/ She sat on a number of committees. 她在幾個委員會里任職。

<span class="vocabulary">**continue**</span> [kən'tɪnju:] 
<span class="definition">vi. continue (as sth) 仍然在某一職位上，即留任：</span>I want you to continue as project manager. 我要你留任專案經理。/ She will continue in her present job until a replacement can be found. 在找到替換人員以前，她將繼續做她目前的工作。
