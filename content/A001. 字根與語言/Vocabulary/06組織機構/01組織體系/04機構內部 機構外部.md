☀ <span class="category">**機構內部 機構外部：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**within**</span> [wɪð'ɪn] 
<span class="definition">prep. [正式] 在…裡面或內部：</span>There is discontent within the farming industry. 農業界內部多有不滿。

<span class="vocabulary">**inside**</span> ['ɪn'saɪd] 
<span class="definition">adj. 從一個團體或組織的內部成員那裡瞭解到的，或由其內部成員所幹的：</span>inside information 內部情報 / Any newspaper would pay big money to get the inside story on her marriage. 任何報紙都願出高價購買關於她婚姻的內情。/ The robbery appeared to have been an inside job. 這次搶劫像是內部人乾的。

<span class="vocabulary">**outside**</span> ['aʊt'saɪd] 
<span class="definition">adj. 只用於名詞前，不屬於本團體（或機構、國家等）的；與本團體無關的：</span>We plan to use an outside firm of consultants. 我們計劃利用外面的諮詢公司。
