☀ <span class="category">**位於方向 在某方向：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**to**</span> [tu:] 
<span class="definition">prep. to the sth (of sth) 位於某個方向：</span>Place the cursor to the left of the first word. 把游標置於第一個單詞的左邊。/ There are mountains to the north. 北面有山。/ His studio is to the right. 他的工作室在右邊。

<span class="vocabulary">**on**</span> [ɒn] 
<span class="definition">prep. 表示方向：</span>on my right 在我右方 / He turned his back on Tom. 他轉過身去背對著湯姆。
