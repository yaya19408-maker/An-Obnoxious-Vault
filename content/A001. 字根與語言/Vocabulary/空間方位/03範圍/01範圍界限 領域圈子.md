☀ <span class="category">**範圍界限 領域圈子：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**range**</span> [reɪndӡ] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, usually sing.]（變動或浮動的）範圍，界限，區間：</span>an increase in the range of 0 to 3 per cent 0到3個百分點的增長幅度 / This was outside the range of his experience. 這超出了他的閱歷。/ ability, age, price range 能力範圍；年齡範圍；價格幅度 / Many factors limit women’s range of job choices. 諸多因素制約著女性選擇工作的範圍。<span class="definition">2 [C, U] 可被看到（或聽到）的距離，即視覺（或聽覺）範圍：</span>hearing, visual（均尤美）range 聽覺範圍；視覺範圍 / It’s outside my range of vision. 它超出了我的視力範圍。/ The receiver has a maximum range of about 30 feet. 這臺接收器的最遠接收範圍大約為30英尺。<span class="definition">vi. 1 range from A to B / range between A and B（在一定的數量、尺寸、程度等範圍內）變化，變動（包括上下兩個頂點在內）：</span>to range in size from A to B 尺寸在A到B之間變動 / They range in price from $3 to $15. 它們的價格在3到15美元之間變化。/ The disease ranges widely in severity. 這種病的嚴重程度千差萬別。<span class="definition">2 range (from A to B) 包括（從…到…）之間的各類事物：</span>She has had a number of different jobs, ranging from chef to swimming instructor. 她做過許多不同的工作，從大廚到游泳教練。/ The conversation ranged widely. 談話所涉及的範圍很廣。/ Accommodation ranges from tourist class to luxury hotels. 住宿條件從經濟型旅館至豪華型賓館不等。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**confines**</span> [ˈkɒnfaɪnz; 美 ˈkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] [正式] confines可指範圍；界限；邊界：</span>The movie is set entirely within the confines of the abandoned factory. 電影的拍攝完全是在這個廢棄的工廠廠區內進行。/ The dim, familiar confines of the room grew clearer as my eyes adjusted to the indoor darkness. 隨著我的眼睛適應了室內的黑暗、房間內昏暗、熟悉的一切變得清楚起來。/ The next two games are in the friendly confines of Wrigley Field. 接下來的兩場比賽在裡格利球場舉行，氣氛友好。/ She returned to the cosy/cozy confines of her lodgings on the second floor. 她回到了自己位於二樓的溫馨住所。/ She stepped into the cool, dark confines of a roadside inn. 她走進了路旁—家涼爽、昏暗的小旅館。/ in the quiet confines of a Bloomington hotel room 在布盧明頓酒店安靜的房間內 / a chance to escape the stuffy confines of the town 逃離那座沉悶的城市的機會 / It is a story that must not escape the confines of this room. 這是一個只限於在這間屋子裡講的故事。/ It took him some effort to leave the comfy confines of his room. 他下了很大的決心才離開了自己舒適的房間。/ Most airmen aren't allowed to leave the confines of the base. 大多數飛行員不允許走出該基地。/ They left the half-lit streets and entered the confines of a cemetery. 他們離開了半明半暗的街道，進入了公墓區。/ He spent three years within the narrow confines of the prison. 他在監獄狹小的空間裡度過了三年。/ The gay community is transcending the narrow confines of the ghetto. 同性戀人群不再侷限於貧民窟的狹窄圈子。/ the wild grass and weeds that grew in the confines of the grandstand 大看臺上叢生的野草 / It is beyond the confines of human knowledge. 這超出了人類的知識範圍。/ issues that go beyond the limited confines of the book 超出本書限定範圍的問題 / She wanted to experience things outside the close confines of family life. 她想體驗—下封閉的家庭生活以外的生活。/ She longed to leave the confines of her household. 她渴望擺脫家庭的束縛。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**scope**</span> [skəʊp; 美 skoʊp]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（一個主題、組織機構、活動等的）範圍：</span>These issues were outside the scope of the article. 這些問題不屬本文論述範圍。/ The subject lies outside the scope of this book. 這個題目超出了本書的範圍。/ This subject lies beyond the scope of our investigation. 這一問題超出了我們的考察範圍。/ the scope of a novel 小說所涉及的範圍 / This is a novel of epic scope and grand passion. 這部小說場面宏大，氣勢磅礴。/ The scope of the exhibition is disappointingly narrow. 展品的範圍很窄，令人失望。/ These criteria were used to determine the scope of the curriculum. 這些標準用以確定課程範圍。/ He promised to widen the organisation's scope of activity. 他許諾拓寬該組織的活動範圍。/ The sheer scope of the project was impressive. 專案的宏大規模令人驚歎。/ The survey is too limited in (its) scope. 調查的範圍太窄了。/ Our powers are limited in scope. 我們的許可權不大。/ These disputes fall within the scope of the local courts. 處理這些糾紛屬於地方法院的職權範圍。/ global, international, national scope 全球範圍；國際範圍；全國範圍 / The geographical scope of product markets has widened since the war. 戰後產品市場的地理範圍擴大了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**spectrum**</span> [ˈspektrəm]
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] 某類事物的範圍、系列：</span>a broad spectrum of interests 廣泛的興趣 / The newspaper covers a broad spectrum of opinion. 這份報紙報道各種觀點。/ The courses cover the full spectrum of levels. 這些課程從初級到高階，各個水平都有。/ ideological, social spectrum 意識形態流派；社會範疇 / age, income spectrum 年齡範圍；收入範圍 / These thinkers represent a wide spectrum of political perspectives. 這些思想家所代表的政治觀點千差萬別，範圍很廣。/ We shall hear views from across the political spectrum. 我們要聽取各個政治派別的看法。/ Politicians across the political spectrum have denounced the act. 各黨派的政客們都對該行為表示譴責。/ There was consensus across the political spectrum. 政壇各階層人士達成了共識。/ She'd seen his moods range across the emotional spectrum. 她見識過他的情緒波動會有多大。/ The two speakers were chosen to represent opposite ends of the spectrum. 選出的兩個發言人分別代表對立的兩級。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**scale**</span> [skeɪl]
<span class="definition">n. [sing, U] ~ (of sth) 範圍，尤指與其他事物相比較時的範圍，且尤指大的範圍：</span>pollution on a massive scale 大範圍的汙染 / On a global scale, 77% of energy is created from fossil fuels. 全球77%的能量產生自化石燃料。           

<span class="vocabulary">**domain**</span> [dəˈmeɪn; dəʊ-; 美 doʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（知識或活動的）領域、範圍，尤指某人所負責的領域或範圍：</span>The care of older people is being placed firmly within the domain of the family. 照顧老人仍然被確認為是家庭範圍的事。/ things that happen outside the domain of the home 發生在家庭以外的事情 / Physics used to be very much a male domain. 物理學曾在很大程度上是男人的領域。/ the great experimenters in the domain of art 藝術領域偉大的實驗者們 / Public domain（不受版權保護的）公有領域 / This information should be in the public domain. 這份資料不受版權保護。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**extent**</span> [ɪkˈstent]
<span class="definition">n. [sing., U] 一個區域的物理範圍：</span>geographical, territorial extent 地理範圍；領土範圍 / a statement defining the extent of Latvia's territory 界定拉脫維亞領土範圍的宣告 / to reduce the extent of deforestation 縮小森林砍伐的範圍 / You can't see the full extent of the beach from here. 從這兒看不到海灘全貌。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**horizon**</span> [həˈraɪzn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl.] （慾望、知識或興趣的）範圍，眼界：</span>She wanted to travel to broaden her horizons. 她想旅行，以開闊眼界。/ The company needs new horizons now. 公司現在需要開拓新的領域。/ As your horizons expand, these new ideas can give a whole new meaning to life. 隨著眼界不斷開闊，這些新觀念會為生活賦予全新的意義。/ By embracing other cultures and genres, we actually broaden our horizons, rather than narrow any existing ones. 透過接納其他文化和體裁，我們實際上拓寬了領域，而不是讓現有的範圍變得狹窄。/ cultural, intellectual, musical, political horizon 文化視野；知識視野；音樂視野；政治視野 / Her trips to Asia have broadened her cultural horizons. 亞洲之行開闊了她的文化視野。/ It is hoped that the course will open up new horizons for students. 這門課程的目的是為學生開拓新的視野。/ Their horizons were limited to events within the town community. 他們的眼界只侷限於本鎮的事情。/ His horizons didn't stretch beyond his next game. 他的心思裡只有下一場比賽。/ They had become aware of possibilities beyond their own limited horizons. 他們已經開始意識到還有他們想象以外的一些可能性。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**gamut**</span> [ˈgæmət]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the gamut可指（某類事物的）全範圍：</span>The course covers a wide gamut of subjects. 該課程涉及範圍廣，涵蓋許多學科。/ The exhibition runs the whole gamut of artistic styles. 這次展覽包括了所有藝術風格的作品。/ The network will provide the gamut of computer services to your home. 這個網路將為家庭提供全方位的計算機服務。/ Varied though the anthology may claim to be, it does not cover the whole gamut of Scottish poetry. 該詩集雖然可能號稱收錄廣泛，但並未涵蓋所有蘇格蘭詩歌。/ She felt she had run the (whole) gamut of human emotions from joy to despair. 她感到自己嚐遍了從喜到悲的人類的一切情感。/ As the story unfolded throughout the past week, I experienced the gamut of emotions: shock, anger, sadness, disgust, confusion. 上週隨著故事情節的展開，我的心情可以說是跌宕起伏：從震驚、氣憤、悲傷到憎惡與困惑。/ The show runs the gamut of 20th century design. 展覽涵蓋20世紀的各種設計。/ The reviews for 'On a Clear Day' ran the gamut from contempt to qualified rapture. 對於《好日子》的影評譭譽參半，既有人表示不值一提，也有人覺得它帶給人一定的驚喜。

<span class="vocabulary">**zone**</span> [zəʊn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 抽象地帶：</span>the twilight zone between living and merely existing zone 介於生活和捱日子之間的模糊狀態 / The Internet has become a free-fire zone for conspiracy theories. 網際網路對各種陰謀論毫無限制。/ relegation zone（英）（體育比賽的）降級區 / Students are unwilling to step outside their comfort zone. 學生們都貪戀自己的舒適區，不願改變。

<span class="vocabulary">**orbit**</span> ['ɔ:bɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.]（人、組織等的）影響範圍，勢力範圍：</span>to be within sb’s orbit 屬於某人的勢力範圍

<span class="vocabulary">**spread**</span> [spred] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] spread (of sth) 涉及的區域或活動範圍：</span>The corporation has a good geographical spread of branches in this country. 該公司在該國開設的分支機構地理分佈相當廣。

<span class="vocabulary">**sight**</span> [saɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 視力所及的距離或範圍，即視力範圍，視野：</span>Get out of my sight! 滾開！/ A dog was in sight. 看見了一條狗。/ Keep their car in sight for as long as you can. 儘量盯住他們的車。/ He kept out of sight behind a pillar. 他待在柱子後面，以免有人看見。/ He won’t let the children leave his sight. 他不讓孩子們離開他的視線。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vision**</span> [ˈvɪʒn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 視野：</span>A bird shot across her vision. 一隻鳥在她眼前掠過。/ She was aware of shapes moving across her field of vision. 她感覺到一些模糊的影子從自己的視野中掠過。/ Someone was standing in my line of vision so I couldn't see the screen.有人擋住了我的視線。所以我看不到螢幕。/ The couple moved outside her field of vision. 這對夫婦離開了她的視野。/ He glimpsed something on the edge of his vision. 他斜眼瞥見了點什麼。/ Jane blocked Cross's vision and he could see nothing. 簡擋住了克羅斯的視線，他什麼也看不見。/ I saw other indistinct shapes that stayed out of vision. 我看見另有一些模糊的影子晃了一下就不見了。

<span class="vocabulary">**compass**</span> ['kʌmpəs] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [正式]（尤指在某種情況下可以達到的）範圍或範疇、界限：</span>the compass of a singer’s voice 歌手的音域 

<span class="vocabulary">**reach**</span> [ri:tʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing., U] 權力或影響力的界限或範圍：</span>Success is out of his reach. 成功對於他來說遙不可及。/ The price puts it out of the reach of most people. 這東西價格太高，令大多數人望而卻步。/ beyond the reach of the law 不受法律的管轄 / the global reach of the Internet 網際網路的全球影響力 / geographic reach 地域影響力 / As an artist, his reach was not limited to Britain. 作為一位藝術家，他的影響力不僅僅限於英國。/ Humans have extended their reach into space. 人類已經將活動範圍延伸至太空。

<span class="vocabulary">**area**</span> ['eərɪə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] area (of sth) 某一學科或活動（或其中的一個方面），即領域：</span>a growth area 增長領域 / an area of activity 活動領域 / an area of interest 感興趣的領域 / an area of research, an area of study 研究領域 / Finance is Mark’s area. 財務是馬克負責的範圍。/ The proposals contain many grey/gray areas. 這些提案有太多含糊不清的地方。/ People with this disability can cope well in most areas of life. 身患這種殘疾的人能夠輕鬆應付生活中的大多數情況。

<span class="vocabulary">**field**</span> [fi:ld] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 某人所從事或感興趣的某個學科或某種活動，即領域、界：</span>specialist field 專業領域 / career field（美）職業領域 / medical field 醫學領域 / academic field 學術領域 / I’m afraid it’s outside my field. 恐怕它不屬於我的專業範圍。/ a field of research 研究領域 / These academics are world leaders in their respective fields. 這些學者都是各自領域的世界級帶頭人。
             
<span class="vocabulary">**battlefield**</span> [ˈbætlfi:ld]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 鬥爭的領域，算是一種比喻用法：</span>瞭解即可       

<span class="vocabulary">**ground**</span> [ɡraʊnd] 
<span class="definition">n. [U]（興趣、知識或思想的）領域，範圍：</span>He managed to cover a lot of ground in a short talk. 他在簡短的談話中涵蓋了許多領域。/ He was back on familiar ground, dealing with the customers. 他又幹起了與顧客打交道的老本行。/ Several researchers have published articles covering this ground. 幾位研究者已經發表了涉及這一領域的文章。/ to break new ground 開闢新的領域
   
<span class="vocabulary">**battleground**</span> ['bætlɡraʊnd;美ˈbætlˌɡraʊnd]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 鬥爭的領域，算是一種比喻用法：</span>the battleground of education 教育這一競爭激烈的領域 / Children's literature is an ideological battleground. 兒童文學是各種意識形態交鋒的戰場。        
                   
<span class="vocabulary">**sector**</span> [ˈsektə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指一國經濟的）領域、部門：</span>independent（英）, private sector 私營部門 / public, state sector 公共部門；國有部門 / non-profit（尤美）, voluntary（英）非營利部門 / economic, industry, market sector 經濟部門；工業部門；市場領域 / agricultural, business, commercial, corporate, export, industrial, manufacturing, media, retial, service sector 農業；商業領域；公司企業；出口部門；工業部門；製造業；媒體領域；零售業；服務業 / banking, financial, financial services（尤英）sector 銀行業；金融業；金融服務業 / energy, oil sector 能源領域；石油行業 / electronics, high-tech, tech, technology, telecom（英）sector 電子業；高科技領域；技術領域；電信行業 / education, schools（英）, university（尤英）sector 教育部門；中小學；高校界 / the chronic underfunding of the education sector 教育部門的長期資金不足 / health, health-care sector 衛生部門；衛生保健部門 / tourism, tourist（尤英）sector 旅遊業 / consumer, household（尤美）sector 消費品行業；家居業 / He went on to a successful career in the private sector. 他繼而在私營機構幹出了一番事業。/ employment opportunities in the higher education sector 高等教育行業的就業機會 / The survey covers a wide range of industry sectors. 該調研覆蓋了範圍廣泛的產業部門。      

<span class="vocabulary">**sphere**</span> [sfɪə(r); 美 sfɪr]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 範圍、領域：</span>a sphere of activity 活動範圍 / nurses, working in all spheres of the health service 在公共醫療保健服務業的各領域工作的護士們 / a sphere of influence 影響範圍 / This area was formerly within the sphere of influence of the US. 這一地區先前屬於美國的勢力範圍。/ They see the region as their natural sphere of influence due to their historical links and a shared language. 基於歷史上的聯絡和共同的語言，他們把該地區看成是自己天然的勢力範圍。/ countries traditionally within the British or American spheres of influence 傳統上處於英國或美國勢力範圍內的國家 / He wanted to spread his ideas to a wider sphere than the school. 他想把自己的觀點傳播到學校以外。/ academic, cultural, domestic, economic, financial, military, political, social 學術領域；文化領域；國內領域；經濟領域；金融領域；軍事領域；政治領域; 社會領域 / The matter is outside my sphere of responsibility. 這件事不歸我負責。        

<span class="vocabulary">**arena**</span> [əˈri:nə]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [正式]（尤指不同群體或國家間的充滿競爭性的）競爭領域、鬥爭舞臺：</span>the international arena 國際舞臺 / the challenges faced by farmers in the international and domestic arenas 農場主面臨的國內外的挑戰 / Oil speculation proved a natural arena for his skills. 在石油業中進行投機活動對他來說遊刃有餘。/ Unions are also active in the wider arena of the state. 工會也活躍在國家更為寬廣的舞臺上。/ academic, commercial, cultural, economic, financial, legal, legislative, political, social arena 學術界；商界；文化界；經濟界；金融界；法律界；政界；社交界 / This observation also applies in the environmental policy arena. 這一評論也適用於環境政策領域。/ digital arena 數碼領域 / the challenges of bringing a business into the online arena 企業轉向線上經營面臨的挑戰 / competitive arena 競爭領域 / He had no desire to enter the political arena. 他無意進入政界。/ The company has been very successful in the commercial arena. 這家公司在商界一直很成功。/ I want to work outside the arena of competition. 我希望在遠離競爭的地方工作。

<span class="vocabulary">**territory**</span> [ˈterətri; 美 -tɔ:ri]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（個人、知識的）領域、（經驗的）範圍：</span>unknown territory 未知領域 / virgin territory 處女地 / unexplored territory 未開拓的領域 / This type of work is uncharted territory for us. 我們從未涉足過這類工作。/ Legal problems are Andy's territory (= he deals with them). 法律問題由安迪負責處理。/ Following the futuristic The Handmaid's Tale, Margaret Atwood's seventh novel, Cat's Eye, returns to more familiar territory. 寫完了具有未來色彩的《女僕的故事》之後，瑪格麗特·阿特伍德的第七部小說《貓眼》迴歸到了人們較熟悉的題材上。/ The writer is back on home territory with his latest novel. 作者在他的最新小說裡又回到了他所熟悉的領域 / Tired of writing detective novels, she began to explore new territory. 她厭煩了寫偵探小說，開始探索新的創作領域。/ Reading from a tedious technical brief for hours on end, he would stray into difficult territory. 連續看上幾個小時枯燥的技術簡報後，他會不自覺地鑽起牛角尖來。/ Many books have covered this territory before. 以前許多書籍涉及過這一領域。/ The parties have been staking out their territory on education. 各方已經明確了各自在教育問題上的立場。

相關詞義延伸：
· range還可指（槍炮或其他武器的）射程、（車輛）加一次油可行駛的路程；
· area還可指足球中球門前的罰球區或禁區；
· colleague還可指一個專業領域的同事；
· stage還可指戲劇界；
· scope還可指（去做某事，或去實現某事的）機會或能力，常翻譯為餘地、空間；
· spectrum還可指光譜。也可指聲波或其它波的譜，即聲譜、波譜、頻譜；
· domain還可指（尤指舊時個人、國家等所擁有或統治的）土地，即領土、領地、勢力範圍。也可指網際網路上的域（是一套網址，可以表明一個網址所屬的類別或地理範圍，以相同字母結尾的網址屬於同一個域，如.com、.org等）。也可指某一變數的所有數值的範圍，即區域、定義域；
· radius還可指（圓的）半徑；半徑長度；
· span還可指某事所持續（或能夠持續）的時間。也可指橋或拱的兩個垂直支撐體之間的部分，即墩距、跨距、跨度。亦可指寬度；
· turf還可指地盤。

周邊詞彙補充：
· band可指（數目、年齡、價格等的）範圍，段（可用於計數或測量之目的）；
· register可指（人的嗓音或樂器的）聲區，聲域，音區，音域。也可指（書面語或口語中）適合特定場合使用的語體風格，即語域；
· limit可指一個區域（或一個地方）的最遠的邊緣，如地界、範圍；
· jurisdiction可指某一法庭或法律體系所管轄的一個地區或國家，如管轄區域；管轄範圍；
· field在物理學中可指力能產生作用的一個區域，即場。也可指資料的一個欄位，或欄；
· public可指擁有相同興趣（或參加同一類活動）的一群人；
· choice可指供選擇的範圍（或不同品種）；
· touch可指在足球或橄欖球運動中邊線以外的區域；
· to可表示範圍或一段時間的結尾或界限；
· reach可指手伸得足夠長以夠到、觸控到或拿到某物；
· within可指在某段時間之內，不超出某段時間。還可指（以某事物為起點）在某段距離之內，不超出某段距離。也可指在…裡面或內部；
· central可指（從很多地區）容易到達的，即交通便利的，四通八達的；
· amplitude可指波（尤指聲音或無線電波）的振幅。還可指（尺寸或數量的）大。
