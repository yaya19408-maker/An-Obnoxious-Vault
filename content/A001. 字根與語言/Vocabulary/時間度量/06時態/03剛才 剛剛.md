☀ <span class="category">**剛才 剛剛：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**just**</span> [dӡʌst] 
<span class="definition">adv. 剛才，方才，剛剛：</span>I’ve just sold my house. 我剛剛賣了自己的房子。

<span class="vocabulary">**hardly**</span> ['hɑːdlɪ] 
<span class="definition">adv. 用於表示某事剛剛開始、剛剛發生等，即剛剛、才：</span>We can't stop for coffee now, we've hardly started. 現在不能停下來喝咖啡，我們剛剛才開始工作呢。

<span class="vocabulary">**fresh**</span> [freʃ] 
<span class="definition">adj. fresh from sth 剛從某地來的；剛有過某種經歷的：</span>students fresh from college 剛剛畢業的大學生 / fresh from her success at the Olympic Games 剛從奧運會凱旋歸來的她 <span class="definition">adv. [尤美，非正式] fresh out of sth 剛供給完（如剛賣完）某東西：</span>Sorry, we’re fresh out of milk. 對不起，牛奶我們剛賣完。

<span class="vocabulary">**new**</span> [nju:] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 new (to sth) 初來乍到的：</span>I am new to the town. 我剛剛來到這座小鎮。/ new arrival, new recruit 剛剛到達的人；新兵 <span class="definition">2 用於構成複合詞，以描述剛剛發生過的某事：</span>his new-found freedom 他剛剛獲得的自由
