☀ <span class="category">**升職 調動：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**promote**</span> [prə'məʊt] 
<span class="definition">vt. promote sb (from sth) (to sth)，常用被動式，（職位）晉升。是此義的常規用詞，與demote相反：</span>He has been promoted to sales manager 他已晉升為銷售經理。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**promotion**</span> [prəˈməʊʃn; 美 -ˈmoʊʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] ~ (to sth) 職位的晉升：</span>Her promotion to Sales Manager took everyone by surprise. 竟然提拔她當銷售經理，叫每個人都感到意外。/ The new job is a promotion for him. 這一新職務對他是擢升。/ a job with excellent promotion prospects 有充分晉升機會的職務 / Consider changing jobs or trying for promotion. 考慮換個工作或者爭取升職。/ rewarding outstanding employees with promotions to higher-paid posts 將出色的員工提升到薪水更高的職位作為獎勵 / The company encourages internal promotion. 公司鼓勵內部晉升。/ I can't get promotion soon, I look for another job. 如果不能很快得到晉升，我就要找別的工作。/ We congratulate James on his well-deserved promotion to Chief Executive. 我們祝賀詹姆斯升為行政總裁，這是他完全應得的。/ promotion opportunities, promotion prospects（英）晉升機會；晉升希望 / her promotion from assistant to associate professor 她從助教到副教授的晉升 / She felt she had little chance of promotion in her job. 她覺得她在這一崗位晉升的機會不大。

<span class="vocabulary">**graduate**</span> ['ɡrædӡueɪt] 
<span class="definition">vi. graduate (from sth) to sth 逐漸晉升到一個更重要的職位上。強調晉升過程的平緩性（而非一蹴而就）：</span>He graduated to general manager of the company. 他晉升為公司的總經理。

<span class="vocabulary">**move**</span> [mu:v] 
<span class="definition">vt. move sb (from ...) (to ...) 變換或調動（某人的工作）：</span>I’m being moved to the New York office. 我要調到紐約辦事處去。<span class="definition">n. [C]（職業的）變換；（興趣的）改變：</span>his move to the chairmanship 他被調任為主席職務 / a sideways move 平級調動
           
<span class="vocabulary">**upgrade**</span> [ˌʌpˈgreɪd]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] ~ sb (to sth) 提拔某人（到一個更高的職位上）：</span>He was upgraded to security guard. 他被擢升為保安。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**ascend**</span> [əˈsend]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. [正式] ~ (to sth)（職位）提高、晉升：</span>Before ascending to the bench, she was a lawyer in a large New York firm. 在升為法官之前，她是紐約一家大型律所的律師。/ Mobutu ascended through the ranks, eventually becoming commander of the army. 蒙博托出身行伍，透過一級級晉升，最終成為了陸軍司令。/ They move freely from one department to another as they ascend the civil service ladder. 隨著仕途晉升，他們可以在部門間隨意調動。       
           
<span class="vocabulary">**ascent**</span> [əˈsent]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.]（職位的）升高，提高：</span>His own career continues its inexorable ascent. 他事業的上升勢頭銳不可當。/ It would be wrong to portray his life as a calculated ascent to power at any cost. 如果把他的一生描述成不惜任何代價一心想向上爬，那就錯了。/ her rapid ascent to power 她迅速掌權

<span class="vocabulary">**advancement**</span> [ədˈvɑ:nsmənt; 美 -ˈvæns-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式]（工作的）晉升：</span>There are good opportunities for advancement within the company if you have the right skills. 如果有合適的技能，在公司內部就有很好的晉升機會。/ her advancement to the position of Supervisor 她晉升為主管 / career advancement 事業的發展 / advancement in the profession 職業的發展
           
<span class="vocabulary">**parachute**</span> [ˈpærəʃu:t]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi.（使）空降（指突然從外部調入某個組織機構）：</span>a consultant who parachutes into corporations and helps provide strategic thinking 空降到公司協助制定戰略思想的顧問 / There was intense speculation 18 months ago that the former foreign secretary might be parachuted into the Scottish Parliament. 18個月前，人們普遍猜測前外交大臣可能會空降至蘇格蘭議會。
