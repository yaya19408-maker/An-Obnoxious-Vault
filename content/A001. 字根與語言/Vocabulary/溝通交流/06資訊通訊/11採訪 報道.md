☀ <span class="category">**採訪 報道：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**interview**</span> ['ɪntəvju:] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] interview (with sb) （常指公開的）記者採訪，訪談：</span>telephone interview 電話採訪 / magazine, media, newspaper, radio, television interview 雜誌採訪；媒體採訪；報紙採訪；電臺採訪；電視採訪 / group interview 集體採訪 / face-to-face interview 面對面的採訪 / exclusive interview 獨家專訪 / live interview 直播的採訪 / interview footage 訪談鏡頭 / interview transcript 採訪的文字記錄 / I get a lot of interview requests from journalists. 我接到很多記者的採訪請求。<span class="definition">vt. interview sb (about sth) 對某人進行媒體採訪或訪談：</span>The Prime Minister declined to be interviewed. 首相婉拒了採訪。

<span class="vocabulary">**cover**</span> ['kʌvə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 在電視、報紙等媒體上報道一次新聞事件；在電視上播出一次事件。本身是一個廣義用詞，與report不同，cover此時僅為及物動詞：</span>The BBC will cover all the major games of the tournament. 英國廣播公司將報道這次錦標賽的所有重要賽事。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**coverage**</span> [ˈkʌvərɪdʒ]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（報紙、廣播或電視上的）新聞報道：</span>tonight's live coverage of the hockey game 今晚曲棍球比賽的現場直播 / Now a special TV network gives live coverage of most races. 現在有一個專門的電視網路對大部分比賽進行現場直播。/ There's been massive television coverage of the World Cup. 有大量關於世界盃的電視報道。| around-the-clock（尤美）, non-stop, round-the-clock, wall-to-wall（尤美）coverage 連續24小時的報道；不間斷的報道；夜以繼日的報道；鋪天蓋地的報道 / media, news, newspaper, press, radio, television, TV coverage 媒體報道；新聞報道；報紙報道；報刊報道；廣播報道；電視報道 / The TV company was given a special award for its news coverage. 這家電視公司獲得了新聞報道特別獎。/ There's live coverage of the game on TV. 電視上有該場比賽的現場直播。/ exclusive coverage 獨家報道 / election, sports, war coverage 選舉報道；體育報道；戰爭報道 / The resignation was given widespread coverage. 這一辭職被廣泛報道。/ The wedding had wide press coverage. 婚禮受到新聞界的廣泛報道。/ The story dominated local news coverage. 這件事佔了當地新聞報道的大半篇幅。/ Media coverage of the march focused on the few fights that broke out. 媒體對示威遊行的報道集中於爆發的幾次鬥毆。/ Most media coverage disapproves of the travellers' lifestyle and values. 大多數媒體報道都不贊成那些旅行者的生活方式和價值觀。

<span class="vocabulary">**angle**</span> ['æŋɡl] 
<span class="definition">vt. 基於某種思維方式（或針對某些觀眾、聽眾等）來呈現資訊、報道等：</span>The programme is angled towards younger viewers. 這個節目的物件是較年輕的觀眾。

<span class="vocabulary">**report**</span> [rɪ'pɔ:t] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. report (on) sth 在報紙、電視等媒體上（以書面或口頭的形式）報道新聞事件。是此義的常規用詞：</span>It was reported that several people had been arrested. 據報道已有數人被捕。/ This is John Hutchins, reporting from Zimbabwe. 這是約翰·哈欽斯從辛巴威為您發回的報道。/ The incident was widely reported in the press. 該事件在新聞界被廣泛報道。<span class="definition">n. [C] report (on/of sth)（尤指已播報或報道出來的）對新聞事件的口頭或書面說明，即報道：</span>media, press report 媒體報道 / magazine, newspaper, radio, television report 雜誌報道；報紙報道；廣播電臺報道；電視報道 / news, traffic, weather report 新聞報道；交通報道；天氣報道 / live report 現場報道 / exclusive report 獨家報道

<span class="vocabulary">**story**</span> ['stɔ:rɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（報紙、雜誌、廣播中的）新聞報道，雖然report是“報道”的常規用詞，但story不僅在詞義上與其難見明顯差別，而且story表此義時也很常用：</span>top stories in the news 新聞報道中的頭條 / exclusive story 獨家新聞 / breaking story（尤美）突發性新聞報道 / feature story（美）專題報道 / scare story 聳人聽聞的報道 / inside story 內幕新聞 / cover, front-page story 封面故事；頭版新聞 / news stories 新聞 / media, newspaper story 媒體報道；報紙報道 / Every newspaper carried the story. 每家報紙都登載了這則報道。

<span class="vocabulary">**commentary**</span> [ˈkɒməntri; 美 ˈkɑ:mənteri]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] ~ (on sth)（尤指廣播電臺或電視臺所作的）某次大事件或賽事的實況報道、現場解說：</span>a sports commentary 體育實況報道 / That programme will include live commentary on the England-Ireland game. 那個節目將包括英格蘭與愛爾蘭比賽的實況報道。/ running（= continuous）commentary 實況追蹤報道 / She kept up a running commentary on the festivities. 她對慶祝活動進行了連續報道。/ Our reporters will give a running commentary (= a continuous one) on the election results as they are announced. 我們的記者將對選舉的公佈結果作實況追蹤報道。/ He gave the listening crowd a running commentary. 他給這一大群聽眾進行現場報道。/ He kept up a running commentary on everyone who came in or went out. 他不斷地對上下場的每一名隊員進行解說。/ accompanying commentary 附帶的解說 / radio, television, TV commentary 電臺實況報道；電視現場解說 / I was listening to the radio commentary on the game. 我在聽電臺的比賽實況解說。/ audio commentary 解說音軌 / colour/color commentary（= giving background information during a sports broadcast)（美）（體育比賽實況廣播中的）背景資訊解說 / commentary box（英）實況報道間

<span class="vocabulary">**publicize**</span> [ˈpʌblɪsaɪz]
<span class="definition">vt. 報道：</span>They flew to Europe to publicize the plight of the refugees. 他們飛往歐洲報道難民的慘狀。/ a widely publicized speech (= that has received a lot of attention on television, in newspapers, etc.) 一篇受到廣泛報道的講話
          
<span class="vocabulary">**publicity**</span> [pʌbˈlɪsəti]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 媒體的報道：</span>adverse publicity 反面的報道 / There has been a great deal of publicity surrounding his disappearance. 他的失蹤已為傳媒廣泛報道。/ There was a lot of negative publicity surrounding the movie. 這部電影有許多負面宣傳。/ There has been a lot of negative publicity about the hospital. 對該醫院有許多不利的報道。/ Taking part in the event will be good publicity for our school. 參加這一活動將是對我們學校的良好宣傳。/ The company had received bad publicity over a defective product. 該公司因為有一種劣質產品而受到媒體攻擊。/ The movie stars were married amid a blaze of publicity.（blaze尤為英式用法）這對電影明星的婚事受到大肆報道。/ The chairman resigned in a storm of publicity over the bonus payments.董事長在有關獎金數額的媒體風暴中辭職。

<span class="vocabulary">**exposure**</span> [ɪkˈspəʊʒə(r); 美 -ˈspoʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（一個人、公司、產品等）在電視、報紙等媒體上被報道：</span>Her new movie has had a lot of exposure in the media. 她的新片媒體有很多報道。/ All the candidates have been getting an enormous amount of exposure on television and in the press. 所有候選人都在電視和報刊上頻頻亮相。/ media, press, television exposure 媒體報道；新聞宣傳；電視宣傳 / He receives regular exposure in the papers.（regular尤為英式用法）他經常在報紙上露面。/ The magazine aims to give exposure to the work of women artists. 這本雜誌的目的是推廣女性藝術家的作品。/ a would-be television personality who is constantly trying to get media exposure 總是試圖得到媒體報道想要成為電視名人的人

<span class="vocabulary">**exclusive**</span> [ɪkˈsklu:sɪv]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 獨家新聞、獨家報道：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**film**</span> [fɪlm] 
<span class="definition">n. [U]（例如電視上播出的）真實事件的新聞片，相當於footage：</span>television news film of the riots 這場暴亂的電視新聞片 / The accident was caught on film. 事故已被拍攝下來。

<span class="vocabulary">**account**</span> [ə'kaʊnt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 對已發生事件的書面或口頭報道。不太常用：</span>media, news（美）account 媒體報道；新聞報道 / the newspaper account of the trial 報紙對這次審判的報道
           
<span class="vocabulary">**journalism**</span> [ˈdʒɜ:nəlɪzəm; 美 ˈdʒɜ:rn-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 為報刊、雜誌、廣播、電視蒐集並撰寫新聞稿件的工作：</span>professional journalism 專業新聞工作 / hard-hitting（尤美）, investigative, watchdog（美）journalism 言辭犀利的新聞報道；調查性的新聞報道；監督性的新聞報道 / popular, tabloid journalism 通俗新聞；小報新聞 / The newspaper's editorial standards have sunk to the level of tabloid journalism. 該報的編輯標準已經淪落到小報新聞的水平了。/ mainstream journalism 主流新聞報道 / yellow journalism（美）黃色新聞（誇張或聳人聽聞的報道）/ gonzo journalism（= reporting in newspapers that tries to shock or excite rather than give true information）（美）譁眾取寵的新聞報道 / chequebook/checkbook journalism（貶）支票新聞（支付重金以獲得獨家採訪權）/ lazy, shoddy（尤美）, sloppy journalism 粗製濫造的新聞 / online journalism 線上新聞 / magazine, newspaper, print journalism 雜誌新聞；報紙新聞；報刊新聞 / broadcast, radio, television journalism 廣播新聞；電臺新聞；電視新聞 / fashion（尤英）, literary, medical, music, science, sports journalism 時尚新聞；文學新聞；醫藥新聞；音樂新聞；科學新聞；體育新聞 / a fine piece of investigative journalism 一篇優秀的調查性新聞報道 / It was an accomplished piece of investigative journalism. 這是一個成功的調查研究報道。/ T'd like to have a career in journalism. 我希望從事新聞工作。/ He began a career in journalism, working for the North London Press Group. 他開始從事新聞工作，為北倫敦報業集團工作。/ the world of journalism 新聞界

周邊詞彙補充：
· spin可指某人對某一事件或情況以某種方式所做的解釋、詮釋與呈現，是一種資訊的報道傾向；
· piece可指電視廣播上的一則報道，是一個簡單用詞；
· appearance可指在媒體上露面。
