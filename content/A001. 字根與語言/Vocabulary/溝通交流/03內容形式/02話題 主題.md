☀ <span class="category">**話題 主題：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**subject**</span> ['sʌbdӡɪkt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 在談話中，可指人們所討論的人、事件或事物，即話題（其本義為處置的主題，是一個基礎用詞，詞義範圍較廣，話題只是其若干義項中的一個）：</span>my own views on the subject 我對此問題的個人看法 / a subject of conversation 交談的話題 / to get onto the subject of human nature 談到人性的話題 / to get off the subject 偏離了主題 / to change the subject 換個話題 / a taboo subject 禁忌話題 / to speak on the chosen subject 就選定的主題發表演講 / his pet subject 他喜歡的話題

<span class="vocabulary">**theme**</span> [θi:m] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（談話或討論等的）主題：</span>the theme of the conference 會議主題 / the main theme of the talk 談話的主題 / He gave a talk on the theme of teenage unemployment. 他就青少年失業這個主題發表了談話。/ Several familiar themes emerged from the discussion. 討論中出現了幾個熟悉的主題。

<span class="vocabulary">**topic**</span> ['tɒpɪk] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 談論的某個主題，即話題。與subject未見明顯差別：</span>a topic of conversation, a topic of discussion 談話的主題；討論的主題 / a topic of speech 演講的主題 / a controversial topic 有爭議的話題 / In the next chapter the writer focuses on the topic of adoption. 在下一章作者著重探討收養這個問題。/ We discussed topics ranging from foreign policy to economics. 我們探討了從外交政策到經濟學等領域的多個主題。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**heading**</span> [ˈhedɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 講話的主題：</span>I've organized what I have to say about unemployment under three main headings. 關於失業問題，我要講的分為三個大標題。

<span class="vocabulary">**area**</span> ['eərɪə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 話題。不太常用：</span>the politically sensitive area of social security 社保這個政治敏感話題
         
<span class="vocabulary">**keynote**</span> [ˈki:nəʊt; 美 -noʊt] 
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.]（演講的）主旨、主題：</span>keynote speech, keynote address, keynote lecture 主旨演講；主題發言；主題講座 / keynote speaker (= a very important one, introducing a meeting or its subject) 主要發言人 / Choice is the keynote of the new education policy. 新教育政策的主導原則是容許選擇。/ He would be setting out his plans for the party in a keynote speech. 他將在其主旨發言中闡述他對該黨的計劃。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**battleground**</span> ['bætlɡraʊnd;美ˈbætlˌɡraʊnd]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 爭論的主題，算是一種比喻用法：</span>the battleground of education 教育這一備受爭議的主題

<span class="vocabulary">**battlefield**</span> [ˈbætlfi:ld]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 爭論的主題，算是一種比喻用法：</span>the domestic battlefield of family life 家庭生活內部爭論的主題
