☀ <span class="category">**抱怨 嘆息：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**sniff**</span> [snɪf] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 抱怨地說；不同意或不以為然地說：</span>‘It’s hardly what I’d call elegant,’ she sniffed.“要我說，這很難稱得上雅緻。”她不以為然地說。

<span class="vocabulary">**sigh**</span> [saɪ] 
<span class="definition">vi. 嘆著氣說，嘆息道：</span>‘Oh well, better luck next time,’ she sighed. “唉，就這樣了，但願下一次運氣好些。”她嘆息道。
