☀ <span class="category">**語氣 語調：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**note**</span> [nəʊt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] note (of sth) 單數名詞，指某人聲音中的語氣、調子等，如：</span>hopeful, optimistic, positive note 充滿希望的語氣；樂觀的聲調；積極的意味 / a note of sneer 一種嘲諷的語氣 / There was a sad note in her voice. 她聲音裡帶著一絲悲傷。/ A note of suspicion entered his voice. 他的聲音裡開始帶有一絲懷疑。/ He sounded a cautionary note. 他的聲音帶有一種警告的意味。/ His opening remarks struck the right note. 他的開幕詞講得十分得體。/ On a more serious note (= speaking more seriously) … 更嚴格地講… / On a slightly different note (= changing the subject slightly), let’s talk about … 咱們略微換一下話題，談談…

<span class="vocabulary">**tongue**</span> [tʌŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 某種說話方式：</span>loose, sharp tongue（均為比喻用法）口風不嚴；說話尖刻 <span class="definition">adj. -tongued 用於構成形容詞，指以某種方式來說話的，即說話…的：</span>sharp-tongued 說話尖刻的
        
<span class="vocabulary">**vein**</span> [veɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [sing, U] 行文（或說話）的某種風格、語氣：</span>A number of other people commented in a similar vein. 其他一些人也以類似的腔調評論。/ ‘And that's not all, ’ he continued in angry vein. “那還不算全部呢。”他生氣地繼續說道。/ It is one of his finest works in a lighter vein. 這是他行文風格輕鬆活潑的作品中最出色的作品之一。/ The girl now replies in similar vein. 這個女孩現在以相似的口吻回答。

周邊詞彙補充：
· intonation可指語音學中的語調，尤指影響語義的語調。
