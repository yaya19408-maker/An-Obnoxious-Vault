☀ <span class="category">**諮詢 徵詢：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**consult**</span> [kən'sʌlt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. consult sb (about sth) 去向某人諮詢資訊或請教建議：</span>Have you consulted your lawyer about this? 你就此事諮詢過你的律師嗎？/ If the pain persists you should consult your doctor. 如果疼痛持續，你就應該找醫生看看。/ I need to consult my teacher about changing my course. 我需要向老師諮詢更改選課的事。/ a consulting engineer 顧問工程師
           
<span class="vocabulary">**consultation**</span> [ˌkɒnslˈteɪʃn; 美 ˌkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U]（做決策前向某人或某個群體所做的）諮詢：</span>consultation document, consultation paper 諮詢檔案 / consultation period 徵求意見階段 / consultation process 諮詢過程 / The Government proposed a common retirement age of 63 in a consultation paper published yesterday. 政府在昨天公佈的徵求意見稿中提出，一般退休年齡為63歲。/ There will be a period of public consultation before a decision is reached. 作出決定之前會有一段公開徵求意見的時間。/ They have taken this decision without any consultation. 他們未徵詢任何意見就作出了這一決定。/ The company has promised wide consultation on its expansion plans. 該公司許諾就其擴張計劃廣泛徵求意見。<span class="definition">2 [C]（向專家請教的）諮詢會面：</span>A personal diet plan is devised after a consultation with a nutritionist. 諮詢營養學家後，制訂了個人飲食方案。/ fees paid for consultation and advice in tax matters 就稅務問題進行諮詢、求得建議而支付的費用 / lega consultation 法律諮詢 / consultation service 諮詢服務 / consultation fee諮詢費 / I had a consultation with a dermatologist. 我向一位皮膚科專家進行了諮詢。
          
<span class="vocabulary">**counselling**</span> [ˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（治療師或其他專家提供的）專業諮詢或建議、輔導：</span>She will need medical help and counselling to overcome the tragedy. 她需要藉助醫學治療和心理諮詢來克服慘劇帶來的影響。/ She brought her husband in for marriage counseling. 她帶丈夫來進行婚姻諮詢。/ group, individual counselling 團體心理諮詢；個人心理諮詢 / professional counselling 專業諮詢 / couples（尤美）, marital（尤美）, marriage（尤美）, marriage guidance（英）, premarital（尤美）counselling 夫妻關係指導；婚姻指導；婚前指導 / family counselling 家庭生活指導 / college（尤美）, school（尤美）, student, youth（尤美）counselling 大學心理輔導；學校心理輔導；學生心理輔導；青年心理輔導 / peer counselling（尤美）朋輩心理輔導 / abortion, genetic counselling 流產心理輔導；遺傳諮詢 / We provide fertility treatment and genetic counselling for couples trying to conceive. 我們為想要生育的夫婦提供不育治療和遺傳諮詢服務。/ bereavement, mental-health（美）, psychological, stress counselling 喪親心理輔導；心理健康輔導；心理諮詢；精神壓力輔導 / career（美）, vocational counselling 職業諮詢 / business, debt counselling 商業諮詢；債務諮詢 / nutritional counselling（尤美）營養諮詢 / The survivors were offered counselling. 給倖存者提供了心理輔導。/ Many of the victims of the tragedy still need counselling. 那場悲劇的許多受害者仍需要心理輔導。/ Their doctor recommended professional counselling. 他們的醫生建議他們接受專業諮詢。/ He was suspended from school and referred for counselling. 他被暫時停學去接受心理輔導。/ counselling centre/center, counselling service 諮詢中心；諮詢服務 / the new student counselling and guidance service 新生諮詢指導服務 / counselling session（一次）諮詢時間 / counselling skills輔導技巧 / counselling for parents and children 親子輔導 / counselling for depression 抑鬱症輔導

<span class="vocabulary">**reference**</span> ['refərəns] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式] reference (to sb/sth)（幫助或意見的）徵求，徵詢：</span>The emergency nurse can treat minor injuries without reference to a doctor. 急救護士不必徵求醫生的意見就可處理輕傷。/ The decision was made without reference to local managers. 此決定未徵求當地主管的意見。

周邊詞彙補充：
· consultant可指在某一學科領域擁有豐富的專業知識，並受僱為其他人在該學科上提供諮詢的人，即顧問。也可指醫院中級別最高的醫生（是某一醫學領域的專家），即高階顧問醫師；
· counsel可指為需要幫助的人提供專業建議，即提供專業諮詢。
