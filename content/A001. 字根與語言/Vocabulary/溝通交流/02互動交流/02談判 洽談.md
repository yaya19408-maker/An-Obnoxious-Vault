☀ <span class="category">**談判 洽談：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**bargain**</span> ['bɑːɡɪn] 
<span class="definition">vi. bargain (with sb) (about/over/for sth) 與某人商定價格、條件等，以達成一個可接受的協議，與negotiate近義，但不如negotiate正式：</span>He was prepared to bargain about money. 他願意就錢的問題進行商議。/ to bargain for a better salary 為爭取提高薪水而談判
           
<span class="vocabulary">**bargaining**</span> [ˈbɑ:gənɪŋ; 美 ˈbɑ:rg-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 討價還價、商談條件等：</span>After much hard bargaining we reached an agreement. 經過一番艱難的討價還價，我們達成了協議。/ Unions were insistent on the right to collective bargaining. 工會堅持集體談判的權利。/ institutional bargaining 制度性商談 / political bargaining 政治談判 / wage bargaining 有關工資的談判 / bargaining position, bargaining power 談判中的地位；討價還價的能力 / Exporters are in a strong bargaining position at the moment. 目前出口商在洽談中處於有利地位。/ bargaining chip, bargaining counter（英）談判的籌碼；談判中的有利條件

<span class="vocabulary">**negotiate**</span> [nɪ'ɡəʊʃɪeɪt] 
<span class="definition">vi. negotiate (with sb) (for/about sth) 嘗試透過正式商討而達成一項協議，即談判，磋商，協商：</span>The government will not negotiate with terrorists. 政府不會和恐怖分子談判。/ We have been negotiating for more pay. 我們一直在為增加工資進行談判。/ Rents are individually negotiated between landlord and tenant. 租金由房東和房客單獨協商。/ We are negotiating for the release of the prisoners. 我們正在就釋放囚犯一事進行談判。/ negotiating skills 談判技巧 <span class="definition">vt. 透過正式商討來安排或同意某事，即商定，達成（協議）：</span>to negotiate a deal, to negotiate a contract, to negotiate a treaty, to negotiate a settlement 達成交易；商定合同；商定條約內容；商定解決措施 / We successfully negotiated the release of the hostages. 我們成功達成了釋放人質的協議。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**negotiation**</span> [nɪˌgəʊʃiˈeɪʃn; 美 -ˌgoʊʃi-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl., U]（尤指政治和商業領域的）談判，協商，磋商：</span>peace, trade, wage negotiations 和談；貿易洽談；工資談判 / They begin another round of negotiations today. 他們今天開始了另一輪的談判。/ to enter into/open/conduct negotiations with sb 和某人開始/展開/進行談判 / The rent is a matter for negotiation between the landlord and the tenant. 租金可以由房東和租戶協商確定。/ A contract is prepared in negotiation with our clients. 我們和客戶協商起草了一份合同。/ The issue is still under negotiation. 這個問題還在商討之中。/ The price is generally open to negotiation. 一般來講，價格可以商量。/ We have had meaningful negotiations and I believe we are very close to a deal. 我們已進行了有益的磋商，我相信我們很快能成交。/ After 10 years of negotiation, the Senate ratified the strategic arms reduction treaty. 經過10年協商，參議院已經正式批准了《削減戰略武器條約》。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**negotiator**</span> [nɪˈgəʊʃieɪtə(r); 美 -ˈgoʊʃi-]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指政治和商業領域的）談判者，且尤指專業談判者：</span>professional, trained negotiator 專業談判者；訓練有素的談判者 / the rebels' chief negotiator at the peace talks 和談中叛亂分子的首席談判代表 / The two American negotiators are calling for substantial cuts in external subsidies. 兩名美國談判代表正在呼籲大量削減外部援助。/ union, hostage, peace, trade negotiator 工會談判代表；人質談判代表；和平談判代表；貿易談判代表

<span class="vocabulary">**broker**</span> [ˈbrəʊkə(r); 美 ˈbroʊ-]
<span class="definition">vt.（尤指國家或政府作為中間人在兩國間）協調、安排或協商一份協議的細節：</span>a peace plan brokered by the UN 由聯合國出面協商的和平計劃 / The United Nations brokered a peace in Mogadishu at the end of March. 3月末，聯合國出面在摩加迪沙進行了和平調解。

<span class="vocabulary">**talk**</span> [tɔ:k] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] talks (between A and B) (on/over sth)（政府或組織之間、或僱主和僱員之間正式的）洽談，會談，談判。也可用單數：</span>high-level talk 高層會談 / formal, official talk 正式會談；官方會談 / private, secret talk 私人商談；秘密商談 / bilateral talk 雙邊會談 / political talk 政治談判 / peace talk 和平談判 / merger, trade talk 兼併談判；貿易談判 / Talks between management and workers broke down over the issue of pay. 勞資雙方就工資的談判破裂了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**tackle**</span> [ˈtækl]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb (about sth)（坦率地）向某人交涉（困難情況）：</span>I tackled him about the money he owed me. 我就他欠我錢的事與他進行了交涉。

相關詞義延伸：
· talk還可指討論，談論，商談，洽談（通常指嚴肅或重大的事）。

周邊詞彙補充：
· reconciliation可指（對人們或群體之間的分歧作出的）調解、和解；
· referee可指爭端的調解人，仲裁員。
