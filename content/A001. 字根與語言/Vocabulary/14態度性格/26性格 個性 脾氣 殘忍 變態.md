☀ <span class="category">**性格、性情：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**character**</span> ['kærɪktə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.]（使不同的人或群體相互區別的）性格：</span>a negative side to his character 他性格中的消極一面 / strong character 堅強的性格 / weak character 軟弱的性格 / It’s not in his character to tell lies. 撒謊不符合他的性格。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**disposition**</span> [ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] 性格、性情：</span>to have a cheerful disposition 性情開朗 / The rides are unsuitable for people of a nervous disposition. 騎馬這一活動不適合天性易緊張的人。/ He was a man of decisive action and an adventurous disposition. 他是一個行動果斷又愛冒險的人。/ Do people have a natural disposition to be good? 人性本善嗎？/ These dogs show a very sociable disposition. 這些狗顯得十分合群。
   
<span class="vocabulary">**temperament**</span> [ˈtemprəmənt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] 人或動物的在行為（或對外界情況作出的反應）中所反應出來的本性，如氣質，性情，性格，秉性：</span>to have an artistic temperament 有藝術家的氣質 / a horse with an excellent temperament 性情溫順的馬 / sunny temperament 陽關的性格 / docile temperament（尤英）溫順的性情 / cool temperament 冷酷的性格 / She's a dreamer and a romantic by temperament. 她生性異想天開，浪漫多情。/ His impulsive temperament regularly got him into difficulties. 他容易衝動，經常因此惹麻煩。/ She was furtive and vicious by temperament. 她本來做事就偷偷摸摸，心腸狠毒。/ He doesn't have the right temperament for the job. 他的性格不適合這份工作。<span class="definition">2 [U] 容易衝動、脾氣暴躁且行為喜怒無常的一種性情：</span>an actor given to displays of temperament 性情喜怒無常的演員 / Mark does have a habit of allowing his temperament to get the better of him. 馬克的確經常由著自己的性子來。/ Some of the models were given to fits of temperament. 有些模特喜怒無常。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**temperamental**</span> [ˌtemprəˈmentl]
<span class="definition">adj. 性情的、性格的、氣質的：</span>They are firm friends in spite of temperamental differences. 他們儘管性格不同，但仍然友情甚篤。

<span class="vocabulary">**personality**</span> [ˌpɜ:səˈnæləti; 美 ˌpɜ:rs-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] 人的（區別於別人的）性格、個性、人格：</span>bright, bubbly, extrovert（尤英）, jovial, lively, outgoing, sparkling, vibrant, vivacious personality 開朗的性格；活潑的個性；外向的個性；樂觀的性格；風趣的性格 / attractive, charming, lovely（尤英）, nice, pleasant, warm personality 迷人的個性；討人喜歡的個性；和藹可親的性格 / her amiable nature and easygoing personality 她和藹的性格和隨和的個性 / dominant, dynamic, forceful, formidable, powerful, strong personality 強勢的個性；堅強的個性；令人敬畏的人格 / authoritarian personality 專制人格 / vulnerable（英）, weak personality 脆弱的個性；軟弱的性格 / She manages to project a very distinct personality. 她設法表現出非常鮮明的個性。/ quirky personality 古怪的個性 / complex personality 複雜的個性 / volatile personality 反覆無常的個性 / creative personality 創造性人格 / dual, multiple, split personality 雙重人格；多重人格；分裂人格 / He developed a split personality after the crash. 那次撞車後他變得人格分裂了。/ Addictive personalities can run in families. 成癮個性會在家族中世代相傳。/ antisocial personality 反社會個性 / Barbara is/has a very forceful personality. 芭芭拉是一個個性很強的人。/ The events of her early life shaped her personality. 她童年經歷的事塑造了她的個性。/ His choice of clothes reflects his personality. 他對衣服的選擇反映了他的個性。/ She has brought out her husband's personality since their relationship began. 從他們談戀愛以來，她就讓丈夫完全釋放出其個性。/ The job didn't really suit my personality. 這項工作並不太適合我的性格。/ personality type 個性型別 / personality characteristic, personality quirk（尤美）, personality trait 性格特點；性格怪癖；性格特徵 / personality disorder, personality flaw, personality problem 人格障礙；人格缺陷；人格問題 / personality development個性發展 / personality change 個性變化 / personality assessment（尤美）, personality profile, personality test個性評估；個性分析；個性測試 / From your personality profile, it seems you're interested in politics. 從你的個性分析結果來看，你似乎對政治感興趣。/ All candidates have to undergo a personality test. 所有候選人都必須接受個性測試。/ There was a personality clash between two members of the committee. 兩名委員會成員性格不合。/ personality conflicts between a faculty member and a student 師生之間的性格衝突 / For the first time she was seeing the more unpleasant aspects of her husband's personality. 她第一次看到了丈夫個性中不那麼令人愉快的方面。/ She stamped her personality on the company. 公司帶有她鮮明的個性色彩。/ His wife has a strong personality. 他妻子的個性很強。/ The children all have very different personalities. 孩子們的性格各不相同。/ He maintained order by sheer force of personality. 他純憑人格力量維護秩序。/ There are likely to be tensions and personality clashes in any social group. 任何社會團體都容易出現關係緊張和性格衝突。/ She has such a kind, friendly personality. 她個性善良友好。/ Through sheer force of personality Hugh Trenchard had got his way. 休·特倫查德完全憑著個人魅力就能隨心所欲。/ He has achieved success by the sheer strength of his personality. 他全憑自己人格的力量獲得了成功。<span class="definition">2 [U] 一個人有趣的或有魅力的品質，如魅力、氣質：</span>We need someone with lots of personality to head the project. 我們需要一位富有魅力的人來主持這個專案。/ a woman of great personality 極有個性的女人 <span class="definition">3 [C] 性格鮮明的人；有突出個性的人：</span>Their son is a real personality. 他們的兒子真是有個性。/ He is such a personality--he is so funny. 他很有個性魅力——非常風趣。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**aura**</span> [ˈɔ:rə]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (of sth)（某人的）氣質：</span>She had an aura of authority. 她給人一種很有權威的感覺。/ He exuded an aura of wealth and power. 他顯露出一種富人和掌權者的氣質。/ She always has an aura of confidence. 她總是滿有信心的樣子。/ The picture seemed to take on the aura of an ancient work of art. 這幅畫似乎帶有古代畫作的風格。/ The sunlight created an aura of beauty around her. 陽光使她渾身顯露著美感。/ I felt an evil aura radiating from him. 他給我一種邪惡的感覺。/ There was always a faint aura of mystery about her. 她總是有點兒神秘兮兮的。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**make-up**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 某人的性格：</span>Jealousy is not part of his make-up. 嫉妒不是他的本質。/ a person's genetic make-up 一個人的遺傳性格 / There was some fatal flaw in his makeup, and as time went on he lapsed into long silences or became off-hand. 他的性格有致命的缺陷，隨著時間的推移，他陷入了久久的沉默，或者變得漠然。/ emotional, mental, psychological make-up 情感特質；精神特質；心理素質 / the unique make-up of each person 每個人的獨特性格

<span class="vocabulary">**streak**</span> [stri:k]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指不好的）性格特徵：</span>strong streak 強勢的個性 / hidden streak 隱藏的個性 / cruel, mean, nasty, ruthless, selfish, vicious, violent streak 殘酷的性格；卑鄙的性格；惡毒的性格；無情的性格；自私的性格；邪惡的性格；暴力的性格 / independent, rebellious, stubborn streak 獨立的性格；叛逆的性格；固執的性格 / adventurous, competitive streak 愛冒險的性格；爭強好勝的性格 / puritanical 清教徒般的性格 / Most of the players have a strong competitive streak. 多數運動員都非常爭強好勝。/ She suddenly revealed a mean streak in her character.她突然間顯露出個性中卑鄙的一面。/ trips for those with an adventurous streak 適合有冒險精神的人的旅行 / There was a streak of eccentricity in the family. 這個家族的人性格有點兒古怪。/ a streak of cruelty 幾分殘忍 / We're both alike--there is a streak of madness in us both. 我們倆很像——都有點瘋。/ He's still got a mean streak. 他這人還是有點損。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**temper**</span> [ˈtempə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing., U] 脾氣、易怒的性情：</span>She broke the plates in a fit of temper. 她一氣之下把盤子摔碎了。/ After an hour of waiting, tempers began to fray (= people began to get angry). 等了一個小時後，大夥兒開始冒火了。/ He had a temper and could be nasty. 他脾氣不大好，有時兇得很。/ His short temper had become notorious. 他的臭脾氣是出了名的。/ He broke the chair in a fit of violent temper. 他大發雷霆，把椅子摔壞了。/ He has a nasty temper. 他脾氣很壞。/ I only just managed to keep my temper with him. 我勉強忍住沒有對他發脾氣。/ She loses her temper easily. 她動不動就發脾氣。/ Tempers flared as the traffic jam became worse. 隨著交通堵塞狀況的惡化，人們火氣越來越大。/ temper tantrum 耍小孩兒脾氣 / He had to learn to keep his temper under control before he could become a teacher. 他得先學會控制自己的脾氣，然後才能當老師。/ He must learn to control his temper. 他得學會捺住性子。<span class="definition">adj. -tempered 用於構成形容詞，指有…脾氣的：</span>good- tempered, bad-tempered 脾氣好；脾氣壞 / a sweet-tempered child 性情溫和的小孩

<span class="vocabulary">**tendency**</span> ['tendənsɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] tendency (for sb/sth) (to do sth) / tendency (to/towards sth) 性情（使人以某種方式來行事的因素，且常指不好的性情或偏好、傾向等）：</span>I have a tendency to talk too much when I’m nervous. 我緊張時總愛嘮叨。/ She has a strong natural tendency towards caution. 她天生非常小心謹慎。/ He displayed artistic tendencies at an early age. 他小時候就顯示出了藝術氣質。/ criminal tendency 犯罪傾向 / homosexual tendency 同性戀傾向
           
<span class="vocabulary">**strain**</span> [streɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] 個人或群體的某個性格傾向、個性、稟性：</span>He had a definite strain of snobbery in him. 他這個人明顯有一股勢利小人的氣味。

<span class="vocabulary">**self**</span> [self] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式] 也可用單數的the self，（有別於別人的）個性、性格等：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**shell**</span> [ʃel]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [sing.] 人的表面性格、表面人格、表面感情、外表：</span>She had developed a shell of indifference. 她養成一副冷漠的外表。/ She had built up a protective shell of indifference around herself. 她給自己建了個冷漠的“保護殼”，躲了進去。<span class="definition">2 [sing.] 某人的自我封閉的外殼，算是一種比喻用法：</span>Her normally shy son had come out of his shell. 她那平時很害羞的兒子已經開始與人交往了。/ He's really come out of his shell since he met Marie. 自從遇見瑪麗以後，他真的變開朗了。/ a lonely boy struggling to emerge from his shell 一個努力想要走出自我封閉的孤獨男孩 / to get/go/retreat/withdraw into your shell 變得羞怯（或內向、孤僻）

<span class="vocabulary">**heart**</span> [hɑːt] 
<span class="definition">adj. -hearted 用於構成形容詞，指有某種性格的：</span>cold-hearted 冷酷的

<span class="vocabulary">**nature**</span> ['neɪtʃə] 
<span class="definition">adj. -natured 用於構成形容詞，指有…性情的：</span>a good-natured girl 脾氣好的女孩

相關詞義延伸：
· character還可指強硬的個人品質，如處理困難或危險情況的能力。也可指（使不同的人或群體相互區別的）品質。亦可指一個人，尤指一個令人討厭的或古怪的人。或可指一個不同尋常的人；
· disposition還可指行為的傾向；
· temperamental還可指容易衝動的、愛發脾氣的、性格乖張、喜怒無常的。

· 表示“品質”、“人品”的詞群見[[01道德 品質]]

☀ <span class="category">**殘忍的、惡毒的、變態的：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**cruel**</span> ['krʊəl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 cruel (to sb/sth) 指人（或其他生命體）有造成痛苦或苦難的慾望的，即殘忍的，殘酷的，殘暴的，冷酷的：</span>a cruel dictator 殘暴的獨裁者 / a cruel regime 殘暴的政權 / I can’t stand people who are cruel to animals. 我無法容忍虐待動物的人。/ Her eyes were cruel and hard. 她目光冷酷。/ Sometimes you have to be cruel to be kind. 有時候為了某人好你就得對他狠。<span class="definition">2 指情況或事件引起痛苦或苦難的：</span>cruel punishment, cruel joke 殘酷的懲罰；挖苦人的笑話 / Her father’s death was a cruel blow. 父親去世對她是一大打擊。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**cruelty**</span> [ˈkru:əlti]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U]（尤指蓄意的） 殘酷，殘忍，殘暴：</span>cruelty to animals 對動物的虐待 / He had been unable to escape the cruelties of war. 他沒能逃脫戰爭的殘酷。/ an act of extreme cruelty 一種極端殘酷的行為 / deliberate cruelty（尤英）蓄意的殘暴 / The deliberate cruelty of his words cut her like a knife. 他故意說的殘酷無情的話對她像刀割一樣。/ casual cruelty 不經意的殘酷 / animal, child（尤英）cruelty 虐待動物；虐待兒童 / Cases of child cruelty occur more often than they are observed. 虐待兒童的事件實際發生的比看到的要多。/ the depths of human cruelty 人類的極端殘忍 / How can you inflict such cruelty on a child? 你怎能如此虐待一個孩子? / Her stepmother showed her nothing but cruelty. 她的繼母對她只有虐待。/ The children suffered mental culmination and neglect. 孩子們受到精神虐待，缺乏關愛。/ She was shocked to see such cruelty. 看到這樣的殘忍行為她很震驚。<span class="definition">2 [C, usually pl.] 殘暴的行為：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**atrocious**</span> [əˈtrəʊʃəs; 美 əˈtroʊ-]

<span class="vocabulary">**tyrannical**</span> [tɪˈrænɪkl]
<span class="definition">adj. 利用自己的權力或權威對別人施加不公平或殘暴的行為的，即殘暴的：</span>one of the world's most oppressive and tyrannical regimes. 世上最壓迫人、最專制的政權之一 / Tyrannical Hollywood film moguls ruled their stars' lives. 好萊塢電影巨頭操縱著旗下明星的生活。/ He killed his tyrannical father with a blow to the head. 他衝著暴戾的父親的頭部猛擊了一下，把他打死了。/ His behavior grew more unpredictable by the day, and increasingly tyrannical. 他的行為越來越難以捉摸，而且愈發殘暴。   

<span class="vocabulary">**barbarous**</span> [ˈbɑ:bərəs; 美 ˈbɑ:rb-]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 極端殘酷的、駭人聽聞的：</span>the barbarous treatment of these prisoners of war 對這些戰俘的殘酷待遇 / barbarous attack 兇殘的進攻 / barbarous crime 駭人聽聞的罪行
           
<span class="vocabulary">**brutal**</span> [ˈbru:tl]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 殘暴的、獸性的：</span>brutal attack, brutal murder, brutal rape, brutal killing 野蠻的攻擊；兇殘的謀殺；野蠻的強姦；兇殘的殺害 / the brutal suppression of anti-government protests 對反政府抗議的殘酷鎮壓 <span class="definition">2 不考慮別人的感情的、冷酷的：</span>With brutal honesty she told him she did not love him. 她冷酷地直接告訴他，她不愛他。<span class="definition">3 殘酷無情的、難以忍受的：</span>The dip in prices this summer will be brutal. 今年夏天價格的大幅下跌將會很殘酷。/ The afternoon sun had been brutal. 下午的太陽酷熱難擋。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**brutality**</span> [bru:ˈtæləti]

<span class="vocabulary">**bestial**</span> [ˈbestiəl; 美 ˈbestʃəl]

 <span class="vocabulary">**fierce**</span> [fɪəs] 
<span class="definition">adj.（尤指人或動物以令人害怕的方式）發怒且具有攻擊性的。如兇猛的、兇狠的、兇殘的：</span>a fierce dog 惡狗 / fierce eyes 兇狠的目光 / He suddenly looked fierce. 他突然面露兇相。/ She spoke in a fierce whisper. 她惡狠狠地低聲說話。
                     
<span class="vocabulary">**ferocious**</span> [fəˈrəʊʃəs; 美 -ˈroʊ-]

<span class="vocabulary">**ferocity**</span> [fəˈrɒsəti; 美 fəˈrɑ:s-]

<span class="vocabulary">**savage**</span> [ˈsævɪdʒ]

<span class="vocabulary">**callous**</span> [ˈkæləs]

<span class="vocabulary">**mean**</span> [mi:n] 
<span class="definition">adj. [尤美] 有可能要發怒或變得暴力的，即要發怒的，要發狂的；脾氣壞的；殘忍的：</span>That’s a mean-looking dog. 那狗看上去很兇啊。/ a mean man 殘忍的人
           
<span class="vocabulary">**harsh**</span> [hɑ:ʃ; 美 hɑ:rʃ]
<span class="definition">adj. 1（言行）無情的：</span>It may seem harsh to criticize him after his death. 在他死後還批評他似乎太無情了。<span class="definition">2（現實、真相）殘酷的、無情的：</span>We had to face up to the harsh realities of life sooner or later. 我們遲早都得正視生活的嚴酷現實。/ The harsh truth is that luck plays a big part in who will live or die. 殘酷的事實是，誰死誰活在很大程度上要靠運氣。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**inhuman**</span> [ɪnˈhju:mən]
<span class="definition">adj. 不人道的、無同情心的、冷酷無情的：</span>inhuman and degrading treatment 不人道的羞辱性對待 / The detainees are often held in cruel and inhuman conditions. 被拘留者的關押環境常常惡劣嚴酷、很不人道。/ We regard their treatment of the prisoners as inhuman. 我們覺得他們對待囚犯很不人道。/ The barbaric slaughter of whales is unnecessary and inhuman. 對鯨的野蠻屠殺既不必要也不人道。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**relentless**</span> [rɪˈlentləs]
<span class="definition">adj. 嚴格的、苛刻的、無情的：</span>a relentless enemy 殘酷的敵人
           
<span class="vocabulary">**ruthless**</span> [ˈru:θləs]
<span class="definition">adj. [貶]（人或其行為）殘酷無情的、殘忍的：</span>a ruthless dictator 殘酷無情的獨裁者 / The way she behaved towards him was utterly ruthless. 她對待他真是無情至極。/ The President was ruthless in dealing with any hint of internal political dissent. 在處理任何內部政治分歧的苗頭時，總統都毫不留情。/ an invasion by a ruthless totalitarian power 殘忍的極權主義國家的侵略
          
<span class="vocabulary">**sadistic**</span> [səˈdɪstɪk]
<span class="definition">adj. 施虐狂的（尤指性施虐狂的）：</span>He took sadistic pleasure in taunting the boy. 嘲諷這孩子讓他感到施虐的快感。/ The prisoners rioted against mistreatment by sadistic guards. 囚犯因不堪忍受獄警施虐而發動了暴亂。/ There was a sadistic streak in him. 他有虐待狂的傾向。           

<span class="vocabulary">**tyrannical**</span> [tɪˈrænɪkl]
<span class="definition">adj.（人、政府或組織）暴君的、殘暴的、專橫的、專制的：</span>He killed his tyrannical father with a blow to the head. 他衝著暴戾的父親的頭部猛擊了一下，把他打死了。/ His behavior grew more unpredictable by the day, and increasingly tyrannical. 他的行為越來越難以捉摸，而且愈發殘暴。/ one of the world's most oppressive and tyrannical regimes 世上最壓迫人、最專制的政權之一
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vicious**</span> [ˈvɪʃəs]
<span class="definition">adj. 1（人或其行為）狂暴的、殘酷的：</span>He was a cruel and vicious man. 他是一個殘忍兇狠的男人。/ He suffered a vicious attack by a gang of white youths. 他遭到一幫白人青年的殘暴襲擊。/ a particularly vicious and brutal crime 一樁極為殘忍、野蠻的罪行 <span class="definition">2（動物）兇猛危險的：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">3（攻擊、批評等）充滿仇恨和憤怒的，如惡毒的、嚴厲的：</span>It is a deliberate, nasty and vicious attack on a young man's character. 這是對一個年輕人品格蓄意的、卑鄙的、惡毒的攻擊。/ her shrewish temperament and vicious tongue 她暴躁的性格和惡毒的言語 / She wrote me a vicious letter. 她給我寫了一封嚴厲的信。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**malevolent**</span> [məˈlevələnt]
<span class="definition">adj. [常用於名詞前] 有惡意的、心腸壞的、存心不良的：</span>Her stare was malevolent, her mouth a thin line. 她目光狠毒，唇緊抿。

<span class="vocabulary">**vengeful**</span> [ˈvendʒfl]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 想報仇的、圖謀復仇的：</span>He was stabbed to death by his vengeful wife. 他被圖謀報復的妻子拿刀捅死了。/ He did not think he was any more cruel, any more vengeful than other men. 他認為自己並不比其他人更冷酷，也不比別人更愛報復。

<span class="vocabulary">**virulent**</span> [ˈvɪrələnt; -rjəl-]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 惡毒的、狠毒的、不共戴天的：</span>virulent criticism 惡意的批評 / virulent nationalism 不共戴天的民族主義 / Now he faces virulent attacks from the Italian media. 現在他面臨著義大利媒體的惡毒攻擊。/ Friends spoke of 'a virulent personal campaign' being waged against him. 朋友們說針對他正在發起“一場惡毒的個人攻擊”。

<span class="vocabulary">**sick**</span> [sɪk] 
<span class="definition">adj. [非正式] 從做古怪或殘忍的事中獲得樂趣，即變態的，病態的：</span>a sick mind 變態的心理 / a sick society 病態的社會 / He’s sick for having a rat as a pet. 他居然把耗子當寵物養，真夠變態的。/ Whoever started the fire must be sick in the head. 放火的人肯定是腦子有病。

相關詞義延伸：
· savage還可指（動物）兇惡地攻擊某人（造成重傷）。

周邊詞彙補充：
· draconian可指（法律、懲罰、措施等）極端殘忍的、嚴酷的、德拉古式的；
· atrocity可指（尤指戰爭中的）殘忍和暴力行為，即殘暴；
· harden可表示使某人變得無情或不再被極端情況影響；
· foul可指極其邪惡或殘忍的。
