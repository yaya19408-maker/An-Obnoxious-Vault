☀ <span class="category">**喜歡、喜愛、熱愛、關愛的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**like**</span> [laɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 1 喜歡，喜愛（即認為…好，有吸引力，足夠符合…標準，令人享受到樂趣等）：</span>She’s nice. I like her. 她人很好，我喜歡她。/ I have always liked Sue and I don’t intend to stop now. 我一直喜歡蘇，現在也不打算放棄。/ Which tie do you like best? 你最喜歡哪條領帶？/ How did you like Japan? 你覺得日本怎麼樣？ <span class="definition">2 喜歡做某事；喜歡以某種方式製作的東西；喜歡某事以某種方式發生：</span>At weekends I like to sleep late. 週末我愛睡懶覺。/ I like to get to airports in good time. 我喜歡提前到機場。/ She’s never liked swimming. 她從不喜歡游泳。/ I like my coffee strong. 咖啡我愛喝濃的。

<span class="vocabulary">**love**</span> [lʌv] 
<span class="definition">vt. 愛；熱愛；疼愛（某人）：</span>I love you. 我愛你。/ his much-loved brother 他至愛的兄弟 / He had become a well-loved member of staff. 他已成為受人喜愛的職員。/ I love my country. 我熱愛我的國家。<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 喜歡或喜愛（某事物或某事）：</span>I really love summer evenings. 我非常喜歡夏天的夜晚。/ I absolutely love your shoes! 我太愛你這雙鞋了！/ He loved the way she smiled. 他喜歡她微笑的樣子。/ He loved her to sing to him. 他喜歡讓她唱歌給他聽。

<span class="vocabulary">**adore**</span> [ə'dɔ:] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 非常愛某人，如愛慕；疼愛。程度較深：</span>It’s obvious that she adores him. 她顯然深深地愛著他。/ She absolutely adores her grandchildren. 她極愛自己的孫輩。<span class="definition">2 [非正式] 非常喜愛（即熱愛）某事物：</span>I simply adore his music! 我簡直太喜歡他的音樂了！/ She adores working with children. 她熱愛為兒童工作。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**worship**</span> [ˈwɜ:ʃɪp; 美 ˈwɜ:rʃɪp]
<span class="definition">vt. 熱愛某人，尤指看不到對方的缺點：</span>She worships her children. 她極度疼愛自己的兒女。/ He worshipped her from afar (= he loved her but did not tell her his feelings). 他暗戀著她。/ She worships the ground he walks on. 她對他的愛達到痴迷的程度。/ She had worshipped him for years. 她仰慕他已有多年。          
           
<span class="vocabulary">**idolize**</span> [ˈaɪdəlaɪz]
<span class="definition">vt. 非常喜愛某人：</span>A tall, soft-spoken woman, she is idolized by her young patients. 她個子高挑，說話輕聲細語，深受年輕患者的喜愛。/ They idolize their kids. 他們溺愛自己的孩子。

<span class="vocabulary">**cherish**</span> [ˈtʃerɪʃ]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 對某人或某事物極其喜愛並對其有一種保護欲，如珍愛、愛護：</span>Children need to be cherished. 兒童需要無微不至的愛護。/ He genuinely loved and cherished her. 他由衷地愛戀並珍惜她。/ her most cherished possession 她最珍愛的物品 / The previous owners had cherished the house. 房子先前的主人非常愛惜這房子。<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 珍視、維護、捍衛（權利、特權或原則）：</span>These people cherish their independence and sovereignty. 這些人民珍視他們的獨立和主權。

<span class="vocabulary">**treasure**</span> ['treӡə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 擁有（或保有）所愛的且極其珍貴的事物，即珍視，珍藏，珍愛，相當於cherish：</span>to treasure the past memories 珍藏往昔的記憶
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**fuss**</span> [fʌs]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ over sb 對某人過分關愛：</span>Auntie Hilda and Uncle Jack couldn't fuss over them enough. 希爾達姨媽和傑克姨父對他們寵愛有加。

<span class="vocabulary">**relish**</span> [ˈrelɪʃ]
<span class="definition">vt. 非常想要做某事（或擁有某東西），即喜歡：</span>to relish a fight, to relish a challenge, to relish a debate 喜歡打架；喜歡挑戰；喜歡爭辯 / to relish the idea of sth 欣賞某種觀點 / I relish the challenge of doing jobs that others turn down. 我喜歡挑戰別人拒絕做的工作。/ He ate quietly, relishing his meal. 他安靜地吃著，細細品味著食物。/ Nobody relishes cleaning the oven. 沒有人喜歡清理烤爐。/ She secretly relished the thought of being alone with him. 一想起與他單獨在一起，她就暗自歡喜。

<span class="vocabulary">**concern**</span> [kən'sə:n] 
<span class="definition">vt. concern yourself with/about sth對某事物感興趣：</span>He didn’t concern himself with the details. 他對細節不感興趣。

<span class="vocabulary">**see**</span> [si:] 
<span class="definition">vt. 看中（某人或某事物的對其有吸引力的品質）：</span>Frankly, I don’t know what Paul sees in her. 坦白地說，我不知道保羅看中她什麼了。

<span class="vocabulary">**fancy**</span> ['fænsɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. [英，非正式] 為某人的性吸引力所吸引，如愛慕：</span>I think she fancies me. 我認為她喜歡我。

<span class="vocabulary">**please**</span> [pli:z] 
<span class="definition">vi. 常用於as或what, where等詞後，表示想要（或選擇、喜歡）做某事：</span>You may stay as long as you please. 你想待多久就待多久。/ She always does exactly as she pleases. 她總是我行我素。/ I’m free now to live wherever I please. 我現在想住哪裡就住哪裡。

<span class="vocabulary">**warm**</span> [wɔ:m] 
<span class="definition">vi. 對一個人或主意產生好感或興趣：</span>His behaviour warmed his neighbours. 他的行為讓他的鄰居們對他產生了好感。

<span class="vocabulary">**dig**</span> [dɪɡ] 
<span class="definition">vt. [舊時，俚語] 非常贊成或喜歡…：</span>瞭解即可
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**cling**</span> [klɪŋ]
<span class="definition">vi. [常貶] ~ (to sb)（尤指情感上）依戀，依附某人：</span>After her mother's death, Sara clung to her aunt more than ever. 薩拉在母親去世後比以往任何時候都更依附於她的姨媽。/ I was terrified he would leave me, so I was clinging to him. 我害怕他會離開我，所以纏著他不放。

<span class="vocabulary">**endear**</span> [ɪnˈdɪə(r); 美 -ˈdɪr]
<span class="definition">vt. endear sb/yourself to sb 使受喜愛、使受歡迎、使受青睞：</span>Their taste for gambling has endeared them to Las Vegas casino owners. 他們因好賭而深受拉斯維加斯賭場老闆的歡迎。/ He has endeared himself to the American public. 他已經贏得了美國民眾的好感。/ Their policies on taxation didn't endear them to voters. 他們的稅收政策並沒使他們受到選民的歡迎。/ She was a talented teacher who endeared herself to all who worked with her. 她是一位深受同事喜愛的有才華的教師。/ She had an unfriendly manner which did not exactly endear her to her colleagues. 她態度不友好，不討同事的喜歡。

☀ <span class="category">**喜歡、喜愛、熱愛、關愛的名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**love**</span> [lʌv] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 對…的一種深深的喜愛（尤指對家庭成員或朋友），即愛；熱愛；慈愛；疼愛。是此義的常規用詞和廣義用詞。程度居於like和adore之間：</span>a mother’s love for her children 母親對孩子的愛 / brotherly love 兄弟般的愛 / love of your country 對祖國的熱愛 <span class="definition">2 [U] 愛情，戀愛：</span>They fell in love with each other. 他們彼此相愛了。/ It was love at first sight. 那是一見鍾情。/ love letter, love note 情書；傳情紙條 / love poem, love song, love story 情詩；愛情歌曲；愛情故事 / secret love 暗戀 <span class="definition">3 [U, sing.] 對樂趣的享受，即喜愛，喜好：</span>a love of learning 對知識的熱愛 / a love of literature 對文學的熱愛 / love of the natural world 對自然界的熱愛 / He’s in love with his work. 他熱愛自己的工作。/ I fell in love with the house. 我喜歡上了這房子。/ I fell in love with the movies. 我非常喜歡這些電影。<span class="definition">4 [C] 非常喜愛的人、事物或活動：</span>Take care, my love. 保重，我的愛人。/ He was the love of my life. 他是我一生中最愛的人。/ Tennis is my first love. 網球是我的第一大愛好。
          
<span class="vocabulary">**liking**</span> [ˈlaɪkɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] ~ (for sb/sth) 對…的喜歡、喜好、享受：</span>He had a liking for fast cars. 他喜歡快車。/ She had a liking for good clothes. 她喜歡高階服裝。/ He bought me records to encourage my liking for music. 他給我買了唱片，希望培養我對音樂的興趣。/ She had taken a liking to him on their first meeting. 她對他一見鍾情。/ I took an instant liking to her. 我立刻就喜歡上了她。/ They have little liking for each other. 他們彼此沒有什麼好感。/ The weather's too hot for my liking. 這天氣太熱了，我不喜歡。/ The food wasn't really to my liking. 食物不太合我的胃口。/ We share a liking for Italian cooking. 我們都喜歡義大利菜。

<span class="vocabulary">**fondness**</span> [fɒndnəs;美ˈfɑndnɪs]
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.] ~ (for sb/sth) 喜愛：</span>He will be remembered by the staff with great fondness. 全體人員將深摯地懷念他。/ a fondness for animals 喜愛動物 / She seems genuinely fondness of the children. 她看上去真心喜歡這些小孩。           

<span class="vocabulary">**beloved**</span> [bɪˈlʌvɪd]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [舊時或文學] 被深愛的一個人，即心愛的人：</span>It was a gift from her beloved. 那是她心愛的人送的禮物。/ He takes his beloved into his arms. 他將心愛之人擁入懷中。

<span class="vocabulary">**like**</span> [laɪk] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] likes可表示喜好或愛好：</span>her likes and dislikes 她的好惡

<span class="vocabulary">**affection**</span> [ə'fekʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, sing.] affection (for sb/sth) 非常喜歡（或熱愛）某人（或某事物）並對其表示關心，可稱為關愛、關懷等：</span>Mr Darcy’s affection for his sister 達西先生對他妹妹的關愛之情 / Children need lots of love and affection. 孩子需要多多疼愛和關懷。/ She had developed a real affection for him. 她逐漸對他產生了真愛。/ He didn’t show his wife any affection. 他沒有向妻子表示一點愛。/ The teacher showed affection to all her students. 這位老師對她所有的學生都充滿了愛。/ She was held in deep affection by all her students. 她的學生都十分愛戴她。/ I have a great affection for New York. 我很喜歡紐約。/ He has a great affection for animals. 他非常喜愛動物。<span class="definition">2 [pl.] [正式] affections 一個人所懷有的愛戀之情：</span>Anne had two men trying to win her affections. 安妮有兩個男人追求。/ The object of his affections was a singer. 他的意中人是個歌手。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**affinity**</span> [əˈfɪnəti]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [正式] ~ (for/with sb/sth) / ~ (between A and B) 喜愛、喜好，對與自己有著某種相似特徵或品質的人或事物的一種熟識感、親近感：</span>Sam was born in the country and had a deep affinity with nature. 薩姆在鄉下出生，特別喜愛大自然。/ He has a close affinity with the landscape he knew when he was growing up. 他對這片從小就瞭解的土地有著一種歸屬感。/ I felt a great affinity with the people of the islands. 我被島上的人深深地吸引。/ There is a natural affinity between British and Asian women. 英國女性和亞洲女性之間有一種自然的親近感。/ He has a natural affinity with numbers. 他天生就對數字有一種親近感。/ Many girls do show an affinity for craft skills. 很多女孩子確實喜愛手工。/ an affinity with animals 對動物的喜愛 / She felt an affinity with earlier poets. 她喜歡較早時期的詩人。/ It's important that you share an affinity with your husband. 和丈夫有共同的愛好十分重要。/ an affinity between the two women 兩個女人之間的親密關係 / Tom feels a great affinity towards Pamela. 湯姆十分喜歡帕梅拉。       
           
<span class="vocabulary">**attachment**</span> [əˈtætʃmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 對某人或某事物的依戀或愛慕：</span>close, deep, passionate, special, strong 親密的感情；深深的譽戀；強烈的愛燕之情；特殊的依戀; 深情 / enduring, lasting attachment 長久的眷戀；恆久的愛慕 / emotional, personal, romantic, sentimental attachment 感情上的眷戀；個人的依戀；浪漫的愛情；牽腸掛肚的眷戀 / I feel no attachment to the countryside. 我對農村沒有感情。/ Prisoners can develop attachments to their guards. 犯人可能會對看守逐漸產生感情。/ her strong attachment to her mother 她對母親強烈的依戀 / a child's strong attachment to its parents 孩子對父母的強烈依戀 / Mother and child form a close attachment. 母子情深。/ As a teenager she formed a strong attachment to one of her teachers. 少女時代的她曾迷上了自己的一個老師。

<span class="vocabulary">**passion**</span> [ˈpæʃn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] ~ (for sb) 強烈的愛情：</span>the expression of love and passion 愛和情慾的表達 / His passion for her made him blind to everything else. 他鐘情於她，達到了不顧一切的地步。/ my passion for a dark-haired, slender boy named James 我對一個名叫詹姆斯的黑頭髮高挑個兒男孩的熱戀 / She didn't believe in grand passion or love at first sight. 她不相信如火的激情或一見鍾情。/ She was his first great passion. 她是他第一個深愛的人。/ animal（舊時）, physical, romantic, sexual passion 獸慾；浪漫的激情；性慾 / They kissed with passion. 他們熱烈地接吻。/ The passion between them had cooled. 他們之間的激情已經冷卻下來了。/ his all-consuming passion for her 他對她痴狂的激情 / They spent a night of passion in a hotel. 他們在旅館裡發生了一夜情。/ the young girl who was the object of his passion 他痴戀的那個女孩 / As time went by the passion cooled. 隨著時間的推移，激情冷卻了。<span class="definition">2 [C] ~ (for sth) 對某事物的酷愛；熱衷的愛好（或活動等）：</span>She had a passion for gardening. 她酷愛園藝。/ His other great passion was Italy. 他酷愛的另一個國家是義大利。/ Music is a passion with him. 他對音樂情有獨鍾。/ He had a secret passion for poetry. 他對詩歌有一種不為人知的鐘愛。/ his lifelong passion for flying 他一生對飛行的酷愛 / They shared a passion for food. 他們都熱愛美食。/ He developed a real passion for acting. 他對錶演產生了真正的熱情。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**crush**</span> [krʌʃ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (on sb) 強烈的愛戀，通常指年輕人對年長者的短暫的熱戀或迷戀：</span>a schoolgirl crush 女學生的迷戀 / I'd got over my schoolgirl crush. 我已經不再像女學生那樣迷戀別人了。/ She had a huge crush on one of her teachers. 她非常愛慕她的一位老師。/ I had a huge crush on her. 我對她愛慕至極。/ She had a crush on you, you know. 她曾愛慕你，你是知道的。
        
<span class="vocabulary">**favourite**</span> ['feɪvərɪt]（美favorite）
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（在同類的人或事物中）所更喜愛的一個人或事物：</span>This song is a particular favourite of mine. 我尤其喜愛這首歌曲。/ family, personal, popular favourite 家庭的最愛；個人的最愛；大眾的最愛 / The forest was a favourite for family walks. 這片森林是人們最喜愛的同家人一起散步的地方。<span class="definition">2 [C] 為某人所寵愛的一個人，比其他人得到更好對待的一個人：</span>She loved all her grandchildren but Ann was her favourite. 她愛所有的孫兒孫女，但最寵安。

<span class="vocabulary">**pet**</span> [pet] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [常貶] 被某人給予過多關注的一個人（常被視作對其他人不公平），即寵兒，紅人，相當於favourite：</span>She’s the teacher’s pet. 她是老師的寶貝疙瘩。

<span class="vocabulary">**treasure**</span> ['treӡə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 單數名詞，指備受寵愛或器重的人：</span>瞭解即可 

<span class="vocabulary">**honey**</span> ['hʌnɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 你所喜歡的（並認為非常好的）一個人，如可愛的人：</span>He can be a real honey when he wants to be. 他高興的時候挺招人喜歡的。

<span class="vocabulary">**lamb**</span> [læm] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 用於慈愛地描述或稱呼某人：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**weakness**</span> ['wi:knɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] weakness (for sth/sb)（對自己非常喜歡的某事物或某人）難以抗拒的，迷戀的，非常喜歡的：</span>I have a real weakness for chocolate. 我簡直無法抵抗巧克力的誘惑。

<span class="vocabulary">**devotion**</span> [dɪ'vəʊʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.]（對某人或某事物的）極大的關愛或支援：</span>His devotion to his wife and family is touching. 他對妻子和家人的關愛感人至深。/ Their single-minded devotion to the care of the dying was admirable. 他們對垂死者的盡心照顧真是令人欽佩。

<span class="vocabulary">**feeling**</span> ['fi:lɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, pl.] feeling (for sb/sth)（對某人或某事物的）愛：</span>I still have feelings for her. 我仍然愛她。
             
<span class="vocabulary">**fuss**</span> [fʌs]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] make a ~ of sb 對某人關愛備至：</span>When I arrived my nephews made a big fuss of me. 我到達的時候，侄子們圍著我噓寒問暖。         

<span class="vocabulary">**enthusiasm**</span> [ɪnˈθju:ziæzəm; 美 -ˈθu:-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] ~ (for sth/for doing sth) 對某事的一種強烈的熱情、興趣、熱忱（非常想要參與其中）：</span>I can't say I share your enthusiasm for the idea. 我可不像你那樣，對這個想法那樣熱心。/ She never lost her enthusiasm for teaching. 她從未失去過教書的熱忱。/ He had a real enthusiasm for the work. 他的確熱衷於這項工作。/ The news was greeted with a lack of enthusiasm by those at the meeting. 與會者對這訊息未表現出多少興趣。/ ‘I don't mind, ’ she said, without much enthusiasm. “我不在乎。”她不冷不熱地說。/ The lack of enthusiasm for unification among most West Germans fills him with disappointment. 大多數西德人都沒有渴望統一的熱情，這令他無比失望。/ Their skill, enthusiasm and running has got
