☀ <span class="category">**服從、遵守、屈從、投降的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**obey**</span> [ə'beɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 告訴你怎麼做就怎麼做，即服從，遵從：</span>to obey a command, to obey an order 服從指揮；執行命令 / to obey the law, to obey the rules 遵守法律；遵守規則 / He had always obeyed his parents without question. 他對父母一向絕對服從。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**conform**</span> [kənˈfɔ:m; 美 -ˈfɔ:rm]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (to sth) 在思想或行為上與群體或社會里的大多數人一致，如順從、順應、隨大流：</span>There is considerable pressure on teenagers to conform. 年輕人被大力要求順應主流。/ Many children who can't or don't conform are often bullied. 很多不能或不去隨大流的小孩常常被人欺負。/ He did not feel obliged to conform to the rules that applied to ordinary men. 他覺得自己沒義務去遵守那些普通人才會去遵守的規定。/ He refused to conform to the local customs. 他拒絕遵從當地的風俗習慣。<span class="definition">vi. ~ to/with sth 遵守一項規則、法律等：</span>The toys fail to conform to current safety standards. 這些玩具不符合現行的安全標準。/ This equipment conforms fully with the latest safety regulations. 這件裝置完全符合最新的安全規定。/ The building does not conform with safety regulations. 這座建築物不符合安全條例。/ The accountant's reports appear to conform with the requirements of professional standards. 會計師的報告看起來符合專業標準。/ The Night Rider lamp has been designed to conform to new British Standard safety requirements. “夜行者”燈的設計符合新的英國安全標準的要求。/ The meat market can continue only if it is radically overhauled to conform with strict European standards. 肉類市場只有經過徹底的大檢查，嚴格遵守歐洲標準，才能繼續營業。/ All companies are required to conform to these rules. 所有公司都必須遵守這些規定。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**comply**</span> [kəmˈplaɪ]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (with sth) 遵守一項規則、一條命令等：</span>They refused to comply with the UN resolution. 他們拒絕遵守聯合國的決議。/ The commander said that the army would comply with the ceasefire. 指揮官說軍隊會遵守停火協議。/ Some beaches had failed to comply with European directives on bathing water. 有些海灘未能遵守歐洲有關浴場的指令。/ Candidates must comply strictly with these instructions. 候選人必須嚴格遵守這些指示。/ When requested to leave, they refused to comply. 被要求離開時，他們拒絕服從。

<span class="vocabulary">**observe**</span> [əb'zə:v] 
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 遵守法律、規則、習俗等：</span>to observe the rules 遵守規則 / to observe the ceasefire 遵守該項停火協議 / to observe three minutes’ silence 默哀三分鐘 / This procedure must be correctly observed. 必須正確遵守這一程式。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**adhere**</span> [ədˈhɪə(r); 美 ədˈhɪr]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ to sth [正式] 遵守、遵循（法律、規章、指示、原則等）：</span>All members of the association adhere to a strict code of practice. 協會的所有成員都遵守一套嚴格的行為規範。/ She adhered to the strict Islamic dress code. 她恪守伊斯蘭教嚴格的著裝規定。/ This principle must be strictly adhered to. 必須嚴格遵守這一原則。/ The guidelines were not always fully adhered to. 這些指導方針並非總是被完全貫徹執行。

<span class="vocabulary">**respect**</span> [rɪs'pekt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 同意不違反法律、原則等，即遵守：</span>to respect the constitution 遵守憲法

<span class="vocabulary">**follow**</span> ['fɒləʊ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 接受建議、指示等並依其行事：</span>to follow a diet 按照規定飲食 / Follow my instructions very carefully. 要認真聽從我的指令。/ Why didn’t you follow my advice? 你為什麼不聽我的勸告？

<span class="vocabulary">**keep**</span> [ki:p] 
<span class="definition">vt. keep your promise/word / keep an appointment 恪守（承諾）；遵守（約定）。是一個簡單用詞：</span>She kept her promise to visit them. 她遵守諾言去看望了他們。/ He failed to keep his appointment at the clinic. 他未能按預約的時間去診所。

<span class="vocabulary">**hold**</span> [həʊld] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. [正式] 遵守（諾言或高尚的行為準則）；使…恪守（諾言或高尚的行為準則）：</span>to hold to a promise, to hold to a commitment 恪守承諾 / to hold to high standards of behaviour 恪守高尚的行為準則 / “I won’t make you marry him.”--“I’ll hold you to that.” “我不會讓你嫁給他的。”——“我要你保證說話算話。”

<span class="vocabulary">**bow**</span> [baʊ] 
<span class="definition">vi. bow to 可表示屈從於壓力或某人的意願：</span>She bowed to her parents and married with a man she doesn’t love. 她屈從了父母，嫁給了一個她不愛的人。

<span class="vocabulary">**submit**</span> [səb'mɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. submit (yourself) (to sb/sth) 接受某人或某事物的權威、控制或更大的掌控力，如投降、屈服、順從；還可指因此而接受某事，即不得已而接受。相當於give up to sb/sth或yield：</span>She refused to submit to threats. 她面對威脅，拒不低頭。/ He submitted himself to a search by the guards. 他只好讓衛兵搜查。/ He voluntarily submitted to their control. 他主動服從他們的控制。/ They were forced to submit to foreign rule. 他們被迫屈服於外國的統治。
     
<span class="vocabulary">**surrender**</span> [səˈrendə(r)]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (yourself) (to sb)（向軍方或警方）投降：</span>The rebel soldiers were forced to surrender. 叛軍被迫投降。/ After three weeks under siege, they surrendered completely. 被圍困三週以後，他們徹底投降了。/ The British formally surrendered on 31 May. 英國人於5月31日正式投降。/ He surrendered voluntarily to his enemies. 他自願向敵人投降。/ The hijackers eventually surrendered themselves to the police. 劫機者最終向警方投降。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**yield**</span> [ji:ld]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (to sth/sb) 屈服：</span>After a long siege, the town was forced to yield. 經過長時間的包圍，這座孤城被迫投降。/ He reluctantly yielded to their demands. 他不情願地屈從於他們的要求。/ Will she yield to growing pressure for her to retire? 面對要求其隱退的壓力越來越大，她會屈服嗎？/ I yielded to temptation and had a chocolate bar. 我經不住誘惑，吃了一大塊巧克力。/ I yielded to an impulse. 我沒有忍住衝動。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**succumb**</span> [səˈkʌm]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (to sth) 抵擋不住（攻擊、疾病、誘惑等），如屈服、屈從：</span>The town succumbed after a short siege. 該城被圍困不久即告失守。/ He finally succumbed to Lucy's charms and agreed to her request. 他最終為露西的魅力所傾倒，答應了她的請求。/ Don't succumb to the temptation to have just one cigarette. 不要經不住誘惑，只抽一支菸也不行。/ The Minister said his country would never succumb to pressure. 首相說他的國家永遠都不會屈服於壓迫。/ I was determined not to succumb to the virus. 我堅決不向病毒屈服。/ people who succumb easily to exploitation 容易被利用的人

<span class="vocabulary">**humour**</span> ['hju:mə]（美humor）
<span class="definition">vt.（為使某人高興而）順應或遷就其願望（雖然該願望並不合理）：</span>She thought it best to humour him rather than get into an argument. 她想最好是順他的意，而不和他爭吵。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**mind**</span> [maɪnd] 
<span class="definition">vt. [美，愛爾蘭] 注意某人所說的話並遵守之，即聽從：</span>And the moral of the story is: always mind your mother! 這個故事的寓意是：一定要聽媽媽的話！
           
<span class="vocabulary">**defer**</span> [dɪˈfɜ:(r)]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ to sb 遵從、聽從（某人），即使不認同其觀點，也遵從對方（因為你尊敬對方，或對方權威）：</span>Doctors are encouraged to defer to experts. 鼓勵醫生聽從專家的意見。

<span class="vocabulary">**owe**</span> [əʊ] 
<span class="definition">vt. owe allegiance/loyalty/obedience (to sb) 正式用法，指忠誠於或服從於位高權重者：</span>瞭解即可

相關詞義延伸：
· conform還可指與某事物的樣式、型別等相一致、相符合、相吻合；
· surrender還可指被迫放棄或交出。也可指在權威方的要求下交出或出示票證、護照等。

周邊詞彙補充：
· listen也可指聽信、聽從；
· acquiesce可指即使不認同，依然接受某事物而不爭辯，即默然接受、預設、默許；
· acommodate可指（透過為某人做事來）幫助對方，如迎合、遷就。

☀ <span class="category">**服從、遵守、屈從、投降的名詞和形容詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**surrender**</span> [səˈrendə(r)]         
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, sing.] ~ (to sb/sth)（向軍方或警方）投降：</span>The allied commander demanded their immediate and unconditional surrender. 盟軍指揮官要求他們立即無條件投降。/ to offer them easy terms of surrender 提出優厚的投降條件 / The villagers were starved into surrender. 村民們飢餓難忍，只好投降。/ The rebels hoisted the white flag of surrender. 叛軍升起了白旗投降。/ He raised his hands in mock surrender. 他舉起雙手假裝投降。/ The division took the surrender of a group of some 500 rebels. 這個師接受了約500名叛軍的投降。<span class="definition">2 [U, sing.] 允許自己被某事物控制，如屈從、屈服：</span>the government's surrender to the nationalists' demands 政府向民粹分子要求的屈服 / They accused the government of a surrender to business interests. 他們指責政府唯工商界的利益是從。/ total personal surrender to Jesus 完全順從耶穌 / Depression is a partial surrender to death. 沮喪是對死亡的一定妥協。
             
<span class="vocabulary">**submission**</span> [səbˈmɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 投降、屈服、歸順。相當於surrender：</span>The emperor demanded total submission from his subjects. 皇帝要求臣民絕對服從他。/ They bombed the town into submission. 他們連續轟炸，這個鎮子被迫投降。/ to beat sb into submission 打得某人投降 / The army intends to take the city or simply starve it into submission. 軍隊打算佔領該城市或乾脆截斷其糧食補給迫使其投降。/ a gesture of submission 投降的手勢 / the patient's submission to the demands of the hospital 病人對醫院要求的服從         

<span class="vocabulary">**adherence**</span> [ədˈhɪərəns; 美 ədˈhɪr-]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（對規則等的）遵守：</span>strict adherence to the rules 嚴格遵守規章制度 / strict adherence to the constitution 嚴格遵守憲法
           
<span class="vocabulary">**conformity**</span> [kənˈfɔ:məti; 美 -ˈfɔ:rm-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] [正式] ~ (to/with sth)（對法律、社會規則、個人意願等的）遵守、遵從：</span>The prime minister is, in conformity with the constitution, chosen by the president. 依照憲法，首相由總統選定。/ conformity to the accepted standards 依照公認的標準 / to bring national laws into conformity with international conventions 使國家法律與國際慣例一致 / The procedure is in strict conformity with standard international practices. 這一程式同標準的國際慣例完全一致。/ They do not observe rigid conformity to the doctrines of the church. 他們不是刻板地遵守教會的教義。<span class="definition">2 [U] 按照大多數人的方式來做事：</span>Excessive conformity is usually caused by fear of disapproval. 過於墨守成規通常都是由於擔心得不到認可。/ Pressure appears to be mounting for conformity in how people speak English. 要求人們講規範英語的壓力似乎越來越大。/ They act in unthinking conformity to customs. 他們總是一味地按風俗習慣行事。/ cultural, ideological, political, religious, social conformity 文化一致；意識形態一致；政治一致；宗教從眾；社會從眾 / a society of outward religious conformity 表面上宗教信仰一致的社會 / Governments often invoke patriotism to enforce conformity. 政府往往利用愛國主義來實現步調一致。/ to achieve conformity between all the plans 實現所有計劃協調一致 / We work to ensure conformity with the customer's wishes. 我們努力確保滿足顧客的願望。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**obedience**</span> [əˈbiːdiəns;美oʊˈbidiəns]
<span class="definition">n. [U] ~ (to sb/sth) 順從、遵守、惟命是從：</span>blind, complete, unquestioning, total obedience 盲目服從；絕對服從；無條件服從；完全服從 / He has acted in obedience to the law. 他是依法行事的。/ She acted in passive obedience to her boss' directions. 她被動地遵照老闆的指示行事。/ He demands absolute obedience from his men. 他要求下屬絕對服從。/ All clergy owed obedience to their superior. 所有神職人員都有服從長上的義務。/ obedience class, obedience school（尤美）, obedience training（= for dogs）訓狗課程；訓狗學校；（狗的）服從性訓練 / The slaves had to swear obedience to their masters. 奴隸不得不發誓服從主人。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**resignation**</span> [ˌrezɪgˈneɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（對艱難或糟糕處境的）順從、聽任、耐心接受：</span>They accepted their defeat with resignation. 他們無可奈何地承認失敗。/ There was no grief in his expression, only deep resignation. 他的表情裡沒有悲痛，只有深深的無奈。/ He sighed with profound resignation. 他極其無奈地嘆了一口氣。/ quiet resignation 默默的順從 / weary resignation 消沉的順從 / Hearing that the train was running late, he sighed in weary resignation. 聽說火車晚點，他無可奈何地嘆了口氣。/ She spoke with resignation. 她無可奈何地講了話。/ a look of resignation 屈從的表情

<span class="vocabulary">**obedient**</span> [əˈbi:diənt]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (to sb/sth) 順從的；聽話的；唯命是從的：</span>an obedient child 聽話的孩子 / What a sweet, obedient little girl she was in the sixth grade. 她上六年級的時候是個多麼可愛溫順的小女孩啊！/ He was very respectful at home and obedient to his parents. 他在家裡對父母十分恭敬順從。/ He was always obedient to his father's wishes. 他一向順從父親的意願。

<span class="vocabulary">**subject**</span> ['sʌbdӡɪkt] 
<span class="definition">adj. subject to sth/sb 在…的權威之下，即受…支配的，服從於…的：</span>to be subject to the discipline 受紀律約束 / Everyone was subject to the whim of the sheikh. 所有人都聽命於酋長，由著他隨心所欲。/ All the priories were made directly subject to the abbot. 所有小修道院都直接由修道院院長管理。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**amenable**</span> [əˈmi:nəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (to sth)（人）容易控制的，願意受…影響的，即順從的：</span>They had three very amenable children. 他們有三個很聽話的孩子。/ He seemed most amenable to my idea. 他似乎對我言聽計從。/ I never had a long-term relationship as I wasn't good enough, or interesting or amenable enough. 因為我不夠優秀，不夠有趣或者不夠順從，所以戀愛關係從來都維持不長。/ The manager was most amenable: nothing was too much trouble. 經理極好說話，什麼都沒太費勁。/ You should find him amenable to reasonable arguments. 你會發現他容易接受合理的觀點。/ The Jordanian leader seemed amenable to attending a conference. 約旦領導人似乎願意參加會議。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**complaisant**</span> [kəmˈpleɪzənt]
<span class="definition">adj. [舊時] 願意接受別人的做法或觀點而不抱怨，別人讓做什麼就做什麼，即對別人順從的、殷勤的：</span>his pretty and complaisant wife 他漂亮又溫順的妻子
           
<span class="vocabulary">**yielding**</span> [ˈji:ldɪŋ]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式]（人）對別人順從的，願意按別人的意願行事的：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**observant**</span> [əbˈzɜ:vənt; 美 -ˈzɜ:rv-]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 嚴格遵守宗教教規和習俗的：</span>a profoundly observant Islamic country 嚴守教規的伊斯蘭國家
          
<span class="vocabulary">**obligatory**</span> [əˈblɪgətri; 美 -tɔ:ri]
<span class="definition">adj. [常為幽默用法] 隨大溜的：</span>In the mid 60s he took the almost obligatory trip to India. 六十年代中期，他也趕時髦到印度一遊。
