<span class="vocabulary">**timetable**</span> ['taɪmteɪbl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [尤英；美常用schedule]（車、船或飛機等交通工具的）時刻表：</span>bus, train timetable 公交車時刻表；列車時刻表 / It said in the timetable that a train was due at 5.30. 時刻表上寫著5點30分有一趟火車到站。
