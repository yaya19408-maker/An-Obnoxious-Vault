☀ <span class="category">**視線 光線：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**blind**</span> [blaɪnd] 
<span class="definition">adj.（汽車中的司機）看不見的：</span>a blind driveway 視線有盲區的車道 / a blind bend 隱蔽的彎道 / a blind corner 隱蔽的拐角

<span class="vocabulary">**block**</span> [blɒk] 
<span class="definition">vt. block the/sb’s view, etc. 透過站在某人前面或站在其透過的路上而阻止其看某事物，即擋住視線：</span>You’re blocking my view. 你把我視線擋住了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**obstruct**</span> [əbˈstrʌkt]
<span class="definition">vt. 遮擋視線：</span>The pillar obstructed our view of the stage. 柱子擋著，我們看不見舞臺。/ Claire positioned herself so as not to obstruct David's line of sight. 克萊爾為了不擋住戴維的視線挪了一下位置。

<span class="vocabulary">**dip**</span> [dɪp] 
<span class="definition">vt. [英] 在夜晚把汽車前燈的遠光調為近光（使其不射入其他司機的眼中）：</span>瞭解即可
