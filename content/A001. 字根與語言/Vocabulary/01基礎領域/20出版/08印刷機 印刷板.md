☀ <span class="category">**印刷機 印刷板：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**press**</span> [pres] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 印刷書籍、報刊等的機器，即印刷機；還可指印刷的過程：</span>printing press 印刷機 / to go to press 付印 / Their new book is in press. 他們的新書正在印刷。/ a story that is hot off the press 剛剛見報的新聞報道 / Stop the presses! Dan has had a haircut!（諷刺用法）特大新聞！丹理髮了！
           
<span class="vocabulary">**printer**</span> [ˈprɪntə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指與計算機相連的）印表機：</span>black-and-white, colour/color, monochrome printer 黑白印表機；彩色印表機；單色印表機 / computer printer 計算機印表機 / office printer 辦公室印表機 / desktop printer 臺式印表機 / dot, matrix, inkjet, laser printer 點陣印表機；噴墨印表機；鐳射印表機 / wide-format printer（美）寬幅印表機 / compatible printer 可相容的印表機 / Connect the printer to your PC. 把印表機和你的個人電腦連線起來。/ You can print to any networked printer in the building. 你可以用本樓的任何一臺連網印表機列印。/ The printer won't print for some reason. 印表機不知道為什麼不能列印了。/ printer cartridge, printer ink, printer toner 印表機墨盒；印表機墨水；印表機碳粉 / printer paper 列印紙 / printer tray 印表機紙盒 / Can you check to see if the printer cable is still plugged into your computer? 你能檢查一下，看看印表機的電線是否還插在電腦上? / printer port 印表機埠 / printer driver 印表機驅動程式 / printer icon 印表機圖示

<span class="vocabulary">**plate**</span> [pleɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（印刷用的金屬或塑膠）印板，圖版：</span>a printing plate 印版
