☀ <span class="category">**規定 許可權：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**provide**</span> [prə'vaɪd] 
<span class="definition">vt. [正式]（法律或規則）規定某事將發生或規定其必須發生，即規定，相當於stipulate：</span>The final section provides that any work produced for the company is thereafter owned by the company. 最後一節規定，此後為公司創作的一切作品均為該公司所有。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**provision**</span> [prəˈvɪʒn]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（法律檔案的）規定：</span>legal, legislative, statutory provision  法律規定；法規 / The same provisions apply to foreign-owned companies. 同樣的規定適用於外資公司。/ The Act contains detailed provisions for appeal against the court's decision. 該法案含有對法庭裁決上訴的詳細規定。/ Under the provisions of the lease, the tenant is responsible for repairs. 按契約規定，房客負責房屋維修。/ The bill's provision for the sale and purchase of land faces stiff opposition from conservatives. 該法案關於土地買賣的規定遭到保守派的強硬反對。

 <span class="vocabulary">**stipulate**</span> [ˈstɪpjuleɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 明確地規定（某事必須被執行或其必須以何種方式被執行）：</span>The constitution stipulated that a general election must be held within 120 days. 憲法規定必須在120天內舉行大選。/ A delivery date is stipulated in the contract. 合同中規定了交貨日期。/ International rules stipulate the number of foreign entrants. 國際規則規定了外國參賽者的人數。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**stipulation**</span> [ˌstɪpjuˈleɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 規定：</span>The only stipulation the bank makes is that you must be in employment. 銀行的唯一規定是你必須有工作。/ My contract includes the stipulation that I have a seat on the board. 我的合同中有我在董事會須佔有一席的規定。/ There's no stipulation as to the amount you can invest. 沒有關於投資額的規定。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**specify**</span> [ˈspesɪfaɪ]
<span class="definition">vt.（尤指透過給出具體的資訊）明確規定：</span>The contract clearly specifies who can operate the machinery. 合同明確規定誰可以操作機器。/ The regulations specify that calculators may not be used in the examination. 考試規則明確規定考試時不得使用計算器。/ Each recipe specifies the size of egg to be used. 每種菜譜都具體規定了所用雞蛋的大小。/ One rule specifies that learner drivers must be supervised by adults. 一條規則明確規定，見習駕駛員必須有成人的監督。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**prescribe**</span> [prɪˈskraɪb]
<span class="definition">vt.（當權者、當局、法律、規則）規定、命令、指示：</span>The prescribed form must be completed and returned to this office. 必須把指定的表格填好並交回本辦事處。/ Police regulations prescribe that an officer's number must be clearly visible. 警政制度規定，警察的番號必須醒目。/ The syllabus prescribes precisely which books should be studied. 教學大綱明確規定了哪些是必讀的書。/ article II of the constitution, which prescribes the method of electing a president. 規定了總統選舉方法的憲法第二條 / Alliott told Singleton he was passing the sentence prescribed by law. 阿利奧特告訴辛格爾頓他是依法判決。/ The curriculum is rigidly prescribed from an early age. 自兒童時起即已開始有嚴格的課程設定。/ Everything about her life was strictly prescribed (= there were strict rules about what she could do). 她生活中的一切都是一板一眼規定好的。/ culturally prescribed gender roles 文化規定的性別角色

<span class="vocabulary">**competence**</span> ['kɒmpɪtəns] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 一個法庭（組織機構、人）在處理某事時所具有的權力，可稱為許可權：</span>The judge has to act within the competence of the court. 法官必須在法庭的許可權範圍內行使權力。/ The commission has no formal competence in cultural matters. 該委員會對文化事務沒有正式管轄權。/ matters that fall outside the court’s competence 屬於法庭許可權以外的事務 / The decisions come within the competence of the committee. 這些決定屬於委員會的職權範圍。/ outside sb’s area of competence 超出某人的許可權範圍
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**jurisdiction**</span> [ˌdʒʊərɪsˈdɪkʃn; 美 ˌdʒʊr-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] [正式] ~ (over sb/sth) / ~ (of sb/sth) (to do sth)（官方組織所具有的）司法權；審判權；管轄權：</span>limited jurisdiction 有限司法管轄權 / The British courts have universal jurisdiction over torture cases. 英國各法院對於虐待案擁有普遍管轄權。/ Under the doctrine of universal jurisdiction, suspected war criminals may be prosecuted irrespective of where they are located. 根據普遍管轄權規定，無論戰犯嫌疑人身處何地，都可以對其進行起訴。/ The commissioners had exclusive jurisdiction to decide. 委員們擁有專屬管轄權進行裁決。/ appellate jurisdiction 上訴管轄權 / civil, criminal, ecclesiastical jurisdiction 民事管轄權；刑事管轄權；教會管轄權 / federal, local jurisdiction 聯邦管轄權；地方管轄權 / The court has no jurisdiction in this case. 法院對本案沒有管轄權。/ The court may exercise its jurisdiction to compel the husband to make a settlement upon his wife. 法院可以行使司法權強制丈夫給予其妻子財產。/ The offshore government claims jurisdiction over the mainland. 海外政府聲稱對大陸擁有管轄權。/ He is subject to the jurisdiction of the Indian courts.他受印度法院的管轄。/ The matter is outside the jurisdiction of UK administrative agencies. 此事不在英國行政機構的管轄範圍內。/ The territory is still under Russian jurisdiction. 這個地區仍屬俄羅斯管轄。/ The matter was not within the jurisdiction of the court. 此事不在法院管轄範圍之內。/ The British police have no jurisdiction over foreign bank accounts. 外國銀行賬戶不在英國警方的管轄許可權之內。/ The Senate committees have exclusive jurisdiction over the FBI. 參議院委員會對聯邦調查局有專屬管轄權。/ She acted beyond the jurisdiction of any teacher. 她的行為超越了任何一個老師的權力範圍。<span class="definition">2 [C] [正式] 某一法庭或法律體系所管轄的一個地區或國家，如管轄區域；管轄範圍：</span>瞭解即可
