☀ <span class="category">**起訴 上訴：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**prosecute**</span> [ˈprɒsɪkju:t; 美 ˈprɑ:s-]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sb) (for sth/doing sth) 正式起訴某人（某項罪行）：</span>The company was prosecuted for breaching the Health and Safety Act. 這家公司被控違反《衛生安全條例》。/ Trespassers will be prosecuted (= a notice telling people to keep out of a particular area). 閒人莫入，違者必究。/ The police decided not to prosecute. 警方決定不予起訴。/ The police have decided not to prosecute because the evidence is not strong enough. 由於證據不足，警方已決定不起訴。/ Photographs taken by roadside cameras will soon be enough to prosecute drivers for speeding. 路邊電子眼拍下的照片很快將足以起訴超速的司機。<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 擔任控方律師：</span>prosecuting counsel, prosecuting lawyer, prosecuting attorney 原告律師 / James Spencer, prosecuting, claimed that the witness was lying. 原告律師詹姆斯·斯潘塞指稱證人在撒謊。/ The attorney who will prosecute the case says he cannot reveal how much money is involved. 將對此案提起公訴的律師說他不能透露具體的涉案金額。
       
<span class="vocabulary">**prosecution**</span> [ˌprɒsɪˈkju:ʃn; 美 ˌprɑ:s-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（被）起訴、檢舉：</span>Yesterday the head of government called for the prosecution of those responsible for the deaths. 昨天，政府首腦要求起訴那些該為這些人的死亡負責的人。/ Prosecution for a first minor offence rarely leads to imprisonment. 因初犯輕罪被控者很少被判監禁。/ criminal, federal（美）, private（英）prosecution 刑事起訴；聯邦起訴；自訴 / He threatened to bring a private prosecution against the doctor. 他威脅要對醫生提起民事訴訟。/ The police brought a prosecution against the driver involved. 警方對涉案的司機提起了訴訟。/ Prosecutions for water pollution more than doubled. 水汙染引起的起訴增加了一倍多。/ immunity from prosecution 免於公訴
           
<span class="vocabulary">**indictment**</span> [ɪnˈdaɪtmənt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] [尤美] 刑事起訴書：</span>criminal，felony（美）indictment 刑事起訴；重罪起訴 / A New York jury brought criminal indictments against the founder of the organization. 紐約的一個陪審團對該組織創始人提起了多項刑事訴訟。/ War crimes indictments were handed down by a UN-backed court.（hand down為美式用法）戰爭罪起訴書是由聯合國支援的法院公佈的。/ an indictment charging theft 盜竊罪起訴書 / Two men were named in the indictment. 起訴書中提及兩個人。/ They issued an indictment against them. 他們起訴了他們。

<span class="definition">2 [U] [尤美] 起訴：</span>Prosecutors may soon seek an indictment on racketeering and fraud charges. 檢舉官可能不久就會以敲詐勒索與欺詐罪名提起訴訟。/ The government's indictment against the three men alleged unlawful trading. 政府在對這三個人的起訴書中指控他們從事非法貿易。/ The federal district court dismissed the indictment. 聯邦地區法院駁回了起訴。

<span class="vocabulary">**sue**</span> [su:; British English also sju:]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sb) (for sth)（通常為索賠而）對…提起訴訟、控告：</span>to sue sb for breach of contract 控告某人違反合同 / to sue sb for $10 million (= in order to get money from sb) 控告某人要求得到1 000萬元 / to sue sb for damages 起訴某人要求賠償損失 / They threatened to sue if the work was not completed. 他們威脅說，如果不完成工作，就要提起訴訟。/ Mr Warren sued him for libel over the remarks. 沃倫先生控告他散佈誹謗性言論。/ The company could be sued for damages. 該公司可能會被起訴，要求其賠償損失。/ He threatened to sue the company for negligence. 他威脅說要以過失罪起訴該公司。/ The water authority was successfully sued over his illness. 就他的疾病對供水部門提起訴訟獲勝。
         
<span class="vocabulary">**appeal**</span> [ə'pi:l] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] appeal (against sth) 向法院或當權者提出的正式請求，請求更改判決，即上訴，申訴：</span>appeal court（英）, appeals court（美）上訴法庭 / appeal hearing 上訴聽證會 / appeals procedure 上訴程式 / On appeal it was held that the judge was correct. 上訴維持了法官的原判。/ an appeal against his conviction of fraud 不服對他犯詐騙罪判決的上訴 / grounds of appeal（英）上訴的理由 / You have the right of appeal to the High Court. 你有權向高等法院提出上訴。/ The players have been suspended pending appeal. 這些選手已被暫停比賽，等待上訴。<span class="definition">vi. appeal (to sb/sth) (against sth) 上訴，申訴：</span>She appealed unsuccessfully against her conviction for murder. 她不服被判謀殺罪提出上訴，但失敗了。/ The company is appealing against the ruling. 公司正對判決提出申訴。/ to appeal to the High Court 向高等法院提出上訴

<span class="vocabulary">**action**</span> ['ækʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 為阻止一個人或公司做某事，或為了使其為其所犯的錯誤進行賠付等而採取的法律程式，即訴訟，起訴：</span>court action 法庭訴訟 / civil, criminal, libel, tort action 民事訴訟；行事訴訟；誹謗訴訟；侵權訴訟 / to bring, to file, to take action 提起訴訟 / He is considering taking legal action against the company. 他正考慮起訴這家公司。

<span class="vocabulary">**suit**</span> [sju:t] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 法律訴訟或起訴，相當於lawsuit：</span>civil suit（尤美）民事訴訟 / divorce suit 離婚訴訟 / antitrust, discrimination suit 反壟斷訴訟；歧視訴訟 / to bring, to file suit 提起訴訟；起訴 / to drop suit 放棄訴訟 / to defend suit 為訴訟辯護 / to win suit 勝訴 / to lose suit 敗訴 / to settle the suit 了結這場官司 / a suit over a disputed estate 一起關於地產糾紛的訴訟 / A suit has been filed against the company. 該公司已被起訴。
   
<span class="vocabulary">**lawsuit**</span> [ˈlɔ:su:t; British English also -sju:t]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 訴訟、起訴：</span>He filed a lawsuit against his record company. 他對給他錄製唱片的公司提起了訴訟。/ The dispute culminated last week in a lawsuit against the government. 上週這一糾紛達到高潮，政府被告上了法庭。/ a lawsuit brought by Barclays Bank 由巴克萊銀行提起的訴訟
           
<span class="vocabulary">**litigation**</span> [ˌlɪtɪˈgeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [法律] 訴訟；打官司：</span>The company has been in litigation with its previous auditors for a full year. 那家公司與前任審計員已打了整整一年的官司。/ The settlement ends more than four years of litigation on behalf of the residents. 這一和解協議結束了代表居民提起的超過四年之久的訴訟。/ civil, commercial, criminal litigation 民事訴訟；商業訴訟；刑事訴訟 / class-action litigation（美）集體訴訟 / the class-action litigation brought by the families of the victims 由受害者家庭提起的集體訴訟 / liability（尤美）, malpractice, tort（美）litigation 責任訴訟；瀆職訴訟；侵權訴訟 / employment, medical litigation 勞動訴訟；醫療訴訟 / The alleged victims have elected to pursue litigation. 自稱受害的人們選擇提起訴訟。/ The payment was made to avoid threatened litigation. 為避免可能的訴訟而付了款。/ litigation costs, litigation expenses 訴訟費 / litigation settlement（尤美）訴訟和解 / litigation finance（美）訴訟經費 / He engaged in endless litigation against the media. 他參與了狀告媒體的曠日持久的訴訟。/ litigation between private parties 私人之間的訴訟 / litigation over water rights 關於用水權利的訴訟 / the cost of litigation 訴訟成本

<span class="vocabulary">**proceeding**</span> [prəˈsi:dɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl.] [正式] ~ (against sb) (for sth) 訴訟；訴訟程式：</span>bankruptcy, divorce, extradition proceedings 破產訴訟；離婚訴訟；引渡訴訟 / to bring legal proceedings against sb 向某人提起法律訴訟 / criminal proceedings against the former prime minister 對前首相的刑事訴訟 / The Council had brought proceedings to stop the store from trading on Sundays. 該委員會已經提起訴訟，禁止這家商店在週日營業。

<span class="vocabulary">**initiation**</span> [ɪˌnɪʃiˈeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式] 訴訟的發起：</span>the initiation of criminal proceedings 提起刑事訴訟

<span class="vocabulary">**bring**</span> [brɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">vt. bring sth (against sb) 正式起訴某人（犯了某罪）。雖然是一個簡單用詞，不過也很常用：</span>to bring a charge against sb, to bring a legal action against sb, bo bring an accusation against sb 控告某人；起訴某人；控告某人

<span class="vocabulary">**file**</span> [faɪl] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. file (for sth) 法律上可指呈交…（以使其能夠得到正式記錄或處理），如提起訴訟、投訴或提出請求。較為正式：</span>to file a claim, to file a complaint, to file a petition, to file a lawsuit 提出索賠；提出申訴；呈交訴狀；提起訴訟 / to file for bankruptcy, to file for divorce 申請破產；申請離婚 / A copy of the notice must be filed with the court. 必須將通知副本送交法院備案。

<span class="vocabulary">**fight**</span> [faɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt. fight sb (for sth) 和某人打官司，是一個形象化用詞：</span>He fought his wife for custody of the children. 他和妻子打官司爭奪孩子的監護權。/ I’m determined to fight the case. 我決意要打這場官司。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**litigate**</span> [ˈlɪtɪgeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. [法律] 提起訴訟、打官司：</span>the cost of litigating personal injury claims in the county court. 在縣級法院打人身傷害索賠官司的費用 / If we have to litigate, we will. 迫不得已的話，我們會提起訴訟。          
           
<span class="vocabulary">**impeach**</span> [ɪmˈpi:tʃ]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb (for sth)（尤指在美國，法院或其它官方團體）控告一個重要的公眾人物犯有某項嚴重罪行，常稱為彈劾：</span>an opposition move to impeach the President 彈劾總統的反對議案

<span class="vocabulary">**commit**</span> [kə'mɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 把某人送交法庭受審：</span>瞭解即可

說明：action和suit表“訴訟”時，①action指為實現個人權利、阻止個人或組織的非法行為、或為懲戒犯罪等而向法院訴諸的一種法律程式，即“訴訟”、“起訴”。在今天，action雖然可指各種訴訟（以前僅指普通法上的訴訟，以區別于衡平法上的訴訟。現在普通法和衡平法已合併，因此action和suit常被作為同義詞而互換使用），但主要指民事訴訟，而刑事訴訟通常用prosecutions；（以上解釋主要出自《元照英美法詞典》）②suit表“訴訟”時，指訴訟的全過程，包括從開始提交訴訟到最後做出裁決甚至執行判決的全過程（與之相比，action的程式一般到判決結束即終止）。《元照英美法詞典》具體做出如下解釋：“在英國，以前suit專門指衡平法上的民事訴訟（普通法上的民事訴訟用action）。現在action已代替suit，既指普通法上的民事訴訟，又指衡平法上的訴訟。但在高等法院家事分庭有關婚姻無效或解除的訴訟中仍用suit。”
