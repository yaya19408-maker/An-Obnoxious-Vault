☀ <span class="category">**銷贓 販贓：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**smuggle**</span> [ˈsmʌgl]
<span class="definition">vt. 將貨物（或人員）秘密或非法地帶進（或帶出）一個國家等，如走私、私運、偷運：</span>to smuggle drugs 走私毒品 / They were caught smuggling diamonds into the country. 他們走私鑽石入境時被發現了。/ goods which have been smuggled into Britain 偷運進入英國的貨物 / Weapons are being smuggled across the border. 武器正被偷運過邊境。/ Friends managed to smuggle him secretly out of the country. 朋友們將他秘密送出了國。/ He managed to smuggle a gun into the prison. 他設法把一支槍偷偷送進了監獄。/ He managed to smuggle out a note from prison. 他設法從獄中偷偷帶出一張紙條。/ Police have foiled an attempt to smuggle a bomb into Belfast airport. 警方挫敗了一場企圖將炸彈偷偷帶入貝爾法斯特機場的陰謀。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**smuggling**</span> [ˈsmʌglɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 走私（罪）：</span>illegal smuggling 非法走私 / cigarette, cocaine, diamond, drug, heroin smuggling 香菸走私；可卡因走私；鑽石走私；毒品走私；海洛因走私 /alien smuggling（美）, human smuggling（尤美）, people smuggling（尤英）偷渡 / They have been charged with people/alien smuggling. 他們被控做偷渡的買賣。/ nuclear smuggling 核走私 / smuggling attempt 走私企圖 / smuggling gang（尤英）, smuggling ring, smuggling operation, smuggling racket（尤英）走私團伙；走私活動；走私行徑 / The two sailors are caught up in a diamond-smuggling racket. 兩名海員因走私鑽石被捕。/ smuggling route 走私路線 / the smuggling of narcotics into the country 偷運麻醉品到該國
           
<span class="vocabulary">**running**</span> [ˈrʌnɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 構成複合詞，指（毒品、槍支等的）走私、偷運：</span>drug-running 毒品走私

<span class="vocabulary">**traffic**</span> ['træfɪk] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] traffic (in sth) 非法交易（如毒品或贓物等）：</span>illegal traffic 非法交易 / drug traffic 毒品交易 / traffic in arms 非法武器交易 / traffic in stolen goods 贓物買賣 <span class="definition">vi. traffic in sth 可表示從事非法買賣或交易：</span>to traffic in drugs 買賣毒品

<span class="vocabulary">**receive**</span> [rɪ'si:v] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. [尤英] 購買（或接受）贓物（即你知道是被盜的物品）：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**black market**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] 黑市；黑市交易：</span>to buy or sell goods on the black market 從事黑市買賣 / a flourishing black market in foreign currency 猖獗的外幣黑市交易 / There is a plentiful supply of arms on the black market. 黑市上有大量武器出售。/ black market food prices 黑市的食品價格 / a thriving black-market trade in antiquities 活躍的古董黑市交易 / You could buy anything you needed on the black market. 在黑市上你需要什麼就能買到什麼。During the war, there was a thriving black market in food. 戰爭期間，黑市食品買賣十分活躍。
