☀ <span class="category">**刑具 鐐銬：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**scaffold**</span> [ˈskæfəʊld; 美 -foʊld]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 斷頭臺；絞刑架：</span>Moore ascended the scaffold and addressed the executioner. 穆爾走上斷頭臺，和劊子手說話。

<span class="vocabulary">**bond**</span> [bɒnd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 複數形式bonds為正式用法，可指囚犯所戴的繩索或鏈子：</span>to release sb from their bonds 給某人除去枷鎖

<span class="vocabulary">**iron**</span> ['aɪən] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.]（尤指舊時）囚犯所戴的鐵製鐐銬：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**shackle**</span> [ˈʃækl]
<span class="definition">n. [p.] shackles 可指（囚犯的）鐐銬、手銬、腳鐐：</span>He unbolted the shackles on Billy's hands. 他開啟了比利的手銬。<span class="definition">vt. 給某人戴鐐銬：</span>the chains that were shackling his legs 銬著他雙腿的鏈子 / She was shackled to a wall. 她被銬在一面牆上。/ The hostage had been shackled to a radiator. 當時人質被銬在暖氣片上。/ The prisoners were kept shackled during the trial. 審判期間，犯人戴著鐐銬。

<span class="vocabulary">**fetter**</span> [ˈfetə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] fetters 尤指舊時囚犯所戴的腳鐐：</span>He saw a boy in fetters in the dungeons. 他在地牢裡看見一個戴著腳鐐的男孩。<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 給（囚犯）上腳鐐：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**stock**</span> [stɒk; 美 stɑ:k]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] stocks可指過去用於懲罰犯人的足枷，尤指在公共場所所戴的足枷：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**chair**</span> [tʃeə] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [美，非正式]（尤指美國用於處決犯人的）電椅；也可指此種電刑：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**rack**</span> [ræk]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（通常用the rack）舊時的拉肢刑具：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**stake**</span> [steɪk]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the stake 指木質火刑柱（以前將人綁在上面燒死作為懲罰）：</span>Joan of Arc was burnt at the stake. 聖女貞德被處以火刑。

<span class="vocabulary">**paddle**</span> ['pædl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [美] 用於懲罰兒童的一片有柄的木頭，可以叫戒尺：</span>瞭解即可
            
<span class="vocabulary">**cane**</span> [keɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 舊時學校用於懲罰學童的竹杖，藤條：</span>to get the cane (= be punished with a cane) 受藤條鞭罰 / Until the 1980s some criminals were still flogged with a rattan cane as a punishment. 直到20世紀80年代,有些罪犯仍被處以笞刑。

<span class="vocabulary">**rod**</span> [rɒd; 美 rɑ:d]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（也可用the rod）[舊時] 責打人用的棍棒：</span>There used to be a saying: ‘Spare the rod and spoil the child.’ 俗語說：“孩子不打不成器。”
