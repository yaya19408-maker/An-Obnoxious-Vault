☀ <span class="category">**立法 生效：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**legislature**</span> [ˈledʒɪsleɪtʃə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [正式] 立法機關：</span>federal, national, provincial, state legislature 聯邦立法機構；國家立法機構；省立法機構；州立法機構 / Democratic-controlled legislature 民主黨控制的立法機構 / She is the youngest woman to be elected to the national legislature. 她是入選國家立法機構的最年輕女性。/ The legislature passed a law to prohibit the dumping of nuclear waste. 立法機構透過法律禁止傾倒核廢物。/ The legislature voted narrowly to table a motion of no-confidence in the government. 立法機構以微弱優勢透過決議，將提出對政府的不信任動議。/ a democratically elected legislature 民主選舉產生的立法機關 / The proposals before the legislature include the creation of two special courts to deal exclusively with violent crimes. 呈交立法機關的提案內容包括成立兩個專門審理暴力犯罪的特別法庭。

<span class="vocabulary">**legislation**</span> [ˌledʒɪsˈleɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 法律的制定和透過的過程，即立法：</span>Legislation will be difficult and will take time. 立法既費力又耗時。/ a letter calling for legislation to protect women's rights 一封呼籲立法保護婦女權益的信 / They are calling for tough legislation to tackle this problem. 他們提出制定嚴格的法律來解決這個問題。/ federal, national, parliamentary, state legislation 聯邦立法；全國立法；議會立法；國家立法 / Anyone has the right to initiate legislation. 任何人都有權提出立法。/ The government is pushing through legislation to ban smoking in all public places. 政府正在力促透過立法，禁止在公共場所吸菸。/ Religious interests may try to block this legislation. 宗教利益集團有可能試圖阻撓這項立法。/ Legislation on this issue is urgently needed. 這個問題急需立法。

<span class="vocabulary">**make**</span> [meɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 制定（規則、法律等）：</span>to make laws 制定法律 / to make regulations 制定規章 / to make a will 立遺囑 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**enactment**</span> [ɪˈnæktmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] [法律]（法律在議會或其它立法機構的）制定、透過：</span>We support the call for the enactment of a Bill of Rights. 我們支援要求透過《權利法案》的呼聲。           

<span class="vocabulary">**application**</span> [͵æplɪ'keɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 一條規則（或法律等）的啟用或生效：</span>strict application of the law 法律的嚴格執行
           
<span class="vocabulary">**operative**</span> [ˈɒpərətɪv; 美 ˈɑ:pərətɪv; -reɪt-]
<span class="definition">adj. [名詞前不常用]（法律等）生效的：</span>This law becomes operative immediately. 本法規即時生效。
