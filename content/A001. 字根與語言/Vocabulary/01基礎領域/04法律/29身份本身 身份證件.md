☀ <span class="category">**身份本身 身份證件：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**identification**</span> [aɪ͵dentɪfɪ'keɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 證明身份的正式檔案：</span>identification card, identification document, identification papers 身份證；身份證明檔案；身份證明文書 / Can I see some identification, please? 請出示一下身份證件好嗎？/ Always carry some identification. 要隨時攜帶能證明身份的證件。/ My only means of identification was my credit card. 唯一能證明我身份的就是我這張信用卡了。

<span class="vocabulary">**identity**</span> [aɪ'dentɪtɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 人或事物的身份、本身、本體。是此義的常規和專業用詞，最為常用：</span>the identity of the killer 殺人兇手的身份 / a false identity 假身份 / stolen identity 盜用的身份 / dual identity 雙重身份 / Shanghai itself has multiple identities. 上海本身就具有多重身份。/ identity bracelet, identity tag 身份識別腕帶；身份牌 / identity card, identity documents, identity papers 身份證；身份證明檔案；身份證明文書 / The victim picked out her attacker in an identity parade.（英）受害人從指認佇列中認出了襲擊她的人。/ identity theft 身份盜用 / identity verification 身份驗證 / Scotland has never lost its identity as a separate nation. 蘇格蘭從未失去其獨立民族的身份。

<span class="vocabulary">**status**</span> ['steɪtəs] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] 一個人（或群體、國家）的法律身份：</span>refugee, immigrant, resident status 難民身份；移民身份；居民身份 / The Institute has now achieved full status as part of the university. 該研究所現在已正式成為這所大學的一部分。/ He argued that the email had no legal status. 他認為電子郵件沒有法律效力。/ A majority voted for fully independent status for the region. 大多數人投票贊成該地區完全獨立。/ The organization has charitable status. 該組織是慈善機構。<span class="definition">2 [U, C, usually sing.]（某人或某事物相對於其他人或事物的）身份：</span>amateur status 業餘身份 / celebrity, star status 名人身份；明星身份 / My outsider status granted me special insights. 我的局外人身份賦予了我獨特的見解。/ He achieved celebrity status. 他成了名人。
