☀ <span class="category">**監獄 牢房：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**cell**</span> [sel] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 監獄或警局看守所的一間牢房：</span>jail, prison, police cell 單人牢房；警察局拘留室 / holding cell 拘留所

<span class="vocabulary">**prison**</span> ['prɪzn] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] 監獄，看守所，相當於jail。是此義的基礎和常規用詞，最為常用：</span>He was sent to prison for five years. 他被關押了五年。/ He went to prison for tax evasion. 他因逃稅而入獄。/ She is in prison, awaiting trial. 她正在拘押候審中。/ local prison 地方監獄 / federal prison 聯邦監獄 / overcrowded prison 人滿為患的監獄 / women’s prison 女子監獄 / prison term 刑期 / prison cell, prison yard 牢房；監獄的院子 / prison population 關押人數 / to get out of prison 出獄 / a maximum-security prison 戒備森嚴的監獄 <span class="definition">2 [U] 關押制度，監禁制度：</span>prison service（英）, prison system 監獄管理機構；監獄制度 / prison reform 監獄改革
           
<span class="vocabulary">**jail**</span> [dʒeɪl]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 監獄：</span>local jail 地方監獄 / high-security jail（尤英）戒備森嚴的監獄 / private jail 私設的監獄 / overcrowded jail 過度擁擠的監獄 / He's gone to jail for fraud. 他已因詐騙罪入獄。/ His lawyer worked hard to keep him out of jail. 為了幫他免除牢獄之，他的律師付出了很大努力。/ She could be out of jail in two years. 她可能在兩年後出獄。/ He was threatened with jail if evidence of a hoax was discovered. 如果發現他欺詐的證據，他就會有入獄的危險。/ She avoided jail by pleading insanity. 她以有精神病為由而免於入獄。/ jail sentence, jail term, jail time 監禁判決；刑期 / jail cell牢房 / riots at a jail 發生在監獄的暴亂 / How long has she been in jail?她坐牢多久了? / There was a fire in the jail last night. 昨夜監獄裡發生了火災。/ She spent a year in jail. 她坐了一年牢。/ He has been released from jail. 他已從監獄裡放出來了。/ a ten-year jail sentence 十年監禁的判刑 / Three prisoners escaped from a jail. 三名囚犯越獄了。

<span class="vocabulary">**can**</span> [kæn, kən] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [美，俚] 監獄，班房，牢房：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**inside**</span> ['ɪn'saɪd] 
<span class="definition">adv. [非正式] 在監獄裡：</span>He was sentenced to three years inside. 他被判三年監禁。
