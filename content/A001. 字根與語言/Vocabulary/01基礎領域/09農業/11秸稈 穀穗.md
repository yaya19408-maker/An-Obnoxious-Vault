☀ <span class="category">**秸稈 穀穗：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**straw**</span> [strɔ:] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 小麥等糧食作物收割後的乾燥的黃色秸稈：</span>bales of straw 成捆的麥稈 / a straw hat 草帽 <span class="definition">2 [C] 一根（小麥等的）秸稈：</span>to chew on a straw 嘴裡嚼著一根麥稈

<span class="vocabulary">**ear**</span> [ɪə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 穀類植物（例如小麥）的穗：</span>ears of rice 稻穗 / ears of wheat 麥穗 / ears of corn 玉米穗
