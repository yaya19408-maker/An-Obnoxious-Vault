☀ <span class="category">**體育器材 運動器械：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**weight**</span> [weɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 體育運動中用於鍛鍊力量的一塊金屬，如槓鈴片；啞鈴等：</span>weight lifting 舉重 / weight training 舉重訓練 / She lifts weights as part of her training. 舉槓鈴（或舉啞鈴）是她鍛鍊的一部分。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**beam**</span> [bi:m]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [尤英] [美式英語通常用balance beam] 體操運動中的平衡木：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**baton**</span> [ˈbætɒn; -tɒ̃; 美 bəˈtɑ:n]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 接力賽的接力棒：</span>to hand over the baton 交接力棒 / Each runner passes the baton to the next. 每名賽跑選手把接力棒傳給下一個人。/ The President handed over the baton (= passed responsibility) to his successor.（比喻用法）總統把權杖傳給了他的繼任者。/ Does this mean that the baton of leadership is going to be passed to other nations?（比喻用法）這是不是意味著要把領導權交給其他國家？
           
<span class="vocabulary">**foil**</span> [fɔɪl]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（擊劍運動用的）花劍（一種又長又細又輕的劍）：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**sabre**</span> [ˈseɪbə(r)] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 擊劍運動用的佩劍（重量較輕）：</span>瞭解即可
