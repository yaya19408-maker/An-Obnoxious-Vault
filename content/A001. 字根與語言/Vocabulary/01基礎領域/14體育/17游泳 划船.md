☀ <span class="category">**游泳 划船：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**swim**</span> [swɪm] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（人）游泳：</span>to swim a mile a day 每天遊一英里 / I can’t swim. 我不會游泳。/ to swim across the river 游到河對岸 <span class="definition">vi. go swimming 可指游泳（作為一種娛樂）：</span>Let’s go swimming. 咱們去游泳吧。<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 游泳：</span>She got up early and went for a swim. 她早早起來游泳去了。/ a quick swim 遊一會兒泳
           
<span class="vocabulary">**stroke**</span> [strəʊk; 美 stroʊk]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 游泳時的一個划水動作：</span>I turned and swam a few strokes further out to sea. 我轉身向海裡又遊了幾下。/ He swam back with long slow strokes. 他游回來時用的是大幅度的緩慢划水。/ You can't swim more than four strokes before you reach the other side. 劃不了四下你就能游到對岸。/ She took a few more strokes to reach the bank. 她又劃幾下，游到了岸邊。<span class="definition">2 [C]（常構成複合詞）一種游泳方式或風格：</span>Butterfly is the only stroke I can't do. 只有蝶泳我不會。/ She spent hours practising the breast stroke. 她花了數小時練習蛙泳。           

<span class="vocabulary">**sprint**</span> [sprɪnt]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 短距離快速游泳，即衝刺：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] 短距離快速游泳等，即衝刺：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**dip**</span> [dɪp] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 一次短時間的游泳，即遊一遊，泡一泡：</span>Let’s go for a dip. 咱們去遊一會兒泳吧。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**plunge**</span> [plʌndʒ]
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] 快速游泳：</span>She went for a plunge. 她去游泳了。

<span class="vocabulary">**float**</span> [fləʊt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 學游泳用的一個輕質漂浮物（如救生圈或浮板）：</span>Floats will provide confidence in the water. 救生圈可以增強在水中的信心。

<span class="vocabulary">**butterfly**</span> ['bʌtəflaɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 蝶泳：</span>the 400m butterfly 400米蝶泳
           
<span class="vocabulary">**backstroke**</span> [ˈbækstrəʊk; 美 -stroʊk]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 仰泳：</span>Can you do (the) backstroke? 你會仰泳嗎？<span class="definition">2 [sing.] 仰泳比賽：</span>He won the 100 metres backstroke (= the race). 他獲得100米仰泳比賽冠軍。

<span class="vocabulary">**breaststroke**</span> [ˈbreststrəʊk; 美 -stroʊk]
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.] 蛙泳：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**freestyle**</span> [ˈfri:staɪl]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 自由式游泳比賽（泳式不限，參加者常選擇爬泳）：</span>the men's 400m freestyle 男子400米自由泳 <span class="definition">2 [U]（常用作形容詞）自由式體育比賽，尤指（游泳、摔跤、滑雪等體育競賽）自由式的：</span>the 100m freestyle swimming event 100 米自由泳比賽 / the reigning European Freestyle Champion 本屆歐洲自由式冠軍 / freestyle skiing 自由式滑雪
           
<span class="vocabulary">**crawl**</span> [krɔ:l]
<span class="definition">n. [sing, U]（通常用the crawl）自由泳：</span>a swimmer doing the crawl 遊自由泳的運動員

<span class="vocabulary">**bathe**</span> [beɪð] 
<span class="definition">vi. [舊時]（到海、河等水體中）享受游泳：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">n. [sing.] [英，非正式]（在海、河等中水體的）游泳：</span>to go for a bathe 去游泳

<span class="vocabulary">**swim**</span> [swɪm] 
<span class="definition">vt. 參加游泳比賽：</span>She swam the 400 metres medley. 她參加了400米混合泳比賽。

<span class="vocabulary">**pool**</span> [pu:l] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 游泳池，相當於swimming pool。是此義的常規用詞：</span>Does the hotel have a pool? 這家旅館有沒有游泳池？/ swimming pool 游泳池 / outdoor pool 戶外游泳池 / children’s pool 兒童嬉水池

<span class="vocabulary">**bath**</span> [bɑːθ] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] [舊時，英] baths可表示公共游泳池（是一種公共建築）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**length**</span> [leŋθ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 游泳池的長度（一端至另一端的距離）：</span>He swims 50 lengths a day. 他每天遊50個游泳池那麼長的距離。

<span class="vocabulary">**crew**</span> [kru:] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] [美]（與其他人在一艘船上所進行的）賽艇運動：</span>I’m thinking of going out for crew this semester (= joining the rowing team). 這學期我打算參加賽艇隊。<span class="definition">2 [C] 賽船的一支劃船團隊，即划船隊：</span>a member of the Cambridge crew 劍橋大學划船隊的一名隊員
           
<span class="vocabulary">**canoeing**</span> [kəˈnu:ɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 皮划艇運動或比賽：</span>They went canoeing in the wilds of Canada. 他們去澳大利亞的荒野河谷中劃獨木舟。

<span class="vocabulary">**length**</span> [leŋθ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（划船等速度比賽中）船的自身長度：</span>瞭解即可

周邊詞彙補充：
· row可指用槳划船，或划船所花的時間。
