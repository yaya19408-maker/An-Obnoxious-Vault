☀ <span class="category">**球門 球網：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**goal**</span> [ɡəʊl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 體育運動（如足球、曲棍球等）的球門：</span>They had only one shot on goal. 他們只有一次射門。/ He kicked the ball into an open goal. 他把球踢入空門。/ Who is in goal for this team? 誰是本隊的守門員？

<span class="vocabulary">**net**</span> [net] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [sing.] the net 在體育運動中可指球門網：</span>The ball went into the net. 球滾進了網。<span class="definition">2 [sing.] the net 可指網球等運動的球網：</span>to hit the ball over the net 擊球過網
