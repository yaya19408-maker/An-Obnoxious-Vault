☀ <span class="category">**滑板 冰刀：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**skateboard**</span> ['skeɪtbɔ:d] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 滑板：</span>a skateboard park 滑板場 / a skateboard ramp 滑板坡道
           
<span class="vocabulary">**runner**</span> [ˈrʌnə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（金屬、塑膠或木製的）條狀滑行裝置，如（雪橇的）滑板；（冰鞋的）冰刀：</span>the runners of a sledge 雪橇的滑板

<span class="vocabulary">**skate**</span> [skeɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（在冰上滑冰用的）冰鞋；冰刀：</span>a pair of skates 一雙冰鞋 <span class="definition"> <span class="definition">2 [C]（帶輪子的）旱冰鞋：</span>a pair of skates 一雙旱冰鞋
           
<span class="vocabulary">**blade**</span> [bleɪd]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 溜冰鞋的冰刀：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**ski**</span> [ski:] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 滑雪板；滑水板：</span>a pair of skis 一副滑雪板 / cross-country ski 越野滑雪板 / The children go to school on skis. 孩子們滑著滑雪板去上學。

<span class="vocabulary">**board**</span> [bɔ:d] 
<span class="definition">n. 趴著進行衝浪的衝浪板：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**binding**</span> [ˈbaɪndɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（滑雪板的）滑雪鞋固定裝置：</span>瞭解即可

周邊詞彙補充：
· sled可指雪橇；
· sledge可指（在雪上或冰上滑行的）雪橇、冰爬犁。
