☀ <span class="category">**門柱 橫樑：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**post**</span> [pəʊst] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] 足球的門柱，相當於goalpost：</span>The ball hit the post and bounced out. 球打在了門柱上，彈了出來。

<span class="vocabulary">**bar**</span> [bɑː] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] the bar在體育運動中為單數名詞，可指球門的橫樑：</span>His shot hit the bar. 他射門擊中球門的橫樑。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**bail**</span> [beɪl]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl.]（在板球運動中）三柱門上的橫木：</span>瞭解即可
