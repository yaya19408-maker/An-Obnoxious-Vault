☀ <span class="category">**改革 革命：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**reform**</span> [rɪ'fɔ:m]  
<span class="definition">vt. 改良一個體系、組織、一部法律等，即改革，改良：</span>to reform the social security system 改革社會保障體系 / The law needs to be reformed. 法律需要進行改革。<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 對一個社會體制、組織、人的行為等所進行的改良或矯正，即改革，改良，改善：</span>political reform 政治改革 / judicial reform 司法改革 / economic reform 經濟改革 / agricultural reform 農業改革 / reforms in housing and education 住房和教育改革 / structural reform 結構改造 / reform movement 改革運動 / reform school（尤美）少年犯管教所
                    
<span class="vocabulary">**shake-up**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (in/of sth)（公司、組織機構、體制等的）重大調整或重組（以提高其運轉效率）：</span>boardroom, government（英）, management shake-up 董事會改組；政府改組；管理層改組 / Police forces face the biggest shake-up in their 150-year history. 警察機關面臨成立150年以來的最大規模改革。/ a major shake-up at the company 對公司的重大改組 / The headline read Local company plans shake-up! 標題是《當地公司計劃重組》。/ a radical shake-up in the chemical industry 化工行業的全面重組 / The paper calls for an urgent shake-up in the health-care system. 這家報紙呼籲衛生保健體制急需改革。/ This case could lead to a shake-up of the prison system. 這一案件可能會引發監獄制度的變革。/ a radical shake-up of the secondary education system 對中學教育體制的重大調整 / Doctors yesterday delivered an overwhelming vote of no confidence in controversial health service shake-ups. 對於備受爭議的公共醫療衛生服務體系的改革整頓舉措，昨天醫生們投出了壓倒多數的不信任票。/ the posts that were scrapped in the recent shake-up 最近改組中被裁掉的職位

<span class="vocabulary">**overhaul**</span> [ˈəʊvəhɔ:l; 美 ˈoʊvərh-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 對體制或方法進行的徹底革新：</span>A radical overhaul of the tax system is necessary. 有必要徹底改革稅制。<span class="definition">vt. 對體制或方法進行徹底革新：</span>Within a year the party had drastically overhauled its structure. 該黨在一年內大刀闊斧地進行了改組。/ The government said it wanted to overhaul the employment training scheme to make it cost effective. 政府表示希望徹底改革就業培訓計劃以實現低成本高效益。

<span class="vocabulary">**revolution**</span> [͵revə'lu:ʃn]  
<span class="definition">n. [C] 很多人所持有的改變一國政府的企圖，尤指透過暴力行動，即革命：</span>violent revolution 暴力革命 / bloodless revolution 不流血的革命 / democratic revolution 民主革命 / proletarian revolution 無產階級革命 / to foment revolution 煽動革命 / the revolution which overthrew the old regime 推翻舊政權的革命
