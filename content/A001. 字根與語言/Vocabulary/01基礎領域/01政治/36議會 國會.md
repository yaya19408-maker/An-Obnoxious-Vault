☀ <span class="category">**議會 國會：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**house**</span> [haʊs]  
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（常寫作House）聚在一起商討並制定一國法律的一群人，可叫做議院；議會；國會：</span>Legislation requires approval by both houses of parliament. 立法須得到議會兩院的一致透過。<span class="definition">2 [sing.] the House可指英國的下議院或上議院（英國上議院叫做“House of Lords”，下議院叫做“House of Commons”，這上下兩院共同組成了英國議會“Parliament of the United Kingdom”。）；也可指美國的眾議院（其全稱是“the United States House of Representatives”，眾議院與參議院“the United States Senate”共同組成了美國國會Congress）：</span>members of the House and Senate 眾議院和參議院的議員
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**chamber**</span> [ˈtʃeɪmbə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一國的議會、議院：</span>the Lower/Upper Chamber (= in Britain, the House of Commons/House of Lords) 下/上議院（英國為the House of Commons / House of Lords）the Chamber of Deputies in the Italian parliament 義大利國會的下議院 / Under Senate rules, the chamber must vote on the bill by this Friday. 根據規定，參議院必須在本星期五以前投票表決此項議案。/ More than 80 parties are contesting seats in the two-chamber parliament. 有80多個黨派在競爭兩院的議席。/ Signor Amato's government has only a 16-seat majority in the Chamber of Deputies. 阿馬託先生的政府在下議院僅以16席的優勢佔有多數席位。/ elected chamber 經選舉產生的議院 / she believes there should be an elected second chamber to replace the House of Lords. 她認為應該由一個經選舉產生的第二院來代替上議院。/ legislative, parliamentary chamber 立法院；行政院 / chamber of commerce 商會

<span class="vocabulary">**lord**</span> [lɔ:d; 美 lɔ:rd]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the Lords 英國國會的上議院，相當於House of Lords：</span>The Lords has/have not yet reached a decision. 上議院尚未做出決定。/ It's very likely the bill will be defeated in the Lords. 該法案很可能會在上議院遭到否決。           

<span class="vocabulary">**congress**</span> [ˈkɒŋgres; 美 ˈkɑ:ŋgrəs]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] Congress 指美國及其他一些國家的國會，議會。其中美國的Congress包括the Senate（參議院）和the House of Representatives（眾議院）：</span>Congress will vote on the proposals tomorrow. 國會明天將對提案進行投票表決。/ President Fujimori suspended Congress and the judiciary. 總統藤森暫停了國會和司法機構的日常活動。/ Within two years of coming to power, he dissolved Congress and the courts. 他執政不到兩年就解散了國會和法院。/ They will have to go to Congress to get the money. 他們將不得不到國會去獲取這筆錢。/ Outraged, he took his case to Congress. 激憤之下，他把案子提交到了國會。/ Congress approved most of the new powers. 國會批准了大部分新的許可權。/ Congress passed a series of important measures. 國會透過了一系列重要措施。/ Congress annually enacts legislation to fund NASA. 國會每年透過立法向國家航空航天局撥款。/ Congress is currently considering legislation that would authorize the US nuclear weapons laboratories to study new types of nuclear weapons. 國會目前正在考慮立法授權美國核武器實驗室研究新型核武器。/ Congress debated the issue at length. 國會對該議題展開了長時間辯論。/ Congress voted to delay a decision. 國會投票決定推遲做出決定。/ Congress appropriated $75 million for the program.（美）國會給該專案撥款7,500萬美元。/ Congress adjourned for the year without approving an economic stimulus package. 國會還沒批准經濟刺激一攬子計劃就進入了年度休會了。/ Congress member 國會成員 / Congress leader 國會領袖 / The Liberals in Congress felt the reforms did not go far enough. 國會中的自由派人士覺得改革還不夠深入。/ legislation for the next session of Congress 國會下一屆會議的立法工作 <span class="definition">2 [C] 用於某些國家的政黨名稱，如國民大會：</span>the African National Congress 非洲國民大會

<span class="vocabulary">**assembly**</span> [əˈsembli]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（也可用Assembly）由選舉產生的、定期開會併為一個地區或國家制定決策或法律的一群人，如議會：</span>elected, representative assembly 選舉出來的議會；代表大會 / constitutional, consultative, general, legislative assembly 立憲會議；協商大會；全體會議；立法會議 / local, national, provincial, regional, state, federal assembly 地方議會；國民議會；省議會；地區議會；州議會；聯邦議會 / the California Assembly 美國加利福尼亞州眾議院 / the UN General Assembly 聯合國大會 / an assembly of party members from the Russian republic 俄羅斯共和國的黨員代表大會 / Power has been handed over to provincial and regional assemblies. 權力已移交給省和地區議會。/ The national assembly has voted to adopt the budget. 國民議會已表決透過預算。/ This country voted for its National Assembly this week. 該國本週進行了國民議會的選舉。/ Provincial assemblies meet once a year. 省議會每年召開一次。/ The assembly voted to delay the legislation to allow further consultation to take place. 議會投票決定暫緩立法以便進一步磋商。/ assembly member 議會議員 / assembly seat 議會席位 / assembly building, assembly room 議會大廈；會議室 / a meeting/session of the assembly 議會的一次會議 / These issues have been discussed in the regional assemblies. 這些問題已經在當地議會里討論過了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**parliament**</span> [ˈpɑ:ləmənt; 美 ˈpɑ:rl-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 議會：</span>federal, national, provincial, regional, state parliament 聯邦議會；國家議會；省議會；地方議會；州議會 / The president will address the Canadian parliament during his trip. 總統在訪問期間將在加拿大議會發表演說。/ The German parliament is called the ‘Bundestag’. 德國的議會稱為Bundestag。/ Parliament today approved the policy, but it has not yet become law. 議會於今天批准了這項政策，但尚未成為法律。/ parliament building 議會大廈

<span class="definition">2 [U] Parliament 指英國議會（包括下議院和上議院）：</span>a Member of Parliament 議會議員 / The issue was debated in Parliament. 議會就這個問題進行了辯論。/ an Act of Parliament 議會法案 / to win a seat in Parliament 贏得議會中的一個席位 / to be elected to Parliament 當選為議會議員 / The bill will come before Parliament next month. 該議案將於下個月提交議會。

<span class="definition">3 [C]（也可用Parliament）一屆議會的會期；（兩次大選之間的）一屆議會：</span>a session of Parliament 一屆議會 / We are now into the second half of the parliament. 我們現已進入了本屆議會的後半任期。/ The legislation is expected to be passed in the next parliament. 預計這項法案將在下一屆議會中透過。/ to dissolve Parliament (= formally end its activities) and call an election 解散議會並下令舉行大選
           
<span class="vocabulary">**senate**</span> [ˈsenət]
<span class="definition">n.（通常用the Senate）1 [sing.] 參議院（美國、澳大利亞、加拿大、法國等國家的兩個立法機構之一；美國許多州議會也設有參議院）：</span>a member of the Senate 參議員 / a Senate committee 參議院委員會 / The Senate is expected to pass the bill shortly. 預計參議院不久就會透過此議案。<span class="definition">2 [sing.] 古羅馬的元老院：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**council**</span> [ˈkaʊnsl]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 被選舉出來統治一個地方的一群人，可以是國家政務委員會，也可以是地方政務委員會：</span>executive, governing, ruling, state council 行政委員會；管理委員會；國務院 / national council 國務委員會 / legislative council 立法委員會 / local council 地方政務委員會 / borough, city, community, county, district, municipal, neighbourhood/neighborhood, parish, provincial, regional, town council 自治市議會；市議會；（蘇格蘭和威爾士的）社群議會；郡政務委員會；區政務委員會；行政堂區議會；省議會；地區議會；鎮政務委員會 / metropolitan, rural, urban council（均尤英） 大都市政務委員會；鄉村政務委員會；城市政務委員會 / tribal council 部落委員會 / elected council 選舉產生的議會 / Many county councils are now controlled by the Conservatives. 保守黨現在控制著很多郡的政務委員會。/ The city council has voted almost unanimously in favour. 市政委員會幾乎一致投票表示贊同。/ council elections 議會選舉 / council member 議會成員 / council representative 議會代表 / council meeting 議會會議 / council chamber 議事廳 / council president 議會主席 / council resolution 議會決議 / council leader, council seat 議會領袖；議會席位 / She's on the borough council. 她是自治市政務委員會的委員。/ Our party won the majority of seats on the city councils. 我們黨贏得了市議會的多數席位。<span class="definition">2 [C] [英] 在市、郡中提供服務（如教育、住房、圖書館管理等服務）的組織機構：</span>Students should apply to their local council for a grant. 學生應該向當地的市政部門申請助學金。/ council employee, council staff, council worker 市政工作人員 / council services 市政部門 <span class="definition">adj.（房屋或公寓）由地方政府所擁有的，人們需要付租金才能居住的：</span>council estate, council flat, council house, council housing 市政住宅區；公租房；市政住房 / council tax（英國根據個人房產的價值收取的）市政稅 / There is a shortage of council housing. 廉租公房房源不足。/ Council tenants around the country are planning a mass lobby of Parliament. 全國各地的市政住房租戶正在計劃組建一個大的議會遊說團。

<span class="vocabulary">**diet**</span> ['daɪət]  
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 一些國家（例如日本）的議會：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**hill**</span> [hɪl]  
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [美，非正式] the Hill可指美國國會大廈（或在此舉行的活動），是Capitol Hill（中文也可叫“國會山”）的非正式用法：</span>瞭解即可
           
相關詞義延伸：
· congress還可指來自不同群體的代表所參與的、對…進行討論和決策的一個（或一系列）大型正式會議，可稱為代表大會。
