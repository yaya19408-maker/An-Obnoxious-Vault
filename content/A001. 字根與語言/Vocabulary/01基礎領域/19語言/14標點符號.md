☀ <span class="category">**標點符號：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**punctuation**</span> [pʌŋktʃu'eɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 標點符號。是此義的常規用詞：</span>mistaken punctuation 用錯的標點符號 <span class="definition">2 [U] 標點符號的使用：</span>He was known for his poor punctuation. 他以很差的標點符號使用而出名。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**comma**</span> [ˈkɒmə; 美 ˈkɑ:mə]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 逗號：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**period**</span> ['pɪərɪəd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [美] 標點符號中的句號或句點，相當於full stop：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**stop**</span> [stɒp] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [英] 句號，與full stop同義：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**colon**</span> [ˈkəʊlən; 美 ˈkoʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 冒號：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**quote**</span> [kwəʊt; 美 kwoʊt]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] quotes可指引號，相當於quotation marks：</span>The word 'remembered' is in quotes. remembered 一詞用引號括了起來。/ double, single quotes 雙引號；單引號 / If you take text from other sources, place it in quotes. 如果你的文字來自其他地方，將其置於引號中。

<span class="vocabulary">**dash**</span> [dæʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 破折號：</span>the dash between the birth date and death date 生卒日期之間的破折號
           
<span class="vocabulary">**semicolon**</span> [ˌsemiˈkəʊlən; 美 ˈsemikoʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 分號：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**exclamation**</span> [ˌekskləˈmeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 驚歎、感嘆；感嘆詞；感嘆句：</span>She took his hand and made an exclamation of shock. 她抓起他的手，發出一聲驚呼。/ He gave an exclamation of surprise. 他發出一聲驚歎。/ She gave a loud exclamation of delight. 她發出一聲喜悅的驚呼。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**bang**</span> [bæŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] [計算機] 感嘆號，即！：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**question mark**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [C] 問號：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**hyphen**</span> [ˈhaɪfn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 連字元：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**ellipsis**</span> [ɪˈlɪpsɪs]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 省略號：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**apostrophe**</span> [əˈpɒstrəfi; 美 əˈpɑ:s-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 作為省字元的撇號（如'63表示1963）：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">2 [C] 作為所有格符號的撇號：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">3 [C] 作為複數符號的撇號：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**bracket**</span> [ˈbrækɪt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [usually pl.]（也可用round bracket）[均英] [美式或正式用法為parenthesis] 括號：</span>angle bracket（英，美）尖括號 / curly bracket（也可用braces，尤美）大括號 / round bracket 圓括號 / square bracket（美式只用brackets）方括號 / My annotations appear in square brackets. 在方括號裡有我給的註解。/ The prices are given in brackets. 價格列在括號中。/ The words in brackets should be deleted. 括號中的字應刪除。/ The numbers outside the curly brackets are the sales figures. 大括號外的數字是銷售量。/ Publication dates are given in brackets after each title. 出版日期括於書名後面。/ Add the numbers in brackets first. 先把括號裡的數字加起來。/ The prices in brackets are special rates for the under 18s. 括號中的價格是年齡在18歲以下的消費者可以享受的特別優惠價。<span class="definition">2 [usually pl.] [美] 方括號，相當於square bracket：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">vt. 用括號括上：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**brace**</span> [breɪs]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 大括號，即{}：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**parenthesis**</span> [pəˈrenθəsɪs]
<span class="definition">n. [usually pl.] [正式] 圓括號。相當於bracket：</span>Irregular forms are given in parentheses. 不規則形式標註在括號內。/ The prices are given in parentheses. 價格在括號內。/ The words in parentheses should be deleted. 括號內的字應該刪掉。/ The numbers outside the parentheses are the sales figures. 括號外的數字是銷售數字。
