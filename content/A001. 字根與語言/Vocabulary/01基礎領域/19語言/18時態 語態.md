☀ <span class="category">**時態 語態：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**tense**</span> [tens] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 動詞的時態：</span>future, past, present tense 將來時；過去時；現在時 / verb tense 動詞時態

<span class="vocabulary">**aspect**</span> ['æspekt] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（動詞的）體（即表示一個動作是發生一次還是發生多次，是已完成還是仍在進行）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**past**</span> [pɑːst] 
<span class="definition">adj. 只作前置定語，（動詞）過去式的：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">n. [sing.] the past 在語法中可指過去時，相當於past tense：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**progressive**</span> [prəˈgresɪv]
<span class="definition">adj.（也可用continuous）在語法中可指（動詞）進行時的：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**present**</span> ['preznt] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the present 現在時，相當於present tense：</span>瞭解即可  

<span class="vocabulary">**future**</span> ['fju:tʃə] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the future 將來時：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**simple**</span> ['simpl] 
<span class="definition">adj.（無需助動詞構成的動詞時態）一般的，簡單的（如在一般現在時或一般過去時中）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**perfect**</span> ['pə:fɪkt] 
<span class="definition">adj. 與動詞完成時有關的：</span>‘I have eaten’ is the present perfect tense of the verb ‘to eat’. I have eaten是動詞eat的現在完成時。<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the perfect（也可用the perfect tense）完成時：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**imperfect**</span> [ɪmˈpɜ:fɪkt; 美 -ˈpɜ:rf-]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.]（也可用the imperfect tense）[語法] the imperfect可指過去未完成時（常稱為過去進行時）：</span>In ‘while I was washing my hair’, the verb is in the imperfect. while I was washing my hair中的動詞是過去未完成時。

<span class="vocabulary">**weak**</span> [wi:k] 
<span class="definition">adj.（動詞的過去式或過去分詞的變化形式）規則的；弱（變化）的（即加-d、-ed或-t）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**voice**</span> [vɒɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the active/passive voice主動語態或被動語態：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**active**</span> ['æktɪv] 
<span class="definition">adj. 主動語態的：</span>In ‘He was driving the car’, the verb is active. 在He was driving the car一句中，動詞是主動語態。<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 也可用active voice，主動語態：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**passive**</span> ['pæsɪv] 
<span class="definition">adj. 被動語態的：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">n. [sing.] 也可用passive voice，被動語態：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**get**</span> [ɡet] 
<span class="definition">aux. 常用作助動詞，替代be動詞構成被動語態：</span>A pane of glass got broken. 一塊玻璃被打碎了。

<span class="vocabulary">**dynamic**</span> [daɪ'næmɪk] 
<span class="definition">adj.（動詞）動態的（即用於描述一個動作而非狀態，且可用於進行時態的，如eat，grow或knock）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**agent**</span> ['eɪdӡənt] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 動作的發出者，即施動者：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**patient**</span> ['peɪʃnt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 動作的受動者：</span>瞭解即可
