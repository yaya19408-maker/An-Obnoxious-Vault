☀ <span class="category">**學科 科目：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**subject**</span> ['sʌbdӡɪkt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 學校的學科，科目（是關於某個學科領域的知識）。是此義的常規用詞：</span>Maths is my favourite subject. 數學是我最喜歡的科目。/ compulsory, main subject 必修科目；主科 / optional subject 選修科目 / art（美）, arts（英）, science subject 文科科目；理科科目 / What subjects are you taking (= studying) this year? 你今年選修了什麼課程？ 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**discipline**</span> [ˈdɪsəplɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 知識領域；（尤指大學的）學科，科目：</span>core, main, major discipline 核心科目；主要科目 / Students are to be tested on the three core disciplines: mathematics，English and science. 學生要考三門核心科目：數學、英語和自然科學。/ When did sociology emerge as a distinct discipline? 社會學是何時成為獨立學科的? / established, traditional discipline 老牌的學科；傳統的學科 / emerging, new discipline 新興學科 / social work and its related disciplines 社會工作及相關學科 / academic, intellectual, scholarly discipline 學術科目；知識學科 / They established psychology as an academic discipline. 他們確立了心理學的學科地位。/ professional discipline（尤美）專業學科 / humanities, science, scientific discipline 人文學科；理科 / There is a lack of communication across disciplines(= between teachers and students of different subjects). 不同學科之間缺乏交流。/ You've got to make sure that people work together across disciplines. 你必須確保讓那些出身不同學科的人一起共事。/ Within a discipline there may be more than one school of thought. 一個學科內可以有不止一個學派。/ academics from diverse academic disciplines 來自不同學科的學者 / multiple disciplines（尤美）多學科 / experts in multiple disciplines 多學科的專家 / The university offers a wide range of disciplines. 大學設有許多不同的學科。/ We're looking for people from a wide range of disciplines. 我們需要各個學科的人才。
