☀ <span class="category">**釣魚 捕魚：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**fish**</span> [fɪʃ] 
<span class="definition">vi. 1 fish (for sth) 為運動休閒等目的而用鉤、網等釣魚或捕魚：</span>to learn to fish 學釣魚 <span class="definition">2 fish (for sth) 為獲取食物而用鉤、網等釣魚或捕魚：</span>to learn to fish 學釣魚 <span class="definition">3 go fishing 為享受釣魚的樂趣而釣魚：</span>Let’s go fishing. 咱們去釣魚吧。<span class="definition">vt. 在某片水域捕魚或釣魚：</span>to fish the lake for ... 在湖裡釣…魚
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fishing**</span> [ˈfɪʃɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 釣魚運動；捕魚活動：</span>They often go fishing. 他們經常去釣魚。/ We enjoyed a day's fishing by the river. 我們在河邊享受了一天垂釣之樂。/ Despite the poor weather the fishing has been pretty good. 儘管天氣惡劣，卻漁獲頗豐。/ This stretch of the river is renowned for its good fishing. 這個河段以能捕到很多魚而出名。/ coarse（英）, deep-sea, drift-net, saltwater, sea fishing（不包括鱒魚和鮭魚的）淡水捕魚；深海捕魚；漂網捕魚；鹹水捕魚；海上捕魚 / game, salmon, shark, trout fishing 釣魚；捕鮭魚；捕鯊魚；捕鱒魚 / She has been big game fishing off the coast of Kenya.她曾在肯亞沿岸釣過大魚。/ commercial, industrial fishing 商業捕魚；工業捕魚 / recreational fishing 休閒釣魚 / illegal fishing 非法捕魚 / fishing equipment, fishing gear, fishing line, fishing rod, fishing tackle 釣魚裝備；釣魚用具；釣線；釣竿；釣具 / fishing boat, fishing craft, fishing fleet, fishing trawler, fishing vessel 捕魚船；漁船隊；拖網漁船；漁輪 / fishing harbour/harbor（尤英）, fishing port, fishing town, fishing village 漁港；漁鎮；漁村 / fishing area, fishing grounds, fishing spot, fishing zone 漁區；漁場；捕魚地帶 / the rich fishing grounds off the coast of Namibia 奈米比亞海岸富饒的漁場 / Just below that bridge is a good fishing spot. 那座橋的正下方是個釣魚的好地方。/ fishing dock, fishing pier（均美）釣魚碼頭 / fishing activity 捕魚活動 / fishing quotas 捕魚配額 / The two countries have signed a new fishing agreement. 兩國簽署了新的捕魚協議。/ fishing ban 捕魚禁令 / fishing rights 釣魚權 / fishing licence/license 捕魚許可證 / fishing expedition, fishing holiday（英）, fishing trip釣魚之旅 / fishing club 釣魚俱樂部 / fishing lodge 釣魚者暫住的小屋 / fishing business, fishing industry 漁業 / fishing methods, fishing techniques 捕魚方法；釣魚技巧 / Ecuador announced a ban on fishing for shrimp. 厄瓜多頒佈了禁止捕蝦的法令。

<span class="vocabulary">**hook**</span> [hʊk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 專指用魚鉤來釣魚：</span>It was the biggest pike I ever hooked. 那是我釣到的最大的狗魚。/ She had managed to hook a wealthy husband.（比喻用法）她成功釣到了一位有錢的丈夫。

<span class="vocabulary">**angle**</span> ['æŋɡl] 
<span class="definition">vi.（常用go angling）（用魚鉤和釣線）釣魚：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**bait**</span> [beɪt]

<span class="definition">vt. 在魚鉤上放誘餌：</span>He baited his hook with pie. 他在魚鉤上裝了點餡餅作釣餌。

<span class="vocabulary">**cast**</span> [kɑːst] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 將釣魚線的一端（如魚鉤）投入河流等水體中：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">n. [C] 投、拋、扔、擲等（尤指丟擲釣魚線）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**bite**</span> [baɪt] 
<span class="definition">vi.（魚）咬餌，咬鉤，上鉤：</span>The fish stopped biting. 魚不再上鉤。<span class="definition">n. [C]（魚的）咬餌，咬鉤：</span>to get a bite 魚上鉤 

<span class="vocabulary">**land**</span> [lænd] 
<span class="definition">vt. 捕到魚，釣到魚：</span>瞭解即可

周邊詞彙補充：
· net可指用網來捕魚。
