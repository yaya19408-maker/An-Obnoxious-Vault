☀ <span class="category">**追趕動物 抓捕動物 馴化動物：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**lasso**</span> [læˈsu:; ˈlæsəʊ; 美 ˈlæsoʊ]
<span class="definition">vt. 用套索套捕（動物）：</span>Cowboys drove covered wagons and rode horses, lassoing cattle. 牛仔們有的趕著大篷車，有的騎著馬，拋套索捕牛。

<span class="vocabulary">**rope**</span> [rəʊp] 
<span class="definition">vt. [尤美] 以扔出圓形套索的方式來抓捕動物，相當於lasso：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**worry**</span> ['wʌrɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt.（狗）透過追趕和咬來攻擊動物（尤指一隻羊）：</span>瞭解即可
                     
<span class="vocabulary">**tame**</span> [teɪm] 
<span class="definition">adj.（動物、鳥等）馴服的：</span>The deer never became tame; they would run away if you approached them. 那些鹿一直沒有被馴服：你一靠近，它們就跑開。<span class="definition">vt. 馴化；馴服（野生動物或禽鳥）：</span>Lions can never be completely tamed. 獅子永遠不能被完全馴化。/ The Amazons were believed to have been the first to tame horses. 據說亞馬孫人最先馴化了馬。
