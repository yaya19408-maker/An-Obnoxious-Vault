☀ <span class="category">**顧客 客戶：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**customer**</span> ['kʌstəmə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 購買產品或服務的顧客，客戶。是此義的基礎用詞：</span>long-standing, long-time, loyal customer 長期的客戶；忠實的客戶 / regular customer 常客 / old customer 老客戶 / repeat customer（美）回頭客 / potential customer 潛在的客戶 / foreign customer 外國客戶 / corporate, retail customer 公司客戶；零售客戶 / customer relation, customer service 客戶關係；客戶服務 / customer demands 客戶需求 / customer loyalty 顧客的忠誠度 / customer complaints 顧客的投訴 / customer base 客戶群
           
<span class="vocabulary">**client**</span> [ˈklaɪənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（提供諮詢或服務的專業人士或專業機構的）客戶，在具體語境下也可翻譯為委託人、當事人，但詞義核心不變，即專業服務的享受者：</span>a solicitor and his client 律師和他的委託人 / a lawyer with many famous clients 擁有許多著名委託人的律師 / Social workers must always consider the best interests of their clients. 社會工作者必須時刻考慮其當事人的最佳利益。/ The company required clients to pay substantial fees in advance. 公司要求客戶預付一大筆費用。/ The company needs to focus on its biggest clients. 公司需要把注意力集中在最大的那些客戶身上。/ business, corporate client 生意上的客戶；公司客戶 / celebrity client 名人客戶 / A new branch has been opened to serve clients in Dallas. 服務於達拉斯客戶的新分公司已開張營業。/ She's so busy that she's not taking on any new clients. 她太忙了，不接新客戶。/ a campaign to attract new clients 吸引新客戶的活動 / On behalf of my client, I would like to remind you of your obligations in this matter. 我謹代表我的當事人提醒你注意在此事中你應盡的義務。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**clientele**</span> [ˌkli:ənˈtel; 美 ˌklaɪənˈtel]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.]（統稱）（商店、飯館、組織機構等的）顧客、客戶、主顧：</span>This pub had a mixed clientele. 這家酒吧什麼樣的客人都有。/ I have built up a loyal satisfied clientele for both African and European clothes. 我已經發展了一批對非洲和歐洲的服裝都很滿意的忠實客戶。/ loyal clientele 忠實的客戶群 / regular, usual clientele 常客；老主顧 / exclusive, fashionable, select, upscale（美）clientele 特定顧客群；趕潮流的顧客群；貴賓客戶群；高階客戶群 / The restaurant has a large regular clientele. 這家餐館有很多常客。/ The boutique caters for a rather select clientele. 這家精晶店為貴賓顧客群服務。/ It takes rime to build（up）a clientele. 建立客戶網需要時間。           
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**shopper**</span> [ˈʃɒpə(r); 美 ˈʃɑ:p-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 購物者；商店的顧客：</span>The streets were full of Christmas shoppers. 街上擠滿了為聖誕節採購的人。/ what the average shopper's spends her money on 普通購物者花錢選購的商品 / frequent（美）, regular（尤英）shopper 經常購物的人 / last-minute shoppers on Christmas Eve 於聖誕夜最後時刻前來購物的人 / personal shopper 代人購物者 / mystery, secret shopper 神秘顧客 / online shopper 線上購物者 / We are a nation of supermarket shoppers.（supermarket尤為英式用法）我們的國人習慣於到超市購物。/ savvy, smart（均尤美）shopper 精明的購物者 / compulsive, frantic, frenzied shopper 購物狂 / serious shopper 熱衷購物的人 / happy shopper 快樂的購物者 / I observed my fellow shoppers rushing from store to store. 我觀察到和我一樣的購物者匆忙趕往一家又一家商店。/ The cheap deals brought out big crowds of shoppers. 低廉的價格吸引了眾多的購物者。/ The street was thronged with shoppers. 街上擠滿了購物的人。/ special offers designed to tempt shoppers 吸引購物者的特價品 / Shoppers rushed to catch the sales. 購物的人趕去買促銷商品。/ Window displays are intended to be attractive to shoppers. 櫥窗陳列是為了吸引顧客。

<span class="vocabulary">**principal**</span> [ˈprɪnsəpl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [術語]（尤指在商務中）你所代表的一個人，即當事人，委託人：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**account**</span> [ə'kaʊnt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 商業中的老顧客，老客戶，尤指在商家處有賒賬記錄的客戶：</span>The company has lost two accounts. 這家公司失去了兩個老客戶。

<span class="vocabulary">**regular**</span> ['reɡjələ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 經常光顧某一商店、酒吧、餐廳等的顧客，即常客，老主顧：</span>one of our regulars 我們的一位老主顧 / regulars at his local bar 他的當地酒吧的常客們
           
<span class="vocabulary">**patron**</span> [ˈpeɪtrən]
<span class="definition">n. [正式] 商店、飯館、酒吧、旅館等的常客、顧客：</span>Patrons are requested not to smoke. 請顧客不要吸菸。/ He spent the night at the Savoy: like so many of its patrons, he could not resist the exclusively English cooking. 他在薩沃伊酒店過了夜：和許多顧客一樣，他無法抗拒精美的英式菜餚。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fixture**</span> [ˈfɪkstʃə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 常客：</span>She was a fixture in New York's nightclubs. 她是紐約夜總會的常客。

<span class="vocabulary">**base**</span> [beɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 商業領域可指公司的客戶群或使用者群：</span>client, customer, subscriber, user base 客戶群；使用者群 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**consumer**</span> [kənˈsju:mə(r); 美 -ˈsu:-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 購買商品或服務的一個人（或團體、國家），即消費者：</span>sophisticated consumer 老練的消費者 / domestic consumer 國內消費者 / green consumer 有環保意識的消費者 / Health-conscious consumers want more information about the food they buy. 注重身體健康的消費者想得到更多有關他們所購買的食物的資訊。/ electricity, energy consumer 電力使用者；能源使用者 / the market for consumer durables 耐用消費品市場 / consumer organization, consumer watchdog 消費者組織；消費者權益保護組織 / consumer culture 消費文化 / a consumer society (= one where buying and selling is considered to be very important) 消費社會 / the consumer prices index（美）, the consumer price index（英）消費品價格指數 / consumer demand, consumer choice, consumer rights 消費者的需求；消費者的選擇；消費者的權利 / Mass culture turns audiences into passive consumers. 大眾文化把受眾都變成了被動消費者。/ Tax cuts will boost consumer confidence after the recession. 減稅將增強消費者在經濟衰退後的信心。/ We are the biggest consumer of tropical hardwoods after Japan. 我們是僅次於日本的第二大熱帶硬木消費國。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**buyer**</span> [ˈbaɪə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（尤指昂貴商品的）買主；買方：</span>potential, prospective, would-be buyer 潛在的買主；可能的買主 / Have you found a buyer for your house? 你的房子找到買主了嗎？/ The houses are ideal for first-time buyers. 這房子非常適合首次置業者。/ home, house（尤英）buyer 買房者 / Car buyers are more interested in safety and reliability than speed. 購車者們更感興趣的是汽車的安全性和可靠性，而不是它的速度。/ buyer beware(= the buyer is responsible for checking the quality of the goods）買方自負責任 / In matters of second-hand cars, your motto should be 'buyer beware'. 購買二手車的原則應當是"買方自負責任"。/ a buyer's market（= when prices are low）買方市場 / In a buyer's market the commodity is plentiful and so its price is not high. 在買方市場的情況下，商品豐富，因此價格不高。<span class="definition">2 [C]（大商店的）採購員，進貨員；買手：</span>I was a buyer for the women's clothing department. 我是女裝部的進貨員。

<span class="vocabulary">**browser**</span> [ˈbraʊzə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 逛商店的人（但並不是真的想買）：</span>a casual browser 閒逛的人

相關詞義延伸：
· client還可指（連線在伺服器上的）客戶機。

周邊詞彙補充：
· receiver可指購買（或接受）被盜的物品（即贓物）的人。
