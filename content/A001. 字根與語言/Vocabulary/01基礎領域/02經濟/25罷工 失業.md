☀ <span class="category">**罷工 失業：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**industrial action**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [U] [尤英] 勞工行動，尤指罷工（以向僱主表示抗議，讓僱主增加薪水或改善工作條件）：</span>Prison officers have decided to take industrial action. 獄警們已決定舉行罷工。/ The union is considering stepping up its industrial action. 工會在考慮加大勞工行動的力度。/ His research indicates an increase in the number of women involved in industrial action. 他的研究表明參與勞工行動的女性人數有所增加。/ The union is threatening industrial action against the company. 工會揚言將對公司採取勞工行動。/ industrial action by air traffic controllers 空中交通管制員的勞工行動 / industrial action in support of demands for a 10% salary adjustment 支援薪金調整10%的勞工行動 / to take industrial action over pay 因為報酬問題而罷工

<span class="vocabulary">**strike**</span> [straɪk] 
<span class="definition">vi. strike (for sth)（工人）罷工：</span>They are set to strike on Tuesday. 他們確定要在星期二舉行罷工。/ Drivers are threatening to strike over pay. 司機們威脅要就工資問題舉行罷工。<span class="definition">n. [C] 工人的罷工，商人的罷市，學生的罷課等：</span>one-day strike 為期一天的罷工 / indefinite strike（尤英）無限期罷工 / wildcat strike 自發性罷工 / nationwide strike 全國性罷工 / hunger strike 絕食抗議 / sit-down strike 靜坐示威 / strike action 罷工行動 / strike movement 罷工運動 / strike leader 罷工領袖 / a strike over pay 有關工資問題的罷工
           
<span class="vocabulary">**stoppage**</span> [ˈstɒpɪdʒ; 美 ˈstɑ:p-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 停工、罷工（作為抗議）：</span>work, workplace（英）stoppage 停工；罷工 / The stoppage was called to protest against wage cuts. 組織這次罷工是為了抗議減薪。/ Mineworkers in the Ukraine have voted for a one-day stoppage next month. 烏克蘭礦工們已投票決定下個月罷工一天。/ They are set to stage a two-hour stoppage next week. 他們定於下週進行兩小時的罷工。

<span class="vocabulary">**out**</span> [aʊt] 
<span class="definition">adv., prep. [英，非正式] 罷工：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**picket**</span> [ˈpɪkɪt]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（罷工期間糾察妥協分子的）糾察員，糾察隊；罷工警戒：</span>mass picket（罷工時勸阻他人上班的）大規模的糾察隊 / flying picket（英）（罷工時由工會派至各處勸說工人參加罷工的）流動宣傳員 / Flying pickets arrived from all over the country. 流動宣傳員從全國各地紛紛抵達。/ They organized a mass picket of the governor's palace. 他們組織了總督府的大規模罷工糾察隊。/ picket duty 糾察任務 / Abuse was hurled at workers who crossed the picket line. 越過罷工糾察線的工人遭到了肆意謾罵。/ picket sign（美）糾察標誌 / angry people with picket signs 帶著糾察標誌的憤怒人群 / Five pickets were arrested by police. 五名糾察隊員被警方逮捕。/ I was on picket duty at the time. 當時我正執行罷工的糾察任務。/ a mass picket of the factory 工廠的大規模罷工糾察隊 / Ten hotels were damaged by pickets in the weekend strike of hotel workers. 在賓館員工週末舉行的罷工中，有十家賓館遭到糾察隊員的破壞。/ The strikers agreed to remove their pickets and hold talks with the government. 罷工者同意撤回糾察隊員，並和政府進行對話。<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 嘗試勸說人們加入罷工；進行罷工糾察：</span>200 workers were picketing the factory. 200名工人在工廠外進行圍廠執行罷工。/ Striking workers picketed outside the gates. 罷工工人圍在大門外擔任糾察（禁止出入）。/ The miners went on strike and picketed the power stations. 礦工開始罷工，並在發電站周圍設定了糾察隊。

<span class="vocabulary">**unemployment**</span> [ʌnɪm'plɒɪmənt] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 失業的事實，還可指失業人數，失業率：</span>involuntary unemployment 非自願性失業 / graduate, youth unemployment 畢業生失業；年輕人失業 / urban unemployment 城鎮失業人數 / Unemployment climbed above two million. 失業人數攀升到兩百萬以上。/ unemployment rate 失業率 <span class="definition">2 [U] 指失業的狀態：</span>They are facing long-term unemployment. 他們正面臨長期失業。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**unemployed**</span> [ˌʌnɪmˈplɔɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. 失業的、下崗的、待業的：</span>How long have you been unemployed? 你失業多久了？/ an unemployed builder 失業的建築工人 / The problem is millions of people are unemployed. 問題是，大批的人都失業了。/ This workshop helps young unemployed people in Grimsby. 該研習班會幫助格里姆斯比的失業青年。/ The closure of the factory left hundreds of men unemployed. 該工廠倒閉導致數百工人失業。

<span class="vocabulary">**jobless**</span> [ˈdʒɒbləs; 美 ˈdʒɑ:b-]
<span class="definition">adj. 沒有工作的，失業的：</span>the number of jobless people 失業人數 / The closure left 500 people jobless. 這次倒閉使500人失業。/ One in four people are now jobless in inner areas like Tottenham and Peckham. 像托特納姆和佩卡姆這樣的內陸地區失業人口達1/4。<span class="definition">n. [pl.] the jobless失業者：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**out of work**</span>
<span class="definition">adj. 失業的、下崗的：</span>a town where half the men are usually out of work 通常有半數男子失業的城鎮 / an out of work actor 沒戲演的演員
