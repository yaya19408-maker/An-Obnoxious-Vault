☀ <span class="category">**匯率 外匯：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**strength**</span> [streŋθ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 一國貨幣相對於他國貨幣的幣值強弱程度或穩定程度：</span>Profits have been helped by the relative strength of the euro against the dollar. 歐元相對於美元較為堅挺，因此收益較高。

<span class="vocabulary">**strong**</span> [strɒŋ] 
<span class="definition">adj.（價格、貨幣、經濟等）行情看漲的，堅挺的，呈現強勢的：</span>strong prices 看漲的物價 / The U.S. dollar continued its strong performance in Tokyo today. 美元今天在東京繼續了其堅挺的表現。/ The yen is getting stronger against the dollar. 日元對美元呈強勢走向。

<span class="vocabulary">**firm**</span> [fə:m] 
<span class="definition">adj. firm (against sth) （一國的貨幣等）價值堅挺的：</span>The euro remained firm against the dollar. 歐元對美元依然堅挺。

<span class="vocabulary">**gain**</span> [ɡeɪn] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. gain (against sth)（貨幣或股票）增值，升值：</span>The shares gained 14p. 股價上漲了14便士。/ The euro gained against the dollar again today. 今天歐元兌換美元的匯率又上升了。

<span class="vocabulary">**appreciate**</span> [ə'pri:ʃɪeɪt] 
<span class="definition">vi.（經過一段時間而）價值上升，即升值，增值，與depreciate相反：</span>Their investments have appreciated over the years. 他們的投資這些年來已經增值。

<span class="vocabulary">**appreciation**</span> [ə͵pri:ʃɪ'eɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.] appreciation (in sth)（經過一段時間）價值的上升，即升值，增值：</span>capital appreciation of the property 房產的資本增值
           
<span class="vocabulary">**depreciate**</span> [dɪˈpri:ʃieɪt]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi.（使）貶值：</span>Cars depreciate in value fairly rapidly. 汽車貶值很快。/ The demand for foreign currency depreciates the real value of local currencies. 對外匯的需求致使本幣的實際價值下跌。/ Countries may also find their currency is depreciating in foreign markets. 一些國家也可能會發現自己的貨幣正在國外市場上貶值。/ The US dollar is expected to depreciate against the pound. 美元預計對英鎊貶值。/ The rupee depreciated by 9 per cent. 盧比貶值了9%。/ Shares continued to depreciate on the stock markets today. 今日股市股價繼續下跌。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**devalue**</span> [ˌdi:ˈvælju:]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. [金融] ~ (sth) (against sth) 使（貨幣）貶值：</span>India has devalued the Rupee by about eleven per cent. 印度已將盧比貶值了約 11%。/ The Pound would be devalued, we were told. 我們被告知英鎊將會貶值。/ The pound was devalued against the US dollar. 英鎊對美元貶值了。/ The Cambodian currency was effectively devalued by 25%. 柬埔寨貨幣實際上貶值了25%。

<span class="vocabulary">**erode**</span> [ɪˈrəʊd; 美 ɪˈroʊd]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. [常用被動式]（通貨膨脹、年齡等）（使）貶值、（使）降價：</span>Competition in the financial marketplace has eroded profits. 金融市場的競爭降低了利潤。/ The value of the dollar began to erode rapidly just around this time. 剛好在這個時候，美元開始迅速貶值。/ Mortgage payments have been eroded (= decreased in value) by inflation. 償還的按揭貸款因通貨膨脹而貶值。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**erosion**</span> [ɪˈrəʊʒn; 美 ɪˈroʊʒn]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（價值的）降低;（貨幣的）貶值：</span>a dramatic erosion of support for the program 對該專案的支援大幅減少 / the steady price erosion that has occurred in Japan 日本出現的價格持續下滑
 
<span class="vocabulary">**weak**</span> [wi:k] 
<span class="definition">adj.（貨幣）疲軟的：</span>a weak currency 疲軟的貨幣 / weak dollar 美元疲軟

<span class="vocabulary">**run**</span> [rʌn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] run on the dollar, pound, etc. 常用單數形式，指美元、英鎊等的拋售：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**float**</span> [fləʊt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 經濟金融領域中可指政府使貨幣匯率自由浮動：</span>The government decided to allow the peso to float freely. 政府決定讓比索自由浮動。

<span class="vocabulary">**exchange**</span> [ɪks'tʃeɪndӡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 各國貨幣之間的兌換：</span>currency exchange 貨幣兌換 / exchange rate 匯率 <span class="definition">vt. exchange A for B 兌換金錢，相當於change：</span>You can exchange your currency for dollars in the hotel. 你可在旅館把你的錢兌換成美元。

<span class="vocabulary">**change**</span> [tʃeɪndӡ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. change sth (into sth) 把一國貨幣兌換成另一國貨幣：</span>Where can I change my traveller’s cheques? 哪裡可以兌換旅行支票？/ to change dollars into yen 把美元兌換成日元

說明：strong和firm表“貨幣堅挺”時，①strong強調行情看漲，突出上漲的趨勢；②firm則側重於表現下跌的可能性極小（而不一定是價格上漲，因為也有可能是價格平穩但不太可能下降）。

exchange和change表“兌換本外幣”時，①二者在詞義上無明顯差別；②exchange通常較為正式。

相關詞義延伸：
· change還可指（同種貨幣之間）整錢和零錢互換，或紙幣與硬幣互換。
