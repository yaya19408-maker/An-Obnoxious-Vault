☀ <span class="category">**財會 審計：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**accountant**</span> [ə'kaʊntənt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一個從事會計行業的人，即會計員，會計師：</span>certified public（美）, chartered（英）accountant 註冊會計師；特許會計師 / professional accountant 專業會計師 / independent accountant 獨立會計師 / company’s chief accountant 公司的總會計師
           
<span class="vocabulary">**accountancy**</span> [əˈkaʊntənsi]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [尤英]（美式英語常用accounting）會計學；會計工作、會計職業：</span>He's sitting his final exams in accountancy. 他正參加會計學的期末考試。/ chartered accountancy（英）特許會計工作 / public accountancy（美）註冊會計師的工作/ It had taken considerable creative accountancy on my part to produce a set of figures that showed us not making any profit at all. 為了炮製出一套數字，表示我們根本沒有盈利，我費了不少"造賬"功夫。/ accountancy firm, accountancy practice（英）會計師事務所；會計慣例 / accountancy body（英）會計團體 / the members of the different professional accountancy bodies 不同行業會計團體的成員 / profession accountancy 會計職業
           
<span class="vocabulary">**auditor**</span> [ˈɔ:dɪtə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（對公司財務狀況進行審計的）審計員、稽核員：</span>company, company's auditor 公司審計員 / internal auditor 內部作審計員 / external, independent, outside auditor 外部的審計員；獨立的審計員；外來的審計員 / county（美）, district, government（英）auditor 縣級審計員；地區審計員；政府的審計員 / The council and district auditors have agreed that the deals were unlawful. 政務委員會和區審計員一致認為這些交易是非法的。/ She is one of the nation's leading auditors of public companies. 她是全國最重要的上市公司審計員之一。/ His firm has been appointed the auditors to the company.（to為英式用法）他的事務所獲指派為該公司做審計。/ an auditor's report 審計報告
