☀ <span class="category">**光臨 惠顧：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**business**</span> ['bɪznɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 顧客的光臨，惠顧：</span>We’re grateful for your business. 感謝您的光顧。

<span class="vocabulary">**custom**</span> ['kʌstəm] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] [英，正式]（英美也用business）顧客或客戶在商店或公司裡購買產品的情況，可稱為惠顧、光顧：</span>Thank you for your custom. Please call again. 謝謝您的惠顧，請下次再來。/ We’ve lost a lot of custom since prices went up. 自從價格上漲以來我們失去了很多生意。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**patronize**</span> [ˈpætrənaɪz; 美 ˈpeɪt-]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 經常光顧一家商店、餐館等：</span>The club is patronized by students and locals alike. 學生和當地居民都經常去那個俱樂部。/ The ladies of Berne liked to patronize the Palace for tea and little cakes. 伯爾尼的女士們喜歡去聯邦宮殿用茶點。
