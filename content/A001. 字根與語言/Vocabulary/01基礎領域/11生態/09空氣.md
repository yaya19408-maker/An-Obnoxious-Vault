☀ <span class="category">**空氣：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**air**</span> [eə] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 空氣：</span>cold, cool air 冷空氣；涼爽的空氣 / clear air 清新的空氣 / damp air 潮溼的空氣 / dry air 乾燥的空氣 / thin air 稀薄的空氣 / air pollution 空氣汙染 / air quality 空氣質量 / air pressure 氣壓 / air current 氣流 / air temperature 氣溫 / air conditioner 空調

<span class="vocabulary">**atmosphere**</span> ['ætməsfɪə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（房間、封閉空間或某處的）空氣：</span>heavy, humid, oppressive, polluted, stuffy atmosphere 沉悶的空氣；潮溼的空氣；壓抑的空氣；被汙染的空氣；悶熱的空氣 / the atmosphere of towns and cities 城鎮的空氣 / These plants love warm, humid atmospheres. 這些植物喜歡溫暖潮溼的空氣。/ Last night’s storm had cleared the atmosphere. 昨夜的暴風雨使空氣變得清新。

<span class="vocabulary">**oxygen**</span> ['ɒksɪdӡən] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 氧氣：</span>atmospheric oxygen 大氣中的氧 / After dark the plants stop producing oxygen. 天黑後植物不再製造氧氣。
