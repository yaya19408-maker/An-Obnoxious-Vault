☀ <span class="category">**體液：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**humour**</span> ['hju:mə]（美humor）
<span class="definition">n. [C] 舊時所認為的存在於人體內的四種體液之一（當時認為能夠影響人的健康和性格）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**blood**</span> [blʌd] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 血，血液：</span>The body lay in a pool of blood. 屍體躺在血泊之中。/ animal, human blood 動物的血；人血 / to lose blood 失血 / to donate blood 獻血 / blood cell 血細胞 / blood test 血液檢查 / blood bank 血庫 / blood circulation 血（液）迴圈 / blood poisoning 敗血症 / blood glucose, blood sugar 血糖 / blood transfusion 輸血 <span class="definition">adj. -blooded 用於構成形容詞，指有某類血的：</span>cold-blooded 冷血的
         
<span class="vocabulary">**bloodstream**</span> [ˈblʌdstri:m]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 體內迴圈的血液；血流：</span>They injected the drug directly into her bloodstream. 他們把藥物直接注射到她的血液裡。/ The disease releases toxins into the bloodstream. 這種病會釋放毒素到血液中。/ The drug is quickly absorbed into the bloodstream. 藥物很快就被吸收進迴圈的血液中。/ Traces of banned substances were detected in his bloodstream. 在他的血液中檢測出了違禁物質的成分。/ Bacteria was introduced into his bloodstream through an unsterile needle. 細菌透過未消毒的針頭進入他的血流。/ Red blood cells transport oxygen through the bloodstream. 紅細胞透過血液迴圈輸送氧。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**circulation**</span> [ˌsɜ:kjəˈleɪʃn; 美 ˌsɜ:rk-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 血液迴圈：</span>Regular exercise will improve blood circulation. 經常鍛鍊會促進血液迴圈。/ I have poor circulation. 我的血液迴圈不好。/ cold spots in the fingers caused by poor circulation 血液迴圈不暢導致的手指上的凍瘡 / to have a massage to stimulate your circulation 以按摩來刺激血液迴圈 / the circulation of the blood 血液迴圈 / Anyone with heart, lung or circulation problems should seek medical advice before flying. 有心肺疾病或血液迴圈問題的人在乘坐飛機前應尋求醫生建議。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**clot**</span> [klɒt; 美 klɑ:t]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 血液的凝塊，相當於blood clot：</span>They removed a clot from his brain. 他們從他的大腦裡取出了血塊。/ He needed emergency surgery to remove a blood clot from his brain. 他需要進行緊急手術以清除腦部的一處血塊。/ a blood clot on her brain 她腦中的血塊 / patients who have a tendency to form clots more readily 易形成血栓的患者

<span class="vocabulary">**juice**</span> [dӡu:s] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl.] 胃內的消化液，即胃液：</span>digestive, gastric juices 消化液；胃液
 
<span class="vocabulary">**mucus**</span> [ˈmju:kəs]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 人體分泌的黏液（如鼻涕）：</span>excess mucus 過多的黏液 / cervical, nasal mucus 宮頸黏液；鼻涕 / sticky, thick mucus 黏稠的黏液 / mucus production, mucus secretion 黏液的產生；黏液的分泌

<span class="vocabulary">**saliva**</span> [səˈlaɪvə]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 唾液、口水：</span>I wiped the saliva off my mouth. 我抹去嘴邊的口水。/ The smell of food causes the saliva to flow. 食物的氣味讓我流口水。

<span class="vocabulary">**spit**</span> [spɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 唾液，唾沫口水：</span>A trickle of spit collected at the corner of his mouth. 一小股口水積聚在了他的嘴角。

<span class="vocabulary">**spittle**</span> [ˈspɪtl]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [舊時] 唾液、口水：</span>Spittle oozed down his jaw. 唾液順著他的下巴流下來。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dribble**</span> [ˈdrɪbl]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [尤英] 口水：</span>There was dribble all down the baby's front. 這嬰兒胸前淌滿了口水。/ His clothes are soaked in dribble. 他的衣服被口水浸溼了。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**sweat**</span> [swet] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 汗，汗水：</span>sweat gland 汗腺 / sweat pore 汗腺孔（或汗孔）/ heavy sweat 大汗 / to drip with sweat 大汗淋漓（或揮汗如雨）/ light sweat 少量汗 / night sweat 盜汗 / The workmen were bathed in sweat. 工人們汗流浹背。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**perspiration**</span> [ˌpɜ:spəˈreɪʃn; 美 ˌpɜ:rs-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 汗水、汗珠：</span>a drop of perspiration 一滴汗珠 / Great beads of perspiration trickled down his forehead. 大滴汗珠順著他的額頭流下來。/ He wiped the perspiration from his brow. 他擦去了額頭的汗水。/ My shirt was soaked in perspiration. 我的襯衫被汗水浸透了。/ Her face was wet with perspiration. 她的臉上滿是汗水。/ Her skin was damp with perspiration. 她的皮膚上汗津津的。/ His hands were wet with perspiration. 他雙手被汗水浸溼了。/ Perspiration ran down his face. 汗水順著他的臉淌下來。/ Perspiration broke out on my skin.我冒汗了。/ Beads of perspiration had formed on his forehead. 他的額頭冒出了豆大的汗珠。/ Beads of perspiration stood out on his forehead. 他的前額上掛著汗珠。/ night sweats and excessive perspiration 夜間盜汗和出汗過多

<span class="vocabulary">**tear**</span> [tɪə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一滴眼淚或淚水：</span>Her eyes filled with tears. 她眼裡噙滿了淚水。/ A tear rolled down his face. 一滴眼淚順著她的面頰流了下來。/ to wipe a tear 擦去淚水 / to fight back the tears 忍住淚水 / angry tears 憤怒的淚水 / happy tear 幸福的淚水 / tear gas 催淚瓦斯 / crocodile tear（比喻用法）鱷魚的眼淚；假慈悲

☀ <span class="category">**激素 因子：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**hormone**</span> [ˈhɔ:məʊn; 美 ˈhɔ:rmoʊn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 激素；荷爾蒙：</span>female, male hormone 雌性激素；雄性激素 / growth, sex hormone 生長激素；性激素 / children who do not produce enough growth hormone 生長激素分泌不足的兒童 / Oestrogen is a female sex hormone. 雌激素是一種雌性荷爾蒙。/ Hormones are secreted into the bloodstream. 分泌的激素進入到血液中。/ hormone production 荷爾蒙的分泌 / hormone therapy, hormone treatment 激素療法；激素治療 / women taking hormone replacement therapy 接受激素替補療法的女性 / hormone deficiency, hormone imbalance 激素缺乏；荷爾蒙失調 / hormone balance, hormone level 激素平衡；激素水平

<span class="vocabulary">**adrenalin**</span> [əˈdrenəlɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 腎上腺素（情緒激動時腎上腺分泌的一種化學物質，能使心跳加速、精力充沛、行動迅速）：</span>The excitement at the start of a race can really get the adrenalin flowing. 起跑時的激動確實能使人熱血沸騰。/ Seeing the crowd really got my adrenalin pumping. 看見這麼多人確實讓我熱血沸騰。/ He felt a surge of pure adrenalin as he won the race.他贏得比賽時感到一陣激動。/ The adrenalin was coursing through his system. 他感到渾身一陣激動。/ adrenalin rush （腎上腺素分泌引起的）一陣興奮 / We'll start with some dancing to get the adrenalin going. 我們先跳一下舞熱熱身。

<span class="vocabulary">**factor**</span> [ˈfæktə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [醫學] 凝血因子：</span>Haemophiliacs have no factor 8 in their blood. 血友病患者的血液中缺乏凝血因子 VIII。

周邊詞彙補充：
· seed可指男子的精液；
· sperm可指精液；
· milk可指人或其他哺乳動物的乳汁；
· body fluid可指體液；
· secretion可指（動植物液體物質的）分泌。也可指分泌物。
