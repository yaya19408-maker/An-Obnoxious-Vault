☀ <span class="category">**減輕 康復：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**relief**</span> [rɪ'li:f] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] relief (from/of sth)（疼痛、症狀等的）消除或減輕：</span>pain relief 疼痛的緩解 / The drugs only provided temporary relief from the pain. 藥物只是短時間內緩解了疼痛。/ symptomatic relief 症狀的緩解
           
<span class="vocabulary">**alleviate**</span> [əˈli:vieɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. 減輕、緩解：</span>Nowadays, a great deal can be done to alleviate back pain. 如今，減輕背部疼痛可以有許多方法。

<span class="vocabulary">**dull**</span> [dʌl] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（疼痛或感情）減輕；使（疼痛或感情）減輕。不常用：</span>to dull the pain for a while 暫時緩解疼痛

<span class="vocabulary">**kill**</span> [kɪl] 
<span class="definition">vt. 緩解疼痛：</span>to kill the pain 緩解疼痛
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**ease**</span> [i:z]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi.（使）痛苦減輕：</span>The pain immediately eased. 疼痛立刻減輕了。/ The pain in my leg gradually eased away. 我腿部的疼痛逐漸消失了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**subside**</span> [səbˈsaɪd]
<span class="definition">vi.（疼痛）緩解：</span>I took an aspirin and the pain gradually subsided. 我服了一片阿司匹林，疼痛逐漸緩解了。

<span class="vocabulary">**soothe**</span> [su:ð]
<span class="definition">vt. 減輕、緩解（身體某部位的緊張、疼痛或不適）：</span>This should soothe the pain. 這個應該能緩解疼痛。/ Take a warm bath to soothe tense, tired muscles. 洗個熱水澡，讓緊張疲勞的肌肉放鬆一下。/ body lotion to soothe dry skin 滋潤乾燥皮膚的潤膚液
        
<span class="vocabulary">**relieve**</span> [rɪˈli:v]
<span class="definition">vt. 解除或減輕（病痛）：</span>to relieve the symptoms of a cold 減輕感冒的症狀 / Drugs can relieve much of the pain. 藥物能夠大大地緩解病痛。

<span class="vocabulary">**respite**</span> [ˈrespaɪt; 美 ˈrespɪt]
<span class="definition">n. [sing., U] ~ (from sth)（病痛的）短暫緩解：</span>The drug brought a brief respite from the pain. 藥物暫時緩解了疼痛。

<span class="vocabulary">**respond**</span> [rɪs'pɒnd] 
<span class="definition">vi. respond (to sth) 改進以作為某種療法的結果，即見起色：</span>He is responding well to the treatment. 他治療效果還不錯。/ The infection did not respond to the drugs. 這些藥物對感染沒有起作用。/ His condition failed to respond to the treatment. 他的病情並未因治療而好轉。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**responsive**</span> [rɪˈspɒnsɪv; 美 -ˈspɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">adj. [名詞前不常用] ~ (to sb/sth) 對治療起反應的：</span>a flu virus that is not responsive to treatment 治療無效的流感病毒

<span class="vocabulary">**recover**</span> [rɪ'kʌvə] 
<span class="definition">vi. 1 recover (from sth)（從傷病中）康復，即恢復健康，痊癒：</span>to recover from an ankle injury 腳踝傷痊癒 / to recover from his operation 他從手術中恢復過來 / He is recovering well. 他恢復得不錯。<span class="definition">2 recover (from sth)（人從不愉快的事件、經歷或時間段中）恢復：</span>to recover from the death of his mother 從他母親去世的悲痛中恢復過來 <span class="definition">vt. 恢復身心狀態（如恢復意識）：</span>to recover her breath 她呼吸恢復正常 / to recover consciousness 恢復意識 / to recover eyesight 恢復視力 / He seemed upset but quickly recovered himself. 他顯得心煩意亂，但很快就靜下心來。/ After a minute she recovered enough to speak. 她歇了一分鐘，有了力氣開口說話。 
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**recovery**</span> [rɪˈkʌvəri]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C, usually sing.] ~ (from sth)（疾病或受傷後的）恢復、康復：</span>My father has made a full recovery from the operation. 我父親手術後已完全康復了。/ She is on the road to (= making progress towards) recovery. 她正在康復之中。/ He made a remarkable recovery from a shin injury. 他的脛骨傷恢復得相當不錯。/ He had been given less than a one in 500 chance of recovery by his doctors. 他的醫生們認為他康復的機率不足1/500。/ Carole, a compulsive pot smoker and alcoholic in recovery 卡蘿爾，一位正在戒斷康復當中的大麻吸食者和酗酒者 <span class="definition">2 [U]（身體或精神狀態的）恢復：</span>the abrupt loss and recovery of consciousness 意識的突然喪失和恢復

<span class="vocabulary">**heal**</span> [hi:l]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (up)（使）康復：</span>The wound hasn't healed properly yet. 傷口還沒有痊癒。/ The wound healed up very nicely. 傷口癒合得很好。/ It took a long time for the wounds to heal. 傷口過了很長時間才癒合。/ The cut healed up without leaving a scar. 傷口癒合沒留下疤痕。/ This will help to heal your cuts and scratches. 這個會有助於治好割傷和擦傷。/ Within six weeks the bruising had gone, but it was six months before it all healed. 青瘀在六週內就消退了，但傷卻是六個月後才全好了。/ If they'd operated and pinned her arm at once, she might have healed by now. 如果他們當時馬上就給她的胳膊做手術並用鋼針固定，她現在可能已經痊癒了。/ It was a chance to heal the wounds in the party (= to repair the damage that had been done).（比喻用法）那是彌合黨內創傷的機會。<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb (of sth) [舊時或正式用法] 治癒病人：</span>the story of Jesus healing ten lepers of their disease 耶穌治癒十個麻風病人的故事
           
<span class="vocabulary">**recuperate**</span> [rɪˈku:pəreɪt]
<span class="definition">vi. [正式] ~ (from sth)（疾病、勞累、受傷等之後）恢復健康或精力：</span>He's still recuperating from his operation. 他動了手術，還在恢復。/ He is recuperating from a serious back injury. 他背部受了重傷，目前正在康復中。/ I went away to the country to recuperate. 我去了鄉下休養。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**rally**</span> [ˈræli]
<span class="definition">vi. 在一段時間的疾病、虛弱等狀態之後變得更健康、更強壯等：</span>He never really rallied after the operation. 手術後，他根本沒有真正康復。/ He rallied enough to thank his doctors. 他身體好得差不多了，於是去感謝他的醫生。          

<span class="vocabulary">**convalesce**</span> [ˌkɒnvəˈles; 美 ˌkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">vi.（疾病後）逐漸康復：</span>She is convalescing at home after her operation. 手術後她正在家休養康復。/ After two weeks, I was allowed home, where I convalesced for three months. 兩週之後，我獲准回家，休養了三個月之後逐漸康復。/ those convalescing from illness or surgery 那些從疾病和手術中康復的人
     
<span class="vocabulary">**mend**</span> [mend] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. [英，過時] 指人病情或傷勢好轉，或使人的病情或傷勢好轉，相當於recover：</span>The cut is mending. 傷口正在癒合。<span class="definition">vi. 指骨折癒合或痊癒：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**rehabilitate**</span> [ˌri:əˈbɪlɪteɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. 使重病患者康復；使某人戒毒或戒酒：</span>Considerable efforts have been made to rehabilitate patients who have suffered in this way. 為幫助遭受這種病痛折磨的病人康復付出了巨大努力。/ a unit for rehabilitating drug addicts 幫助吸毒者恢復正常生活的機構
           
<span class="vocabulary">**rehabilitation**</span> [ˌri:əˌbɪlɪˈteɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 康復：</span>medical, physical rehabilitation 醫療康復；身體復原 / alcohol, drug rehabilitation 戒酒康復；戒毒康復 / a specialist drug and alcohol rehabilitation unit 戒毒戒酒專業康復室 / cardiac, pulmonary, stroke rehabilitation（均為醫學用語）心臟的康復；肺部的康復；中風的康復 / rehabilitation centre/center, rehabilitation clinic, rehabilitation unit 康復中心；康復診所；康復室 / a drug rehabilitation centre/center 戒毒康復中心 / rehabilitation services 康復服務機構 / rehabilitation process, rehabilitation programme/program, rehabilitation scheme（英）康復過程；康復計劃；康復方案 / He entered an alcohol rehabilitation programme/program. 他參與了一項戒酒康復計劃。

<span class="vocabulary">**return**</span> [rɪ'tə:n] 
<span class="definition">vi.（身體狀態）重現，恢復，相當於reappear或resurface。需要注意的是，該詞指的是“恢復”到原來的狀態，如果原來的狀態是疾病或疼痛，那麼恢復的結果也是疾病或疼痛。所以該詞並非純粹表示“疾病痊癒”：</span>My strength was slowly returning to me. 我的體力慢慢恢復了。/ Her symptoms returned periodically. 她的症狀時不時地出現。/ His headache returned instantly. 他很快頭痛復發了。/ the tears that threatened to return 眼看又要流出來的眼淚 <span class="definition">n. [C] 單數名詞，return (of sth) 身體狀態的恢復，重現，相當於reappearance：</span>a full return of health 完全恢復健康
   
<span class="vocabulary">**restore**</span> [rɪˈstɔ:(r)]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb/sth to sth 把身體狀況恢復至先前的條件：</span>He is now fully restored to health. 他現在完全恢復了健康。/ We will restore her to health but it may take time. 我們會讓她恢復健康，但可能需要一些時間。/ The operation restored his sight (= made him able to see again). 手術使他恢復了視力。

周邊詞彙補充：
· layoff可指（勞累或受傷的人）休養、療養。
