☀ <span class="category">**運動 神經：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**motor**</span> ['məʊtə] 
<span class="definition">adj. [術語] 只作前置定語，與身體的肌肉運動有關的；與（控制身體的）運動神經有關的：</span>motor activity 肌肉運動 / motor and sensory functions 運動功能和感覺功能

<span class="vocabulary">**nervous**</span> ['nə:vəs] 
<span class="definition">adj. 與神經系統有關的（且常影響人的心理或情緒的）：</span>nervous condition, nervous disease, nervous disorder 神經系統疾病；神經紊亂 / She was in a state of nervous exhaustion. 她的神經處於極度疲勞的狀態。

<span class="vocabulary">**message**</span> ['mesɪdӡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 大腦傳輸給身體某個部位（或某個身體部位傳輸給大腦）的資訊：</span>The message arrives in your brain in a fraction of a second. 資訊瞬間便可傳輸到大腦。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**nerve**</span> [nɜ:v; 美 nɜ:rv]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 神經：</span>pinched（美）, trapped（英） nerve 受壓迫的神經 / sensitive nerve 敏感的神經 / damaged nerve 受損的神經 / facial, optic, sciatic, spinal nerve 面神經；視神經；坐骨神經；脊神經 / peripheral nerve 外周神經 / sensory nerve 感覺神經 / The nerve runs from the eye to the brain. 這根神經連線眼睛和大腦。/ The nerves transmit pain. 這些神經傳導痛覺。/ nerve bundle, nerve cell, nerve ending, nerve fibre, nerve sheath, nerve tissue 神經束；神經細胞；神經末梢；神經纖維；神經鞘；神經組織 / nerve pathway 神經通路 / nerve activity, nerve function 神經活動；神經機能 / nerve impulse, nerve signal 神經衝動；神經訊號 / nerve stimulation 神經刺激 / nerve damage, nerve injury 神經損傷；神經受損 / nerve disease, nerve dysfunction, nerve paralysis 神經疾病；神經功能障礙；神經麻痺 / nerve pain 神經疼痛 / nerve gas 神經毒氣 / The message travels along the nerve to the brain. 訊號沿著神經傳輸至大腦。/ He's been off work with a trapped nerve in his back. 他因背部神經受到壓迫而不再工作。/ Cutting the nerves to the stomach does not affect hunger. 切斷通向胃部的神經不會影響飢餓感。/ Intense pain shot through every nerve in his body. 一陣劇痛襲向他全身的每一根神經。/ Every nerve in her body was tense. 她的每一根神經都繃得緊緊的。/ My remarks about divorce had unwittingly touched a raw nerve.（比喻用法）我關於離婚的言論無意間觸到了別人的痛處。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**impulse**</span> [ˈɪmpʌls]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [術語] 神經脈衝：</span>nerve, neural impulse 神經衝動 / A neuron generates an electrical impulse. 神經元產生電脈衝。/ Nerve impulses are transmitted to the brain. 神經脈衝被傳送至大腦。
