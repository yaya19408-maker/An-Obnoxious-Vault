☀ <span class="category">**清洗 包紮：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**clean**</span> [kli:n] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 清潔傷口：</span>to cleane the wound 清洗傷口

<span class="vocabulary">**bathe**</span> [beɪð] 
<span class="definition">vt. 用水清洗某事物（尤指身體部位）：</span>to bathe the wound 清洗傷口
           
<span class="vocabulary">**syringe**</span> [sɪˈrɪndʒ]
<span class="definition">vt. 用注射器清洗耳朵：</span>I had my ears syringed. 我的耳朵已用注射器清洗乾淨。

<span class="vocabulary">**tape**</span> [teɪp] 
<span class="definition">vt. [美] tape sth (up) 用繃帶包紮傷口：</span>to get the cut taped up 用繃帶把傷口包紮起來

<span class="vocabulary">**dress**</span> [dres] 
<span class="definition">vt. 清潔、治療和包紮傷口：</span>to dress the cut 包紮傷口

<span class="vocabulary">**bandage**</span> ['bændɪdӡ] 
<span class="definition">vt. bandage sth (up) 用繃帶包紮（身體部位）：</span>Apply a dressing to the wound and bandage it. 在傷口上敷上敷料並用繃帶包紮起來。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**strap**</span> [stræp]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ sth (up) 給傷口包紮、打繃帶。相當於bandage：</span>I have to keep my leg strapped up for six weeks. 我的腿必須打六星期繃帶。
