☀ <span class="category">**虛弱 羸弱：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**feeble**</span> [ˈfi:bl]
<span class="definition">adj. 身體非常虛弱的：</span>a feeble old man 衰弱的老人 / The heartbeat was feeble and irregular. 心搏無力，心律不齊。/ He told them he was old and feeble and was not able to walk so far. 他告訴他們，他年老體弱，不能走那麼遠。

<span class="vocabulary">**fragile**</span> ['frædӡaɪl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 身體虛弱的、容易生病的。強調身體的脆弱性：</span>Her father is now 86 and in fragile health. 她的父親現在86歲，身體虛弱。/ I’m feeling a bit fragile after the heavy work. 繁重的工作之後，我覺得身子有點發虛。

<span class="vocabulary">**weak**</span> [wi:k] 
<span class="definition">adj. 身體虛弱不強壯的。是此義的基礎用詞和常規用詞，最為常用：</span>She is still weak after her illness. 她病後仍然虛弱。/ He was weak with hunger. 他因飢餓而身體虛弱。/ His legs felt weak. 他覺得兩腿發軟。/ She suffered from a weak heart. 她心臟不好。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**emaciated**</span> [ɪˈmeɪʃieɪtɪd; ɪˈmeɪs-]
<span class="definition">adj.（常指因疾病或飢餓而）消瘦的、虛弱的、憔悴的：</span>horrific television pictures of emaciated prisoners 電視上骨瘦如柴的囚犯們的恐怖畫面 / his severely emaciated body 他那瘦骨鱗峋的身軀
           
<span class="vocabulary">**susceptible**</span> [səˈseptəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. 易受傷害的、易患病的、體質過於敏感的：</span>Some of these plants are more susceptible to frost damage than others. 這些植物中有一些較其他的易受霜凍危害。/ Walking with weights makes the shoulders very susceptible to injury. 負重行走時肩膀很容易受傷。/ Salt intake may lead to raised blood pressure in susceptible adults. 鹽的攝入可能導致易病的成年人血壓升高。/ Diesel exhaust is particularly aggravating to certain highly susceptible individuals. 某些體質高度敏感的人尤其反感柴油廢氣。/ The operation had left her susceptible to infection. 手術使她特別容易受感染。/ Some dogs are genetically susceptible to the disease. 有些狗先天易得這種病。

<span class="vocabulary">**vulnerable**</span> [ˈvʌlnərəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (to sb/sth) 身體上脆弱的、易受傷害的、易患病的：</span>She looked very vulnerable standing there on her own. 她獨自站在那裡，看上去弱不禁風。/ In cases of food poisoning, young children are especially vulnerable. 遇到食物中毒，幼兒尤其容易受危害。/ People with high blood pressure are especially vulnerable to diabetes. 有高血壓的人尤其易患糖尿病。/ The virus attacks the immune system, leaving your body vulnerable to infections. 這種病毒攻擊人的免疫系統，使身體容易受到各種感染。/ Plants that are growing vigorously are less likely to be vulnerable to disease. 生命力強的植物不容易得病。/ Hippos are uniquely vulnerable to drought. 河馬特別受不了乾旱。/ Old people are particularly vulnerable members of our society. 老年人是社會中尤為明顯的弱勢群體。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vulnerability**</span> [ˌvʌlnərəˈbɪləti]
<span class="definition">n. [U] ~ (of sb/sth) (to sth) 身體上的脆弱性：</span>genetic vulnerability 基因中的弱點 / evidence of a genetic vulnerability to cancer 表明基因有易患癌症性質的證據 / physical vulnerability 身體上的脆弱性 / the vulnerability of newborn babies to disease 新生嬰兒容易患病 

<span class="vocabulary">**delicate**</span> ['delɪkɪt] 
<span class="definition">adj.（人）不強壯且容易生病的，即虛弱的。也強調身體的脆弱性，不太如fragile常用：</span>delicate child, delicate constitution 纖弱的孩子；纖弱的體質
           
<span class="vocabulary">**frail**</span> [freɪl]
<span class="definition">adj.（尤指老人）身體瘦弱的：</span>Mother was becoming too frail to live alone. 母親已逐漸衰弱到無法獨居。/ She lay in bed looking particularly frail. 她躺在床上，看上去特別虛弱。/ frail hand 瘦弱的手 / frail health 虛弱的健康狀況 / frail physique 瘦弱的體格
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**frailty**</span> [ˈfreɪlti]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 體質的虛弱：</span>She died after a long period of increasing frailty. 在很長一段時間裡，她身體日漸虛弱，最後離開人世。/ Despite increasing physical frailty, he continued to write stories. 儘管身體越來越虛弱，他仍然繼續寫小說。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**infirm**</span> [ɪnˈfɜ:m; 美 ɪnˈfɜ:rm]
<span class="definition">adj. 有病且體弱的，尤指長期病弱的或年老體弱的：</span>her aging, infirm husband 她年邁體弱的丈夫 / mentally, physically infirm 心理脆弱；身體虛弱

<span class="vocabulary">**infirmity**</span> [ɪnˈfɜ:məti; 美 -ˈfɜ:rm-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 長期的體弱，長期的生病：</span>We all fear disability or infirmity. 我們都害怕傷殘或體弱。/ the infirmities of old age 老年體弱 / mental, physical infirmity 心理脆弱；體弱多病 / age and infirmity（文學用法，尤英）年邁體弱 /those incapable of supporting themselves by reason of age and infirmity 因為年老體弱不能養活自己的那些人

<span class="vocabulary">**distressed**</span> [dɪˈstrest]
<span class="definition">adj. 身體遭遇痛苦的；身體條件差的：</span>When the baby was born, it was blue and distressed. 這嬰兒出生時全身發青，非常虛弱。

<span class="vocabulary">**trick**</span> [trɪk] 
<span class="definition">adj. [美] 只用於名詞前，（身體部位）虛弱有病的：</span>a trick knee 膝軟
           
<span class="vocabulary">**buckle**</span> [ˈbʌkl]
<span class="definition">vi.（雙腿或膝蓋因虛弱、勞累）發軟：</span>Mcanally's knees buckled and he crumpled down onto the floor. 麥卡納利雙膝發軟，癱倒在地板上。/ His right leg buckled under him. 他的右腿發軟打戰。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**unfit**</span> [ʌn'fɪt] 
<span class="definition">adj. [尤英]（一個人）身體健康狀況不佳，（因未進行體育鍛煉而）不健康的：</span>The captain is still unfit and will miss tonight’s game. 隊長身體欠佳，將不會出席今晚的比賽。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**weaken**</span> [ˈwi:kən]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi.（使）身體變虛弱：</span>She felt her legs weaken. 她覺得兩腿無力。/ Malnutrition obviously weakens the patient. 營養不良明顯導致病人身體虛弱。

<span class="vocabulary">**sap**</span> [sæp]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth / ~ sb (of sth) 使某人更虛弱：</span>The hot sun sapped our energy. 火辣辣的太陽烤得我們虛軟無力。/ I was afraid the sickness had sapped my strength. 我擔心患病已讓我元氣大傷。           

<span class="vocabulary">**go**</span> [ɡəʊ] 
<span class="definition">vi. 身體機能不能正常發揮作用：</span>Her sight is beginning to go. 她的視力開始下降。

<span class="vocabulary">**fail**</span> [feɪl] 
<span class="definition">vi. 1（身體部位）停止運轉，即出故障、失靈：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">2（健康或視力）變弱：</span>Her eyesight is failing. 她的視力日漸衰退。/ failing health 健康惡化

<span class="vocabulary">**wastage**</span> [ˈweɪstɪdʒ]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 疾病（或食物不足）導致的身體部位的衰弱過程（即變得又瘦又小的過程）：</span>muscle wastage（英）肌肉萎縮 / Patients need exercise to prevent muscle wastage. 病人需要鍛鍊以防止肌肉萎縮。/ This can lead to bodily weakness and muscle wastage. 這樣可能會導致身體虛弱和肌肉萎縮。/ the terrible wastage of his lungs 他的肺功能嚴重萎縮

相關詞義延伸：
· feeble還可指讓人感覺內心無力的。

周邊詞彙補充：
· linger可指仍然活著但是變得更弱，如苟延殘喘、奄奄一息。
