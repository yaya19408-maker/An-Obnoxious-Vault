☀ <span class="category">**脈搏 脈率：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**pulse**</span> [pʌls] 
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] 脈搏；或脈搏一分鐘的次數，即脈率：</span>Fear sent her pulse racing. 她嚇得脈搏急速跳動。/ fast, racing, rapid pulse 快速跳動的脈搏 / slow pulse 緩慢跳動的脈搏 / steady pulse 穩定的脈搏 / strong pulse 強脈搏 / weak pulse 弱脈搏 / pulse rate 脈搏率 <span class="definition">vi. 有節奏地跳動，搏動：</span>My temples is pulsing a little. 我的太陽穴跳動得有點利害。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**throb**</span> [θrɒb; 美 θrɑ:b]
<span class="definition">vi. 強烈而有規律地發出聲音（如搏動）：</span>The blood was throbbing in my veins. 血液在我的靜脈中有節律地湧動著。/ The vein at his temple throbbed angrily. 他太陽穴上的血管憤怒地跳動著。
