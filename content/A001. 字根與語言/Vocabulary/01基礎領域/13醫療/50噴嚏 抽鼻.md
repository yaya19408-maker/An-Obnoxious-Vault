☀ <span class="category">**噴嚏 抽鼻：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**sneeze**</span> [sni:z] 
<span class="definition">vi. 打噴嚏：</span>I’ve been sneezing all morning. 我上午一直打噴嚏。/ I sneezed on him. 我朝他打了個噴嚏。/ She suddenly felt the urge to sneeze. 她突然想打噴嚏。<span class="definition">n. [C] 噴嚏，或噴嚏聲：</span>to give a violent sneeze 打個大噴嚏 / Sneezes can spread infections. 打噴嚏能傳播傳染病。

<span class="vocabulary">**sniff**</span> [snɪf] 
<span class="definition">vi.（尤指哭泣、感冒等時）發出聲音地把空氣從鼻子吸入，即抽鼻子：</span>I couldn’t stop sneezing. 我一個勁兒地打噴嚏。/ She wiped her eyes and sniffed loudly. 她抹了抹眼睛，哧哧地抽著鼻子。<span class="definition">n. [C] 抽鼻子；抽鼻子聲：</span>His sobs soon turned to sniffs. 不多時，他的嗚咽變成了啜泣。
