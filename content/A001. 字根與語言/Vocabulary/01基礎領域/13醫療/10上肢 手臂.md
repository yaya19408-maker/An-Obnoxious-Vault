<span class="vocabulary">**limb**</span> [lɪm]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（人或動物的）一隻胳膊或腿、翅膀：</span>Two arms reached around her flailing limbs. 兩隻手臂抱住了她胡亂飛舞的四肢。/ I eased my stiff limbs into the hot bath. 我小心翼翼地把僵直的腿放進熱洗澡水裡。/ The missing limb did not lessen the quality of the cat's life. 少了一條腿並沒有影響那隻貓的生活質量。/ amputated, severed limb 短肢 / artificial, prosthetic limb 假肢；義肢 / lower, upper limb 下肢；上肢 / The animal is able to stand up on its hind limbs. 這隻動物能夠用後腿站立。/ people who have lost limbs in battle 在戰鬥中失去肢體的人們 / I stretched my limbs lazily as I sat up. 我坐起來的時候懶懶地伸展了一下四肢。/ limb development, limb growth 肢體的發育 / limb amputation 截肢 / For a while, she lost the use of her limbs. 好一會兒她四肢都動彈不得。/ She would be able to stretch out her cramped limbs and rest for a few hours. 她將能伸展一下蜷縮的四肢，休息幾個小時。<span class="definition">adj. -limbed用於構成形容詞，指有…肢（或翼、翅膀）的：</span>long-limbed 四肢細長 / loose-limbed 四肢柔軟靈活

<span class="vocabulary">**arm**</span> [ɑːm] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 臂，胳膊：</span>powerful arm 有力的胳膊 / arm muscle 臂部肌肉 / The child lay in its mother’s arms. 孩子躺在母親的懷裡。/ She carried the dog under one arm. 她把小狗夾在腋下。
                   
<span class="vocabulary">**elbow**</span> [ˈelbəʊ; 美 -boʊ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 肘、肘部：</span>He's fractured his elbow. 他肘部骨折。/ He slipped and fell, badly bruising an elbow. 他滑倒了，一隻胳膊肘嚴重擦傷。/ left elbow 左肘關節 / sharp elbow 尖突的肘關節 / dislocated, fractured elbow 脫位的肘關節；骨折的肘部 / sore elbow 疼痛的肘關節 / He rested one elbow on the wall as he spoke. 他講話時把一隻胳膊肘支在牆上。/ She opened her eyes and propped herself up on one elbow to look at him. 她睜開眼，單肘撐起身子看著他。/ He caught her elbow to steady her. 他抓住她的肘部穩住她。/ She jabbed him with her elbow. 她用胳膊肘捅他。/ She thrust her elbow into her attacker's face. 她用肘部猛擊襲擊者的臉。 / I banged my elbow on the table as I got up. 我站起來時胳膊肘碰上了桌子。/ Extend your arms without locking your elbows. 伸展你的胳膊，放鬆肘部 / elbow joint, elbow ligament 肘關節；肘部韌帶 / elbow injury 肘傷 / elbow pain, elbow problem 肘部疼痛；肘傷 / elbow surgery肘部手術 / elbow pad 護肘 / The office cubicles give you hardly any elbow room. 在辦公室隔間裡幾乎伸不開胳膊。/ A voice at my elbow said, 'Please sit down!' 離我很近的一個聲音說："請坐!" / The whole of his arm below the elbow was badly burned. 他肘部以下嚴重燒傷。/ She slid a hand under his elbow to guide him. 她悄悄地把手挽到他的胳膊肘下領著他。/ He took his guest by the elbow and steered him in the direction of the bar. 他挽著客人的胳膊領他朝酒吧的方向走去。/ He raised himself on one elbow and looked at the bedside clock. 他用一隻胳膊肘撐起身子看了看床邊的鐘。/ He was up to his elbows in hot water. 他的雙手浸在深及胳膊肘的熱水中。/ She was cradling a small bundle in the crook of her elbow. 她用手臂挎著一個小包。/ She dug her elbow into Jim's ribs to remind him not to give the secret away. 她用肘部截了一下吉姆的肋骨，提醒他不要洩露秘密。/ He had been rubbing elbows with celebrities.（比喻用法，美）他一直與一些名流過從甚密。

<span class="vocabulary">**palm**</span> [pɑ:m]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（手腕和手指之間的）手掌、掌心：</span>He held the bird gently in the palm of his hand. 他把小鳥輕輕地託在掌心。/ sweaty palm 汗津津的手掌 / soft palm 柔軟的半掌 / warm palm 溫暖的手掌 / flat, open palm 張開的手掌；攤開的手心 / upturned palm 朝上的手掌 / outstretched palm 張開的手掌 / He held up a palm for silence. 他舉起手掌示意安靜。/ He spread his palms in a gesture of openness. 他展開手掌，以示坦誠。/ He cupped his empty palms together. 他捧起空空的雙手。/ He went over to the wall and placed his palm on it. 他走到牆邊，用手掌撐牆。/ I pressed my palm to the wound to stop the bleeding. 我將手掌壓在傷口上止血。/ She slapped her palm against the desk in anger. 她生氣地用手拍擊桌子。/ Dornberg slapped the table with the palm of his hand. 多恩伯格用手拍著桌子。/ He rubbed his palms against his jeans. 他在牛仔褲上擦了擦手掌。/ He ran his palm up and down Holly's shoulder. 他把手放在霍利的肩膀上來回摸。/ She read people's palms and told fortunes. 她給人看手相算命。/ Hold out your arms with your palms facing down. 伸出雙臂，手心向下。/ His right palm was resting against my neck. 他把右手放在我的脖子上。/ palm reader, palm reading 看手相者；看手相 / palm print 手掌印 / The metal felt hot against my palms. 我感覺掌心的金屬很熱。/ He took her hand between his palms and squeezed it. 他雙手緊緊握住她的手捏了一捏。/ He showed me the coins in his palm. 他給我看他手心裡的硬幣。

"這些我們該如何處理?"他朝手提箱一揮拇指問道。/ He smiled and raised a thumb in greeting. 他微微一笑，豎起拇指表示問候。/ I stuck out a thumb and a car stopped immediately. 我伸出大拇指，馬上有一輛車停了下來。/ I sat there twiddling my thumbs until the manager finally appeared.（比喻用法）我坐在那兒百無聊賴，直到經理終於出現。/ thumb nail（通常用thumbnail）/ 拇指指甲 / I picked up the beetle carefully between finger and thumb. 我用食指和拇指小心地捏起甲蟲。/ He made a thumbs-up sign through the window to tell us everything was fine.他隔著窗子豎起大拇指，告訴我們一切順利。
