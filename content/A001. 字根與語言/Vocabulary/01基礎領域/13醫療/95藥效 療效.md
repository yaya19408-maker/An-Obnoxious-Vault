☀ <span class="category">**藥效 療效：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**powerful**</span> ['paʊəfl] 
<span class="definition">adj.（藥物等）對身體有強烈作用的：</span>powerful drug 有奇效的藥物
           
<span class="vocabulary">**potent**</span> [ˈpəʊtnt; 美 ˈpoʊ-]
<span class="definition">adj. 對人的身體或頭腦有很強影響力的：</span>a potent drug 猛藥 / The drug is extremely potent, but causes unpleasant side effects. 這種藥藥效極強，但會產生不良的副作用。/ a very potent alcoholic brew 烈性酒精飲料 / The vodka must have been pretty potent stuff. 這種伏特加酒勁兒肯定相當大。

<span class="vocabulary">**strong**</span> [strɒŋ] 
<span class="definition">adj.（對身體或精神）有很強影響力的。此時與powerful並無本質區別：</span>a strong drug 強效藥物 
  
<span class="vocabulary">**effective**</span> [ɪˈfektɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. 有效的，能產生預期效果的，能成功的：</span>Simple antibiotics are effective against this organism. 一般的抗生素能夠有效對抗這種微生物。/ Aspirin is a simple but highly effective treatment. 阿司匹林藥片治療方法簡便，效果卻非常顯著。/ drugs that are effective against cancer 治療癌症的有效藥物 / The drug is generally effective in reducing pain. 這種藥通常對疼痛有緩解作用。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**effectiveness**</span> [ɪˌfek'tɪvnɪs; 美əˈfɛktɪvnɪs]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 有效性：</span>The drug's long-term effectiveness has yet to be established. 這種藥物的長期效果還有待驗證。/ clinical, therapeutic, treatment effectiveness 臨床效果；治療效果 / You may need to increase the dose to achieve maximum effectiveness. 你可能需要加大劑量才能取得最大療效。/ The drugs work well at first but gradually lose their effectiveness. 這些藥的效果起初非常好，但後來逐漸失效。/ They're doing tests to evaluate the effectiveness of this herb as an antiseptic. 他們正透過試驗來評估這種草藥作為殺菌劑的有效性。/ the effectiveness of penicillin in controlling bacterial infection 青黴素在控制細菌感染方面的效用

<span class="vocabulary">**effect**</span> [ɪ'fekt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] effect (on sb/sth) 藥效，療效：</span>the beneficial effect of exercise 鍛鍊的好處 / calming, soporific effect 鎮靜效果；催眠效果 / curative, therapeutic effect 治療效果 / placebo effect 安慰劑效果 / the effects of alcohol 酒精的作用 / The drug exerts a powerful effect on the brain. 這種藥對大腦影響很大。/ The medicine started to take effect after a few minutes. 幾分鐘後藥物開始見效。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**side effect**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [usually pl.] 藥物的副作用：</span>common side effect 常見的副作用 / physical side effect 對身體的副作用 / drug side effect 藥物的副作用 / This drug has no known side effects.這種藥尚未發現有任何副作用。/ The treatment has some unfortunate side effects. 這一療法有一些不好的副作用。/ The patient suffered severe side effects from the drug. 這種藥副作用很大，病人很是受苦。/ The medication can have adverse side effects on the patient. 這種藥物可能對病人有不良副作用。/ positive side effect 正面的副作用 / The drug has the beneficial side effect of lowering the patient's blood pressure. 這種藥有降低病人血壓的良性副作用。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**potency**</span> [ˈpəʊtnsi; 美 ˈpoʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（藥物、毒藥或其它化學物質等的）效力、藥力、藥效：</span>If you keep a medicine too long, it may lose its potency. 藥物存放太久，可能會失去效力。/ Sunscreen can lose its potency if left over winter in the bathroom cabinet. 如果把防曬霜在盥洗室的壁櫥裡放一個冬天，就有可能失效。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**tolerate**</span> ['tɒləreɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 能夠耐受一種藥物，（對藥物）有耐受性：</span>She tolerated the chemotherapy well. 她對這次化療的耐受力很強。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**tolerance**</span> [ˈtɒlərəns; 美 ˈtɑ:l-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] ~ (to sth) 某人或某事物對某種物質的耐受性：</span>Tolerance to alcohol decreases with age. 對酒精的耐受力隨著年齡遞減。/ As with any drug taken in excess, your body can build up a tolerance to it. 和過量服用任何其他藥物一樣，身體會對它產生耐藥性。
