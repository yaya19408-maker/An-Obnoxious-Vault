☀ <span class="category">**出血 流膿：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**bleed**</span> [bli:d] 
<span class="definition">vi.（尤指因受傷或患病而）出血，流血：</span>Your head is bleeding. 你的頭流血了。/ He slowly bled to death. 他慢慢地失血而死。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**bleeding**</span> [ˈbli:dɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 流血；失血：</span>uncontrollable bleeding 流血不止 / Press firmly on the wound to stop the bleeding. 用力壓住傷口止血。/ Some drugs can cause bleeding from the small intestine. 有些藥物會引起小腸出血。

<span class="vocabulary">**haemorrhage**</span> [ˈhemərɪdʒ]
[英] [美用hemorrhage]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 體內大出血：</span>a massive brain/cerebral haemorrhage 重度腦溢血 / The patient died from acute cerebral haemorrhage. 患者死於急性腦溢血。/ Twelve hours later she suffered a massive brain haemorrhage. 十二小時後她腦部大量出血。/ He was checked for any signs of haemorrhage. 他接受檢查，以確定是否有出血跡象。/ These drugs will not be used if hemorrhage is the cause of the stroke. 藥品禁忌症：出血性中風忌用。<span class="definition">vi.（尤指體內）大出血：</span>I haemorrhaged badly after the birth of all three of my sons. 生我三個兒子時，我每次都出現產後大出血。/ If this is left untreated, one can actually haemorrhage to death. 如果這種情況得不到治療的話，人就會因失血過多而死亡。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fester**</span> [ˈfestə(r)]
<span class="definition">vi.（傷口或破口）變得嚴重感染，如化膿、潰爛：</span>festering sores 膿瘡 / Many of the children are afflicted by festering sores. 很多孩子深受潰爛創痛的折磨。/ festering wounds 化膿的傷口 / The wound is festering, and gangrene has set in. 傷口在潰爛，已經生了壞疽。

<span class="vocabulary">**weep**</span> [wi:p] 
<span class="definition">vi.（傷口）產生液體，即流出或滲出：</span>His legs were covered with weeping sores. 他的雙腿有多處紅腫流膿的傷口。

周邊詞彙補充：
· shed可指（尤指在戰爭中）造成傷亡；使流血。
