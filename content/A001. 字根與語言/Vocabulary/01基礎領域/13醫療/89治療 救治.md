☀ <span class="category">**治療 救治：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**treat**</span> [tri:t] 
<span class="definition">vt. treat sb (for sth) (with sth)（對疾病或損傷進行）治療：</span>Doctors treated him with aspirin. 醫生用阿斯匹林給他治療。/ He was treated for a minor head wound. 他因頭部輕傷而接受治療。/ The drug is effective at treating depression. 該藥對抑鬱症有效。

<span class="vocabulary">**treatment**</span> ['tri:tmənt] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] treatment (for sth)（對疾病或創傷的）治療，療法；或使某人看起來（或讓其自己感覺）更好的做法。是此義的常規用詞，極為常用：</span>long-term treatment 長期治療 / inpatient treatment 住院治療 / clinical treatment 臨床治療 / radiation treatment 放射療法 / cancer treatment 癌症治療 / dietary treatment 食療 / beauty（尤美）, hair, spa treatment 美容；美髮；水療 / a course of treatment 一個療程 / treatment process 治療過程 / treatment plan 治療計劃 / He responded well to treatment. 他的治療效果不錯。

<span class="vocabulary">**cure**</span> [kjʊə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 治癒（疾病或損傷）：</span>to cure his injury 治癒他的傷 / This disease cannot be cured. 這種病治不好。<span class="definition">2 治癒（患病或受傷的人或動物）：</span>Will you be able to cure him, Doctor? 醫生，你能把他治好嗎？/ He was now completely cured of his illness. 他現在徹底痊癒了。<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] cure (for sth)（治癒傷病的）藥物或療法。表“療法”時，treatment一般指具體的療法種類或名稱，而cure常表示療法的有效程度：</span>instant, miracle, wonder cure 快速療法；神奇療法；特效療法 / rest cure 靜養療法 / There is no known cure for the disease. 這種疾病目前還無法醫治。/ If the disease is detected early, it has a cure. 如果早點發現，這病是可以醫治的。<span class="definition">2 [C] 治療，或治癒的過程：</span>No one can effect a cure if the disease has spread too far. 如果這種疾病已嚴重擴散，那麼沒有人能夠醫治。/ These drugs can sometimes effect miraculous cures. 這些藥有時有神奇的療效。/ miraculous cure 奇蹟般的痊癒 / complete, permanent cure 完全治癒；根治 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**remedy**</span> [ˈremədi]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (for/to sth)（針對疾病或疼痛的）療法、治療：</span>traditional remedy 傳統療法 / folk, home remedy 民間療法；家庭療法 / an excellent home remedy for sore throats 治療咽喉疼痛的極佳的家庭療法 / alternative, homoeopathic, natural remedy 非傳統療法；順勢療法；天然療法 / natural remedies to help overcome winter infections 有助於治療冬季傳染病的自然療法 / They're hoping to find a remedy for the condition. 他們希望找到一種醫治這種疾病的方法。/ She tried various remedies, but none of them worked. 她嘗試了各種療法，卻沒有一種見效。
                       
<span class="vocabulary">**physiotherapy**</span> [ˌfɪziəʊˈθerəpi; 美 ˌfɪzioʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 物理療法，理療：</span>He'll need intensive physiotherapy. 他需要高強度理療。/ I'm undergoing regular physiotherapy for a back problem.我因為背傷正在接受常規理療。/ physiotherapy clinic, physiotherapy department 理療診所；理療科 / physiotherapy treatment 物理治療 / physiotherapy equipment 理療裝置
           
<span class="vocabulary">**therapy**</span> [ˈθerəpi]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C]（針對某人特定病情的）治療方案、治療方法：</span>conventional drug therapy 常規藥物療法 / Most leukaemia patients undergo some sort of drug therapy (= treatment using drugs). 絕大多數白血病患者都會接受某種藥物治療。/ alternative, complementary, natural therapies (= treatments that do not use traditional drugs) 替代療法；補充療法；自然療法 / hormonal therapies 激素療法 <span class="definition">2 [U] 心理治療。相當於psychotherapy：</span>a therapy group 心理治療小組 / She's in therapy. 她在接受心理治療。/ In therapy, she began to let go of her obsession with Mike. 在接受治療的過程中，她開始逐漸從對邁克的迷戀中解脫出來。/ He is having therapy to conquer his phobia. 他在接受治療以克服自己的恐懼症。

<span class="vocabulary">**regime**</span> [reɪˈʒi:m]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一些人為保持健康或美麗而遵循的一套飲食、鍛鍊或健美規則，如養生法，相當於regimen：</span>exercise, fitness, health, training 鍛鍊規則；健身規則；訓練規則 / dietary regime 飲食規則 / drug, treatment regime 藥物療法；治療方法 / A strict regime of exercise was imposed on the children. 在孩子當中強制推行了一項嚴格的鍛鍊規則。/ He suggested to me that I follow his fitness regime. 他建議我學習他的健身方法。/ He has a new fitness regime to strengthen his back. 他有一個增強背部力量的新健身計劃。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**regimen**</span> [ˈredʒɪmən]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [醫學，或正式] 為保持（或增進）健康而遵循的一系列飲食和鍛鍊規則（或遵循的某種療法），即養生之道、養生法：</span>Whatever regimen has been prescribed should be rigorously followed. 不管制訂的是什麼樣的養生計劃，都要嚴格遵守。/ strict regimen 嚴格的飲食起居 / a sensible daily regimen of vitamin supplements 補充維生素的日常合理養生 / dietary regimen 飲食養生 / exercise, fitness, training, workout regimen 鍛鍊養身之道；健身之道；訓練計劃 / drug regimen 藥物療法 / treatment regimen 治療方法 / antibiotic, chemotherapy regimen 抗生素療法；化學療法 / dosage, dose regimen 劑量使用 / We used a combined regimen of chemotherapy and radiation therapy. 我們採用了化療和放射治療相結合的方案。/ She follows a strict fitness regimen. 她執行嚴格的健身計劃。/ a treatment regimen comprising two antibiotics 採用兩種抗生素的治療方案

<span class="vocabulary">**analyse**</span> ['ænəlaɪz]（英）（美analyze）
<span class="definition">vt. 對…進行精神（或心理）分析（或治療），相當於psychoanalyse：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**analysis**</span> [ə'nælɪsɪs; ə'næləsɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 精神分析（或療法）；心理分析（或療法），相當於psychoanalysis：</span>In analysis the individual resolves difficult emotional conflicts. 在心理分析治療法中，個人可以化解嚴重的情感衝突。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**psychiatry**</span> [saɪˈkaɪətri]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 精神病治療：</span>a department of psychiatry 精神病科

<span class="vocabulary">**radiation**</span> [͵reɪdɪ'eɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 對疾病所採用的放射性療法，也可叫radiation therapy：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**course**</span> [kɔ:s] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] course (of sth) 疾病治療或服藥等的療程：</span>to have a course of antibiotics 服用一個療程的抗生素 / She’s been put on a course of injections. 她接受了一個療程的針劑治療。

<span class="vocabulary">**save**</span> [seɪv] 
<span class="definition">vt. save sb/sth (from sth/from doing sth) 拯救生命：</span>to save sb’s life 救某人的命 / Doctors were unable to save her. 醫生未能把她救活。/ She saved a little girl from falling into the water. 她救下一個眼看要落入水中的小女孩。

☀ <span class="category">**具體治療手段：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**manipulate**</span> [məˈnɪpjuleɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [術語] 醫學上指正骨、治療脫臼或對肌肉進行推拿：</span>to manipulate my leg 對我的腿實施推拿術
           
<span class="vocabulary">**manipulation**</span> [məˌnɪpjuˈleɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] [術語] 正骨：</span>manipulation of the bones of the back 對脊椎骨的推拿治療 / manual, physical manipulation 手法治療；物理治療
            
<span class="vocabulary">**acupuncture**</span> [ˈækjupʌŋktʃə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 中國的針刺療法，即針灸：</span>All of those who received the true acupuncture experienced pain relief. 所有接受了針刺治療的人都感到疼痛減輕了。/ I was recommended a course of acupuncture. 我被建議接受一個療程的針刺治療。/ I'm having acupuncture for a bad back. 我正在接受針刺治療背痛。/ In China, acupuncture has been used to treat people for thousands of years. 針刺療法已在中國使用了數千年。/ acupuncture point （針刺）穴位 / Needles are inserted into specific acupuncture points in the body. 針刺入人體特定的穴位。/ acupuncture needle 針灸針
          
<span class="vocabulary">**massage**</span> [ˈmæsɑ:ʒ; 美 məˈsɑ:ʒ]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（對身體進行的）按摩（以減輕肌肉和關節的痛苦）：</span>gentle, relaxing, soothing massage 輕柔的按摩；放鬆身心的按摩 / back, body, foot, full-body, neck, scalp massage 背部按摩；身體按摩；足部按摩；全身按摩；頸部按摩；頭皮按摩 / deep-tissue, shiatsu, Swedish massage 深層組織按摩；指壓式按摩；瑞典式按摩 / therapeutic massage 治療性按摩 / cardiac, heart massage 心臟按壓 / They managed to revive the injured driver with cardiac massage. 他們透過心臟按壓使受傷司機甦醒了過來。/ The therapist gave me a massage to ease the pain. 治療師給我按摩以減輕疼痛。/ massage parlour/parlor(= usually a place where men pay to have sex with women)（打著按摩幌子的）妓院 / massage chair, massage table 按摩椅；按摩臺 / massage oil 按摩油 / massage therapist, massage therapy 按摩師；按摩療法 / Alex asked me if I wanted a massage. 亞力克斯問我是否想做按摩。/ Massage isn't a long-term cure for stress. 按摩對緩解壓力並非長期有效。<span class="definition">vt. 按摩、推拿：</span>He massaged the aching muscles in her feet. 他給她按摩腳上疼痛的肌肉。/ She continued massaging her right foot, which was bruised and aching. 她繼續按摩又腫又疼的右腳。/ He massaged her back with scented oil. 他用芳香油按摩她的後背。/ Massage it lightly with your fingertips. 用你的指尖輕輕地按摩。/ if you wish to massage your family and friends yourself 如果你希望親自為家人及朋友按摩的話 / to massage sb's ego (= to make sb feel better, more confident, attractive, etc.)（比喻用法）增強某人的自信心
           
<span class="vocabulary">**knead**</span> [ni:d]
<span class="definition">vt.（尤指為放鬆或減輕疼痛）揉捏，按摩，推拿（肌肉等）：</span>She felt him knead the aching muscles. 她感到他在按摩她痠痛的肌肉。/ She gently kneaded the muscles in his back. 她輕輕地給他按摩後背。

周邊詞彙補充：
· rub可指用手在身體部位上前後擦、搓、揉；
· complementary可指補充醫學的（不同於西醫的治療方法，如針灸、順勢療法）。
