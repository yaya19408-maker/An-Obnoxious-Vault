☀ <span class="category">**組織 器官：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**heart**</span> [hɑːt] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 心臟（常在左側）：</span>to have a weak heart 心臟不好 / heart attack 心臟病發作 / heart failure 心力衰竭 / heart rate 心率 / heart operation 心臟手術 / His heart is still beating. 他的心臟仍在跳動。<span class="definition">2 [C] [文學] 人體胸部（對應心臟的部位）：</span>She clasped the photo to her heart. 她把照片緊緊地摟在懷裡。/ The bullet had passed less than an inch from his heart. 子彈從距他的心臟不到一英寸處穿過。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**liver**</span> [ˈlɪvə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 肝臟：</span>liver cancer, liver cirrhosis, liver damage, liver disease, liver dysfunction, liver failure, liver injury 肝癌；肝硬化；肝損害；肝病；肝功能失調；肝衰竭；肝損傷 / liver cell, liver enzyme, liver tissue 肝細胞；肝酶；肝組織 / liver function 肝功能 / liver biopsy, liver transplant, liver transplantation 肝組織活檢；肝移植 / enzymes in the liver 肝臟裡的酶 / cirrhosis of the liver 肝硬化 / bile acids returning to the liver 回到肝臟的膽汁酸 / a nationwide appeal for a donor liver 向全國發出的尋找供體肝臟的呼籲

<span class="vocabulary">**lung**</span> [lʌŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 肺，肺臟：</span>a pair of lungs 一對兒肺 / lung ache, lung hurt 肺部疼痛 / lung capacity, lung power 肺活量 / lung cancer 肺癌 / The smoke was beginning to choke her lungs. 煙開始嗆得她難以呼吸。

<span class="vocabulary">**inside**</span> ['ɪn'saɪd] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] insides 可指人的腸胃：</span>She was so nervous, her insides were like jelly. 她緊張得六神無主。
                     
<span class="vocabulary">**bowel**</span> [ˈbaʊəl]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl.] [醫學] 腸：</span>large, small bowel 大腸；小腸 / irritable bowel 易過敏的腸道 / irritable bowel syndrome 腸易激綜合徵 / bowel action, bowel function, bowel habit, bowel movement 排便；排便功能；排便習慣 / Patients are asked to report any change in bowel habit. 病人被要求報告大便習慣的任何變化。/ He's been having painful bowel movements. 他排便一直很痛苦。/ bowel frequency 排便頻率 / bowel wall腸壁 / bowel cancer, bowel disease, bowel disorder, bowel obstruction, bowel problems 腸癌；腸道疾病；腸道紊亂；腸梗阻；腸道問題 / bowel biopsy, bowel surgery腸道活組織檢查；腸外科手術 / to empty/move/open your bowels (= to pass solid waste out of the body)（均為醫學術語，move尤為美式用法）解大便
           
<span class="vocabulary">**intestine**</span> [ɪnˈtestɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [usually pl.] 腸：</span>large, small intestine 大腸；小腸 / lower, upper intestine 腸下部；腸上部 / bacteria in the small intestine 小腸中的細菌 / This area is always tender to the touch if the intestines are not functioning properly. 如果腸功能不正常，這個部位碰一下就會感到疼痛。/ This vitamin is absorbed through the walls of the small intestine. 這種維生素透過小腸壁被吸收。

<span class="vocabulary">**gut**</span> [gʌt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（人的）腸道：</span>healthy gut 健康的腸道 / gut flora（術語）, gut wall 腸內菌群；腸壁 <span class="definition">2 [pl.] guts 人的內臟：</span>I don't like movies that are full of blood and guts.（比喻用法）我不喜歡充滿血腥鏡頭的電影。/ a pain in your gut（美）, a pain in your guts（英）肚子疼 / I had a terrible pain in my guts after eating too many plums. 我李子吃得太多了，肚子疼得厲害。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**colon**</span> [ˈkəʊlən; 美 ˈkoʊ-]

<span class="vocabulary">**stomach**</span> ['stʌmək]

<span class="vocabulary">**tonsil**</span> [ˈtɒnsl; 美 ˈtɑ:nsl]

相關詞義延伸：
· liver還可指動物的可供烹飪和食用的肝；
· kidney還可指動物的可供烹飪和食用的腎臟，即腰子。

周邊詞彙補充：
· tissue可指（人、動植物細胞的）組織；
· organ可指器官；
· brain和nut可表示大腦；
· wall可指（身體器官或動植物細胞等的）外壁。
