☀ <span class="category">**醫療 衛生：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**medical**</span> ['medɪkl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 常作前置定語，指與傷病或其治療有關的，即醫療的，醫學的：</span>medical treatment 醫學治療 / medical research 醫學研究 / medical history 病史 / medical records 病歷 / medical school 醫學院 / medical certificate 健康證明 <span class="definition">2 常作前置定語，指內科的：</span>medical and surgical treatment 內科和外科療法
           
<span class="vocabulary">**clinical**</span> [ˈklɪnɪkl]
<span class="definition">adj. [只用於名詞前] 臨床的、臨床診斷的：</span>clinical research (= done on patients, not just considering theory) 臨床研究 / clinical training (= the part of a doctor's training done in a hospital) 臨床培訓 / clinical trials of a drug 藥物的臨床試驗 / The first clinical trials were expected to begin next year. 第一批臨床實驗有望明年開始。/ a clinical psychologist 臨床心理學家 / She regarded her patients from a purely clinical standpoint. 她純粹從醫治的角度來對待病人。

<span class="vocabulary">**health**</span> [helθ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 提供醫療服務的工作，即衛生，保健，醫療：</span>health insurance 醫療保險 / the Department of Health 衛生部 / community health care 社群醫療保健 / health system 醫療體系 / health issue, health problem, health needs 健康問題；保健需求 / health education 健康教育 / health clinic 衛生所
    
<span class="vocabulary">**hygiene**</span> [ˈhaɪdʒi:n]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（與生活或工作環境有關的、對疾病起預防作用的）衛生：</span>dental, food（英）, oral, personal, public hygiene 牙齒衛生；食物衛生（或食品衛生）；口腔衛生；個人衛生；公共衛生 / infections that are the result of poor food hygiene 食物不衛生引發的感染 / Many skin diseases can be prevented by good personal hygiene. 良好的個人衛生可以預防多種皮膚疾病。/ Good hand hygiene by hospital staff is vital in preventing infection. 醫務人員良好的手部衛生對於預防感染是至關重要的。/ In the interests of hygiene, please wash your hands. 為了衛生，請洗手。/ hygiene regulations, hygiene standards（均尤英）衛生規章制度；衛生標準 / The restaurant was in breach of food hygiene regulations.（英）飯店違反了食物衛生規章制度。/ hygiene habits（尤美）, hygiene practices 衛生習慣 / children with poor hygiene habits 有不良衛生習慣的小孩 / hygiene item（美）, hygiene product 衛生用品 / feminine hygiene products, personal hygiene products 女性衛生用品；個人衛生用品 / standards of hygiene 衛生標準

<span class="vocabulary">**sanitation**</span> [ˌsænɪˈteɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（尤指清除人類廢棄物的）衛生裝置、衛生體系：</span>public sanitation 公共衛生 / sanitation facility, sanitation services, sanitation system 公共衛生設施；公共衛生服務；公共衛生體系 / sanitation department（尤美）公共衛生部門 / sanitation engineer, sanitation worker（均尤美）環衛工人；清潔工 / the direct link between disease and lack of adequate sanitation 疾病與缺乏足夠衛生設施之間的直接關係 / disease resulting from poor sanitation 衛生條件差導致的疾病


周邊詞彙補充：
· medicine可表示醫學、醫療。
