☀ <span class="category">**注射 輸血：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**inject**</span> [ɪnˈdʒekt]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (into yourself/sb/sth) / ~ yourself/sb/sth (with sth) 給人或動物的身體注射藥物或其它物質：</span>to inject drug 注射藥物 / to inject cocaine 注射可卡因 / to inject heroin 注射海洛因 / His son was injected with strong drugs. 他兒子被注射了烈性麻醉藥。/ Adrenalin was injected into the muscle. 往肌肉裡注射了腎上腺素。/ She has been injecting herself with insulin since the age of 16. 她從16歲起就開始自行注射胰島素。/ The technique consists of injecting healthy cells into the weakened muscles. 該技術是把健康細胞注射到衰弱的肌肉裡面。/ The snake injects the venom immediately after biting its prey. 蛇在咬住獵物後會立刻向獵物注射毒液。<span class="definition">vt. ~ A (into B) / ~ B (with A) 用針頭給某事物注射液體：</span>Chemicals are injected into the fruit to reduce decay. 水果注入了化學藥品以防腐壞。/ The fruit is injected with chemicals to reduce decay. 水果注入了化學藥品以防腐壞。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**injection**</span> [ɪnˈdʒekʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（藥物或其它物質的）注射：</span>He was treated with penicillin injections. 他接受了青黴素注射。/ An anaesthetic was administered by injection. 麻醉劑已注射入體內。/ daily injections of insulin 每天的胰島素注射 / intramuscular, intravenous injection 肌肉注射；靜脈注射 / painkilling injection 鎮痛注射/ tetanus injection（尤英）（美式英語通常用tetanus shot）打破傷風針 / insulin, penicillin injection 胰島素注射；青黴素注射 / BotoxTM, collagen injection 肉毒桿菌毒素注射；膠原蛋白注射 / booster injection（尤英）（美式英語通常用booster shot）加強注射 / Can I bring my dog in for his booster injection? 我可以帶我的狗來打加強針嗎? / lethal injection（執行死刑的）毒劑注射 / In some states execution is by lethal injection. 有些州以注射毒劑的方式執行死刑。/ He had to have a tetanus injection after injuring himself.（尤英）弄傷自己後他不得不打了一支破傷風針。/ They gave her an injection to stop the pain.（尤英）他們給她打了一針止痛。/ The rats received a daily injection of the drug. 這些老鼠每天注射一次這種藥。/ The best treatment is antibiotics, preferably by injection. 最佳的治療方法是使用抗生素，能夠注射就更好。/ an injection against whooping cough 防治百日咳的針劑 / injections for diabetes 治療糖尿病的針劑 / an injection into the vein 靜脈注射 / Both groups received a second injection with the same solution. 兩組都接受了同樣藥劑的第二次注射。

<span class="vocabulary">**shot**</span> [ʃɒt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 非正式，尤用於美式英語，指藥物的注射，相當於injection：</span>a shot of penicillin 一針青黴素 / allergy, flu shot 過敏注射；流感注射 / The applause acted on her like a shot of adrenalin.（比喻用法）掌聲猶如給她注射了一劑強心劑。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**infuse**</span> [ɪnˈfju:z]
<span class="definition">vt. [醫學] 將藥物或其它物質慢慢輸入一個人的靜脈：</span>瞭解即可           

<span class="vocabulary">**jab**</span> [dʒæb]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [英，非正式] 注射接種（以防止染上某種疾病）、預防針：</span>Did you have a flu jab this year? 今年你打過流感預防針了嗎？/ tetanus, typhoid jab 破傷風預防針；傷寒預防針 / painful anti malaria jabs 很疼的瘧疾預防針           

<span class="vocabulary">**vaccinate**</span> [ˈvæksɪneɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] ~ sb (against sth) 給人或動物接種疫苗：</span>I was vaccinated against tetanus. 我接種了破傷風疫苗。/ Have you had your child vaccinated against whooping cough? 你給你的孩子打過百日咳疫苗了嗎？/ Dogs must be vaccinated against distemper. 狗必須接種犬瘟熱疫苗。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vaccination**</span> [ˌvæksɪˈneɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 疫苗的接種：</span>flu, measles, MMR, rubella, smallpox vaccination 流感疫苗接種；麻疹疫苗接種；麻腮風三聯疫苗接種；風疹疫苗接種；天花疫苗接種 / routine vaccination 常規疫苗接種 / emergency vaccination 緊急接種 / compulsory, mandatory（尤美）vaccination 強制接種 / voluntary vaccination 自願接種 / mass, widespread vaccination 大規模的接種；廣泛的疫苗接種 / childhood vaccination 兒童疫苗接種 / booster vaccination 強化免疫接種 / I had a routine tetanus booster vaccination last year. 去年我接種了常規破傷風加強針。/ vaccination campaign, vaccination programme/program, vaccination schedule（尤美）疫苗接種運動；疫苗接種計劃；疫苗接種時間表 / vaccination rate 疫苗接種率 / Vaccination rates remain below 75% of the at-risk adult population. 高危成年人的疫苗接種率仍然低於75%。/ a vaccination against tetanus 破傷風疫苗的接種 / Make sure your vaccinations are up to date. 一定要接種最新的疫苗。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**transfusion**</span> [trænsˈfju:ʒn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（對人或動物進行的）輸血。相當於blood transfusion：</span>blood transfusion 輸血 / emergency transfusion 緊急輸血 / transfusion unit 輸血單位
