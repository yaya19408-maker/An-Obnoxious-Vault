☀ <span class="category">**診斷 會診：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**diagnose**</span> [ˈdaɪəgnəʊz; -ˈnəʊz; 美 ˌdaɪəgˈnoʊs]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ sb (as/with) (sth) / ~ sth (as sth) 診斷疾病：</span>The test is used to diagnose a variety of diseases. 此項化驗可用於診斷多種疾病。/ The illness was diagnosed as cancer. 此病診斷為癌症。/ He has recently been diagnosed with angina. 他最近被診斷出患有心絞痛。/ He was diagnosed (as) a diabetic when he was 64. 他64歲時被診斷患有糖尿病。/ people with clinically diagnosed diabetes 臨床診斷出患有糖尿病的人 / The soldiers were diagnosed as having flu. 這些士兵經診斷患了流感。/ Her condition was wrongly diagnosed by the doctor. 她的病醫生診斷錯了。/ newly diagnosed patients 新確診的病人 / He was diagnosed with leukaemia. 他經診斷患有白血病。/ Susan had a mental breakdown and was diagnosed with schizophrenia. 蘇珊精神崩潰，被診斷為得了精神分裂症。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**diagnosis**</span> [ˌdaɪəgˈnəʊsɪs; 美 -ˈnoʊ-]
（複數形式為diagnoses）<span class="definition">n. [C, U] ~ (of sth) 病因的診斷：</span>diagnosis of lung cancer 肺癌的診斷 / They are waiting for the doctor's diagnosis. 他們正在等待醫生的診斷結論。/ An accurate diagnosis was made after a series of tests. 準確的診斷是在一系列的檢查後作出的。/ I need to have a second test to confirm the diagnosis. 我需要再進行一次檢查以確診。/ Symptoms may not appear for some weeks, so diagnosis can be difficult. 症狀可能在幾周裡都不會出現，所以診斷起來會比較困難。/ Without the results of the test, the doctor could only make a tentative diagnosis. 沒有檢驗結果，醫生只能作出初步的診斷。/ Early diagnosis is critical for successful treatment. 儘早診斷對成功治療很關鍵。/ medical, psychiatric diagnosis 醫學診斷；精神病學診斷 / clinical diagnosis of schizophrenia 精神分裂症的臨床診斷 / AIDS, cancer diagnosis 艾滋病診斷；癌症的診斷 / The doctor cannot give a diagnosis without knowing the full medical history. 不瞭解全部的病史，醫生無法作出診斷。/ Further tests have confirmed the diagnosis. 進一步的化驗已經證實了所作的診斷。/ Patients may suffer from some of the symptoms years before diagnosis. 患者可能在診斷出疾病之前多年已出現其中一些症狀。/ the diagnosis of the disease 疾病的診斷 / They are using new methods of diagnosis. 他們在使用新的診斷方法。

<span class="vocabulary">**palpate**</span> [pælˈpeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [醫學] 透過觸控身體部位來檢查疾病，如觸診：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**clinic**</span> ['klɪnɪk] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] [尤英] 門診時間，或會診時間：</span>The antenatal clinic is on Monday. 產前檢查時間為星期一。<span class="definition">2 [C]（醫院中實習生所進行的）臨床實習，臨床教學：</span>瞭解即可 
             
<span class="vocabulary">**consultation**</span> [ˌkɒnslˈteɪʃn; 美 ˌkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（向專家請教的）諮詢會面；（尤指）就診：</span>A personal diet plan is devised after a consultation with a nutritionist. 諮詢營養學家後，制訂了個人飲食方案。/ You should seek expert consultation.（expert尤為美式用法）你應該找專家就診。/ medical, psychiatric, surgical consultation 就診；精神科諮詢；外科手術諮詢 / The practice provides medical consultations for drug addicts. 這種做法為吸毒成癮者提供醫療諮詢。/ I had a consultation with a dermatologist. 我向一位皮膚科專家進行了諮詢。/ Consultation with a rheumatologist should be sought.（美）應該向風溼科專家求診。<span class="definition">2 [C] 會診（即若干醫生共同討論某個病人的病情和治療方案）：</span>瞭解即可         

<span class="vocabulary">**surgery**</span> [ˈsɜ:dʒəri; 美 ˈsɜ:rdʒ-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] [英]（醫生、牙醫、獸醫的）接診時間：</span>morning, afternoon, evening surgery 上午應診時間；下午應診時間；晚間應診時間 / surgery hours 應診時間 / Is there a surgery this evening? 今晚有沒有醫生應診？/ His surgery always ends at eleven. 他的出診時間總是到11點結束。/ I’ll do morning surgery, if you like. 如果你願意，我上午接診吧。/ She has a morning surgery. 她上午接診。/ He holds surgery from 10 a.m. to 1p.m. 他上午10點到下午1點接診。
