☀ <span class="category">**軍階 軍銜：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**rank**</span> [ræŋk] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 軍隊的級別，即軍銜。是此義的常規用詞：</span>officers of senior rank 有高階軍銜的軍官 / He was assigned the rank of Commander. 他被授予海軍中校軍銜。

<span class="vocabulary">**grade**</span> [ɡreɪd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [美] 軍銜：</span>lieutenant junior grade 中尉軍銜
           
<span class="vocabulary">**rating**</span> [ˈreɪtɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 軍隊的軍階：</span>瞭解即可
 
<span class="vocabulary">**flash**</span> [flæʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [英]（佩戴在軍服上以表明一個人軍階的）條或小布片，如肩章、臂章等：</span>瞭解即可 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**stripe**</span> [straɪp]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（軍裝上表示等級或服役年限的）條、槓（常為V字型）：</span>a soldier with a corporal's stripes on his arms 胳膊上彆著下士臂章計程車兵 / He'd lost his stripes for slovenliness and cheek. 他因散漫、無禮而丟了軍職。

<span class="vocabulary">**bust**</span> [bʌst]
<span class="definition">vt. [非正式] [尤美] 降低某人的軍銜（作為一種懲罰）：</span>瞭解即可

· 表示“等級”的詞群見[[01程度 等級]]
