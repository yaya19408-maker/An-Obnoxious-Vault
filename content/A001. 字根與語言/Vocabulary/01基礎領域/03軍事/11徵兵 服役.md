☀ <span class="category">**徵兵 服役：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**draft**</span> [drɑːft] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [尤美] the draft可指徵兵或徵用，相當於conscription：</span>the military draft 徵兵 / to avoid the draft 逃避兵役 <span class="definition">vt. [美] 常用被動式，徵兵，相當於conscript：</span>He was drafted into the U.S. Army. 他應徵加入美國陸軍。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**recruit**</span> [rɪˈkru:t]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 招募新人（使其加入一個武裝部隊）：</span>Soldiers were recruited from the local villages. 士兵都是從當地村莊招募的。/ Some of the men were recruited into the army. 一些男子被徵召入伍。<span class="definition">vt. 透過招募新人來組建一支軍隊、團隊等：</span>to recruit a task force 組建特遣部隊
           
<span class="vocabulary">**recruitment**</span> [rɪˈkruːtmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 士兵的招募：</span>army, military recruitment 新兵招募 / the forced recruitment of children as soldiers 強制性徵召兒童兵 

<span class="vocabulary">**conscript**</span> [kənˈskrɪpt]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] ~ sb (into sth) [尤英] [美常用draft] 將某人徵召入伍：</span>He was conscripted into the army in 1939. 他於1939年應徵入伍。/ He was conscripted into the German army. 他應徵加入德國軍隊。/ Peter was conscripted like every other young man. 和所有其他年輕人一樣，彼得應徵入伍。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**conscription**</span> [kənˈskrɪpʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [尤英] [美通常用the draft] 徵兵、徵募：</span>All adult males will be liable for conscription. 所有成年男子都有義務應徵入伍。/ universal conscription 全民兵役制 / compulsory, forced conscription 義務兵役制；強制兵役制 / the forced conscription of boys into the army 強徵男青年服兵役 / his decision to introduce military conscription 他就實行兵役製作出的決定 / He injured himself to avoid conscription. 他自傷以逃避服兵役。

<span class="vocabulary">**enlist**</span> [ɪnˈlɪst]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sb) (in/into/for sth) / ~ (sb) (as sth)（使）入伍、從軍：</span>They both enlisted in 1915. 他倆都是1915年入伍的。/ to enlist as a soldier 入伍當兵 / He was enlisted into the US Navy. 他應徵參加了美國海軍。/ Michael Hughes of Lackawanna, Pennsylvania, enlisted in the 82nd Airborne 20 years ago. 來自賓夕法尼亞州拉克萬納的邁克爾·休斯20年前參軍，在第82空降師服役。/ He enlisted as a private in the Mexican War. 在墨西哥戰爭中他參軍成為一名二等兵。/ Thousands of recruits had been enlisted and partly trained. 招募了數千新兵，並對他們進行一定程度的訓練。

<span class="vocabulary">**serve**</span> [sə:v] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. serve (sb) (as sth) / serve (in/on/with sth) / serve (under/with sb)（軍人為國家而）效力，服役：</span>to serve in the military 在軍隊服役 / soldiers who have served their country well for many years 忠心效力國家多年計程車兵們 / He served with 92nd Airborne. 他在第92空降師服役。/ He served as a captain with distinction in the army. 他曾是一名戰功赫赫的陸軍上尉。

<span class="vocabulary">**service**</span> ['sə:vɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl, U]（在軍隊中所服的）兵役，是此義的常規用詞：</span>to see service（尤英）服役 / to go into, to join the services 服兵役；參軍 / to do a year’s military service 服一年兵役 / service member（美）軍人 / his military service record during the Vietnam War 他在越南戰爭期間的服役記錄 / He died on active service. 他在服役期間犧牲。

<span class="vocabulary">**tour**</span> [tʊə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] tour of duty可指在部隊服役，此時遠不如service常用：</span>The soldiers all used to do a six-month tour of duty in Northern Ireland. 士兵過去都要在北愛爾蘭服役六個月。

<span class="vocabulary">**volunteer**</span> [͵vɒlən'tɪə] 
<span class="definition">vi. volunteer (for sth) 未被強迫而主動加入部隊等，如自願參軍，當志願兵等：</span>to volunteer for military service 自願服兵役

周邊詞彙補充：
· reinforce可指透過派遣更多人員或給予更多裝備而使軍隊等更強大，如增兵。
