☀ <span class="category">**防禦：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**defence**</span> [dɪ'fens]（美defense）
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 防禦，保衛領土使其免受攻擊：</span>national, territorial defence 國防；領土防禦 / air defence 防空 / civil defence 民防 / nuclear defence 核防禦 / defence against attacks from the north 抵禦來自北方的進攻 / weapons of defence 防禦武器 / soldiers who died in defence of our country 為保衛我們的祖國而獻身的戰士 <span class="definition">2 [C, U] defence (against sth) 對敵人起防禦作用的事物：</span>coastal, sea defence 海岸防禦工事；海上防禦工事 / missile defence 導彈防禦工事 / The town walls were built as a defence against enemy attacks. 城牆是為防禦敵人攻擊而修建的。/ They are building up defences along the river. 他們正在加固沿河的防禦工事。<span class="definition">3 [U] 國防機構，國防體系：</span>the Ministry of Defence（英）國防部 / the Department of Defense（美）國防部 / defence minister（英國的）國防部長 / Defense Secretary（美國的）國防部長 / defence capacity, defence force, defence forces, defence system 國防能力；國防力量；國防部隊；國防體系/ defence policy, defence strategy 國防政策；國防戰略 / defence expert 國防專家 / defence industry 國防工業 / defence budget 國防預算
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**defensive**</span> [dɪˈfensɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. 防衛的、防禦的：</span>Troops took up a defensive position around the town. 部隊在全城採取了守勢。/ The Government hastily organized defensive measures against the raids. 政府急忙佈置了防衛措施抵禦空襲。/ a largely defensive campaign 以防禦為主的戰役

<span class="vocabulary">**moat**</span> [məʊt; 美 moʊt]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（城堡等周圍的）護城河（起對敵防禦的作用）：</span>The castle had a deep moat which emptied into the lake. 城堡有一條很深的流入湖裡的護城河。

<span class="vocabulary">**defend**</span> [dɪ'fend] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. defend (sb/yourself/sth) (from/against sb/sth) 防禦或保衛領地，以使其免遭攻擊：</span>Troops have been sent to defend the borders. 已派出部隊去守衛邊疆。/ The city was heavily defended against attack. 這座城市嚴密設防以抵禦攻擊。/ They attacked the left flank, which has only lightly defended. 他們攻擊了防禦薄弱的左翼。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fortify**</span> [ˈfɔ:tɪfaɪ; 美 ˈfɔ:rt-]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (against sb/sth) 構築防禦工事，尤指築城防禦：</span>a fortified castle 設防的城堡 / British soldiers working to fortify an airbase in Bahrain 在巴林一空軍基地築防的英國士兵

☀ <span class="category">**部署：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**deploy**</span> [dɪˈplɔɪ]
<span class="definition">vt. [術語] 部署或排程（軍隊或武器）：</span>2 000 troops were deployed in the area. 那個地區部署了2 000人的部隊。/ 15 000 UN peacekeepers are currently deployed in the country, 目前有 15，000 名聯合國維和士兵部署在這個國家。/ The president said he had no intention of deploying ground troops. 總統稱並不打算部署地面部隊。/ the US-made Patriot anti-missile system which was deployed in the Gulf war 在海灣戰爭中部署的美國製造的“愛國者”反導彈系統 / At least 5 000 missiles were deployed along the border. 沿邊境至少部署了5 000枚導彈。/ Tanks were deployed effectively during the long campaign. 在這場曠日持久的戰役中坦克部隊得到了有效的部署。

<span class="vocabulary">**deployment**</span> [dɪ'plɔɪmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（部隊、軍事裝備的）部署、調集、排程：</span>the deployment of troops into townships 在城鎮中部署部隊 / force（尤美）, military, troop deployment 武力部署；軍事部署；軍隊部署

☀ <span class="category">**埋伏：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**ambush**</span> [ˈæmbʊʃ]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 伏擊某人、埋伏：</span>enemy ambush 敵軍的埋伏 / The soldiers set up an ambush on the road. 士兵在路上設了埋伏。/ They staged an ambush on an army patrol. 他們策劃了對一支陸軍巡邏隊的伏擊。/ We ran into an ambush in the valley. 我們在山谷裡中了埋伏。/ Two soldiers were killed in a terrorist ambush. 兩名士兵遭到恐怖分子伏擊而死亡。/ The soldiers lay in ambush for the enemy troops. 戰士們趴在埋伏圈裡，等著敵軍的到來。/ They were lying in ambush, waiting for the aid convoy. 他們埋伏起來，等著襲擊增援車隊。/ The gunmen, lying in ambush, opened fire, killing the driver. 埋伏的槍手開槍打死了司機。<span class="definition">vt. 伏擊、埋伏：</span>The guerrillas ambushed them near the bridge. 游擊隊員在大橋附近伏擊了他們。/ The Guatemalan army says rebels ambushed and killed 10 patrolmen. 瓜地馬拉軍方說叛亂分子伏擊打死了10名巡邏兵。
