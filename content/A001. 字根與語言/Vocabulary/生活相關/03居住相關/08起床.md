☀ <span class="category">**起床：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**rise**</span> [raɪz] 
<span class="definition">vi. [正式]（從躺著的姿勢）站起來、坐起來：</span>He was accustomed to rising early. 他習慣於早起。/ He managed to rise to a sitting position. 他設法坐了起來。

<span class="vocabulary">**arise**</span> [ə'raɪz] 
<span class="definition">vi. [舊時或文學] 起床：</span>He arose at dawn. 他黎明即起。

<span class="vocabulary">**surface**</span> ['sə:fɪs] 
<span class="definition">vi. [非正式] 起床：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**up**</span> [ʌp] 
<span class="definition">adj. 起床的：未上床；起床：</span>These days they were up at the crack of dawn. 這些天他們在破曉時就起床。/ I stayed up late last night. 我昨晚熬夜了。/ He’s up and about again after his illness.（英）他病癒後又能起來活動了。
