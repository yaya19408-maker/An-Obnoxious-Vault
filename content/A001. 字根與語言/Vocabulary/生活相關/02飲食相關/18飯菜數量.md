☀ <span class="category">**飯菜數量：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**light**</span> [laɪt] 
<span class="definition">adj.（一頓飯）少量的：</span>light supper, light snack 簡單的晚餐；簡單的小吃 / I just want something light for lunch. 我午飯稍微吃點就夠了。

<span class="vocabulary">**serve**</span> [sə:v] 
<span class="definition">vt.（食物的量）足夠…吃：</span>This dish will serve four. 這盤菜夠四個人吃。 

<span class="vocabulary">**fill**</span> [fɪl] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] your fill (of food/drink) 你能吃飽或喝足的量：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**swallow**</span> ['swɒləʊ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一次所吞嚥的食物或飲料的數量，可稱為一口：</span>瞭解即可

周邊詞彙補充：
· bite可指（從更大塊食物上咬下的）一口食物。

☀ <span class="category">**剩飯剩菜：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**leftover**</span> [ˈleftəʊvə(r); 美 -oʊv-]
<span class="definition">n. [usually pl.] 吃剩的食物：</span>Refrigerate any leftovers. 把剩菜放進冰箱裡。/ The only food I ever got was the family's leftovers. 我所得到的唯一食物就是這家人的殘羹剩飯。     

<span class="vocabulary">**scrap**</span> [skræp]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] scraps可表示剩飯剩菜：</span>Give the scraps to the dog. 把剩菜餵狗吧。/ The pigs are fed on food scraps. 這些豬喂的是殘羹剩飯。/ He removed the last scraps of food from his plate. 他清理掉盤子裡最後一點兒食物。
