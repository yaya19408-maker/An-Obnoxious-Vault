☀ <span class="category">**款式 樣式：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**style**</span> [staɪl] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 某種設計的款式，樣式，尤指服裝款式。是此義的常規用詞：</span>We stock a wide variety of styles and sizes. 我們有各種款式各種尺碼的貨品。/ She was dressed in casual style. 她穿著一身休閒裝。/ a flamboyant style of dress 華麗的著裝風格 <span class="definition">2 [C] 指服裝的時髦，時尚：</span>style-conscious teenagers 講究時髦的青少年 / Short skirts are back in style (= fashionable). 短裙又流行起來了。 

<span class="vocabulary">**cut**</span> [kʌt] 
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.]（服裝所剪裁出來的）式樣或款式：</span>the elegant cut of her dress 她的連衣裙的優雅款式 

<span class="vocabulary">**trend**</span> [trend] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 衣著時尚；風尚：</span>Linen is on trend again this summer. 亞麻衣料今年夏天再次流行。

<span class="vocabulary">**season**</span> ['si:zn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（一年當中服裝的）流行期：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**loose**</span> [lu:s] 
<span class="definition">adj. 衣服（穿起來）寬鬆的：</span>a loose shirt 寬大的襯衣

<span class="vocabulary">**full**</span> [fʊl] 
<span class="definition">adj.（衣服）肥大的，寬鬆的：</span>a full skirt 寬裙

<span class="vocabulary">**straight**</span> [streɪt] 
<span class="definition">adj.（服裝）直筒型（非緊身）的：</span>a straight skirt 直筒裙

<span class="vocabulary">**low**</span> [ləʊ] 
<span class="definition">adj.（衣服）領口開得低的：</span>a dress with a low neckline 領口低的連衣裙

<span class="vocabulary">**long**</span> [lɒŋ] 
<span class="definition">adj.（衣服）長的，即完全或大部分覆蓋腿或臂的：</span>a long skirt 長裙 / a long-sleeved shirt 長袖襯衫 

<span class="vocabulary">**brief**</span> [bri:f] 
<span class="definition">adj.（衣服）短的，沒有覆蓋住身體的很多部位的：</span>a brief skirt 短裙

<span class="vocabulary">**tight**</span> [taɪt] 
<span class="definition">adj. 衣服緊身的或貼身的（有時不舒服的）：</span>a tight pair of jeans 一條緊身牛仔褲 / These shoes are much too tight. 這雙鞋太緊了。/ The new sweater was a tight fit. 這件新毛衣很貼身。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**skinny**</span> [ˈskɪni]
<span class="definition">adj.（衣服）緊身的：</span>a skinny sweater 緊身套頭衫
           
<span class="vocabulary">**flare**</span> [fleə(r); 美 fler]
<span class="definition">vi.（連衣裙等）底部展開、呈喇叭形展開：</span>The sleeves are tight to the elbow, then flare out. 袖子在肘部收緊，接著逐漸展開。/ a simple black dress, cut to flare from the hips 一條簡潔的黑裙，剪裁成從胯部向外展開的式樣 <span class="definition">n. [C] 逐漸展開的一種形狀，如喇叭形：</span>a skirt with a slight flare 下襬略張開的裙子

<span class="vocabulary">**model**</span> ['mɒdl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 時裝模特兒：</span>fashion model 時裝模特兒 / female model 女時裝模特兒
