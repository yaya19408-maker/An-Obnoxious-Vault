☀ <span class="category">**親屬 親戚：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**relative**</span> ['relətɪv] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 親戚，親屬。是此義的常規用詞，最為常用：</span>close, near relative 近親 / distant relative 遠親 / blood, family relative 血親；家庭成員 / immediate relative 直系親屬 / living relative 在世的親屬 / deceased relative 已故親屬 / a relative by marriage 姻親

<span class="vocabulary">**relation**</span> [rɪ'leɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一位親屬或親戚，與relative在詞義上未見明顯差別：</span>a relation by marriage 姻親 / close, near relation 近親 / distant relation 遠親 / blood relation 血親 / What relation is Rita to you? 麗塔是你的什麼親戚？

<span class="vocabulary">**relationship**</span> [rɪ'leɪʃnʃɪp] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] relationship (between A and B) 一個家族之間的兩人之間的關係，如血緣關係、姻親關係：</span>a father-son relationship 父子關係 / blood, family, kinship relationship 血緣關係；家庭關係；親屬關係 / to have a distant relationship with royalty 與王室有遠親關係 / ‘What’s the relationship between you and Tony?’ ‘He’s my cousin.’ “你和託尼是什麼關係？”“他是我堂兄。”/ What relationship are you to Pat? 你是帕特的什麼人？
           
<span class="vocabulary">**kin**</span> [kɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] [舊時或正式] 統稱家屬、親屬：</span>close, near kin 近親 / Marriage between close kin is prohibited. 禁止近親結婚。/ blood kin（尤美）血親 / extended kin 大家庭的成員 / distant kin 遠親 / female, male kin 女性親戚；男性親戚 / kin group（通常用kin-group）, kin network（均為術語）血緣集團；血緣網路 / kin relationships 親屬關係 / One of the drivers was fatally injured; his next of kin has been informed. 其中一名駕駛員受了致命傷，已通知其至親。/ She has gone to live with her husband's kin. 她住到丈夫的親戚家裡去了。           

<span class="vocabulary">**kindred**</span> [ˈkɪndrəd]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [pl.] [舊時或正式] 統稱家人、親屬：</span>The offender made proper restitution to the victim's kindred. 違法者對受害人親屬給予了適當的補償。<span class="definition">2 [U] [舊時或正式] 親屬關係、血緣關係：</span>ties of kindred 親屬關係

<span class="vocabulary">**direct**</span> [dɪ'rekt, daɪ-] 
<span class="definition">adj. 只作前置定語，（長輩與晚輩的關係）直系的，嫡系的：</span>a direct descendant of the King 國王的嫡系後裔

<span class="vocabulary">**close**</span> [kləʊz] 
<span class="definition">adj.（家庭關係）親近的：</span>close relatives 近親

<span class="vocabulary">**near**</span> [nɪə] 
<span class="definition">adj. near relative/relation（用於描述親屬關係）近親的。與close未見明顯差別：</span>near relatives 近親
           
<span class="vocabulary">**immediate**</span> [ɪˈmi:diət]
<span class="definition">adj. [只用於名詞前]（關係）直系的：</span>The funeral was attended by her immediate family (= her parents, children, brothers and sisters) only. 只有她的直系親屬參加了葬禮。

<span class="vocabulary">**connection**</span> [kə'nekʃən] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] connections 可指親屬，但非近親，即遠親：</span>She is British but also has German connections. 她是英國人，但也有德國親戚。

<span class="vocabulary">**cousin**</span> ['kʌzn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一個遠房親戚，遠親。與connections未見明顯差別，但connections為複數名詞：</span>He’s a distant cousin of mine. 他是我遠房的一個表親。

<span class="vocabulary">**distant**</span> ['dɪstənt] 
<span class="definition">adj. 只用於名詞前，（親戚）遠房的，遠親的：</span>a distant cousin 遠房堂兄弟 / a distant relative 遠房親戚

<span class="vocabulary">**remote**</span> [rɪ'məʊt] 
<span class="definition">adj. 只用於名詞前，（人的親戚）遠房的，親戚關係較遠的。與distant在詞義上未見明顯差別：</span>a remote cousin 遠房堂親

<span class="vocabulary">**related**</span> [rɪˈleɪtɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (to sth/sb) 屬同一家族的；有親屬關係的：</span>Are you related to Margaret? 你與瑪格麗特是一家人嗎？/ We're distantly related. 我們是遠親。/ He claims to be distantly related to the British royal family. 他聲稱自己與英國王室是遠親。/ people in countries like Bangladesh who have been able to show they are related to a spouse or parent living in Britain 能夠證明自己和生活在英國的配偶或父母有親屬關係的孟加拉國等國家的公民
