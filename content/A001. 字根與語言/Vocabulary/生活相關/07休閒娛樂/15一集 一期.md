☀ <span class="category">**一集 一期：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**part**</span> [pɑːt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（電視系列片的、尤指單獨播出的）集：</span>The final part will be shown next Sunday evening. 最後一集將於下星期天晚上播出。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**instalment**</span> [ɪnˈstɔ:lmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（電視連續劇的）一集：</span>daily instalment 每天一集

<span class="vocabulary">**edition**</span> [ɪ'dɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（廣播、電視節目的）一期，一輯：</span>Tonight’s edition of ‘Panorama’ looks at unemployment. 今晚這輯《全景》探討的是失業問題。/ There will be a live edition of the show tomorrow night. 明晚會有現場演出。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**episode**</span> [ˈepɪsəʊd; 美 -soʊd]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（電視連續劇或廣播連續劇的）一集、（雜誌連載故事的）一節：</span>The final episode will be shown next Sunday. 下週日將播出最後一集。/ It happened in the final episode of ‘Star Trek’. 這件事發生在《星際迷航》的最後一集裡。Don't miss next week's exciting episode! 別錯過下週激動人心的一集！/ classic episode 經典的一集 / pilot episode 試播的一集 / previous episode 上一集 / I caught a few episodes of seasons one and two. 我趕上了第一、二季裡面的幾集。/ The next episode has not yet been filmed. 下一集還未拍攝出來。
