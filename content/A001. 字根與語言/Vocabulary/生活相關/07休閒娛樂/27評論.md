☀ <span class="category">**評論：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**preview**</span> ['pri:vju:] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 在一部電影或一檔電視節目對公眾播放之前，報刊雜誌所對其進行的預先評述或報告：</span>a preview of next week’s programmes 下週節目預告 <span class="definition">vt. 在電影或電視節目等正式對公眾播放之前進行觀看，併為報刊雜誌撰寫觀看評價：</span>The film was previewed in last week’s issue. 本刊上週對該部影片作了預評。

<span class="vocabulary">**review**</span> [rɪ'vju:] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 在媒體上對書籍、戲劇或電影等所做的評論：</span>film（尤英）, movie（尤美）review 影評 / The show has poor reviews in the press. 媒體對這個節目的評價不高。/ The book received mixed reviews. 對這本書的評論褒貶不一。/ Their new musical opened to glowing reviews. 他們的新音樂劇受到熱烈好評。/ His review appeared in yesterday’s paper. 他的評論刊登在昨天的報紙上。/ review article 評論文章 <span class="definition">vt. 為書籍、戲劇、電影等作品寫評論：</span>The movie has been favourably/favorably reviewed on various websites. 這部影片在很多網站上受到了好評。
