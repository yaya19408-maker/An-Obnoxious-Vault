☀ <span class="category">**撰稿 供稿：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**contribute**</span> [kən'trɪbju:t] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. contribute (sth) (to sth) 為報刊、雜誌、廣播節目或電視節目撰稿：</span>She contributed a number of articles to the magazine. 她給這家雜誌撰寫了一些稿件。/ a photographer who contributed regularly to ‘The Face’ 定期向《面孔》雜誌投稿的一位攝影師

<span class="vocabulary">**file**</span> [faɪl] 
<span class="definition">vt.（新聞記者）傳送報道給自己的僱主（如報社）：</span>He had to rush back to the office and file a housing story before the secretaries went home. 他不得不趕回辦公室，在秘書們回家之前提交一份有關住房的報道。
