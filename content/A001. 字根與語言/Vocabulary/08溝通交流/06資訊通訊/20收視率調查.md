☀ <span class="category">**收視率調查：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**rating**</span> [ˈreɪtɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] the ratings 可指電視或廣播節目的收視率、收聽率：</span>audience. TV rating 收聽率；收視率 / prime-time rating（尤美）黃金時間收視率 / The network's prime-time ratings are up 150%. 該電視網的黃金時間收視率上升了150%。/ The show continues to garner high ratings.（garner尤為美式用法）該節目持續擁有很高的收視率。/ At this stage the series was getting good ratings. 現階段這部連續劇收視率很高。/ Bringing her on the show was a cynical attempt to boost the ratings. 讓她上節目純粹就是想提高收視率。/ The ratings went shooting up overnight. 收視率一夜之間飆升。/ The show's ratings have dipped sharply. 這個節目的收視率銳減。/ rating battle, rating war 收視率競爭；收視率大戰 / His new sitcom was a ratings success. 從收視率看，他的新情景喜劇很成功。/ The show has gone up in the ratings. 這個節目的收視率上升了。/ It has been ousted from top spot in the TV ratings. 它已不再位居電視排行榜首位了。/ Eurocops was beaten in the ratings on Channel 4 by Kate and Allie, Hill Street Blues and St Elsewhere. 在第4頻道的收視率調查中《歐洲警察》被《凱特和阿莉》、《希爾街的布魯斯》和《何方神聖》打敗。/ CBS's ratings again showed huge improvement over the previous year. 與前一年相比，哥倫比亞廣播公司的收視率再次大幅提高。
