☀ <span class="category">**傳送訊息 播送轉播：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**relay**</span> ['ri:leɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. relay sth (to sb) 接收並向某人傳送資訊、新聞等：</span>to relay the message to sb 將這個訊息轉達給某人 / The information was relayed to him by email. 透過電子郵件將資訊轉達給了他。

<span class="vocabulary">**fax**</span> [fæks]（正式為facsimile）
<span class="definition">vt. fax sth (to sb) / fax sb (sth) 用傳真機向某人傳送信件或訊息等：</span>Could you fax it to me? 你能把它傳真給我嗎？

<span class="vocabulary">**flash**</span> [flæʃ] 
<span class="definition">vt.（透過無線電、計算機等）快速傳送（資訊）：</span>News of their triumph was flashed around the world. 他們勝利的訊息迅速傳遍了全世界。

<span class="vocabulary">**send**</span> [send] 
<span class="definition">vt. 傳送資訊、訊號等：</span>to send a message 發資訊 / to send a signal 傳送訊號 / to send an email 傳送電子郵件
           
<span class="vocabulary">**route**</span> [ru:t; North American English also raʊt]
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 按某條線路來傳送資訊：</span>Satellites route data all over the globe. 衛星向全球各地傳遞資訊。

<span class="vocabulary">**appear**</span> [ə'pɪə] 
<span class="definition">vi.（節目）將被播報：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**appearance**</span> [ə'pɪərəns] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] 被廣播：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**relay**</span> ['ri:leɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. relay sth (to sb) 播放（電視或廣播訊號），也可叫轉播：</span>The programme was relayed by satellite to its audiences. 這個節目透過衛星向其觀眾進行了轉播。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**televise**</span> [ˈtelɪvaɪz]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 用電視播送：</span>a nationally televised debate 全國電視播送的辯論 / to televise a novel 用電視播送小說 / The speech will be televised live. 這次演講將由電視直播。/ The Grand Prix will be televised by the BBC. 汽車大獎賽將由英國廣播公司進行電視轉播。

<span class="vocabulary">**network**</span> ['netwə:k] 
<span class="definition">vt. 同時在若干個不同的地區播報廣播或電視節目，即聯播：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**radio**</span> ['reɪdɪəʊ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 用無線電向某人傳送訊息：</span>The warning was radioed to headquarters. 透過無線電向總部發出了警報。/ The officer radioed for advice. 那名軍官發電請求指示。

<span class="vocabulary">**carry**</span> ['kærɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 指廣播發布一則報導，即播出、報道：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**transmit**</span> [trænsˈmɪt; trænz-]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth) (from…) (to…) 傳送、傳輸（廣播或電視訊號、計算機資料或其它電子資訊等）：</span>a short-wave radio that can transmit as well as receive 收發兩用的短波無線電裝置 / signals transmitted from a satellite 從衛星傳送來的訊號 / The ceremony was transmitted live by satellite to over fifty countries. 典禮透過衛星向五十多個國家進行了實況轉播。/ The information is electronically transmitted to schools and colleges. 資訊透過電子方式傳送到各所學校和學院。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**transmission**</span> [trænsˈmɪʃn; trænz-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U]（電子資料、電子訊號、資訊或廣播、電視節目的）播送，發射，傳送，傳輸：</span>the transmission of computer data along telephone lines 計算機資料沿電話線的傳輸 / a break in transmission (= of a radio or television broadcast) due to a technical fault 技術故障造成的播送中斷 / the fax machine and other forms of electronic data transmission 傳真機及其他形式的電子資料傳輸 / An extra copy of each document was supplied for onward transmission to head office.（onward為英式用法）每份檔案都備了一份副本傳給總部。/ data, information, voice transmission 資料傳輸；資訊傳輸；音訊傳輸 / facsimile, fax transmission 傳真傳送 / light, power transmission 光透射；電力輸送 / money transmission（尤英）匯款 / cable, radio, satellite, television transmission 有線傳輸；無線電傳輸；衛星傳輸；電視傳輸 / analogue/analog, digital, electronic, wireless transmission 模擬傳輸；數字傳輸；電子傳輸；無線傳輸 / live transmission 現場直播 / There will be simultaneous transmission of the concert on TV and radio. 電視和收音機將同步轉播這場音樂會。/ transmission equipment, transmission technology 轉播裝置；轉播技術 / transmission by satellite 衛星傳送 <span class="definition">2 [C]（電臺或電視播送的）節目：</span>radio, television, TV transmission 廣播節目播放；電視節目播放 / satellite transmission 衛星轉播 / test transmission 測試播送 / a live transmission from Sydney 來自悉尼的現場直播

<span class="vocabulary">**air**</span> [eə] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. [尤美] 播送廣播或電視節目；節目被播送：</span>to air a documentary 播放一部紀錄片

<span class="vocabulary">**pipe**</span> [paɪp] 
<span class="definition">vt. 常用被動式，用線纜傳輸（聲音或訊號）：</span>The speech was piped over a public address system. 講話經廣播系統傳送出去。

<span class="vocabulary">**broadcast**</span> ['brɔ:dkɑːst] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 播送（電視或廣播節目）：</span>The concert will be broadcast live tomorrow evening. 音樂會將於明晚現場直播。/ We will broadcast live from the ship. 我們將從船上做實況直播。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**beam**</span> [bi:m]
<span class="definition">vt. 用電子裝置長距離發射廣播或電視訊號，如播送：</span>Live pictures of the ceremony were beamed around the world. 典禮經實況轉播傳到世界各地。/ The interview was beamed live across America. 這次訪談向全美國現場直播。/ The live satellite broadcast was beamed into homes across America. 此次衛星直播被髮送到美國的千家萬戶。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**syndicate**</span> [ˈsɪndɪkət]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 把（電視節目等）出售給多個媒體：</span>Today his programme is syndicated to 500 stations. 今天他的節目將在 500 多家電視臺播出。
