☀ <span class="category">**哭著說 笑著說：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**sob**</span> [sɒb] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. sob sth (out) 哭泣的同時說某事，即哭訴，抽噎著說：</span>‘I hate him,’ she sobbed. “我恨他。”她抽噎著說。/ He sobbed out his troubles. 他哭著訴說了自己的煩惱。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**whimper**</span> [ˈwɪmpə(r)]
<span class="definition">vi. 發出低弱哭聲的同時說，即抽抽搭搭地說：</span>‘Don't leave me alone, ’ he whimpered. “別丟下我不管。”他嗚咽著說。/ 'Let me go,' she whimpered. 'You're hurting me.' “放開我，”她抽泣道，“你弄痛我了。”/ She whimpered something inaudible. 她嗚咽著說了些什麼，聽不清。

<span class="vocabulary">**smile**</span> [smaɪl] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 微笑地說（或表達）：</span>She smiled her thanks. 她笑了笑表示感謝。/ ‘Perfect,’ he smiled. “好極了。”他微笑著說。
