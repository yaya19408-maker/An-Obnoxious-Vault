☀ <span class="category">**有爭議的：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**controversial**</span> [͵kɒntrə'və:ʃl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 引起很多氣憤的公共討論和反對意見的，即引起爭議的，有爭議的：</span>a highly controversial topic 頗有爭議的話題 / a controversial figure 有爭議的人物 / a politically controversial plan to build a new road 政治上有爭議的築路計劃
           
<span class="vocabulary">**contentious**</span> [kənˈtenʃəs]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 [正式] 很可能引起爭論的、有爭議的：</span>contentious topic, contentious subject 有爭議的話題；有爭議的主題 / Both views are highly contentious. 兩種觀點都很有爭議。/ Abortion is a highly contentious issue. 墮胎是一個非常有爭議的話題。/ a country where land prices are politically contentious 因土地價格引發政治爭議的國家 <span class="definition">2 [正式] 愛爭論的；充滿爭論的：</span>a contentious meeting 爭論不休的會議 / Rodney was a cheerful, elegant and gregarious if rather contentious man. 羅德尼儘管非常喜歡與人爭辯，卻是個陽光、優雅、合群的人。

<span class="vocabulary">**disputable**</span> [dɪˈspju:təbl]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 可質疑的；有爭辯餘地的；可商榷的：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**debatable**</span> [dɪˈbeɪtəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. 有爭議的、可爭辯的、未定的：</span>a highly debatable point 很有爭議的觀點 / It is highly debatable whether conditions have improved for low-income families. 低收入家庭的生活狀況是否已得到改善是一個頗有爭議的問題。/ Whether we can stay in this situation is debatable. 在這種情況下我們能否留下還很難說。/ It is debatable whether or not antibiotics would make any difference. 抗生素能否起到什麼作用還無法定論。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**divisive**</span> [dɪˈvaɪsɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. [貶] 使人們分裂成意見不同的群體的：</span>He believes that unemployment is socially divisive and is leading to the creation of an underclass. 他認為失業會造成社會分化，導致貧困階層的產生。/ A referendum would be divisive. 公投將產生分歧。/ Abortion has always been a divisive issue. 人工流產一直是個引發爭議的問題。/ to be politically divisive, to be racially divisive 政治上分裂；種族上分化
