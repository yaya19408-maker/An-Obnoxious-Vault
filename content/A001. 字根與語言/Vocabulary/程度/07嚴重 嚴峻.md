☀ <span class="category">**嚴重、嚴峻的形容詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**serious**</span> ['sɪərɪəs] 
<span class="definition">adj.（問題或情況）糟糕且使人擔心或害怕的，即嚴重的，危急的：</span>a serious problem 嚴重的問題 / a serious difficulty 嚴重的困難 / a serious illness 嚴重的疾病 / a serious offence 嚴重的罪行 / to cause serious injury 導致重傷 / to cause serious damage 造成嚴重破壞 / to pose a serious threat to public security 對公共安全構成嚴重威脅 / The consequences would be very serious. 後果將會很嚴重。/ a serious allegation 嚴重的指控 / This situation could be very serious for her. 情況對她十分不利。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**critical**</span> [ˈkrɪtɪkl]
<span class="definition">adj.（局勢、病情等）嚴重的；不確定的且可能有危險的，如危急的：</span>a critical moment in our country's history 我國歷史上的危急關頭 / The German authorities are considering an airlift if the situation becomes critical. 如果局勢變得危急，德國當局將考慮實施空運。/ Its day-to-day finances are in a critical state. 其日常財務狀況十分糟糕。/ The first 24 hours after the operation are the most critical. 病人手術後頭24小時是最危險的。/ Ten of the injured are said to be in critical condition. 據說傷者中有10人情況危急。/ One of the victims of the fire remains in a critical condition. 大火的一位受害者依然病情危急。

<span class="vocabulary">**severe**</span> [sɪ'vɪə] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 極為惡劣的，十分嚴重的。嚴重的程度較深：</span>severe weather conditions 惡劣的天氣狀況 / severe cash flow problems 嚴重的現金流問題 / a severe winter 嚴冬 / to suffer severe losses 遭受嚴重損失 / His injuries are severe. 他的傷勢很重。/ Shortages of professional staff are very severe. 專業人員短缺問題十分嚴重。/ an exceptionally severe frost 特別嚴重的霜凍 <span class="definition">2 極其困難的，需要很強技能或能力的：</span>to be a severe test of stamina 對耐力的嚴峻考驗
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fearful**</span> [ˈfɪəfl; 美 ˈfɪrfl]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] [只用於名詞前]（形勢）嚴重的：</span>The region is in a fearful recession. 該地區處於嚴重的經濟衰退中。
       
<span class="vocabulary">**horrific**</span> [həˈrɪfɪk]
<span class="definition">adj.（事故、傷勢等）十分嚴重的：</span>Her injuries were horrific. 她的傷勢極為嚴重。

<span class="vocabulary">**dreadful**</span> [ˈdredfl]
<span class="definition">adj. [尤英] [常用於名詞前] 嚴重的：</span>They suffered dreadful injuries. 他們嚴重受傷。

<span class="vocabulary">**stern**</span> [stɜ:n; 美 stɜ:rn]
<span class="definition">adj. 嚴峻的、難對付的：</span>We face stern opposition. 我們遇到激烈的反對。

<span class="vocabulary">**gross**</span> [grəʊs; 美 groʊs]
<span class="definition">adj. [只用於名詞前] [法律]（罪行等）非常嚴重的且不可接受的：</span>gross indecency, gross negligence, gross misconduct 嚴重猥褻；嚴重過失；嚴重瀆職 / a gross violation of human rights 嚴重侵犯人權 / The company were guilty of gross negligence. 該公司犯有嚴重過失。/ an act of gross injustice 極端不公的法案          
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dire**</span> [ˈdaɪə(r)]
<span class="definition">adj. [常用於名詞前] [正式] 極其嚴重的：</span>living in dire poverty 生活赤貧 / dire warnings, dire threats 嚴重的警告；嚴重的威脅 / dire predicament, dire peril, dire shortage 嚴重的困境；巨大的危險；極度的短缺 / Such action may have dire consequences. 這種行為可能產生嚴重後果。/ A government split would have dire consequences for domestic peace. 政府的分裂將會給國內和平帶來嚴重後果。/ He was in dire need of hospital treatment. 他急需入院治療。/ We're in dire need of your help. 我們急需你的幫助。/ The firm is in dire straits (= in a very difficult situation) and may go bankrupt. 這家公司已陷入極度困境之中，可能會破產。

<span class="vocabulary">**nasty**</span> [ˈnɑ:sti; 美 ˈnæsti]
<span class="definition">adj. 嚴重的：</span>a nasty accident 嚴重事故 / an extremely nasty murder 令人髮指的謀殺 / a nasty injury 重傷 / My little granddaughter caught her heel in the spokes of her bicycle — it was a very nasty wound. 我小孫女的腳跟夾到腳踏車的輪輻裡了，傷得很嚴重。/ Lili had a nasty chest infection. 莉莉胸部感染很嚴重。

<span class="vocabulary">**acute**</span> [ə'kju:t] 
<span class="definition">adj. 指情況十分嚴重的，嚴峻的。與severe類似，嚴重程度較深，二者未見明顯差別：</span>an acute economic crisis 嚴峻的經濟危機 / an acute shortage of oil 石油的嚴重短缺 / the acute problems of environmental pollution 嚴重的環境汙染問題
           
<span class="vocabulary">**profound**</span> [prəˈfaʊnd]
<span class="definition">adj. 嚴重的：</span>profound disability 嚴重殘疾 / profound disagreement 嚴重的分歧
           
<span class="vocabulary">**massive**</span> [ˈmæsɪv]
<span class="definition">adj.（疾病）十分嚴重的：</span>He suffered a massive heart attack. 他的心臟病嚴重發作了。

<span class="vocabulary">**terrible**</span> ['terəbl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 造成極大傷害的；傷害非常嚴重的：</span>a terrible accident 重大事故 / He had suffered terrible injuries. 他受了重傷。<span class="definition">2 只用於名詞前，用於表示某事物糟糕的程度極深的，也可稱為嚴重的：</span>terrible mistake 嚴重的錯誤 
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**appalling**</span> [əˈpɔ:lɪŋ]
<span class="definition">adj. [非正式] 極為嚴重的：</span>I developed an appalling headache. 我頭痛欲裂。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**grave**</span> [greɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式]（形勢、感情等）非常嚴重且重大的、讓人擔憂的，嚴峻的、深切的：</span>The police have expressed grave concern about the missing child's safety. 警方對失蹤孩子的安全深表關注。/ The consequences will be very grave if nothing is done. 如果不採取任何措施後果將會是非常嚴重的。/ We were in grave danger. 我們處於極大的危險之中。/ He said that the situation in his country is very grave. 他說他的國家情況十分危急。/ I have grave doubts that the documents tell the whole story. 我對這些檔案能否說明所有的問題深表懷疑。

<span class="vocabulary">**heavy**</span> ['hevɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj.（在數量、程度等方面）超出一般水平的，通常指比一般情況更糟的，如嚴重的：</span>heavy frost 嚴重霜凍

<span class="vocabulary">**desperate**</span> ['despərɪt] 
<span class="definition">adj.（一種情況）極其嚴重的，或極其危險的：</span>The children are in desperate need of love and attention. 這些孩子非常需要愛心和關懷。/ a desperate shortage of clean water 潔淨水的嚴重短缺

<span class="vocabulary">**extreme**</span> [ɪk'stri:m] 
<span class="definition">adj. 不尋常的；嚴重的。常翻譯為“極端的”，程度在本組幾乎最深：</span>in extreme circumstances 在極端情況下 / Don’t go doing anything extreme like leaving the country. 千萬不要做出諸如離開國家之類的極端行為。/ a extreme example 極端事例 / extreme weather conditions 極端天氣狀況

<span class="vocabulary">**bad**</span> [bæd] 
<span class="definition">adj. 嚴重的、劇烈的。是一個簡單用詞：</span>You’re heading for a bad attack of sunburn. 你會被嚴重曬傷的。/ The engagement was a bad mistake. 這婚約是一項大錯。/ My headache is getting worse. 我頭痛越來越厲害了。

<span class="vocabulary">**deep**</span> [di:p] 
<span class="definition">adj. 嚴重的：</span>a deep economic recession 嚴重的經濟衰退 / deep divisions within the party 黨內的嚴重分歧

<span class="vocabulary">**big**</span> [bɪɡ] 
<span class="definition">adj. 嚴重的。算是一種非正式用法：</span>a big mistake 嚴重的錯誤 / Her problem was just too big for her to tackle on her own. 她的問題太嚴重了，她無法獨力應付。

<span class="vocabulary">**large**</span> [lɑːdӡ] 
<span class="definition">adj.（正被討論的問題）嚴重的：</span>the already large problem of under-age drinking 已頗為嚴重的未成年人酗酒問題 

<span class="vocabulary">**major**</span> ['meɪdӡə] 
<span class="definition">adj. [美] 只作表語，指嚴重的：</span>Never mind--it’s not major. 別擔心——這不嚴重。

☀ <span class="category">**嚴重的名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**severity**</span> [sɪˈverəti]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 嚴重，嚴重性：</span>A prison sentence should match the severity of the crime. 刑期長短要和罪行輕重一致。/ The sentence passed today reflects the severity of the crime. 今天透過的判決表明罪行十分嚴重。/ The chances of a full recovery will depend on the severity of her injuries. 能否徹底康復取決於她受傷的嚴重程度。/ The medicine can reduce the severity of symptoms. 這種藥能減輕症狀。/ the severity of the problem 問題的嚴重性 / He frowned with mock severity. 他沉下臉來，裝出一副嚴厲的樣子。/ She wagged her finger with mock severity. 她故作嚴厲地擺了擺手指。/ The elaborate facade contrasts strongly with the severity of the interior. 精緻的門面同室內的簡樸形成強烈反差。           

<span class="vocabulary">**seriousness**</span> [ˈsɪəriəsnəs; 美 ˈsɪr-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.] 嚴重：</span>Only later did he realize the full seriousness of his actions. 他後來才意識到自已行為的嚴重性。The sentence imposed in this case demonstrates the seriousness of environmental crimes. 對該案的判決體現出破壞環境罪行的嚴重性。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**enormity**</span> [ɪˈnɔ:məti; 美 ɪˈnɔ:rm-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] the ~ of sth（問題、罪行等的）嚴重性：</span>It makes no sense to belittle the enormity of the disaster which has occurred. 對於已經發生的災難，其嚴重性不該小視。/ People are still coming to terms with the enormity of the disaster. 人們仍在力圖接受這一災難帶來的嚴重惡果。/ The full enormity of the crime has not yet been revealed. 這一罪行的嚴重性還沒有充分揭示出來。/ It took time for the full enormity of the attack to sink in.（full尤為英式用法）需要一定的時間人們才能充分認識到這場襲擊的嚴重惡果。/ It's difficult to grasp the sheer enormity of the tragedy. 很難真正理解這場悲劇的嚴重程度。Her words brought home the enormity of what was happening. 她的話讓人意識到所發生事情的嚴重性。

<span class="vocabulary">**depth**</span> [depθ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U]（情況的）嚴重程度：</span>the depth of the crisis 此次危機的嚴重程度

<span class="vocabulary">**gravity**</span> ['ɡrævɪtɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式]（情況、事態或事件等的）極端的嚴重性，（該嚴重性會引起擔憂）相當於seriousness：</span>the gravity of the situation 情況的嚴重性 / the gravity of the offence 罪行的嚴重程度
