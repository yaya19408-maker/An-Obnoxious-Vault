☀ <span class="category">**不聽 聽不見 失聰：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**lose**</span> [lu:z] 
<span class="definition">vt. 聽不見（某事）：</span>His words were lost in the applause. 他的講話讓掌聲淹沒了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**inaudible**</span> [ɪnˈɔ:dəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (to sb)（聲音）聽不見的：</span>The whistle was inaudible to the human ear. 這種嘯聲人耳聽不到。/ His voice was almost inaudible. 他的話音低得幾乎聽不見。/ The sound is inaudible to the human ear. 這種聲音人耳是聽不見的。/ Animals are able to hear high-pitched sounds that are inaudible to humans. 動物可以聽到人類所聽不到的高頻聲音。/ His voice faded until it was almost inaudible. 他的聲音越來越弱，直到幾乎聽不見。

周邊詞彙補充：
· deaf可表示生理上耳聾的、失聰的。也可表示不願意聽或不願意去注意的，即充耳不聞的。
