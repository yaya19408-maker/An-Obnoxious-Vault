☀ <span class="category">**聽起來 聽上去：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**sound**</span> [saʊnd] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the sound of sth 透過聽或看（從…處）所得到的感覺、印象：</span>They’ll lose the match. I don’t like the sound of that. 他們將輸掉這場比賽。這訊息不大好哇。/ He is a good man by the sound of it. 聽起來他是一個好人。<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 聽起來，聽起來像，聽起來讓人覺得…：</span>She sounded a bit worried. 她聽起來有點焦慮不安。/ It sounds like a good idea to me, does it really work? 在我聽來那像個好主意，但真管用嗎？<span class="definition">adj. -sounding 用於構成形容詞，指聽起來…的：</span>fine-sounding words 聽上去華而不實的話語 / an England-sounding name 聽著像英國人的名字
