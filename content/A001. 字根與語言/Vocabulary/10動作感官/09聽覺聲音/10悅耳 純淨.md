☀ <span class="category">**悅耳 純淨：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**sweet**</span> [swi:t] 
<span class="definition">adj.（聲音）悅耳的，好聽的：</span>a sweet voice 甜潤的嗓音

<span class="vocabulary">**smooth**</span> [smu:ð] 
<span class="definition">adj.（嗓音或音樂）悅耳的，圓潤的，沒有任何粗糙感或令人不悅的聲音的：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**soft**</span> [sɒft] 
<span class="definition">adj.（聲音）輕的，輕柔的（常是悅耳的），相當於quiet：</span>soft background music 輕柔的背景音樂 / a soft voice 柔和悅耳的嗓音

<span class="vocabulary">**musical**</span> ['mju:zɪkl] 
<span class="definition">adj.（聲音）悅耳的，音樂般的：</span>She has a musical voice. 她有著悅耳的嗓音。
             
<span class="vocabulary">**mellow**</span> [ˈmeləʊ; 美 -loʊ]
<span class="definition">adj.（聲音）柔和悅耳的：</span>the mellow background music 舒緩的背景音樂 / His voice was deep and mellow and his speech had a soothing and comforting quality. 他的嗓音低沉悅耳，他的講話能撫慰人心。         

<span class="vocabulary">**supple**</span> [ˈsʌpl]
<span class="definition">adj. 聲音悅耳的：</span>the haunting didgeridoo, the teasing keyboards and the supple bassline 縈繞心頭的迪吉里杜管奏出的曲調，戲謔挑逗的電子鍵盤聲和低沉悅耳的貝司音樂

<span class="vocabulary">**crisp**</span> [krɪsp]
<span class="definition">adj.（聲音）清脆悅耳的：</span>The recording sounds very crisp, considering its age. 考慮到這錄音已年代久遠，聽起來聲音還是挺清楚的。

<span class="vocabulary">**liquid**</span> ['lɪkwɪd] 
<span class="definition">adj. [文學]（聲音）清脆的，純淨的，流暢的：</span>the liquid song of a blackbird 烏鶇清脆的叫聲
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fluid**</span> [ˈflu:ɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式]（音樂）流暢優美的：</span>fluid guitar playing 流暢優美的吉他演奏

<span class="vocabulary">**pure**</span> [pjʊə] 
<span class="definition">adj.（聲音）非常清晰的、純正的、完美的：</span>a pure voice 純淨的嗓音
           
<span class="vocabulary">**breezy**</span> [ˈbri:zi]
<span class="definition">adj.（音樂）明快的：</span>This album is bright, breezy and playful. 這張專輯音樂輕鬆明快，趣味十足。
