☀ <span class="category">**著色 上色：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**colour**</span> ['kʌlə]（美color）
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（用顏料、彩色鉛筆等）為…著色。是將名詞用作動詞：</span>a colouring book 塗色畫冊 / He drew a monster and coloured it green. 他畫了一個怪物，把它塗成綠色。/ to colour your hair 染髮
           
<span class="vocabulary">**tinge**</span> [tɪndʒ]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] ~ sth (with sth)（輕微地）給…著色，給…染色：</span>white petals tinged with blue 略帶藍色的白花瓣
           
<span class="vocabulary">**tint**</span> [tɪnt]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] ~ sth (with sth) 為某事物輕微染色；給某事物略微著色：</span>Eyebrows can be tinted with the same dye. 眉毛可以染成同樣的顏色。/ Glass bottles are filled with water tinted with food colourings. 玻璃瓶中盛滿了被食用色素染過色的水。

<span class="vocabulary">**stain**</span> [steɪn] 
<span class="definition">vt. 用液體著色劑給…著色（或染色）：</span>They stained the floors dark brown. 他們把地板塗成了深棕色。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dye**</span> [daɪ]
<span class="definition">vt. 用染料給…染色：</span>to dye fabric 給織物染色 / The women prepared, spun and dyed the wool. 女人們處理羊毛，紡羊毛並給羊毛染色。/ She had dyed black hair. 她將她的黑頭髮染了色。/ She dyed her hair blonde. 她把頭髮染成了金黃色。

<span class="vocabulary">**flood**</span> [flʌd] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 指顏色突然傳遞到某處，或覆蓋…：</span>The colour flooded her cheeks. 她雙頰刷的一下紅了。

周邊詞彙補充：
· tint可指染髮。
