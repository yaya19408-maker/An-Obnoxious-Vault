☀ <span class="category">**窺視 偷看：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**peek**</span> [pi:k]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (at sth) 窺視；偷看：</span>No peeking! 禁止窺探！/ I couldn't resist peeking in the drawer. 我不由得偷看了一下抽屜裡面。/ He peeked out from behind the door. 他從門後面偷看。/ She peeked at the audience from behind the curtain. 她從帷幕後面窺視了一下觀眾。/ On two occasions she had peeked at him through a crack in the wall. 她曾兩次透過牆縫窺視他。/ The child peeked through a crack in the door. 這個孩子透過門縫愉看。/ Daniel peeked cautiously over his shoulder. 丹尼爾小心翼翼地轉過頭偷看了一下。/ I was tempted to peek around the corner. 我很想躲在拐角處偷看一下。/ She peeked at the clock to see the time. 她偷看了一下時鐘上的時間。/ I wandered around the house, peeking into various rooms. 我在房子周圍徘徊，窺視各個房間。<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 窺視：</span>I took a quick peek inside. 我匆匆向裡面偷看了一眼。/ a sneak peek at the new cartoon 瞄了一眼新漫畫 / Brooke couldn't help but sneak a peek at him. 布魯克忍不住偷偷地看了他一下。/ Go ahead and take a peek at your presents. 去吧，去瞄一眼給你的禮物。/ a fascinating peek behind the scenes of the Oprah Winfrey Show 對奧普拉·溫弗瑞秀的幕後的津津有味的一瞥 / We got a peek into rooms that are normally closed to the public. 我們偷看了一下通常不對公眾開放的房間。/ I dared a little peek through a crack in the door. 我壯著膽子透過門縫窺視了一下。

<span class="vocabulary">**peep**</span> [pi:p]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (at sth)（尤指透過小孔）窺視，偷看：</span>We caught her peeping through the keyhole. 她從鎖孔偷看時，被我們撞著了。/ The child peeped through a crack in the door. 小孩子透過門上的縫隙偷看。/ He was peeping at her through his fingers. 他從指縫偷看她。/ Could I just peep inside? 我能不能看一眼裡邊？/ Children came to peep at him round the doorway. 孩子們圍在門口偷看他。/ He peeped out cautiously from behind the door. 他小心翼翼地從門後往外偷看。/ Now and then she peeped to see if he was noticing her. 她時不時地偷眼看他是否在注意自己。/ She was tempted to peep at the letter. 她忍不住偷看那封信。<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] 偷偷一瞥：</span>Dave took a quick peep at the last page. 戴夫迅速地瞟了一下最後一頁。/ I noticed him take a little peep at his watch. 我注意到他瞥了一眼他的表。/ The movie gives us a peep behind the curtain at a Broadway musical. 這部電影讓我們得以一窺百老匯音樂劇的幕後情況。/ a peep into the private life of a world leader一窺世界領導人的私人生活 / I took a peep through the keyhole. 我從鑰匙孔偷看了一眼。
