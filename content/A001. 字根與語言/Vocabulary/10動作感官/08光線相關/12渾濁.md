☀ <span class="category">**渾濁：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**cloudy**</span> ['klaʊdɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. 指液體不清澈的、不透明的，即渾濁的：</span>the cloudy water 渾濁的水 / The beer looked cloudy. 啤酒看起來渾濁不清。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**opaque**</span> [əʊˈpeɪk; 美 oʊ-]
<span class="definition">adj.（玻璃、液體等）不透明的、不透光的、渾濁的：</span>opaque glass 不透明的玻璃 / opaque tights 不透明的連褲襪 / As the lens becomes more opaque, the patient notices a falling off in vision. 隨著晶狀體變得更渾濁，病人會感到視力減弱。/ These crystals are completely opaque and nearly black. 這些水晶一點兒也不透明，幾乎是黑的。/ The glasses looked slightly opaque. 這副眼鏡看起來有點兒模糊。/ The windows were nearly opaque with grime. 窗戶滿是汙垢，幾乎不透光。/ Add the rice to the pan and cook until it becomes opaque. 把米倒進鍋裡，煮至透明。

周邊詞彙補充：
· muddy可指（一種液體）含泥的，不乾淨的，渾濁的。
