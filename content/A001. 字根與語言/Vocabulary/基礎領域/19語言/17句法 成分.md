☀ <span class="category">**句法 成分：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**subject**</span> ['sʌbdӡɪkt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 主語：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**subjective**</span> [səb'dӡektɪv] 
<span class="definition">adj. 只用於名詞前，主語的，主格的：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**object**</span> ['ɒbdʒɪkt, -dʒekt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 賓語（直接賓語和間接賓語）：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**objective**</span> [əbˈdʒektɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. [只用於名詞前]（語法上）賓格的：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**complement**</span> [ˈkɒmplɪment; 美 ˈkɑ:m-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [語法] 補足語、補語：</span>In the sentences ‘I'm angry’ and ‘He became a politician’, ‘angry’ and ‘politician’ are complements. 在句子I 'm angry和He became a politician中，angry和politician為補語。

<span class="vocabulary">**relative**</span> ['relətɪv] 
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 指代前面的一個名詞、句子或句子的一部分的：</span>In ‘the man who came’, ‘who’ is a relative pronoun and ‘who came’ is a relative clause. 在the man who came中，who是關係代詞，而who came是關係從句。

<span class="vocabulary">**front**</span> [frʌnt] 
<span class="definition">vt.（為強調而將句子的某一部分）前置：</span>瞭解即可
          
<span class="vocabulary">**embed**</span> [ɪmˈbed]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 語言學中可指在一個句子中嵌入另一個句子。（在I'm aware that she knows句中，she knows為內嵌句）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**imperative**</span> [ɪmˈperətɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. [只用於名詞前] [語法] 表示命令的、祈使的：</span>an imperative sentence 祈使句 <span class="definition">n. [C] [語法] 祈使語氣；表示祈使語氣的動詞：</span>In ‘Go away!’ the verb is in the imperative. Go away!中的動詞是祈使語氣。/ ‘Go away!’ is an imperative. Go away! 是祈使句。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**indicative**</span> [ɪnˈdɪkətɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. [只用於名詞前]（語法）陳述的；指示的：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">n. [sing.]（語法）the indicative可指陳述語氣的動詞形式：</span>In ‘Ben likes school’, the verb ‘like’ is in the indicative. 在Ben likes school中，動詞like是陳述語氣。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**clause**</span> [klɔ:z]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [語法] 從句：</span>main, subordinate clause 主句；從句 / In the sentence ‘They often go to Italy because they love the food’, ‘They often go to Italy’ is the main clause and ‘because they love the food’ is a subordinate clause. 在They often go to Italy because they love the food這個句子裡，They often go to Italy是主句，because they love the food是從句。/ conditional, dependent, finite, independent, infinitive, non-finite, relative clause 條件從句；從屬從句；限定從句；獨立從句；非限定從句；關係從句 / non-restrictive, restrictive clause 非限定性從句；限定性從句 / a restrictive relative clause 限定性關係從句 / adverbial, nominal, noun clause 狀語從句；名詞性從句；名詞從句 / The completed action is in the main clause. 完成的動作包含在主句中。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**parenthesis**</span> [pəˈrenθəsɪs]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 插入語：</span>After a long parenthesis, she returned to the matter in hand. 插了很長一段話後，她重新做手頭的事。/ I add, in parenthesis, that I doubt whether such a place exists. 我補充一句、我懷疑這樣一個地方是否存在。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**tag**</span> [tæg]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [語言學] 附加語（為加強語氣，如Yes, I do一句中的I do）：</span>瞭解即可

周邊詞彙補充：
· complex可指（單詞或句子）複合的；
· restrictive可指（關係從句）限制性的。
