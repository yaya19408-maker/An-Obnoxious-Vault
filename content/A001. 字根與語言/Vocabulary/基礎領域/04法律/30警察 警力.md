☀ <span class="category">**警察 警力：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**force**</span> [fɔ:s] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 武力，肢體力量：</span>The release of the hostages could not be achieved without the use of force. 不使用武力不可能使人質獲釋。/ The regulations allow the use of force if necessary. 法規允許在必要時使用武力。/ The rioters were taken away by force. 聚眾鬧事者被強行帶走。<span class="definition">2 [C] the force 為單數名詞，在英式英語裡可指警察部門：</span>He joined the force twenty years ago. 他二十年前加入了警隊。/ police force 警察隊伍
           
<span class="vocabulary">**squad**</span> [skwɒd; 美 skwɑ:d]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（專門處理某類犯罪的）警察分隊：</span>anti-terrorist（尤英）, bomb, bomb-disposal（英）, crime（尤英）, drug（英）, drugs（英）, flying（英）, fraud（英）, murder（英）, police, riot, vice squad 反恐小組；爆破小組；拆彈小組；刑警隊；緝毒隊；快速特警隊；反欺詐小組；兇殺案專案組；警察小分隊；防暴隊；掃黃查賭緝捕隊 / The serious crime squad have taken over the investigation.（英）這項調查已由重案組接管。/ an early-morning raid by a police squad 警察小分隊進行的一次清晨突擊搜查 / squad detective, squad officer（均英）小隊探員；小隊警員 / a murder squad detective 兇殺案小組的偵探 / squad car 巡邏警車 / The building was evacuated and the bomb squad called. 大樓裡進行了人員疏散，並叫來了拆彈小組。/ The club is under investigation by the fraud squad. 反詐騙調查組正在對該俱樂部進行調查。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**task force**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [C]（警隊中臨時組建並被派往某地以處理危機的）小型特遣隊：</span>police task force 警察特遣隊等 / drug, terrorism task force 緝毒特別小組；反恐特別小組 / anti-drug, anti-terrorism task force 禁毒專門小組；反恐專門小組

<span class="vocabulary">**contingent**</span> [kənˈtɪndʒənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（警隊的）分隊：</span>police contingent 警察小分隊
 
<span class="vocabulary">**police**</span> [pə'li:s] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl]（通常用the police）警察部門，是一種警務組織，也可叫警方；還可指在警察局工作的一個人，即警察。是此義的基礎通用詞：</span>I’ll call the police. 我要叫警察了。/ armed, mounted police 武裝警察；騎警 / plainclothes police 便衣警察 / undercover police 臥底警察 / riot, traffic police 防爆警察；交通警察 / military, secret police 軍事警察（即憲兵）；秘密警察 / airport police 機場警察 / thought police（比喻用法）思想警察 / police detective, police officer, police official 警探；警官 / police spokesman 警方發言人 / police station 派出所 / police car 警車 / police dog 警犬 / police investigation 警方調查 / police interrogation 警方審問 

<span class="vocabulary">**policeman/policewoman**</span> [pə'li:smən, -wʊmən] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 男警察或女警察：</span>senior policeman 高階警察 / ordinary policeman 普通警察 / local policeman 地方警察 / uniformed policeman 穿制服的警察 / plainclothes, undercover policeman 便衣警察；臥底警察 / armed policeman 武裝警察 / military policeman 憲兵 / traffic policeman 交通警察 / border policeman 邊防警察 / off-duty policeman 休班警察 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**constable**</span> [ˈkʌnstəbl; 美 ˈkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（英國等國家中最低階別的）警察、警員，相當於police constable：</span>police constable 警員 / Chief, Detective constable 警察局長；警探 / uniformed constable 穿制服的警察 / The force hopes to increase the number of its beat constables. 警隊希望增加片區巡警的數量。/ Special constables provide part-time assistance for the regular police force. 特別警察部分時間為正規警察部隊提供協助。/ the rank of constable 警察的級別 / The constable had reasonable grounds for arresting her. 警察有合理的理由逮捕她。/ He was a constable at Sutton police station. 他是薩頓警察局的警員。/ Constable Stuart Clark 斯圖爾特·克拉克警員 / Have you finished your report, Constable? 警察，你的報告完成了嗎？<span class="definition">2 [C]（美國城鎮中參與維持秩序、級別低於sheriff的）巡官：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**P.C.**</span> 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（英式英語）Police Constable的縮寫，表示級別最低的警務人員，即警察；警員：</span>PC Tom Marsh 警員湯姆·馬什
           
<span class="vocabulary">**cop**</span> [kɒp; 美 kɑ:p]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 警察：</span>Somebody call the cops! 去個人報警啊！/ children playing cops and robbers 在玩警察抓壞蛋遊戲的孩子們 / a TV cop show 電視警察節目 / Frank didn't like having the cops know where to find him. 弗蘭克不想讓警察知道去哪裡可以找到他。/ traffic cop 交通警察
           
<span class="vocabulary">**copper**</span> [ˈkɒpə(r); 美 ˈkɑ:p-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [英，非正式] 警察：</span>your friendly neighbourhood copper 你們友善的社群警察

<span class="vocabulary">**super**</span> ['sju:pə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [英，非正式]（英國的）警官；（美國的）警長：</span>瞭解即可 

<span class="vocabulary">**officer**</span> ['ɒfɪsə] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 常用作稱謂，相當於police officer。既可表示“警官”（是“警官”的基礎通用詞），也可表示“警察”：</span>the officer in charge of the case 負責此案的警察 / Yes, officer, I saw what happened. 是，警察先生，我看到了發生的事。/ law enforcement, police officer 執法官員；警官 / arresting, investigating officer 負責逮捕工作的警員；負責調查工作的警員 / the duty officer 值班警察 / plain-clothes officer 便衣警察 / undercover officer 秘密警察 / Officers raided an address in the Pittsburgh area. 警方突然搜查匹茲堡某處。<span class="definition">2 [C] 美式英語中可指警察的頭銜：</span>Officer Tom 湯姆警官
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**commander**</span> [kəˈmɑ:ndə(r); 美 -ˈmæn-]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（縮寫為Cdr）（英國的）倫敦高階警官：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**lieutenant**</span> [lefˈtenənt; 美 lu:ˈt-]
（縮寫為Lieut.或Lt）<span class="definition">n. [C]（美國警察的）級別較高的警官：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**commissioner**</span> [kəˈmɪʃənə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [美式英語中也可用police commissioner] 警察局長、警長：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**captain**</span> ['kæptɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 美國警察局的副巡官或美國消防署的中隊長，可以是頭銜：</span>a former police captain 一位前任警察局副巡官
            
<span class="vocabulary">**marshal**</span> [ˈmɑ:ʃl; 美 ˈmɑ:rʃl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一些美國城市的警察局長或消防局長：</span>A federal marshal was killed in a shoot-out. 槍戰中一名聯邦警察局局長身亡。/ a Cleveland county fire marshal 克利夫蘭縣的消防隊長          
           
<span class="vocabulary">**inspector**</span> [ɪnˈspektə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（縮寫為Insp）（英國警察中職位低於警司高於巡佐的）巡官、督察：</span>Last week gunmen attacked Mogadishu airport killing a police inspector. 上週持槍歹徒襲擊了摩加迪沙機場，殺死了一名警察巡官。/ I got on the phone to Inspector Joplin at Scotland Yard. 我與倫敦警察廳的喬普林巡官透過電話了。/ Chief, detective inspector（均英）總督察；探長 / He reached the rank of inspector. 他達到了巡官的級別。<span class="definition">2 [C]（美國警察中僅次於總警監的）督察長：</span>San Francisco police inspector Tony Camileri 舊金山警察局督察長託尼·卡米萊裡           

<span class="vocabulary">**sergeant**</span> [ˈsɑ:dʒənt; 美 ˈsɑ:rdʒ-]
（縮寫為Sergt, Sgt）<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（英國警察的）巡佐：</span>the unit headed by Sergeant Bell 由貝爾巡佐帶領的分隊 / police sergeant 巡佐 / detective sergeant（英國的）探長 / Detective Sergeant Peter Wiles 彼得·懷爾斯探長 / desk, duty 值班警長 / Visitors to the police station should report to the duty sergeant. 造訪警局者應當知會值班警長。/ custody sergeant（英國的）監獄守衛 <span class="definition">2 [C]（美國警察的）警佐：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**deputy**</span> [ˈdepjuti]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（在美國）協助地方治安官辦案的警官：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**rank**</span> [ræŋk] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] 警察等組織中某人所具有的級別，即警銜：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">2 [C] 站成行列的警察：</span>The police broke ranks and started hitting people with their batons. 警察散開隊形並開始用警棍打人。

<span class="vocabulary">**recruit**</span> [rɪˈkru:t]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 新警員：</span>police recruit 新警察

<span class="vocabulary">**entrant**</span> [ˈentrənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (to sth) 新警員：</span>new women entrants to the police force 新加入警察部隊的女警察

<span class="vocabulary">**cadet**</span> [kəˈdet]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 警校學員：</span>police cadet 警校學員
           
<span class="vocabulary">**reinforcement**</span> [ˌri:ɪnˈfɔ:smənt; 美 -ˈfɔ:rs-]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] reinforcements可指被派到某地的（警隊的）增援警力：</span>police reinforcement 增援警力 / The crowd was very large and police reinforcements were called in. 群眾人數太多，因此召集了警察來增援。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**presence**</span> [ˈprezns]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 被派到某地去處理事情的一群警察：</span>police presence 派駐的警察 / There was a strong police presence throughout the demonstration. 整個示威遊行期間都有一大批警察在場。/ The march went ahead in spite of the overwhelming police presence. 遊行隊伍不顧眾多警察在場繼續前進。/ The government is maintaining a heavy police presence in the area. 政府在這地區派駐了大批警察。
        
<span class="vocabulary">**patrol**</span> [pəˈtrəʊl; 美 pəˈtroʊl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 警察巡邏隊、巡邏車隊、巡邏船隊：</span>police patrol 警察巡邏隊 / patrol aircraft, patrol boat, patrol car, patrol craft, patrol vehicle, patrol vessel 巡邏機；巡邏船；巡邏車；巡邏艇 / Every police car and foot patrol in the area is on full alert. 該地區每輛警車和每名步行的巡警都處於高度警戒狀態。/ The highway patrol has sealed off the area. 公路巡警已經封鎖了該地區。

<span class="vocabulary">**coroner**</span> [ˈkɒrənə(r); 美 ˈkɔ:r-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 驗屍官：</span>The coroner issued a burial certificate. 驗屍官出具了入殮證明書。/ The coroner ordered an investigation into the man's death. 驗屍官要求對這個人的死亡進行調查。/ The coroner recorded a verdict of accidental death. 驗屍官得出了意外死亡的結論。/ a coroner's court, a coroner's inquest 死因調查法庭；驗屍 / a coroner's report, a coroner's verdict 驗屍報告；驗屍結論 / the coroner's office 驗屍處
