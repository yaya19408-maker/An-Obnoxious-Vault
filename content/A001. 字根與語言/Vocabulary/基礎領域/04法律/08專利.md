☀ <span class="category">**專利：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**patent**</span> ['peɪtnt, 'pætnt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 專利權；專利證書：</span>to be protected by patent 受專利保護 / Edison took out a patent on the light bulb. 愛迪生獲得了電燈泡的專利權。<span class="definition">adj. 1 與專利有關的，專利的：</span>patent applications 專利申請 / patent laws 專利法 / patent protection 專利權保護 / patent office 專利局 <span class="definition">2 指產品由某公司專利生產或專利經銷的：</span>patent medicines 專利藥品 / a patent product 專利產品 <span class="definition">vt. 獲得專利權：</span>The university has patented the invention. 那所大學已獲得了該項發明的專利。
