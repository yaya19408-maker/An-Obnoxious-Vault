☀ <span class="category">**法院 法庭：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**court**</span> [kɔ:t] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] 法院，法庭：</span>civil, criminal court 民事法庭；刑事法庭 / military court 軍事法庭 / circuit, district（均美）court 巡迴法院；地區法院 / Appeal Court, High Court（均為英格蘭和威爾士用法）上訴法院；高等法院 / higher, superior court 上級法院；高階法院 / supreme court 最高法院 / lower court 下級法院 / juvenile court 少年法庭 / divorce court 離婚法庭 / court clerk 法庭書記員 / court trial 庭審 / court summons 法庭傳票 / court decision, court ruling 法院判決；法院裁決 / court appearance 出庭 / court date 庭審日期 / court procedure 庭審程式 / court costs 訴訟費 / to go to court 打官司 / to take sb to court 把某人告上法庭 <span class="definition">2 [sing.] the court 可指全體出庭人員，尤指審判人員，如法官和陪審員：</span>Please tell the court what happened. 請向法庭陳述事件的經過。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**tribunal**</span> [traɪˈbju:nl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 處理某一問題或爭議的、帶有權威性的一種法庭：</span>independent tribunal 獨立法庭 / international, local（尤英） tribunal 國際法庭；地方法庭 / special tribunal 特別審判庭 / higher, highest tribunal 上級法庭；最高法庭 / inferior tribunal（英）下級法庭 / judicial（尤英）, military tribunal 審判庭；軍事法庭 / ad hoc, revolutionary tribunal 特設法庭；革命法庭 / criminal, war crimes tribunal 刑事法庭；戰爭罪行法庭 / expert tribunal（英）專家仲裁處 / employment, industrial, transport tribunal（均英） 勞工法庭；勞資法庭；運輸法庭 / appeal, arbitration, disciplinary tribunal（均英） 上訴法庭；仲裁法庭；紀律法庭 / medical tribunal（英）醫療法庭 / fact-finding tribunal 事實調查法庭 / A war crimes tribunal was set up to prosecute those charged with atrocities. 成立了一個戰爭罪行法庭來對那些被控犯有暴行的戰犯提起公訴。/ He could face a military tribunal. 他可能要面臨軍事法庭的審判。/ The tribunal sentenced him to twenty years in prison. 法庭判他二十年徒刑。/ He stood trial before an international tribunal. 他受到國際法庭的審訊。/ The case was referred to an industrial tribunal. 該案件被提交到勞資法庭。/ She told the tribunal that she was a victim of sex discrimination. 她向特別法庭陳述說自己是性別歧視的受害者。/ The tribunal found for her employers (= judged that they were right). 特別法庭作出了有利於她的僱主的裁定。/ tribunal decision 特別法庭的裁決 / tribunal hearing 特別法庭聽審 / tribunal procedure, tribunal proceedings 特別法庭訴訟程式 / tribunal chairman, tribunal member, tribunal panel 特別法庭審判長；特別法庭審判員；特別法庭審判組 / The fee for the player will be decided by tribunal. 那名球員的轉會費將由仲裁委員會裁定。/ She took her case to a tribunal. 她把案件提交特別法庭審理。/ His case comes before an industrial tribunal in March. 他的訟案將於三月在勞資法庭受審。

<span class="vocabulary">**box**</span> [bɒks] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 法庭中的專席（均與其他人的座位分開）：</span>jury, witness box 陪審團席；證人席
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dock**</span> [dɒk; 美 dɑ:k]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 法庭的被告席：</span>He's been in the dock (= on trial for a crime) several times already. 他已多次受審。/ She was in the dock on charges of attempted fraud. 她因被控詐騙未遂而受審。/ an outburst from the dock 被告的情緒激動 / The defendant stood in the dock. 被告站在被告席上。/ What about the odd chance that you do put an innocent man in the dock? 若是你真的把一個無辜的人送上被告席了呢？
