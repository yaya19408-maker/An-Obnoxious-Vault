☀ <span class="category">**栽贓 嫁禍：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**plant**</span> [plɑːnt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] [非正式] 用來栽贓的物品：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">vt. plant sth (on sb) 給某人栽贓，即把…（如武器、毒品等）放在某人處以使人們錯誤地認為此人犯了罪：</span>He claimed that the drugs had been planted to incriminate him. 他聲稱毒品是有人放在他那裡的，目的是要栽贓給他。
