☀ <span class="category">**資本 資金：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**asset**</span> [ˈæset]
<span class="definition">n. [usually pl.] 資產：</span>Her assets include shares in the company and a house in France. 她的財產包括公司的股份和在法國的一座房子。/ By the end of 1989 the group had assets of 3.5 billion francs. 到1989年底該集團擁有的資產達35億法郎。/ The company's net assets are worth millions. 這家公司的淨資產價值幾百萬。/ business, company, corporate asset 公司資產 / commercial, financial asset 商業資產；金融資產 / current, liquid, disposable asset 流動資產；可支配資產 / capital, fixed, fund, non-cash（尤英）, non-monetary, physical, property asset 資本資產；固定資產；資金資產；非現金資產；非貨幣資產；有形資產；不動產 / tangible, intangible asset 有形資產；無形資產 / They have hidden assets in banks around the world. 他們在世界各地的銀行都有隱蔽資產。/ overseas asset 海外資產 / personal asset 個人資產 / national, public, state asset 國有資產；公共財產；國家資產 / He transferred all his assets into his wife's name. 他把所有資產都轉移到了妻子的名下。/ to dispose asset 處理資產 / The courts can order a company's assets to be frozen. 法庭可以下令凍結公司的資產。/ to seize asset 沒收資產 / asset price, asset value 資產價格；資產價值 / asset management 資產管理 / asset sale 資產出售 / assets and liabilities 資產和負債
 
<span class="vocabulary">**capital**</span> ['kæpɪtl] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [sing.] 用來投資或創辦商業機構的一大筆錢，即資本；啟動資金：</span>risk, venture capital 風險資本 / start-up capital 啟動資金 / fixed capital 固定資本 / working capital 營運資本 / human, intellectual, political（均為比喻用法）capital 人力資本；智力資本；政治資本 / to raise capital 融資 <span class="definition">2 [U] 不包含利潤的那一部分資金，即本金，本錢：</span>capital and interest 本金和利息 <span class="definition">3 [U] 公司或個人所擁有的財富或財產（可指用於生產產品的建築或機械裝置）：</span>Our capital is all tied up in property. 我們的資金都套牢在房地產上無法動用。/ capital assets 資本資產 / equity capital 股本 / capital costs 資本成本 / capital accumulation, capital growth 資本積累；資本增值 / capital gain 資本收益 / a return on the capital 資本回報 / capital market 資本市場 / capital outflow 資本外流
           
<span class="vocabulary">**principal**</span> [ˈprɪnsəpl]
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] [金融] 借給某人（或投資）以賺取利息的一筆錢，即本金：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**finance**</span> ['faɪnæns] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] finance (for sth) 用於運營企業或專案、開展活動的資金（美式英語通常用financing）：</span>cheap finance 低息貸款 / private, public finance 私人資金；公共資金 / equity finance 股本融資 / housing, mortgage finance 住房貸款；按揭貸款 / the finance for education available to local government 當地政府可用的教育資金 / to provide finance for the project 為該專案提供資金 / a source of finance 資金來源 <span class="definition">vt. 為一個專案提供資金，與fund近義：</span>privately, publicly finance 私人出資；公共出資 / the money needed to finance the redevelopment 再開發所需要的資金 / The project was financed jointly by the Chinese and British governments. 該專案由中英兩國政府聯合出資。/ He took a job to finance his stay in Germany. 他找了一份工作以賺錢支付在德國的費用。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fund**</span> [fʌnd]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 為某一用途所專門儲蓄的或拿出來用的一筆錢，如基金、專款：</span>a disaster relief fund 賑災專款 / the company's pension fund 公司的養恤基金 / the International Monetary Fund 國際貨幣基金組織 / a scholarship fund for undergraduate engineering students 提供給工程學本科生的獎學金基金 / appeal fund（英）募款基金 / charitable, charity fund 慈善基金 / disaster, relief fund 救災基金；救濟基金 / emergency, reserve fund 應急基金；儲備金 / hardship fund（英）困難（救助）基金 / benevolent（尤英）, campaign, compensation, insurance, investment, memorial, prize, social, trust fund 慈善基金；競選基金；賠償基金；保險基金；投資基金；紀念基金；獎勵基金；社會基金；信託基金 / a benevolent fund for retired actors 為退休演員設立的慈善基金 / retirement fund 退休基金 / equity, hedge, mutural fund 股票基金；對沖基金；共同基金 / offshore fund 海外基金 / They set up an investment fund to provide money for their retirement. 他們設立了一個投資基金為退休生活提供資金。/ They don't want to draw on the fund unless they have to. 除非不得已，否則他們不想動用基金。/ The fund was invested in a range of state bonds. 該基金投資於一系列國有債券。/ fund holder, fund investor, fund manager, fund provider, fund trustee 基金持有人；基金投資人；基金經理；基金提供者；基金受託人 / fund account, fund management 基金賬戶；基金管理 / fund performance 基金業績 / fund value 基金價值 <span class="definition">2 [pl.] funds指可供花費的金錢，尤指為某一特定目的給予的資金、現款：</span>government funds, state funds, school funds, church funds 政府資金；國有資金；學校資金；教會資金 / private funds, public funds 私人資金；公共資金 / The hospital is trying to raise funds for a new kidney machine. 這家醫院正設法募集資金購買一臺新的血液透析器。/ The concert will raise funds for research into Aids. 這場音樂會將為艾滋病研究籌款。/ The project has been cancelled because of lack of funds. 這個專案因缺乏資金已經撤銷。/ I'm short of funds at the moment─can I pay you back next week? 我目前缺錢，下週還你行嗎？/ There are only limited funds available. 可用的資金有限。/ We have insufficient funds to pay for the building work. 我們沒有足夠的資金支付建築工程。/ The school is appealing for funds to invest in new equipment. 這所學校正在為購買新裝置呼籲捐款。/ Funds will be made available to ensure the provision of hospital services. 將提供資金以確保醫院服務正常。/ to withhold funds 扣押資金 / to deposit, to withdraw funds 存入資金；撤回資金 / The government is to channel more funds into local projects. 政府將調撥更多資金用於地方工程。/ The funds are earmarked for the health sector. 這些資金是指撥給衛生部門的。/ Funds from the event will support the work of the hospice. 此次活動募集的款項將用於資助臨終關懷醫院的工作。/ The current account offers savers instant access to funds. 活期賬戶使存款人可以隨時取錢。/ the flow of funds between various economic sectors 各個經濟部門之間的資金流動 / The project was hampered by lack of funds. 該工程因資金短缺而受阻。<span class="definition">vt. 為某事（通常指官方事項）提供資金：</span>a dance festival funded by the Arts Council 由藝術委員會資助的舞蹈節 / The museum is privately funded. 這家博物館由私人提供資金。/ a government-funded programme 政府資助專案 / The Bush Foundation has funded a variety of faculty development programs. 布什基金會已經資助了許多教員發展專案。/ The airport is being privately funded by a construction group. 該機場是由一個建築集團私人出資興建的。/ The venture is funded entirely by its board of directors. 這家企業的資金全部由董事會提供。/ fully funded day care for our children 資金充足的日間託兒所 / The GDPC is not directly funded by the taxpayer. GDPC的資金不是直接由納稅人提供的。/ a plan jointly funded by central and local government 有中央和地方政府共同出資的一項計劃 / This money will help to fund administration costs. 這筆錢將用於行政支出。
                                 
<span class="vocabulary">**funding**</span> [ˈfʌndɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（政府或機構為某一目的提供的）資金、基金：</span>development, education, research, science funding 發展基金；教育基金；研究基金；科學資金/ start-up, venture funding 啟動資金；企業資金 / library, school, university funding 圖書館基金；學校基金；大學基金 / The refusal to provide extra funding for schools caused a storm. 拒絕為學校提供額外資金這件事引發了一場軒然大波。/ It gave us more time to raise the necessary funding. 這使我們有更多時間籌集所需的資金。/ Most of our funding comes from private sources. 我們的大部分資金來自私人資助。/ We need to make sure that the funding goes to areas where it is most needed. 我們必須確保資金流向最需要的地方。/ About 70% of current funding has gone on schools. 大約 70% 的現有資金給了學校。/ funding crisis 資金危機 / £25 million in funding 2，500萬英鎊資金 / The school has attracted funding from a number of sources. 這所學校吸引了來自多方面的資金。/ Present levels of funding have forced the school to close. 現有的資金狀況迫使這所學校停辦。/ Many colleges have seen their funding cut. 許多學院的經費都遭到了削減。<span class="definition">2 [U] 資金（或基金）的提供：</span>There have been large cuts in government funding for scientific research. 政府提供的科研資金已大幅度削減。/ They hope for government funding for the scheme. 他們希望政府為這個計劃提供資金。/ The President has promised full funding for the plans. 總統承諾為這些計劃提供全額的資金。/ the industry's annual funding requirement 該行業每年要求的撥款 | emergency funding 應急撥款 / Half of the research posts depend on outside funding. —半的研究職位依靠外部撥款。/ funding package（尤英）一攬子資助計劃 / funding arrangement, funding formula 資助計劃；資助方案 / funding agency, funding body 資助機構 / funding bill（美）資助議案 / an increase in funding to drug rehabilitation clinics 對戒毒所增加資金投入

<span class="vocabulary">**foundation**</span> [faʊnˈdeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（為某一目的所建立的）基金會：</span>charitable, private foundation 慈善基金；私人基金（會）/ research foundation 研究基金會 / a private foundation for the arts一個為藝術的私人基金會 / the National Foundation for Educational Research 全國教育研究基金會 / The money will go to the San Francisco AIDS Foundation. 這筆錢將交給舊金山艾滋病基金會。

<span class="vocabulary">**grant**</span> [grɑ:nt; 美 grænt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (to do sth) 政府或機構劃撥下來的一筆有特定用途的錢款，即撥款：</span>student grants (= to pay for their education) 學生助學金 / He has been awarded a research grant. 他得到一筆研究經費。/ They'd got a special grant to encourage research. 他們得到一筆用以鼓勵科研的特別經費。/ Unfortunately, my application for a grant was rejected. 很遺憾，我的助學金申請被拒絕了。/ The school has received a large capital grant to improve its buildings. 學校收到一大筆撥款用以改造校舍。/ maintenance grant（英）生活補助金 / emergency grants for special needs for items such as clothing 為衣物之類物品的特別需求而緊急調撥的款項 / discretionary grant 可自由支配的補助 / project grant 專案補助 / You may be eligible for a clothing grant. 你可能有資格申請服裝補助。/ You can get a grant if you've lived in the area for three years. 在這一地區居住滿三年，就可以得到一筆補助金。/ The government has awarded a 3.5 million pound grant for the restoration of the opera house. 政府已撥款350萬英鎊來修繕歌劇院。/ The theatre's annual grant from the Arts Council has been cut. 該劇院從藝術委員會得到的年度補助被削減了。/ The program offers grants to small businesses. 該計劃為小企業提供資助。

<span class="vocabulary">**pot**</span> [pɒt] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [尤美] the pot可指一群人為了做某事（如為了買食物）所共同出的錢，即所湊的錢：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**pocket**</span> ['pɒkɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] 用於談論你不得不花的一筆錢，算是一種借代用法（用口袋來指代金錢）：</span>We have holidays to suit every pocket. 我們有適合各種程度消費的度假方式。/ to pay for the meal out of my own pocket 我自己掏腰包付飯錢 / The company has very deep pockets (= to have a lot of money). 該公司資金雄厚。

<span class="vocabulary">**purse**</span> [pə:s] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.]（對於一個人、組織或政府來說）現成可用的一筆錢：</span>We have holidays to suit every purse. 我們有適合不同消費計劃的度假安排。/ public purse 國庫 / purse strings 錢袋上的收口繩；財權 / to hold the purse strings 管錢 / The government will have to tighten the purse strings. 政府將不得不收緊錢袋。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**handout**</span> [ˈhændaʊt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [常貶] 政府（例如為了促進商業活動）給予個人或組織機構的撥款，之所以常含貶義，是因為人們認為金錢的領受者並沒有做什麼而白白拿到了這筆錢：</span>the tendency of politicians to use money on vote-buying handouts rather than on investment in the future. 政客把錢用於可以拉選票的福利上而不是用作未來投資的趨勢 / The president called foreign aid a handout that fosters dependence on wealthy nations. 總統認為外援是不勞而獲之財，會助長本國對富國的依賴性。

<span class="vocabulary">**liquid**</span> ['lɪkwɪd] 
<span class="definition">adj. 金融領域可指易變為現金的（即易變現的）：</span>liquid assets 流動資產
           
<span class="vocabulary">**leverage**</span> [ˈli:vərɪdʒ; 美 ˈlev-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [美，金融] 資本與負債比率。相當於gearing：</span>瞭解即可

相關詞義延伸：
· capital還可指用資金來啟動公司的一個群體，即資方。

周邊詞彙補充：
· refund可指（尤指由於支付太多，或由於退貨而產生的）被退還的款項；
· chip可指談判中的籌碼、討價還價的資本。算是一種比喻用法。
