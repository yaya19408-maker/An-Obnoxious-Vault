☀ <span class="category">**經濟 金融：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**economist**</span> [ɪˈkɒnəmɪst; 美 ɪˈkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 經濟學家：</span>academic, government, professional economist 理論經濟學家；政府經濟學家；專業經濟學家 / classical, free-market, Keynesian, liberal, neoclassical economist 古典主義經濟學家；自由市場主義經濟學家；凱恩斯主義經濟學家；自由主義經濟學家；新古典主義經濟學家 / conservative, mainstream, progressive economist 保守經濟學家；主流經濟學家；進步經濟學家 / agricultural, political 農業經濟學家；政治經濟學家

<span class="vocabulary">**economy**</span> [ɪˈkɒnəmi; 美 ɪˈkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（通常用the economy）（一國或一地區的）經濟：</span>agrarian, agricultural, capitalist, free-market, industrial, industrialized, knowledge-based, liberal, market, mixed, monetary, planned, political, rural, socialist economy 農村經濟；農業經濟；資本主義經濟；自由市場經濟；工業經濟；工業化經濟；知識經濟；自由經濟；市場經濟；混合經濟；貨幣經濟；計劃經濟；政治經濟；鄉村經濟；社會主義經濟 / domestic economy 國內經濟 / the increasingly competitive global economy 競爭日趨激烈的全球經濟 / underground economy 地下經濟 / The black economy booms when there is high unemployment. 失業率偏高，黑市經濟就會繁榮起來。/ Each party has its own strategy for building a strong economy. 各政黨都有壯大經濟的策略。/ The government was accused of failing to run the economy competently. 政府被指責未能有效地搞好經濟。/ Agriculture was the backbone of the economy. 從前農業是經濟的支柱。/ A small manufacturing sector inhibits growth in the economy. 製造業規模太小有礙經濟增長。/ the size of the economy 經濟規模 / The economy is in recession. 經濟處於衰退之中。/ The government devalued the currency to try to revive the flagging economy. 政府使貨幣貶值以振興衰退的經濟。/ The Japanese economy grew at an annual rate of more than 10 per cent. 日本經濟以年增長率超過10%的速度增長。<span class="definition">2 [C] 就經濟體制而言的國家，可稱為經濟體：</span>Ireland was one of the fastest-growing economies in Western Europe in the 1990s. 在20世紀90年代愛爾蘭是西歐經濟發展最快的經濟體之一。/ Zimbabwe boasts Africa's most industrialised economy. 辛巴威是非洲工業化程度最高的經濟體。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**economics**</span> [ˌi:kəˈnɒmɪks; ˌekə-; 美 -ˈnɑ:m-]
<span class="definition">n. [pl. U] 經濟因素；（商業或社會領域的）經濟情況、經濟體制：</span>The economics of the project are very encouraging. 這項工程的經濟情況非常令人鼓舞。/ He is regarded as a committed supporter of a radical free-market economics policy. 他被視為激進的自由市場經濟體制政策的堅定支持者。/ the economics of the third world 第三世界的經濟體制
 
<span class="vocabulary">**finance**</span> ['faɪnæns] 
<span class="definition">n. [U]（尤指政府或商業組織）管理資金的活動，即金融、財務、財政：</span>a diploma in banking and finance 銀行與金融學文憑 / international finance 國際金融 / finance company 金融公司 / finance industry 金融業 / company finance 公司財務 / personal finance 個人財務 / finance director 財務總監 / government, public, state finance 政府財政；公共財政；國家財政 / finance officer 財政官員 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fiscal**</span> [ˈfɪskl]
<span class="definition">adj. 與政府財政（尤指稅收）有關的，即財政的：</span>fiscal deficit 財政赤字 / fiscal regime 財政制度 / fiscal year 財政年度（簡稱財年）/ fiscal reforms 財政改革 / fiscal crisis 財政危機 / in 1987, when the government tightened fiscal policy 在1987 年，當時政府緊縮財政政策
           
<span class="vocabulary">**banking**</span> [ˈbæŋkɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 銀行業：</span>She's thinking about a career in banking. 她正在考慮從事銀行業。/ business, commercial, corporate banking 商業銀行業務；企業金融服務 / central banking 中央銀行業務 / investment, merchant banking 投資銀行業務；商人銀行業務 / retail, wholesale banking 零售銀行業務；批發銀行業務 / private banking 私人銀行業務 / domestic, national banking 國內銀行業務；國家銀行業務 / offshore banking 離岸銀行業務 / global, international banking 國際銀行業務 / electronic, Internet, online banking 電子銀行服務；網上銀行服務 / At first consumers were wary of online banking. 開始時顧客對電子銀行服務非常謹慎。/ banking system 銀行體系 / banking market 銀行業務市場 / banking group 銀行集團 / banking business, banking industry, banking sector 銀行業；銀行部門 / banking community 銀行界 / I mainly use the Internet banking service. 我主要使用網銀業務。/ This software is used in banking. 該軟體用於銀行業務中。

<span class="vocabulary">**reserve**</span> [rɪ'zə:v] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 常用複數，（資源、貨幣或黃金的）儲備：</span>capital, cash, currency, foreign-exchange, gold reserve 資本儲備；現金儲備；貨幣儲備；外匯儲備；黃金儲備 / dollar reserve 美元儲備 / reserve funds 儲備金 / reserve account 準備金賬戶 / energy, oil reserve 能源儲備；石油儲備
                       
<span class="vocabulary">**invest**</span> [ɪnˈvest]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth) (in sth) 向企業投資：</span>Now is a good time to invest in the property market. 現在是對房地產市場投資的好時機。/ He invested his life savings in his daughter's business. 他把一生的積蓄投資到了女兒的企業。/ They intend to invest directly in shares. 他們打算直接投資股票。/ He invested all our profits in gold shares. 他把我們所有的收益都投資在黃金股上了。<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth) (in/on sth)（組織機構、政府等）把資金投入…：</span>The government has invested heavily in public transport. 政府已對公共交通投入了大量資金。/ The college is to invest $2 million in a new conference hall. 這所學院計劃投入200萬元建造新的會議大廳。/ In his time managing the club he has invested millions on new players. 他在管理俱樂部期間投入了幾百萬培養新運動員。/ the European Investment Bank, which invested £100 million in Canary Wharf 向卡納裡碼頭投資了1億英鎊的歐洲投資銀行

<span class="vocabulary">**investment**</span> [ɪnˈvestmənt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] ~ (in sth)（行為意義上的）投資：</span>business, industrial, infrastructural, manufacturing investment 商業投資；工業投資；基礎設施投資；製造業投資 / investment income 投資收益 / investment bank, investment company, investment firm 投資銀行；投資公司 / investment adviser, investment analyst, investment banker, investment manager 投資顧問；投資分析師；投資銀行家；投資經理 / a business plan to encourage new investment 鼓勵新投資的商業計劃 / It took two years before I recouped my investment. 我花了兩年時間才收回投資。/ She felt the time was right to realize her investment, and sold all her shares. 她感覺投資變現的時機到了，於是拋售了所有股票。/ When buying shares, it's wise to spread your investment over several companies. 買股票時，分散投資若干家公司是明智之舉。/ investment account, investment fund 投資賬戶；投資基金 / investment portfolio 投資組合 / investment from American pension funds 來自美國養老基金的投資 / investment in local industry 對當地工業的投資 / losses made on investments in stocks and bonds 股票和債券投資的損失 / He lost a lot of money through poor investments. 由於投資不當，他虧了很多錢。/ The country's infrastructure is crumbling because of inadequate investment. 該國的基礎設施因為投資不足而日益破損。/ to encourage foreign investment 鼓勵外國投資 / This country needs investment in education. 這個國家需要對教育進行投資。/ He said the government must introduce tax incentives to encourage investment. 他說政府必須推出稅收激勵政策來鼓勵投資。/ One of the most important changes concerns the investment of pension contributions. 最重要的一個變化涉及養老保險基金的投資。<span class="definition">2 [C] 投入的金錢或事物：</span>a minimum investment of $10 000 1萬元的最低投資額 / an investment of twenty-eight million pounds 2,800萬英鎊的投資金額 / You'll be able to earn an average rate of return of 8% on your investments. 你將能獲得 8% 的平均投資收益率。/ an initial investment of $5 million 500萬美元的先期投資 / a high return on my investments 我的投資的高收益 / Our investments are not doing well. 我們的投資境況不佳。/ We bought the house as an investment (= to make money). 我們買這所房子作為投資。/ She sees art as a long-term investment strategy. 她把藝術品投資視為長期投資策略。/ I don't really like modern art but I bought it as an investment. 我並非真的喜歡現代藝術，購買藝術品只是作為一種投資。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**transfusion**</span> [trænsˈfju:ʒn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] ~ of sth 追加投資；（資金的）注入：</span>The project badly needs a transfusion of cash. 這個專案急需追加現金投資。

<span class="vocabulary">**speculate**</span> [ˈspekjuleɪt]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (in/on sth) 商業投機（即透過倒賣貨物、不動產、股票等來獲利，同時面臨著損失的風險）：</span>He likes to speculate on the stock market. 他喜歡炒股。/ Big farmers are moving in, not in order to farm, but in order to speculate with rising land prices. 大農場主正在不斷湧進來，不是為了耕作，而是要利用不斷上漲的地價進行投機。/ The banks made too many risky loans which now can't be repaid, and they speculated in property whose value has now dropped. 各銀行貸出去太多高風險貸款，目前無法收回，他們還進行地產投機，而地產的價值目前已經下跌了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**speculation**</span> [ˌspekjuˈleɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] ~ (in sth) 商業投機：</span>financial speculation 金融投機 / market, stock-market（尤美）speculation 市場投機；股市投機 / currency, land, property（尤英）, real estate（美）speculation 貨幣投機；土地投機；房地產投機 / speculation in oil 石油投機 / speculation on the stock market 股市上的投機

說明：finance和fiscal在漢語中有時均翻譯為“財政”，但finance是個名詞，且除了指政府管理資金的活動（即“財政”）外，還可指公司等管理資金的活動（即“財務”）；而fiscal是個形容詞，僅表示與政府資金或公共資金（尤其是與稅收）相關的。二者作前置定語時，存在一定交集。

相關詞義延伸：
· economics還可指經濟學。

周邊詞彙補充：
· leverage可指舉債投資。
