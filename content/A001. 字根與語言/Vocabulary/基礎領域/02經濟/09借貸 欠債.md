☀ <span class="category">**借貸 欠債：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**creditor**</span> [ˈkredɪtə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 債權人；債主；貸方：</span>The company said it would pay in full all its creditors. 該公司稱將償清所有債權人的債務。/ They agreed to repay their creditors over a period of three years. 他們同意三年內向債主還清欠款。/ creditor country, creditor nation 債權國 / foreign creditor 外國債權人 / private creditor 私人債主 / large creditor 大債主 / secured, unsecured creditor 有擔保的債權人；無擔保的債權人
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**loan**</span> [ləʊn; 美 loʊn]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（銀行等機構或個人）貸出去的款：</span>to take out/repay a loan (= to borrow money/pay it back) 取得/償還貸款 / bank loans with low interest rates 銀行低息貸款 / It took three years to repay my student loan (= money lent to a student). 我花了三年的時間才還清我的學生貸款。/ The country has no access to foreign loans or financial aid. 該國得不到任何外國貸款或經濟援助。/ The president wants to make it easier for small businesses to get bank loans. 總統希望能讓小企業更方便地獲得銀行貸款。/ bad, non-performing loan 壞賬；不良貸款 / business, consumer, personal loan 商業貸款；消費貸款；個人貸款 / bank loan 銀行貸款 / temporary loan 臨時貸款 / bridge（美）, bridging（美）loan 過渡性貸款 / home, mortgage loan 按揭貸款 / auto（美）, car loan 汽車貸款 / interest-free loan 無息貸款 / fixed-rate loan 固定利率貸款 / guaranteed（美）, secured, unsecured loan 擔保貸款；抵押貸款；無抵押貸款 / home-equity loan（美）住房抵押貸款 / outstanding loan 未償還貸款 / loan application 貸款申請 / loan interest 貸款利息 / loan facility（尤英）信貸服務 / loan officer（美）放款員 / loan shark 放高利貸者 <span class="definition">vt. [尤美] ~ sth (to sb) / ~ (sb) sth 將…（尤指錢）貸給或借給某人：</span>The bank is happy to loan money to small businesses. 銀行樂於貸款給小型企業。/ A friend loaned me $1 000. 有一位朋友借給我1 000元。

<span class="vocabulary">**debit**</span> [ˈdebɪt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 從銀行賬戶中取出來的一筆錢，即借項：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**mortgage**</span> [ˈmɔ:gɪdʒ; 美 ˈmɔ:rg-]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（也可用非正式的home loan）從銀行（或類似機構）借出的一筆用於購置房產、土地等的款項（需要在一定年限內還清），即房屋抵押貸款，也可叫按揭貸款。可指此種錢款，也可指此種按揭貸款協議：</span>an increase in mortgage rates 房屋抵押貸款利率的提高 / a mortgage on the house 一項房產按揭 / a mortgage of £60 000 6萬英鎊的按揭貸款 / monthly mortgage repayments 每月償還的按揭貸款 / big, huge mortgage 大筆按揭貸款 / small mortgage 小額按揭 / Banks often offer their employees cheap mortgages. 銀行經常給員工提供低息按揭貸款。/ reverse mortgage（美）反向抵押 / fixed-rate mortgage 固定利率按揭 / home, residential mortgage 住房抵押貸款；住宅抵押貸款 / He raised the money by taking out a second mortgage on his house. 他透過對自己的房屋辦理二次抵押籌集了這筆錢。/ We have a big mortgage. 我們得到了一大筆按揭貸款。/ They were having trouble getting a mortgage. 他們辦理按揭貨款遇到了困難。/ We'll have to take out a second mortgage to pay for this wedding! 我們將不得不辦理第二筆按揭來支付婚禮費用! / He wasn't earning enough to pay the mortgage. 他掙的錢不夠還按揭。/ There are penalties if you want to redeem your mortgage early. 如果你想提前還清按揭貸款，需要交違約金。/ They fell behind with/on their mortgage, so their home was repossessed.（on為美式用法；with為英式用法）他們沒有按時還按揭款，所以房子被收走了。/ mortgage payment, mortgage repayment（英）按揭償付；按揭還款 / They were struggling to keep up with their mortgage payments. 他們千方百計按時償付按揭款。/ mortgage application 抵押貸款申請 / mortgage loan 抵押貸款 / mortgage credit 抵押信貸 / mortgage debt抵押債務 / mortgage business, mortgage industry, mortgage market 抵押貸款業務；抵押貸款行業；抵押貸款市場 / mortgage broker, mortgage company, mortgage lender 按揭經紀人；按揭公司；按揭放貸者 / mortgage finance抵押融資 / mortgage product 抵押產品 / mortgage insurance 住房抵押貸款保險 / mortgage portfolio抵押資產組合 / mortgage refinancing 轉按揭 / I couldn't get a mortgage on the property. 我未辦成該房產的按揭。

<span class="vocabulary">**borrow**</span> ['bɒrəʊ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. borrow (sth) (from sb/sth) 從一個人（或銀行）借錢（並同意以後歸還）：</span>She borrowed £2 000 from her parents. 她向她的父母借了2000英鎊。/ to borrow heavily 借很多錢

<span class="vocabulary">**lend**</span> [lend] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. lend sth (to sb) / lend (sb) sth（銀行或金融機構）向某人貸款（獲得貸款的人在一段時間後歸還，並支付利息），相當於loan：</span>They refused to lend us the money. 他們拒絕向我們貸款。

<span class="vocabulary">**owe**</span> [əʊ] 
<span class="definition">vt. owe sth (to sb) (for sth) / owe (sb) sth (for sth) 欠債，欠錢：</span>She still owes her father £3 000. 她還欠她父親3000英鎊。/ The country owes billions of dollars to foreign creditors. 這個國家欠外國債權人數十億元。/ How much do I owe you for the groceries? 買這些雜貨我得給你多少錢？/ I’m still owed three days’ leave.（比喻用法）還欠我三天假呢。

<span class="vocabulary">**credit**</span> ['kredɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 從銀行裡借的錢，即所貸的款：</span>bank credit 銀行信貸 / long-term credit 長期貸款 / consumer credit 消費信貸 / credit limit 信用額度 / credit market, credit union 信貸市場；信用合作社 / to get credit 得到貸款 / to refuse further credit to the company 拒絕再給那家公司貸款

<span class="vocabulary">**debt**</span> [det] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 所欠的一筆錢，即債務：</span>credit card debts 信用卡借款 / outstanding debt 未償債務 / bad debt 呆賬 / corporate debt 企業債務 / government debt 政府債務 / national debt 公債 / external debt 外債 / gambling debt 賭債 / debt collection 收債 / debt payment 債務償還 / debt burden 債務負擔 / debt relief 債務免除 / debt crisis 債務危機 <span class="definition">2 [U] 負債情況，尤指無力償還的情況：</span>I was deeply in debt at that time. 我當時負債累累。/ The company is £4 million in debt. 這家公司負債400萬英鎊。/ to get out of debt 擺脫債務
           
<span class="vocabulary">**liability**</span> [ˌlaɪəˈbɪləti]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl.] 一個人或一家公司所欠的債務，即負債：</span>The company had assets of $138 million and liabilities of $120.5 million. 該公司資產為1.38億美元，負債為1.205億美元。/ The company has liabilities to its employees. 這家公司欠員工的債。/ substantial liabilities 大量債務 / financial, tax liabilities 財物負債；稅務負債 / He wants to know the precise amount of the liabilities he is taking over. 他想知道自己將要承擔的債務的確切數字。/ There is enough money to cover existing liabilities. 有足夠的錢償還現有債務。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**arrears**</span> [əˈrɪəz; 美 əˈrɪrz]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] 應付的欠款，尤指逾期的欠款：</span>debt, mortgage, rent, tax arrears 欠債；拖欠的按揭貸款；所欠租金；積欠稅款 / They fell into arrears with their rent. 他們拖欠了租金。/ The government has agreed to pay all arrears owed to members of the armed forces. 政府已同意付清所有拖欠的軍餉。/ The country had accumulated arrears of s80 million on loans of s400 million. 該國4億美元的貸款已累計拖欠8，000萬美元。/ She was struggling to pay off her mortgage arrears. 她正努力償清她的按揭欠款。/ The authorities must try to recover arrears of rent. 當局必須設法追回拖欠的租金。/ By this time the arrears amounted to E12 000. 到目前為止，欠款累計達到12，000英鎊。/ She was six months in arrears with the mortgage. 她拖欠了六個月的按揭款。/ You will be paid monthly in arrears. 你將在每月工作完成後獲得酬勞。/ There have been sharp increases in arrears on interest payments. 拖欠支付利息的現象急劇增加。/ They have promised to pay the arrears over the next five years. 他們保證在隨後的五年內償付逾期債款。/ These 50,000 arrears cases represent a tiny fraction of all home owners. 這5萬個欠款案例只代表所有業主中的一小部分人。/ the 300,000 households who are more than six months in arrears with their mortgages 拖欠抵押貸款超過6個月的30萬戶家庭 / debtor countries which fall into arrears with the banks 拖欠銀行貸款的債務國 / Unemployment benefit is paid fortnightly in arrears. 失業救濟金延後兩個星期支付。

<span class="vocabulary">**bond**</span> [bɒnd] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 金融領域可指政府或企業發行的債券：</span>bond market 債券市場 / high-yield, long-term bond 高收益債券；長期債券 / corporate, government, treasury bond 公司債券；政府債券；國庫券 / junk bond 高風險債券 <span class="definition">2 [C] 按揭貸款，或按揭貸款協議：</span>to pay off a bond 償清按揭貸款 / to take out a second bond 申請第二次按揭 / bond rates (= of interest) 按揭貸款利率
           
<span class="vocabulary">**stock**</span> [stɒk; 美 stɑ:k]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] [英] 以固定利率借給政府的一筆錢，也可指此種官方憑證，即公債、公債券：</span>government stock 政府債券           

<span class="vocabulary">**IOU**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 欠條、借據，是I owe you的縮寫形式：</span>an IOU for £20 一張20英鎊的借據

<span class="vocabulary">**back**</span> [bæk] 
<span class="definition">adj. 只作前置定語，拖欠的：</span>back pay 欠薪 / back rent 欠租金 / back taxes 欠稅 <span class="definition">adv. 償還：</span>to pay back a debt 償還債務
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**overdue**</span> [ˌəʊvəˈdju:; 美 ˌoʊvərˈdu:]
<span class="definition">adj.（到期）未付的，未償還的：</span>overdue payment, overdue library book 逾期的欠款；逾期未還的圖書 / The rent is now overdue. 現在房租已屬拖欠。/ Teachers have joined a strike aimed at forcing the government to pay overdue salaries and allowances. 教師加入了旨在迫使政府支付拖欠薪水和津貼的罷工。

<span class="vocabulary">**indebted**</span> [ɪnˈdetɪd]
<span class="definition">adj.（國家、政府等）負債的：</span>a list of the fifteen most heavily indebted nations 十五個負債最重的國家的名單 / America's treasury secretary identified the most heavily indebted countries. 美國財政部長指出了負債最多的那些國家。/ The company is heavily indebted. 這家公司負債累累。/ I did not want to be financially indebted to him. 我不想欠他錢。/ indebted to the bank欠銀行錢
           
<span class="vocabulary">**liable**</span> [ˈlaɪəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. [名詞前不常用] ~ (for sth)（法律上）負有償付責任的：</span>You will be liable for any damage caused. 你必須對造成的任何損失負賠償責任。/ The airline's insurer is liable for damages to the victims' families. 航空公司所投的保險公司有責任向受害者家屬賠付損害賠償金。/ Failure to provide insurance rendered him liable to prosecution. 他可能會因沒有提供保險而被起訴。/ The court ruled he could not be held personally liable for his wife's debts. 法庭裁定他個人不負有償付妻子債務的責任。/ They could be found liable for the entire amount. 他們可能會被判負擔全部費用。/ Partners are jointly and severally (= together and individually) liable for a partnership's debts. 合夥人共同連帶承擔合夥公司的債務。/ She's fully liable for the company's debts.她對公司的債務負全責。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**solvent**</span> [ˈsɒlvənt; 美 ˈsɑ:l-]
<span class="definition">adj. [名詞前不常用] 有償付能力的；無債務的：</span>They're going to have to show that the company is now solvent. 他們必須要證明公司目前具備償還能力。

<span class="vocabulary">**behind**</span> [bɪ'haɪnd] 
<span class="definition">adv. behind (with/in sth) 拖欠（付款）或積壓（工作）：</span>She’s fallen behind with the payments. 她已經拖欠付款了。/ He was terribly behind in his work. 他積壓了大量工作。

<span class="vocabulary">**present**</span> [prɪ'zent] 
<span class="definition">vt. 向某人提交支票或賬單（該支票或賬單需要接收者兌付或支付）：</span>A cheque presented by Tom was returned by the bank. 銀行退回了湯姆提交的支票。/ to presente a bill 送來一份賬單

<span class="vocabulary">**collect**</span> [kə'lekt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 收取別人欠你的錢款，即收欠款、收賬（如到其住宅處去收賬）：</span>collect rent, collect debts, collect tax 收租金；討債；徵稅

<span class="vocabulary">**recover**</span> [rɪ'kʌvə] 
<span class="definition">vt. recover sth (from sb/sth) 全額收回（已花掉、投資或被拖欠的錢款）：</span>Legal action is being taken to recover the money. 正採取法律途徑來收回那筆錢。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**recuperate**</span> [rɪˈku:pəreɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 收回、挽回（花掉或損失的錢）：</span>He hoped to recuperate at least some of his losses. 他希望至少挽回一部分損失。

<span class="vocabulary">**forgive**</span> [fə'ɡɪv] 
<span class="definition">vt. [正式]（一家銀行、一個國家等）免除對方的債務：</span>The government has agreed to forgive a large part of the debt. 政府同意免除一大部分債務。

<span class="vocabulary">**service**</span> ['sə:vɪs] 
<span class="definition">vt. [術語] 支付借款（或稱債務）的利息：</span>The company can no longer service its debts. 那家公司已無力支付債務利息。

<span class="vocabulary">**clear**</span> [klɪə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 還清或清償（所有債務或貸款）：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">adj. clear of sth 償清了債務的，是此義的常規用詞：</span>We are finally clear of debt. 我們終於償清了債務。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**redeem**</span> [rɪˈdi:m]
<span class="definition">vt. 償清債務：</span>to redeem a loan 清償貸款 / The amount required to redeem the mortgage was £358,587. 需要償還的抵押貸款總計358,587英鎊。   
                  
<span class="vocabulary">**redemption**</span> [rɪˈdempʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [金融]（債務的）償還：</span>redemption of the loan 償付貸款
           
<span class="vocabulary">**repay**</span> [rɪˈpeɪ]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (to sb) / ~ (sb) (sth) 償還（借的錢）：</span>to repay a mortgage 償還按揭貸款 / The loan must be repaid in full by December 31. 貸款必須在12月31日以前全部還清。/ The proceeds from the sale will be used to repay the loan. 銷售所得將用來償還貸款。/ I owe them a debt that cannot easily be repaid. 我欠他們一筆錢，不容易償還。/ This money must be repaid to the bank. 這筆錢必須還給銀行。/ When are you going to repay them? 你什麼時候把錢還給他們？/ I'll repay the money I owe them next week. 我將在下個星期償還欠他們的錢。/ I fully intend to repay them the money that they lent me. 我非常想把他們借給我的錢還給他們。/ It will take 30 years to repay the loan. 還清貸款需要30年的時間。/ He advanced funds of his own to his company, which was unable to repay him. 他把自己的資金預付給他的公司，而公司卻不能把錢償還給他。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**repayment**</span> [rɪˈpeɪmənt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 債務的償還：</span>The loan is due for repayment by the end of the year. 這筆借款要在年底還清。/ The loans are due for repayment in July 2015.（be due for為英式用法）貸款將在2015年7月到期。/ He failed to meet last Friday's deadline for repayment of a 114m loan. 他沒能在上星期五的最後期限前償還1.14億的貸款。/ Brazil is putting forward a new debt repayment plan. 巴西即將提出一個新的債務償付計劃。/ Interest will be refunded in the event of early repayment of the loan. 提前還貸的話利息可以退還。/ regular repayment 定期償還 / monthly repayment 每月還款 / capital, principal repayment 資本償還；本金償還 / interest repayment 利息償還 / mortgage repayment（英）抵押借款償還 / The bank has demanded repayment of the loan. 銀行已發出償還貸款的要求。/ repayment period 償還期 / repayment plan, repayment schedule, repayment terms 償還計劃；償還時間表；償還條款 / a ten-year repayment option 10年分期還款的選項 / repayment rate 償還率 / repayments on the mortgage 按揭還款 / repayment to the fund 對基金的償還 <span class="definition">2 [C, usually pl.] 向一個人或組織機構（如銀行）定期償還的一筆錢：</span>We were unable to meet (= pay) the repayments on the loan. 我們無法支付貸款的分期償還額。/ mortgage repayments 按揭貸款的償還款項 / They were unable to meet their mortgage repayments on the loan. 他們不能按時償還抵押貸款。/ You can pay it off or make a minimum repayment. 你可以一次付清或先付最低分期償付款。/ Repayments can be spread over 25 years. 還款可分25年付清。/ The repayments can be made directly from your current account. 還款可以直接從往來賬戶上扣除。/ They are struggling to keep up their loan repayments.（keep up為英式用法） 他們努力堅持償還貸款。

<span class="vocabulary">**discharge**</span> [dɪsˈtʃɑ:dʒ; 美 -ˈtʃɑ:rdʒ]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 清償（債務）：</span>The goods will be sold for a fraction of their value in order to discharge the debt. 貨物將被賤價賣出以清償債務。<span class="definition">n. [U] [正式]（債務的）清償：</span>the discharge of debts 債務的清償
 
<span class="vocabulary">**square**</span> [skweə] 
<span class="definition">adj. [非正式] 彼此不欠錢的，兩清的：</span>Here’s the £10 I owe you--now we’re square. 這是我欠你的10英鎊——這下我們兩清了。

說明：collect和recover表“收賬”時，①collect指具體的收賬行為，如到其住宅處去收賬；②recover指“全額收回”已花掉、投資或被拖欠的錢款，通常是概念和結果意義上的收賬，而不太側重指收賬的具體行為，比如“去年的收賬很困難”，一般用recover，“我一會兒去他們公司收賬”，一般用collect。在欠款的整個recover事件中，往往包含了一次或數次collect的行為。

相關詞義延伸：
· bond還可指保釋金。
