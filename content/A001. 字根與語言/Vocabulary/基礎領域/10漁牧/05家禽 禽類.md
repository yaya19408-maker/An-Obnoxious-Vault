☀ <span class="category">**家禽 禽類：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**poultry**</span> [ˈpəʊltri; 美 ˈpoʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] 家禽（如雞、鴨、鵝）：</span>free-range poultry（尤英）散養的家禽 / domestic poultry 家禽 / to keep poultry（尤英）飼養家禽 / to raise poultry（美）飼養家禽 / He keeps/raises rabbits and poultry. 他飼養兔子和家離。/ They keep/raise poultry on the farm. 他們在農場裡飼養家禽。/ poultry farm, poultry farming, poultry industry 家禽飼養場；家禽飼養；家禽飼養業 / Most poultry farmers have to rely on commercially manufactured feeds. 多數飼養家禽的農場主不得不依賴商業化生產的飼料。

<span class="vocabulary">**chicken**</span> ['tʃɪkɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（飼養以供下蛋或食用的）雞：</span>battery（英）, corn-fed, free-range chicken 層架式雞籠裡飼養的雞；糧食餵養的雞；放養的雞 / live chicken 活雞 / chicken farmer 養雞場場主 / chicken coop, chicken run 雞籠；雞圈
