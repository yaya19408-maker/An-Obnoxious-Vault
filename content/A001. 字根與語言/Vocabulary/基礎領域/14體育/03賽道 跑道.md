☀ <span class="category">**賽道 跑道：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**track**</span> [træk] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（人、動物、汽車、腳踏車等）賽跑或賽車等運動的跑道：</span>the athletics track 田徑跑道 / race track（通常用racetrack）, running track 賽跑跑道 / indoor, outdoor track 室內跑道；室外跑道 / training track 訓練跑道 / oval track 橢圓形跑道 / fast track（賽車的）高速賽道 / dog, greyhound track 賽狗跑道 / track race 場地賽 / track condition 賽道狀況
           
<span class="vocabulary">**lane**</span> [leɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 比賽中一個人的賽道；游泳池中一個人的泳道：</span>Drawn in lane three, she had all her rivals in sight. 她抽到第三道，所有對手盡收眼底。/ The pool is divided into three sections with a crawler lane for beginners. 泳池分三部分，有為初學者而設的慢泳道。/ The Australian in lane four is coming up fast from behind. 第四道的澳大利亞運動員正從後面快速追趕上來。

<span class="vocabulary">**straight**</span> [streɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [美也用straightaway]（賽道的）直道部分；直道：</span>瞭解即可 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**stretch**</span> [stretʃ]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] 速度比賽終點的直道部分。相當於straight：</span>the finishing/home stretch 終點直道 / The campaign has entered its final stretch.（比喻用法）競選已進入最後衝刺階段。/ She felt a renewed burst of energy for the final stretch. 在最後一段，她感到能量重新爆發了出來。

<span class="vocabulary">**circuit**</span> ['sə:kɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 環形賽車道：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**run**</span> [rʌn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 滑雪或其它運動中的坡道：</span>ski, toboggan run 滑雪道；雪橇滑道

<span class="vocabulary">**lap**</span> [læp] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（跑道等的）一圈：</span>to complete twenty laps 跑完二十圈 / to be overtaken on the final lap 在最後一圈被超過 / to do a lap of honour/honor 繞場一週慶祝勝利 / I skated a victory lap around the rink. 我繞著滑冰場滑了一圈，慶祝勝利。/ He was swimming laps in the pool. 他正在泳池裡一圈一圈地遊。
