☀ <span class="category">**柵欄 籬笆：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**fence**</span> [fens] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 柵欄，圍欄，籬笆：</span>stone（美）, wire, wooden fence 石質柵欄；金屬絲柵欄；木頭柵欄 / electric fence 電柵欄 / backyard fence（美）後院的柵欄 / boundary, security fence 分界的柵欄；安全防護的柵欄 / the fence line separating the United States from Mexico 將美國與墨西哥隔開的柵欄邊境線 / She leaned over the fence. 她斜靠在籬笆上。/ a fence around the site 場地的圍欄

<span class="vocabulary">**grid**</span> [grɪd]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（金屬或木製的、相互平行或交叉的）格子，格柵，柵欄：</span>a grid of ironwork 鐵格柵           

<span class="vocabulary">**picket**</span> [ˈpɪkɪt]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指柵欄的）尖木樁，尖板條：</span>a picket fence 尖板條柵欄

<span class="vocabulary">**wire**</span> ['waɪə] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the wire 可指金屬絲編制的柵欄，如鐵絲網：</span>They cut the perimeter wire and escaped. 他們剪斷了環繞四周的鐵絲網逃跑了。/ Behind the wire, the prisoners were exercising. 鐵絲網內，囚犯們正在操練。
     
周邊詞彙補充：
· hedge可指栽種在一起的一排灌木或小樹，常在田地、園子或道路的邊緣，即樹籬。
