☀ <span class="category">**敵方 我方：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**side**</span> [saɪd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（戰爭中的）一方或一派：</span>opposing side 敵對方 / losing, winning side 戰敗方；獲勝方 / There were casualties on both sides of the conflict. 衝突雙方均有傷亡。/ He switched sides and joined the opposition. 他轉換立場，加入了敵對派。<span class="definition">vi. side with another 可表示在戰爭中支援某方；side against another 可表示站在一起反對…：</span>They might be siding with the rebels. 他們可能會支援叛亂者。

<span class="vocabulary">**enemy**</span> ['enɪmɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [Sing., C] 敵國，敵軍：</span>traditional enemy 宿敵 / external enemies 外敵 / enemy army 敵軍 / enemy defences, enemy lines, enemy positions 敵軍防禦工事；敵軍防線；敵軍陣地 / enemy camp 敵營 / enemy action, enemy attack, enemy fire 敵方行動；敵方襲擊；敵方火力 / enemy country 敵國 / enemy aircraft, enemy ship 敵機；敵艦 / behind enemy lines 在敵後 / The document must not fall into enemy hands. 檔案一定不能落入敵人手中。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**hostile**</span> [ˈhɒstaɪl; 美 ˈhɑ:stl; -taɪl]
<span class="definition">adj. 屬於敵軍的：</span>hostile territory 敵方領土 / The city is encircled by a hostile army. 該市被敵軍包圍了。

<span class="vocabulary">**aggressor**</span> [əˈgresə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 侵略者、挑釁者（即在打架或戰鬥中首先發起攻擊者，可以是一個人、團體，也可以是一個國家）：</span>They have been the aggressors in this conflict. 他們一直是這次衝突中的挑釁方。

<span class="vocabulary">**invader**</span> [ɪnˈveɪdə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 對別國進行武裝入侵的一支軍隊或一個國家，還可指此種軍隊中的一個士兵，即侵略者：</span>a foreign invader 外國侵略者 / They prepared to repel the invaders. 他們準備趕走侵略軍。/ The invaders were only finally crushed when troops overcame them at Glenshiel in June 1719. 1719 年 6 月，部隊在格蘭舍一役擊敗了入侵者，直至此時他們才最終覆滅。/ The white blood cells attack cells infected with an invader.（比喻用法）白細胞攻擊受到感染的細胞。
