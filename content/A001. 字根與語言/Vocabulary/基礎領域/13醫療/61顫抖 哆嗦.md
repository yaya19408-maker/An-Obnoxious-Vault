☀ <span class="category">**顫抖 哆嗦：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**quake**</span> [kweɪk] 
<span class="definition">vi. quake (with sth)（人因非常恐懼或緊張而）發抖，顫抖，哆嗦，相當於tremble：</span>I just stood there quaking with fear. 我站在那兒，嚇得發抖。

<span class="vocabulary">**shake**</span> [ʃeɪk] 
<span class="definition">vi. 1 shake (with sth) 身體由於寒冷、恐懼、緊張、憤怒、哭泣、大笑等原因而顫抖，發抖，哆嗦，相當於tremble：</span>He was shaking with fear. 他嚇得發抖。/ He roared with laughter, shaking in his chair. 他放聲大笑，身子在椅子裡直顫。/ My hands started shaking nervously. 我的手開始緊張得抖個不停。/ My body was shaking from the cold. 我冷得渾身發抖。/ She was shaking with anger. 她氣得渾身發抖。/ to shake with sobs 一邊抽泣一邊發抖 <span class="definition">2 shake (with sth)（某人的嗓音）顫抖（通常由於緊張、煩惱或生氣）：</span>His voice shaking with rage. 他氣得聲音發抖。<span class="definition">n. [pl.] [非正式] the shakes可表示身體由於恐懼、疾病、酒精攝入過量等所發生的顫抖，哆嗦：</span>I always get the shakes before exams. 考試前，我總是緊張得發抖。
          
<span class="vocabulary">**shaky**</span> [ˈʃeɪki]
<span class="definition">adj.（聲音或身體因疾病、緊張、害怕、寒冷等而）顫抖並感覺虛弱的，如顫巍巍的：</span>Her voice sounded shaky on the phone. 電話裡她的聲音聽著發顫。/ Her voice was low and shaky with emotion. 她的聲音低沉，因激動而有些顫抖。/ The old man was very shaky on his feet. 老人站在那兒，顫巍巍的。/ We have all had a shaky hand and a dry mouth before speaking in public. 當眾發言前，我們全都手腳發抖，嘴巴發乾。/ Even small operations can leave you feeling a bit shaky. 即使是小手術也會讓你有點戰戰兢兢。/ The experience had left him rather shaky. 這次經歷使他膽戰心驚。           

<span class="vocabulary">**shudder**</span> [ˈʃʌdə(r)]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (with sth) / ~ (at sth) 因寒冷、擔憂、恐懼、厭惡或激動而發抖、打顫、戰慄：</span>Elaine shuddered with cold. 伊萊恩冷得瑟瑟發抖。/ She shuddered involuntarily as he approached her. 她向她走來時，她不由自主地顫抖起來。/ The sight of the dead body made them shudder. 一看到死屍，他們就戰慄起來。/ She shuddered at the memory of school exams. 想起學校的考試，她就一陣哆嗦。/ Joe shuddered in disgust. 喬厭惡得直髮抖。/ A deep sigh shuddered through her body. 她哆哆嗦嗦地深深嘆了一口氣。/ His whole body shuddered with fury. 他氣得渾身發抖。/ I shuddered at the thought of going back to school. 一想到要回學校上學我就哆嗦。/ Just thinking about the accident makes me shudder. 只要一想起那場事故，我就發抖。/ Alone in the car, she shuddered with fear. 她一個人待在車裡，害怕得直哆嗦。/ I shuddered at the thought of all the trouble I'd caused. 一想到我闖的禍，就不寒而慄。/ I shudder to think how much this is all going to cost (= I don't want to think about it because it is too unpleasant). 想想這一切得花多少錢，我就發怵。/ I shudder to think what would have happened if he hadn't acted as quickly as he did. 我不敢設想如果他沒有反應那麼快的話會發生什麼事。/ Lloyd had urged her to eat caviar. She had shuddered at the thought. 勞埃德鼓動她吃魚子醬。可她一想到這個就打哆嗦。<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.]（因寒冷、擔憂、恐懼、厭惡或激動等而引起的）發抖、打顫、戰慄：</span>She gave a little shudder when she touched his clammy hand. 摸到他溼冷的手時，她微微一顫。/ He repressed a shudder of disgust. 他忍著沒有因厭惡而顫抖。/ The sight of the body sent a shudder through him. 看到那具屍體，他渾身一陣戰慄。/ A shudder of pain racked his body. 他全身痛苦地顫抖著。/ He remembered that awful moment with a shudder. 想起那可怕的時刻，他一陣戰慄。/ a shudder of relief 解脫的顫抖 / a shudder of fear 害怕的顫抖 / She gave an involuntary shudder. 她不由自主地抖了一下。

<span class="vocabulary">**tremble**</span> ['trembl] 
<span class="definition">vi. tremble (with sth) 不能自控地顫抖或哆嗦（尤指由於非常緊張、興奮、激動、生氣、害怕等）：</span>trembling hands 顫抖的雙手 / My legs were trembling with fear. 我嚇得雙腿直髮抖。/ Her voice trembled with excitement. 她激動得聲音顫抖。/ She was trembling with anger. 她當時正氣得發抖。<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.]（也可用trembling[C, U]）顫抖、顫慄或哆嗦的一陣感覺、運動或聲音：</span>a tremble of fear 恐懼引起的顫抖 / She tried to control the trembling in her legs. 她竭力控制住顫抖的雙腿。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**tremor**</span> [ˈtremə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（例如由於寒冷、恐懼等引起身體或聲音不由自主的）顫抖、哆嗦：</span>hand, muscle tremor 手哆嗦；肌肉顫抖 / He felt a tremor in his arms. 他感到胳膊不由自主地顫動。/ An uncontrollable tremor shook his mouth. 他的雙唇控制不住地顫抖。/ There was a slight tremor in his voice. 他的聲音略微有點兒顫抖。/ He couldn't control the tremor in his voice. 他無法抑制聲音的顫抖。/ He managed to make his short speech without a tremor. 他總算完成了簡短的發言而沒有發抖。/ She felt a tremor run down her back when she saw him. 看見他的時候，她感到不寒而慄。/ a tremor of fear 恐懼的顫抖 / He felt a tremor of apprehension. 他感到一陣恐懼。/ nervous tremor 焦慮的戰慄 / Her expression sent a tremor of anxiety through him. 她的表情使他感到一陣戰慄。/ He felt a tiny tremor of excitement as he glimpsed the city lights. 瞥見城市的燈光時，他興奮得有一點兒發抖。           

<span class="vocabulary">**quiver**</span> [ˈkwɪvə(r)]
<span class="definition">vi. 輕微顫動、輕微抖動：</span>Her lip quivered and then she started to cry. 她嘴唇微微一顫就哭了起來。/ Her bottom lip quivered and big tears rolled down her cheeks. 她的下唇顫抖著，豆大的淚珠順著面頰滾落下來。<span class="definition">vi.（人或聲音由於憤怒、恐懼、激動等）顫抖，發抖，打哆嗦：</span>Cooper arrived, quivering with rage. 庫珀來了，氣得直打哆嗦。/ Mack made his voice quiver with fear on these last two words. 麥克說出最後這兩個字時，嚇得聲音顫抖。<span class="definition">n. [C] 讓身體微顫的一種感情；身體部位的微顫：</span>He felt a quiver of excitement run through him. 他覺得渾身上下都興奮起來。/ Jane couldn't help the quiver in her voice. 簡不禁聲音顫抖。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**shiver**</span> [ˈʃɪvə(r)]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (with sth)（人）（因寒冷、恐懼、激動等）顫抖、哆嗦：</span>to shiver with cold, to shiver with excitement, to shiver with pleasure 冷得發抖；激動得發抖；高興得發抖等 / Don't stand outside shivering─come inside and get warm! 別站在外面凍得打哆嗦了——進來暖暖身子吧！/ He shivered in the cold. 他凍得發抖。/ Her body ached and shivered from the frosty air. 冰冷的空氣凍得她全身疼痛，不停地打戰。/ He shivered at the thought of the cold, dark sea. 那寒冷黑暗的大海，他想想都嚇得發抖。/ I was sitting on the floor shivering with fear. 我坐在地板上，嚇得發抖。/ He shivered at the thought of having to run for election.（比喻用法）他一想到要參加競選就渾身哆嗦。/ He shivered at the thought of it. 他一想到這件事就打哆嗦。/ She shivered involuntarily as he approached her. 他離她越來越近，她不由自主地顫抖起來。/ His cruel and callous comments made me shiver. 他殘酷無情的批評令我膽寒。<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（因寒冷、恐懼、激動等）顫抖、哆嗦：</span>A shiver of unease ran through the audience. 一陣不安的戰慄在觀眾中迅速蔓延。/ The sound of his voice sent shivers down her spine. 一聽見他的說話聲，她就背上一陣陣發冷。/ His appearance sent a cold shiver down her spine. 他的出現令她脊樑骨一陣發涼。/ He felt a cold shiver of fear run through him. 他嚇得打了一個寒戰。/ ‘I'm scared, ’ she admitted, with a shiver. 她顫抖著承認說：“我嚇壞了！”/ a shiver of fear 嚇得發抖 / Her beautiful voice sends shivers down your spine. 她那甜美的聲音令人震顫。/ a delicious shiver of pleasure 快樂的戰慄 / She gave a little shiver and laughed. 她身子微顫，笑了起來。/ He felt a shiver of excitement. 他興奮得發抖。/ She tried to suppress a shiver of anticipation. 她試圖控制住自己滿懷期待的顫抖。<span class="definition">2 [pl.] the shivers指（因恐懼或發高燒）寒戰、寒噤：</span>I don't like him. He gives me the shivers. 我不喜歡他，一見他我就不寒而慄。/ Just thinking about flying gives me the shivers. 一想到要坐飛機我就不寒而慄。/ I get the shivers when I think about it. 一想到這件事我就不寒而慄。/ Symptoms include headaches, vomiting and the shivers. 症狀包括頭痛、嘔吐和打寒噤。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**chatter**</span> [ˈtʃætə(r)]
<span class="definition">vi.（牙齒）因為冷或害怕而打顫：</span>She was so cold her teeth chattered. 她凍得牙齒咯咯響。<span class="definition">n. [U] 牙齒的打顫聲：</span>the chatter of teeth 牙齒打顫的咯咯聲

<span class="vocabulary">**knock**</span> [nɒk] 
<span class="definition">vi.（膝蓋）打哆嗦（例如由於恐懼）：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**wobbly**</span> [ˈwɒbli; 美 ˈwɑ:bli]
<span class="definition">adj. 1 [非正式]（尤指因為害怕、生病或筋疲力盡而）顫抖的，打戰的，顫巍巍的，站不穩的：</span>He's still a bit wobbly after the operation (= not able to stand firmly). 他動了手術之後仍然有點兒站不穩。/ She could not maintain her balance and moved in a wobbly fashion. 她無法保持平衡，走路顫顫巍巍。/ Ryan was exhausted by the flight and walked off with wobbly legs to find Clark. 飛行旅程讓瑞安筋疲力盡，他雙腿顫抖著走出來尋找克拉克。/ When I stood up my legs went all wobbly.（go尤為英式用法）我站起來時感覺雙腿直打顫。<span class="definition">2 [非正式]（某人的聲音）發抖的、顫抖的（如要哭時聲音顫抖的）：</span>'So that's why I want to go home,' he said in a wobbly voice. “所以這就是我要回家的原因，”他聲音顫抖地說。/ the wobbly singing of the choir 唱詩班發顫的歌聲
