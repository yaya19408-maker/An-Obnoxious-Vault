☀ <span class="category">**發熱 咳嗽：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**fever**</span> ['fi:və] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 指身體體溫高於正常水平，即發燒，發熱：</span>She had a very high fever. 她發高燒。/ mild, slight fever 低燒 / persistent fever 持續發燒 / drugs which can help to bring down the fever 有助於退燒的藥

<span class="vocabulary">**fever**</span> ['fi:və] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] [舊時] 主要用於構成複合詞，指某類熱病：</span>dengue fever 登革熱 / rheumatic, scarlet fever 風溼熱；猩紅熱
           
<span class="vocabulary">**glandular fever**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [U] 腺熱；傳染性單核細胞增多症：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**mononucleosis**</span> [ˌmɒnəʊˌnju:kliˈəʊsɪs; 美 ˌmɑ:noʊˌnu:kliˈoʊsɪs]
<span class="definition">n. [U] [醫學] 單核細胞增多症；腺熱。相當於glandular fever：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**dry**</span> [draɪ] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 指皮膚或頭髮乾枯的：</span>dry and cracked lips 乾裂的嘴唇 / dry hair 乾燥的髮質 <span class="definition">2 乾咳的：</span>a dry hacking cough 猛烈的乾咳

<span class="vocabulary">**cough**</span> [kɔ:f] 
<span class="definition">vi. 咳嗽：</span>to begin to cough violently 開始劇烈地咳嗽 / I can’t stop coughing. 我咳嗽不止。/ to have a coughing fit 一陣咳嗽 / to cough politely 斯文地咳嗽 <span class="definition">vt. cough sth (up) 把…（如血、痰等）從喉嚨或肺中咳出：</span>to cough blood 咳血 / to cough mucus 咳出粘液 / to cough phlegm 咳痰 <span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 咳嗽，或咳嗽聲：</span>Coughs can spread infections. 咳嗽能傳播疾病。/ He gave a little cough to attract my attention. 他輕輕地咳了一聲以引起我的注意。<span class="definition">2 [C] 使人咳嗽的疾病：</span>to have a dry cough 乾咳 / to have a persistent cough 頑固的咳嗽 / to have a hacking cough 頻繁地咳嗽 / I can’t seem to shake off the cough. 我的咳嗽好像老也好不了。/ to stifle a cough 忍住咳嗽 / cough medicine, cough syrup 止咳藥；止咳糖漿
           
<span class="vocabulary">**hawk**</span> [hɔ:k]
<span class="definition">vt. 用力咳痰：</span>He hawked and spat. 他用力咳了咳，吐了口痰。
