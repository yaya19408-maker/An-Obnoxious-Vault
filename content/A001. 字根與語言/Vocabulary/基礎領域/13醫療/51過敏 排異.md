☀ <span class="category">**過敏 排異：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**react**</span> [rɪ'ækt] 
<span class="definition">vi. 生物體接觸某種或某些物質（如吃進食物、施用藥物、吸入…、觸控…等）後發生不良反應：</span>to react to certain food additives 對某些食品新增劑產生不良反應 / Someone allergic to milk is likely to react to cheese. 對牛奶過敏的人可能會對乳酪也有不良反應。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**reaction**</span> [riˈækʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 身體對藥物、化學物質等作出的生理反應，尤指壞的生理反應：</span>to have an allergic reaction to a drug 對某藥物有過敏反應 / Every year, 5000 people have life-threatening reactions to anaesthetics. 每年有5,000人對麻醉劑產生致命反應。/ a delayed reaction to the drugs 對藥物的延遲的不良反應 / inflammatory reaction 炎性反應 / A small minority of patients suffer an adverse reaction to the treatment. 少數病人對該療法有不良反應。/ She had a very bad allergic reaction to the peanuts. 她對花生嚴重過敏。/ Common foods which cause this kind of reaction are fish, eggs, and shellfish. 引發這種不良反應的常見食物包括魚、蛋和貝類。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**allergy**</span> [ˈælədʒi; 美 ˈælərdʒi]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] ~ (to sth)（醫學上的）過敏反應或變態反應：</span>Food allergies can result in an enormous variety of different symptoms. 食物過敏會引發很多不同的症狀。/ Eggs are common sources of food allergies in children.雞蛋是兒童常見的食物過敏源。/ milk, nut, peanut, penicillin, pollen allergy 牛奶過敏；堅果過敏；花生過敏；青黴素過敏；花粉過敏 / respiratory allergy 呼吸道過敏 / Hair and feathers can trigger allergies. 毛髮和羽毛能引發過敏。/ allergy sufferer 過敏患者 / allergy specialist過敏疾病專家 / allergy symptom 過敏症狀 / allergy attack 過敏發作 / allergy test過敏試驗 / allergy drug, allergy medication（尤美）, allergy medicine（尤美）, allergy shot（美）抗過敏藥物；抗過敏藥的皮下注射 / In severe cases your doctor may recommend allergy shots. 如果情況嚴重，醫生會建議打幾針抗過敏藥。/ allergy season 過敏季節 / He developed an allergy to pollen. 他患了花粉過敏。/ I have an allergy to animal hair. 我對動物毛過敏。/ Allergy to cats is one of the commonest causes of asthma. 對貓類過敏是哮喘病發作最常見的誘因之一。

<span class="vocabulary">**allergic**</span> [ə'lə:dʒɪk] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 allergic (to sth) 對…過敏的。是“過敏”的常規用詞：</span>I’m allergic to cats. 我對貓過敏。<span class="definition">2 由過敏引起的：</span>an allergic rash 過敏性皮疹 / to cause allergic reactions 引起過敏反應
   
<span class="vocabulary">**sensitive**</span> ['sensɪtɪv] 
<span class="definition">adj. sensitive (to sth)（對低溫、光、食物等）反應過敏的（即反應過快或過大）：</span>sensitive areas of the body 身體的敏感區域 / My teeth are very sensitive to cold food. 我的牙齒對冷食過敏。/ These drugs can make skin extremely sensitive to sunlight. 這些藥物會造成皮膚對日照極度敏感。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**sensitivity**</span> [ˌsensəˈtɪvəti]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C, usually pl.] [術語] 對食物、低溫、光照等的敏感性、過敏性：</span>food sensitivity 食物過敏 / allergies and sensitivities 過敏反應 / Some children develop a sensitivity to cow's milk. 有的孩子對牛奶過敏。/ The eyes of some fish have a greater sensitivity to light than ours do. 有些魚的眼睛對光比我們敏感。/ Migrating birds show extreme sensitivity to air currents. 遷徙中的鳥對氣流極為敏感。           

<span class="vocabulary">**hypersensitive**</span> [ˌhaɪpəˈsensətɪv; 美 -pərˈs-]
<span class="definition">adj. ~ (to sth)（對某些化學物質、藥物、光等）極度敏感的：</span>Her skin is hypersensitive. 她的皮膚過敏。

<span class="vocabulary">**reject**</span> [rɪ'dӡekt] 
<span class="definition">vt.（身體）排斥或排異（移植過來的新器官）：</span>The organs are automatically rejected by the immune system. 這些器官受到免疫系統的自動排斥。

<span class="vocabulary">**intolerance**</span> [ɪnˈtɒlərəns; 美 -ˈtɑ:l-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] [術語]（對食物、藥物等）的過敏、不耐受：</span>food intolerance 食物不耐受 / glucose, lactose intolerance 葡萄糖不耐受；乳糖不耐受 / an intolerance to milk/diary products 對乳製品過敏
            
<span class="vocabulary">**intolerant**</span> [ɪnˈtɒlərənt; 美 -ˈtɑ:l-]
<span class="definition">adj. [術語]（對食物、藥物等）過敏；不耐受的：</span>recipes for people who are gluten intolerant 谷蛋白過敏人士的食譜
