☀ <span class="category">**症狀 症候：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**symptom**</span> ['sɪmptəm] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（疾病的）症狀，症候，通常用symptoms：</span>Symptoms include a headache and fever. 症狀包括頭痛和發熱。/ typical, common symptoms 典型症狀；常見症狀 / clinical symptoms 臨床症狀 / mild symptoms 輕微的症狀 / respiratory symptoms 呼吸系統症狀 / allergic, asthma, flu, menopausal symptoms 過敏症狀；哮喘症狀；流感症狀；更年期症狀 / depressive symptoms 抑鬱症症狀 / If symptoms do not improve after a week, see your doctor. 一週後如果症狀不見改善，請就醫。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**syndrome**</span> [ˈsɪndrəʊm; 美 -droʊm]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（某一疾病的）綜合徵、綜合症狀：</span>PMS or premenstrual syndrome 經前綜合徵 / This syndrome is associated with frequent coughing. 這種綜合徵與經常咳嗽有關。/ Irritable bowel syndrome seems to affect more women than men. 女性比男性更易患腸易激綜合徵。/ The syndrome is more likely to strike those whose immune systems are already below par. 這種綜合徵更容易對那些免疫系統已經低於正常水平的人構成威脅。/ acute syndrome 急性綜合徵 / clinical syndrome 臨床綜合徵 / Asperger's syndrome 阿斯伯格綜合徵 / This phenomenon has become known as the ‘Californian syndrome’. 這種現象已被稱為“加利福尼亞綜合徵”。/ This syndrome mostly affects women in their forties. 這種綜合徵多出現在四十多歲的女性身上。/ Chronic fatigue syndrome is characterized by severe physical and mental fatigue. 慢性疲勞綜合症的特點是極度的身體和精神疲勞。

<span class="vocabulary">**complication**</span> [ˌkɒmplɪˈkeɪʃn; 美 ˌkɑ:m-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl.] [醫學] 併發症：</span>She developed complications after the surgery. 她手術後出現了併發症。/ Blindness is a common complication of diabetes. 失明是糖尿病常見的併發症。/ He died of complications from a heart attack. 他死於心臟病發作引起的併發症。/ acute, dangerous, life-threatening, serious, severe complications 急性併發症；危及生命的併發症；嚴重的併發症 / fatal complications 致命的併發症 / chronic, long-term complications 慢性併發症；長期併發症 / minor complications 輕微的併發症 / significant complications 嚴重的併發症 / common complications 常見併發症 / rare complications 罕見併發症 / local complications 區域性併發症 / potential complications 可能的併發症 / birth, diabetic, medical, post-operative, pregnancy complications 生育併發症；糖尿病併發症；醫療併發症；術後併發症；妊娠併發症 / She developed complications two weeks after the treatment. 她在治療兩個星期後出現併發症。/ Complications develop if the drug is not used correctly. 這種藥物使用不當會引起併發症。/ complications rates from eye surgery 眼科手術的併發症發生率 / complications with her pregnancy 她懷孕時出現的併發症 / He died of complications from cancer surgery. 他死於癌症手術引發的併發症。/ the devastating complications of diabetes 糖尿病的嚴重併發症 / The treatment carries a high risk of complications. 這種療法很容易引起併發症。/ He died from complications related to diabetes. 他死於與糖尿病相關的併發症。
