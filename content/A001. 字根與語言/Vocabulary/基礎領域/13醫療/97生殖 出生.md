☀ <span class="category">**生殖 出生：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**period**</span> ['pɪərɪəd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 婦女的月經期：</span>monthly periods 月經 / heavy, light period 月經量多；月經量少 / menstrual period 經期 / When did you last have a period? 你上一次月經是什麼時候來的？/ I have my period and don’t feel too great. 我來月經了，感覺不太舒服。/ period cramps（尤美）, period pains（尤英）痛經
           
<span class="vocabulary">**menopause**</span> [ˈmenəpɔ:z]
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.]（通常用the menopause）女性的絕經期（通常在50歲左右）：</span>to reach (the) menopause 到更年期 / early, premature menopause 過早停經 / a disease that can afflict women past the menopause（past the menopause為英式用法）更年期後的婦女易患的一種疾病 / The male menopause affects men who are approaching middle age.（英）即將步入中年的男入會經歷男性更年期。/ Male menopause affects men who are approaching middle age.（美）即將步入中年的男人會經歷男性更年期。

<span class="vocabulary">**presentation**</span> [͵prezən'teɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 醫學中可指胎兒的臨產胎位：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**bear**</span> [beə] 
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 生孩子：</span>She has borne him a daughter. 她為他生了個女兒。

<span class="vocabulary">**have**</span> [hæv] 
<span class="definition">vt. 生孩子。是一個簡單用詞：</span>She’s going to have a baby. 她快生孩子了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pregnancy**</span> [ˈpregnənsi]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 懷孕、孕期：</span>Many women experience sickness during pregnancy. 許多婦女在懷孕期都會有噁心現象。/ It would be wiser to cut out all alcohol during pregnancy. 在懷孕期間最好滴酒不沾。/ She was exhausted by eight pregnancies in 13 years. 13年中懷孕8次讓她疲憊不堪。/ early pregnancy 妊娠早期 / late pregnancy 妊娠晚期 / full-term, post-term pregnancy 足月的妊娠；超過預產期的妊娠 / She had a difficult pregnancy with her first child. 她懷第一個孩子時很困難。/ high-risk pregnancy 高危妊娠 / emergency surgery for an ectopic pregnancy 宮外孕的急診手術 / twins or other multiple pregnancies 雙胞胎或其他多胎妊娠 / teen（尤美）, teenage pregnancy 十幾歲少女懷孕 / out-of-wedlock, unwed pregnancy（均尤美）婚外懷孕；未婚懷孕/ unintended, unplanned, unwanted pregnancy 意外的懷孕；計劃外的懷孕；不希望的懷孕 / There are thousands of unwanted teenage pregnancies every year. 每年都有數千例少女意外懷孕事件。/ They took no precautions to avoid pregnancy. 他們沒有采取避孕措施。/ 50% of all pregnancies are not planned. 有50%的妊娠是計劃外的。/ The doctor confirmed my pregnancy and told me to come back at 12 weeks. 醫生證實我懷孕，並告訴我在第 12 周時再來。/ the difficult decision about whether to continue with a pregnancy 對是否終止妊娠的艱難決定 / I tried hard to hide my pregnancy. 我極力隱瞞我懷孕的事實。/ the right of a woman to terminate her pregnancy 女性終止妊娠的權利 / Here's what can happen as your pregnancy progresses. 這是隨妊娠的進展可能發生的情況。/ women whose pregnancies have gone to term 妊娠足月的婦女 / pregnancy test 驗孕 / a home pregnancy test kit 一套家用驗孕用具 / Teen pregnancy rates have fallen. 少女懷孕比例有所下降。/ pregnancy complications 妊娠併發症 / Lower blood pressure is common in early pregnancy. 在懷孕早期血壓降低是很常見的。/ You will be tested throughout your pregnancy. 在整個妊娠期你都會接受檢查。/ a woman who intends to carry her pregnancy to term 一位打算懷孕至足月的女性

<span class="vocabulary">**conception**</span> [kənˈsepʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 懷孕、受孕：</span>the moment of conception 受孕的一刻 / Six weeks after conception your baby is the size of your little fingernail. 懷孕六個星期後，胎兒的大小相當於小拇指的指甲。/ Teenage conceptions have risen steadily in the last ten years. 過去十年中，少女懷孕的現象不斷增多。/ conception probability, conception rate 受孕機率；受胎率 / Sex identity is fixed at conception. 在受孕時嬰兒的性別就確定了。

<span class="vocabulary">**pregnant**</span> ['preɡnənt] 
<span class="definition">adj.（女人或雌性動物）懷孕的：</span>pregnant woman 孕婦 / She’s three months pregnant. 她懷孕三個月了。/ She was pregnant with twins. 她懷上了雙胞胎。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**conceive**</span> [kənˈsi:v]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 女子懷孕：</span>She is unable to conceive. 她不能懷孕。/ Their first child was conceived on their wedding night. 他們的第一個小孩是在新婚之夜懷上的。/ the Christian belief in Jesus as being immaculately conceived 認為耶穌成胎時蒙受天恩而未沾染原罪的基督教信仰

<span class="vocabulary">**carry**</span> ['kærɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. be carrying sb 可表示懷孕、懷胎：</span>She was carrying twins. 她懷上了雙胞胎。

<span class="vocabulary">**deliver**</span> [dɪ'lɪvə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 deliver a baby 接生，助產：</span>The baby was delivered by Caesarean section. 這個嬰兒是剖腹產下的。<span class="definition">2 be delivered of a baby 正式用法，指生孩子，分娩：</span>She was delivered of a healthy boy. 她生下一個健康的男孩兒。/ The baby was delivered safely on Tuesday night. 嬰兒在星期二晚上安全地生了下來。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**delivery**</span> [dɪˈlɪvəri]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 分娩的過程：</span>easy, difficult delivery 順產；難產 / premature, preterm delivery 早產 / breech delivery 臀位分娩 / vaginal delivery 陰道分娩/ Caesarean, Caesarean-section, C-section（美）delivery 剖宮產；剖腹產 / forceps delivery 產鉗分娩 / The figures show an increase in forceps deliveries. 資料顯示產鉗分娩數量增多。/ delivery room, delivery ward (= in a hospital, etc.) 產房；產科病房 / She had a very easy delivery with her second child. 她第二胎分娩很順利。/ In the end, it was an easy delivery: a fine baby boy. 分娩過程最終非常順利，生了個健康的男嬰。/ Premature birth is three times more likely for twins, and delivery at 36 to 38 weeks is normal. 雙胞胎的早產率要比一般的高出兩倍，在36到38周之間分娩很正常。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**induce**</span> [ɪnˈdju:s; 美 -du:s]
<span class="definition">vt. [醫學] 引產、催生：</span>an induced labour 引產 / We'll have to induce her. 我們得給她引產。/ He might decide that it is best to induce labour. 他也許決定最好引產。

<span class="vocabulary">**labour**</span> ['leɪbə]（美labor）
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（作可數名詞時常用單數）指分娩的一段時間或過程：</span>difficult, easy labour 難產；順產 / labour ward（英）產房 / labour pains 分娩時的陣痛 / She was in labour for ten hours. 她分娩長達十個小時。

<span class="vocabulary">**birth**</span> [bə:θ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（嬰兒的）出生；分娩：</span>the birth of his grandson 他孫子的出生 / He has been deaf from birth. 他先天失聰。/ normal birth 正常分娩；順產 / difficult birth 難產 / premature, preterm birth 早產 / illegitimate birth 非婚生 / home, hospital birth 在家中分娩；在醫院分娩 / She gave birth to a baby boy. 她生了一個小男孩。/ to attend the birth 接生 / birth certificate 出生證明 / birth date 出生日期 / birth defect 出生缺陷 / birth mother 生母 / birth attendant 助產士 / birth rate 出生率 / births, marriages and deaths 出生、結婚和死亡
                     
<span class="vocabulary">**childbirth**</span> [ˈtʃaɪldbɜ:θ; 美 -bɜ:rθ]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 分娩的過程、生孩子的過程：</span>pregnancy and childbirth 懷孕及分娩 / His wife died in childbirth. 他妻子生孩子的時候死了。/ natural childbirth 自然分娩 / childbirth educator（美）分娩指導師 / childbirth class（尤美）分娩課

<span class="vocabulary">**confinement**</span> [kənˈfaɪnmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] [正式或舊時] 分娩的一段時間（包括分娩前和分娩中的時間）：</span>the expected date of confinement 預產期 / a hospital confinement 在醫院分娩 / There has been a movement to support women seeking home confinement. 一場支援女性在家分娩的運動一直在進行。

<span class="vocabulary">**abortion**</span> [ə'bɔ:ʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 人工流產，墮胎：</span>abortion laws 有關墮胎的法律 / I’ve always been anti-abortion. 我一直反對墮胎。/ illegal abortion 非法墮胎 / abortion clinic 人工流產診所 / abortion pill 墮胎藥 / abortion debate 關於墮胎的辯論
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**miscarriage**</span> [ˈmɪskærɪdʒ; British English also ˌmɪsˈk-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 流產：</span>to have a miscarriage 流產 / The pregnancy ended in miscarriage at 11 weeks. 懷孕到第11周便流產了。/ Her first pregnancy ended in miscarriage. 她第一次懷孕流產了。/ early miscarriage 早期流產 / threatened miscarriage 先兆流產 / spontaneous miscarriage 自然流產 / recurrent miscarriage 複發性流產 / Smoking during pregnancy increases the risk of miscarriage. 孕期吸菸會增加流產的危險。

<span class="vocabulary">**obstetrics**</span> [əbˈstetrɪks]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 產科學：</span>The training covers extended periods in obstetrics and gynaecology. 該培訓包括產科學和婦科學的增加課時。

<span class="vocabulary">**gynaecology**</span> [ˌgaɪnəˈkɒlədʒi; 美 -ˈkɑ:l-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 婦科學；婦科：</span>瞭解即可

☀ <span class="category">**避孕 節育：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**precaution**</span> [prɪˈkɔ:ʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] precautions可指避孕措施：</span>We didn't take any precautions and I got pregnant. 我們沒有采取任何避孕措施，所以我懷孕了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**contraception**</span> [ˌkɒntrəˈsepʃn; 美 ˌkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 避孕、節育；避孕法、節育法。相當於birth control：</span>to give advice about contraception 就避孕方法提供諮詢 / Use a reliable method of contraception. 採用安全的避孕方法。/ artificial contraception 人工避孕 / emergency contraception 緊急避孕 / oral contraception 口服藥避孕 / A lot of couples now use contraception. 許多夫妻現在都實行避孕。/ They never used any contraception. 他們從來沒使用過任何避孕方法。

<span class="vocabulary">**contraceptive**</span> [ˌkɒntrəˈseptɪv; 美 ˌkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 避孕藥；避孕器具；避孕措施：</span>oral contraceptives 口服避孕藥 / emergency contraceptive 緊急避孕藥 / prescription contraceptive（美）處方避孕藥

<span class="definition">adj. 避孕的、節育的：</span>a contraceptive pill 避孕藥丸 / contraceptive advice, contraceptive precautions, contraceptive methods 避孕諮詢；避孕措施；避孕方法
           
<span class="vocabulary">**condom**</span> [ˈkɒndɒm美 ˈkɑːndəm]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 避孕套：</span>a used condom 用過的避孕套

相關詞義延伸：
· abortion還可指墮胎手術。

周邊詞彙補充：
· born可指（從母體中）出生，出世。表示出生的結果或事實，而非過程；
· clone可指採用無性繁殖技術對動植物進行的複製，即克隆。
