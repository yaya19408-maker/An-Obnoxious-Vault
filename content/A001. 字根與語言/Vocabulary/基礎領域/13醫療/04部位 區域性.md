☀ <span class="category">**部位 區域性：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**part**</span> [pɑːt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（人的）身體部位：</span>the parts of the body 身體各部位 / He lost part of his foot. 他的一隻腳殘廢了。

<span class="vocabulary">**place**</span> [pleɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 身體上的某處。與part未見明顯區別：</span>He broke his arm in three places. 他胳膊上有三處骨折。/ The pain is always in the same place. 疼痛總是在同一個地方。

<span class="vocabulary">**spot**</span> [spɒt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 身體部位：</span>sore, tender spot 痛處 / He usually wears a hat to hide his bald spot. 他總是戴著帽子遮住頭上光禿禿的地方。/ sensitive spot（常為比喻用法）敏感部位（或痛處）
           
<span class="vocabulary">**region**</span> [ˈri:dʒən]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 身體的部位、區域（通常指有某個特性或問題的部位）：</span>the pelvic region 骨盆區 / the frontal region of the brain 大腦的額區 / pains in the abdominal region 腹部的疼痛 / brain, lumbar region 腦部；腰部 / tissue from the mouth region 口腔組織

<span class="vocabulary">**zone**</span> [zəʊn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（身體上尤指有別於周圍的）區域或部分：</span>the vulnerable zones of the body 身體的脆弱區域

<span class="vocabulary">**local**</span> ['ləʊkl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 常作前置定語，醫學上可指（身體上）區域性的：</span>local anaesthetic

<span class="vocabulary">**root**</span> [ru:t] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 頭髮、牙齒、指甲、舌頭的根部：</span>the roots of teeth 牙根

<span class="vocabulary">**ball**</span> [bɔ:l] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] ball of the foot/hand（大拇指與手掌之間或腳趾與腳掌之間的）球狀部位：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**quick**</span> [kwɪk] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the quick可指指甲下的柔軟的敏感的紅肉：</span>She has bitten her nails down to the quick. 她咬指甲都咬到了活肉。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**crook**</span> [krʊk]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ of your arm/elbow/leg 臂彎、肘彎；膝蓋的後側拐彎處：</span>She hid her face in the crook of her arm. 她把臉掩在臂彎裡。
