☀ <span class="category">**藥店 藥房：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**chemist**</span> ['kemɪst] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [英] chemist’s 可指（通常也出售肥皂、化妝品等的）藥店，藥房：</span>chemist’s shop, chemist shop, the chemist’s 藥房
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pharmacy**</span> [ˈfɑ:məsi; 美 ˈfɑ:rm-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 賣藥的商店或櫃檯，即藥店、醫藥櫃檯：</span>Make sure you understand exactly how to take your medicines before you leave the pharmacy. 在離開藥房之前，一定要弄明白自己的藥該怎麼吃。/ the pharmacy section of the drugstore 雜貨店的藥房 / community, local pharmacy 社群藥房；當地藥房 / Internet, online pharmacy 網際網路藥店；網上藥店 / retail pharmacy 零售藥店 / pharmacy business, pharmacy chain 製藥業；連鎖藥房 / pharmacy counter 醫藥櫃檯 / Research is bringing more effective new drugs to pharmacy shelves. 研究可使更有療效的新藥登上藥房的貨架。/ The cream is available from pharmacies without prescription.（from為英式用法，美式用法為at或in）這種藥膏可在藥店買到，無需提供處方。<span class="definition">2 [C] 醫院的藥房、配藥室：</span>hospital pharmacy 醫院藥房 / dispensing pharmacy 配藥房
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pharmacist**</span> [ˈfɑ:məsɪst; 美 ˈfɑ:rm-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [英] pharmacist's（複數形式為pharmacists）指藥店：</span>They sell vitamin supplements at the pharmacist's. 那藥店賣維生素補劑。

<span class="vocabulary">**drugstore**</span> [ˈdrʌgstɔ:(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [美] 美國兼售其它貨物（如化妝品、家居用品、飲料、小吃等）的藥店：</span>瞭解即可
