☀ <span class="category">**骨肉 管道：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**flesh**</span> [fleʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 人的肉：</span>the pale pink flesh 粉白色的肉 / The weapon tore into his flesh. 武器穿進了他的肉裡。/ The injury was only a flesh wound. 這只是皮肉傷。/ The story made his flesh creep (= made him feel afraid). 這個故事讓他毛骨悚然。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**muscle**</span> [ˈmʌsl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 肌肉：</span>calf, neck, thigh muscle 小腿肌肉；脖頸肌肉；大腿肌肉 / This exercise will work the muscles of the lower back. 這樣的運動可以鍛鍊腰部的肌肉。/ He didn't move a muscle (= stood completely still). 他一動不動地站著。/ Keeping your muscles strong and in tone helps you to avoid back problems. 保持肌肉強壯發達有利於預防背部疾病。/ The muscles in my face tensed. 我臉上的肌肉繃緊了。/ He is suffering from a strained thigh muscle. 他的大腿肌肉拉傷了，疼得很。/ muscle cell 肌細胞 / muscle fibre/fiber 肌纖維 / muscle tissue 肌肉組織 / muscle mass 肌肉塊 / muscle group 肌肉群 / muscle function 肌肉的功能 / muscle activity 肌肉活動 / muscle ache, muscle pain, muscle cramp, muscle fatigue, muscle paralysis, muscle stiffness, muscle strain, muscle weakness 肌肉疼痛；肌肉痙攣；肌肉疲勞；肌肉麻痺；肌肉僵硬；肌肉拉傷；肌肉無力 / muscle wasting 肌肉萎縮 / muscle loss 肌肉減少 / muscle contraction, muscle relaxation, muscle spasm, muscle tension, muscle twitch 肌肉收縮；肌肉放鬆；肌肉痙攣；肌肉緊張；肌肉抽搐 / muscle definition, muscle tone 肌肉線條；肌肉力度 / muscle size 肌肉塊頭 / muscle endurance, muscle strength 肌肉耐力；肌力 / muscle repair 肌肉修復 / muscle building 肌肉健美 / muscle builder 肌肉健身器 / muscle man（通常用muscleman）肌肉男 / muscle shirt（美）（顯示發達肌肉的）圓領無袖健美衫
           
<span class="vocabulary">**tendon**</span> [ˈtendən]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（連結肌肉和骨骼的）腱，肌腱：</span>damaged, torn tendon 損傷的肌腱；撕裂的肌腱 / Achilles, hamstring tendon 跟腱；膕腱 / tendon injury 肌腱損傷 / He had surgery for a torn tendon in his elbow. 他因肘部一處肌腱撕裂動了手術。

<span class="vocabulary">**hamstring**</span> [ˈhæmstrɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 膝蓋後的五根肌腱之一，連線著大腿的肌肉和小腿的骨頭，即膕繩肌腱：</span>a hamstring injury 膕繩肌腱受傷 / She's pulled a hamstring. 她拉傷了一條膕繩肌腱。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**ligament**</span> [ˈlɪgəmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 韌帶：</span>ankle, elbow, knee ligament 踝關節韌帶；肘部韌帶；膝部韌帶 / ligament damage, ligament injury, ligament problem（尤英）, ligament tear 韌帶受傷；韌帶問題；韌帶撕裂 / ligament surgery 韌帶手術 / I've torn a ligament. 我的韌帶撕裂了。/ He suffered torn ligaments in his knee. 他膝關節韌帶拉傷了。

<span class="vocabulary">**fat**</span> [fæt] 
<span class="definition">n. [U]（人或動物的）脂肪，肥肉：</span>body fat 身體脂肪 / baby（美）, puppy（英）fat 嬰兒肥；兒童的肥胖 / to put on fat 長胖 / Exercise helps you burn off excess fat. 運動有助於消耗多餘的脂肪。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**grease**</span> [gri:s]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 皮膚分泌的油脂：</span>the grease in his hair 他頭髮上的油 / His hair is thick with grease. 他的頭髮油乎乎的。

<span class="vocabulary">**bone**</span> [bəʊn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（人或動物的）骨頭：</span>to suffer broken bones 骨折 / bone loss 骨質流失 / ankle bone 踝骨 / animal, human bone 動物的骨頭；人的骨頭 / bone cancer 骨癌 / He’s all skin and bone after his illness. 他病後瘦得皮包骨頭。<span class="definition">adj. -boned 用於構成形容詞，指有某類骨頭的：</span>fine-boned 骨質細密的
           
<span class="vocabulary">**skeleton**</span> [ˈskelɪtn]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（支撐人或動物的）骨骼、骨架；骨骼標本：</span>The human skeleton consists of 206 bones. 人的骨骼由206塊骨頭組成。/ dinosaur, fish skeleton 恐龍骨架；魚骨架 / external, internal skeleton 外骨骼；內骨骼 / a partial skeleton of an undiscovered species 未知物種的部分骨架 / The other skeleton belonged to a young man. 另一具骨架是一名年輕男子的。            

<span class="vocabulary">**cartilage**</span> [ˈkɑ:tɪlɪdʒ; 美 ˈkɑ:rt-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 軟骨：</span>a serious knee cartilage injury 膝部軟骨組織嚴重受傷          
           
<span class="vocabulary">**spine**</span> [spaɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 脊柱、脊椎。相當於backbone：</span>fractured spine 脊椎骨折 / cervical, lumbar spine 頸椎；腰椎 / the nerves in the spine 脊椎神經 / the base of the spine 尾椎骨 / the length of sb’s spine 脊椎長度 / curvature of the spine 脊椎彎曲 / send shivers down sb’s spine（比喻用法）使人毛骨悚然 / An icy chill slid up my spine.（比喻用法）我覺得一股寒氣順著脊背躥上來。/ These speeches form the spine of his election campaign.（比喻用法）這些演講構成了他競選活動的主幹。/ Their protests stiffened the spines of party activists.（比喻用法）他們的抗議堅定了該黨積極分子的信念。/ The legislature must grow a spine and demand this.（美，比喻用法）立法機構必須而且需要長志氣。
    
<span class="vocabulary">**rib**</span> [rɪb]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 肋骨：</span>He sustained a broken/cracked/fractured rib. 他的肋骨斷了。/ rib cage胸廓 / rib injury 肋骨受傷 / I woke him up with a poke in the ribs. 我戳了一下他的肋骨把他弄醒了。/ Stop poking me in the ribs! 別捅我肋骨了！/ Her heart was thumping against her ribs. 她的心臟緊抵著肋骨砰砰跳動。/ My face was covered with bruises and I had a broken rib. 我的臉上全是瘀傷，肋骨還斷了一根。

<span class="vocabulary">**disc**</span> [dɪsk]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 椎間盤：</span>herniated（尤美）, prolapsed, slipped disc 突出的椎間盤；滑出的椎間盤 / He's been off work with a slipped disc (= one that has moved from its correct position, causing pain). 他因椎間盤突出一直未上班。/ I had slipped a disc and was frozen in a spasm of pain. 我有一個椎間盤錯位了，疼得我動彈不得。
<span class="vocabulary">**skull**</span> [skʌl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 顱骨、頭骨、頭蓋骨：</span>human skull 人的顱骨 / He suffered a fractured skull in an accident. 他在一次事故中顱骨骨折。/ the angel of death with his grinning skull 咧嘴微笑的死神骷髏頭 / skull fracture 顱骨骨折 / The ship was flying a pirate flag with the skull and crossbones on it. 那條船掛著一面畫有骷髏頭的海盜旗。/ The bottle had a skull and crossbones symbol on it. 瓶子上有一個骷髏頭標誌。
      
<span class="vocabulary">**cheekbone**</span> [ˈtʃi:kbəʊn; 美 -boʊn]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（眼睛下方的）顴骨：</span>high, prominent cheekbone 高高的顴骨；突出的顴骨 / She was very beautiful, with high cheekbones. 她顴骨高高的，非常漂亮。/ chiselled/chiseled, perfect, sharp cheekbone 輪廓分明的顴骨；完美的顴骨；清晰的顴骨 / The hollows beneath his cheekbones showed his stress. 他雙頰深陷，看得出他壓力很大。/ A flush of anger appeared on her cheekbones. 怒氣上衝，她頓時雙頰飛紅。/ The skin stretched taut over his cheekbones. 他的雙顴皮包骨頭。

<span class="vocabulary">**radius**</span> [ˈreɪdiəs]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [解剖學]（前臂上的）橈骨：</span>瞭解即可                      

<span class="vocabulary">**pelvis**</span> [ˈpelvɪs]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 骨盆：</span>broken, fractured pelvis 破損的骨盆；骨折的骨盆 / narrow, wide pelvis 狹窄的骨盆；寬大的骨盆

<span class="vocabulary">**hip**</span> [hɪp]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 髖骨：</span>dislocated hip, fractured hip 脫臼的髖關節；骨折的髖骨 / hip fracture 髖骨骨折 / hip injury 髖關節損傷 / hip pain 髖關節疼痛 / hip operation（尤英）, hip surgery（尤美） 髖骨手術 / hip bone, hip joint, hip socket 髖骨；髖關節；髖臼 / a hip replacement operation 髖部復位手術 / She broke her hip in the fall. 她摔倒時折斷了髖骨。/ He had his leg amputated at the hip. 他被高位截肢了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**joint**</span> [dʒɔɪnt]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（身體的）關節：</span>elbow, hip, knee joint 肘關節；髖關節；膝關節 / aching, achy（尤美）, inflamed, painful, sore, stiff, swollen joint 疼痛的關節；發炎的關節；僵硬的關節；腫脹的關節 / artificial joint 人工關節 / inflammation of the knee joint 膝關節發炎 / Her joints ache if she exercises. 她一運動關節就疼。/ He's going to have his hip joint replaced. 他準備接受髖關節置換。/ He dislocated his elbow joint. 他的肘關節脫臼了。/ Synovial fluid lubricates the joints. 滑液能潤滑關節。/ It is an inflammatory condition affecting the joints. 這是一種影響關節的炎症。 / He slowly stood up, joints creaking in protest. 他慢慢站起身來，關節發出嘎巴嘎巴的抗議聲。/ The joint should be able to move freely. 關節應該能活動自如。/ You have fluid in the joint. 你這個關節部位有積液。/ the joint between the lower and upper parts of the arm 小臂與大臂之間的關節 / She fell and put her knee out of joint.（英）她跌倒了，膝關節脫了臼。
    
<span class="vocabulary">**knuckle**</span> [ˈnʌkl]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 手指的關節，即指關節：</span>Brenda's knuckles were white as she gripped the arms of the chair. 布倫達握緊椅子扶手時指關節都變白了。/ swollen knuckle 腫脹的指關節 / He rubbed his hands together, cracking his knuckles as he tried to control his anger. 他搓著雙手，指關節噼啪作響，竭力控制著怒火。/ knuckle bone（美式英語中通常用knucklebone）指（關）節骨 / She was rapped over the knuckles by her boss for criticizing the company in the press.（比喻用法）她因為在報刊上批評公司而受到了老闆的斥責。

<span class="vocabulary">**passage**</span> ['pæsɪdӡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 體內用以輸送氣體、液體等的管道。是一種簡單的基礎用詞，任何一種管道都是一種passage：</span>nasal passage 鼻腔通道 / air passage 氣管 / back passage 直腸

<span class="vocabulary">**canal**</span> [kə'næl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 體內的各種管道，用於輸送血液、空氣、食物等。不如passage常用：</span>alimentary canal 消化道

<span class="vocabulary">**tube**</span> [tju:b] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 身體內部的管道（用於輸送氣體、液體等），常為柔軟的管道：</span>bronchial, capillary, Eustachian, Fallopian, neural tube 支氣管；毛細管；咽鼓管；輸卵管；神經管
           
<span class="vocabulary">**airway**</span> [ˈeəweɪ; 美 ˈerweɪ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [醫學]（從鼻子到喉嚨、肺部的）呼吸氣道：</span>an inflammation of the airways 呼吸道炎症
           
<span class="vocabulary">**artery**</span> [ˈɑ:təri; 美 ˈɑ:rt-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 動脈：</span>main, major artery 主動脈 / blocked, clogged artery 堵塞的動脈；阻塞的動脈 / narrowed artery 變窄的動脈 / severed artery 切斷的動脈 / The surgeon will widen her arteries. 外科醫生將給她擴張動脈。/ Too much fatty food will make your arteries more likely to clog up. 吃太多的高脂肪食物會阻塞動脈。/ artery wall 動脈壁 / artery blockage 動脈堵塞 / She suffers from hardening of the arteries. 她患有動脈硬化。/ patients suffering from blocked arteries 患動脈栓塞的病人
       
<span class="vocabulary">**vein**</span> [veɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 靜脈：</span>jugular vein 頸靜脈 / hepatic, pulmonary, renal vein（均為醫學術語）肝靜脈；肺靜脈；腎靜脈 / Many veins are found just under the skin. 許多靜脈血管就在皮膚下面。/ A blue vein throbbed in his fore-head.他額頭上的一根青筋突突直跳。/ broken（尤英）, spider（尤美）, thread（尤英）, varicose vein 破裂的靜脈；蛛狀靜脈曲張；絲狀脈紋；曲張的靜脈 / She uses make-up to hide the thread veins in her cheeks. 她靠化妝來遮掩面頰上的紅血絲。/ He felt the adrenalin coursing through his veins. 他感覺到自己血脈賁張。/ The philosopher Seneca opened his veins in his bath. 哲學家塞內加在浴缸裡割開了自己的靜脈。/ The veins stood out on her throat and temples. 她的脖子和太陽穴處青筋暴起。/ There's a drop of Irish blood in her veins. 她有一點兒愛爾蘭血統。

<span class="vocabulary">**vessel**</span> [ˈvesl]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（人或動物的）血管，脈管：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**blood vessel**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [C] 血管：</span>major blood vessel 大血管 / broken, burst blood vessel 破裂的血管 / He burst a blood vessel in a fit of coughing. 他一陣猛咳，咳破了一根血管。/ blood vessels supplying nutrition to the skin 給皮膚提供營養的血管

<span class="vocabulary">**socket**</span> ['sɒkɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 某事物表面上的一個弧形中空空間（其他部分可安裝在此或在其中隨意運動），如窩：</span>arm, eye, hip, shoulder, tooth socket 臂關節臼；眼窩；髖關節臼；肩關節臼；牙槽 / His eyes bulged in their sockets. 他的兩眼從眼窩裡鼓出來。
