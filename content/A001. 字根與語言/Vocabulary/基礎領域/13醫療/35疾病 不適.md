☀ <span class="category">**疾病 不適：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**disease**</span> [dɪ'zi:z] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（人、動植物所患的）疾病（常由細菌或感染引起）：</span>the rapid spread of disease in the area 疾病在這一地區的迅速傳播 / common disease 常見病 / obscure disease 疑難怪病 / acute disease 急性病 / degenerative disease 退行性疾病 / incurable disease 絕症 / curable disease 可治癒的疾病 / infectious disease 傳染性疾病 / inherited disease 遺傳性疾病 / childhood disease 兒童疾病 / tropical disease 熱帶疾病 / blood-borne diseases 血液傳染病 / occupational disease 職業病 / heart, liver disease 心臟病；肝病 / mental disease 精神病 / sexually transmitted disease 性傳播疾病 / respiratory disease 呼吸系統疾病等 / viral disease 病毒性疾病 / Parkinson’s disease 帕金森病 / foot-and-mouth, mad cow disease 口蹄疫；瘋牛病 / cause of disease 病因 / the symptoms of a disease 疾病的症狀

<span class="vocabulary">**ill**</span> [ɪl] 
<span class="definition">adj. [尤英；美常用sick] 常作表語，指患病的：</span>He was taken ill suddenly. 他突然病倒了。/ He is terminally ill with cancer. 他的癌症已到了晚期。/ He was lying ill in bed. 他臥病在床。/ terminally ill 身患絕症 / mentally, physically ill 精神上患病；身體上患病 / to be ill from malnutrition 營養不良
           
<span class="vocabulary">**ailing**</span> [ˈeɪlɪŋ]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] 有病的且沒有好轉跡象的：</span>She looked after her ailing father. 她照顧有病的父親。/ He is said to be ailing at his home in Washington. 據說他身體欠佳，正呆在華盛頓的家中。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**ailment**</span> [ˈeɪlmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 疾病、病痛，尤指輕病或小病：</span>common, minor, trivial ailment 常見病；小病；微恙 / chronic, serious ailment 慢性病；重病 / childhood ailment 兒童期疾病 / physical ailment 身體疾病 / mysterious ailment 怪病 / heart, respiratory, stomach ailment 心臟病；呼吸道疾病；胃病 / I got all the usual childhood ailments. 所有常見的兒童期疾病我都得過。/ The pharmacist can assist you with the treatment of common ailments. 藥劑師能幫你治療一般的病痛。

<span class="vocabulary">**illness**</span> ['ɪlnɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 身體或精神上患病的狀態，即疾病，病，生病：</span>I have missed a lot of school through illness. 我因病已經耽誤了很多課。/ the first case of illness from the Ebola virus 由埃博拉病毒引起的首個病例 <span class="definition">2 [C] 某種疾病；患病期：</span>terminal illness 絕症 / treatable illness 可以治療的疾病 / longstanding illness 沉痾 / degenerative illness 退行性疾病 / brief, short illness 短期疾病 / acute, chronic illness 急性病；慢性病 / Kerry died of a sudden illness. 克里暴病而亡。/ infectious illness 傳染病 / genetic illness 遺傳性疾病 / painful illness 折磨人的疾病 / common illness 常見病 / depressive, mental, psychological illness 抑鬱症；精神疾病；心理疾病 / flu-like, food-borne（美）, respiratory, smoking-related, viral illness 流感樣病症；食源性疾病；呼吸系統病症；與吸菸有關的疾病；病毒性疾病 / childhood illness 兒童疾病

<span class="vocabulary">**sick**</span> [sɪk] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1（身體或精神上）生病的，有病的：</span>a sick child 生病的孩子 / She’s very sick. 她病得很重。/ I can’t afford to get sick. 我生不起病。/ chronically（尤英）, extremely sick 慢性患病；病危 / mentally sick 患精神病 / She was sick with cancer. 她患了癌症。/ John’s not in the office today. He’s off sick. 約翰今天沒來上班，請了病假。<span class="definition">2 [尤英] 常作表語，想嘔吐的，即噁心的：</span>I feel sick. 我想吐。/ She got up and was sick in the sink. 她起身往水池裡嘔吐起來。/ The very thought of food made him feel sick. 一想到食物他就想吐。<span class="definition">3 –sick 用於構成複合詞，指由於乘船或飛機等而感到想吐的，即暈船，暈機，暈車：</span>carsick 暈車 / airsick 暈機 / seasick 暈船 <span class="definition">n. [U] [英，非正式] 嘔吐物：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**sickness**</span> ['sɪknɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 生病或不健康（的狀態）：</span>to have one week on sickness 生一星期的病 / insurance against sickness and unemployment 疾病和失業保險 / He’s been off work because of sickness. 他因病而沒有上班。/ I tricked my father by feigning sickness. 我裝病騙我父親。/ sickness absence（英）因病缺席 / sickness benefit（英）疾病補助金 / sickness rates 發病率 <span class="definition">2 [U, C, usually sing.] 某類疾病：</span>acute, chronic, severe sickness 急性病；慢性病；重病 / air, car sickness 暈機；暈車 / altitude, mountain sickness 高原病；高山病 / decompression, radiation, sleeping sickness 減壓病；放射病；昏睡病 <span class="definition">3 [U] [尤英] 噁心，嘔吐：</span>The sickness gradually passed. 噁心的感覺逐漸消退。/ Several workers complained of feelings of sickness and headaches. 幾個工人表示感到噁心、頭痛。
      
<span class="vocabulary">**dreadful**</span> [ˈdredfl]
<span class="definition">adj. [尤英]（看上去或感覺到）病得不輕的、極度疲憊的：</span>Jane looked dreadful (= looked ill or tired). 簡看上去臉色很不好。

<span class="vocabulary">**complaint**</span> [kəmˈpleɪnt]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指不嚴重、常影響身體某部位的）疾病：</span>Eczema is a common skin complaint which often runs in families. 溼疹是一種常見的皮膚病，通常具遺傳性。/ minor complaint 小病 / chronic complaint 慢性病 / Not being able to sleep at night is a very common complaint. 晚上睡不好覺是很常見的毛病。/ back, chest complaint 背部疾病；胸部疾病 / physical complaint 身體疾病 / medical complaint 疾病 / He has a minor skin complaint. 他有輕微的皮膚病。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**discomfort**</span> [dɪsˈkʌmfət; 美 -fərt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 輕微的病痛，輕微的身體不適：</span>bodily, physical discomfort 身體不適 / abdominal, chest discomfort 腹部不適；胸部不適 / You will experience some minor discomfort during the treatment. 治療中你會稍感不適。/ She carried her left arm at an awkward angle, as if it were causing her discomfort. 她的左胳膊很不自然地抬著，好像它讓她不舒服似的。/ Steve had some discomfort, but no real pain. 史蒂夫有些不適，但不是真疼。/ He became conscious of a growing discomfort. 他感到越來越不舒服。/ Some of the patients complained of discomfort. 其中一些病人主訴有不適感。/ I didn't have much discomfort after the operation. 手術後我並未覺得很不舒服。/ Not eating late at night should help to relieve the discomfort. 避免深夜進食應有助於減輕不適。/ He appeared to be in great discomfort. 他看上去很難受。/ You should be able to drive without discomfort after about two weeks. 大約兩週之後你就可以開車了，不會感到任何不適。/ the discomfort associated with wearing a wig 戴假髮引起的不適 <span class="definition">2 [C] [正式] 讓人不舒服的事物；讓人輕微疼痛的事物：</span>the discomforts of camping 露營的不適 / reducing the physical discomforts and difficulties faced by women 減少女性身體上的不適和不便

<span class="vocabulary">**bug**</span> [bʌg]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 輕微的傳染病：</span>nasty bug 嚴重的疾病 / flu, stomach, tummy bug 流感；胃腸傳染病 / He's off work with a flu bug. 他因為得了流感不能上班。/ A stomach bug has been going around at school. 一種腸胃病在學校流行起來。/ I picked up a bug in the office. 我在辦公室被傳染了疾病。

<span class="vocabulary">**malady**</span> [ˈmælədi]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（舊時用法）疾病：</span>He was stricken at twenty-one with a crippling malady. 他21歲時突患致殘重疾。
         
<span class="vocabulary">**wrong**</span> [rɒŋ] 
<span class="definition">adj. 只作表語，wrong (with sb/sth) 造成問題或困難的，不正常的，有毛病的：</span>There is nothing physically wrong with him. 他的身體沒有任何毛病。/ I have something wrong with my foot. 我的腳有點兒不對勁。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**nausea**</span> [ˈnɔ:ziə; ˈnɔ:siə]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（疾病或對某事的厭惡所導致的）噁心；反胃：</span>A wave of nausea swept over her. 她覺得一陣噁心。/ Nausea and vomiting are common symptoms. 噁心嘔吐是常見的症狀。/ I was overcome with a feeling of nausea. 我覺得想吐。/ Some people have nausea and vomiting after surgery. 有些人手術後會噁心嘔吐。/ The nausea had passed and I felt better. 噁心感消失了，我感覺好些了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**lurch**</span> [lɜ:tʃ; 美 lɜ:rtʃ]
<span class="definition">vi.（突然感到恐怖或激動時）胃猛地一跳（或動）：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">n. [usually sing.]（突然感到恐怖或激動時的）胃猛地一跳（或動）：</span>John felt a lurch of fear in his stomach. 約翰感到胃部一陣痙攣。

<span class="vocabulary">**turn**</span> [tə:n] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（胃）作嘔，噁心；使（胃）作嘔，噁心：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**heave**</span> [hi:v]
<span class="definition">vi. 噁心：</span>He gasped and heaved and vomited again. 他又開始又喘又吐起來。/ My stomach heaved and I felt sick. 我的胃翻騰起來，直想嘔吐。/ The thought of it makes me heave. 一想到那事我就噁心。

<span class="vocabulary">**gag**</span> [gæg]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (on sth) 不能吞下且幾乎要嘔吐，如噎得作嘔：</span>She gagged on the blood that filled her mouth. 她因嘴裡充滿了血而作嘔。/ I knelt by the toilet and gagged. 我跪在馬桶邊，一陣噁心。           
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vomit**</span> [ˈvɒmɪt; 美 ˈvɑ:m-]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth up) / ~ sth 嘔吐：</span>The smell made her want to vomit. 那氣味使得她想要吐。/ Any product made from cow's milk made him vomit. 任何乳製品都會讓他嘔吐。/ They gave her salty water to make her vomit. 他們給她喝鹽水好讓她吐出來。/ He had vomited up his supper. 他把晚飯吃的東西都吐了出來。/ He vomited up all that he had eaten for lunch. 他把午飯吃的東西全吐了出來。/ The symptoms include headaches, nausea and vomiting. 病症包括頭痛、噁心和嘔吐。/ vomiting and diarrhoea 嘔吐腹瀉 / The injured man was vomiting blood. 那受傷的人在吐血。/ She began to vomit blood a few days before she died. 她臨死前幾天開始吐血。<span class="definition">n. [U] 嘔吐物：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**hurl**</span> [hɜ:l; 美 hɜ:rl]
<span class="definition">vi. [美，俚語] 嘔吐：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**spew**</span> [spju:]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth) (up) [英，非正式] 嘔吐：</span>He spewed up on the pavement. 他在人行道上吐了。/ She spewed up the entire meal. 她把吃的飯全吐出來了。/ He's pissed. Let's get out of his way before he starts spewing. 他很惱火。趁他還沒有發作，我們趕快離他遠點兒。              
 
<span class="vocabulary">**woozy**</span> [ˈwu:zi]

<span class="vocabulary">**upset**</span> [ʌp'set] 
<span class="definition">vt. upset sb's stomach 使某人在吃了…或喝了…之後感到腸胃不適：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">adj. an upset stomach 可表示腸胃不適或腹瀉的：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">n. [C] 腸胃病（讓人想嘔吐或腹瀉）：</span>a stomach upset 拉肚子
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dysentery**</span> [ˈdɪsəntri; 美 -teri]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（作為一種疾病的）痢疾：</span>amoebic dysentery 阿米巴痢疾 / an attack of dysentery 痢疾的發作

<span class="vocabulary">**bend**</span> [bend] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] the bends可指潛水員因過快浮出水面所造成的劇烈疼痛和呼吸困難，即減壓病，潛涵病：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**failure**</span> ['feɪljə] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 身體部位的疾病或功能衰竭（身體部位出現故障時可稱為疾病，不過failure是從身體部位的功能運轉之正常與否的角度來描述的疾病）：</span>heart, kidney, liver, organ failure 心力衰竭；腎臟衰竭；肝功能衰竭；器官衰竭

<span class="vocabulary">**condition**</span> [kən'dɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 因不可能治癒而長期患有的疾病：</span>medical condition 疾病 / life-threatening, serious condition 危及生命的疾病；重病 / benign condition 沒有危險的疾病 / chronic condition 慢性病 / an incurable heart condition 一種無法治癒的心臟病 / mental condition 精神病 / a rare skin condition 一種罕見的皮膚病 / treatable condition 可以醫治的疾病 / The insurance will not cover you for a pre-existing condition. 該保險不承保你已患的疾病。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**collapse**</span> [kəˈlæps]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C, usually sing.] 重病：</span>She was on the verge of nervous collapse. 她幾乎精神崩潰。/ He was in a state of mental and physical collapse. 他身心崩潰。        

<span class="vocabulary">**trouble**</span> ['trʌbl] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 疾病，常是伴隨著疼痛的疾病：</span>back, heart trouble 背部不適；心臟不適
           
<span class="vocabulary">**defect**</span> [ˈdi:fekt; dɪˈfekt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 身體缺陷：</span>birth, congenital, genetic defect 出生缺陷；先天缺陷；遺傳缺陷 / All lambs are checked for birth defects when they are born. 所有羔羊出生時都要檢查是否有先天缺陷。/ developmental, physical, visual, eye, speech defect 發育缺陷；身體缺陷；視力缺陷；語言缺陷 / The child had a mild heart defect. 這孩子有輕微的心臟缺陷。/ He has a congenital heart defect. 他有先天性心臟缺陷。/ He was born with a hearing defect. 他患有先天性聽力缺損。/ This is a physical defect that cannot be cured. 這種身體缺陷是無法治癒的。/ character defect 性格缺陷 / a defect of her character 她性格上的缺陷
           
<span class="vocabulary">**deficit**</span> [ˈdefɪsɪt]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 生理或精神上的缺陷：</span>attention, cognitive, neurological deficit 注意力缺陷；認知障礙；神經功能缺損 / His teacher thought he had some sort of cognitive deficit. 他的老師認為他認知方面有某種缺陷。           

<span class="vocabulary">**handicap**</span> [ˈhændikæp]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] [變得過時，有時具有冒犯性] 長期的生理或智力缺陷（如弱智、殘疾）（患病者失去了運用某個身體部位或智力的能力）：</span>mental, physical, visual handicap 智力缺陷；生理缺陷；視力缺陷 / Despite her handicap, Jane is able to hold down a full-time job. 簡儘管有生理缺陷，卻能夠保住一份全職工作。/ He lost his leg when he was ten, but learnt to overcome his handicap. 他10歲時失去了一條腿，但他學會了克服自己的殘疾。           

<span class="vocabulary">**impediment**</span> [ɪmˈpedɪmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 言語障礙（如口吃、結巴）：</span>John's slight speech impediment made it difficult for his mother to understand him. 約翰有輕微的言語障礙，這令母親很難理解他的話。

<span class="vocabulary">**disorder**</span> [dɪsˈɔ:də(r); 美 -ˈɔ:rd-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 身體某部位的機能失調、紊亂、疾病、不適：</span>serious, severe, chronic, common, rare, genetic, inherited disorder 嚴重的紊亂；慢性病；常見病；罕見病；遺傳病 / blood, brain, bowel disorder 血液病；大腦功能障礙；鬧肚子 / circulatory, eating disorder 迴圈障礙；進食障礙 / Anorexia is a common eating disorder. 厭食症是一種常見的進食障礙。/ physical disorder 身體不適 / behavioural, emotional, mental, nervous, neurological, personality, psychiatric, psychological disorder 行為障礙；情緒障礙；心理障礙；神經錯亂；神經性障礙；人格障礙；精神失常；心理障礙 / attention deficit, bipolar, obsessive compulsive, post-traumatic stress, seasonal affective disorder 注意力障礙；雙相型障礙；強迫症；創傷後應激障礙；季節性情感障礙 / disorders of the digestive system 消化系統紊亂 / She suffers from a rare blood disorder. 她患有一種罕見的血液病。/ Her doctor diagnosed a theroid disorder. 她的醫生診斷她為甲狀腺疾病。/ a rare nerve disorder that can cause paralysis of the arms 一種可致手臂癱瘓的罕見的神經疾病 / a psychiatrist who specializes in eating disorders 一位專門治療飲食失調的精神病專家
           
<span class="vocabulary">**disturbance**</span> [dɪˈstɜ:bəns; 美 -ˈstɜ:rb-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 某人的頭腦或身體機能不能正常運轉的一種狀態，如失調、紊亂：</span>behavioural, emotional, mental, psychiatric, psychological disturbance 行為障礙；情緒失常；精神紊亂；心理失調 / teenagers suffering from a kind of psychological disturbance 受心理失調困擾的青少年 / menstrual, visual disturbance 月經紊亂；視覺障礙 / sleep disturbance 睡眠紊亂 / mood, personality disturbance 心境障礙；人格障礙 / 9% of children have a serious emotional disturbance. 9%的孩子有嚴重的情緒障礙。/ Many patients reported sleep disturbances after taking the drug. 很多患者報告服藥後產生了睡眠紊亂。/ a disturbance in liver function 肝功能失調 / behavioural disturbances in children 兒童的行為障礙 / the treatment of certain heart rhythm disturbances 某種心律失常的治療方法

說明：表“疾病”時，①disease幾乎是最廣義的“疾病”。第一，雖然可指人（或動植物）所患的、常由細菌或感染引起“疾病”，但幾乎可指任何的健康不佳之狀況；第二，該詞可用於構成很多疾病的名稱（以表示具體疾病）。且其在此方面的構詞能力遠超本組其它詞；第三，無論身體上的還是精神上的、無論輕度還是重度、無論急性還是慢性、無論短期還是長期的“疾病”，都在其涵蓋範圍之內；②illness首先比sickness稍微正式；其次，illness一般是一種長期疾病（有時也可表示短期疾病）；第三，可以是肉體上的疾病，也可以是精神上的疾病（如精神失常）；第四，有時可用於構成具體疾病的名稱，但在此方面遠遠不如disease；此外，當其為可數名詞時，表示“某種疾病”或“患病期”；③sickness與illness有時可互換。但是，第一，sickness雖然可指長期和短期疾病，但通常用來指短期疾病；第二，一般指急性病和由外部原因導致的疾病；第三，尤其在英式英語中可指“噁心”、“嘔吐”類的疾病；第四，可用於構成具體疾病的名稱。在此方面的構詞能力比illness稍強，但依然遠不如disease。

周邊詞彙補充：
· syndrome可指綜合徵：
· epidemic可指疾病的流行；
· pandemic可指疾病在全國或全球範圍內的流行，即大流行；
· frigid可指（女子）性冷淡的。
