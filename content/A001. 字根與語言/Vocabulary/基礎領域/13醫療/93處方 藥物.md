☀ <span class="category">**處方 藥物：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**prescribe**</span> [prɪˈskraɪb]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ (sb) sth (for sth)（醫生）給…開處方、開藥；讓…採用（療法）：</span>Valium is usually prescribed to treat anxiety. 安定劑通常用於治療焦躁。/ He may be able to prescribe you something for that cough. 他也許能給你開一些咳嗽藥。/ Our doctor diagnosed a throat infection and prescribed antibiotic and junior aspirin. 我們的醫生診斷是喉部感染，並且開了一些抗生素和小劑量阿司匹林。/ She took twice the prescribed dose of sleeping tablets. 她服用了雙倍醫囑劑量的安眠藥片。/ The drug can no longer be legally prescribed. 開這種藥物不再合法。/ These drugs are widely prescribed to control high blood pressure. 這些藥物被廣泛地用在控制高血壓的處方里。/ It can be prescribed as a form of preventive medicine. 這種藥物可以作為一種預防藥來開。/ drugs prescribed for high blood pressure 為高血壓開的藥 / This drug is often prescribed for women with heart trouble. 這種藥通常是為有心臟疾患的女性開的。/ a drug prescribed to patients with an obsessive compulsive disorder 給強迫症患者開的一種藥物

<span class="vocabulary">**prescription**</span> [prɪ'skrɪpʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] prescription (for sth)（醫生開的）處方，藥方：</span>prescription drug, prescription medicine 處方藥 / The drug will require a physician’s prescription. 這種藥需要醫生的處方。/ The doctor gave me a prescription for antibiotics. 醫生給我開了抗生素。/ the usual prescription for asthma 治療哮喘病常開的處方 <span class="definition">2 [U] 開處方；開藥：</span>the prescription of drugs 開藥
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pharmaceutical**</span> [ˌfɑ:məˈsu:tɪkl; -ˈsju:-; 美 ˌfɑ:rməˈsu:-]
<span class="definition">adj. [只用於名詞前] 製藥的、配藥的、賣藥的：</span>a Swiss pharmaceutical company 一家瑞士製藥公司 / pharmaceutical products 藥物 / the pharmaceutical industry 製藥業 <span class="definition">n. [usually pl.] [術語] 藥物：</span>the development of new pharmaceuticals 新藥的開發 / the pharmaceuticals industry 藥物業 / Antibiotics were of no use, neither were other pharmaceuticals. 抗生素沒有效果，其他藥物也沒有作用。

<span class="vocabulary">**drug**</span> [drʌɡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 藥，藥物。是一種統稱：</span>prescribed drugs 處方藥 / wonder drug 特效藥 / anti-cancer, anti-inflammatory drug 抗癌藥；消炎藥 / psychoactive drug 精神藥物 / non-prescription, over-the-counter drug 非處方藥 / performance-enhancing drug 興奮劑 / drug company 醫藥公司 / drug prices 藥品價格 / a powerful drug against tuberculosis 治療肺結核的強效藥 / to take drugs 服藥 / a course of pain-killing drugs 一個療程的止痛藥 / The drug has some bad side effects. 這種藥有些不好的副作用。

<span class="vocabulary">**prescription**</span> [prɪ'skrɪpʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（醫生開的）藥，藥品，藥物：</span>a prescription charge 處方藥費 / I’m not sleeping even with the prescription the doctor gave me. 我服了醫生給我開的處方藥還是睡不著。

<span class="vocabulary">**medicine**</span> ['medɪsn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 藥物（尤指藥水和口服藥）：</span>powerful, strong medicine 強效藥 / allergy, cough medicine 過敏藥；咳嗽藥 / anti-inflammatory medicine 消炎藥 / medicine for a chest infection 治療胸部感染的藥 / herbal medicine 草藥 / prescription medicine 處方藥 / non-prescription（尤美）, over-the-counter medicine 非處方藥 / medicine chest 藥箱 / medicine dropper 藥用滴管 / to mix some medicine 配一些藥 / shortage of medicine 藥品短缺 / Did you take your medicine? 你吃過藥了嗎？
                       
<span class="vocabulary">**medication**</span> [ˌmedɪˈkeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 藥、藥物：</span>prescribed, prescription medication 處方藥 / non-prescription（尤美）, over-the-counter medication 非處方藥 / regular medication 長期用藥 / antidepressant, anti-inflammatory, antiviral medication 抗抑鬱藥物；消炎藥；抗病毒藥物 / allergy, asthma, diabetes medication 治療過敏症的藥物；治療哮喘的藥物；治療糖尿病的藥物 / oral, topical medication 口服藥；外用藥 / herbal medication（美）草藥 / She stopped the medication because of side effects. 因為有副作用，她停藥了。/ He talked with his doctor about changing his medication. 他與大夫談了換藥的事。/ He is on regular medication for his fits.他長期服藥治療昏厥症。/ to be on medication 進行藥物治療 / Are you on any medication? 你在服用什麼藥嗎？/ Are you currently taking any medication? 你在服用什麼藥嗎？/ Many flu medications are available without a prescription. 許多流感藥不用處方就可以買到。/ She stopped taking the prescribed medications. 她不再服用醫生開的藥。

<span class="vocabulary">**draught**</span> [drɑ:ft;美dræft]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [舊時或文學] 液體形式的藥物，即藥水、飲劑：</span>a sleeping draught (= one that makes you sleep) 一服安眠飲劑          

<span class="vocabulary">**remedy**</span> [ˈremədi]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (for/to sth)（針對疾病或疼痛的）藥品：</span>herbal remedy 草藥 / He took a herbal remedy for his hay fever. 他服用草藥治療花粉熱。/ over-the-counter remedy 非處方藥 / cold, cough, hangover remedy 感冒藥；咳嗽藥；宿醉藥 / The player insists that he merely took a cold remedy and not a banned substance. 該運動員堅稱他只是服用了感冒藥而不是什麼禁藥。/ One dose of the remedy is sufficient. 這藥吃一服就夠了。/ The remedy was given in different strengths to a group of volunteers. 藥按照不同的濃度分發給了一組志願者。/ The remedies are all prepared from wild flowers. 這些藥物都是從野花中提取的。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**capsule**</span> ['kæpsju:l] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 裝藥（如藥粉或藥劑）的膠囊：</span>fish oil, vitamin capsule 魚油膠囊；維生素膠囊 / To avoid capture, he swallowed a cyanide capsule. 為了不當俘虜，他吞下了一顆氰化物膠囊。<span class="definition">2 [C]（醫學或科學上用的裝固態物質或液體的）小容器（常是塑膠容器）：</span>He first implanted a capsule into the animal’s brain. 他首先將膠囊植入那個動物的大腦。

<span class="vocabulary">**pill**</span> [pɪl] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（用於吞服而非咀嚼的）圓形藥丸，藥片（或維生素丸片等）：</span>diet, sleeping, vitamin pill 減肥藥丸；安眠藥片；維生素片 / prescription pill（尤美）處方藥 / You’ll have to take all these pills? 你得把這些藥全吃了。<span class="definition">2 [sing.] the pill 可指口服避孕藥：</span>She had been on the pill for two years. 她曾服用了兩年的避孕藥。/ contraceptive pill 避孕藥 / abortion pill 墮胎藥

<span class="vocabulary">**tablet**</span> ['tæblɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [尤英] 藥片，片劑，此時相當於pill：</span>half a tablet of aspirin 半片阿斯匹林 / Take two tablets with water before meals. 每次兩片，飯前用水送服。/ sleeping, vitamin tablet 安眠藥片；維生素片

<span class="vocabulary">**spray**</span> [spreɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（尤用於構成複合詞）噴劑：</span>nasal spray 噴鼻劑 / aerosol spray 氣霧劑

<span class="vocabulary">**drop**</span> [drɒp] 
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] drops 可指滴入眼睛、耳朵或鼻子裡的液體藥品，即滴劑：</span>eye drops 眼藥水 / to have these drops in his eyes 把這些藥水滴進他的眼睛裡
           
<span class="vocabulary">**ointment**</span> [ˈɔɪntmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 藥膏、軟膏：</span>antiseptic ointment 抗菌軟膏 / He put some ointment on the cut. 他在傷口上塗了些藥膏。/ A range of ointments and creams is available for the treatment of eczema. 有一系列治療溼疹的油膏和乳膏可以買到。/ He received ointment for his flaking skin. 醫生給他開了治療脫皮的軟膏。

<span class="vocabulary">**preparation**</span> [͵prepə'reɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 已被製備出來的，用作藥物的一種物質，如製劑：</span>a pharmaceutical preparation 藥劑

<span class="vocabulary">**herb**</span> [hə:b] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 藥用的草：</span>traditional herb 傳統的藥草

<span class="vocabulary">**oil**</span> [ɒɪl] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 藥品的油：</span>cod liver, fish, omega-3 oil 魚肝油；魚油；富含歐米伽-3的油
           
<span class="vocabulary">**plaster**</span> [ˈplɑ:stə(r); 美 ˈplæs-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（也可用sticking plaster）[均英] [英美也可用Band-Aid] 膏藥、創可貼、護創膠布：</span>瞭解即可           
           
<span class="vocabulary">**patch**</span> [pætʃ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 戒菸貼片：</span>nicotine patches 尼古丁戒菸貼片

<span class="vocabulary">**antibiotic**</span> [ˌæntibaɪˈɒtɪk; 美 -ˈɑ:tɪk]
<span class="definition">n. [usually pl.] 抗生素（如青黴素）：</span>The doctor put her on antibiotics (= told her to take them). 醫生要她服用抗生素。/ Your doctor may prescribe a course of antibiotics. 你的醫生可能會開一個療程的抗生素。/ Did you remember to take your antibiotics? 你記著吃抗生素了嗎？/ Meat from animals that receive antibiotics could not be classified as organic. 注射過抗生素的動物的肉不能歸為有機食品。/ I got an antibiotic for my ear infection. 我買了治療耳部感染的抗生素。<span class="definition">adj. 抗生素的：</span>an antibiotic drug 抗生素藥 / effective antibiotic treatment 有效的抗生素治療
           
<span class="vocabulary">**antiseptic**</span> [ˌæntiˈseptɪk]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 抗菌物質、抗菌劑：</span>Mint is a mild antiseptic. 薄荷是一種溫和的殺菌劑。/ She bathed the cut with antiseptic. 她用消毒劑浸洗傷口。/ You should put some antiseptic on that cut. 你應該在傷口上擦些殺菌劑。<span class="definition">adj. 1 能夠防止感染的，即抗菌的：</span>antiseptic cream, antiseptic lotion, antiseptic wipes 抗菌乳膏；抗菌洗液；抗菌擦劑 / These vegetables and herbs have strong antiseptic qualities. 這些蔬菜和草本植物有很強的殺菌性。<span class="definition">2 無菌的、消過毒的：</span>Cover the burn with an antiseptic dressing. 在燒傷處包上無菌敷料。            

<span class="vocabulary">**astringent**</span> [əˈstrɪndʒənt]
<span class="definition">adj. [術語]（液體或乳劑）能使皮膚或傷口收斂的、止血的：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">n. [C]（用於化妝品或藥物中的）收斂劑、止血劑：</span>瞭解即可          

<span class="vocabulary">**antidote**</span> [ˈæntidəʊt; 美 -doʊt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (to sth) 解毒藥：</span>There is no known antidote to the poison. 這種毒的解藥尚未發現。/ When he returned, he noticed their sickness and prepared an antidote. 他回來後注意到了他們的病狀，於是配製瞭解毒劑。/ The doctor administered an antidote. 醫生用了一種解毒藥。/ Quinine is a natural antidote for this fever. 奎寧是治療這種熱病的天然藥物。/ We do not have an effective antidote to this poison. 我們沒有針對這種毒的有效解毒劑。           

<span class="vocabulary">**sedation**</span> [sɪˈdeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 藥物鎮靜：</span>The attack victim is currently in hospital, under heavy sedation. 被襲者目前在醫院，服用了大劑量鎮靜藥物。/ His mother was under sedation after the boy's body was brought back from Germany. 男孩的屍體從德國運回後，他的母親一直靠服藥保持鎮靜。

<span class="vocabulary">**sedative**</span> [ˈsedətɪv]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 鎮靜劑。相當於tranquillizer：</span>powerful, strong sedative 強力鎮靜劑；強效鎮靜劑 / The doctor gave her a mild sedative to help her sleep. 醫生給了她一種藥性平和的鎮靜劑來幫她入睡。/ They use opium as a sedative, rather than as a narcotic. 他們把鴉片用作鎮靜劑而不是毒品。

<span class="vocabulary">**tonic**</span> [ˈtɒnɪk; 美 ˈtɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] 奎寧水，湯力水（一種味微苦、常加於烈性酒中的有氣飲料）：</span>a gin and tonic 一杯杜松子酒奎寧水 / Keeler sipped at his gin and tonic. 基勒小口抿著他加了奎寧水的杜松子酒。/ low-calorie tonics 低熱量的奎寧水 <span class="definition">2 [C]（尤指疲乏時服用的）補藥、滋補藥品：</span>herbal tonics 滋補草藥 / Britons are spending twice as much on health tonics as they were five years ago. 英國人如今在健康補藥上花的錢是五年前的兩倍。/ Ginseng is generally known for its tonic properties. 人參以其滋補功效而廣為人知。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**balm**</span> [bɑ:m]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C, usually sing.]（也可用balsam）（某類樹木、植物中提取的）香脂油（昔日用於療傷等）：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">2 [U, C, usually sing.] 有香味的、常被用於緩解疼痛或軟化皮膚的一種液體或乳膏：</span>lip balm 護唇膏
  
<span class="vocabulary">**repellent**</span> [rɪˈpelənt]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 驅蟲劑：(an) insect repellent 驅蟲劑 / mosquito repellent 驅蚊劑

<span class="vocabulary">**contraceptive**</span> [ˌkɒntrəˈseptɪv; 美 ˌkɑ:n-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 避孕藥：</span>oral contraceptives 口服避孕藥 / emergency contraceptive 緊急避孕藥 / prescription contraceptive（美）處方避孕藥

<span class="vocabulary">**vaccine**</span> [ˈvæksi:n; 美 vækˈsi:n]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 疫苗：</span>effective, safe vaccine 有效的疫苗；安全的疫苗 / flu, measles, rubella vaccine 流感疫苗；麻疹尤美；風疹疫苗 / The polio vaccine has saved millions of lives. 脊髓灰質炎疫苗挽救了數以百萬計的生命。/ live vaccine 活疫苗 / a live vaccine containing the polio virus含有脊髓灰質炎病毒的活疫苗 / one dose of BCG vaccine 一劑卡介苗 / The vaccine protects babies and children against tuberculosis. 這種疫苗能防止嬰幼兒染上肺結核。/ a vaccine to prevent rubella 預防風疹的疫苗 / $28 million raised for AIDS vaccine research 為艾滋病疫苗研究而籌集的2，800萬美元 / vaccine production 疫苗生產 / vaccine maker, vaccine manufacturer 疫苗製造者；疫苗生產商 / vaccine programme/program 疫苗計劃 / vaccine supply 疫苗供應 / vaccine shortage 疫苗短缺 / Researchers are trying to develop a vaccine against the disease. 研究人員正在努力研製預防這種疾病的疫苗。/ a vaccine for meningitis 預防腦膜炎的疫苗 / There is no vaccine against HIV infection. 現在還沒有防艾滋病病毒傳染的疫苗。/ Anti-malarial vaccines are now undergoing trials. 抗瘧疾的疫苗正在試驗階段。/ Seven million doses of vaccine are annually given to British children. 英國孩子每年要接種七百萬劑疫苗。

<span class="vocabulary">**dose**</span> [dəʊs; 美 doʊs]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（藥的）一劑，一服：</span>Repeat the dose after 12 hours if necessary. 如果需要，12小時後再服一劑。/ One dose of penicillin can wipe out the infection. 一劑青黴素就能消除感染。/ a strong dose of painkillers 大劑量止痛藥 / maximum dose 最大劑量 / cumulative dose 累計劑量 / standard dose 標準劑量 / lethal dose 致死劑量 / patients who receive high doses of this drug 接受高劑量的這種藥的病人 / I had forgotten to take my dose of antibiotic. 我忘記服用一劑抗生素。/ The nurse will administer the correct dose. 護士會按正確的劑量給藥。/ She needs to be given a daily dose of the medicine. 這種藥她需要每天用一定的劑量。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dosage**</span> [ˈdəʊsɪdʒ; 美 ˈdoʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.]（通常指藥物的）規律服用的劑量、用量：</span>high, low dosage 大劑量；小劑量 / correct, proper（尤美）, recommended dosage 正確的劑量；合適的劑量；推薦的劑量 / standard, usual dosage 標準劑量；常用劑量 / daily dosage 每日劑量 / Always take the correct dosage. 一定要用正確的劑量。/ Do not exceed the recommended dosage. 切勿超過規定劑量。/ He was put on a high dosage of vitamin C. 給他服了大劑量的維生素C。
               
<span class="vocabulary">**overdose**</span> [ˈəʊvədəʊs; 美 ˈoʊvərdoʊs]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（一次用藥的）過量：</span>a drug/drugs overdose 藥物劑量過大 / She took a massive overdose of sleeping pills. 她服用了過量的安眠藥。/ heroin overdose 海洛因過量 / fatal, lethal overdose 致命的過量用藥；致死的過量用藥 / accidental overdose 意外的過量用藥 / Each year, one in 100 girls aged 15-19 takes an overdose. 每年，每100名15至19歲的少女中就有1位嗑藥過量。/ In 1970, guitarist Jimi Hendrix died of a drug overdose. 1970年，吉他手吉米·亨德里克斯因服藥過量而死亡。
