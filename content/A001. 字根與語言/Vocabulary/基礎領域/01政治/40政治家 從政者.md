☀ <span class="category">**政治家 從政者：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**statesman**</span> ['steɪtsmən]  
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一位英明幹練、賢明謹慎並受人尊敬的政治領袖，通常級別較高，有明確的褒義色彩：</span>the party’s elder statesman 該黨元老 / a world statesman 國際政治家 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**statesperson**</span> [ˈsteɪtspɜ:sn; 美 -pɜ:rsn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 英明的、經歷豐富且受人尊敬的政治家：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**politician**</span> [pɒlɪ'tɪʃn]  
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 職業從政者，尤指政府成員。一般可指各層次的從政者，與statesman有一定交集，但statesman的褒義色彩比較濃厚，而politician此時為中性色彩：</span>popular, senior politician 受歡迎的政治家；政治元老 / career, professional politician 職業政治家 / local politician 地方從政者 / left-wing politician 左翼政治家 / Republican politician 共和黨從政者 <span class="definition">2 [C] 政客，為貶義。politician還可擴充套件為組織內的投機鑽營者，也含貶義：</span>corrupt politician 腐敗的政客 / to lobby politicians 遊說政客

<span class="vocabulary">**hack**</span> [hæk]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [貶] 二流政客，即對其政黨太過衷心但得不到應有的地位的人：</span>Far too many party hacks from the old days still hold influential jobs. 太多以前的老黨棍依然身居要職。           

<span class="vocabulary">**public servant**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [C] 公務員：</span>瞭解即可
