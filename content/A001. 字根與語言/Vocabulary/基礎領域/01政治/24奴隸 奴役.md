☀ <span class="category">**奴隸 奴役：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**slave**</span> [sleɪv]  
<span class="definition">n. [C] 奴隸：</span>plantation slave 種植園奴隸 / slave master, slave owner 奴隸主 / slave trade 奴隸貿易 / slave society 奴隸社會 / slave uprising 奴隸起義 / slave wages（比喻用法）微薄的血汗錢

<span class="vocabulary">**slavery**</span> ['sleɪvərɪ]  
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 作為奴隸的狀態，即奴隸身份：</span>to be sold into slavery 被賣為奴 / child slavery 兒童奴役 / racial slavery 種族奴役 / economic, wage slavery 經濟奴役；工資奴役 / to live in slavery 遭受奴役 / to free women from domestic slavery 把女性從家庭的奴役下解放出來 <span class="definition">2 [U]  擁有奴隸的做法，即蓄奴，奴隸制。與freedom相反：</span>long-term existence of slavery 奴隸制的長期存在 / plantation slavery 種植園奴隸體制 / slavery issue 奴隸制問題 / the abolition of slavery 奴隸制的廢除 / the institution of slavery 奴隸制度
