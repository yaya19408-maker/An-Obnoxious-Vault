☀ <span class="category">**官僚 腐化：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**style**</span> [staɪl]  
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] style (of sth) 做事的方式，作風：</span>an autocratic style of leadership 專制的領導作風 / political style 政治風格

<span class="vocabulary">**bureaucratic**</span> [͵bjʊərəʊ'krætɪk]  
<span class="definition">adj. 官僚的，官僚主義的，常含貶義：</span>bureaucratic procedures 官僚程式 / The organization is highly bureaucratic. 該組織非常官僚。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**bureaucracy**</span> [bjʊəˈrɒkrəsi; 美 bjʊˈrɑ:k-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] [常貶]（政府或組織機構的）官僚主義；官僚作風：</span>cumbersome, excessive, unnecessary bureaucracy 繁瑣多餘的手續；繁文縟節 / administrative, corporate bureaucracy 行政的規章手續；公司的規章手續 / People usually complain about having to deal with too much bureaucracy. 人們經常抱怨不得不應付太多的繁文縟節。/ The organization has promised to eliminate unnecessary bureaucracy. 該組織已承諾消除不必要的規章制度。<span class="definition">2 [U, C]（政府的）官僚體制；實行官僚體制的國家：</span>huge, large, massive, vast bureaucracy 龐大的行政體系 / bloated bureaucracy 臃腫的行政體系 / federal, government, state bureaucracy 聯邦官僚機構；政府官僚機構；國家行政機構 / the power of the state bureaucracy 國家官僚體制的權力 / State bureaucracies can tend to stifle enterprise and initiative. 國家的官僚體制往往會扼殺人們的事業心和積極性。/ living in a modern bureaucracy 生活在一個現代官僚體制中
           
<span class="vocabulary">**bureaucrat**</span> [ˈbjʊərəkræt; 美 ˈbjʊr-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [常貶]（組織機構或政府部門的）官僚主義者：</span>The economy is still controlled by bureaucrats. 經濟依然被官僚們所掌控。/ faceless bureaucrat 千人一面的官員 / He was just another faceless bureaucrat. 他只不過是一個典型的機械刻板的官員。/ corrupt bureaucrat 腐敗官僚 / unelected bureaucrat 非選舉產生的官員 / senior bureaucrat 高階官僚 / mid-level bureaucrat（美）中層官員 / federal, government, state bureaucrat 聯邦政府官僚；政府官僚；國家官僚 / career bureaucrat 職業官員 / battles between political appointees and career bureaucrats 政治任命人員與職業官僚之間的鬥爭
           
<span class="vocabulary">**red tape**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [U] [貶]（不必要的）繁文縟節；（造成辦事效率低下的）官僚作風：</span>administrative red tape 行政上的繁文縟節 / government, governmental（美）red tape 政府的繁文縟節 / How do we deal with all this red tape? 我們如何應對所有這些繁文縟節? / The little money that was available was tied up in bureaucratic red tape. 能用的那點兒小錢被繁文縟節拖得遙遙無期。

<span class="vocabulary">**corrupt**</span> [kə'rʌpt]  
<span class="definition">adj. 1（人或組織）貪汙的，營私舞弊的，即腐敗的：</span>morally, politically corrupt 道德敗壞的；政治腐敗的 / corrupt officials accepting bribes 接受賄賂的貪官汙吏 / a corrupt city council 貪汙腐敗的市議會 / The whole regime is thoroughly corrupt. 整個政權徹底腐敗了。 <span class="definition">2（行為）不誠實或不道德的：</span>corrupt practices 徇私舞弊 / The whole system is inefficient and corrupt. 整個體系都效率低下並且腐敗墮落。<span class="definition">vt. 對某人有壞的影響，使其行為不道德或不誠實，即讓某人墮落或腐化：</span>He was corrupted by power and ambition. 權力與野心使他腐化墮落。/ the corrupting effects of great wealth 巨大財富的腐蝕作用
           
<span class="vocabulary">**corruption**</span> [kəˈrʌpʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（尤指當權者的）腐敗、貪汙、受賄：</span>Corruption was rife before the election. 選舉之前，腐敗盛行。/ financial, political corruption 財政腐敗；政治腐敗 / high-level, official corruption 高層腐敗；官員腐敗 / corporate（尤美）, government, police, public（美）corruption 公司的腐敗；政府的腐敗；警察的腐敗；公共部門的腐敗 / He strongly attacked corruption in the government. 他猛烈抨擊政府的腐敗。/ This police unit was established to fight corruption. 這個警察機構是為了打擊貪汙腐敗而建的。/ She was brought down by a corruption scandal. 她因貪腐醜聞而下臺。/ corruption allegation, corruption charge 腐敗指控 / The corruption allegations proved false. 有關腐敗的指控被證明是不實的。/ corruption investigation, corruption probe 腐敗調查 / corruption case 腐敗案 / corruption among high-ranking officials 高階政府官員中的腐敗 / corruption in government 政府腐敗 / a culture of corruption 腐敗文化 / allegations of bribery and corruption 對賄賂與貪汙的指控 / The President faces 54 charges of corruption and tax evasion. 總統面臨54項腐敗和逃稅的指控。/ Distribution of food throughout the country is being hampered by inefficiency and corruption. 低效和腐敗行為阻礙了食品在全國的配送。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**degenerate**</span> [dɪˈdʒenəreɪt]
<span class="definition">adj. 道德墮落的：</span>the degenerate attitudes he found among some of his fellow officers 他在一些警隊同僚身上發現的腐化態度
