☀ <span class="category">**教義：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**doctrine**</span> [ˈdɒktrɪn; 美 ˈdɑ:k-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（宗教的）教義：</span>Catholic, Christian, Islamic doctrine 天主教教義；基督教教義；伊斯蘭教教義 / church doctrine 教會的教義 / religious 宗教教義 / orthodox doctrine 正統學說 / false doctrine 偽學說 / the doctrine of reincarnation 輪迴轉世說 / She rejected the traditional Christian doctrines. 她摒棄了傳統的基督教教義。/ The Church welcomed all who were considered sound in doctrine. 教會歡迎所有能夠闡釋正統教義的人士。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**teaching**</span> [ˈti:tʃɪŋ]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl., U] 宗教教義：</span>views that go against traditional Christian teaching 與傳統基督教教義相違的觀點 / the teachings of Jesus 耶穌的教義 / their teachings on sexuality and marriage 他們關於性和婚姻的教義 / biblical, church, religious teaching《聖經》教義；教會教義；宗教教義

<span class="vocabulary">**word**</span> [wə:d] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] the Word可指《聖經》及其教義或教導：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**grace**</span> [greɪs]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 上帝對人類的恩典：</span>It was only by the grace of God that they survived. 承蒙天恩，他們才倖免於難。/ It was only by the grace of God that no one died. 承蒙上帝保佑才無人死亡。/ divine, heavenly, sublime, supernatural grace 神的恩典；天國的恩惠；上帝的慈悲；超自然的恩典 / sovereign grace 至高無上的恩典 / sacramental grace 聖事的恩佑 / The mantra bestows Siva's grace upon the devotee. 曼恆羅將溼婆神的恩典賜予虔誠的信徒。

☀ <span class="category">**聖經 福音：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**bible**</span> [ˈbaɪbl]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [sing.] the Bible可指基督教的《聖經》（包括《舊約》和《新約》）：</span>Holy Bible《聖經》/ The Bible teaches that all people are equal before God. 《聖經》教導說，在上帝面前人人平等。/ Bible reading, Bible story, Bible verse 《聖經》讀物；《聖經》故事；《聖經》詩篇 / Bible camp（尤美）, Bible school《聖經》夏令營；《聖經》學校 / Bible study《聖經》研究 / You can read the story of Noah in the Bible. 你能在《聖經》裡讀到諾亞的故事。<span class="definition">2 [sing.] the Bible可指猶太教的《聖經》（包括《律法書》、《先知書》以及《聖錄》）：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">3 [C] 一冊（基督教或猶太教的）《聖經》：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**scripture**</span> [ˈskrɪptʃə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] Scripture（也可用複數形式的Scriptures）可指《聖經》：</span>the Holy Scriptures《聖經》 / You won't find this moral precept in the scriptures. 你在《聖經》中找不到這種道德準則。<span class="definition">2 [pl.] scriptures可指（某宗教的）聖典、經文、經典：</span>a quote from scriptures 引自經文的語句 / the sacred scripture of the Buddhists 佛教經文 / ancient scriptures 古經文 / Buddhist, Christian, Hindu, Jewish scriptures 佛教經文；基督教經文；印度教經文；猶太教經文 / She read from the scriptures. 她朗讀經文。

<span class="vocabulary">**gospel**</span> [ˈgɒspl; 美 ˈgɑ:spl]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（也可用Gospel）福音書（《聖經·新約》中記錄耶穌基督生平和教誨的四卷書之一）：</span>the Gospel according to St John 約翰福音 / St Mark's Gospel《馬可福音》/ the parable in St Matthew's Gospel《馬太福音》中的寓言故事 / Synoptic Gospel（= the Gospels of Matthew, Mark and Luke in the Bible）同觀福音（指《聖經·新約》中的《馬太福音》，《馬可福音》和《路加福音》）/ an illustrated and illuminated manuscript of the four gospels 配有插圖、裝飾華美的四福音書手抄本 / apocryphal, canonical gospel 外典福音；正典福音 <span class="definition">2 [sing.]（也可用the Gospel）耶穌的事蹟和教誨，即福音：</span>I didn't shirk my duties. I visited the sick and I preached the gospel. 我沒有逃避職責，我探望病人，也宣講福音。/ She preached the Christian gospel to the poor and destitute. 她向窮人和赤貧者宣揚基督的福音。/ Thousands came to hear the gospel. 成千上萬的人都來聽福音。/ gospel message, gospel story 福音傳達的資訊；福音故事
       
<span class="vocabulary">**prophet**</span> [ˈprɒfɪt; 美 ˈprɑ:f-]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] the Prophets可指（《〈聖經〉舊約》和《希伯來聖經》中的）先知書：</span>瞭解即可
 
<span class="vocabulary">**revelation**</span> [ˌrevəˈleɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（上帝的）啟示，即對上帝意旨的揭示，或揭示出的上帝意旨：</span>The whole system was based on divine revelation in the Scriptures. 整個體系都建立在《聖經》的神聖啟示的基礎之上。/ He claimed to know these things by divine revelation. 他聲稱透過神的啟示知道了這些事情。/ He claimed to have had a revelation from God. 他聲稱從上帝那兒得到了自示。
