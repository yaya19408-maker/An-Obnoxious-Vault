☀ <span class="category">**工業 礦業：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**industry**</span> ['ɪndəstrɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 工業，尤指在工廠裡將原材料製造成產品的行業總稱：</span>heavy, light industry 重工業；輕工業 / chemical industry 化學工業 / a sector of industry 工業領域 / commerce and industry 工商業

<span class="vocabulary">**steel**</span> [sti:l] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 鋼鐵工業：</span>Steel used to be important in South Wales. 以前鋼鐵工業在南威爾士很重要。/ a steel worker 鍊鋼工人 / a steel town 鋼城

<span class="vocabulary">**mine**</span> [maɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 地下的礦：</span>coal, copper, diamond, salt, tin, etc. mine 煤礦；銅礦；金剛石礦；鹽礦；錫礦等 / deep, underground mine 深礦；地下礦 / abandoned, disused mine 廢礦 / mine shaft（通常用mineshaft）礦井 / mine owner 礦主 / to go down a mine 下礦井
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pit**</span> [pɪt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 煤礦，相當於coal mine：</span>He went down the pit (= started work as a miner ) when he left school.（go down為英式用法）他中學一畢業就當礦工了。/ They extract the mineral from open pits. 他們在露天礦裡開採礦物。/ Most boys in the village worked in the pits. 鎮上的大多數男孩在礦井裡工作。/ pit village（英）礦井村 / pit closure 礦井的關閉
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**vein**</span> [veɪn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 礦層；礦脈；岩脈：</span>a vein of copper 銅礦層 / a vein of gold 金礦脈 / a rich and deep vein of limestone 儲量豐富的深層石灰石礦脈
           
<span class="vocabulary">**seam**</span> [si:m]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 地下岩石間的一層薄薄的煤層或其它礦物層：</span>narrow seam 狹窄礦層 / They struck a rich seam of iron ore. 他們開出一個富鐵礦層。/ The average UK coal seam is one metre thick. 英國煤層的平均厚度為一米。/ The book is a rich seam of information.（比喻用法）這本書是一座豐富的知識寶庫。

<span class="vocabulary">**working**</span> [ˈwɜ:kɪŋ; 美 ˈwɜ:rk-]
<span class="definition">n. [usually pl.]（礦山或採石場的）礦坑，巷道：</span>housing which was built above old mine workings 修建在老礦坑上的房屋

周邊詞彙補充：
· mineral可指礦物、礦物質。

☀ <span class="category">**開採：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**mine**</span> [maɪn] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 常用被動式，指開掘礦藏，即採礦、開礦：</span>to mine for coal 開採煤炭           

<span class="vocabulary">**exploit**</span> [ɪkˈsplɔɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (for sth) 為商業或工業用途而開採某事物：</span>No minerals have yet been exploited in Antarctica. 南極洲的礦藏還未開採。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**exploitation**</span> [ˌeksplɔɪˈteɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 對石油、礦藏等的開採：</span>commercial exploitation of the mineral resources in Antarctica 南極洲礦物資源的商業開採 / sustainable exploitation 可持續開發 / mineral, resource exploitation 礦藏開採；資源開發
           
<span class="vocabulary">**miner**</span> [ˈmaɪnə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 礦工；採礦者：</span>coal, copper, gold, lead, tin, uranium miner 煤礦工人；銅礦工人；金礦工人；鉛礦工人；錫礦工人；鈾礦工人
           
<span class="vocabulary">**blowout**</span> [ˈbləʊaʊt; 美 ˈbloʊ-]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（石油或天然氣的）井噴：</span>瞭解即可
