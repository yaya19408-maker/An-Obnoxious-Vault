☀ <span class="category">**畢業 結業：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**graduate**</span> ['ɡrædӡueɪt] 
<span class="definition">vi. 1 graduate (in sth) / graduate (from ...) 從大學獲得學位，尤指獲得學士學位，即大學畢業：</span>to graduate in English 獲得英語學士學位 / to graduate from Oxford with a degree in Politics 從牛津大學畢業，獲政治學學士學位 <span class="definition">2 [美] graduate (from ...) 畢業，尤指中學畢業：</span>to graduate from high school 從高中畢業 <span class="definition">vt. [美] graduate sb (from sth) 授予某人學位或畢業文憑等：</span>The university graduated 2000 students last year. 去年這所大學有2000人畢業。

<span class="vocabulary">**graduation**</span> [ɡrædӡu'eɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 大學畢業，或美國高中畢業：</span>college, university graduation 大學畢業 / graduation gown 學士服 / She managed to find a job immediately after graduation. 她剛畢業就找到了一份工作。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**matriculate**</span> [məˈtrɪkjuleɪt]
<span class="definition">vi. [南非英語] 從學校順利畢業：</span>瞭解即可

相關詞義延伸：
· graduation還可指畢業典禮。

周邊詞彙補充：
· convocation可指（大學的）學位授予典禮，畢業典禮。為美式正式用法。
