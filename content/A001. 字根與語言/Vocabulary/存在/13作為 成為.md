☀ <span class="category">**作為的介詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**as**</span> [æz] 
<span class="definition">prep. 用於描述某人擁有某一職業，或事物具有某種功能，即作為，當作：</span>She works as a courier. 她的職業是導遊。/ You can use that glass as a vase. 你可以把那個玻璃杯當作花瓶用。/ The news came as a shock. 訊息傳來，令人震驚。/ She had been there often as a child. 她小時候常去那裡。

☀ <span class="category">**成為的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**make**</span> [meɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 使成為：</span>The terrorists made it known that tourists would be targeted. 恐怖分子宣稱遊客將成為他們襲擊的目標。
