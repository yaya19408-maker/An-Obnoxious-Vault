☀ <span class="category">**檢查、調查、考查、審查的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**check**</span> [tʃek] 
<span class="definition">vt. check sth (for sth) 檢查某事物，以看其是否正確，安全或可接受：</span>Check the container for cracks. 檢驗容器是否有裂縫。/ Check the oil and water before setting off. 出發前應檢視一下油和水。/ Check your work before handing it in. 交作業以前先檢查一遍。/ to check these figures 檢查這些數字 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi. check (with sb) 查明…是否正確或真實，或查明其是否像你想的一樣，即確定：</span>Go and check (that) I’ve locked the door. 去看一下我是不是把門鎖上了。/ to check that the electricity is switched off 確定電源已經切斷 / I’ll go and check. 我去看看。/ I just want to check where we will meet. 我只是想確定一下我們在哪兒見面。/ I checked with her to see if she needed any help. 我問她是否需要什麼幫助。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**verify**</span> [ˈverɪfaɪ]
<span class="definition">vt. 核實…的真實性或準確性：</span>to verify authenticity 核實真實性 / We have no way of verifying his story. 我們無法核實他所說的情況。/ Please verify that there is sufficient memory available before loading the program. 請在核實有足夠的記憶體後再安裝程式。/ I'll leave you to verify whether these claims are true. 我讓你來查核這些說法是否屬實。/ I verified the source from which I had that information. 我核實了我所獲訊息的來源。/ A clerk simply verifies that the payment and invoice amount match. 職員只是核實付款和發票上的數額是否一致。/ Further experiments will be carried out to verify this result. 需要進一步的實驗以核實這個結果。

<span class="vocabulary">**examine**</span> [ɪɡ'zæmɪn] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 非常細緻地思考（或研究）一個想法、主題、學科等，可稱為考查、考察、調查、審查等：</span>These ideas will be examined in more detail in Chapter 10. 這些觀點將在第10章做更為詳細的探討。/ It is necessary to examine how the proposals can be carried out. 有必要研究一下怎樣才能實施這些方案。/ Each case must be carefully examined. 每一種情況都必須經過仔細考查。/ to examine your work 檢查你的工作 / to examine the effects of climate change 考察氣候變化帶來的影響 / to examine ways of doing business 探索做生意的方法 <span class="definition">2 examine sth/sb (for sth) 仔細觀察某事物或某人，以看其是否有問題，或為查詢一個問題的原因。可稱為（仔細）檢查、檢驗：</span>The goods were examined for damage on arrival. 貨物到達時檢查是否有破損。/ The doctor examined her but could find nothing wrong. 醫生給她做了檢查，但沒發現什麼問題。/ to examine a plant 觀察植物 / to examine the data 檢查資料 / to examine the note 檢查筆記 / The room was examined minutely for clues. 為了尋找線索，這間屋子被仔細地檢查過了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**scrutinize**</span> [ˈskru:tənaɪz] [英式也用scrutinise]
<span class="definition">vt. 仔細檢視、認真檢查：</span>She leaned forward to scrutinize their faces. 她探身向前，端詳他們的面容。/ Her purpose was to scrutinize his features to see if he was an honest man. 她的目的是透過仔細觀察他的相貌以判斷他是否誠實。/ The statement was carefully scrutinized before publication. 宣告在發表前經過仔細審查。/ All parts of the aircraft are closely scrutinized for signs of wear or damage. 所有部件都經過仔細檢查，以確定是否有磨損或毀壞的跡象。/ Lloyds' results were carefully scrutinized as a guide to what to expect from the other banks. 對勞埃德銀行的業績進行了仔細分析，以確定對其他銀行的表現可以作何種期待。           

<span class="vocabulary">**investigate**</span> [ɪnˈvestɪgeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 調查（情況、事件、罪案等的事實、原因、過程等）：</span>The FBI has been called in to investigate. 聯邦調查局奉命進行調查。/ The allegations have not yet been properly investigated. 這些指控尚未經過適當調查。/ Police are investigating possible links between the murders. 警察正在調查這些謀殺案之間是否存在關聯。/ The two officers were being investigated by the director of public prosecutions. 兩名警官正在接受檢察官的審查。/ Gas officials are investigating the cause of an explosion which badly damaged a house in Hampshire. 煤氣系統的官員正在調查一起瓦斯爆炸的起因，這起事故嚴重毀壞了漢普郡的一所房屋。/ The company has pledged to investigate claims that its products are unsafe. 公司承諾對其產品存在的安全隱患的投訴進行調查。/ ‘What was that noise? ’ ‘I'll go and investigate.’（非正式用法）“那是什麼聲音？”“我去看一下。” <span class="definition">vt. ~ sb (for sth) 調查某人的性格、活動等：</span>He is being investigated for illegal business dealings. 他因為涉嫌非法交易而正在接受調查。/ This is not the first time he has been investigated by the police for fraud. 這不是警方第一次調查他是否有欺詐行為。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**enquire**</span> [ɪnˈkwaɪə(r)]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. [較正式] 調查：</span>A commission has been set up to enquire into alleged malpractice.（into尤為英式用法）調查被指控的瀆職行為的委員會已經成立。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vet**</span> [vet]
<span class="definition">vt. [英] 審查某人過去的生活和職業（以決定其是否適合某一工作），尤指對將要從事涉及軍事或政治機密工作的人員進行審查、調查：</span>All candidates are carefully vetted for security reasons. 由於安全的緣故，所有的求職申請人都要經過嚴格的審查。/ She was secretly vetted before she ever undertook any work for me. 她在為我工作以前曾受到過秘密審查。/ People who marry into the Royal Family will have to be vetted much more carefully in future. 那些與皇室成員結婚的人未來將受到更細緻的調查。/ We are introducing new security vetting procedures.（尤英）我們正在推行新的安全檢查流程。<span class="definition">vt. 仔細檢查或審查某事物的內容、質量等（以使其符合權威人士的要求）：</span>All reports are vetted before publication. 所有報道都要經過仔細檢查後才能發表。/ He can find no trace of a rule requiring research to be vetted before publication. 他找不到任何要求研究成果在發表前需經審查的規定。/ All objects are vetted by a distinguished panel of experts. 所有物品都交由一個著名的專家小組檢查鑑定。/ All goods are carefully vetted for quality before they leave the factory. 所有商品在出廠之前都經過仔細的質量檢查。

<span class="vocabulary">**ascertain**</span> [ˌæsəˈteɪn; 美 ˌæsərˈt-]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 找出或查明關於某事的真實情況或正確資訊：</span>It can be difficult to ascertain the facts. 可能難以查明事實真相。/ I ascertained that the driver was not badly hurt. 我已查清，駕駛員傷勢不重。/ It should be ascertained that the plans comply with the law. 須要弄清楚，這些計劃要合法。/ The police are trying to ascertain what really happened. 警方正設法查清到底發生了什麼。/ Could you ascertain whether she will be coming to the meeting? 請你弄清楚她來不來開會好嗎？/ It must be ascertained if the land is still owned by the government. 必須確定這塊土地是否仍屬於政府所有。/ Through doing this, the teacher will be able to ascertain the extent to which the child understands what he is reading. 這樣一來，老師就能確定孩子們對他所讀的內容理解多少了。/ Once they had ascertained that he was not a spy, they agreed to release him. 一旦確認他並非間諜，他們即同意將他釋放。/ The geographical spread of the industry in the 16th century is hard to ascertain, for much of it is poorly recorded. 該行業在第16世紀的地理分佈狀況很難查清，因為歷史記錄很少。           

<span class="vocabulary">**probe**</span> [prəʊb; 美 proʊb]
<span class="definition">vt.（尤指用細長的工具）探查：</span>The doctor probed the wound for signs of infection. 醫生檢查傷口是否有感染的跡象。/ The surgeon would pick up his instruments, probe, repair and stitch up again. 外科醫生會拿起他的工具，探查、修復然後再重新縫合。/ Dr Amid probed around the sensitive area. 阿米德醫生在敏感區周圍檢查。/ He probed the mud with his knife. 他用刀子在淤泥裡探尋。/ The bird uses its long beak to probe for worms. 這支鳥用其長喙探尋蟲子。<span class="definition">vt. 探查敵人的薄弱之處（例如透過用一小股兵力襲擊特定區域來探查該區域的敵方兵力情況）：</span>He probes the enemy's weak positions, ignoring his strongholds. 他偵察敵人兵力薄弱的位置，對其要塞不予理睬。/ Squads of prison officers have been probing the rioters' defences. 幾隊獄警一直都在探察暴亂者的防衛情況。<span class="definition">vi. 做為比喻用法也可指探索或調查：</span>to probe deeper and deeper into the secrets of the universe 逐漸深入地探索宇宙的奧秘 / The police were probing into her personal life. 警方正在調查她的私生活。/ The more they probed into his background, the more inflamed their suspicions would become. 他們越調查他的背景，疑團就越多。/ a TV programme that probed government scandals in the 1990s 追查20世紀90年代政府醜聞的電視節目 / For three years, I have probed for understanding. 3年來，我一直都想弄明白。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**poll**</span> [pəʊl; 美 poʊl]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 對…進行民意調查：</span>Over 50% of those polled were against the proposed military action. 民調中有超過50%的人反對擬議的軍事行動。/ More than 18,000 people were polled. 超過1.8萬人接受了民意測驗。/ Audiences were going to be polled on which of three pieces of contemporary music they liked best. 將對觀眾進行民意調查，詢問他們在3部當代音樂作品中最喜歡哪一部。/ It was a disappointing result for the Greens who polled three percent. 這個結果讓綠黨感到失望，他們只獲得了3%的選票。/ The result showed he had polled enough votes to force a second ballot. 結果顯示，他已獲得了足夠把對手拖入二次投票的票數。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**survey**</span> [ˈsɜ:veɪ; 美 ˈsɜ:rveɪ]
<span class="definition">vt. 對某個群體做民意調查、進行民意測驗：</span>We surveyed 500 smokers and found that over three quarters would like to give up. 我們對500名吸菸者進行了調查，發現有超過四分之三的人願意戒菸。/ Business Development Advisers surveyed 211 companies for the report. 企業發展顧問公司為此報告調查了 211 家公司。/ Only 18 percent of those surveyed opposed the idea. 被調查者中只有 18%的人反對該主張。<span class="definition">vt. 仔細檢視、審視（某事物的總體，尤指為了獲取其總體印象）。相當於inspect：</span>She surveyed his appearance critically. 她挑剔地打量著他。/ He surveyed himself in the mirror before going out. 出門前他對著鏡子把自己審視了一番。/ He pushed himself to his feet and surveyed the room. 他站起來仔細打量整間屋子。/ I surveyed the scene from my window. 我透過窗戶看到了這一幕。/ The next morning we surveyed the damage caused by the fire. 次日清早我們檢視了火災的破壞情況。/ He surveys American politics with a conservative world view. 他用一種保守的世界觀審視美國政治。

<span class="vocabulary">**inspect**</span> [ɪn'spekt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 inspect sth/sb (for sth) 仔細檢視…，尤指檢視一切是否正常，相當於examine。與check的一個含義也極為類似，有時可互換：</span>The teacher walked around inspecting their work. 老師走來走去檢查他們的作業。/ Each party in the case is entitled to inspect the documents held by the other. 案件各方均有權檢視對方所持有的檔案。/ He inspected the water tank for cracks. 他檢查水箱是否存在裂縫。/ to inspect the goods before signing for them 在簽收之前對貨物進行檢驗 / The plants are regularly inspected for disease. 對這些植物定期做病害檢查。<span class="definition">2 官方視察，正式視察，常是領導對機構等進行視察。是“視察”的常規用詞：</span>to inspect the factory 視察工廠

<span class="vocabulary">**test**</span> [test] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 test sth (on sth/for sth) / test sth out（對產品、機器、物質等）進行測試，檢測（以看其是否質量過關、執行良好或為發現更多資訊）：</span>to test the product 檢測產品 / to test the car 測試汽車 / to test the substance 檢測物質 / You should test your brakes regularly. 你應該定期檢查剎車。/ Squeeze the fruit to test for ripeness. 捏一下水果檢視成熟程度。/ tried and tested techniques 經過檢驗的技術 <span class="definition">2 檢驗理論、假說等：</span>to test out his hypothesis 驗證他的假說 / to test the theory experimentally 用實驗檢驗一下這個理論

<span class="vocabulary">**review**</span> [rɪ'vju:] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 仔細審度一種形勢或一個體系，以察看其是否存在問題，或是否有需要改進之處：</span>The president reviewed the economic situation. 總統審度了經濟形勢。/ Safety procedures are being urgently reviewed after the accident. 事故發生後，人們現在正在緊急檢查安全程式。/ to review the figure 審查數字 / to review your budget 審查你的預算 / to review the case 審查案件 <span class="definition">2 [尤美] 檢查一件作品，看其是否存在錯誤，即校閱，稽核：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**audit**</span> [ˈɔ:dɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. 對公司的賬目進行審計：</span>Each year they audit our accounts and certify them as being true and fair. 他們每年對我們進行賬務稽核，以確保其真實無誤。

<span class="vocabulary">**screen**</span> [skri:n] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1（公司、組織等）對員工或想要成為其員工的人進行調查或審查（以看其是否可靠）：</span>Government employees may be screened by the security services. 政府僱員可能要接受安全部門的審查。/ They will screen all their candidates. 他們將審查他們所有的求職者。/ All foster parents are carefully screened. 所有的養父母都經過仔細甄選。<span class="definition">2 檢查某事物（以看其是否合適或你是否想要它，如為確保沒有攜帶武器等而檢查人或行李）：</span>to screen baggage on a X-ray machine 在X光機上檢查行李
           
<span class="vocabulary">**sift**</span> [sɪft]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (through) sth 仔細檢查（以決定哪個重要、有用，或發現…重要）：</span>We will sift every scrap of evidence. 我們將細查每一點證據。/ Crash investigators have been sifting through the wreckage of the aircraft. 調查墜機事件的專家一直在仔細檢查飛機殘骸。/ They will try to sift out the winners and the losers. 他們將努力選出勝方和敗方。/ He's mentally sifting for truths. 他在腦子裡細細思忖想找出事實真相。/ They spent days carefully sifting through the evidence. 他們花了很多天細查證據。/ I spent hours sifting through those heavy art books. 我花了好幾個小時翻看那些沉甸甸的畫冊。

<span class="vocabulary">**see**</span> [si:] 
<span class="definition">vt.（透過檢視、打聽、等待）弄清，瞭解，如察看：</span>Let me just see what the next song is. 讓我看看下一首歌是什麼。/ Go and see what the kids are doing. 你去看看孩子們在幹什麼。/ He came over to see what was going on. 他走過來看發生了什麼事。

<span class="vocabulary">**explore**</span> [ɪk'splɔ:] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. explore (sth) (for sth) 在某片地域或某個國家穿行以期對其進行了解，如勘探、勘察、探索、考察：</span>The city is best explored on foot. 最好是徒步考察這個城市。/ to explore the land 勘查土地 / companies exploring for oil 石油勘探公司

<span class="vocabulary">**visit**</span> ['vɪzɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt.（正式地）視察或巡視（例如執行檢查任務，或給出指導意見）：</span>government inspectors visiting schools 視察學校的政府檢察員

<span class="vocabulary">**run**</span> [rʌn] 
<span class="definition">vt. run a test/check (on sth) 對…進行測試或檢查：</span>to run a test on the blood samples 對血樣進行化驗
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pursue**</span> [pəˈsju:; 美 pərˈsu:]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 繼續查詢，即追查、追究：</span>We have decided not to pursue the matter. 我們決定不再追究這件事。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**censor**</span> [ˈsensə(r)]
<span class="definition">vt. 審查、檢查（書刊、戲劇、電影、書信、媒體等）：</span>The military-backed government has heavily censored the news. 有軍方撐腰的政府對新聞進行了嚴格審查。/ The movie has been heavily censored. 這部電影受到嚴格審查。/ The news reports had been heavily censored. 這些新聞報道受到嚴格審查。

周邊詞彙補充：
· audit可指對公司的賬目進行審計；
· prospect可指在一片區域內尋找黃金、礦物、石油等。

☀ <span class="category">**檢查、調查、考查、審查的名詞和感嘆詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**check**</span> [tʃek] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] check (for/on sth) 檢查，檢視（是否安全、正確、狀況良好）：</span>a check of the engine 檢查發動機 / A thorough check is made before the bags are put on the plane. 行李裝上飛機前要做細緻檢查。/ a routine check by police 警方的例行檢查 / annual check 年檢 / random, spot check 隨機檢查；突擊檢查 / medical check 體格檢查 / security check 安全檢查 / ID check（英）身份查驗 / stock check（英）庫存檔點 / quality check 質量檢測 <span class="definition">2 [C] check (on sb/sth) 調查；審查（以找出更多資訊）：</span>The police ran a check on the registration number of the car. 警方對那輛車的牌照號碼進行了調查。/ background, credit check 背景審查；信用審查

<span class="vocabulary">**examination**</span> [ɪɡ͵zæmɪ'neɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 非常仔細的檢視或考慮，尤指看其是否有問題，或找出問題的原因，即審查；調查；考查；考察：</span>an examination of the area 對這一地區的查勘 / a microscopic examination of the cell 對細胞進行顯微鏡檢查 / On closer examination it was found that the signature was not genuine. 經過進一步認真檢查發現簽名是偽造的。/ Your proposals are still under examination. 你的提議仍在審查之中。/ These figures warrant closer examination. 這些資料須進行更加仔細的檢查。/ His ideas do not stand up to close examination. 他的主意經不起仔細琢磨。/ The chapter concludes with a brief examination of some of the factors causing family break-up. 本章結束時簡要探討了引起家庭破裂的某些因素。/ This argument requires examination from several angles. 這個論點需要從不同角度進行驗證。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**inspection**</span> [ɪnˈspekʃn]

· test還可指（對實力等的）考驗。也可指（困難狀況等）考驗（實力、能力等）；
· probe還可指不載人的航天探測器。也可指醫生用的、對人體內部進行檢查的探針。亦可指科研人員所用的、可以放進某事物內部以進行測試或記錄資訊的一個小裝置，如探測儀；
· audit還可指工商業的審計（如財務審計、稅務審計）。

周邊詞彙補充：
· inspector可指（對學校、工廠等進行視察的）視察員、巡視員。還可指（英國公共汽車或火車上的）查票員。也可指（建築物）鑑定人（尤指為房屋買主鑑定房屋狀況的一個人）；
· post-mortem可指對屍體進行醫學檢查以查詢死因，即驗屍。還可指（尤指事情失敗後）反思或剖析，即事後分析、事後檢討；
· autopsy可指驗屍，屍體解剖（以探查死因）；
· sense可指（機器）發現並記錄…；
· service可指對機器的檢修；
· project可指學生對某一學科所做的細緻研究或調查；
· moderate可指檢視不同閱卷人所打分數是否公平一致，即稽核評分。為英式用法；
· sweep可指各電視公司進行的收視率調查（以找出最受歡迎的節目，尤指為計算廣告費）。
