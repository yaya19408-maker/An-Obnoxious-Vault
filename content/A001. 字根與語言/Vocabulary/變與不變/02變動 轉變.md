☀ <span class="category">**變動、轉變、變革的動詞和副詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**swing**</span> [swɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. swing (from A) (to B) / swing (between A and B) 從一種意見（或情緒、態度、感情等）轉變到另一種意見（或情緒、態度、感情等）；或使某人轉變意見或情緒等：</span>The state has swung from Republican to Democrat. 這個州原先支援共和黨，現在倒向了民主黨。/ His emotions swung between fear and curiosity. 他時而害怕，時而好奇。/ The game could swing either way. 這場比賽勝負未卜。/ I managed to swing them round to my point of view. 我設法使他們轉而接受了我的觀點。/ Opinion swung heavily to the left. 輿論嚴重轉向左傾。

<span class="vocabulary">**switch**</span> [swɪtʃ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. switch (sth) (over) (from sth) (to sth) / switch (between A and B)（從一者到另一者）轉變或轉移；使…（從一者到另一者）轉變或轉移：</span>to switch to a market economy 向市場經濟轉變 / to switch between documents on screen 在螢幕上的檔案之間切換 / to switch between TV channels 切換電影片道 / When did you switch jobs? 你們什麼時候調動工作的？/ My mother’s interest had switched to my health. 我母親的興趣已轉移到我的健康上來了。/ My mind suddenly switched back to my conversation with Jeremy. 我的思緒突然回到了與傑里米的談話上。/ He decided to switch tactics. 他決定改變策略。/ He switched his allegiance from the British to the French. 他不再效忠英國，轉而效忠法國。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**shift**</span> [ʃɪft]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth) (from…) (to…)（使）從一地移動到另一地：</span>Like many plays, this one shifts back and forth in time and place. 和許多劇一樣，這部劇也是在時空上來回變換。/ The action of the novel shifts from Paris to London. 小說情節從巴黎移到了倫敦。/ Government grants are being shifted away from the capital to the regions. 政府撥款正逐步從首都轉向地方。/ He shifted his gaze from the child to her. 他把目光從孩子身上移到她身上。/ Could you help me shift some furniture? 你能幫我挪幾件傢俱嗎？/ He stopped, shifting his cane to his left hand. 他停了下來，將手杖移到左手。/ He shifted from foot to foot. 他從一隻腳換到另一隻腳。/ She shifted her weight from one foot to the other. 她把身體的重量從一隻腳換到另一隻腳上。/ She shifted uncomfortably in her chair. 她在椅子上不安地動來動去。<span class="definition">vi. ~ (from…) (to/towards/toward…)（一種情況、觀點、態度、政策等）變動：</span>Public attitudes towards marriage have shifted over the past 50 years. 50年來，公眾對婚姻的態度已經改變。/ Attitudes to mental illness have shifted in recent years. 最近幾年對精神病的態度已有所改變。/ His sympathies rapidly shifted to the side of the workers. 他的同情心很快轉到了工人一邊。/ The balance of power shifted away from workers towards employers. 力量均勢從工人向僱主傾斜。/ These changes will shift the balance more towards/toward science subjects. 這些變化將進一步使天平向理科傾斜。/ The emphasis should be shifted more towards Parliament. 重點應更多地轉向議會。/ I felt the advantage had suddenly shifted away from us. 我感到我們突然失去了優勢。/ constantly shifting alliances 不斷變更的同盟 <span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (from…) (to/towards/toward…) 改變對某事的觀點或態度，或改變做事方式：</span>We need to shift the focus of this debate. 我們需要轉換一下辯論的焦點。/ The new policy shifted the emphasis away from fighting inflation. 新政策不再把重點放在抑制通貨膨脹上。<span class="definition">vt. ~ responsibility/blame (for sth) (onto sb) 轉嫁責任，既可以是轉嫁工作上的職責，也可以是轉嫁做錯事的責任：</span>He tried to shift the blame for his mistakes onto his colleagues. 他自己犯了錯誤，卻試圖把責任推給同事。/ It was a vain attempt to shift the responsibility for the murder to somebody else. 試圖將謀殺的罪責轉嫁給他人是徒勞的。/ to shift the blame onto the government 把犯錯的責任推給政府 / Their husbands try to shift the blame by accusing them of having 'suspicious minds'. 她們的丈夫指責她們“疑心太重”，試圖以此來推諉責任。/ We cannot simply shift the responsibility onto someone else. 我們不能簡單地把責任推給別人。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**convert**</span> [kənˈvɜ:t; 美 -ˈvɜ:rt]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth) (from sth) (into/to sth)（使）…轉換或轉變到另一種形式、目的、體制等：</span>The hotel is going to be converted into a nursing home. 那家旅館將被改建成私人療養院。/ the entrepreneur who wants to convert County Hall into an hotel 想把市政廳改建成旅館的企業家 / By converting the loft, they were able to have two extra bedrooms. 把閣樓改造一下，他們就可以多出兩間臥室。/ Save money by converting your car to unleaded. 將車改裝一下，使用無鉛汽油，這樣可以省錢。/ The programme to convert every gas burner in Britain took 10 years. 完成對英國所有煤氣灶的改造花了10年時間。/ We've converted from oil to gas central heating. 我們已經把中央供熱系統由燃油改成了燃氣。/ What rate will I get if I convert my dollars into euros? 如果我把美元兌換成歐元，匯率是多少？/ Converting metric measurements to U.S. equivalents is easy. 將公制計量換算成美製計量非常簡單。/ The signal will be converted into digital code. 訊號會被轉變為數字程式碼。/ naturally occurring substances which the body can convert into vitamins 自然產生的能被身體轉化成維生素的物質 <span class="definition">vi. ~ into/to sth 能被轉換或轉變到另一種形式、目的、體制等：</span>a sofa that converts into a bed 可改作床用的沙發 <span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sb) (from sth) (to sth)（使）某人改變宗教或信仰：</span>He converted from Christianity to Islam. 他由基督教改信伊斯蘭教。/ She was soon converted to the socialist cause. 她不久便轉而獻身於社會主義事業了。/ He was a major influence in converting Godwin to political radicalism. 戈德溫變成政治激進主義者主要是受他的影響。/ If you try to convert him, you could find he just walks away. 如果你試圖改變他的信仰，他就會走開。<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sb) (from sb) (to sth)（使）改變（觀點、習慣等）：</span>I've converted to organic food. 我改吃有機食物了。/ I didn't use to like opera but my husband has converted me. 我過去不喜歡歌劇，但我丈夫改變了我。

<span class="vocabulary">**transform**</span> [træns'fɔ:m] 
<span class="definition">vt. transform sth/sb (from sth) (into sth) 1 改變某事物的形態，相當於convert：</span>The photochemical reactions transform the light into electrical impulses. 光化學反應使光變為電脈衝。<span class="definition">2（某事物的外觀或性質、特徵）完全改變，尤指使其變得更好：</span>A new colour scheme will transform your bedroom. 新的色彩調配將使你的臥室煥然一新。/ It was an event that would transform my life. 那是能夠徹底改變我一生的一件事。/ The riots radically transformed the situation. 暴亂徹底改變了局勢。/ He seems to have been miraculously transformed into a first-class player. 他似乎奇蹟般地變成了一名一流球員。/ The place was transformed from a quiet town into a busy port. 這個地方由一個安靜的小鎮變為繁忙的港口。/ the power of religion to transform our hearts and minds 能夠改變心靈和思想的宗教力量

<span class="vocabulary">**translate**</span> [træns'leɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. translate (sth) (into sth) 變成（或被變成）另一種形式：</span>to translate words into action 把言語化為行動 / to translate these ideas into action 把這些想法付諸行動 / I hope all the hard work will translate into profits. 我希望所有的辛勤勞動都會有回報。/ Higher sales won’t necessarily translate into profits. 銷售量的提升並不一定會轉變為利潤。/ The lost trade revenue ultimately translated into job losses at home. 損失的貿易收入最終轉化為國內的失業。/ The story translates well to the screen. 這部小說適宜改編為電影。/ Teacher expectations do not automatically translate themselves into student results. 教師的期待並不能自動轉化為學生的成績。/ to translate the theory into simple concepts 把理論轉化為簡單的概念
           
<span class="vocabulary">**transition**</span> [trænˈzɪʃn; -ˈsɪʃn]
<span class="definition">vi.（從一種狀態或條件）轉變、過渡（到另一種狀態或條件）：</span>Most of the discussion was on what needed to be done now as we transitioned from the security issues to the challenging economic issues. 隨著我們由安全議題轉向具有挑戰性的經濟議題，討論最多的就是目前需要做些什麼。/ There was a significant decline in the size of the business as the company transitioned to an intellectual property company. 隨著公司向智慧財產權公司轉型，其業務規模顯著下降。

<span class="vocabulary">**jump**</span> [dӡʌmp] 
<span class="definition">vi. jump (about) (from sth to sth) 突然轉變（或切換）主題或話題、題目等：</span>to jump about from one topic to another 轉換話題 / The story then jumps from her childhood in New York to her first visit to London. 故事接著從她在紐約的童年一下子轉到她第一次去倫敦訪問。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**leap**</span> [li:p]
<span class="definition">vi. 驟變：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**flow**</span> [fləʊ] 
<span class="definition">vi.（變化）穩定且持續地向一個方向移動，即流動：</span>The songs flow seamlessly into one another. 這些歌曲自然流暢地從一首過渡到另一首。/ Some of these changes will flow directly from the legislation. 這些變化中有一些將會直接來自於立法。

<span class="vocabulary">**turn**</span> [tə:n] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. turn (sth) (around) 指比賽出現逆轉；使比賽出現逆轉（即另一選手或隊伍開始贏得比分或贏得比賽等）：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**mutate**</span> [mju:ˈteɪt; 美 ˈmju:teɪt] 
<span class="definition">vi. 轉變成一個新形式：</span>Rhythm and blues mutated into rock and roll. 節奏布魯斯演變成為搖滾樂。/ Overnight, the gossip begins to mutate into headlines. 一夜之間，傳聞變成了報紙的頭條新聞。

<span class="vocabulary">**over**</span> ['əʊvə] 
<span class="definition">adv. 用於談論變換位置的某人或某事物：</span>He’s gone over to the enemy. 他已變節投敵。/ Please change the wheels over. 請把輪子調個個兒。/ Hand over the money! 把錢交出來！

相關詞義延伸：
· shift還可作為美式用法，指駕駛的過程中換擋。

周邊詞彙補充：
· transcribe可指以書寫的方式來記錄想法、話語或資料；或把想法、話語或資料轉成另一種書寫形式。還可指改編樂曲，使其適合於其他樂器或聲部。

☀ <span class="category">**變動、轉變、變革的名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**swing**</span> [swɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（意見、情緒、情感、態度、輿論或情況的）改變（即由一者改變為另一者，且尤指突然的、大的改變）；也可指這種改變的程度：</span>Voting showed a 10% swing to Labour. 投票顯示10%的人轉而支援工黨。/ violent swings in policy 政策的急劇改變 / mood swings 情緒波動 / swing state（尤美）搖擺州（指投票意向不斷變化的州）/ a sharp swing in the attitudes of economists 經濟學家觀點的急劇轉變 / the swing away from science in schools 學校偏離理科的轉變

<span class="vocabulary">**switch**</span> [swɪtʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] switch (in/of sth) / switch (from A to B) 指從一者到另一者所進行的轉變（尤指突然且徹底的轉變）：</span>She made the switch from full-time to part-time work. 她從全職工作轉為兼職工作。/ a policy switch 政策的轉變 / a tactical switch（尤英）戰術的轉變 / He’s a classical singer who has made the switch over to pop. 他是古典歌手，已改唱流行歌曲。/ their abrupt switch in allegiance 他們立場的突然轉變
           
<span class="vocabulary">**shift**</span> [ʃɪft]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] ~ (in sth) 時空位置或方向的改變：</span>a shift in direction 方向的改變 <span class="definition">2 [C] 其它方面的轉變、改變、變遷：</span>climate, cultural, demographic, population, ideological, policy, power shift 氣候變化；文化變遷；人口變化；意識形態轉變；政策改變；權力轉移 / paradigm shift 思維模式的轉變 / These climate shifts occurred over less than a decade. 氣候變化是在不到十年內出現的。/ a shift in public opinion 民意的改變 / a shift in attitude, a shift in perspective 態度的轉變；視角的轉變 / The moment marked a significant shift in attitudes to the war. 這一刻標誌著對戰爭的態度有了重大轉變。/ I detected a subtle shift towards our point of view. 我覺察到了一絲傾向贊同我方觀點的細微變化。/ a shift in emphasis, a shift in mood, a shift in tone 著重點的變化；心情的變化；語氣的變化 / a shift in focus, a shift in policy, a shift in strategy 焦點的改變；政策的改變；戰略的轉變 / one factor which may explain the president's policy shift 也許可以解釋總統政策變化的一個因素 / These proposals represent a dramatic shift in policy. 這些提案表明了政策的重大變化。/ the shift away from direct taxation 從直接稅收的轉變 / a sudden shift to the right in politics 政治上的突然右傾 / a shift towards part-time farming 向兼營農業的轉變 / The industry has undergone a fundamental shift in recent years. 該行業近年來經歷了根本性的變革。/ The toy industry is undergoing a seismic shift as more products are manufactured overseas. 因越來越多的產品在海外生產，玩具行業正經歷一場巨大的變革。/ the many shifts between verse and prose that occur in Shakespeare 莎士比亞作品中在詩體和散文體之間的諸多轉換
           
<span class="vocabulary">**transformation**</span> [ˌtrænsfəˈmeɪʃn; 美 -fərˈm-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] ~ (from sth) (to/into sth) 徹底改變、徹底轉變：</span>The way in which we work has undergone a complete transformation in the past decade. 在過去的十年裡，我們的工作方式經歷了徹底的變革。/ the country's transformation from dictatorship to democracy 這個國家由獨裁到民主的轉變 / cultural, economic, historical, institutional（尤美）, intellectual, personal, physical, political, social, spiritual, structural, technological transformation 文化轉變；經濟轉變；歷史轉變；制度轉變；思想轉變；個人轉變；物理轉變；政治轉變；社會轉變；精神轉變；結構轉變；技術轉變 / A startling cultural transformation occurred in post-war Britain. 戰後英國的文化出現了令人驚訝的變化。/ transformation process 轉變過程 / The transformation from disused docks into cultural venue took three years. 由廢棄的船塢變成文化場所花了三年的時間。/ This decision marked a fundamental transformation in policy. 這一決定標誌著政策發生了根本性的轉變。/ Japan's transformation into an economic superpower 日本成為超級經濟大國的轉變 / Russia's transformation to a market economy 俄羅斯向市場經濟的轉變 / What a transformation! You look great. 真是判若兩人！你看上去真神氣。/ He was struggling to make the transformation from single man to responsible husband. 他正在努力使自己由單身漢轉變為有責任心的丈夫。/ Going to college brought about a dramatic transformation in her outlook. 上大學使她的觀念發生了巨大的變化。<span class="definition">2 [U]（用於南非）民主改革：</span>a lack of transformation in the private sector 在私營部門缺乏民主改革
           
<span class="definition">**U-turn**</span>
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 政策、行為等方面的180度大轉彎、徹底轉變（常是尷尬的態度大轉彎）：</span>a humiliating U-turn by the Prime Minister 首相極不光彩的180度大轉變 / a U-turn on education policy 教育政策大變革 / the U-turns he made on economic policy and pit closures 他在經濟政策和煤窯關閉方面的那些180度大轉變 / The government has made a spectacular U-turn on taxes. 政府在稅收方面作出重大調整。/ a government U-turn over plans to cut social security payments 政府削減社會保障支出方案的重大調整

<span class="vocabulary">**movement**</span> ['mu:vmənt] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] movement (away from/towards sth)（社會中的人們的想法、態度、觀點、行為、政策等的）逐步轉變，相當於trend：</span>a movement towards greater sexual equality 朝著更高的性別平等的轉變 / the movement toward democracy in Latin America 拉丁美洲朝向民主的漸進

<span class="vocabulary">**translation**</span> [træns'leɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] translation (of sth) into sth 轉變到一種不同形式的過程，即轉變；轉化：</span>the translation of theory into practice 從理論到實踐的轉化
           
<span class="vocabulary">**transition**</span> [trænˈzɪʃn; -ˈsɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] ~ (from sth) (to sth) / ~ (between A and B)（從一種狀態或條件到另一種狀態或條件的）轉變、過渡：</span>We need to ensure a smooth transition between the old system and the new one. 我們得確保新舊制度間的平穩過渡。/ This course is useful for students who are in transition (= in the process of changing) from one training programme to another. 對轉換培訓專案的學生來說，這一課程是有幫助的。/ orderly, peaceful transition 有序的過渡；和平的過渡 / democratic transition 民主過渡 / The negotiators hoped to bring about a smooth transition to an interim administration. 談判人員希望促成向臨時政府的平穩過渡。/ The company was slow to make the transition from paper to computer. 公司由紙張辦公過渡到電腦辦公的轉變很遲緩。/ The ceremony marks the transition of the student to graduate status. 這個儀式標誌著由學生到畢業生的身份轉變。/ Her transition from girl to woman was complete. 她已經從一個女孩變為一個女人。/ transition period, transition process 過渡時期；過渡過程 / transition phase 過渡階段 / transition committee, transition team 過渡委員會；過渡團隊 / He will remain head of state during the transition to democracy. 在向民主政體過渡期間，他仍然是國家元首。/ The country is in transition from an agricultural to an industrial society. 這個國家正從農業社會轉變為工業社會。/ This training course aims to smooth the transition between education and employment. 這個訓練課程的目的就是實現從學校到就業之間的平穩過渡。/ the transition from school to full-time work 從學校到全日工作的過渡階段 / a state of transition 過渡狀態

<span class="vocabulary">**revolution**</span> [͵revə'lu:ʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] revolution (in sth)（對很多人產生影響的）一種條件（工作方式、信念等）的巨大改變，即鉅變，重大變革：</span>agricultural, computer, industrial, information, social revolution 農業變革；計算機革命；工業革命；資訊革命；社會變革 / a revolution in people’s leisure activities 人們休閒活動的巨大變化 / Marketing has undergone a revolution in recent years. 市場營銷近年經歷了一場革命。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**upheaval**</span> [ʌpˈhi:vl]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 引起很多混亂、擔憂、問題或麻煩的大變化，即劇變：</span>cultural, economic, political, social upheaval 文化劇變；經濟變革；政治劇變；社會劇變 / violent upheaval 劇烈變革 / revolutionary upheaval 革命劇變 / The company underwent a massive upheaval after the takeover. 收購之後該公司變動巨大。/ He rose to power during the political upheavals of the 1990s. 他在20世紀90年代政治劇變期間上臺執政。/ Wherever there is political upheaval, invariably there are refugees. 哪裡有政治劇變，哪裡就一定有難民。/ the latest upheavals in the education system 最近教育制度上的種種變革 / I can't face the upheaval of moving house again. 我無法忍受再次搬家的折騰。/ Having a baby will mean the greatest upheaval in your life. 有了孩子意味著你的生活要發生重大的變化。/ a period of emotional upheaval 情緒波動很大的時期          

<span class="vocabulary">**convert**</span> [kənˈvɜ:t; 美 -ˈvɜ:rt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (from sth) (to sth) 改變宗教、信仰、觀點等的人；皈依者：</span>Catholic, Jewish convert 天主教的皈依者；猶太教的皈依者 / a convert to Islam 改信伊斯蘭教的人 / converts from other faiths 來自其他宗教信仰的皈依者 / Pope Clement was a convert from paganism. 克雷芒教皇是來自異教的皈依者 / a convert to the cause 一個轉而支援這項事業的人
           
<span class="vocabulary">**conversion**</span> [kənˈvɜ:ʃn; 美 -ˈvɜ:rʒn; -ʃn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] ~ (from sth) (into/to sth)（形式、用途、體制或制度的）轉變、轉換、改變：</span>the conversion of farm buildings into family homes 農場建築物改建為家庭住宅 / the conversion of disused rail lines into cycle routes 將廢棄的鐵路線改成腳踏車道 / A loft conversion can add considerably to the value of a house. 將閣樓改造一下就可以使房子的價值大增。/ a firm which specializes in house conversions (= turning large houses into several smaller flats/apartments) 專營房屋改建的公司 / A local building company will carry out the conversion of the farm buildings into business units. 當地一家建築公司將把農場建築改造成商業用房。/ a metric conversion table (= showing how to change metric amounts into or out of another system) 公制換算表 / Consult the conversion table to figure out the weight in kilos. 查查換算表，計算出以千克為單位的重量。/ There are no charges for currency conversion. 貨幣兌換不收費。/ data conversion 資料轉換 / file conversion（計算機的）檔案格式轉換 / conversion from analogue/analog to digital data 從模擬資料轉換成數字資料 / energy conversion 能量轉化 / Cheap solar energy conversion has been the dream of scientists since the 1970s. 自20世紀70年代以來，科學家一直夢想著進行低成本的太陽能轉化。/ a conversion kit that lets your vehicle run on non-polluting fuel 使車輛依靠無汙染燃料執行的轉換裝備 <span class="definition">2 [U, C] ~ (from sth) (to sth)（宗教或信仰的）改變；皈依；歸附：</span>the conversion of the Anglo-Saxons by Christian missionaries 基督教傳教士使盎格魯——撒克遜人的信仰改變 / his conversion from Judaism to Christianity 他由猶太教改信基督教 / his conversion to Christianity 他皈依基督教 / It's hard to trust the President's conversion. 總統信仰的改變讓人難以相信。/ Christian, religious, spiritual conversion 皈依基督教；宗教上的皈依；精神上的皈依 / deathbed conversion 臨終的皈依 / the forced conversion of Jews during the Inquisition 宗教法庭對猶太人的強迫皈依 / In the 90s he underwent a religious conversion. 20 世紀90年代，他經歷了宗教信仰的轉變。/ conversion experience 信仰轉變的經歷 / her overnight conversion to market economics（比喻用法）她突然轉到了市場經濟學 <span class="definition">3 [C] barn/loft ~（尤指為居住而）改建的房屋：</span>瞭解即可

相關詞義延伸：
· revolution還可指革命；
· swing還可指突然改變方向、使…突然改變方向；
· switch還可指上下扳動的（電力裝置或機器的）閘、（鐵路的）轉轍器或道岔；
· translate還可指翻譯。也可指以某種方式來理解…、給予…某種含義；
· translation還可指翻譯。也可指譯文、譯本、譯作；
· shift還可指工作的輪班。也可指輪班職工。

周邊詞彙補充：
· range可指（在一定的數量、尺寸等範圍內）變化，變動（包括上下兩個頂點在內）；
· reversible可指（步驟、過程、行動或疾病）可逆的、可恢復原狀的。
