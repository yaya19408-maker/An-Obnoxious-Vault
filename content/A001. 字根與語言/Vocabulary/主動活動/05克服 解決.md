☀ <span class="category">**克服、解決的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**overcome**</span> [͵əʊvə'kʌm] 
<span class="definition">vt.（在自己達成某事的過程中）成功處理（或管控）阻礙事情進展的問題，即克服，解決：</span>She overcame injury to win the Olympic gold medal. 她克服了傷痛，贏得了奧運會金牌。/ to overcome their differences on the issue 彌合在該問題上的分歧 / He eventually overcame his disability. 他最終克服了自己的缺陷。/ Therapy helped her to overcome her fear. 治療幫助她戰勝了恐懼。/ methods designed to overcome these problems 用於克服這些難題的方法
           
<span class="vocabulary">**conquer**</span> [ˈkɒŋkə(r); 美 ˈkɑ:ŋ-]
<span class="definition">vt. 成功地處理、應對、克服、控制…：</span>The only way to conquer a fear is to face it. 克服恐懼的唯一方法是正視恐懼。/ I was certain that love was quite enough to conquer our differences. 我相信愛足以克服我們的種種差異。/ He has never conquered his addiction to smoking. 他一直未能戒除煙癮。/ Mount Everest was conquered (= successfully climbed) in 1953. 珠穆朗瑪峰於1953年被征服。

<span class="vocabulary">**settle**</span> ['setl] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 結束一場爭論或爭端；解決分歧或糾紛。是“解決”的常規用詞：</span>to settle dispute, to settle argument, to settle difference, to settle conflict 解決爭端；解決爭論；解決分歧；解決衝突 / to settle out of court 庭外和解 / The matter has not yet been finally settled. 事情還沒有最終解決。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**resolve**</span> [rɪˈzɒlv; 美 rɪˈzɑ:lv]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 為問題或困難找到一個可接受的解決方案，即解決（問題或困難）。相當於settle：</span>to resolve an issue, to resolve a dispute, to resolve a conflict, to resolve a crisis 解決問題；解決爭端；解決衝突；解決危機 / Both sides met in order to try to resolve their differences. 雙方會晤以努力解決分歧。/ They met in a last-ditch attempt to resolve their differences. 他們的會晤是為化解分歧所作的最後努力。/ The two countries have failed to resolve their differences on this. 兩國未能化解在此事上的分歧。/ We must find a way to resolve these problems before it's too late. 我們必須及時找到解決這些問題的方法。/ The matter is not yet fully resolved. 這件事還沒有完全解決。/ We hope that the dispute can be resolved peacefully. 我們希望能夠和平地解決爭端。/ The crisis was finally resolved through high-level negotiations. 危機最終透過高層談判得以消除。/ The family feuding could resolve itself. 家族恩怨可以自行化解。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**solve**</span> [sɒlv; 美 sɑ:lv]
<span class="definition">vt. 找到一種方法來解決一個問題或處理一個不利局面，即解決，處理：</span>Attempts are being made to solve the problem of waste disposal. 正在想辦法解決廢物處理的問題。/ Their domestic reforms did nothing to solve the problem of unemployment. 他們的國內改革未能解決失業問題。<span class="definition">vt. 為某事找到正確的答案或解釋，即解答、破解：</span>to solve an equation, to solve a puzzle, to solve a riddle 解方程；解難題；解謎 / to solve a crime, to solve a mystery 破案；解開奧秘 / We may now be able to get a much better idea of the true age of the universe, and solve one of the deepest questions of our origins. 現在，我們也許能夠更加清楚地瞭解宇宙生成的年代，並且解答關於人類起源最為深奧的問題之一。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fix**</span> [fɪks] 
<span class="definition">vt. 解決問題，或處理一個糟糕的情況以使人滿意：</span>to fix the problem 解決問題

<span class="vocabulary">**cure**</span> [kjʊə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 解決（問題）。有一種“糾正”的色彩。不太常用：</span>to cure the country’s economic troubles 解決該國的經濟困難
           
<span class="vocabulary">**remedy**</span> [ˈremədi]
<span class="definition">vt. 解決：</span>to remedy a problem 解決問題           

<span class="vocabulary">**grapple**</span> [ˈgræpl]
<span class="definition">vi. 努力設法解決問題：</span>I was grappling to find an answer to his question. 我正在努力解決他的問題。/ The new government has yet to grapple with the problem of air pollution. 新政府還需盡力解決空氣汙染問題。/ The economy is just one of several critical problems the country is grappling with. 經濟問題只是該國正竭力解決的幾個關鍵問題之一。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**clinch**</span> [klɪntʃ]
<span class="definition">vt. 提供解決辦法、解決：</span>Evidently this information clinched the matter. 顯然，這一訊息使問題得以最終解決。/ That was the clue which clinched it for us. 就是那個線索幫我們最終解決了問題。/ a clinching argument 讓人折服的論證
           
<span class="vocabulary">**crack**</span> [kræk]
<span class="definition">vt.（尤指經過反覆思考）找到問題的解決辦法等，找到解決難題的方法：</span>to crack the enemy's code 破譯敵人的密碼 / He has finally cracked the system after years of painstaking research. 經過多年的努力研究，他終於找到了破解這個系統的辦法。/ After a year in this job I think I've got it cracked!（非正式用法）幹了一年後，我覺得我已知道怎樣做好這項工作了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**contend**</span> [kənˈtend]
<span class="definition">vi. 解決、克服（一個問題或困難）：</span>瞭解即可
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**navigate**</span> [ˈnævɪgeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. 找到正確方法（應對困難或複雜的情況）：</span>We next had to navigate a complex network of committees. 我們下一步必須設法使各級委員會予以透過。/ During childhood each of us has to navigate a pathway through a series of developmental stages. 我們每個人在童年時都要經歷和應對不同的成長階段。/ This outlook helped her to navigate through her later years with success. 這種觀念幫她安度了晚年。/ She managed to successfully navigate the complex world of corporate finance. 她設法成功地駕馭了公司錯綜複雜的財務狀況。

<span class="vocabulary">**mediate**</span> [ˈmi:dieɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (in sth) / ~ (between A and B)（在兩者或多者之間）調解、調停、斡旋：</span>The Secretary-General was asked to mediate in the dispute. 有人請秘書長來調解這次爭端。/ An independent body was brought in to mediate between staff and management. 由一個獨立機構介入，在勞資之間進行調解。/ to mediate differences, to mediate problems 調解分歧；調解問題 / My mom was the one who mediated between Zelda and her mom. 我媽媽充當了澤爾達和她媽媽之間的調解人。/ United Nations officials have mediated a series of peace meetings between the two sides. 聯合國官員已經促成了雙方的一系列和平會談。<span class="definition">vt. 為存在分歧的各方找到解決方法：</span>They mediated a settlement. 他們找到了一個解決方案。

☀ <span class="category">**解決的名詞和形容詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**reconciliation**</span> [ˌrekənsɪliˈeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [sing., U] ~ (between A and B) / ~ (with sb)（對人們或群體之間的分歧作出的）調解、和解：</span>Their change of policy brought about a reconciliation with Britain. 他們的政策改變促成了與英國的和解。/ an appeal for reconciliation between Catholics and Protestants 天主教徒和新教徒之間達成和解的訴求 / national reconciliation 民族和解 / political, racial（美）reconciliation 政治和解；種族和解 / He sought reconciliation with those he had stolen from. 他想與那些曾被他盜竊的人尋求和解。/ He is striving to bring about a reconciliation between the two conflicting sides. 他正在努力使衝突雙方達成和解。/ The rebel leader called for reconciliation with the armed forces. 叛軍首領呼籲和軍隊和解。/ reconciliation agreement 和解協議 / reconciliation process和解程序 / In a bold gesture of reconciliation, the government released the rebel leader. 政府以大膽的和解姿態釋放了叛軍首領。/ a policy of reconciliation, a spirit of reconciliation 和解政策；和解精神 / The couple have separated but he wants a reconciliation. 夫婦倆已經分居了，但是他想要和解。/ The pair are bravely attempting a reconciliation. 這對男女正在盡力嘗試和解。/ the power of love to bring renewal and reconciliation 可化解怨氣、重修舊好的愛的力量 / She attempted reconciliation with her estranged brother. 她試圖跟疏遠的哥哥和好。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**mediation**</span> [ˌmi:diˈeɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 調停，調解，斡旋：</span>UN mediation 聯合國的翰旋 / The conflict ended through the mediation of the United Nations. 這次衝突透過聯合國的調解平息了。/ multiparty mediation 多方斡旋 / divorce mediation 離婚調解 / Unless management accepts mediation, the strike will never be resolved. 除非資方接受調解，否則罷工不會停止。/ mediation efforts, mediation process 調解努力；調解過程 / under international mediation 在國際社會的翰旋下 / mediation by the prime minister between the two sides 首相在兩方之間進行的調解

<span class="vocabulary">**settlement**</span> ['setlmənt] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 達成協議的行為，如解決，處理：</span>the settlement of a dispute 爭端的解決 / a settlement agreement 和解協議
           
<span class="vocabulary">**resolution**</span> [ˌrezəˈlu:ʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, sing.]（問題、分歧等的）解決或消除：</span>The government is pressing for an early resolution of the dispute. 政府正在不斷敦促早日解決這起糾紛。/ the successful resolution of a dispute involving UN inspectors in Baghdad 涉及聯合國駐巴格達觀察員的爭端的成功解決 / in order to find a peaceful resolution to the crisis 為了和平解決此次危機 / Hopes of a peaceful resolution to the conflict were fading. 和平解決衝突的希望日漸渺茫。/ methods of conflict resolution 解決衝突的方法 / the non-violent resolution of conflict 衝突的非暴力解決 / the likelihood of achieving a satisfactory resolution to the problem 妥善解決該問題的可能性

<span class="vocabulary">**fix**</span> [fɪks] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 問題的解決方案，尤指簡易的、臨時性的解決方案：</span>short-term, temporary fix 權宜之計；暫時的解決辦法 / long-term, permanent fix 長期的解決辦法；永久的解決辦法 / a technical fix 技術補救方法 / a bug fix（程式）缺陷補丁 / They made a quick fix of part of the problem. 他們迅速對部分問題採取了補救措施。

<span class="vocabulary">**answer**</span> ['ɑːnsə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] answer (to sth) 問題的解決方案或解決辦法：</span>We are aware of this problem, but we do not have the answer. 我們意識到了這個問題，但是沒有解決辦法。/ If you really want to save time, this machine will provide the answer. 如果你真的想節省時間，這臺機器就能幫你。/ The answer came to him in a flash. 他突然想出瞭解決辦法。/ The obvious answer would be to cancel the party. 明擺著的解決辦法是取消聚會。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**solution**</span> [səˈlu:ʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] ~ (to sth) 問題的解決辦法，或艱難局面的處理手段：</span>There's no simple solution to this problem. 這個問題沒有簡單的解決辦法。/ the ability to sort out simple, effective solutions to practical problems 找出簡單有效的方法解決實際問題的能力 / Although he has sought to find a peaceful solution, he is facing pressure to use greater military force. 儘管他試圖尋求和平解決，但現在卻面臨使用更多武力的壓力。/ Attempts to find a solution have failed. 試圖找到解決辦法的種種努力全都失敗了。/ Do you have a better solution? 你有更好的解決辦法嗎？

<span class="vocabulary">**cure**</span> [kjʊə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] cure (for sth)（問題等的）解決方法，或（糟糕狀況等的）改善措施：</span>the magic cure for inflation 解決通貨膨脹的神奇方法 / a cure for poverty 解決貧困問題的措施 / Science cannot provide a cure for all the world’s problems. 科學不可能解決世界上所有的問題。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**remedy**</span> [ˈremədi]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] ~ (for/to sth)（問題或困難處境的）處理方法、改進措施、補救措施：</span>There is no simple remedy for unemployment. 失業問題沒有簡單的解決辦法。/ The remedy lies in the hands of the government. 解決良策掌握在政府手中。/ a remedy for economic ills 解決經濟問題的良方妙策 / There are a number of possible remedies to this problem. 這個問題有許多可能採取的解決辦法。/ Desperate remedies were resorted to in the search for food. 為尋找食物採取了極端的做法。/ When the reservoir becomes blocked, the only remedy lies in cleaning the entire system. 水庫堵塞，唯一的解決辦法就是疏浚整個系統。/ The agreement states that he has a remedy against the subcontractor. 協議稱他有一種對付分包商的辦法。<span class="definition">2 [C] [法律] ~ (against sth)（透過法律程式的）解決方法：</span>legal remedy 法律手段 / Holding copyright provides the only legal remedy against unauthorized copying. 持有版權是制止盜版的唯一法律手段。/ Your best remedy is to go to the small claims court. 解決你問題的最好辦法是去小額索賠法院。/ They will have to seek a judicial remedy for breach of contract. 他們將不得不尋求司法手段來解決合同違約的問題。/ They advised him to exhaust all other remedies before applying to court. 他們建議他在訴諸法律之前先試試其他的辦法。

<span class="vocabulary">**soluble**</span> [ˈsɒljəbl; 美 ˈsɑ:l-]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式]（一個問題）可解決的；可解的：</span>瞭解即可

相關詞義延伸：
· solution還可指謎題或數學題的答案。

周邊詞彙補充：
· do可指找到…的答案（即解答）、解決某事；
· wrestle可指嘗試（或努力）去處理（或解決）困難的事情（或問題）；
· outstanding可指（款項、工作、困難等）尚未支付的、尚未完成的、尚未解決的等；
· accommodation可指達成協議的過程。
