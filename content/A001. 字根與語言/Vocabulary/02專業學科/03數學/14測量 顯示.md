☀ <span class="category">**測量 顯示：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**measure**</span> ['meӡə] 
<span class="definition">vt. measure (sb/sth) (for sth)（以標準單位來）測量，度量（尺寸、數量等）。是此義的常規用詞，最為常用：</span>A ship’s speed is measured in knots. 船速以節測量。/ to measure the level of radiation in the atmosphere 測量大氣層中的輻射等級 / measuring instruments 測量儀器 / Measure the length and width. 測量一下長和寬。linking verb.（尺寸、長度、數量等）量度的數值為…：</span>The main bedroom measures 12ft by 15ft. 主臥室寬12英尺，長15英尺。/ The pond measures about 2 metres across. 這個池塘寬約2米。/ It measures 20 yards from side to side. 從這邊到那邊的距離為20碼。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**measurement**</span> [ˈmeʒəmənt; 美 ˈmeʒərm-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（通常以數字形式呈現的）測量結果：</span>We took lots of measurements. 我們得到了許多測量結果。/ The measurements are extraordinarily accurate. 測量結果極為精確。<span class="definition">2 [U, C] 測量、計量（是一種行為或過程）：</span>Measurement of blood pressure can be undertaken by practice nurses. 測量血壓可以由實習護士完成。/ the metric system of measurement 公制度量衡 / Accurate measurement is very important in science. 在科學領域，精確的測量非常重要。/ Objective measurement is difficult with such poor equip-ment. 用這樣蹩腳的裝置很難進行如實的測量。/ quantitative measurement 定量測量 / lab（非正式）, laboratory, scientific measurement 實驗室測量；科學測量 / length, pressure, temperature measurement 長度的度量；壓力的測量；溫度的測量 / measurement instrument, measurement system, measurement technique, measurement tool 測量儀器；測量系統；測量技術；測量工具 / measurement scale, measurement unit 計量標準；計量單位 / a scale of measurement, a unit of measurement 計量標準；計量單位 / measurement error 測量誤差
           
<span class="vocabulary">**determination**</span> [dɪˌtɜ:mɪˈneɪʃn; 美 -ˌtɜ:rm-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] [術語] 測定：</span>Both methods rely on the accurate/precise determination of the pressure of the gas. 兩種方法都依賴於對氣體壓力的準確測定。

<span class="vocabulary">**gauge**</span> [geɪdʒ]
<span class="definition">vt. 用儀器測量：</span>precision instruments that can gauge the diameter to a fraction of a millimetre 可測出直徑為若干分之一毫米的精密儀器 / He gauged the wind at over thirty knots. 他測出風速在30節以上。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**meter**</span> [ˈmi:tə(r)]
<span class="definition">vt. 用儀表計量（耗用的煤氣、電等）：</span>Only a third of these households thought it reasonable to meter water. 這些家庭當中只有三分之一認為用水錶計量是合理的。/ Metered taxis are relatively inexpensive. 打表計價的計程車相對便宜。

<span class="vocabulary">**sound**</span> [saʊnd] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. [術語]（用垂線或聲納等）測量海洋、湖泊、井等的深度：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**take**</span> [teɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 計量中可指量取或測定…。是一個簡單用詞：</span>to take sb’s temperature 給某人量體溫 / to take blood pressure 量血壓
           
<span class="vocabulary">**survey**</span> [ˈsɜ:veɪ; 美 ˈsɜ:rveɪ]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（對一個地區進行的）勘測、測繪（以繪製地圖或制定計劃）：</span>archaeological, geological, geophysical, land, seismic考古勘測；地質勘測；地球物理勘測；土地勘測；地震勘測 / structural survey（尤英）結構勘察 / A structural survey of the property revealed serious defects. 對該房產的結構勘察顯示其存在嚴重的缺陷。/ full survey 全面勘察 / an aerial survey (= made by taking photographs from an aircraft) 空中勘測 / The scientists conducted two aerial surveys followed by two ground surveys. 科學家們在兩次地面勘測後又進行了兩次空中勘測。<span class="definition">vt. 對某地進行勘測、測繪（以繪製地圖或為建築工程做前期準備）：</span>Scarborough Council commissioned geological experts earlier this year to survey the cliffs. 斯卡伯勒市政會在今年早些時候委託地質專家對這些懸崖進行勘測。

<span class="vocabulary">**indicate**</span> ['ɪndɪkeɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt.（測量儀器）顯示某一量度：</span>The temperature gauge indicates 90˚F. 溫度計顯示90華氏度。

<span class="vocabulary">**tell**</span> [tel] 
<span class="definition">vt.（儀器）提供關於…的資訊，即顯示。是一個擬人用詞：</span>This gauge tells you how much fuel you have left. 這個油表顯示還剩多少燃料。

<span class="vocabulary">**say**</span> [seɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 無被動語態，（裝置）提供某資訊或某指示：</span>The clock said four minutes past eleven when we set off. 我們出發時，時鐘顯示十一點零四分。

<span class="vocabulary">**record**</span> [rɪ'kɔ:d] 
<span class="definition">vt.（測量儀器）顯示（某一量度或數量）：</span>to record the electrical activity of the brain 顯示大腦的電流活動 / The thermometer recorded a temperature of 36℃. 溫度計顯示溫度達到了36攝氏度。

<span class="vocabulary">**register**</span> ['redӡɪstə] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 測量儀器（或其他事物透過測量儀器）顯示（讀數），或記錄（讀數）：</span>The earthquake registered 7.6 on the Richter scale. 這次地震顯示為里氏7.6級。/ It will only register on sophisticated X-ray equipment. 只有在先進的X光裝置上它才會顯示出來。/ The stock exchange has registered huge losses this week. 本週證券交易遭到重創。

<span class="vocabulary">**read**</span> [ri:d] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 指測量儀器顯示某一資料（如重量、壓力等）：</span>The thermometer read 110 degrees Fahrenheit. 溫度計顯示為華氏110度。/ What does the thermometer read? 溫度計的讀數是多少？<span class="definition">2 從測量儀器上讀取資訊：</span>to read a thermometer 讀取溫度計上的溫度 / to read the ammeter 讀取安培表

<span class="vocabulary">**reading**</span> ['ri:dɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（測量儀器上的）讀數：</span>normal reading 正常讀數 / false reading 錯誤讀數 / meter, thermometer reading 計量器讀數；溫度計讀數 / blood-pressure, pressure, temperature reading 血壓讀數；壓力讀數；溫度讀數 / A reading of 25 or more is abnormally high. 讀數在25或25以上都屬於異常之高。

周邊詞彙補充：
· time可指測定…所需要或花費的時間，即計時；
· clock可指測定…穿行的速度；
· sensitive可指能夠測量出微小的變化的，即對微小變化敏感的。
