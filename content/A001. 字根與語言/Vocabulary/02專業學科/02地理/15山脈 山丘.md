☀ <span class="category">**山脈 山丘：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**range**</span> [reɪndӡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 一條（或一組）山脈（可以是mountains或hills）。強調一種整體感：</span>the great mountain range of the Alps 雄偉的阿爾卑斯山脈

<span class="vocabulary">**mountain**</span> ['maʊntɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 高山；山嶽。強調一種高聳的雄偉氣勢：</span>great mountain 高聳的山巒 / small, low mountain 小山；低矮的山 / volcanic mountain 火山 / holy, sacred mountain 聖山 / mountain range 山系；山脈 / mountain cave 山洞 / mountain village 山村 / the bottom of a mountain 山腳 / Mountains loom in the distance. 遠方的群山隱隱可見。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**mount**</span> [maʊnt]
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] Mount（縮寫為. Mt）（在現代英語裡僅用於地名）山、山峰：</span>Mt Everest 珠穆朗瑪峰 / Mount Kilimanjaro 乞力馬扎羅山 / St Michael's Mount 聖邁克爾山

<span class="vocabulary">**hill**</span> [hɪl] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 山丘；小山。高度通常比mountain低：</span>gently rolling hills 緩緩起伏的丘陵 / the distant blue hills 遠處的青山 / the houses which dotted the hill 零星散佈在山上的房子 / a hill overlooking the wide valley below 俯瞰下方寬闊谷地的小山 / hill village（英）山村 / hill country 多山的國家 / hill slope 山坡 / the bottom of a hill 山腳

<span class="vocabulary">**rock**</span> [rɒk] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 站立在地面的一大塊岩石聚合體，即石山：</span>the Rock of Gibraltar 直布羅陀山

<span class="vocabulary">**rise**</span> [raɪz] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 向上傾斜的一片土地，即（向上的）斜坡；小丘；小山，相當於slope。與hill有交集，但在此交集部分不如hill常用，rise可看作是一種形象化用法：</span>a small rise 一座小山
              
<span class="vocabulary">**slope**</span> [sləʊp; 美 sloʊp]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 土地的斜坡：</span>a grassy slope 長滿草的斜坡 / The town is built on a slope. 這座城建在斜坡上。/ the continental slope off the American coast 美洲海岸線上的大陸坡 / The street must have been on a slope. 這條街一定是建在一個斜坡上。/ a slope leading down to the river 通往下面河邊的一個斜坡 / The lower slopes rise quite gently. 較低處的斜坡坡度和緩。/ a west-facing slope overlooking the river 俯瞰河流的西向斜坡 / I scrambled down the icy slope. 我爬下了冰封的斜坡。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**gradient**</span> [ˈgreɪdiənt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [美式也尤用grade] 地面的坡度、傾斜度：</span>The football pitch was on a slight gradient. 足球場在一個緩坡上。/ a hill with a gradient of 1 in 4 (or 25%) 傾斜度為1:4（或25%）的小山

<span class="vocabulary">**hump**</span> [hʌmp]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 某事物的平面（尤指地面）上的一個大隆起物：</span>the dark hump of the mountain in the distance 遠處高大的黑魆魆的山 / The path goes over a large hump by a tree before running near a road. 小道越過一處傍樹的大圓丘，然後沿著一條大路向前延伸。        
           
<span class="vocabulary">**mound**</span> [maʊnd]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 大型土丘或石丘、小丘、小山崗：</span>a Bronze Age burial mound 青銅時代的墳冢 / a small tree on a grassy mound 蔥翠的山崗上的一棵小樹 / The castle was built on top of a natural grassy mound. 這座城堡建在一個天然的綠草如茵的小丘上。/ The church stands on a high mound just outside the village. 教堂就矗立在村外的一處高地上。

<span class="vocabulary">**sierra**</span> [siˈerə]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤用於地名）（尤指西班牙和美洲的）鋸齒狀山脈：</span>the Sierra Nevada 內華達山脈
           
<span class="vocabulary">**spur**</span> [spɜ:(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 從山巒或山丘上凸出來的一塊高地，即山嘴、尖坡，也可指山的支脈：</span>瞭解即可    

<span class="vocabulary">**scar**</span> [skɑː] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 小山或懸崖上的一塊岩石裸露、沒有草木的地方，即裸岩：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**mountainous**</span> ['maʊntɪnəs] 
<span class="definition">adj. 多山的：</span>a mountainous region 多山的地區
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**hillside**</span> [ˈhɪlsaɪd]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（小山的）山坡，山腰：</span>The crops will not grow on exposed hillsides. 在裸露的山坡上莊稼沒法生長。/ Our hotel was on the hillside overlooking the lake. 我們的旅館位於可俯瞰湖水的小山坡上。/ steep hillside 陡峭的小山坡 / rocky hillside 岩石嶙峋的山坡 / On our left was a wooded hillside. 我們的左邊是一個樹木叢生的山坡。/ I was back in wine country, with terraced hillsides on both sides of the road. 我回到葡萄酒之鄉，道路兩旁的山坡上都是梯田。/ distant hillside 遠處的山坡 / Many of the chalets are set on a hillside reached by steps. 許多小木屋搭建在拾級可上的山坡上。/ Tiny flowers dotted the hillside. 山坡上點綴著許多小花。/ hillside vineyard 丘陵葡萄園 / hillside town, hillside village 山城; 山村 / A stream tumbles down the steep hillside. 一條小溪沿著陡峭的山坡奔流而下。/ The house was on a hillside outside the town. 房子在郊外的山坡上。/ We clambered up the hillside to the ridge above. 我們沿著山坡費力地爬上了山脊。/ the hillside above the town 俯瞰全鎮的山坡

<span class="vocabulary">**slope**</span> [sləʊp; 美 sloʊp]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually pl.] 山坡：</span>the eastern slopes of the Andes 安第斯山脈東坡 / The vineyards on the south-facing slopes get more sunshine. 南坡上的葡萄園日照更充裕。/ Saint-Christo is perched on a mountain slope 聖克里斯托教堂坐落在一個山坡上。/ the lower slopes of the Himalayas 喜馬拉雅山脈較低處的山坡 / There were more skiers further up the slope. 在山坡高一些的地方滑雪者更多。/ There was snow on the higher slopes of the mountain. 更高處的山坡上有積雪。/ We clambered up the steep, rocky slope. 我們沿著陡峭多石的山坡向上爬。/ Rocks and boulders rolled down the slopes of the crater. 岩石和巨礫順著火山口外的斜坡滾落下來。/ The vineyard covers the slope. 葡萄園遮住了整個山坡。/ We camped on an open mountain slope. 我們在開闊的山坡上紮營。/ the bottom of a slope 坡底 / dry（英）, ski slope 人造滑雪坡；滑雪坡 / She plans to hit the ski slopes this winter. 這個冬天她打算去滑雪場。/ He spends all winter on the slopes (= skiing). 整個冬天他都在山坡上滑雪。/ bunny（美）, nursery（英） slope（初學滑雪者的）平緩練習坡地 /

<span class="vocabulary">**peak**</span> [pi:k]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 山的頂峰；也可指帶有頂峰的山：</span>mountain peak 山峰 / snow-capped, jagged peaks 積雪覆蓋的山峰；嶙峋怪異的山峰 / We looked up at the rocky peaks towering above us. 我們抬頭看高高聳立在我們上方岩石嶙峋的山峰。/ The climbers made camp halfway up the peak. 登山者在半山腰設定營地。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**summit**</span> [ˈsʌmɪt]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 某事物（尤指山頂）的最高點、頂點：</span>Snow gleamed on the summit of Mount Simon. 西蒙山山頂白雪皚皚。/ Maroon Peak is the highest summit in its group. 馬倫峰是群峰中最高的。/ very summit（尤英）峰頂 / rocky, snow-capped summit 多岩石的峰頂；積雪覆蓋的峰頂 / mountain summit 山頂 / 2000-foot summit 2,000 英尺高的山頂 / a 2030-foot summit 2,030英尺高的山頂 / We finally arrived at the summit. 我們終於抵達了峰頂。/ We reached the summit at noon. 中午時分我們抵達峰頂。/ summit attempt 登頂嘗試 / The blizzard forced them to delay their summit bid. 暴風雪迫使他們推遲登頂。/ We were standing at the summit of the highest mountain in India. 我們正站在印度最高峰的峰頂。/ They reached base camp, 30 000 feet below the summit. 他們抵達了離峰頂3萬英尺的大本營。/ The climbers planted a flag on the summit. 登山者在山頂上豎起一面旗幟。/ This path leads to the summit. 這條路通往山頂。/ the summit of his career（比喻用法）他事業的頂峰
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pike**</span> [paɪk]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [方言] 英格蘭北部的山峰：</span>瞭解即可
          
<span class="vocabulary">**ridge**</span> [rɪdʒ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 山脊；山脈：</span>mountain ridge 山脊 / walking along the ridge 沿著山脊行走 / We took the high pass over the ridge. 我們從山脊的高山口透過。/ We clambered up the hillside to the ridge above. 我們沿著山坡費力地爬上了山脊。/ the north-east ridge of Mount Everest 珠穆朗瑪峰的東北部 / We finally reached the crest of the ridge. 我們終於達到了山脈的最高峰。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**brow**</span> [braʊ]
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] 山丘的山脊、坡頂：</span>The path disappeared over the brow of the hill. 小徑過山頂後消失了。/ He was on the look-out just below the brow of the hill. 他就在山脊下負責警戒。
       
<span class="vocabulary">**crest**</span> [krest]
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] ~ (of sth) 山頂：</span>We finally reached the crest of the ridge. 我們終於到達了山脈的最高峰。/ We stood on the crest of the hill. 我們站在山頂上。

<span class="vocabulary">**pinnacle**</span> [ˈpɪnəkl]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（尤指山頂的）突兀的尖巖：</span>A walker fell 80ft from a rocky pinnacle. 一位徒步者從一塊80英尺高的尖錐形岩石上摔了下來。

周邊詞彙補充：
· steep可指（斜坡、山等）陡的；陡峭的。

☀ <span class="category">**懸崖 峭壁：**</span>          
<span class="vocabulary">**cliff**</span> [klɪf]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 懸崖、峭壁，常指海洋邊緣的懸崖或峭壁：</span>high, towering cliff 高高的懸崖；高聳的懸崖 / low cliff 低矮的峭壁 / jagged cliff 嶙峋的峭壁 / limestone cliff 石灰岩懸崖 / coastal, sea, mountain cliff 海邊的懸崖；山上的絕壁 / a long line of cliffs surrounding the bay 環繞海灣的一長溜絕壁 / The cliffs fall away to the north. 峭壁向北傾斜。/ Rugged sandstone cliffs rose up from the beach. 嶙峋的砂岩峭壁矗立於海灘。/ Steep cliffs towered above the river. 陡峭的懸崖俯瞰著那條河。/ crumbling cliffs 坍塌的懸崖 / cliff edge, cliff face, cliff ledge, cliff side, cliff top, cliff wall 懸崖邊緣；崖面；懸崖巖脊；懸崖側面；懸崖頂；崖壁 / cliff path, cliff walk 懸崖上的小徑 / The hotel was perched high on a cliff overlooking the sea. 旅館高高地建在懸崖上，俯瞰大海。/ a castle perched high on the cliffs above the river 高高聳立在臨河峭壁上的城堡 / the base/bottom/foot of the cliff 懸崖底部 / the chalk cliffs of southern England 英格蘭南部的白堊質峭壁 / The car rolled over the edge of a cliff. 汽車從懸崖邊上滾下去了。

<span class="vocabulary">**crag**</span> [kræg]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 高且陡的巖質懸崖、峭壁、絕壁：</span>a castle set on a crag above the village 位於村子上方懸崖上的城堡 / mountain crag 山崖 / We slowly climbed the steep face of the crag. 我們沿著懸崖的峭壁慢慢攀爬。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**precipice**</span> [ˈpresəpɪs]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 非常陡峭且高聳的懸崖、山或岩石：</span>瞭解即可

周邊詞彙補充：
· vein可指岩脈。
