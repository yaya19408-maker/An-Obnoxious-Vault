☀ <span class="category">**地質災害：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**volcano**</span> [vɒl'keɪnəʊ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 火山：</span>active, dormant volcano 活火山；休眠火山 / submarine, undersea volcano 海底火山

<span class="vocabulary">**erupt**</span> [ɪ'rʌpt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（火山）爆發；（岩漿、煙等從火山中）噴出：</span>The volcano could erupt at any time. 這座火山隨時可能爆發。/ An immense volume of molten lava was erupted. 大量熔岩噴發出來。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**eruption**</span> [ɪˈrʌpʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（火山的）爆發、噴發：</span>It was the biggest eruption of Vesuvius for some years. 這是維蘇威火山這些年來最大的一次爆發。/ volcanic clouds from explosive eruptions 爆裂式噴發產生的火山雲 / minor eruption 小爆發 / volcanic, volcano（美）eruption 火山爆發 / a major volcanic eruption 火山大爆發 / The temple was destroyed in a violent eruption. 那座廟毀於一次猛烈的火山爆發。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**extinct**</span> [ɪkˈstɪŋkt]
<span class="definition">adj.（火山）不再活躍的、死的：</span>Its tallest volcano, long extinct, is Olympus Mons. 它最高的火山是早已熄滅的奧林波斯山。

<span class="vocabulary">**earthquake**</span> ['ə:θkweɪk] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 地震（非正式用法為quake）：</span>strong earthquake 強震 / minor earthquake 輕微地震 / undersea earthquake 海底地震 / an earthquake measuring 5.8 on the Richter scale 里氏5.8級地震 / earthquake zone 地震帶 / earthquake victim 地震遇難者

<span class="vocabulary">**quake**</span> [kweɪk] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 地震：</span>The quake destroyed many buildings. 地震摧毀了許多建築。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**tremor**</span> [ˈtremə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 輕微的地震：</span>an earth tremor 地動 / minor, slight tremor 輕微的地震 / severe tremor 劇烈的地面顫動

<span class="vocabulary">**focus**</span> ['fəʊkəs] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 地震的震源：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**flood**</span> [flʌd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 洪水或水災：</span>flash flood 驟發的洪水 / summer flood 夏季洪水 / flood damage 洪澇災害 / flood victim 洪災災民 / flood prevention, flood relief 防汛；排澇 / The river was in flood. 河水氾濫了。<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 1（如河流或管道）灌滿水；使灌滿水：</span>The kitchen flooded. 廚房被水淹了。<span class="definition">2 河流泛濫：</span>The river flooded. 河水氾濫了。/ The river flooded acres of farming land. 河水淹沒了大片農田。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**deluge**</span> [ˈdelju:dʒ]
<span class="definition">n. [usually sing.] 突然的大暴雨：</span>About a dozen homes were damaged in the deluge. 在這場暴雨中大約有十幾家房屋被毀。<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] [正式] 洪水淹沒或沖毀一個地方：</span>At least 150 people are believed to have died after two days of torrential rain deluged the capital. 連續兩天的滂沱大雨致使首都積水成災，據信至少已有 150 人死亡。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**rapids**</span> [ˈræpɪdz]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] 河的急流，通常指岩石上的急流：</span>to shoot the rapids (= to travel quickly over them in a boat) 穿過急流 / Those of you looking for adventure can shoot the rapids. 你們中想要冒險的人可以穿過急流。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**torrent**</span> [ˈtɒrənt; 美 ˈtɔ:r-; ˈtɑ:r-]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 移動非常快的大量水，如急流、湍流、洪流：</span>a torrent of water 湍急的水流 / After heavy rain, the little stream becomes a raging torrent. 暴雨過後，小溪變成了洶湧的洪流。/ Torrents of water gushed into the reservoir. 急流湧入了水庫。/ a torrent of rain 傾盆大雨 / The rain came down in torrents, and we could see nothing. 大雨傾盆而下，我們看不見任何東西。/ The rain was coming down in torrents. 大雨如注。      
          
<span class="vocabulary">**landslide**</span> [ˈlændslaɪd]
<span class="definition">n. [C]（也可用landfall）從山坡或懸崖的斜坡上滑落的大量土石等，如滑坡：</span>The floods caused a landslide. 洪水導致了山體滑坡。/ The storm caused landslides and flooding in Savona. 風暴致使薩沃納發生塌方和洪災。/ The village was destroyed by a landslide. 該村毀於山體滑坡。

<span class="vocabulary">**slide**</span> [slaɪd] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 大量岩石或泥土從山上的突然落下，相當於landslide：</span>to start a slide of loose stones 引發鬆散石塊的崩塌
           
<span class="vocabulary">**avalanche**</span> [ˈævəlɑ:nʃ; 美 ˈævəlæntʃ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 山坡上落下的大量雪、冰和岩石，即雪崩、山崩：</span>alpine villages destroyed in an avalanche 在一場雪崩中被摧毀的高山村莊 / The avalanche struck the ski resort in the late afternoon. 下午晚些時候雪崩襲擊了滑雪勝地。/ They were killed by an avalanche in the Swiss Alps. 他們在瑞士阿爾卑斯山的一次雪崩中罹難。/ They died in an avalanche. 他們死於一場雪崩。
