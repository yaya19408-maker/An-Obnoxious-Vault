☀ <span class="category">**泉水 噴泉：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**spring**</span> [sprɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 泉（即水從地下自然地流出地表的一個地方）；也可指泉水：</span>deliciously cool spring water 甘洌的泉水 / hot, thermal spring 溫泉 / bubbling spring 汩汩的泉水 / mineral, natural spring 礦泉；天然泉水 / mountain, underground spring 山泉；地下泉水 

<span class="vocabulary">**fountain**</span> ['faʊntɪn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（用於裝飾公園、園林、院落等的）人工噴泉：</span>drinking（尤英）, water（美）fountain（公共場所的）噴泉式飲水器 / ornamental fountain 裝飾性噴泉 / A white marble fountain played in the middle of the square. 一座白色大理石噴泉在廣場中央噴水。
