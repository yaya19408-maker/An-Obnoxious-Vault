<span class="vocabulary">**sponge**</span> [spʌndʒ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 海綿（一種海洋動物，體圓而柔軟）：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**school**</span> [sku:l] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 遊動的魚群或其他水生動物群：</span>a school of fish 一群魚 / a school of dolphins 一群海豚

<span class="vocabulary">**frog**</span> [frɒɡ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 青蛙：</span>the croaking of frogs 蛙鳴 / The frog jumped into the pond. 青蛙跳進了池塘。/ frogs’ legs (= eaten as food) 田雞腿

<span class="vocabulary">**ancestor**</span> ['ænsestə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 動物的原種（即某個現代動物的祖先）：</span>The two species share a common ancestor. 這兩個物種有一個共同的祖先。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**ancestry**</span> [ˈænsestri]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing., U] 物種的祖先：</span>The two species have developed from a common ancestry. 這兩個物種源自同一個祖先。/ Humans share a common ancestry with chimpanzees. 人類與黑提程擁有共同的祖先。

<span class="vocabulary">**descent**</span> [dɪˈsent]
<span class="definition">n. [U] ~ (from sb)（動物的）祖先，相當於ancestry：</span>Humans and other apes followed separate lines of descent from a common ancestor. 人類和其他猿類從一個共同的祖先進化而來，但屬於不同的世系。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**descend**</span> [dɪˈsend]
<span class="definition">vi.（動物等）是…的後裔：</span>The breed is almost directly descended from the Eurasian wild boar. 這一品種幾乎是歐洲野豬的直系後代。
