☀ <span class="category">**通向 通往：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**lead**</span> [li:d] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（道路、小路或門）通向或通往（某個方向或地方）：</span>The track led us through a wood. 我們沿著那條小道穿過了樹林。/ a path leading from the village to the old church 從村莊通向古老教堂的一條小道 / the door that led to the garden 通往園子的門 / Often there are discoveries which lead nowhere.（比喻用法）有些發現往往無果而終。

<span class="vocabulary">**go**</span> [ɡəʊ] 
<span class="definition">vi. go (from ...) to ...（從一地）通向或延伸（至另一地）：</span>I want a rope that will go from the top window to the ground. 我想要一根可從頂樓窗戶垂到地面的繩子。/ a road that goes from A to B 從甲地通向乙地的一條路

<span class="vocabulary">**open**</span> ['əʊpən] 
<span class="definition">vi. open into 可表示通向另一個地方（常指通向更大的一個地方）：</span>The corridor opened into a small room. 這條走廊通向一個小房間。

<span class="vocabulary">**run**</span> [rʌn] 
<span class="definition">vi. 從一地導向（或伸展，延伸向）另一地：</span>He had a scar running down his left cheek. 他左臉上豎著一道傷疤。/ The road runs parallel to the river. 這條路和這條河是平行的。/ We ran a cable from the lights to the stage. 我們從電燈那裡拉了一條電纜通到舞臺。
