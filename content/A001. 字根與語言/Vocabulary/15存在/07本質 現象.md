☀ <span class="category">**本質、實質、精髓：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**nature**</span> ['neɪtʃə] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 事物的本質、性質、基本特徵：</span>I’m not clear about the exact nature of their relationship. 我不清楚他們的確切關係。/ limited nature 侷限性 / seasonal nature 季節性 / uncertain nature 不確定性 / the subjective nature of perception 感知的主觀性 / abstract nature 抽象性 / complex nature 複雜性 / the dual nature of man as a physical and spiritual being 人作為肉體和精神存在的雙重性 / the sensitive nature of the case 案件的敏感性 / practical nature 實用性 / Their problems are of an economic nature. 他們的問題是經濟問題。/ competitive nature 競爭性 / the nature of the task 任務的性質 / the true nature of the world 世間的真諦 / physical, sexual nature 身體特徵；性特徵 / the true nature of the mountain 這座山的真實特點
           
<span class="vocabulary">**essence**</span> [ˈesns]
<span class="definition">n. [U] ~ (of sth) 某事物最重要的、使其成其為自身的品質或特徵，即本質、實質、精髓：</span>His paintings capture the essence of France. 他的畫描繪出法國的神韻。/ The essence of consultation is to listen to, and take account of, the views of those consulted. 當顧問的精髓就在於傾聽並考慮諮詢者的觀點。/ the essence of life 生活的本質 / The girl has her own spiritual essence. 這個女孩具有獨特的精神氣質。/ human essence 人的本質 / They distilled the essence of their message into three principles. 他們將自己的資訊精髓提煉為三個原則。/ His paintings embody the very essence of the post-war years. 他的畫體現了戰後的基本情況。/ His work misses the essence of what Eastern religion is about. 他的作品未能抓住東方宗教的本質。/ His theory was not new in essence. 他的理論實質上並不新穎。/ In essence (= when you consider the most important points), your situation isn't so different from mine. 從本質上講，你我的情況並非相差很遠。/ The freedom to pick your leaders is the essence of a democracy. 自由選舉領導人是民主政治的精髓所在。/ Time is of the essence. 時間是至關重要的。

<span class="vocabulary">**spirit**</span> ['spɪrɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [sing.] 某事物的典型（或最重要的）品質或屬性，如精髓，基本精神，本質：</span>the spirit of the age 時代精神 / She embodies the spirit of revolution. 她體現了革命精神。/ a spirit of adventure 冒險精神 / democratic spirit 民主精神 / creative spirit 創新精神 / entrepreneurial spirit 事業心 / The movie is true to the spirit of the book. 影片忠實於原著精神。<span class="definition">2 [U] 某事物的真實意義或目的，可稱為實質：</span>Obey the spirit, not the letter of the law. 要依照法律的精神實質，而不是字面意思。/ an edict that violates the spirit of the Geneva Convention 違背《日內瓦公約》精神的法令 

<span class="vocabulary">**gut**</span> [gʌt]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] guts 可指某事物最重要的部分，如核心、實質、要點：</span>the guts of the problem 問題的實質 / She has a reputation for getting at the guts of a subject and never pulling her punches. 她以善抓問題實質、說話一針見血著稱。/ The guts of the reactor have to be hauled out of the pressure vessel. 核反應堆的核心構件必須從壓力罩中取出來。

周邊詞彙補充：
· attribute可指某人或某事物的品質、屬性、特質或特徵。

☀ <span class="category">**現象、表面：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**phenomenon**</span> [fɪ'nɒmɪnən] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（自然或社會中的）現象（尤指未被充分理解的現象）：</span>a global phenomenon 全球現象 / natural, cultural, physical, social phenomenon 自然現象；文化現象；物理現象；社會現象 / supernatural phenomenon 超自然現象 / The phenomenon occurs in the early stages of pregnancy. 這種現象出現在懷孕早期。/ The movie has become a summer phenomenon. 看電影成了人們消夏的一種方式。<span class="definition">2 [C] phenomenons 在美國可指非常成功或令人印象非常深刻的一個人或事物：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**surface**</span> ['sə:fɪs] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.]（情況的）表面：</span>Rage bubbled just below the surface of his mind. 怒火在他心中燃燒，隨時可能迸發。/ Back home, things appear, on the surface, simpler. 回到家後，事情表面上看起來簡單一些。/ The investigation barely scratched the surface of the city’s drug problem. 這次調查只是觸及了該城市毒品問題的表面。
