☀ <span class="category">**存在的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**be**</span> [bi:] 
<span class="definition">linking verb. there is/are 存在，有：</span>Is there a God? 上帝存在嗎？/ Once upon a time there was a princess … 從前有一位公主… / I tried phoning but there was no answer. 我試著打過電話，但沒人接。/ There’s a bank down the road. 沿馬路不遠有一家銀行。/ Was there a pool at the hotel? 酒店裡有游泳池嗎？

<span class="vocabulary">**exist**</span> [ɪɡ'zɪst] 
<span class="definition">vi. 存在；實有；出現於某地或某種情況中：</span>Does life exist on other planets? 其他行星上有生命嗎？/ The problem only exists in your head, Jane. 這個問題不過是你的想象，簡。/ On his retirement the post will cease to exist. 他退休後這個職位就會撤掉。/ The charity exists to support victims of crime. 設立這個慈善機構是為了援助罪案受害者。/ I didn’t think people like that existed any more. 我認為再也沒有那樣的人了。/ The technology did not yet exist. 那時這種技術尚不存在。/ Ideas do not exist independently of the language that expresses them. 思想不會脫離其語言表達形式而獨立存在。

<span class="vocabulary">**survive**</span> [sə'vaɪv] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（人或生物在危險的情況下，如事故或疾病中）存活：</span>to battle to survive in icy seas 在冰冷的海洋中掙扎求生 / Lung cancer patients are lucky to survive for five years. 肺癌患者能活五年就算是萬幸了。/ He narrowly survived several assassination attempts. 幾次暗殺行動讓他差點兒喪命。/ She survived through two world wars. 她經歷了兩次世界大戰而倖存。/ They survived on roots and berries. 他們靠吃根莖和漿果活了下來。/ His five chickens survived to adulthood. 他的五隻小雞活到了成年。/ Seedlings survive better in stony soil. 幼苗在多石的土壤中能更好地存活。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**emerge**</span> [iˈmɜ:dʒ; 美 iˈmɜ:rdʒ]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (from sth)（從困境或不好的經歷中）倖存下來：</span>She emerged from the scandal with her reputation intact. 醜聞過後，她安然無恙，名聲絲毫未受影響。           

<span class="vocabulary">**viable**</span> [ˈvaɪəbl]
<span class="definition">adj. [生物學] 能獨立存活併發育的：</span>viable organisms 可存活的生物 / Five viable pregnancies were established. 已確定五個胎兒能夠成活。

<span class="vocabulary">**occur**</span> [ə'kə:] 
<span class="definition">vi. 在某處存在：</span>Sugar occurs naturally in fruit. 水果天然含糖分。

<span class="vocabulary">**live**</span> [lɪv] 
<span class="definition">vi. 繼續存在：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pertain**</span> [pəˈteɪn; 美 pərˈt-]
<span class="definition">vi. [正式] 在某種情況下（或在某個時間）存在：</span>Living conditions are vastly different from those pertaining in their country of origin. 生活條件與他們祖國大不相同。

周邊詞彙補充：
· coexist可指（尤指和平地）在某地或某時共存。

☀ <span class="category">**存在的名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**being**</span> ['bi:ɪŋ] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 存在。為正式和文學用法：</span>How do you think the world came into being? 你認為世界是怎樣形成的？/ The organization came into being in 2003. 該組織成立於2003年。

<span class="vocabulary">**existence**</span> [ɪɡ'zɪstəns] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] 存在，實有。是一個常規用詞：</span>I was unaware of his existence until today. 直到今天我才知道有他這麼個人。/ a manuscript in existence 現存的手稿 / the possible existence of life beyond Earth 地球之外可能有生命存在 / The idea has been in existence for centuries. 這種觀念已經存在了好幾個世紀。/ the industry’s continued existence 該行業的繼續存在 / The organization came into existence ten years ago. 該組織成立於十年前。/ They are striving to bring into existence a new kind of society. 他們正努力建立一個新型社會。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**presence**</span> [ˈprezns]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U]（人、組織機構等在某地的）存在：</span>the increased presence of Asian actors in Hollywood 亞洲演員在好裝塢的人數有所增加 / The company now has a strong presence in Germany. 該公司目前在德國有很強的勢力。/ The company plans to expand its presence in emerging markets. 該公司計劃擴大其在新興市場的佔有率。/ He remained a dominant presence in the art world. 他在藝術界仍占主導地位。/ the overwhelming presence of the church in daily lives 教會在日常生活中壓倒性的勢力 / the pervasive presence of the Web 網路的普遍存在 / He's become a ubiquitous presence on talk shows. 脫口秀節目中已少不了他。/ the visible presence of campus security 可以看得到的校園安全措施 / a small business that doesn't have a Web presence 沒有網站的小企業 / Establishing a strong presence on the Internet is a top priority. 在網際網路上做大做強是當務之急。/ Fox's efforts to create a Web presence for the movie 福克斯公司為該電影建立網頁的努力 / I could see no signs of human presence. 我看不到人類存在的跡象。<span class="definition">2 [U]（事物或物質）存在於某地或某事物中：</span>The test can identify the presence of abnormalities in the unborn child. 這項化驗能鑑定胎兒是否有不正常現象。/ The somewhat acid flavour is caused by the presence of lactic acid. 這種微酸的味道是因為含有乳酸造成的。/ the presence of a carcinogen in the water 水中含有的一種致癌物質 / These chemicals could indicate the presence of water on the planet. 這些化學物質可能表明這顆行星上有水。/ Tests confirmed the presence of the disease. 檢驗證實了該疾病的存在。<span class="definition">3 [C, usually sing.] [文學] 感覺在附近的人（或鬼魂）：</span>She felt a presence behind her. 她覺得有什麼東西跟在背後。/ She felt a presence in the room.她覺得房子裡有什麼東西存在。/ The forest was dark and silent, haunted by shadows and unseen presences. 這個森林黑暗寂靜，經常有幽靈和看不見的鬼怪出沒。/ She started to be affected by the ghostly presence she could feel in the house. 她覺得這座房子裡有可怕的鬼怪，開始變得神經緊張。/ I felt as though there was some ghostly presence. 我覺得好像有個幽靈在場。/ Flame has always symbolized a divine presence. 火焰一直象徵著神聖的存在。/ She sensed an evil presence, and it was growing stronger. 她感覺到有某種邪惡的東西，而且這種感覺越來越強烈。

<span class="vocabulary">**occurrence**</span> [əˈkʌrəns; 美 əˈkɜ:r-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 存在的事物：</span>The program counts the number of occurrences of any word, within the text. 這個程式可以統計任何單詞在文字中出現的次數。<span class="definition">2 [U] ~ (of sth) 存在：</span>These mild fits are quite a common occurrence in babies. 這種輕微的病症在嬰兒中很常見。/ words with a high frequency of occurrence 高頻詞

<span class="vocabulary">**survival**</span> [sə'vaɪvl] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U]（人或生物在危險情況下的）存活：</span>If cancers are spotted early there’s a high chance of survival. 如果癌症早被發現，存活的機率就高。/ survival skills, survival strategy 生存技能；生存策略 / survival guide, survival kit 生存指南；救生包 / survival instinct 求生本能 / Darwin’s theory of the survival of the fittest 達爾文的適者生存理論 <span class="definition">2 [C] survival (from sth) 倖存的事物，殘存物：相當於relic：</span>The ceremony is a survival from ancient Greek times. 這種儀式是從古希臘時期遺留下來的。

<span class="vocabulary">**survivor**</span> [səˈvaɪvə(r); 美 sərˈv-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（尤指災難、事故、疾病等險境、巨大困境、苦難或不幸的）倖存者、生還者、挺過巨大困難者：</span>crash, Holocaust, rape, shipwreck, tsunami survivor 撞毀事故倖存者；大屠殺倖存者；性侵倖存者；海難倖存者；海嘯倖存者 / cancer, stroke survivor 癌症倖存者；中風倖存者 / the recovery process for breast cancer survivors 乳腺癌倖存者的康復過程 / The navy helped pick up the survivors. 海軍幫忙撈起了倖存者。/ The emergency services searched all night for crash survivors. 應急服務機構整夜都在搜尋墜機事故倖存者。/ The bomb blast left no survivors. 爆炸事件中沒有人倖免於難。/ survivor guilt（心理學術語）倖存者的內疚感 / She is wrestling with survivor guilt. 作為倖存者，她還在負疚感中掙扎。/ a survivor from the Titanic "泰坦尼克"號的倖存者 / the sole/only survivor of the massacre 那場大屠殺的唯一倖存者 / The plane crashed in an area of dense jungle. There were no survivors. 飛機墜落在一個叢林茂密的地區，無人生還。/ This book is written with survivors of child sexual abuse in mind. 這本書關注的是童年時遭受過性騷擾的人。/ She'll cope. She's one of life's great survivors (= sb who deals very well with difficult situations). 她能挺過去。生活中什麼樣的困難她都能對付。/ Above all Susie is a great survivor, with a bright, indomitable spirit. 總之，蘇茜不畏艱難，開朗樂觀、不屈不撓。<span class="definition">2 [C] 活到現在的人；留存至今的事物：</span>There are only a few survivors from the original team (= members who remain in it while others have been replaced). 最初的隊員只剩下幾名了。/ Rafferty was the sole survivor from the successful Ireland team of 1988. 拉弗蒂是 1988 年紅極一時的愛爾蘭隊碩果僅存的球員。/ a black 1950 Cyclops, which is the only known survivor of 30 test cars built by Rover 路虎製造的 30 輛試驗車中目前僅存且為人所知的就是一輛 1950 年的黑色 Cyclops 遙控機械排爆車。/ This grand park is a rare survivor from the 18th century. 這座恢宏的公園是罕見的18世紀遺產。
 
<span class="vocabulary">**coincidence**</span> [kəʊ'ɪnsɪdəns] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] [正式] 同時存在，並存：</span>the coincidence of inflation and unemployment 通貨膨脹與失業的並存
           
<span class="vocabulary">**entity**</span> [ˈentəti]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [正式] 獨立存在物，即實體：</span>abstract entity 抽象實體 / artificial entity 人工實體 / biological, physical entity 生物實體；物理實體 / living entity 活體 / the earth as a living entity 作為一個有生命的實體的地球

相關詞義延伸：
· presence還可指被派到某地去處理事情的一群人（尤指一群士兵）。

☀ <span class="category">**存在的其它詞性：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**present**</span> ['preznt] 
<span class="definition">adj. present (in sth) 不作前置定語，（事物或物質、情況等）存在於某一地點或某一事物中：</span>Levels of pollution present in the atmosphere are increasing. 大氣中的汙染程度正在加深。/ Traces of arsenic were present in the body. 屍體中有微量砒霜。/ This special form of vitamin D is naturally present in breast milk. 這種特殊形式的維生素D天然存在於母乳中。/ Bacteria are naturally present in the environment. 細菌天然存在於環境當中。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**extant**</span> [ekˈstænt; ˈekstənt]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式]（非常古老的東西）尚存的、現存的：</span>extant remains of the ancient wall 尚存的古城牆遺蹟 / Two fourteenth-century manuscripts of this text are still extant. 這個文字仍有兩份14世紀手抄本存世。/ The oldest extant document is dated 1492. 現存最古老的文獻出自1492年。

<span class="vocabulary">**alive**</span> [ə'laɪv] 
<span class="definition">adj. 不作前置定語，指繼續存在：</span>The factory is trying to stay alive by cutting costs. 這家工廠試圖透過削減成本以維持生存。/ to keep the old traditions alive 保留傳統 / The old customs are still very much alive in this region. 這些舊習俗在這一地區仍很盛行。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**living**</span> [ˈlɪvɪŋ]
<span class="definition">adj. [只用於名詞前] 正被使用的；正在被實施的：</span>living languages (= those still spoken) 現用語言 / a living faith 仍有人信奉的信仰

<span class="vocabulary">**there**</span> [ðeə] 
<span class="definition">adv. 1 there is, are, was, were, etc. 表示存在或發生：</span>There’s a restaurant around the corner. 拐角處有一家餐館。/ There are two people waiting outside. 有兩個人正在外面等候。/ I don’t want there to be any misunderstanding. 我不希望有任何誤解。/ There remains the problem of finance. 財務問題仍然存在。/ Suddenly there was a loud bang. 突然發出砰的一聲巨響。<span class="definition">2 存在的；現成可用的，可得到的：</span>My old school was still there. 我的母校依然存在。/ The money’s there if you need it. 你若需要用錢就來取好了。

<span class="vocabulary">**in**</span> [ɪn] 
<span class="definition">prep. 用於表明一種狀態或條件：</span>I was badly hurt in quarreling with him. 在和他的爭吵中我很受傷。/ a young people in her twenties 一個二十幾歲的年輕人 / I fell in love with her. 我愛上她了。/ in trouble 限於困境 / in a hurry 急急忙忙 / I looked at him in surprise. 我驚異地看著他。/ Kindness is in her nature. 她天性善良。

<span class="vocabulary">**on**</span> [ɒn] 
<span class="definition">prep. 用於描述一個活動或狀態：</span>I’m on holiday. 我在度假。

周邊詞彙補充：
· get可用於表明某事發生或存在；
· at可指處於…狀態；
· latent可指存在但還不是非常明顯、活躍或成熟的，如潛伏的、隱藏的。

☀ <span class="category">**不在：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**non-existent**</span>
<span class="definition">adj. 不存在的：</span>a non-existent problem 不存在的問題 / ‘How's your social life? ’ ‘Non-existent, I'm afraid.’ “你的社交生活如何？”“我恐怕沒有社交生活。”/ Hospital beds were scarce and medicines were practically non-existent. 當時醫院病床緊缺，藥物根本所剩無幾。/ Hygiene was non-existent: no running water, no bathroom. 衛生根本談不上：沒有自來水、沒有衛生間。/ You'll take everything you're offered yet your own generosity is virtually non-existent. 你倒是來者不拒，照單全收，可你自己實際上卻一毛不拔。/ Typewriters have become almost non-existent in offices. 辦公室裡已經幾乎沒有打字員了。/ The hotel turned out to be completely non-existent. 最終發現這家旅館根本不存在。/ Crime is almost non-existent in these communities. 在這些社群幾乎不存在犯罪現象。

· absence可指（某人或某事物的）不存在或不可獲得；absent可指不存在於某事物中、不在。
