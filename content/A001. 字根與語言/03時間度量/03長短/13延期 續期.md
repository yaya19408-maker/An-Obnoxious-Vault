☀ <span class="category">**延期 續期：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**extension**</span> [ɪk'stenʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] extension (of sth) 對某事所允許的一段額外的時間，即延長期，放寬的期限：</span>a visa extension 延長簽證 / The pub had an extension on Christmas Eve.（英）這家酒吧已獲准在聖誕夜延長營業時間。/ She got an extension for writing her essay. 她獲准延期完成論文。/ The player has signed a five-year contract extension. 該運動員已續簽了五年的合同。
   
<span class="vocabulary">**renew**</span> [rɪˈnju:; 美 -ˈnu:]
<span class="definition">vt. 延長（執照、合同等的）有效期；使續簽：</span>to renew a licence, to renew a lease, to renew a subscription, to renew a contract 延長執照的期限；續簽租約；續訂；續簽合約 / Larry's landlord threatened not to renew his lease. 拉里的房東威脅說不跟他續租。/ The job was for a fixed term and the contract is not being renewed. 這個工作有固定期限，而且合同不能續簽。/ How do I go about renewing my passport? 我該如何去續簽護照？/ I'd like to renew these library books (= arrange to borrow them for a further period of time). 我想續借這幾本圖書館的書。/ Membership must be renewed annually. 會員資格必須每年更新一次。

<span class="vocabulary">**renewal**</span> [rɪˈnju:əl; 美 -ˈnu:əl]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（執照、合同等的）續簽、延期：</span>Her contract is coming up for renewal in the spring. 她的合同春天就應該續簽了。/ The premium should be received in this office within fourteen days of the renewal date. 保險費用需在14天的續約期內交到此辦公室。/ The lease comes up for renewal at the end of the month. 本租約到月底需要辦理展期。/ licence/license, membership renewal 更換執照；會員資格的延期 / He sought renewal of the grant. 他申請繼續領取補助金。/ You can contact us at the number shown on your last renewal notice. 你可以按最後一個更新通知上的號碼與我們聯絡。/ renewal fee 展期費用

<span class="vocabulary">**prolong**</span> [prəˈlɒŋ; 美 -ˈlɔ:ŋ; -ˈlɑ:ŋ]
<span class="definition">vt. 延長某事的時間：</span>Might it be possible to prolong life indefinitely? 有可能無限延長壽命嗎? / The operation could prolong his life by two or three years. 這次手術可使他多活兩三年。/ Doctors commented that some patients deliberately prolong their treatment. 醫生說有些病人故意延長治療時間。/ The actual action of the drug can be prolonged significantly. 這種藥物實際起作用的時間可以大大延長。/ We do not want to prolong the meeting unnecessarily.我們不想不必要地延長開會時間。/ Mr Chesler said foreign military aid was prolonging the war. 切斯勒先生說國外的軍事援助拉長了戰事。/ Don't prolong the agony (= of not knowing sth) ─just tell us who won! 別賣關子了——快說誰贏了！
        
<span class="vocabulary">**extend**</span> [ɪkˈstend]
<span class="definition">vt. 使…持續更長時間：</span>to extend a deadline, to extend a visa 延長最後期限；延長簽證 / an extended contract 延期的合同 / The repayment period will be extended from 20 years to 25 years. 償還期將從20年延長到25年。/ The show has been extended for another six weeks. 展覽會又延長了六週。/ They have extended the deadline by twenty-four hours. 他們已經將最後期限延長了24小時。/ Careful maintenance can extend the life of your car. 精心保養可延長汽車壽命。
