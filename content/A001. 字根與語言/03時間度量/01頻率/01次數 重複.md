☀ <span class="category">**次數 重複：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**frequency**</span> [ˈfri:kwənsi]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C] 某事的發生率、重複率：</span>Accidents of this sort are happening with increasing frequency. 此類事故發生得越來越頻繁了。/ Fatal road accidents have decreased in frequency over recent years. 近年來致命交通事故發生率已經下降。/ The drug can reduce the frequency and severity of attacks. 這種要能降低發病的次數和嚴重程度。/ The frequency of Kara's phone calls increased rapidly. 卡拉的電話飛速增多。/ The tanks broke down with increasing frequency. 坦克越來越頻繁地熄火。/ a society with a low frequency of stable marriages 婚姻穩定率低的社會 / The program can show us word frequency (= how often words occur in a language). 這個程式可給我們顯示詞頻。

<span class="vocabulary">**rate**</span> [reɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 某事發生的頻率：</span>New diet books appear at a rate of nearly one a week. 飲食方面的新書以幾乎每週一本的頻率出版。

<span class="vocabulary">**time**</span> [taɪm] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（做某事或某事發生的）次，回；還可指時機：</span>every time 每次 / next time 下次 / He failed his driving test three times. 他考了三次駕駛執照都沒透過。/ When was the last time you saw her? 你上次是什麼時候見到她的？
             
<span class="vocabulary">**occasion**</span> [əˈkeɪʒn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 某事發生的那個時間，即…的時候，某次：</span>On this occasion, as it happens, the engine started immediately. 這一次碰巧引擎一下子就啟動了。/ On this particular occasion, Joe wasn't there. 在這個特殊場合，喬卻不在。/ On one occasion he even rang me in the middle of the night. 有一次他甚至半夜給我打電話。/ I often think fondly of an occasion some years ago at Covent Garden. 我時常愉快地回想起幾年前在科文特花園的一個時刻。/ I've met him on several occasions. 我曾見過他幾次。/ I have stayed there on a number of occasions. 我曾在那兒住過多次。/ It was the first of many such occasions. 這是第一次，這種情況後來發生過很多次。/ Mr Davis has been asked on a number of occasions. 戴維斯先生曾多次被問及。/ The police were called out on 24 separate occasions. 警察奉命出動了24次。/ They have been seen together on two separate occasions. 他們有兩次被人看見在一起。/ I can remember very few occasions when he had to cancel because of ill health. 我記得他因為健康不佳而被迫取消的情況絕無僅有。         

<span class="vocabulary">**once**</span> [wʌns] 
<span class="definition">adv. 僅僅一次；一次：</span>I’ve only been there once. 我只去過那裡一次。/ He cleans his car once a week. 他每週洗一次汽車。<span class="definition">pron. 一次：</span>“Have they been to visit you yet?”--“Just the once, yeah.” “他們已經去看過你了嗎？”——“來過，就那一次。”
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**recur**</span> [rɪˈkɜ:(r)]
<span class="definition">vi. 再次發生；多次發生、反覆出現：</span>This theme recurs several times throughout the book. 這一主題在整部書裡出現了好幾次。/ a theme that was to recur frequently in his work 一個在他的作品中反覆出現的主題 / a recurring nightmare she has had since childhood 一個從她童年起就不斷出現的噩夢 / recurring illness 反覆發作的疾病 / This is a constantly recurring problem which we must deal with. 這個問題反覆出現，我們必須予以處理。

<span class="vocabulary">**recurrence**</span> [rɪˈkʌrəns; 美 -ˈkɜ:r-]
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing., U] 再次發生：</span>attempts to prevent a recurrence of the problem 防止問題再次發生的努力 / Police are out in force to prevent a recurrence of the violence. 警方出動大量警力防止暴力事件再次發生。/ cancer, disease, tumour/tumor, ulcer recurrence 癌症復發；疾病復發；腫瘤復發；潰瘍復發 / He has suffered a recurrence of his hamstring injury. 他的膕繩肌傷復發。/ a potentially fatal disease with a high recurrence rate 一種複發率很高的致命性疾病 / a high risk of recurrence 高復發風險
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**relapse**</span> [rɪˈlæps; ˈri:læps]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U] 舊病復發：</span>to have/suffer a relapse 舊病復發 / acute relapse 急性的復發 / A wide range of emotionally stressful events may trigger a relapse. 多種令人情緒緊張的事情都可能引起舊病復發。/ A drug counsellor/counselor advises patients on relapse prevention. 藥物顧問指導病人防止舊病復發。/ The therapy can reduce the relapse rate for multiple sclerosis. 這種療法可以減少多發性硬化的複發率。/ Even in relapse there will be times when the patient's general condition improves. 即便在復發期間，病人仍會有好轉的時候。/ a risk of relapse 舊病復發的危險 <span class="definition">vi. ~ (into sth)（某人）重新陷入先前不好的狀態：</span>They relapsed into silence. 他們又都沉默不語。/ 'I wish I did,' said Phil Jordan, relapsing into his usual gloom. “我要是那樣做了就好了，”菲爾·喬丹說道，又像往常一樣消沉起來。/ It is by no means clear that the financial markets will not relapse into panic. 現在還不能確定金融市場不會重新陷入恐慌。<span class="definition">vi. 舊病復發、再次發病：</span>In 90 per cent of cases the patient will relapse within six months. 90%的病人在六個月內會再次發病。/ He relapsed into his old bad habits. 他重新染上了惡習。/ Two days after leaving the hospital she relapsed into a coma. 出院兩天後，她再度昏迷。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**flare**</span> [fleə(r); 美 fler]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ up（疾病、傷勢）突然復發或再次疼痛：</span>Students often find that their acne flares up before and during exams. 學生們經常發現在考前和考試期間他們的粉刺會嚴重起來。

<span class="vocabulary">**repetition**</span> [ˌrepəˈtɪʃn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] 重複去做或說，即重做、重說、重複：</span>learning by repetition 透過重複來學習 / If you can, perform multiple repetitions of this exercise on each hand. 如果可能，每隻手都反覆進行這項練習。/ She said I should avoid repetition of words in my essay. 她說我的作文應該避免詞語的重複使用。/ the mindless repetition of the official line 對官方路線的機械重複 / the rote repetition of mythological formulas（尤美）對神話慣用詞語的機械重複 / Blues is a musical form that uses a lot of repetition. 藍調音樂是一種不斷重複旋律的音樂。/ At the risk of repetition (= although I have already mentioned this), it is worth mentioning again that young children are particularly vulnerable to accidents in the home. 雖然我已經說過了，但我還是要重複一遍，小孩子在家裡尤其容易出事。<span class="definition">2 [C] 常指令人不快的事件的重複出現、重演：</span>We do not want to see a repetition of last year's tragic events. 我們不想看到去年的悲劇重演。/ Today the city government has taken measures to prevent a repetition of last year's confrontation. 今天市政府已經採取措施，防止去年的衝突重演。/ Collins wants to avoid repetition of the confusion that followed the discovery in 1989 of the cystic fibrosis gene. 科林斯想要避免1989年發現囊腫性纖維化致病基因後的那種混亂局面再次出現。<span class="definition">3 [U, C] 重複的文字內容：</span>He could also have cut out much of the repetition and thus saved many pages. 他原本也可以砍去許多重複的內容，從而縮減許多頁。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**repetitive**</span> [rɪˈpetətɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. 1（說話或做事）重複多次以致乏味的：</span>a repetitive task 重複乏味的任務 / factory workers who do repetitive jobs 從事重複性工作的工廠工人 / Suddenly music that seemed dull and repetitive comes alive. 突然間，似乎索然無味且反反覆覆的音樂生動了起來。<span class="definition">2 被重複多次的：</span>a repetitive pattern of behaviour 重複的行為模式 / problems that occur as the result of repetitive movements 反覆運動引起的問題

<span class="vocabulary">**duplication**</span> [ˌdju:plɪˈkeɪʃn; 美 ˌdu:-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（某人執行任務過程中）不必要的重複（因為先前已經做過了）：</span>Duties have been reassigned to avoid wasteful duplication of work. 為避免重複勞動浪費資源，任務已經重新分派。/ The new procedures should reduce duplication of medical care and treatment. 新的流程應當減少重複治療。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**replay**</span> [ˈri:pleɪ]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式] 被重複的事情；重複出現的事物：</span>This election will not be a replay of the last one. 這次選舉將不會是上一次的重演。

<span class="vocabulary">**again**</span> [ə'ɡeɪn] 
<span class="definition">adv. 1 再一次；又一次。是此義的常規用詞：</span>When will I see you again? 我何時能再見到你？/ Once again, the train was late. 火車又晚點了。/ again and again 一再 <span class="definition">2 用於讓某人把某事再說一遍：</span>Could you say it again, please? 請再說一遍好嗎？ 

<span class="vocabulary">**over**</span> ['əʊvə] 
<span class="definition">adv. 又一次、再一次。不如again常用：</span>He repeated it several times over until he could remember it. 他重複了幾遍直到能記住為止。/ It’s all wrong--you’ll have to do it over.（美）完全錯了。你得重做一遍。

<span class="vocabulary">**around**</span> [ə'raʊnd] 
<span class="definition">adv. this time around或to come around可表示又一次地。不常用：</span>He has declined to get involved this time around. 他這次又拒絕了介入。

<span class="vocabulary">**after**</span> ['ɑːftə] 
<span class="definition">prep. ... after ... 用於表明某事多次（或不斷地）發生：</span>day after day of hot weather 日復一日的炎熱天氣 / time after time 一再

<span class="vocabulary">**for**</span> [fɔ:] 
<span class="definition">prep. 用於表示某事發生的場合：</span>I’m warning you for the last time! 我最後一次警告你！/ He was married for the second time. 他第二次結婚了。

<span class="vocabulary">**back**</span> [bæk] 
<span class="definition">adv. 再度變得時尚：</span>Short skirts are back. 短裙再度流行起來。

相關詞義延伸：
· frequency還可指頻繁。也可指（聲波、電磁波、振動、訊號等的）頻率；
· replay還可指重新播放（錄影、錄音等）。

周邊詞彙補充：
· pulse可表示脈搏、脈率，也可表示音樂中有規律的節拍（常由擊鼓發出），還可表示（電流、光、聲等的）脈衝；
· yet在yet another、yet more、yet another again中用於強調數目、數量或次數的增加，即又、再、另。常用度與around近似；
· repeat可指重做某事、某事以同樣的方式重複發生或出現、食物在口中留有餘味、重播的電視（或廣播）節目、與以前發生的事件極其相似的一次事件、音樂中的重複段或反覆的部分；
· iteration可指（計算過程的）迭代（即重複一個計算過程，或重複一套指令）。還可指計算機的新版軟體；
· periodicity可指週期性；
· rhythm可指聲音的節奏、韻律。還可指運動的節奏。也可指變化的節奏（即有規律的變化）、事件的節奏；
· reproduce可指再次產生；以相同的方式使某事再次發生。
