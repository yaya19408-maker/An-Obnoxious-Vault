<font color="#f79646">含義：走</font>
<font color="#f79646">變體：無</font>
<font color="#f79646">來源：拉丁單詞varicare（意為to straddle、to spread one's legs或to walk awkwardly，即兩腿叉開坐、伸腿或彆扭地走）</font>
<font color="#f79646">用途：構成走相關詞彙</font>

prevaricate v. 支吾，搪塞；說謊
prevarication n. 支吾，搪塞；說謊
