<font color="#f79646">含義：縫合</font>
<font color="#f79646">變體：rhapho, rhaphy, rrhaphy</font>
<font color="#f79646">來源：希臘單詞rhaptein（意為to sew或to stitch，即縫紉或縫合）</font>
<font color="#f79646">用途：構成縫合相關詞彙</font>

achillorrhaphy n. 跟腱縫合術
angiorrhaphy n. 血管縫合術    
aneurysmorrhaphy n. 動脈瘤縫閉術
aortorrhaphy n. 主動脈縫合術
arteriorrhaphy n. 動脈縫合術；動脈修補術
autorrhaphy n. 自體皮片蓋合術

cardiorrhaphy n. 心臟修補術，心肌縫合術
cecorrhaphy n. 盲腸縫合術
colorrhaphy n. 結腸縫合術
cheilorrhaphy n. 唇裂修復術
cholecystenterorrhaphy n. 膽囊小腸縫合術
