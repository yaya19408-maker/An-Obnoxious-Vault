**<center>1. trachel</center>**

- ‌‌‌<font color="#f79646">含義：子宮頸</font>
- ‌‌‌<font color="#f79646">變體：trachelo</font>
- ‌‌‌<font color="#f79646">來源：希臘單詞trachēlos（意為neck，即脖子）</font>
- ‌‌‌<font color="#f79646">用途：構成子宮頸相關詞彙</font>

<font color="sky blue">**trachelitis**</font> n. 頸部炎症；子宮頸炎 

<font color="sky blue">**endotrachelitis**</font> n. 子宮頸內膜炎

<font color="sky blue">**hematotrachelos**</font> n. 子宮頸積血

<font color="sky blue">**trachelorrhaphy**</font> n. 子宮頸縫合術

<font color="sky blue">**hysterotrachelorrhaphy**</font> n. 宮頸裂傷縫合術

<font color="sky blue">**tracheloplasty**</font> n. 子宮頸成形術

<font color="sky blue">**hysterotracheloplasty**</font> n. 子宮頸成形術

<font color="sky blue">**trachelectomy**</font> n. 子宮頸切除術（切除子宮頸和陰道的上部）

<font color="sky blue">**hysterotrachelectomy**</font> n. 子宮頸切除術

<font color="sky blue">**hysterotrachelotomy**</font> n. 子宮頸切開術

**<center>2. cervic</center>**

- ‌‌‌<font color="#f79646">含義：子宮頸</font>
- ‌‌‌<font color="#f79646">變體：cervico</font>
- ‌‌‌<font color="#f79646">來源：拉丁單詞cervix（意為neck或nape，即脖子或頸背）</font>
- ‌‌‌<font color="#f79646">用途：構成子宮頸相關詞彙</font>

<font color="sky blue">**cervical**</font> adj. 子宮頸的

<font color="sky blue">**cervicitis**</font> n. 宮頸炎，子宮頸炎

<font color="sky blue">**cervical cancer**</font> 子宮頸癌，宮頸癌

<font color="sky blue">**cervicoscope**</font> n. 子宮頸鏡

<font color="sky blue">**cervicectomy**</font> n. 宮頸切除術，子宮頸切除術
