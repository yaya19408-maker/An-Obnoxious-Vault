<font color="sky blue">**trachelitis**</font> n. 頸部炎症；子宮頸炎 

<font color="sky blue">**endotrachelitis**</font> n. 子宮頸內膜炎

<font color="sky blue">**hematotrachelos**</font> n. 子宮頸積血

<font color="sky blue">**trachelorrhaphy**</font> n. 子宮頸縫合術

<font color="sky blue">**hysterotrachelorrhaphy**</font> n. 宮頸裂傷縫合術

<font color="sky blue">**tracheloplasty**</font> n. 子宮頸成形術

<font color="sky blue">**hysterotracheloplasty**</font> n. 子宮頸成形術

<font color="sky blue">**trachelectomy**</font> n. 子宮頸切除術（切除子宮頸和陰道的上部）

<font color="sky blue">**hysterotrachelectomy**</font> n. 子宮頸切除術

<font color="sky blue">**hysterotrachelotomy**</font> n. 子宮頸切開術
