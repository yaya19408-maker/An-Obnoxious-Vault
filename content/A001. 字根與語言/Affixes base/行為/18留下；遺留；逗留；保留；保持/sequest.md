<font color="#f79646">含義：沒收</font>
<font color="#f79646">變體：sequestr</font>
<font color="#f79646">來源：拉丁單詞sequestrare（意為to seize and hold，即沒收或扣押）</font>
<font color="#f79646">用途：構成沒收或扣押等詞彙</font>

sequester v. 沒收，扣押
sequestrate v. 沒收，接收；扣押（債務人的房產等）
sequestration n. 扣押
