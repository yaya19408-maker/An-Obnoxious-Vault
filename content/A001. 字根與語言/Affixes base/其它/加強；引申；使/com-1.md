<font color="#f79646">含義：使</font>
<font color="#f79646">變體：無</font>
<font color="#f79646">來源：拉丁字首com（意為to emphasize the meaning of the root word，即強調詞根的含義）</font>
<font color="#f79646">用途：構成使相關詞彙</font>

commove v. 使騷動；使激動；使激烈搖動
compress v. 壓縮  
commix v. 混合  
commemorate v. 紀念，慶祝
