<font color="#f79646">含義：反駁</font>
<font color="#f79646">變體：futable</font>
<font color="#f79646">來源：拉丁單詞fututus（意為discredited或refuted，即不足信的或被反駁的）</font>
<font color="#f79646">用途：構成反駁相關詞彙</font>

confute v. 駁斥，駁倒
refute v. 駁斥，駁倒
refutable adj. 可駁倒的
