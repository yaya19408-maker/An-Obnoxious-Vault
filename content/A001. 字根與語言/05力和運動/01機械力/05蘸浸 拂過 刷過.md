☀ <span class="category">**蘸浸 拂過 刷過：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**dip**</span> [dɪp] 
<span class="definition">vt. dip sth (into sth) / dip sth (in) 把…快速放入液體中然後將其拿出，即蘸，浸：</span>He dipped the brush into the paint. 他拿畫筆蘸了蘸顏料。/ Dip your hand in to see how hot the water is. 把手伸進去看看水有多熱。/ The fruit had been dipped in chocolate. 這水果蘸過巧克力醬。/ Quickly dip the tomatoes in boiling water. 在沸水中把西紅柿快速地焯一下。

<span class="vocabulary">**brush**</span> [brʌʃ] 
<span class="definition">n. [sing.] 經過…的時候對其進行的輕輕的一個觸控，即輕擦著掠過：</span>the brush of her lips on his cheek 她的嘴唇在他臉上的輕輕一碰 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi. brush (against/by/past) sb/sth 輕輕擦過或掠過某人或某物：</span>Her long hair brushed my shoulder. 她的長髮輕輕拂過我的肩膀。/ She brushed past him. 她和他擦肩而過。/ His hand accidentally brushed against hers. 他的手無意之中碰了一下她的手。/ He brushed her lips with his. 他輕輕地吻了一下她的嘴唇。/ The leaves brushed her cheek. 葉子輕輕拂過她的面頰。/ She carefully avoided brushing against the man sitting beside her. 她小心翼翼，避免碰到坐在她旁邊的男子。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**swipe**</span> [swaɪp]
<span class="definition">vt. 刷磁卡（如信用卡）：</span>Swipe your card through the phone, then dial. 在電話機上刷卡，然後撥號。

<span class="definition">n. [C] [尤英] 刷磁卡的動作：</span>swipe card（尤英）磁卡 / Students use swipe cards rather than cash for meals. 學生刷卡用餐，不用現金。
