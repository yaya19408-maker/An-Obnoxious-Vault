<span class="vocabulary">**date**</span> [deɪt] 
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] [英]（在某個時間與某人的）約見，約見時間：</span>Call me next week and we’ll try and make a date. 下週打電話給我，我們爭取定個見面時間。<span class="definition">2 [C]（與男女朋友或潛在的男女朋友的）約會：</span>I have a date with Bob. 我和鮑勃有個約會。/ lunch date 午餐約會 / first date 初次約會 / blind date 相親 <span class="definition">3 [C] [尤美]（男女朋友）約會的物件：</span>My date is meeting me at seven. 我的物件七點鐘與我見面。<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. [尤美] 與某人談戀愛或約會：</span>She’s been dating Ron for several months. 她與羅恩談戀愛已有數月。
