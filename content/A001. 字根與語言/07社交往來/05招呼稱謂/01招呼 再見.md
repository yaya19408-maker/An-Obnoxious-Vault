☀ <span class="category">**招呼 再見：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**hail**</span> [heɪl]
<span class="definition">vt. [文學] 跟某人打招呼；叫某人以吸引其注意力：</span>A voice hailed us from the other side of the street. 街對面有個聲音招呼我們。/ Jill saw him and hailed him. 吉爾看到了他，並和他打招呼。/ Suddenly, a voice hailed us and there was Miss Quigley. 忽然傳來一聲招呼，原來是奎格利小姐。

<span class="vocabulary">**hello**</span> ['hələʊ, he'ləʊ] 
<span class="definition">exclamation., n.（遇見某人、接聽電話或引起某人注意時）用於問候對方：</span>Hello John, how are you? 哈羅，約翰，你好嗎？/ Hello, is there anybody there? 喂，有人嗎？/ Say hello to Liz for me. 替我向利茲問好。/ to exchanged hellos 相互打招呼

<span class="vocabulary">**hi**</span> [haɪ] 
<span class="definition">exclamation. [非正式] 用於打招呼：</span>Hi guys! 嗨，夥計們！/ Hi, there! How’re you doing? 喂！你好嗎？

<span class="vocabulary">**bid**</span> [bɪd] 
<span class="definition">vt. bid (sb) good morning, farewell, etc. 向某人問候、道別等，為正式用法：</span>I bade farewell to Tom. 我告別了湯姆。

<span class="vocabulary">**bye**</span> [baɪ] 
<span class="definition">exclamation. [非正式] 再見，再會：</span>Bye! See you next week. 再見！下星期再會。/ to wave bye-bye 揮手道別

<span class="vocabulary">**cheers**</span> [tʃɪəz] 
<span class="definition">exclamation. [英，非正式] 再見，相當於goodbye：</span>Cheers then. See you later. 告辭了。再見！
           
<span class="vocabulary">**farewell**</span> [ˌfeəˈwel; 美 ˌferˈwel]
<span class="definition">exclamation. [正式] 再見：</span>瞭解即可
