☀ <span class="category">**感激、感謝的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**thank**</span> [θæŋk] 
<span class="definition">vt. thank sb (for sth/for doing sth) 感謝某人。是此義的常規用詞，日常生活中最為常用：</span>I would like to thank you all most sincerely. 我要向你們所有人致以最真誠的感謝。/ He thanked her warmly for the meal. 他熱情地感謝了她的款待。

<span class="vocabulary">**appreciate**</span> [ə'pri:ʃɪeɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt. 對某人所做的某事表示感激；歡迎某事（其賓語是事情）。相對正式：</span>We do really appreciate your help. 我們對您的幫助深表感謝。/ Your support is greatly appreciated. 十分感謝您的支援。/ I would appreciate it if you paid in cash. 假如您用現金付款的話，我會不勝感激。/ We would appreciate you letting us know of any problems. 如有任何問題，請告訴我們。/ I don’t appreciate being treated like a second-class citizen. 我不願被人當作二等公民。

<span class="vocabulary">**acknowledge**</span> [ək'nɒlɪdӡ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 對自己所得到的幫助向對方給予公開感謝：</span>I gratefully acknowledge financial support from several local businesses. 我對本地幾家企業的資助表示感謝。

<span class="vocabulary">**recognize**</span> ['rekəɡnaɪz]（英也用recognise）
<span class="definition">vt. 對某人所做過（或達成）的某事表示正式感謝：</span>His services to the state were recognized with the award of a knighthood. 他被封為爵士，以表彰他對國家的貢獻。

<span class="vocabulary">**owe**</span> [əʊ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 owe sth to sb / owe sb sth 欠人情：</span>I owe a debt of gratitude to all my family. 我很感激我的全家人。/ You owe me a favour! 你還欠我個人情哦！/ I think you owe us an explanation. 我認為你應當給我們一個解釋。/ I think we’re owed an apology. 我認為得有人向我們道歉。<span class="definition">2 owe sth to sb/sth / owe sb sth 歸因於，或把成功歸功於，感激，得益於：</span>He owes his success to hard work. 他的成功是靠勤奮工作。/ The play owes much to French tragedy. 這部戲頗受法國悲劇的影響。/ I owe everything to him. 我的一切都歸功於他。/ I knew that I owed the surgeon my life. 我明白外科醫生救了我的命。<span class="definition">3 應給予（某人應得的感激、尊重、忠誠）：</span>We all owe him more respect. 我們都應當給予他更多尊重。

☀ <span class="category">**感激、感謝的名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**thanks**</span> [θæŋks]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] ~ (to sb) (for sth) 表示感激的言辭或行動，如感謝、謝意、感激：</span>How can I ever express my thanks to you for all you've done? 對你所做的一切，我怎麼才能表達謝意？/ Thanks are due to all those who worked so hard for so many months. 感謝這麼多月來辛勤工作的每一個人。/ She murmured her thanks. 她低聲道謝。

<span class="vocabulary">**gratitude**</span> [ˈgrætɪtju:d; 美 -tu:d]
<span class="definition">n. [U] ~ (to sb) (for sth) 感激之情、感謝之情：</span>He smiled at them with gratitude. 他向他們笑了笑表示謝意。/ I would like to express my gratitude to everyone for their hard work. 我要對所有辛勤勞動的人表示感謝。/ She was presented with the gift in gratitude for her long service. 這禮物贈送給她以表達對她長期服務的感激之情。/ I owe you a great debt of gratitude (= feel extremely grateful). 我對你感激不盡。/ We owe her a deep debt of gratitude for her services. 對於她的付出，我們感激不盡。/ deep, profound gratitude 發自內心的感激；深切的感激 / heartfelt, sincere gratitude 衷心的感謝；真誠的感謝 / the very deep gratitude I felt towards/toward her 我對她懷有的深切感激之情 / You have our undying gratitude. 我們對你永存感激之情。/ They deserve our gratitude for all the work they do. 他們所做的一切值得我們感激。/ His kindness earned him her eternal gratitude. 他的友善令她永存謝意。/ He almost wept in gratitude when he saw the money. 看到那筆錢時，他幾乎感激涕零。/ I remember them with gratitude. 我心存感激地記著他們。/ my gratitude at her thoughtfulness 我對她的體貼的感激之情 / I sent him some money as a token of my gratitude. 我寄了些錢給他聊表謝意。/ a feeling of gratitude, a sense of gratitude 感激之情

<span class="vocabulary">**appreciation**</span> [ə͵pri:ʃɪ'eɪʃn] 
<span class="definition">n. [U] appreciation (of/for sth) 對某事所懷有的感激之情：</span>I would like to express my appreciation and thanks to you all. 我想對你們所有人表達我的感激和謝意。/ to extend appreciation（正式，尤美） 謹致謝忱
           
<span class="vocabulary">**acknowledgement**</span> [əkˈnɒlɪdʒmənt; 美 əkˈnɑ:l-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C, U] 對某人的感謝（可以是一種話語，也可以是一種行為，還可以是作為謝禮的物品）：</span>I was sent a free copy in acknowledgement of my contribution. 我收到一本贈刊，表示對我投稿的謝意。/ The flowers were a small acknowledgement of your kindness. 這些花聊表謝意，感謝你的好心幫助。<span class="definition">2 [C, usually pl.]（尤指作者寫在一本書的卷首的，對幫助過自己的人們表示的）公開致謝、鳴謝：</span>The benefit to the donor was an acknowledgment of the donation in a printed bulletin. 捐贈者被印入捐贈公示榜以示感謝。/ Grateful acknowledgment is made for permission to reprint. 為獲准重印致以深切感謝。

<span class="vocabulary">**debt**</span> [det] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 常用單數，人情債，恩情：</span>to acknowledge debt 承認欠人情 / to repay a debt 還人情債 / I will be forever in Ruth’s debt for the excellent advice she gave me. 露絲給了我很好的建議，我會永遠感激她。/ We owe a great debt of gratitude to our families for their support. 我們非常感激家人們給予的支援。

<span class="vocabulary">**star**</span> [stɑː] 
<span class="definition">n. [C, usually sing.] [非正式] 對某人為你做某事表示非常感激；或用於表示讚歎：</span>Thanks! You’re a star! 多謝多謝！你真是個大好人！

☀ <span class="category">**感激、感謝的其它詞性：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**thankful**</span> ['θæŋkfl] 
<span class="definition">adj. thankful (for sth) / thankful (to do sth) / thankful (that ...) 常不用於名詞前，指為已發生的某件好事（或未發生的某件壞事）而感到愉悅，如感激的、欣慰的。其感激的物件通常是上蒼或命運：</span>I was thankful that he hadn’t been hurt. 他沒有受傷，我感到很欣慰。/ I’ll be forever thankful to John. He saved my life. 我會終生感激約翰。他救了我的命。/ We were thankful for the chance to rest. 我們慶幸有這個機會休息一下。

<span class="vocabulary">**grateful**</span> ['ɡreɪtfl] 
<span class="definition">adj. 1 grateful (to sb) (for sth) / grateful (to do sth) / grateful (that ...)（由於某人做了對你好的事或按照你的要求做了某事而）感到（或表現出）感謝的，即感激的。其感激的物件通常是人或事情：</span>I’m really grateful for your help. 對你的幫助我真的感激不盡。/ Kate gave him a grateful smile. 凱特感激地對他笑了笑。/ I’m just grateful the injury is not as bad as we’d feared. 傷勢並不像我們擔心的那麼嚴重，我對此真是慶幸不已。/ She seems to think I should be grateful to have a job at all. 她似乎認為我有份工作就該謝天謝地了。<span class="definition">2（尤用於一封書信或一個正式場合）用於提出一個請求，如感激不盡，請：</span>I would be grateful if you could send the completed form back as soon as possible. 請儘快將表格填好寄回。/ We would be grateful for any information you can give us. 如能提供資訊我們將感激不盡。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**obliged**</span> [əˈblaɪdʒd]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] [名詞前不可用]（對某人）感激的、感謝的：</span>I'm much obliged to you for helping us. 承蒙相助，本人不勝感激。/ I would be obliged if you could read it to us. 您若能把它讀給大家聽，我將不勝感激。/ I'd be obliged if you would keep this to yourself. 如蒙保守這個秘密，我將感激不盡。/ Much obliged for your assistance. 對您的幫助我不勝感激。/ Thank you very much indeed, Doctor, I am extremely obliged to you. 大夫，太感謝您了，您真是我的大恩人。

<span class="vocabulary">**indebted**</span> [ɪnˈdetɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. [正式] ~ (to sb) (for sth)（對幫助過你的人）感激的：</span>I am deeply indebted to him for his help. 我非常感激他對我的幫助。/ I am deeply indebted to my family for all their help. 我深深感激我的家人給我的所有幫助。/ I am deeply indebted to all the doctors and nurses who treated me. 我深深地感激所有救治我的醫生和護士。/ Thank you--I am forever indebted to you. 謝謝你—-我會對你永懷感激之情。

<span class="vocabulary">**glad**</span> [ɡlæd] 
<span class="definition">adj. glad of sth / glad if ... 對某事表示感激的。其感激的物件通常是事物或事情：</span>She was very glad of her warm coat in the biting wind. 在刺骨的寒風中她有暖融融的大衣真是謝天謝地。/ I’d be glad of your help. 你若能幫助我，我會非常感激。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**thanks**</span> [θæŋks]
<span class="definition">exclamation. ~ (for sth/doing sth) 用於表示感激，如感謝、謝謝：</span>Thanks for lending me the money. 多謝您借錢給我。/ Many thanks for your support. 多謝您的支援。/ ‘How are you? ’ ‘Fine, thanks. ’ (= thanks for asking) “你好嗎？”“好，謝謝。” <span class="definition">exclamation. 用於委婉地接受別人的好意，如好的、謝謝：</span>‘Would you like a coffee?’ ‘Oh, thanks.’ “來杯咖啡好嗎？”“好的，謝謝。”/ ‘Here's the change.’ ‘Thanks very much.’ “這是找你的零錢。”“非常感謝。” <span class="definition">exclamation. no thanks 用於婉言謝絕別人給你的…，如不用了、謝謝：</span>‘Would you like some more? ’ ‘No thanks.’ “再要一點兒嗎？”“不要了，謝謝。” 

<span class="vocabulary">**cheers**</span> [tʃɪəz] 
<span class="definition">exclamation. [英，非正式] 謝謝，相當於thank you：</span>瞭解即可
