☀ <span class="category">**殺的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**kill**</span> [kɪl] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 殺死，使死亡。是此義的基礎用詞：</span>He admitted killing her but said it was unintentional. 他承認殺死了她，但他說不是故意的。/ They plotted to kill the dictator. 他們密謀刺殺那位獨裁者。/ Cancer kills thousands of people every year. 每年數以千計的人死於癌症。/ Their daughter was tragically killed in a road accident. 他們的女兒在一次車禍中慘死。/ Tiredness while driving can kill. 疲勞駕駛會出人命的。/ He tried to kill himself with sleeping pills. 他試圖服安眠藥自殺。/ soldiers killed in battle 在戰鬥中陣亡計程車兵 / The animals are killed quickly and humanely. 那些動物被迅速人道地宰殺。/ I bought a spray to kill the weeds. 我買了一種噴劑可以除草。/ Don’t kill yourself trying to get the work done by tomorrow. It can wait.（比喻用法）別為了趕著明天把事情幹完而累壞身體。這事可以等一等嘛。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**eliminate**</span> [ɪˈlɪmɪneɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 殺死某人，尤指殺死一個敵人或對手：</span>Most of the regime's left-wing opponents were eliminated. 這個政權的左翼反對派多數已被除掉。/ He declared war on the government and urged right-wingers to eliminate their opponents. 他向政府宣戰，並煽動右翼分子幹掉他們的對手。/ The radio station claimed that 87,000 'reactionaries' had been eliminated. 廣播電臺聲稱已經消滅了87,000名“反動分子”。           

<span class="vocabulary">**spill**</span> [spɪl]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb's blood 殺死某人：</span>He is prepared to spill the blood of a million people. 他準備殺掉100萬人。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**dispose**</span> [dɪˈspəʊz; 美 dɪˈspoʊz]
<span class="definition">vi. [正式] dispose of sb/sth 殺死某人：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**murder**</span> ['mə:də] 
<span class="definition">vt. 蓄意且非法殺害某人，即謀殺：</span>the murdered woman 被殺害的婦女 / He was brutally murdered. 他慘遭殺害。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**assassinate**</span> [əˈsæsɪneɪt; 美 -sən-]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 暗殺一個重要人物或名人，尤指為政治目的而暗殺：</span>The prime minister was assassinated by extremists. 首相遭極端分子暗殺。/ a plot to assassinate the president 刺殺總統的陰謀 / The plot to assassinate Martin Luther King had started long before he was actually killed. 刺殺馬丁·路德·金的計劃在實施前醞釀已久。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**massacre**</span> [ˈmæsəkə(r)]
<span class="definition">vt. 屠殺大量人口：</span>300 civilians are believed to have been massacred by the rebels. 據信有300位平民被叛亂分子殘殺。/ Troops indiscriminately massacred the defenceless population. 軍隊肆無忌憚地屠殺手無寸鐵的民眾。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**slaughter**</span> [ˈslɔ:tə(r)]
<span class="definition">vt. 宰殺動物（常為吃肉）：</span>Lack of chicken feed means that chicken farms are having to slaughter their stock. 雞飼料的缺乏意味著養雞場將不得不殺掉飼養的雞。/ The animals are all humanely slaughtered. 這些動物都是人道屠宰的。<span class="definition">vt. 屠殺大量動物：</span>Whales and dolphins are still being slaughtered for commercial gain. 仍然有人為了商業利益大肆捕殺鯨魚和海豚。/ elephants illegally slaughtered by poachers 被偷獵者非法屠殺的大象 / Thousands of birds were slaughtered for their feathers. 人們為得到羽毛而捕殺了數以千計的鳥。<span class="definition">vt. 暴力屠殺大量人口：</span>Men, women and children were systematically slaughtered and villages destroyed. 村莊被毀，男人、女人和兒童慘遭蓄意殺戮。/ Hundreds of innocent civilians were cruelly slaughtered. 數百名無辜平民慘遭殺害。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**slay**</span> [sleɪ]
<span class="definition">vt. [舊時或文學用法] 在戰爭或搏鬥中殺死…：</span>St George slew the dragon. 聖喬治殺死了那條龍。<span class="definition">vt. [尤美]（尤用於報刊）殺害某人：</span>Two passengers were slain by the hijackers. 兩名乘客遭劫機者殺害。/ Two Australian tourists were slain. 兩名澳大利亞遊客被殺。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**execute**</span> [ˈeksɪkju:t]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] ~ sb (for sth) 殺死某人，尤指依法處死：</span>He was executed for treason. 他因叛國罪被處死。/ The prisoners were executed by firing squad. 這些犯人已由行刑隊槍決。/ He was executed by lethal injection earlier today. 他於今天早些時候被注射處死。/ He was executed as a martyr to his Catholic faith. 他因為信仰天主教而被處決成了殉道者。/ innocent people who are wrongly executed 被錯誤處死的無辜的人們 / One group claimed to have executed the American hostage. 一個組織聲稱已經處決了那名美國人質。

<span class="vocabulary">**butcher**</span> ['bʊtʃə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 屠宰或宰殺動物以獲取食用肉：</span>瞭解即可 <span class="definition">2 殘忍而暴力地殺人，即屠殺，殺戮：</span>The terrorist had butchered five people in the restaurant. 該名恐怖分子在這家餐廳裡屠殺了五個人。

<span class="vocabulary">**destroy**</span> [dɪ'strɒɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 故意殺死一隻動物（通常因其有病或不被需要）：</span>to humanly destroy 人道地殺死 / The horse fell and had to be destroyed. 這匹馬倒下了，只好把它殺死。

<span class="vocabulary">**commit**</span> [kə'mɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt. commit suicide 自殺：</span>He tried to commit suicide. 他曾試圖自殺。

<span class="vocabulary">**choke**</span> [tʃəʊk] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 窒息而死；使窒息而死：</span>The girl choked to death after breathing in smoke. 那位女孩吸入煙霧後窒息而亡。/ He choked to death when a fish bone got stuck in his throat. 他因一根魚刺卡住喉嚨窒息而死。<span class="definition">vt.（透過卡住喉嚨而）使停止呼吸：</span>The men choked him with his tie. 那些人用他的領帶把他勒死了。/ He may have been choked. 他可能是被掐死的。

<span class="vocabulary">**strangle**</span> [ˈstræŋgl]
<span class="definition">vt. 對某人的喉嚨或脖子施加壓力致其死亡，如掐死、扼死、勒死：</span>to strangle sb to death 把某人掐死 / He strangled her with her own scarf. 他用她自己的圍巾把她勒死了。/ He tried to strangle a border policeman and steal his gun. 他企圖勒死一名邊防警察並偷走他的槍。/ He was almost strangled by his parachute harness straps. 他差點被自己的降落傘吊帶勒死。

<span class="vocabulary">**suffocate**</span> [ˈsʌfəkeɪt]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi.（使）窒息而死：（把…）悶死：</span>He put the pillow over her face and suffocated her. 他用枕頭捂住她的臉，把她憋死了。/ The couple were suffocated by fumes from a faulty gas fire. 由於劣質煤氣取暖器漏氣，這對夫婦窒息而死。/ They were suffocated as they slept. 他們睡覺時窒息而死。/ He either suffocated, or froze to death. 他要麼是悶死的，要麼是凍死的。/ Many dogs have suffocated in hot cars. 許多狗在熱烘烘的汽車裡給悶死了。

<span class="vocabulary">**smother**</span> [ˈsmʌðə(r)]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb (with sth) 用…把某人的臉蓋住，使其悶死：</span>He smothered the baby with a pillow. 他用枕頭把嬰兒悶死了。     

<span class="vocabulary">**drown**</span> [draʊn]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. 淹死：</span>Two children drowned after falling into the river. 兩個孩子掉進河裡淹死了。/ He had attempted to rescue the drowning man. 他曾試圖去救那個溺水的男人。/ She tried to drown herself. 她試圖投水自殺。/ He was drowned at sea. 他淹死在海里。/ They had drowned the unwanted kittens. 他們把沒人要的小貓淹死了。/ Forty-eight people have drowned after their boat capsized during a storm. 他們的船在暴風雨中傾覆後有 48 人溺亡。/ A child can drown in only a few inches of water. 兒童在只有幾英寸深的水中就可能溺亡。

<span class="vocabulary">**electrocute**</span> [ɪˈlektrəkju:t]

[常用被動式] 使某人觸電受傷或死亡：</span>The boy was electrocuted when he wandered onto a railway track. 男孩閒逛踩到鐵軌上時觸電死亡。/ Three people were electrocuted by falling power-lines. 三個人碰到落下的電纜，觸電身亡。/ He accidentally electrocuted himself. 他意外觸電身亡。<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 用電刑處死：</span>He was electrocuted for a murder committed when he was 17. 他因17歲時犯下的謀殺罪而被處以電刑。

<span class="vocabulary">**gas**</span> [ɡæs]

<span class="vocabulary">**knife**</span> [naɪf]

☀ <span class="category">**滅絕：**</span>

<span class="definition">vt. 滅絕一群人或動物裡的所有成員：</span>A huge effort was made to exterminate the rats. 滅鼠花了大力氣。/ They have a real fear that they'll be exterminated in the ongoing civil war. 他們十分害怕自己會在持續的內戰中被徹底消滅。 
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vanish**</span> [ˈvænɪʃ]
<span class="definition">vi. 如（動植物）滅絕：</span>Near the end of Devonian times, thirty percent of all animal life vanished. 在泥盆紀末期，30％的動物物種都滅絕了。/ all the species of birds that had vanished from Iowa 艾奧瓦州所有消失了的鳥類物種 / This plant is vanishing from our countryside. 這種植物在我們的鄉村逐漸消失。/ the vanishing woodlands of Europe 瀕於絕跡的歐洲林地  / The people who built this temple have long since vanished. 修建這座廟宇的人早已不在了。

<span class="vocabulary">**extinct**</span> [ɪkˈstɪŋkt]
<span class="definition">adj.（生物物種）不復存在的、滅絕的、絕種的：</span>an extinct species 已滅絕的物種 / It is 250 years since the wolf became extinct in Britain. 狼在英國已經滅絕250年了。/ the bones of extinct animals 滅絕動物的骨骼 / The species was presumed extinct. 這一物種據推定已絕種。/ Dinosaurs are, of course, long extinct. 當然，恐龍滅絕已經很長時間了。/ The numbers of these animals have been falling steadily and they are now almost extinct. 這些動物數量持續下降，現在幾近滅絕。
          
<span class="vocabulary">**extinction**</span> [ɪkˈstɪŋkʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（生物物種的）滅絕：</span>An operation is beginning to try to save a species of crocodile from extinction. 一項努力拯救一個鱷魚物種、使其免於滅絕的行動已經開始。/ Many species have been shot to the verge of extinction. 很多物種已經被獵殺到瀕臨滅絕。/ the mass extinction of the dinosaurs 恐龍的大規模滅絕 / species extinction 物種滅絕 / Modern farming methods have led to the total extinction of many species of wild flowers. 現代耕作方法導致許多種野花完全絕種了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**disappearance**</span> [-ˈpɪərəns; 美 -ˈpɪr-]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（人、動植物）滅絕：</span>Modern farming practice has led to the virtual disappearance of this bird. 現代化的耕作方式導致這種鳥瀕於滅絕。/ the disappearance of many species of plants and animals from our planet 許多動植物物種從我們這顆行星上的消失

相關詞義延伸：
· smother還可指壓制、扼殺、鎮壓某種活動或過程。

周邊詞彙補充：
· annihilate可指消滅、殲滅、徹底毀滅；
· eradicate可指徹底根除…（尤指根除壞事物）。
