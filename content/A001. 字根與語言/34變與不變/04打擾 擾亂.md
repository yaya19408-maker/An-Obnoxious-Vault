☀ <span class="category">**打擾、擾亂、騷擾的動詞、形容詞和名詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**disturb**</span> [dɪ'stə:b] 
<span class="definition">vt. 當某人正在試圖去工作（或睡覺等）時，對其進行干擾、打擾或妨礙：</span>I’m sorry to disturb you, but can I talk to you for a moment? 對不起，打擾你一下，我能跟你談一會兒嗎？/ If you get up early, try not to disturb everyone else. 如果你起得早，儘量不要打擾別人。/ Do not disturb (= a sign placed on the outside of the door of a hotel room, office, etc.) 請勿打擾（旅館房間、辦公室等門上的提示牌）/ The patient awoke early after a disturbed night. 病人折騰了一夜，很早就醒了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**disrupt**</span> [dɪsˈrʌpt]
<span class="definition">vt. 擾亂、打亂、使某事中斷（使其不能順利進行）：</span>Demonstrators succeeded in disrupting the meeting. 示威者成功地擾亂了會議。/ Bus services will be disrupted tomorrow because of the bridge closure. 明日公共汽車將因大橋停止通行而受影響。/ Anti-war protesters disrupted the debate. 反戰示威者們打斷了辯論。/ The drought has severely disrupted agricultural production. 旱災已嚴重影響了農業生產。/ The bad weather has seriously disrupted supplies of food. 惡劣的天氣嚴重影響了食物供應。/ They warned that climate change could potentially disrupt economic activity. 他們警告說氣候變化有可能擾亂經濟活動。/ The attacks are designed to disrupt plans for the elections. 這些攻擊意欲擾亂選舉計劃。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**disruption**</span> [dɪsˈrʌpʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C]（對一個事件、系統或過程的）擾亂（使其不能順利進行下去）：</span>We aim to help you move house with minimum disruption to yourself. 我們的宗旨是幫您搬家，並儘量減少它給您帶來的不便。/ disruptions to rail services 對鐵路交通的干擾 / The strike is expected to cause delays and disruption to flights from Britain. 預計罷工將導致英國航班的延誤和中斷。/ The rail strike is causing major disruptions at the country's ports. 鐵路罷工使該國港口陷入了一片混亂。/ how to organize the building work so as to cause minimum disruption 如何組織建築施工以儘量減少其干擾 / economic, family, political, social disruption 經濟混亂；家庭破裂；政治混亂；社會分裂 / the effects of family disruption during childhood 童年時期家庭破裂造成的影響 / traffic disruption 交通混亂 / The war has led to supply disruption of crude oil. 戰爭導致原油供應中斷。/ The city suffered some disruption due to a bomb scare. 因炸彈引發恐慌，城市內出現了一些混亂。/ The bombing campaign caused massive disruption to industry. 轟炸對工業造成了大規模的破壞。/ an unexpected disruption in Internet service 網際網路服務的意外中斷
           
<span class="vocabulary">**disruptive**</span> [dɪsˈrʌptɪv]
<span class="definition">adj. 引起混亂的、擾亂性的、製造混亂的（導致某事不能順利繼續下去）：</span>She had a disruptive influence on the rest of the class. 她攪擾了班上其他的學生。/ Alcohol can produce violent, disruptive behavior. 酒精會引發暴力和破壞性行為。/ The process of implementing these changes can be very disruptive to a small company. 對於小公司來說，實行這些變革可能會帶來滅頂之災。/ a highly disruptive group of students 一群非常愛搗蛋的學生 / potentially disruptive elements in society 社會上潛在的破壞分子 / to be socially disruptive 對社會有破壞性 / Long working hours are very disruptive to home life. 工作時間過長嚴重影響家庭生活。

<span class="vocabulary">**interrupt**</span> [͵ɪntə'rʌpt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. interrupt (sb/sth) (with sth) 做某事以打斷某人正在做的事，即打擾：</span>Sorry to interrupt, but there’s someone to see you. 對不起打擾一下，有人要見你。/ They were interrupted by a knock at the door. 他們被敲門聲打斷了。/ The morning’s work was constantly interrupted by phone calls. 上午的工作不斷被電話打斷。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**interruption**</span> [ˌɪntəˈrʌpʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [C, U]（一個活動或情景的）阻斷因素，干擾因素：</span>I managed to work for two hours without interruption. 我總算不受打擾地連續工作了兩小時。/ I found it hard to work with all the noise and constant interruptions. 有那麼多噪聲和不斷的干擾，我覺得很難工作下去。

<span class="vocabulary">**bother**</span> ['bɒðə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 打斷（或打擾）某人；（當對方不想與你交談時）搭話煩擾：</span>Stop bothering me when I’m working. 我工作時別來煩我。/ Let me know if he bothers you again. 他要是再攪擾你，就告訴我。/ Sorry to bother you, but there’s a call for you. 很抱歉打擾你一下，有你的電話。

<span class="vocabulary">**worry**</span> ['wʌrɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. worry sb (with sth) 煩擾、打擾或使某人不安：</span>The noise never seems to worry her. 這噪音似乎從不讓她厭煩。/ Don’t keep worrying him with a lot of silly questions. 別老用許多愚蠢的問題打擾他。/ Don’t worry the driver with unnecessary requests. 不要提些沒必要的要求來打擾駕駛員。

<span class="vocabulary">**upset**</span> [ʌp'set] 
<span class="definition">vt. 打亂或攪亂一個計劃、情況等：</span>He arrived an hour late and upset all our arrangements. 他遲到了一個小時，把我們的一切安排都打亂了。

<span class="vocabulary">**violate**</span> ['vaɪəleɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 攪擾別人的安寧等：</span>The peace of this island community has been repeatedly violated. 小島居民的安寧一再遭到攪擾。

<span class="vocabulary">**annoy**</span> [ə'nɒɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 使某人不舒服或不能放鬆，即打擾、騷擾，相當於bother：</span>A fly were beginning to annoy. 一隻蒼蠅開始惹得我心煩。
            
<span class="vocabulary">**annoyance**</span> [əˈnɔɪəns]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] 煩惱：</span>He could not conceal his annoyance at being interrupted. 他因受擾而難掩怒色。/ A look of intense annoyance crossed his face. 他臉上現出極度煩惱的表情。/ He wore an expression of pure annoyance. 他一臉十分煩惱的表情。<span class="definition">2 [C] 令人煩惱的事物或事情：</span>Inconsiderate neighbours can be more than an annoyance. 不為他人著想的鄰居會非常惹人厭。/ A leaking roof is just one of life's petty annoyances. 屋頂漏水只是生活中的一小件煩心事。

<span class="vocabulary">**harass**</span> [ˈhærəs; həˈræs]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 騷擾、侵擾某人：</span>She claims she has been sexually harassed at work. 她聲稱在工作中受到過性騷擾。/ A woman reporter complained one of them sexually harassed her in the locker room. 一名女記者投訴說，他們中有一人在更衣室裡對她進行性騷擾。/ He has complained of being harassed by the police. 他投訴受到警方侵擾。We are almost routinely harassed by the police. 警方几乎隔三差五地就找我們的麻煩。/ to racially harass 種族侵擾
                       
<span class="vocabulary">**harassment**</span> [ˈhærəsmənt;美həˈræsmənt]
<span class="definition">n. [U] 騷擾：</span>Another survey found that 51 per cent of women had experienced some form of sexual harassment in their working lives. 另一個調查發現，51%的女性在工作中曾遭遇過某種形式的性騷擾。/ racial harassment 種族侵擾 / constant, continual harassment 不斷的騷擾 / They are complaining about harassment by the police 他們投訴說遭到警方的騷擾。/ a victim of harassment 騷擾的受害者

<span class="vocabulary">**hound**</span> [haʊnd]
<span class="definition">vt. 糾纏某人（尤指為了從其身上得到…或向其問問題）：</span>Newcomers are constantly hounding them for advice. 新人總是一刻不停地纏著向他們討教。

<span class="vocabulary">**persecute**</span> [ˈpɜ:sɪkju:t; 美 ˈpɜ:rs-]
<span class="definition">vt. [常用被動式] 故意一直騷擾某人，讓其麻煩不斷：</span>Why are the media persecuting him like this? 新聞媒體為什麼總這樣揪住他不放？/ He said his first wife persecuted him with her unreasonable demands. 他說他的第一任妻子總是拿無理要求糾纏他。/ Tom was bullied by his father and persecuted by his sisters. 湯姆受到父親的欺壓和姊妹們的糾纏。     
           
<span class="vocabulary">**plague**</span> [pleɪg]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb/sth (with sth)（尤指透過要求某事物、要求別人的注意等而）給某人造成煩擾或麻煩，如糾纏：</span>Rock stars have to get used to being plagued by autograph hunters. 搖滾歌星必須習慣歌迷要求籤名的糾纏。/ I'm not going to plague you with a lot more questions, Miss Culver. 卡爾弗小姐，我不會再問更多的問題來煩你了。/ Tommy Cook had been plaguing Pinner for months. 湯米·庫克已糾纏平納好幾個月了。

<span class="vocabulary">**invade**</span> [ɪnˈveɪd]
<span class="definition">vt. 以一種煩人的方式干擾、襲擾某事物：</span>Do the press have the right to invade her privacy in this way? 新聞界有權以這種方式干擾她的私生活嗎？

<span class="vocabulary">**intrude**</span> [ɪnˈtru:d]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (on/into/upon sth) 對某人的生活、感情等的打擾、侵擾：</span>The sound of the telephone intruded into his dreams. 電話鈴聲把他從夢中擾醒了。/ legislation to stop newspapers from intruding on people's private lives 禁止報章侵犯他人私生活的立法 / Do you feel anxious when unforeseen incidents intrude on your day? 當意外事件干擾了你的生活時,你會感到焦慮嗎？/ There are times when personal feelings cannot be allowed to intrude. 有些時候是不允許夾雜個人感情的。   
           
<span class="vocabulary">**haunt**</span> [hɔ:nt]
<span class="definition">vt. 鬼魂在某地出沒或纏著某人：</span>a castle which is reputedly haunted（reputedly尤為英式用法）據說鬧鬼的一座城堡 / His ghost is said to haunt some of the rooms, banging a toy drum. 據說他的鬼魂經常敲著玩具鼓出沒在其中的一些房間裡。/ A headless rider haunts the country lanes. 一個無頭騎士常出沒於鄉間的小路上。/ He said he would come back to haunt her. 他說他會變成鬼魂回來糾纏她。<span class="definition">vt.（不愉快的事）縈繞於腦際，難以忘卻：</span>The memory of that day still haunts me. 我的腦海中常常回想起那天的情景。/ For years she was haunted by guilt. 多年來她一直感到愧疚。/ the great fear that still haunts her 她至今仍然揮之不去的巨大恐懼 / The decision to leave her children now haunts her. 拋下孩子們的決定現在總讓她不安。/ He will be haunted forever by his failed attempt to rescue the children. 未能成功解救孩子們的經歷將使他永難釋懷。/ He would always be haunted by that scene in Well Park. 他永遠無法擺脫威爾公園裡的那一幕。/ These visions continued to haunt her for many years. 多年來這些景象一直在她腦海裡縈繞。<span class="definition">vt. 長期不斷地困擾（某人）：</span>That decision came back to haunt him in later life. 那個決定使他後半生一直不安。/ The stigma of being a bankrupt is likely to haunt him for the rest of his life. 破產的恥辱可能會讓他餘生不得安寧。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**obsession**</span> [əbˈseʃn]
<span class="definition">n. [U] ~ (with sth/sb) 某人滿腦子都是某人或某事的一種非正常狀態，如著魔；困擾：</span>Her fear of flying is bordering on obsession. 她怕坐飛機幾乎到了不可救藥的地步。

<span class="vocabulary">**agitate**</span> [ˈædʒɪteɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. 使某人憤怒、焦慮或緊張：</span>The thought of them getting her possessions when she dies agitates her. 一想到他們可能會在她死後得到她的財產，她就心緒不安。

<span class="vocabulary">**broken**</span> ['brəʊkən] 
<span class="definition">adj. 常用於名詞前，被打擾的：</span>a night of broken sleep 睡得不踏實的一夜
           
<span class="vocabulary">**stuck**</span> [stʌk]
<span class="definition">adj. [不用於名詞前] ~ with sb/sth [非正式] 擺脫不掉某人或某事物的：</span>I was stuck with him for the whole journey. 一路上我一直沒能擺脫他。/ I know you think I'm stupid, but we're stuck with each other, aren't we? 我知道你認為我愚蠢，但我們倆誰都擺脫不了誰，不是嗎？/ Many people are now stuck with expensive fixed-rate mortgages. 許多人目前都有高額的定息按揭纏身。           

<span class="vocabulary">**disturbance**</span> [dɪˈstɜ:bəns; 美 -ˈstɜ:rb-]
<span class="definition">n. [U, C, usually sing.] 某人在做某事的過程中（或某事在正常進行中）受到的打擾、干擾；也可指此種打擾或干擾的行為：</span>The building work is creating constant noise, dust and disturbance. 建築施工不斷製造噪音、灰塵和干擾。/ Buildings should create minimum disturbance to the environment. 應將建築物對環境的不利影響降到最低。/ The home would cause less disturbance to local residents than a school. 養老院對當地居民造成的干擾會比學校小。/ environmental disturbances caused by roads and traffic 道路和交通對環境造成的干擾 / disturbance from noisy lorries 來自卡車噪聲的干擾 / The traffic causes serious disturbance to residents. 往來的車輛嚴重干擾居民。/ an unacceptable level of disturbance to occupiers of adjacent properties 對鄰近物業住戶構成的不可接受的干擾 / a place where you can work without disturbance 你可以不受干擾，安心工作的一個地方 / She moved the nest very carefully to prevent disturbance to the birds. 她小心翼翼地移動鳥巢，以免驚擾了鳥。/ the major disturbance of marine life caused by oil spillages 石油洩漏對海洋生物造成的重大危害 / the disturbance of the local wildlife by tourists 遊客對當地野生動物的滋擾 / The animals are very sensitive to disturbance and have never bred in captivity. 這些動物對外界的干擾很敏感，且從未被圈養過。/ a disturbance in the usual pattern of events 對平常事情發展狀況的干擾
           
<span class="vocabulary">**intrusion**</span> [ɪnˈtru:ʒn]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U, C] ~ (into/on/upon sth) 對一種情況（或人們的生活）造成侵擾的事物：</span>government, governmental, media, press intrusion 政府的侵入；媒體的侵擾 / This is a governmental intrusion on the freedom of the press. 這是政府對新聞自由的侵犯。/ I really resented his intrusion in a family matter. 我真的很厭惡他插手家庭事務。/ media intrusion into the lives of celebrities 媒體對名人生活的侵擾 / an unwarranted intrusion upon the singer's privacy 對歌手隱私的無端侵犯 / They claim the noise from the new airport is an intrusion on their lives. 他們聲稱新機場的噪音侵擾了他們的生活。/ I felt it was a grotesque intrusion into our lives. 我感覺那是對我們生活的一種干擾，讓人感到荒唐可氣。/ This was another example of press intrusion into the affairs of the royals. 這是新聞界侵擾王室成員私事的又一例項。/ intrusion into private grief 對個人不幸的攪擾 <span class="definition">2 [U, C] ~ (into/on/upon sth) 對別人之間的私密交談的打擾：</span>I hope you don't mind this intrusion, Jon. 喬恩，希望你別介意這次打擾。/ Forgive/Pardon my intrusion, but I need to speak with you urgently. 恕我冒昧，但我有急事需要和您說。

相關詞義延伸：
· harass還可指不斷攻擊敵人，即騷擾敵人；
· restless還可指沒有真正休息或睡眠的。

周邊詞彙補充：
· jam可指干擾無線電或電子訊號，使其難以被接收或被清晰聽到；
· trouble可指打擾某人（因你對其有所請求）；
· noise可指噪音。也可指干擾性的電子訊號。還可指（對重要或有用資訊起干擾作用的）垃圾資訊；
· invasion可指對某人或某事的干預、干擾；
· interfere可指干涉、干預、插手、介入（某事）。還可指妨礙一個活動或程序；
· persecute可指故意一直騷擾某人，讓其麻煩不斷；
· intrusive可指太明顯、太直接等以致於對別人造成打擾或侵擾的，如唐突的。
