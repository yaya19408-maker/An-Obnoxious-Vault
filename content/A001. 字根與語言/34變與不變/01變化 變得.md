☀ <span class="category">**變化、改變的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**change**</span> [tʃeɪndӡ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 1 變得不同，即變化，改變，如變更一個狀態、位置或方向。是一個基礎且常規的用詞，含義極廣，也極常用：</span>Jane has changed a lot since she went to college. 自從上大學以來，簡變了很多。/ Our way of life has changed dramatically. 我們的生活方式發生了巨大的變化。/ Her voice changed subtly. 她的嗓音有些微妙的變化。/ Attitudes to marriage are changing fast. 人們對婚姻的態度變化很快。<span class="definition">2 使…變得不同，即使變化，使改變：</span>Fame hasn’t really changed him. 名聲並沒有使他有絲毫改變。/ Computers have changed the way people work. 計算機改變了人們的工作方式。<span class="definition">3 change (sb/sth) (from A) to/into B（使）某人或事物從一個狀態或形式變換到另一狀態或形式：</span>Caterpillars change into butterflies. 毛蟲變成蝴蝶。/ The lights changed from red to green. 交通燈已由紅變綠。/ The place had changed out of all recognition. 這個城鎮變得讓人完全認不出來了。/ His anger changed to sadness. 他的怒火變成了悲哀。<span class="definition">4 在不同的狀態、位置或方向之間轉換、變更：</span>Leaves change colour in autumn. 樹葉在秋天改變顏色。/ The wind has changed direction. 風向已經變了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**alter**</span> [ˈɔ:ltə(r)]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi.（使）某人或某事物等變得不同：</span>He had altered so much I scarcely recognized him. 他變得我幾乎認不出來了。/ He had not altered greatly in the last ten years. 過去十年中他變化不大。/ Little had altered in the village. 村子裡幾乎沒有什麼變化。/ The landscape has been radically altered, severely damaging wildlife. 地貌徹底改變，嚴重損害了野生動物。/ They have never altered their programmes by a single day. 他們一天也未變更過他們的節目。/ Prices did not alter significantly during 2004. 2004年期間，價格沒有大的變化。/ Nothing can alter the fact that we are to blame. 錯在我們，這是無法改變的事實。/ Unemployment has come down slightly but this does not alter the fact that it is still a major problem. 失業率下降了一點兒，但這不能改變失業問題仍是一大困擾的事實。/ It doesn't alter the way I feel. 這並沒有改變我的感受。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**vary**</span> [ˈveəri; 美 ˈveri; ˈværi]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (with sth) / ~ (from sth to sth) / ~ (between A and B) 根據情況變化：</span>You are welcome to vary the diet. 我們鼓勵不時變換飲食。/ The menu varies with the season. 選單隨季節而變動。/ Prices vary according to the type of room you require. 價格隨所要求的戶型而有所變化。/ The cost of the alcohol duty varies according to the amount of wine in the bottle. 酒稅稅額隨酒瓶容量的大小而有所不同。/ Class numbers vary between 25 and 30. 班級的數目從25到30不等。/ ‘What time do you start work?’ ‘ It varies.’ “你幾點鐘開始工作？”“沒準兒。” / The speed of the car hardly varied. 汽車的速度幾乎沒有改變。/ the continuously varying intensities of natural light 自然光不斷變換的強度 / What can be found will inevitably vary according to the area under study. 因為研究範圍不同，研究結果不可避免會有所不同。/ Personal preference naturally varies. 個人的偏愛喜好自然各不相同。/ The drug is available in several forms, and dosages vary accordingly. 這種藥有幾種劑型，服用劑量也相應不同。/ Dictionaries are produced with specific markets in mind, and their contents vary accordingly. 各種詞典根據特定的目標市場編寫發行，內容也相應地有所不同。/ The availability of good medical care tends to vary inversely with the need for it. 優質醫療服務的可及度往往與需求成反比。/ Voting patterns vary geographically. 選舉模式因地域而有所不同。/ The leaf's size varies widely according to the area where it grows. 葉片的大小按生長地區的不同存在著廣泛的差異。/ Access to this information varies by social class. 獲取這一資訊的途徑因社會階層的不同而變化。/ Costs are likely to vary depending on where you live. 費用很有可能隨居住地的不同而變化。The danger of a heart attack varies with body weight. 心臟病發作的危險隨體重而變化。/ Gender roles vary across cultures. 不同文化裡的性別角色各不相同。/ Services offered vary among the main banks. 各大銀行提供的服務不盡相同。/ The rate of growth varies considerably between different industries. 不同產業的增長率存在相當大的差異。/ The doses used vary between 2 and 10mg/kg. 用量從每千克2毫克到10毫克不等。/ The situation varies slightly from country to country. 在不同的國家情況稍有不同。/ Its speed varies from 20 mph to 35 mph. 速度在每小時20英里至35英里之間變動。/ a study of how moral values vary with/over time 對道德價值觀如何隨時間而變化的研究 <span class="definition">vt. 略微改動：</span>The job enables me to vary the hours I work. 這項工作使我能夠調整工作時間。

<span class="vocabulary">**move**</span> [mu:v] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 變化，改變…，也可叫轉變，相當於shift（如改變話題；更改時間；轉變狀態、活動、觀點、態度或立場等）：</span>The government has not moved on this issue. 政府在這個問題上沒有轉變。/ Let’s move the meeting to Wednesday. 我們把開會時間改到星期三吧。/ Let’s move to another subject, Dan. 丹，我們換個話題吧。/ The Labour Party has moved to the right and become like your Democratic Party. 工黨已經轉向右翼，變得像你們民主黨了。/ Everyone thought I was crazy to go back, but I wouldn’t be moved. 人人都認為我回去是瘋了，可我是不會動搖的。<span class="definition">vt. move sb (from ...) (to ...) 變換或調動（某人的工作、班級等），改變（興趣等）。相當於transfer：</span>I’m being moved to the New York office. 我要調到紐約辦事處去。/ He moved from being a part-time tutor to being a lecturer in social history. 他從一名兼職助教轉變成了一名社會史講師。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**transfer**</span> [trænsˈfɜ:(r)]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth/sb) (from…) (to…)（使）轉移到不同的地點：</span>The film studio is transferring to Hollywood. 這家電影製片廠正遷往好萊塢。/ How can I transfer money from my bank account to his? 怎麼才能把我賬戶上的錢轉到他的賬戶上呢？/ to transfer assets abroad 向國外轉移資產 / I couldn't transfer all my credits from junior college.（尤美）我無法把我在專科學校的所有學分都轉過來。/ If I spend a semester in Madrid, will my credits transfer?（尤美）如果我在馬德里上一學期的課，我的學分能轉過來嗎？/ The patient was transferred to another hospital. 患者轉送到了另一家醫院。/ He was transferred from Weston Hospital to Frenchay. 他從韋斯頓醫院轉院到了法蘭查。/ Remove the wafers with a spoon and transfer them to a plate. 用勺子抄起薄餅，把它們轉盛到盤子裡。/ She transferred the gravy into a gravy boat. 她把肉汁倒入船形滷汁盤。<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sb) (from…) (to…)（使）某人轉移到一個新的工作崗位、學校、情況等：</span>Children usually transfer to secondary school at 11 or 12. 兒童通常在11或12歲時升讀中學。/ He transferred to UCLA after his freshman year. 他讀完大學一年級後，轉學到加利福尼亞大學洛杉磯分校。/ Ten employees are being transferred from the sales department. 十名僱員已調離銷售部。/ I was transferred to the book department. 我被調到圖書部。/ I suspect that she is going to be transferred. 我猜想她要被調走了。<span class="definition">vt.&vi.（使）轉移感情：</span>Joe had already transferred his affections from Lisa to Cleo. 喬已移情別戀，把愛意從莉薩轉移到了克利奧身上。/ I realized she'd transferred all her love from me to you. 我意識到她已把所有的愛從我身上轉移到了你身上。<span class="definition">vt.&vi. [尤英] ~ (sb) (from…) (to…) 在體育運動中可指（使）某人轉會（尤指職業足球隊）：</span>He transferred to Everton for £6 million. 他以600萬英鎊的轉會費轉到埃弗頓隊。/ He was transferred from Spurs to Arsenal for a huge fee. 他以鉅額轉會費從熱刺隊轉到阿森納隊。/ Nick Barmby who transferred from Spurs to Middlesbrough. 從熱刺隊轉會到米德爾斯伯勒隊的尼克·巴爾姆比 / He was transferred from Crystal Palace to Arsenal for £2.5 million. 他以 250 萬英鎊的身價從水晶宮隊轉入阿森納隊。<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (sth) (from sth) (to sth)（使）轉存、轉錄（資訊、音樂、想法等）：</span>You can transfer data to a disk in a few seconds. 你可以在幾秒鐘內將資料轉存到磁碟上。/ Such information is easily transferred onto microfilm. 這種資訊很容易轉存到縮微膠捲上。/ Data is easily transferred electronically. 利用電子手段傳輸資料很容易。/ systems to create film-quality computer effects and then transfer them to film 創造膠片級電腦畫質並將之轉錄到膠片的系統 <span class="definition">vt.&vi. 轉移技能：</span>Skills cannot be transferred directly from a trainer to a trainee. 技能無法直接從培訓師轉給受訓者。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**transplant**</span> [trænsˈplɑ:nt; trænz-; 美 -ˈplænt]
<span class="definition">vt. <span class="vocabulary">**budge**</span> [bʌdʒ]

<span class="vocabulary">**veer**</span> [vɪə(r); 美 vɪr]

[術語]（風）改變方向：</span>The wind had veered from the west to north-by-west. 風向已經改變，從西風轉為西北風。/ As it strengthened the wind was veering southerly. 隨著風力加強，風向也向南偏轉。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**lurch**</span> [lɜ:tʃ; 美 lɜ:rtʃ]
<span class="definition">vi.（在不同的行動、態度之間）左衝右撞，搖擺不定：</span>The state government has lurched from one budget crisis to another. 州政府陷入一個又一個的預算危機。/ The first round of multilateral trade talks has lurched between hope and despair. 首輪多邊貿易談判在希望與絕望間來回搖擺。/ Their relationship seems to lurch from one crisis to the next. 他們的關係好像坎坷不平，危機不斷。

相關詞義延伸：
· alter還可指修改衣服，使其更合身；
· distort還可指歪曲或改變事實、觀點等使其不再正確或真實。

周邊詞彙補充：
· shift可指（使）從一地移動到另一地。還可指駕駛的過程中換擋，此時為美式用法；
· mangle可指（因發音、書寫錯誤或表達不清晰而）扭曲，改變（詞語、資訊的意思）；破壞某事物（例如透過蹩腳的朗誦或演奏來糟蹋一首詩或一首樂曲）；
· contort可指扭曲，變形；
· screw可指扭曲（臉）。
