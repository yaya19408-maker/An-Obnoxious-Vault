<span class="vocabulary">**divide**</span> [dɪ'vaɪd] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. divide (sth) (up) (into sth) 分開或分成各部分；使…分開或分成各部分。相當於split up：</span>The cells began to divide rapidly. 細胞迅速分裂。/ A sentence can be divided up into meaningful segments. 一個句子可以劃分成有意義的各個部分。/ The physical benefits of exercise can be divided into three factors. 鍛鍊對身體的好處可以分成三個方面。/ Divide the pastry in half and roll out each piece. 把麵糰一分為二，把每塊都擀平。/ This report is divided broadly into two parts. 該報告大致分為兩部分。/ They were randomly divided into three groups. 他們被隨機分成三組。/ the point where the river divides in two 河流的分岔點

<span class="vocabulary">**separate**</span> ['sepəreɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. separate (sth) (from/and sth)（事物自己）劃分成不同的部分或組；將某事物劃分成不同的部分或組：</span>Stir the sauce constantly so that it does not separate. 不停地攪動沙司，免得出現分層。/ It is impossible to separate belief from emotion. 信仰和感情是分不開的。/ Separate the eggs (= separate the yolk from the white). 把蛋黃和蛋清分開。/ Make a list of points and separate them into ‘desirable’ and ‘essential’. 列出各點，把它們分成“可取的”和“絕對必要的”兩類。

<span class="vocabulary">**split**</span> [splɪt] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. split (sth) (into sth) 劃分成兩個或多個部分；使某事物劃分成兩個或多個部分：</span>She split the class into groups of four. 她按四人一組把全班分成若干小組。/ The results split neatly into two groups. 結果恰巧分成兩類。/ Split the coconut in half. 把椰子一分為二。/ He split the log into several pieces. 他把木頭劈成幾塊。

<span class="vocabulary">**section**</span> ['sekʃn]

<span class="vocabulary">**subdivide**</span> [ˈsʌbdɪvaɪd; ˌsʌbdɪˈvaɪd]
