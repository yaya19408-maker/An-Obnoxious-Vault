☀ <span class="category">**參加的動詞：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**join**</span> [dӡɒɪn] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 成為一個組織機構（或公司、俱樂部等）的一員，即參加、加入。是此義的基礎和常規用詞：</span>to join a class, to join a company, to join a political party 參加一個班級；加入一家公司；加入一個政黨 / to join the ranks of the unemployed（比喻用法）加入失業大軍 <span class="definition">vt. 1 參加到其他人正在做的事情中，或與其他人一道去某地：</span>Will you join us for lunch? 和我們一起吃午飯好嗎？/ Over 200 members of staff joined the strike. 200多名僱員參加了罷工。/ I’m sure you’ll all join me in wishing Ted and Laura a very happy marriage. 我相信大家願意與我共祝泰德和勞拉喜結良緣。/ Farmers can join together to get better prices. 農戶們可以聯合起來爭取更好的價格。/ I wish he would join in with the other children. 但願他能和其他孩子一起玩兒。<span class="definition">2 加入一群人的佇列或行列：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**participate**</span> [pɑː'tɪsɪpeɪt] 
<span class="definition">vi. [較正式] participate (in sth) 參加或參與某一活動：</span>She didn’t participate in the discussion. 她沒有參加討論。/ to participate in the running of the company 參與公司運作 / to participate in the decision-making process 參與決策過程 / to participate in the study 參與研究 / Other countries were invited to participate in the project. 其他國家也受邀來參與這一專案。

<span class="vocabulary">**enter**</span> ['entə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 無被動式，成為一個機構的一名成員（即加入機構）：</span>to enter school, to enter university 考入學校；考入大學 <span class="definition">vt.＆vi. enter (for sth) / enter sb (in/for sth) 報名參加（一場考試或比賽等）；為某人報名（讓其參加一場考試或比賽等）：</span>How many students have been entered for the exam? 有多少學生報名參加考試？/ 300 children entered the competition. 300名孩子報名參加了比賽。

<span class="vocabulary">**engage**</span> [ɪnˈgeɪdʒ]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. engage in sth / engage sb in sth（使）參與（某項活動）：</span>I have never engaged in the drug trade. 我從未參與過毒品交易。/ You can engage in croquet on the south lawn. 你可以參加南邊草坪上的槌球遊戲。/ The party is attempting to engage young voters politically. 這個政黨設法讓年輕選民參與政治。/ people who actively engage in shaping the world they live in 積極參與改造所生存的世界的人 / They constructively engage critics in debates. 他們建設性地使批評家們參與辯論。/ The army was regularly engaged in combat. 陸軍經常參與戰鬥。/ be reluctant to, be unwilling to engage 不願（讓某人）參與…

<span class="vocabulary">**involve**</span> [ɪnˈvɒlv; 美 ɪnˈvɑ:lv]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb (in sth/in doing sth) 使某人參加、加入、介入：</span>We want to involve as many people as possible in the celebrations. 我們希望參加慶典的人越多越好。/ Parents should involve themselves in their child's education. 父母應當參與孩子的教育。/ methods that actively involve students in learning 各種使學生積極學習的方法 / Noel and I do everything together, he involves me in everything. 我和諾埃爾什麼事情都一起做，他讓我參與所有的事。/ Before too long he started involving me in the more confidential aspects of the job. 不久,他便開始讓我介入工作中更機密的方面。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**indulge**</span> [ɪnˈdʌldʒ]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ in sth 參加、參與，尤指參與違法活動：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**make**</span> [meɪk] 
<span class="definition">vt. 成為（某隊成員）；得到（某職位）。是一個簡單用詞，不太常用：</span>The athletes are just happy to make the team. 那些運動員很高興加入該隊。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**initiate**</span> [ɪˈnɪʃieɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. ~ sb (into sth)（尤指在秘密儀式上）使某人加入一個群體：</span>Hundreds are initiated into the sect each year. 每年有好幾百人被接納到這個教派中。/ In many societies, young people are formally initiated into their adult roles. 在很多社會里，年輕人經過正式儀式才算成年。/ the ceremony that initiated members into the Order 吸收成員加入會社的儀式
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pledge**</span> [pledʒ]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. [美] 宣誓參加美國大學生聯誼會：</span>Do you think you'll pledge this semester? 這學期你想加入大學生聯誼會嗎？/ My brother pledged Sigma Nu. 我哥哥宣誓加入西格瑪NU聯誼會。

<span class="vocabulary">**settle**</span> ['setl] 
<span class="definition">vi. 在一個組織內安定下來：</span>The kids settled happily into their new school. 孩子們愉快地融入了他們的新學校。

<span class="vocabulary">**crash**</span> [kræʃ] 
<span class="definition">vt. [非正式] 未受邀請而參加或出席一個聚會，相當於gatecrash：</span>瞭解即可
