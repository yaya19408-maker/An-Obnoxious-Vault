<span class="vocabulary">**thread**</span> [θred] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 思想（或特徵）的線索、脈絡；（將某事物的各不同部分聯絡起來的一個）思路：</span>A common thread runs through these discussions. 這些討論都貫穿著一條共同的主線。/ narrative, plot thread 敘事線索；情節線索 / Police have not been able to find a common thread linking the victims. 警方尚未找到把受害者串聯起來的線索。/ The speaker lost his thread halfway through the talk. 講話者講到一半亂了思路。/ She struggled against all the interruptions to keep the thread of her argument. 她努力排除各種干擾以確保自己論證的思路。/ The author eventually picks up the various threads of the plot and weaves them into a masterly conclusion. 作者最終將情節的各條線索收攏到一起，編出了一個高明的結局。/ A continuous thread runs through all the versions of the legend. 一條連貫的主線貫穿著這個傳說的各種版本。/ On studying the different historians’ accounts, common threads emerge. 研究不同歷史學家的記載，會發現其中有共同的線索。

<span class="vocabulary">**journey**</span> ['dӡə:nɪ] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 人的心理從一種狀態變化（或發展）到另一種狀態的歷程，即心路歷程：</span>to describe a journey of self-discovery 描述一段自我發現的心路歷程 / inner, intellectual, spiritual journey 內心之旅；求知之旅；精神旅程 / He made the emotional journey back to the house he grew up in. 他的思緒回到了那所伴他長大的房子。
