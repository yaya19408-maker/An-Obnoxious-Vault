<span class="vocabulary">**improve**</span> [ɪm'pru:v] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 變得比以前更好；使某人或某事物比以前更好。如改進、改善。是一個常規用詞：</span>His quality of life has improved. 他的生活質量改善了。/ The doctor says she should continue to improve (= after an illness). 醫生說她還會繼續康復。/ to improve my French 提高我的法語水平 / The situation has improved dramatically. 情況已大為改善。/ Working and living conditions have generally improved. 工作和生活條件總體上有所改善。/ The weather should continue to improve. 天氣應該會繼續好轉。/ to improve efficiency 提高效率 / to improve performance 提高業績 / the improved version of the website 網站的改良版
           
<span class="vocabulary">**improvement**</span> [ɪmˈpru:vmənt]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U] ~ (in/on/to sth) 改善；改進：</span>Sales figures continue to show signs of improvement. 銷售額持續顯示出增加的跡象。/ There is still room for improvement in your work. 你的工作尚有改進的餘地。/ We expect to see further improvement over the coming year. 我們期望來年會有更進一步的改善。<span class="definition">2 [C] ~ (in/on sth) 改進之處；改善的事物：</span>the dramatic improvements in organ transplantation in recent years 近幾年器官移植方面取得的巨大進步 / an improvement in Anglo-German relations 英德關係的改善 / This is a great improvement on your previous work. 你的工作比先前有很大進步。/ improvements to the bus service 公共汽車服務的改善 / The new Prime Minister is an improvement on his predecessor. 新上任的總理比他的前任要強。/ The system we introduced in 1980 has been a great improvement. 我們 1980 年引進的系統有了很大改進。
                     
<span class="vocabulary">**perfect**</span> [pə'fekt]
<span class="definition">vt. 使某事物完善或完美，或盡己所能來變得完善或完美：</span>As a musician, she has spent years perfecting her technique. 身為音樂家，她多年來在技藝上精益求精。

<span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (up) 增強（信心）；鼓舞（士氣）：</span>to bolster sb's confidence, to bolster sb's courage, to bolster sb's morale 增加某人的信心；增加某人的勇氣；提高某人計程車氣 <span class="definition">vt. ~ sth (up) 鞏固（地位）；加強：</span>to bolster regime 鞏固政權 / Falling interest rates may help to bolster up the economy. 利率下降可能有助於啟用經濟。/ Britain is free to adopt policies to bolster its economy. 英國可以自由制定政策以振興經濟。

相關詞義延伸：
· upgrade還可指提拔某人（到一個更高的職位上）。也可指提高技能；
· fortify還可指構築防禦工事，尤指築城防禦。

周邊詞彙補充：
· heighten可指（使）（情感）加強，提高，增加。

<span class="vocabulary">**reform**</span> [rɪ'fɔ:m] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 改進自己或別人的行為：</span>She thought she could reform him. 她覺得她可以使他洗心革面。/ He has promised to reform. 他許諾要改過自新。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**ameliorate**</span> [əˈmi:liəreɪt]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 使某事物變得更好，即改善、改進、改良：</span>to ameliorate effect 改善效果 / Steps have been taken to ameliorate the situation. 已經採取措施以改善局面。/ Nothing can be done to ameliorate the situation. 做什麼也無法使情況好轉。/ He expected me to do something to ameliorate his depression. 他希望我做點什麼好讓他不那麼沮喪。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**burnish**</span> [ˈbɜ:nɪʃ; 美 ˈbɜ:rnɪʃ]
<span class="definition">vt. 改善形象：</span>The European Parliament badly needs a president who can burnish its image. 歐洲議會急需一位能改善其聲望的主席。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**thaw**</span> [θɔ:]
<span class="definition">vt.&vi. ~ (out)（使）（關係、氣氛等）回暖，緩和，改善，即變得更友好且更不受拘束：</span>At least this second meeting had helped to thaw the atmosphere. 這第二次會議至少起到了緩和氣氛的作用。/ It took up to Christmas for political relations to thaw. 直到聖誕節政治關係才有所改善。/ Relations between the two countries thawed a little after the talks. 談判後兩國關係緩和了些。<span class="definition">n. [sing.] ~ (in sth)（敵對國家之間）關係的緩和：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**refined**</span> [rɪˈfaɪnd]
<span class="definition">adj.（機器或過程）經過完善的、經過提升的：</span>a more refined engine 更加完善的引擎 / This technique is becoming more refined and more acceptable all the time. 這種技術變得越來越完善，越來越受人歡迎。/ refined version 精煉版本
           
<span class="vocabulary">**fortified**</span> ['fɔ:tɪfaɪd;美'fɔtəfaɪd]
<span class="definition">adj. 加強的：</span>The town was heavily fortified against attack 這個鎮大大增強了防禦攻擊的能力。

相關詞義延伸：
· build還可指（壓力、速度等）逐漸增大；
· refined還可指（一種物質）提純的，即精煉的、精製的。

周邊詞彙補充：
· contribute可指增加、改進、新增（到某事物）；
· rise可指（風）開始颳得更猛烈、（情感）在內心中增強；
· reinforcements可指被派到某地的（軍隊的）援軍、（警隊的）增援警力。
