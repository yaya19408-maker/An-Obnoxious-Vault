☀ <span class="category">**清晰 模糊：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**clear**</span> [klɪə] 
<span class="definition">adj. 容易聽到的：</span>The voice on the phone was clear and strong. 電話裡的聲音清晰洪亮。
          
<span class="vocabulary">**distinct**</span> [dɪˈstɪŋkt]
<span class="definition">adj. 容易被聽到的，如清楚的、清晰的：</span>His voice was quiet but every word was distinct. 他說話聲音不大，但字字清晰。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**clarity**</span> [ˈklærəti]
<span class="definition">n. [U]（聲音的）清晰，清楚：</span>This remarkable technology provides far greater clarity than conventional x-rays. 這項卓越技術的清晰度比常規的X光高得多。/ the clarity of sound on a CD 鐳射唱片的清晰音質

<span class="vocabulary">**dull**</span> [dʌl] 
<span class="definition">adj.（聲音）不清晰的；低沉的：</span>The gates shut behind him with a dull thud. 他出去後門砰的一聲悶響關上了。<span class="definition">vt.＆vi.（聲音）變得低沉；使（聲音）變得低沉：</span>The endless rain seemed to dull all sound. 連綿不斷的陰雨似乎使所有的聲音都變得沉悶起來。

<span class="vocabulary">**thick**</span> [θɪk] 
<span class="definition">adj. thick (with sth)（嗓音）深沉且不像平常一樣清楚的，可以叫做嘶啞的（尤指由於疾病或情感異常所致）：</span>His voice was thick with emotion. 他激動得話都說不清楚。
