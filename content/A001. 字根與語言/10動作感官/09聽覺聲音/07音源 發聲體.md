☀ <span class="category">**音源 發聲體：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**speaker**</span> ['spi:kə] 
<span class="definition">n. [C]（收音機、電腦或音樂裝置上）聲音出來的地方，即揚聲器，喇叭：</span>left speaker 左側喇叭 / front speaker 前置揚聲器 / built-in stereo speakers 內建立體聲揚聲器 / He put me on the speakerphone. 他跟我通話時開了擴音。
                      
<span class="vocabulary">**loudspeaker**</span> [ˌlaʊdˈspi:kə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C]（公共場合的）揚聲器、擴音器、喇叭：</span>Their names were called over the loudspeaker. 喇叭裡叫著他們的名字。/ A woman was addressing the crowd through a loudspeaker. 一位女工正透過喇叭向人群講話。/ Loudspeakers broadcast the football results. 喇叭廣播了足球賽結果。/ Loudspeakers blared militant songs. 喇叭裡高聲放著激昂的歌曲。/ loudspeaker announcement 喇叭裡傳來的通知 / loudspeaker system 擴音系統 / Christmas songs blared from loudspeakers. 響亮的聖誕歌曲聲從喇叭裡傳了出來。/ We heard the news over the loudspeaker. 我們透過擴音器聽到了新聞。<span class="definition">2 [C] [過時]（收音機或樂器的）揚聲器、喇叭：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**microphone**</span> [ˈmaɪkrəfəʊn; 美 -foʊn]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 麥克風、話筒：</span>to speak into the microphone 對著麥克風講話 / boom, cordless, headset, radio, underwater, wireless microphone 懸吊式麥克風；無繩麥克風；耳機麥克風；無線電麥克風；水下麥克風；無線麥克風 / condenser microphone 電容式麥克風 / There's a place to plug in an external microphone. 有個插入外接麥克風的地方。/ built-in microphone 內建式麥克風 / directional, omnidirectional microphone 定向傳聲器；全向傳聲器 / concealed, hidden microphone 隱藏的麥克風；竊聽器 / live, open microphone 接通的麥克風；開著的麥克風 / Unfortunately we were near an open microphone and all his colleagues heard what we said. 不幸的是，我們緊靠著一隻開著的麥克風，他所有的同事都聽到了我們所說的一切。/ Their remarks were picked up by the hidden microphones. 他們的話被暗藏的麥克風傳了出去。/ He was wearing a hidden microphone. 他身上有竊聽器。/ The microphone picks up the surrounding sounds. 話筒採集到周圍的聲音。/ microphone input（尤美）, microphone jack 麥克風輸入口；麥克風插孔 / microphone stand 麥克風架 / microphone headset 麥克風耳機 / Alex Jones is back behind the microphone for a new series. 亞歷克斯·瓊斯又回到麥克風後為一個新的系列節目錄音。/ Interviewees are placed in front of the microphone and grilled. 參加面試的人被安排在麥克風前接受提問。

<span class="vocabulary">**earphones**</span> [ˈɪəfəʊnz; 美 ˈɪrfoʊnz]
<span class="definition">n. [pl.] earphones可指耳機：</span>stereo earphones 立體聲耳機 / MP3 player earphones MP3音樂播放器耳機 / a pair/set of earphones 一副耳機 / She was listening to her MP3 player through earphones. 她正戴著耳機聽mp3。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**bug**</span> [bʌg]
<span class="definition">n. [C] [非正式]（微型）竊聽器：</span>There was a bug on the phone. 電話裝有竊聽器。
