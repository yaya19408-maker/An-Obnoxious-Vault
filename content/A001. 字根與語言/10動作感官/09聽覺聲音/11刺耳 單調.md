<span class="vocabulary">**flat**</span> [flæt] 
<span class="definition">adj.（嗓音）未表現出任何情感的；音調沒什麼太大變化的。即平淡的；單調的：</span>Her voice was flat and expressionless. 她的聲音平淡而呆板。

<span class="vocabulary">**rough**</span> [rʌf] 
<span class="definition">adj.（聽起來）令人不快或不舒服的：</span>a rough voice 刺耳的聲音
           
<span class="vocabulary">**harsh**</span> [hɑ:ʃ; 美 hɑ:rʃ]
<span class="definition">adj. 刺耳的、難聽的：</span>It's a pity she has such a loud harsh voice. 可惜她的嗓門太大太難聽了。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**brittle**</span> [ˈbrɪtl]
<span class="definition">adj.（聲音）尖利刺耳的：</span>a brittle laugh 尖利的笑聲

<span class="vocabulary">**sharpen**</span> ['ʃɑːpən] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 聲音變得尖銳刺耳；使聲音變得尖銳刺耳：</span>瞭解即可
