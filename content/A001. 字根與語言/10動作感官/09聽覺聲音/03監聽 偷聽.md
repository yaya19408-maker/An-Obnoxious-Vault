☀ <span class="category">**監聽 偷聽：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**tap**</span> [tæp] 
<span class="definition">n. [C] 將電話安裝竊聽器以進行竊聽的行為：</span>a phone tap 電話竊聽
           
<span class="vocabulary">**eavesdrop**</span> [ˈi:vzdrɒp; 美 -drɑ:p]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (on sb/sth) 偷聽、竊聽別人說話：</span>We caught him eavesdropping outside the window. 我們撞見他正在窗外偷聽。/ The housemaid eavesdropped from behind the kitchen door. 女傭在廚房門後偷聽。/ The government illegally eavesdropped on his telephone conversations. 政府非法竊聽了他的電話。

<span class="vocabulary">**monitor**</span> ['mɒnɪtə] 
<span class="definition">vt. 監聽（電話、外國廣播等）以獲取資訊：</span>瞭解即可  
           
<span class="vocabulary">**bug**</span> [bʌg]
<span class="definition">vt. 竊聽（談話）：</span>I found out my phone was bugged. 我發現我的電話被竊聽了。/ They were bugging his telephone conversations. 他們在竊聽他的電話交談。/ a bugging device 竊聽器

<span class="vocabulary">**through**</span> [θru:] 
<span class="definition">prep. see, hear, etc. through sth 隔著某個物體（或物質）聽到：</span>to hear the conversation through the wall 隔著牆聽別人交談
