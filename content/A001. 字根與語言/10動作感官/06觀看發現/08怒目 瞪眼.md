☀ <span class="category">**怒目 瞪眼：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**glare**</span> [ɡleə] 
<span class="definition">vi. glare (at sb/sth) 憤怒地看…，即怒目而視，相當於glower：</span>He glared at me silently. 他默默地怒視著我。/ I looked at her and she glared angrily back. 我看著她，她生氣地回瞪了我一眼。<span class="definition">n. [C] 長久憤怒的目光：</span>to give sb a hostile glare 含敵意地瞪著某人 / She fixed her questioner with an icy glare. 她冷冷地盯著審問者。/ She shot a warning glare at her companion. 她用眼神警告她的同伴。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**goggle**</span> [ˈgɒgl; 美 ˈgɑ:gl]
<span class="definition">vi. [過時，非正式] ~ (at sb/sth)（尤指由於驚奇而）瞪大眼睛看：</span>She goggled at me. 她瞪大眼睛望著我。/ He goggled in bewilderment. 他困惑地瞪著眼睛。           

<span class="vocabulary">**scowl**</span> [skaʊl]
<span class="definition">vi. ~ (at sb/sth) 怒視某人或某事物：</span>She scowled at the two men as they entered the room. 兩個男人進屋時，她怒目而視。/ He scowled at her before stalking out of the room. 他瞪了她一眼，而後大步走出房間。<span class="definition">n. [C] 憤怒的眼神或表情：</span>He looked up at me with a scowl. 他抬起頭來，皺著眉頭看著我。
                   
<span class="vocabulary">**blaze**</span> [bleɪz]
<span class="definition">vi. [正式] ~ (with sth) 怒視：</span>Her eyes were blazing with fury. 她的雙眼燃燒著怒火。/ He got to his feet and his dark eyes were blazing with anger. 他站起身來，烏黑的眼睛裡噴射著怒火。/ Eva stood up and indignation blazed in her eyes. 伊娃站了起來，眼睛裡燃燒著憤怒。

<span class="vocabulary">**eyeball**</span> [ˈaɪbɔ:l]
<span class="definition">vt. [非正式] 非常直接（有時不禮貌或不友好）地看…，如瞪著：</span>瞭解即可

周邊詞彙補充：
· pop可指（眼睛因驚訝或興奮而）突然完全睜大。
