☀ <span class="category">**觀察 端詳：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**watch**</span> [wɒtʃ] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. 看著…一段時間，注意發生的事情，即注視，觀看，觀察。是此義的常規用詞：</span>to watch television, to watch a football game, to watch a show 看電視；看足球比賽；看錶演 / I spent hours patiently watching the eagles. 我花了數小時耐心地觀察鷹。/ We watched for any sign of change in the weather. 我們留意著天氣變化的任何跡象。/ Watch what I do, then you try. 你注意看我的動作，然後試著做。/ She watched helplessly as her husband was dragged away. 她眼睜睜地看著丈夫被拖走了。/ ‘Would you like to play?’ ‘No thanks--I’ll just watch.’ “你想玩嗎？”“不啦，謝謝。我就看看。”

<span class="vocabulary">**observe**</span> [əb'zə:v] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. [正式]（尤指為了解…而）仔細觀察：</span>to observe the planet 觀測那顆行星 / to observe the patient 觀察那個病人 / to observe his behaviour 觀察他的行為 / to observe the class 觀摩課堂 / You can learn a lot by simply observing. 僅僅透過觀察就能學到很多東西。/ He was being observed from the window. 別人正透過窗戶觀察他。/ She stood there, quietly observing the domestic scene. 她站在那兒默默地看著家中的這一幕。

<span class="vocabulary">**observation**</span> [ˌɒbzəˈveɪʃn; 美 ˌɑ:bzərˈv-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [U]（尤指為了學習而進行的）仔細觀察：</span>Most information was collected by direct observation of the animals' behaviour. 大部分資訊都是透過直接觀察動物的行為收集到的。/ careful observation of the movement of the planets 對行星運動的仔細觀測 / results based on scientific observations 根據科學觀測得來的結果 / systematic observation 系統的觀察 / casual observation 漫不經心的觀察 / empirical observation 經驗式的觀察 / visual observation 目測 / clinical observation 臨床觀察 / astronomical observation 天文觀測 / observation field 實地觀測 / There's a lot to be learned from simple observation. 透過簡單觀察就能學到很多東西。/ In hospital she'll be under observation all the time. 醫院會對她二十四小時密切觀察。/ She was admitted to the hospital for observation. 她被留院觀察。/ The police have had him under observation for several weeks. 警方已監視他數週了。/ The suspect is being kept under observation (= watched closely by the police). 嫌疑人正受到監視。/ We managed to escape observation (= we were not seen). 我們設法避開了人們的注意。/ The survey was based on direct observation of over 500 schools. 這項調查以對500多所學校進行的直接觀察為基礎。/ an artist with acute powers of observation 具有敏銳觀察力的藝術家 / observation post, observation tower (= a place from where sb, especially an enemy, can be watched) 瞭望哨；瞭望塔 <span class="definition">2 [C] 透過仔細觀察而得出的結果：</span>This book contains observations about the causes of addictions. 該書包括有關成癮原因的觀察發現。

<span class="vocabulary">**consider**</span> [kən'sɪdə] 
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 仔細看…，如端詳：</span>He stood there, considering the painting. 他站在那裡，凝視著那幅畫。

<span class="vocabulary">**scan**</span> [skæn] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. scan sth (for sth) 仔細察看每一個部分（常為尋找某個事物或人）：</span>to scan her face 端詳她的臉 / to scan the room 仔細檢視那個房間 / to scan the crowd for his daughter 在人群中尋找他的女兒 / My eyes scanned across the horizon. 我朝天邊放眼望去。

<span class="vocabulary">**study**</span> ['stʌdɪ] 
<span class="definition">vt. 仔細觀看…以找出（或發現）…，即審視，端詳：</span>to study his face 端詳他的臉 / to study the menu 仔細看選單 / to study the photograph 仔細觀察照片 / She picked up the letter and studied it carefully. 她拿起信仔細看起來。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**scrutinize**</span> [ˈskru:tənaɪz]
<span class="definition">vt. [英式也用scrutinise] 仔細檢視：</span>She leaned forward to scrutinize their faces. 她探身向前，端詳他們的面容。/ Her purpose was to scrutinize his features to see if he was an honest man. 她的目的是透過仔細觀察他的相貌以判斷他是否誠實。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**peer**</span> [pɪə(r); 美 pɪr]
<span class="definition">vi.（尤指看不清時）仔細看：</span>We peered into the shadows. 我們往陰暗處仔細瞧。He went to the window and peered out. 他走到窗前仔細往外瞧。/ A face was peering down at him. 一張臉俯視下來打量著他。/ She peered around the corner. 她躲在拐角處張望。/ His pale blue eyes peered anxiously at Vic. 他淡藍色的眼睛焦慮地注視著維克。/ She kept peering over her shoulder. 她不停地回頭看。/ He peered closely at the photograph. 他聚精會神地端詳著相片。/ I had been peering at a computer print-out that made no sense at all. 我一直盯著看一張毫無意義的計算機列印稿。/ He watched the Customs official peer into the driver's window. 他看見海關官員透過駕駛座車窗仔細地往裡看。
           
<span class="vocabulary">**appraise**</span> [əˈpreɪz]
<span class="definition">vt. [正式] 打量：</span>an appraising glance, an appraising look 打量的一瞥；打量的目光 / She stepped back to appraise her workmanship. 她後退一步，鑑賞她作品的藝術性。/ His eyes coolly appraised the young woman before him. 他雙眼冷靜地打量著面前的年輕女子。/ Gloria slid an appraising eye over the rest of the rack before reaching for the dress. 格洛麗亞以審視的眼光向貨架上其餘的衣服掃了一遍，才伸手去拿那件連衣裙。

<span class="vocabulary">**read**</span> [ri:d] 
<span class="definition">vt. 1 read sb’s mind/thoughts 看透某人的心思或想法：</span>He read my mind. 他看透了我的心思。<span class="definition">2 read sb’s lips 讀唇，即觀察某人的嘴唇來理解其所表達的意思：</span>瞭解即可
           
<span class="vocabulary">**pore**</span> [pɔ:(r)]
<span class="definition">vi. pore over sth 仔細打量、審視：</span>瞭解即可

<span class="vocabulary">**drop**</span> [drɒp] 
<span class="definition">vt.＆vi. [正式] 眼睛向下看：</span>Her eyes dropped to her lap. 她雙目低垂，看著自己的腿。

☀ <span class="category">**觀察者：**</span>
<span class="vocabulary">**viewer**</span> [ˈvju:ə(r)]
<span class="definition">n. [C] 觀看者；觀察者：</span>the relationship between the art object and the viewer 藝術品與其觀賞者之間的關係 / Some of her art is intended to shock the viewer. 她的一些藝術旨在震動觀賞者。/ viewers of the current political scene 當前政治局面的觀察家
           
<span class="vocabulary">**observer**</span> [əbˈzɜ:və(r); 美 -ˈzɜ:rv-]
<span class="definition">n. 1 [C] 觀察者；目擊者：</span>Observers say the woman pulled a knife out of the bunch of flowers and stabbed him in the neck. 目擊者說那名女子從花束中拔出一把刀來，扎進了他的頸部。/ According to observers, the plane exploded shortly after take-off. 據目擊者說，飛機起飛後不久就爆炸了。/ To the casual observer (= somebody who does not pay much attention), the system appears confusing. 乍看起來，這個系統好像條理不清。/ A casual observer would have taken them to be three men out for an evening stroll. 乍一看的話，還以為他們三人是晚上出來散步的。<span class="definition">2 [C]（會議、課程等的）觀察員、旁聽者（其只傾聽而不參與討論）：</span>A team of British officials were sent as observers to the conference. 一組英國官員被派去做大會觀察員。<span class="definition">3 [C] 被派往某地去監督重大事件或局勢（尤指為確保事情順利進行，或為了向人們傳達權威機構的指示）的觀察員：</span>The president suggested that a UN observer should attend the conference. 總統建議聯合國應派一名觀察員參加本次會議。<span class="definition">4 [C] 對某些事件、形勢等進行研究的專家（這些專家經常研究局勢並對此發表評論，且預測事態的發展趨勢），如觀察家；觀察員；評論員：</span>Observers say the events of the weekend seem to have increased support for the opposition. 觀察家說週末發生的事件似乎為反對派贏得了更多支援。/ Political observers believe that a new cabinet may be formed shortly. 政治觀察家認為新內閣可能很快就會組建。/ a royal observer 王室觀察家

相關詞義延伸：
· watch還可指注視或觀察…，以防止潛在的危險或問題，或能夠適時採取行動。還可指站崗、放哨。

周邊詞彙補充：
· focus可指（眼睛）調節焦距（或使其調節焦距）以清晰地看到…，即對焦、看。
