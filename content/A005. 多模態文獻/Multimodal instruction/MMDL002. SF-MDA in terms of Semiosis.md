The SF-MDA approach has, for example, led to the study of ==the functionality of language, visual images== and mathematical symbolism in mathematical discourse, and theorization of how linguistic, visual and mathematical symbolic choices combine to construct reality in ways which extend beyond what is possible using linguistic resources alone (O’Halloran, 2005, 2007a, 2007b). In addition, SF-MDA provides a transdisciplinary bridge across traditionally distinct fields of study. For example, the SF-MDA approach to mathematical discourse involves mathematics, linguistics, semiotics, studies of visualization and mathematics education. ==The SF-MDA approach holds much promise for conceptualizing and analysing **multimodal semiosis**.==

**Intersemiosis 符號間過程; 符際過程:**
Theorization of intersemiosis is central for the investigation of the processes which results in a <u>convergence of meaning</u> (<u>co-contextualizing</u> relations of parallelism) and/or <u>divergence of meaning</u> (<u>re-contextualizing</u> relations of dissonance) in multimodal texts 
(O’Halloran, 1999, 2007b; Royce, 1998, 2002; Thibault, 2000).

The modelling of intersemiosis has taken various forms. For example, Cheong (2004) conceptualizes the semantic expansions of ideational meaning in print advertisements in terms of the *Bi-Directional Investment of Meaning (雙向意義投資: 即視-文語義擴展)* across visual and linguistic components. The resulting *Contextualization Propensity (CP) (語境化傾向: 信息的實指性程度，與解釋空間成反比)* of the linguistic and visual selections opens up an *Interpretative Space* (IS) in which the *Semantic Effervescence (SE) (語義活躍程度)* can be assessed. Cheong’s (2004) approach foregrounds the importance of the contextualizing relations which open up a space of interpretance.
> Bi-Directional Investment of Meaning $\left\{ \begin{array}{rcl} {\mbox{Contextualization Propensity{}}}\\{\mbox{Interpretative Space}} \end{array}\right.$ (互成反比)
> $\longrightarrow$ 評估Semantic Effervescence (SE) 語義活躍程度
> **Intersemiosis即多符號之間的互動和意義創造**

**Intrasemiosis 符號內過程; 內符際性**
(1) <u>Intrasemiosis concerns the meaning within different semiotic resources</u> (O’Halloran 2004), in order to engender a new meaning or set of meanings in the context of a connectedness and interweaving between modes (Lim 2004: 239; Kress et al. 2001:25).
(2) In multimodality research, <u>semiotic effects limited to one semiotic mode are some-times called "intrasemiosis",</u> whereas relations between modes are termed "intersemiosis" or "intermodality" (cf. Forceville & Urios-Aparisi 2009;Wildfeuer 2012;Wildfeuer , 2013.)
(3)  The texual metafunction is described as a “structural configuration…in relation to the total communication process - expressing its operational relevance” (Language 46). Thus, textual metafuntion analyses <u>the internal coherence and cohesion of the different elements of a text (‘intrasemiosis’)</u> and how the text itself makes sense with its relevant environment forming up a meaningful whole (with the previous mentioned modes – ‘intersemiosis’) (Kress and Van Leeuwen 176).
>**Intrasemiosis即限於單一符號系統內意義的連貫和凝聚, 與intersemiosis成互補關係**
>(一符號多意義 & 多符號一意義)

**Transsemiosis 跨符號過程**
Transsemiosis is <u>a semiosis that operates when a traveler travels from one semiosphere to another.</u> We have to suppose that there is a traveler whose behavior is semiotically interesting. Trans-semiosis is based on the following three concepts: first, <u>semiosis, which consists of three things: the sign or representamen, the object, and the interpretant</u>; second, <u>the interpreter who operates the semiosis</u>; and third, <u>the semiosphere the interpreter lives in, a space characterized as having a center and a periphery.</u> This paper defends the thesis that the semiosphere determines the semiosis and the trans-semiosis transforms the semiosphere.

**Metasemiosis 元符號過程; 後設符號過程**
$\in$ metasemiotics 元符號學; 後設符號學 $\left\{ \begin{array}{rcl} {\mbox{Metasemantics 後設語義學{}}}\\{\mbox{Metapragmatics 後設語用學}} \end{array}\right.$
> *metasign 元符號:*
> 元符号是 “符号之符号” 或 “再现之再现”。使用元符号是人类所独有的能力，在人类世界中普遍存在，但是在部分情况下，元符号性可能更加明显。元符号性陡升，既会推动符号增值，亦会抹除原初意义，然而其结果都往往是符号与事物相分离，符号自身推动符号演进。
> 符号 (sign) 所关涉的过程是意识对事物的媒介化再现 (representation)，而元符号 (metasign) 所关涉的过程， 则是对这种再现的再现 (re-representation)。如果某一一一符号所指称的不是个事物， 而是另外一个符号，那么它就是元符号。(赵毅衡，2017, p.271)

**Semiotization 符號化**
$\longrightarrow$ 衍生intrasemiosis (純推論，缺證)
(1) 符号化，即对感知进行意义解释，是人对付经验的基本方式。符号化能赋予世界给我们的感知以意义。只要符号化，哪怕看来完全没有意义，也可能被解释出意义。在符号化问题上，起始有三步：一是思维主体确定某物“有某功能” (functionality)，二是归类为“用于什么目的” (purposes)，三是由此命名“叫做什么” (nominalization)。
符号化取决于人的解释，这个人不仅是社会的人，同时还是个别的人。他的解释行为不仅受制于社会文化，也受制于此时此刻他个人的主观意识。在符号解释中，社会文化的规定性，经常有让位于解释者意志的时候。

**Resemiotization 再符號化** (意義的符號轉移，意義不變，變的是符號/模態)
$\longrightarrow$ 衍生intersemiosis, intermodality, intermediality (純推論，缺證)
(1) Resemiotization is meant to provide the analytical means for (a) <u>tracing how semiotics are translated from one into the other</u> as social processes unfold, as well as for (b) asking <u>why these semiotics (rather than others) are mobilized</u> to do certain things at certain times.
(2) Resemiotization is about how meaning making shifts from context to context, from practice to practice, or from one stage of a practice to the next.
(3) 多模态的研究旨在说明人们在表达意义时对不同符号特性的掌握和运用，以及对不同符号体系之间相互关系的了解。因此<u>一种模态被另一种模态替代实际上是一种符号资源转入另一种符号资源的过程</u>，也就是‘再符号化’。再符号化的任务是分析：（1）一种符号资源是如何随着社会进程转成另一种符号资源的？（2）为什么是这些符号资源而不是另一些符号资源在某个时期被动用来表达某种意义。如果能把这两个问题理清，便不难回答多元符号学中的非任意性问题，也有助于我们正确把握对不同模态的运用。 [(resemiotization)](http://www.semiotics.net.cn/index.php/view/index/isms/3446)
(4) 多模态小品的出现也回答了‘重新符号化’所提出的课题，即人们<u>从一种模态发展为更多地使用另一种模态</u>，这与社会进程是分不开的。

**Desemiotization 去符號化** (即**Materialization 物化**)
(1) 物符号化反过来的过程就是“去符号化”（desemiotization），或称“物化”，即让符号载体失去意义，降解为使用物。如: "买一套《莎士比亚全集》放书架做装饰"/"被符号化的雨花石，有可能重新成为一颗只有使用性的石子，例如房子易主成为丢弃物:/"一位女生悠然自得地用新版百元人民币点烟"等
