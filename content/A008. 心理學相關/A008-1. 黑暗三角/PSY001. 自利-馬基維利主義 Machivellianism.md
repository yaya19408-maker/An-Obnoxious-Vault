# Machiavellianism
![[The_Dark_Triad.png]]
馬基維利主義象徵高傲、操縱他人傾向、冷酷、為達目的不擇手段、低同理心、高目標取向。可能由家庭或原生創傷引起，並拒絕一切外在幫助和關懷，是一種極端崇尚自我實現的人格表現。
Extension: [villaincore](https://aesthetics.fandom.com/wiki/Villaincore#Spotify) 反派核: 用自己的力量取得自己所想要的，並且做自己的主導者。
## **Psychology**
- A psychological trait forming the dark triad with narcissism and psychopathy. (黑暗三角: 馬基維利主義、自戀人格、心理病態)
	- in contrast, the light triad scale (2018) qualifies empathy, compassion, and altruism. (光明三角: 同理心、憐憫心、利他主義)
		- I think people are mostly good. (信人性本善)
		- I enjoy listening to people from all walks of life. (喜了解他人)
		- When I talk to people, I am rarely thinking about what I want from them. (別無所圖)
- It often correlates with several immoral behaviors such as **deception, lying, cheating, theft, the paucity of empathy concerning interpersonal relationships, indifference, and self-interest.**
- **Five Factor Model, the Big Five:**
	- antagonism: manipulativeness, cynicism, selfishness, callousness, and arrogance.
		- 操縱他人，憤世嫉俗，自私自利
	- planfulness: deliberation and orderliness.
	- agency 主體性: 
		- achievement-striving, assertiveness, self-confidence, emotional invulnerability, activity, and competence.
		- 指的是成功和個體化的動機
		- communion 共融性
			- 指與他人融合併支持團體利益的動機。
			- 馬基維利主義 $\in$ 高主體性和低共融性
				- self-construal 自我識解
				- 擁有馬基維利主義精神的人不僅希望實現目標，而且可以為了目的而不惜犧牲他人，或至少不考慮他人的利益。
- possible causes:
	- **environmental**:
		- childhood maltreatment and neglect, social reinforcement of manipulative behaviors from an early age, and poor family functioning.
			- 童年不當對待、忽視、從小就社會強制或惡意操縱行為取向和家庭的不作為
		- be influenced by the shared-environment (i.e. sibling groups) (跟兄弟姊妹爭糖吃)
		- Láng, András; Lénárd, Kata (2015). "The relation between memories of childhood psychological maltreatment and Machiavellianism". Personality and Individual Differences. 77: 81–85. doi: 10.1016/j.paid.2014.12.054
			- punishment specifically led to the "emergence of deceitful and exploitative interpersonal tactics." The authors of the study concluded that these results "might give further support for the idea that Machiavellian personality traits are possible strategic responses to childhood adversities".
				- 因馬基維利主義做出的戰略性反應映射了童年的弱勢待遇。
		- Láng, András (2015). "Machiavellianism and early maladaptive schemas in adolescents". Personality and Individual Differences. 87: 162–165. doi: 10.1016/j.paid.2015.07.039
			- the traits of Machiavellianism to be a response to early maladaptive schemas (EMS), essentially coping mechanisms for emotional deprivation, mistrust, abuse, and caregiver rejection.
				- 馬基維利主義的特徵象徵著早期適應不良基模 (EMS，結合十八種基模的群組，童年和青少年時期的核心需求未滿足、或有害童年經驗造成的結果所形成的基模。)，從而引發幾個主要機制如情感剝奪、不信任感、虐待和不接受他人照料。
	- **genetic**:
		- The co-occurrence of alexithymia and Machiavellianism was most heavily influenced by genetic factors, and to a lesser but significant extent by non-shared environmental factors."
			- 述情障礙和馬基維利主義很常經基因途徑共同出現，並且或多或少因非共享環境而外顯。
- **hot and cold empathy 冷熱同理心**
	- cold empathy, or cognitive empathy: 了解他人觀點或精神狀態
		- the understanding of how others might react to one's actions or a certain event
	- hot empathy, or emotional empathy: 以適當情感回應他人情感狀態
		-  the emotional reaction others might have to the emotions of another person.
	- 馬基維利主義較少(甚至缺乏)情緒同理心 (難共情)，而較多認知同理心 (所以才看起來很冷漠)。也有研究說兩者都缺乏 (沒有罪惡感或悔恨，帶著他人事物與我何干的心態)。更有甚者發現馬基維利主義對他人負面情緒很敏銳，從而利用這點操控他人
- **unemotionality**
	- One of the primary traits of machiavellianism is a detached, unemotional attitude and lack of affect in regards to others.
		- 沒有情緒波瀾，不怎麼親近他人
	-  "Machiavellians do not inhabit the realm of emotion in the same way as others, yet they use it to manipulate others. They do not experience feelings, empathy, or morality in normative ways. yet they are consummate manipulators and deceivers precisely by playing upon these sentiments and convictions in others. Thus they induce in others the guilt they hardly feel themselves."
		- 不會想到他人的感受、同理心或道德觀，並精準控制他人。
	- High Machs may experience intense worries when things do not go their way, but are unable to express it. 
		- 高馬基維利主義者在不如願時可能會很憂心忡忡，但不會表達。(alexithymia 述情障礙: 不會想到/表達自己/他人情緒)
	- High Machs have a cool and rational character and a proself orientation and showed that their lack of group orientation may account for their low cooperation in social dilemmas.
		- 高馬基維利主義者有激進自我實現(proself)取向，在社交場合經常體現低合作意願和向心力。
- **neuroticism/depression**
	- neuroticism: 神經質和馬基維利主義之間關聯說法不一
		- Studies using measures of the Big Five personality traits have variously found positive or no correlation between Machiavellianism and Neuroticism.
	- depression: 憂鬱症和馬基維利主義呈負相關 (MACH越高，憂鬱越低)
		- One study even noted that depressed males "were significantly less Machiavellian than were nondepressed males". They then estimate that "depression in males, then, may be of a more self-aggressive, self-destructive nature".
			- 罹患憂鬱症的男性更傾向於自我毀滅，而非自我實現
- **motivation (sex, achievement, sociality, winning at any cost)**
	- 馬基維利主義尺度上的人的動機與冷淡的自私和純粹的工具性有關，而那些在特質上的人則被認為是通過重複的方式追求他們的動機（例如：性、成就和社交）。
	- 對高馬赫數動機的最新研究發現，他們將金錢、權力和競爭放在首位，專注於不懈的成就和不惜一切代價的勝利。而不是將社區建設、自我愛心和家庭承諾放在首位。
## **Neuroscience**
1. 神經影像學研究突出了背外側前額葉皮層(dorsallateral prefrontal cortex, dlPFC)對馬基維利主義傾向的影響。但是，關於左和右dlPFC對馬基雅維主義的因果作用的知識仍然不清楚。
2. 在大量腦損傷患者（N = 129）和非腦損傷對照參與者（N = 37）中測量了總體馬基維利傾向，以及兩個子量表（即馬基維利觀點和馬基維利戰術）。確定是否可以通過腦損傷來改變馬基維利主義的傾向。
3. 根據dlPFC損傷將患者分為四組：左dlPFC損傷，右dlPFC損傷，非dlPFC損傷和健康對照組，發現左dlPFC損傷總體上增加了馬基維利主義，尤其是馬基維利主義的觀點（觀點），但並未調節行為（戰術）。至關重要的是，在控制了一般和情緒智力，語言能力，同理心和精神病理學後，左dlPFC損傷預示了更高的馬基維利主義。
4. 確立了左dlPFC在調節馬基維利主義觀點中的因果作用，並表明人們可以持有馬基維利主義觀點而不必認可馬基維利主義策略。
