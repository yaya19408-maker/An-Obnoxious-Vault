<center>Traumagenic</center>
<center>創傷型</center>

<p align="justify">Traumagenic is a system or thoughtform generated from a traumatic event. It includes neuroses like DID, OSDD, p-DID. In addition to aforementioned conditions, the healthy multiplicity after the recovery is defined as traumagenic as well.</p>
<p align="justify">創傷型是指經創傷事件而生成的系統或意識體，並涵蓋了解離性身分疾患 (Dissociative Identity Disorder, DID) & 其他特定解離性疾患 (Other Specified Dissociative Disorder, OSDD) & 部分解離性身分疾患 (Partial Dissociative Identity Disorder, p-DID)。除了以上情形外，病癒的健康多意識體系統也算是創傷型。</p>
