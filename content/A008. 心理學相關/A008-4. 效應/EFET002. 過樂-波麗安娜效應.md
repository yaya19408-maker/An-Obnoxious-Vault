# 波麗安娜效應 Pollyannaism
# **積極偏誤 Positivity Bias**
- *Where*: the 1913 novel Pollyanna (少女波麗安娜)
- *Who* wrote it: Eleanor H. Porter
- *Why*: 裏頭一個女孩玩"快樂遊戲" (在每種情境下找到快樂事物)
- *When*: 1969
- *Who named it*: Boucher 和 Osgood
- *What*: the tendency for people to remember pleasant items more accurately than unpleasant ones.
---
Plot: an orphaned little girl, who is sent to live with her Aunt Polly, who is known for being stiff, strict, and proper. When thrown into this environment, Pollyanna seeks to keep and spread her optimism to others. This beloved literary character's story shares the message that despite how hard things may seem, a sunny disposition can turn anyone and anything around. 
一位孤兒被送去跟她冥頑不靈又待人苛刻的波麗姨母同住。進入這個新環境後，無論情勢多險峻，波麗安娜 (也就是孤兒) 總是散播正能量，做人們的小太陽。
