# **Villaincore**
## **反派核**
---
#core 
Villaincore is an aesthetic which encompasses being yourself and doing everything in your power to get what you want, by any means necessary. 
Villaincore is sometimes associated to trauma, as victims can use the idea of becoming someone so powerful they would be feared (and often getting revenge) through their bad experiences as a coping mechanism.
反派核是圍繞著"靠自己的力量、不擇手段地取得心中所想"的美學。
有時和創傷有所連結 (見 [[Traumacore]])，兩者都能見證受害者以強大自我為中心思想並運用自己的痛苦經驗達成震攝對方和復仇。

		- chain and chaincuffs 鎖鏈和鏈銬

	- 權力
		- crosses 十字
		- crowns 皇冠
	- test tubes and scientific/medical equipment 試管和科學/醫療設備
	- animals
		- darkness
			- crows
			- ravens
			- snakes
			- cats
			- wolves
		- royalty and battle

		- weaponry (daggers, swords, and revolvers (左輪))
- Colors: red and black & white (mainly), deep grays, purple, green, gold, silver, neon colors
