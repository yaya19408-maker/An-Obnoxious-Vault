# Research Question
- ask questions in particular way, often about people's **perspectives** or **experiences**
- inquiry: what is intriguing and interesting in a particular area, phenomenon, or problem, $\neq$ questions
	- RQ: experiential, perspectivistic
	- inquiry: phenomenological
- task-based learning (inquiry)
	- $\rightarrow$ communicative competence/performance (surface structure)
- research process and research cycle
	- study design
		- what data is required? (textual data)
		- how to collect the data?
		- how to analyze the data?
	- research cycle
		- the ideal way in which things should work (plan)
		- iterative rather than linear
	- research process
		- the way things actually happen
- evidence/trustworthiness
	- triangulation
	- peer-review with the professionals
	- interview-analysis-interview
