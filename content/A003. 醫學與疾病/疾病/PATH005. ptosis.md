Ptosis/blepharoptosis
		Neurogenic ptosis 神經性眼瞼下垂
			Inclusion: third cranial nerve palsy 第三對腦神經麻痺
					Symptom: 
					1. Extropia 外斜視
					2. Nystagmus 眼球震顫
					3. [[Ipsilateral mydriasis]] 同側瞳孔放大
				Horner's syndrome 霍納氏症候群
					Symptom: 
					1. Miosis/myosis 瞳孔縮小
					2. Interal carotid dissection 內頸動脈剝離
				Myasthenia gravis 重症肌無力
					Symptom:
					1. Unilateral/bilateral ptosis 單邊/雙邊眼瞼下垂
					2.  [[Diplopia]] 複視
					3. Thymoma 胸腺瘤
					Treatment:
					1. Ice packing 冰敷
					2. Acetylcholine receptor antibody (AchRAb) 
					血清乙醯膽鹼受體抗體 (上升) (homeostasis)
		Myogenic ptosis 肌肉性眼瞼下垂
			[[Pathology]]:
			1. Dysgenesis of the levator muscle 先天提眼瞼肌發育不良
			2. Muscular dystrophy 肌肉失養症
			3. Chronic progressive external ophthalmoplegia (CPEO)
			慢性進行性外眼肌麻痺
		Aponeurosis/involutional ptosis 腱膜性/退化性眼瞼下垂
		Mechanical ptosis 機械性眼瞼下垂
			[[Pathology]]: 
			1. [[Chalazion]] (上眼皮)霰粒腫: 油脂阻塞毛孔
			2. Edema 水腫
