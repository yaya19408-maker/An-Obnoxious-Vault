# VINDICATE
- **vascular 血管**
	- stroke 中風, TIA (transient ischemic attack, 短暫性腦缺血發作
	- SAH (subarachnoid hemorrhage，蜘蛛網膜下腔出血)
- **inflammatory/infectious 發炎; 炎症/感染**
	- meningitis 腦膜炎
	- encephalitis 腦炎
	- sepsis 敗血症
- **neoplastic 腫瘤; 贅生**
	- primary brain tumor 原發性腦瘤
	- metastasis 癌細胞轉移
- **degenerative/deficiency 退化; 退行性/缺乏**
	- Alzheimer's disease 阿茲海默症
	- Hungtington's disease (HD) 亨丁頓舞蹈症
	- dementia 失智
- **idiopathic/iatrogenic/intoxication 病因不明/醫源性/中毒**
	- narcotics 藥物成癮
	- alcohol intoxication or withdrawal 酒精成癮或戒斷
- **congenital 先天**
	- epilepsy 癲癇 (postictal state 發作後狀態)
- **autoimmune/allegic 自體免疫/過敏**
	- CNS lupus 紅斑性狼瘡感染中樞神經系統
	- neurosarcoidosis 神經性類肉瘤病; 神經性結節病
- **traumatic 創傷**
	- traumatic brain injury 創傷性腦損傷
	- traumatic epidural or subdural hematoma 創傷性硬脊膜外或硬脊膜下血腫
- **endocrine 內分泌**
	- hypoglycemia 低血糖
	- hypo/hyperthyroidism 甲狀腺功能低下; 甲亢
	- hypo/hypernatremia 低/高鈉血症
	- hypercalcemia 高鈣血症
	- hepatic/uremic encephalopathy 肝性/尿毒性腦病變
