# **e-Picture Book Creating (e-PBC) in the Children's Literature Classrooms**
#paper
## **Literature Review**
### **Picture Book Creating (PBC)/English Picture Book Creating (EPBC) & pcture books learning**
1. Wang (2008) investigated forty-four night-program AFL majors’ perceptions of the assignment, PBC. Data collection included a five-point questionnaire, the instructor’s journal entries, the students’ picture books, the students’ reflection papers, and the class blog postings ( http://e3.nfu.edu.tw/ecampus3/learn/ ). The results of the study showed that the PBC helped them learn about picture books (M=4.27) and develop their critical thinking ability (M=4.57) 
	1. 在2008年教44位應外夜校生，使用5度李克特、教師日誌、繪本作品、反思學習單和部落格貼文(e3的作業紀錄)。
	2. 繪本創作促進對繪本的認知和批判思考 (M=4.57)
2. Wang, Chiu, and Wu (2010) explored how fifty-two day-program college students’ related two assignments, EPBC and English Puppet Show DVD Creating (EPSDC), to learning about picture books and critical thinking boost. The sources of data collection included a 6-point-scaled questionnaire, the instructor’s journal entries, the students’ reflection papers, the class blog postings (http://e3.nfu.edu.tw/ecampus3/learn/), the students’ picture books and their English puppet show DVDs. The results of the study showed that the student participants tended to agree with the effectiveness of the two assignments on helping them learn about picture books and increasing critical thinking ability from the EPBC (M=4.92 and 5.14 respectively) and from the EPSDC (M= 4.84 and 5.14 respectively). 
	1. 王、邱 (我們不認識)、吳在2010年教52位應外日間部學生，派發2份作業 (英語繪本創作 & 英語布袋戲DVD創作) 來探討繪本和批判思考成效。
	2. 6度李克特、教師日誌、反思學習單、e3作業、繪本作品和DVD
	3. 認可英語繪本創作和布袋戲提升繪本認知和批判思考 (M=4.92, 5.14) (M=4.84, 5.14)
3. Moreover, following discussion is one previous study which indicated one e-work assignment could help learners to increase their critical thinking ability and technology literacy. Wang, Ting, Lin, Yen, and Su (2013) looked into sixty-one Taiwanese college students’ responses to a place-based-learning (PBL)-oriented e-Work Creating activity. Data sources included a questionnaire that included a space for free comments, the students’ e-works, the students’ reflection papers, class observations, and follow-up e-mail interviews. The results of the study indicated the e-work could help the students develop their critical thinking ability, learn to use technology (e.g., “InAlbum, PowerDirector, camera, and video recorder” (p. 60)), and learn about the place (i.e. Yunlin County), among others.  
	1. 有鑑於前面研究提升了批判思考和科技素養，Wang, Ting, Lin, Yen, and Su (2013) 對61位大學生探討地方社區學習(PBL)導向電子創作活動。
	2. 提出意見的問卷、電子作品、反思學習單、觀課、e-mail訪談
	3. 電子作品可以發展批判思考、科技運用和認識地方特色
### **Critical Thinking**
- The critical thinking process requires students to examine their own and other people’s values and/or opinions carefully and to express their thoughts, feelings, ideas, and concerns to others clearly (Royster, 1996).
	- 學生要想批判思考，就得先審視自己和他人價值觀與(或)意見並清楚表達出來自己所想。
- Harris and Hodges (1995) point out that critical thinking is a logical thinking process about “the characteristic of creativity and criticism in literature and other arts” (p. 50). 
	- 批判思考就是在文學和其他形式藝術中經由批判其特色和創意的邏輯思考過程。
-  Facione and Facione (1994) define critical thinking as a process in which learners actively evaluate and analyze information in concepts, synthesize and apply concepts, and conclude the final options. 
	- 批判思考就是針對學習者在評估並分析資訊後的概念合併和運用，並且最後得出最佳選項。
- Chance (1986) views critical learners as those who need to gain skills about how to analyze facts, how to generate and organize ideas, how to defend and compare opinions, how to draw inferences and evaluate arguments, and finally how to achieve the best practice solution.
	- 批判學習就是學著分析事實、生成並組織想法、維護和比較意見、避免干擾和評估爭論，以及找出最佳解。
- Critical-literacy perspectives (Boyd & Howe, 2006) empower students to become aware of the basic elements of social justice, and eventually offer students an opportunity to their and other people’s positions within society. 
	- 批判素養觀點讓學生能看清社會正義其中的基本要素，以及在社會之中找到歸屬。
-  Wang, Wang, Wu and Kuo (2005/2006) specify critical thinking as a process in which students learn how to use their previous knowledge and experience to originate their works by brainstorming new ideas, respecting different opinions, negotiating with opponents, communicating and cooperating with team-members.
	- Peter認為真正的批判思考是要會運用先備知識和經驗、搭配brainstorming、尊重他人意見、和反對聲音調解與團隊合作。
- In his study on educational bibliotheraphy, Wang (2006) also refers to critical thinking as “an ability to respond considerately to diverse points of view, understand beyond the surface text, learn the way to position human beings including themselves, input their minds with the meaning of mutual help and possibly carry out the spirit of social justice in the way of empathy” (p. 18). 
	- 在教育書目療法之中，Peter說道批判思考是體面回覆不同觀點的聲音、要深度意會文字、換位思考、站在彼此角度，以同理心找出社會正義精神。
- In the “Conceptualizing Critical Reading and Thinking” paper by Pugh (Wang, 2004), she particularizes empathy as the ability to vicariously experience other people’s cognition, emotion, and action. 
	- 同理心被特別定義為間接感受到他人認知、情緒和行為。
- Oxman-Michelli (1991) defines creativity in rules of critical thinking as the involvement of the continuous interplay among the production, connection, appraisal, alternation, and integration of ideas. 
	- 批判思考之中的創造力參與了生產、連結、評鑑、替換和概念整合的連續交互作用。
- Sharman, Garry, and Beuke (2004) specify imagination as the ability to generate more perceptual detail after comprehending the learning and reading materials.  
	- 想像力會增添更多讀後的感官細節。
-  Fowler (2009) argues that the best definition of critical thinking should be made by the person who needs to utilize critical thinking concept as a process to solve problems in a particular situation. 
	- 最好形容批判思考的話應該是"在需要解決特定難題時的能力"。
