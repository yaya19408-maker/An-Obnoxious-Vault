- 制定問題需要大量學科知識和研究方法
- 審視問題，感受將想法制定成值得研究的問題的複雜性
- 制定問題就是辨識方向
- 找到"about"
- 剛開始會困惑很正常，重要的是先搞清楚研究問題，後續都好說
- 研究問題的來源
	- 4Ps
		- the required info
			- **people** (individual or collective)
				- individuals
				- organizations
				- groups
				- communities
		- info that you need to collect to find answers to your service research questions
			- **problems** (what you want to figure out)
				- issues
				- situations
				- associations
				- needs
				- population composition
				- profiles
			- **programs**
				- contents
				- structure
				- outcomes
				- attributes
				- satisfaction
				- consumers
				- providers
			- **phenomena**
				- cause and effect
				- relationships
				- phenomenology
- 根據興趣、研究大小 (資源和時間的需求量是否在掌控範圍之內)、概念的測量方式 (尤其是量化)、專業程度、關聯性、數據是否可用和道德議題來選題
