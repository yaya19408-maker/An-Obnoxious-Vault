***Diastereomer 非對映異構物***
- Definition:
>$\because$ Non-mirror image, non-identical stereoisomers
$\therefore$ presented when plural stereoisomers are partly different on configurations
>如果只有立體中心 (Stereocenter)不同，那該對diastereomer即為 [[Epimerism]]
- 分為:
	- [[Conformerism]] 構象異構
	- Cis-Trans isomerism 順反異構
	- [[Epimerism]] 差向異構/表異構
	- [[Atropisomerism]] 阻轉異構
- - - 
- Diastereoselectivity 非對映異構選擇性
	- 有機反應中對單/多個非對映異構物的偏好
>Diastereoselectivity is the preference for the formation of 1 or multiple diastereomer over the other in an organic reaction. 
- - -
- Syn and Anti 順式/反式(面向) $\neq$ Cis and Trans 順反
	- 用於區別開鏈分子中 ${sp}^{3}$ 混成鍵的成對非對映異構物之面向，只用在之字形鏈投射 (zig-zag projection)的情況中。
>When the single bond between the two centres is free to rotate, cis/trans descriptors become invalid. Two widely accepted prefixes used to distinguish diastereomers on sp³-hybridised bonds in an open-chain molecule are **syn** and **anti**.
- - -
- Erythro and Threo 赤式/蘇式 (限醣類 (saccharide)情況)
	- Erythro 赤式: 兩同取代基排在同一側，但zig-zag則會呈現兩側
		- 源自four-carbon aldoses erythrose 四碳醛醣赤藻醣
	- Threo 蘇式: 兩同取代基排在兩端 (antipodes)
		- 源自threose 蘇糖
- Examples: Threonine 蘇胺酸 & Allothreonine 別蘇胺酸
