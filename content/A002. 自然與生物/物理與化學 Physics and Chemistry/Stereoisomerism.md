Stereoisomerism/Spatial isomerism 立體異構/空間異構 
>same molecular formula and sequence of bonded atoms (constitution), but differ in the three-dimensional orientations of their atoms in space.
>同分子式，同原子鍵組成，但不同排列 $\leftrightarrow$ Structural isomer 結構異構物
- - -
- Stereoselectivity 立體異構選擇性
	- 因親電分子接近立體中心反應而產生扭轉和空間相互作用而產生的性質
>attributed to torsional and steric interactions in the [[Stereocenter]] resulting from electrophiles approaching the [[Stereocenter]] in reaction.
- [[Stereocenter]]/Stereogenic center 立體 (源)中心
	- 連結多個原子的中心點
