- Atropisomerism/Steric optically active isomerism 阻轉異構/位阻光活性異構
	- Definition:
		- 因凡得瓦形變導致的單鍵阻轉能量落差
	- Atropimerization 阻轉異構化

>Energy differences of a single bond due to hindered rotation (阻轉) by steric strain/van der Waals strain (位阻形變/凡得瓦形變)
- - - 
- Variations:
	- Atropodiastereomer 阻轉非對映異構物
		- $\longrightarrow$ Conformers' substituents contain axial chirality
		- 構型異構物的取代基具軸掌性
	- Atropoenantiomer 阻轉對映異構物
		- $\longrightarrow$ Conformers' substituents contain axial achirality
		- 構型異構物的取代基具非軸掌性
- - - 
- Separation:
	- 可透過手性拆分 (Chiral resolution) 完成以達到阻轉異構選擇性(Atroposelectivity)
