- Hybridization 混成
	- Mix atomic orbitals to form new hybridized orbitals
		- Mentioned with VSEPR theory to explain the shape of molecules
			- Valence Shell Electron-Pair Repulsion theory 
				- 價層電子對互斥論

- $\huge{Orbitals}$
	- **S** orbital (Sharp spectrum 銳系光譜) s軌域
	- **P** orbital (Principal spectrum 主系光譜) p軌域
	- **D** orbital (Diffuse spectrum 漫系光譜) d軌域
	- **F** orbital (Fundamental spectrum 基系光譜) f軌域
- $\huge{Quantum}$ $\huge{Numbers}$
	- 主量子數 Principal quantum number ($\large{n}$)
		- ***Orbital distance/energy***
		- $\normalsize{n}$ $\uparrow$ electron energy $\uparrow$ orbital volume $\uparrow$ 
		- Shell 殼層
			- K $\longrightarrow$ n=1
			- L $\longrightarrow$ n=2
			- M $\longrightarrow$ n=3
			- N $\longrightarrow$ n=4
	- 角動量量子數 Angular momentum quantum number ($\large{l}$)
		- ***Orbital shapes/types***
			- $\huge{l=0,1,2,3...(n-1)}$
				- 第n層有n種軌域
	- 磁量子數 Magnetic quantum number (m)
		- ***Orbital amount***
			- 各 $\large{l}$ 值的數量加總起來
		- $\longrightarrow$ $\huge{n=1}$ $\longrightarrow$ $\huge{l=0(s), m=0\rightarrow\mbox{2x0+1=1}}$$\longrightarrow$ 1 type, 1 orbital
		- $\longrightarrow$ $\huge{n=2}$ $\left\{ \begin{array}{rcl} {l=0(s)}\\{l=1(p), m=0, \pm{1}\rightarrow\mbox{2x1+1=3}} \end{array}\right.$ $\longrightarrow$ 2 types, 4 orbitals
		- $\longrightarrow$ $\huge{n=3}$ $\left\{ \begin{array}{rcl} {l=0(s), m=0}\\{l=1(p)}\\{l=2(d), m=0, \pm{1}, \pm{2}\rightarrow\mbox{2x2+1=5}} \end{array}\right.$ $\longrightarrow$ 3 types, 9 orbitals
		- $\longrightarrow$ $\huge{n=4}$ $\left\{ \begin{array}{rcl} {l=0(s)}\\{l=1(p)}\\{l=2(d)}\\{l=3(f), m=0, \pm{1}, \pm{2}, \pm{3} (7)} \end{array}\right.$ $\longrightarrow$ 4 types, 16 orbitals
		- $\longrightarrow$ $\left\{ \begin{array}{rcl} \huge{l=n-1}\\\huge{m=2l+1}\end{array}\right.$
	- 自旋量子數 Spin quantum number (s)
		- ***Orbital spin direction/electron numbers***
		- To determine containable electron number (2 electrons only) per orbital
			- $\huge{+\frac{1}{2}\rightarrow\mbox{clockwise}}$ 
			- $\huge{-\frac{1}{2}\rightarrow\mbox{counter-clockwise}}$ 
- $\huge{\mbox{Every n (主量子數) contains:}}$ 
	- $\large{\mbox{n types of orbitals}}$
	- $\large{n ^{2}}$ $\large{\mbox{個 orbitals}}$
	- $\large{\mbox{2x}n^{2}個electron}$

- $\huge{\mbox{3d orbital=}}$
	- $\large{\mbox{(3, 2, +2, -}\frac{1}{2})\longrightarrow\mbox{n=3, l=2, m=5, s=clockwise}}$
		- 3 represents the $3^{rd}$ layer of shell $\rightarrow$ n=3, while d represents l=3-1=2, m=0, $\pm{1}$, $\pm{2}\mbox{(+2 is searchable in the orbital)}$
	- $\large{\mbox{(3, 2, -1, +}\frac{1}{2})\longrightarrow\mbox{n=3, l=2, m=5, s=counterclockwise}}$
		- 3 represents the $3^{rd}$ layer of shell $\rightarrow$ n=3, while d represents l=3-1=2, m=0, $\pm{1}$, $\pm{2}\mbox{(-1 is searchable in the orbital)}$
- $\huge{\mbox{9p orbital=}}$
	- $\large{\mbox{n=9, l=8, m=17 (0, }\pm{1}\mbox{ to }\pm{8})}$  
		- ***Once n $\geq$ 2 (1p), p is possible to exist***
			- **1s, 2p, 3d, 4f, 5g, 6h, 7i...**
