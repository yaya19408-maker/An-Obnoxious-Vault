- 空間反演對稱性/宇稱 (Spatiality)
	- Parity is to examine the physical consistency of an inverted object through the mirror
	- 宇稱即驗證鏡內翻轉的客體之物理性質是否一致
		- E.g. Newton's 2nd Law
			- $\overrightarrow{F} = m\overrightarrow{a}$   $\underrightarrow{P-transformation}$    (-F)=m (-a)
				- If corresponds, Newton 2nd Law is P-symmetry
	- To attest the consistency of ==levorotaion and dextrorotaion== (左旋及右旋粒子是否一致)(See also: [[Chirality]] 手性)
- C and P-symmetry are interrelated 
	- (CP, Charge conjugation parity 電荷共軛宇稱)
	- 只要空間 (P)不對稱，性質 (C)也會不對稱
		- 正反物質 $\longrightarrow$ 自旋相反
			-  ${左旋}_{有弱力} \underleftrightarrow{Symmetric} {右旋反}_{有弱力}$ $\rightarrow{CP對稱}$
		- 左旋 $_{有弱力}$ $\underrightarrow{P-transformation}$ 右旋 $_{無弱力}$ $\rightarrow{P不對稱}$
		- 左旋 $_{有弱力}$ $\underrightarrow{C-transformation}$ 左旋反 $_{無弱力}$ $\rightarrow{C不對稱}$
- $\longrightarrow$ 左旋&右旋反shares CP (Cross-symmetric)
- CP violation CP破壞 $\longrightarrow$ [[T-symmetry]] violation
	- Neutral particle oscillation
	- $正粒子^{+}$    $\underleftrightarrow{弱力}$    $反粒子^{-}$ 
		- Time: $t_{1}$ ≠ $t_{2}$
			- Matter ≠ anti-matter
			- Matter amount is much more than anti-matter amount
