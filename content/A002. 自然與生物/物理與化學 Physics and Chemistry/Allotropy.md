- **Allotropy 同素異形性**
	- Allotrope 同素異形體
	- $\in $ [[Polymorphism]] 多態
		- [Polytypism](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/0898122188902477) *(crystallography)* 多型
	- *Definition*
		- Allotropy *(property)* 同素異形性
			- Different structures shared with the same elements (DERIVATIONS) (ELEMENTS ONLY)
		- Allotrope *(product)* 同素異形體
			- *Carbon (C)*
				- Charcoal/Coal 木炭/煤炭
				- Graphite 石墨
					- Hexagonal lattice 六角晶體
				- Graphene 石墨烯
					- Single sheet of graphite 單層石墨
				- Diamond 鑽石
					- The cubic lattice of tetrahedra 四面立方晶體
				- Fullerene 富勒烯/Buckyball 巴基球 (Buckminsterfullerene)
					- Sphere/Tube/ [[ellipsoid 橢球]] 球狀/管狀/橢球狀 
				- Lonsdaleite 藍絲黛爾石/朗斯德碳
					- Hexagonal diamond 六方金剛石
				- Amorphous carbon 無定形碳
					- Uncertain form, hyperreactive
				- Carbyne/Linear acetylenic carbon 卡拜/直鏈乙炔碳/線性碳 $-(C≡ C)_{n}$
				- Carbon nanotubes 奈米碳管
					- Cylindrial nanostructure  圓柱狀奈米結構
				- [Schwarzites](https://phys.org/news/2018-08-schwarzites-long-sought-carbon-graphene-fullerene.html) (first made in 2018) 碳籠
					- Topologically minimal surfaces
				- [Cyclocarbon](https://www.cyclocarbon.com/) 環碳/ Cyclo[18]carbon 環18碳
				- Glassy carbon 玻璃碳
				- [Superdense carbon allotropes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Superdense_carbon_allotropes) 超密集碳同素異形體
		- Nanoallotropes 奈米同素異形體 (Allotropes of nanomaterials)
			- Nanoporous 多奈米孔洞
			- 同化學組成，異奈米尺寸 (nanoscale, 即原子大小不同)
			- Applied in ultra-small electronic device manufacturing
		- *Etymology*
			- Allos ἄλλος (others) 他; 異
			- Tropos τρόπος (form) 形
		- *Confusion*
			- [[Allotrophy or Xenophagy]] 異源吞噬
