- 見 [[Chirality]]
- Chirality centers 手性中心 = Asymmetric center 非對稱中心 (a type of [[Stereocenter]])
	- $\notin$ axial, planar, and helical chirality 軸面，平面和螺旋手性
	- $\notin$ rotoreflection symmetries (${S}_{2n}$) 瑕旋轉/旋光反射
	- $\notin$ reflection (${C}_{S}$) 平面光反射

- Enantiopure drugs 純對映異構藥品
	- Enantiopurity 對映異構純度
		- 已證實某些含高純度對映異構之成分能提升治療效力 (efficacy)
		- 如:
			- Propoxyphene 普帕西芬 (Analgesic 止痛藥 $\rightarrow$ Opioid 鴉片) 
				- (現已禁用，$\because$ Cardiotoxicity/Cardiac Arrhythmicity risk)
				- Propoxyphene-Acetaminophen 普帕西芬-乙醯胺酚
				- Dextropropoxyphene 右普帕西芬 (analgesic)
					- 藥名Darvon (anadrome of Novrad) $\rightarrow$ extend patentability (專利性)
				- Levoropropoxyphene 左普帕西芬 (antitussive 止咳藥)
					- 藥名Novrad (anadrome of Darvon) $\rightarrow$ extend patentability (專利性)
- Enantioselective preparation 對映異構選擇性製備
	- Precursor 前軀體
	- Chiral catalyst 掌性催化劑 $\rightarrow$ Chiral resolution 手性拆分
		- Or called enantiomeric resolution 對映異構拆分
	- Kinetic resolution 動能拆分
	- Asymmetric synthesis 非對稱合成
		- Or called enantioselective synthesis 對映異構選擇性合成
- Enantiomer self-disproportionation 對映異構體自身不成比例
	- [Enantiomer_self-disproportionation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enantiomer_self-disproportionation)
	- Enantioenriched 對映異構飽和
- Quasi-enantiomer 準對映異構體
