- 細分
	- **[[C-symmetry]]** (Charge conjugation symmetry) C對稱 (電荷共軛對稱)
		- ***(Property Equivalence)***
	- **[[P-symmetry]]** (Parity symmetry/transformation/inversion) 宇稱/空間反演對稱
		- ***(Spatiality Equivalence)***
	- **[[T-symmetry]]** (Time reversal symmetry) 時間反演對稱
		- ***(Temporality Equivalence)***

- ***Symmetry***
	- Rotate a square by $90^{o}$
		- Discrete rotational symmetry 離散旋轉對稱性
	- Rotate a square by $132^{o}$
		- ≠ Continuous rotational symmetry 不滿足連續旋轉對稱性
	- A circle ＝Discrete continuous rotational symmetry
