- Allotrophy/Xenophagy 異源吞噬
	- (Entomology) categorically eating change 昆蟲飲食改變
	- Xenophagy → extreme & sudden, allotrophy →  gradual
	- (Microbiology) a cell directs autophagy (自體吞噬，吃自己) against pathogens (病原體) to activate antiviral defenses/immune response. 
		- 細胞吃自己來防毒，進而對抗病原體
	- (Ecology) the property of eutrophication (優養化)
