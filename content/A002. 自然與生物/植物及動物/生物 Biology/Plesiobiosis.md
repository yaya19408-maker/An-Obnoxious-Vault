- Plesiobiosis, the most basic form of interspecific associations in ants, denotes occasional or regular nesting of heterospecific colonies of certain species pairs in close proximity to each other without biological interdependence. 

- Plesiobionts differ from each other both in morphology and in behaviour (e.g., in their foraging strategies), and at least one of the plesiobiotic pair is a submissive species.
- - - 
- Plesiobiosis 異種共棲 $\in$ 物種間 (interspecific)或不同物種 (heterospecific)不仰賴其他生物 (without biological interdependence)的緊密群落
- Plesiobiont 異種共棲體 $\in$ 樣貌 (morphology)和覓食策略 (foraging strategies)相異的物種，且一對異種共棲體中至少有一對階層較低 (at least one of the plesiobiotic pair is a submissive species)
