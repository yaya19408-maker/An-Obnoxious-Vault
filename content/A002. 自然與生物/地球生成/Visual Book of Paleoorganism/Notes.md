**[[Primordial soup]] 原始湯；太古濃湯**
	生命起源的假說
	(the hypothesis of life forms)
**Coacervation 聚滴現象**
	[[coacervate]] 聚滴
		溶液中的溶質聚集在一起並形成獨立液滴或凝膠相
		(Solutes aggregate into individual droplets or gel phase in the solution.)
**Flocculation 絮凝**
	液體中的細小懸浮顆粒相互吸引並聚集成顆粒團塊或絮質
	(The tiny suspending particles (colloid) attract and aggregate together into flocculent mass.)
**(Submarine) hydrothermal vent 海底熱泉**
	產出地熱的海底裂縫
	(a fissure on the seafloor/ at the bottom of the ocean which releases geothermal heated water)
