### Principle of Compositionality 複合性原理/組合原則/弗雷格原理

>[!Definition of Compositionality]+
> $\longrightarrow$ Philosophy of language & Semantics (concept)
> **Constitution** $\left\{ \begin{array}{rcl} \huge{\mbox{Productivity 生產力}}\\\huge{\mbox{Systematicity 系統性}}\end{array}\right.$
> Understanding whole sentence by using a priori fragmentation.
> 
![Compositionality. Take the example of oscillatory membranes. In order... |  Download Scientific Diagram](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/348781832/figure/fig2/AS:984459654488082@1611724880617/Compositionality-Take-the-example-of-oscillatory-membranes-In-order-to-analyze-them.png)

>[!Instantiations]+
>***Python Programming***
>>If (temperature > 30):
>>	Print ("It's hot.")
>>Else:
>>	Print ("It's not hot.")
>- - -
>***Sentence Composition:***
>John plays the piano, and Mary sings beautifully. $\longrightarrow$ Composed sentence
> $\underrightarrow{Divided}$ $\left\{ \begin{array}{rcl} \mbox{John plays the piano.}\\\mbox{Mary singss beautifully.}\end{array}\right.$ 
> - - -
> ***Phrase composition:***
> <span style="background:rgba(74, 82, 199, 0.2)">Red rose</span> with a <span style="background:rgba(240, 200, 0, 0.2)">fragrant aroma.</span>
> - - -
> Mathematical composition:
> $\because$ a=3, b=5
> $\therefore$ a+b=8
> - - -
> ***Logical composition (Syllogism, Deductive reasoning)***
> - Original Proposition >>>
> 	- Humans are mortal. (Major premise)
> 	- Kafka is human. (Minor premise)
> - Composed Proposition >>>
> 	- Kafka is mortal. (Conclusion)
