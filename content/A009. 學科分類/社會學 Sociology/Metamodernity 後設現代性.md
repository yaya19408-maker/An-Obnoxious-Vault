# Metamodernity 後設現代性
探討現代生活中的浪漫與矛盾

When the word “metamodernity” is searched, Google’s suggestion is “metamodernism”. The only relevant result on the first page is the book of the same name by Lene Rachel Andersen. Conversely, the search for “metamodernism” results in varied content popular and scientific articles, books, videos, and others. This is to be expected, because we are more used to talking about isms, and because they are the terms dealing with arts, philosophy and culture in the strict sense. Nevertheless, it is important to note that there is also a certain circumspection when it comes to theorising metamodernity. Perhaps that is due, on the one hand, to the failing of the concept of postmodernity, and on the other, to the “death” of grand categories reaffirmed by poststructuralism.
**當你搜尋「metamodernity」這個詞時，Google 的建議是「metamodernism」。在第一頁中唯一相關的結果是 Lene Rachel Andersen 所著的同名書籍。相反，搜尋「metamodernism」則會出現各種內容，包括熱門和科學文章、書籍、視頻等。這是可以預期的，因為我們更習慣於談論「主義」，而這些術語主要涉及藝術、哲學和文化。然而，需要注意的是，對於理論化「metamodernity」，仍存在一定的謹慎態度。這可能一方面是因為後現代性的概念失敗，另一方面則是因為後結構主義所強調的大類別的「死亡」。**

Metamodernity is an alternative to both modernity and postmodernism, a cultural code that presents itself as an opportunity if we work deliberately towards it. It is a vision, an option and a possible future scenario. As a cultural code, metamodernity contains both indigenous, premodern, modern, and postmodern cultural elements and thus provides social norms and a moral fabric for intimacy, spirituality, individuality, and complex thinking. It has the potential to protect our cultures and cultural heritage as the economy, the internet and exponential technologies are going global and disrupting our current modes of societal organization and governance.
**Metamodernity 是對現代性和後現代主義的替代選擇，是一種文化代碼，如果我們有意識地朝著這個方向努力，它將提供一個機會。這是一種願景、一種選擇、一種可能的未來場景。作為一種文化代碼，metamodernity 包含了土著、前現代、現代和後現代的文化元素，從而為親密關係、精神性、個體性和複雜思維提供了社會規範和道德紋理。它具有保護我們的文化和文化遺產的潛力，因為經濟、互聯網和指數技術正在全球化並擾亂我們目前的社會組織和治理模式。**

Metamodernity is a cultural possibility through which we can enjoy the cultural heritage that allows us meaning making at a deep existential and emotional level. It also allows us to look forward in ways not yet familiar to us. Metamodernity can allow us to appreciate the entire historical human experience as a meaningful and connected whole. This human experience in all its many aspects is not something we are above or even outside, we are embedded in it. We belong in it and it can allow us to seek out different kinds of knowledge and wisdom in different places for different purposes. Personal intimacy, strong communities, solid science, robust institutions, spirituality, faith, cultural heritage, satire, facts, knowledge, personal freedom, responsibility, and a sense of belonging and connectedness are all crucial. They serve different purposes in our lives, and each one of them provides an irreplaceable part of a meaningful life in a complex world. Metamodernity offers to contain and promote it all. Neither modernity nor postmodernity were anticipated, they just emerged, but thanks to modernity and postmodernity we now have knowledge about ourselves, our heritage and the deeper structures in global development that we never had before, and we, both as individuals, as societies and as a species, therefore have options and choices we never had before. Metamodernity is a cultural code, but it is also a choice; it is something we can choose to co-create. I would say that it is a choice we need to make, as opposed to just waiting for whatever will emerge.
**Metamodernity 是一種文化的可能性，通過它我們可以享受能夠在深層存在和情感層面上賦予我們意義的文化遺產。它也讓我們能夠以我們尚不熟悉的方式展望未來。Metamodernity 可以讓我們欣賞整個人類歷史經驗，將其視為一個有意義且相互聯繫的整體。這種人類經驗的各個方面並不是我們超越或置身之外的東西，我們是嵌入其中的。我們屬於它，它可以讓我們在不同的地方為不同的目的尋求不同種類的知識和智慧。個人親密關係、強大的社區、穩固的科學、健全的機構、精神性、信仰、文化遺產、諷刺、事實、知識、個人自由、責任感以及歸屬感和連接感，這些都是至關重要的。它們在我們的生活中扮演著不同的角色，每一個都為在複雜世界中擁有有意義的生活提供了不可替代的一部分。Metamodernity 提供了一個包含和促進這一切的機會。無論是現代性還是後現代性，它們都不是被預見的，它們只是自然出現，但由於現代性和後現代性，我們現在對自己、對我們的遺產以及全球發展中更深層的結構有了前所未有的了解，因此，無論是作為個人、社會還是物種，我們都有了前所未有的選擇。Metamodernity 是一種文化代碼，但它也是一種選擇；這是我們可以選擇共同創造的東西。我認為這是一個我們需要做出的選擇，而不是僅僅等待無論將會出現什麼。**
