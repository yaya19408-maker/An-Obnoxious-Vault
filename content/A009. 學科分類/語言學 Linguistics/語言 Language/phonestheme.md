# Phonestheme
a pattern of sounds systematically paired with a certain meaning in a language.

A phonestheme is different from a phoneme (a basic unit of word-differentiating sound) or a morpheme (a basic unit of meaning) because it does not meet the normal criterion of compositionality.
(不屬於emic and etic此類基本單位)
(歸類於皮爾斯符號學的icon)

Phonesthemes (a technical term for meaningful sound patterns) are sub-morphemic units that play a role of morphemes but have been traditionally distinguished from them by being non-compositional (but see Rhodes Rhodes, 2006 for an opposite view).
