## What is an AI? (DEFINITION)
- the capability of a digital computer pr computer-controlled robot
	- to perform tasks commonly associated with intelligent beings.

	- decision-making
	- creativity
	- autonomy
- Key areas
	- machine learning
	- deep learning

- Enterprises

	- Meta

	- Google

## What is Machine translation (MT)?
- **Definition of MT**
	- as an automatic translation of text from one language to another
- **History of MT**
	- in the 1950s
	- by Warren Weaver
	- rule-based systems
		- linguistic rules and dictionaries
			- hard to handle ambiguous language and grammar complexities
	- Georgetown-IBM Experiment (1954)
		- for political usage (Russian to English)
- **Statistical Machine Translation (SMT) 統計機翻**
	- ex: early versions of Google Translate
	- function: using large copora of bilingual text
	- use statistical method based on bilingual text corpora
	- introduce probabilities to translation 
		- but struggled with fluency and accuracy
- **Neural Machine Translation (NMT) 神經網絡機翻** and AI involvement
	- ex: DeepL, the current version of Google Translate
	- function: neurological simulation, high accuracy
	- introduce AI and neural networks to improve translation by learning contextual information, not just word-for-word ("**contextual**")
- **Large Language Model (LLM) 大語言模型**
	- pre-trained large language models, which generate human-like text and power chatbots
## Evolution of MT: From Rule-based to AI-powered
- real-time language translation and communication
- example: **SYSTRAN** (early EU translation)
- **SMT**: Google translate (pre-2016)
- **NMT**: Google translate (post-2016)
- **LLM**: ChatGPT (post-2022)
- populat MT tools
	- Google Translate
	- DeepL
	- Microsoft Translator
- voice recognition: Google assistant
- customization and domain specialization
	- terminologies, jargons, idiolects, etc.
#### How AI Enhances MT
- **Machine Learning & Deep Learning**
	- use large datasets to train models that understand context, semantics, and syntax
	- 直譯 (formal): word-for-word
	- 意譯 (functional): sense-for-sense
	- deep learning models used by NMT to handle long-range dependencies in sentences
	- Improved context understanding in phrases
		- He went to the bank
			- polysemy
				- financial bank or
				- river bank
			- amphiboly
- **Benefits** of AI in MT
	- improved quality
	- context awareness
	- faster translation speed
	- adaptation to specific domains/disciplines
- **Pros and Cons** of AI in MT
	- Pros
		- speed and cost-effectiveness
			- real-time translation services
		- accuracy and fluency
	- Cons
		- human vs. MT
			- examples of scenarios where human translation is preferred.
		- challenges
			- cultural references, idiom, jargons
			- poetry (emotional, creative, context-heavy translations)
			- human post-editing is required
- AI for repetitive tasks but still requires human oversight for nuance and creativity
- **future implications**
	- future integration with AR/VR
	- personalized translation models (customization, idiolect) 個人化翻譯模型 (客製化，個人方言)
		- real-time voice translation for formal and international occasions 國際正式場合即時人聲翻譯
		- personal preference 個人偏好
	- other trends
		- multimodal translation 多模態翻譯
		- AI-driven localization AI在地化 (翻作本地語言)

# 2024.09.18 AI Translation
## How to review a tranlation?
- **Measurements 衡量方法:**
	- human
	- machine
- **Machine Translation Post-Editing (MTPE) 機器後編修**: 先機翻再人工檢查
	- steps 步驟:
		- prepare the ST
		- run MT system
		- review and edit the output
			- review:
				- read the entire MT output first
					- you can read ST first
				- compare the ST
			- edit:
				- correct errors (grammar, meaning, style)
				- ensure consistent terminology
					- jargons
				- adapt cultural references if needed
					- pun
					- phrase
					- idioms
					- proverbs
					- dialect (glottolect, sociolect, idolect...)
				- improve fluency and readability (legibility)
		- quality assurance
	- types 種類:
		- light post-editing: for essential corrections only
		- full-post editing: for human translation quality
	- best practices 最好具備:
		- style guides: 行規、格式要求 
		- glossaries: for CONSISTENCY
		- track common MT errors to improve future outputs
		- balance speed with quality requirements
		- tools 工具:
			- CAT (Computer-Assisted Translators) 電腦輔助翻譯器
			- QA checkers (spell check) 品質檢測(拼字檢查)
			- terminology management systems 術語管理系統
- **Translation Review**
	- **criteria**
		- accuracy 準不準
		- fluency 流不流暢
		- style 文體
		- terminology consistency 術語是否一致
		- target audience appropriateness 受眾/使用者
	- **structure**
		- standardized quality assessment framework
			- LISA
			- QA Model
			- SAE J2450
			- MQM
	- **different types**
		- Bilingual review. Compare ST and TT
		- Monolingual review. Check TT for readability and naturalness
			- 給人看譯文有沒有理解錯誤
		- Back translation. Translate the TT back to the ST to check for major discrepancies
	- **common errors**
		- mistranslations
		- omisions or additions
		- grammar and syntax issues
		- spelling and punctuation errors
		- inconsistent terminologies
	- **context and purpose**
		- ensure the translation fits the intended use
			- technical
			- legal
			- marketing
		- verify cultural appropriateness
	- **scoring system for MT**
		- assign weights (權重) to different error types
			- mistranslation is the most severe, and then omission
		- calculate overall quality scores
	- **constructive feedback**
		- offer specific examples of issues found
		- suggest improvements
			- track common errors to refine translation processes
			- update style guides and glossaries based on reviews

# 2024.09.25 Machine Translation
## Comparison
- MT (shortcomings)
	- lacks an accurate depiction of context.
	- found to be lexically poorer than human translations.
	- the loss of lexical diversity through MT
		- poses an issue in the automatic translation of literature, which can fundamentally alter the reading experience and is not just about conveying information, but about creating an aesthetic and emotional experience through language.
			- nuance and connotation
			- stylistic variation
			- cultural context
- contextual rephrasing
	- make translated sentences less ambiguous and awkward
- prompt
	- learn to make your language more accurate, delicate and logical (rather tham bland, poor, and illogical)
	- read, write, translate (more times)
	- perspectives and empathy
- genre 文類；文體 $\approxeq$ text type, style, category
	- 中: 散文、韻文、小說
	- 西: 史詩 epic、敘述體 narrative、抒情詩 lyric、詩歌 poetry、戲劇 drama
	- 論說文、記敘文、新聞稿、合約
	- Reiss' Text Types: informative (資訊正確), expressive (忠於原著), operative (公眾呼籲)
